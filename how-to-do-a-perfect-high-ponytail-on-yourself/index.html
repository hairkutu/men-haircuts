How To Do A Perfect High Ponytail On Yourself

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having to go to the salon every time you want a perfect high ponytail? Well, you don't have to anymore! In this article, we will show you how to do a perfect high ponytail on yourself in just a few simple steps.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get into the step-by-step process, let's first talk about the things you will need to achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • A brush or comb
  • A hair tie
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step by step

Now that you have everything you need, let's start with the step-by-step process:

  1. Start by brushing or combing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair at the crown of your head and hold it with one hand.
  3. With your other hand, take the hair tie and wrap it around your hair once or twice, depending on the thickness of your hair.
  4. On the last wrap, instead of pulling your hair all the way through, leave a small loop of hair sticking out.
  5. Take that loop and pull it down over the hair tie, hiding it from sight.
  6. Fluff up your ponytail to give it some volume and shape.
  7. Use hairspray to keep your ponytail in place (optional).

And there you have it, a perfect high ponytail that you did all on your own!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • If you have a lot of flyaways, use a small amount of hairspray or gel to keep them in place.
  • If you want a sleeker look, use a brush to smooth out your hair before tying it into a ponytail.
  • If you want a more voluminous look, tease your hair at the crown before tying it into a ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a perfect high ponytail:

Q: How high should the ponytail be?

A: It's up to your personal preference, but a good rule of thumb is to tie it at the crown of your head.

Q: Can I do a perfect high ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes, but it may be more difficult to achieve the same level of volume as with longer hair.

Q: How do I prevent my ponytail from slipping down?

A: Use a hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after doing a high ponytail. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Now that you know how to do a perfect high ponytail on yourself, you can rock this hairstyle anytime, anywhere! With a little practice, you'll be a pro in no time.
