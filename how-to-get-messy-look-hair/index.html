How To Get Messy Look Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks effortlessly cool, the messy look hair is the way to go. This hairstyle has been popular for years and shows no signs of going out of style anytime soon. Getting the messy look hair is easy, but you need to know the right techniques to achieve it. In this article, we will show you how to get the messy look hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Messy Look Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Messy Look Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Messy Look Hair

How to Get Messy Look Hair

The key to achieving the messy look hair is to keep your hair looking natural and undone. This hairstyle works best for people with medium to long hair. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Start with clean hair.
  • Avoid using too much product.
  • Use the right products.
  • Use your fingers to create texture.
  • Don't overthink it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect messy look hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or grime and make your hair easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it until it's damp. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can make your hair look too styled.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a product that is lightweight and won't make your hair look greasy. We recommend using a texturizing spray, sea salt spray or a styling cream.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers, style your hair in a messy and undone way. Create texture by twisting and scrunching your hair with your fingers.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a light hold hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place without making it look too styled.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Look Hair

Here are some tips to keep your messy look hair looking great:

  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.

FAQs

Q: Can I get the messy look hair with short hair?

A: The messy look hair works best for people with medium to long hair. However, you can still achieve a similar look with shorter hair by using the right products and styling techniques.

Q: Can I use a hairdryer to style my hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a hairdryer as it can make your hair look too styled. Instead, towel dry your hair and let it air dry naturally.

Hair Care for Messy Look Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and in good condition:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

Now that you know how to get the messy look hair, it's time to give it a try. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve this effortless hairstyle in no time. Remember to keep it natural and undone, and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
