How To Use Hair Pick A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly or textured hair, a hair pick may be an essential tool in your hair care routine. A hair pick is a comb with long, widely spaced teeth that can help you detangle and style your hair without causing too much damage. In this article, we'll take you through the steps of using a hair pick and share some tips to make the most out of this tool.

How to Use Hair Pick

Before using a hair pick, make sure your hair is clean, detangled, and moisturized. You can use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to hydrate your hair and make it easier to comb through.

Step 1: Start at the Ends

Begin by holding your hair at the roots with one hand and using the hair pick with the other. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up towards the roots. This will help you avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, which can cause breakage and damage.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Once you've reached the roots, use the hair pick to gently detangle any knots or tangles in your hair. Be patient and use small, gentle strokes to avoid causing damage to your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is detangled, you can use the hair pick to style your hair. You can create volume by picking your hair at the roots or create defined curls by picking your hair in sections.

Tips for Using Hair Pick

Here are some tips to make the most out of your hair pick:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb First

If your hair is very curly or tangled, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before using a hair pick. This will make it easier to use the hair pick without causing too much damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help hydrate your hair and make it easier to comb through. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair before using a hair pick.

Tip 3: Don't Use a Hair Pick on Wet Hair

Using a hair pick on wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a hair pick.

FAQs about Using Hair Pick

Q: Can I use a hair pick on straight hair?

A: While a hair pick is designed for curly and textured hair, you can still use it on straight hair to create volume or add texture.

Q: How often should I use a hair pick?

A: You can use a hair pick as often as you like, but be careful not to overuse it. Using a hair pick too often can cause damage to your hair.

Q: Can a hair pick cause hair breakage?

A: Yes, using a hair pick too aggressively or on wet hair can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Use a hair pick gently and on dry hair to avoid causing damage.

Hair Care for Curly and Textured Hair

Using a hair pick is just one part of a healthy hair care routine for curly and textured hair. Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using a hair pick correctly, you can keep your curly and textured hair looking healthy and beautiful.
