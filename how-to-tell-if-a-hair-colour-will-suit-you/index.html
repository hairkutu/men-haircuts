How To Tell If A Hair Colour Will Suit You

Description

Choosing a hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which shade will suit you best. There are many factors to consider, including your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to tell if a hair colour will suit you, and how to choose the perfect shade for your skin tone and style.

How to

There are several ways to determine if a hair colour will suit you:

1. Consider Your Skin Tone:

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. If you have warm undertones, you should stick to warm shades like golden blondes, caramel browns, and copper reds. If you have cool undertones, you should opt for cooler shades like ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

2. Look at Your Eye Colour:

Your eye colour can also help you determine which hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, you should go for warmer shades that will bring out the warmth in your eyes. If you have brown eyes, you can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

3. Consider Your Natural Hair Colour:

Your natural hair colour can also help you determine which shade will suit you best. If you have dark hair, you may want to stick to darker shades like chocolate browns or deep reds. If you have light hair, you can experiment with lighter shades like platinum blondes or pastel colours.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to choose the perfect hair colour:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone:

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

2. Look at Your Eye Colour:

Determine which shades will bring out the warmth or coolness in your eyes.

3. Consider Your Natural Hair Colour:

Determine which shades will complement your natural hair colour.

4. Consult with Your Hairstylist:

Your hairstylist can help you choose the perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour.
  • Take into account the maintenance required for different hair colours.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colours before committing to a permanent change.
  • Consider the season when choosing a hair colour.

FAQ

What hair colours are best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with shades like golden blondes, caramel browns, and copper reds.

What hair colours are best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with shades like ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but you should choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take extra care of it to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a colour-protecting shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid hot showers and styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your new hair colour looks great and stays healthy.
