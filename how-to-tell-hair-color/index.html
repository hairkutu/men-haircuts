How To Tell Hair Color A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you curious about how to tell hair color? Maybe you're trying to identify your own hair color or want to know the color of someone else's hair. Whatever the reason, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to identify hair color with ease.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Accurately Identifying Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Hair Color

How to Tell Hair Color

There are a few different ways to tell hair color:

  • Visual inspection of the hair
  • Asking the person directly
  • Using a color chart or swatches

Each of these methods has its advantages and disadvantages. Visual inspection can be subjective, and asking the person directly may not be possible or accurate. Using a color chart or swatches can be helpful but may not be an exact match.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to identify hair color with accuracy, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Observe the hair in good lighting to see its true color.
  2. Look for the undertones in the hair, which can be warm or cool.
  3. Identify the level of the hair color, which ranges from 1 (black) to 10 (lightest blonde).
  4. Determine the hair color family, such as blonde, brown, red, or black.
  5. Take note of any highlights or lowlights in the hair.

Tips for Accurately Identifying Hair Color

Here are a few additional tips to help you accurately identify hair color:

  • Use natural light or a bright light source to get the best view of the hair color.
  • Compare the hair to a color chart or swatch to get an idea of the color family.
  • Consider the person's skin tone when identifying the hair color.
  • Take note of any changes in the hair color over time, as hair color can fade or change with age or environmental factors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about identifying hair color:

What if the person has dyed their hair?

If the person has dyed their hair, it can be more difficult to identify the natural hair color. Look for any regrowth or areas where the color has faded to get a better idea of the natural hair color.

Can hair color change over time?

Yes, hair color can change over time due to a variety of factors, including age, hormones, and environmental factors. It's important to take note of any changes in the hair color over time.

What is the best lighting to identify hair color?

Natural light or a bright light source is the best lighting to identify hair color. Avoid dim or yellow-tinted lighting, as this can alter the appearance of the hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Hair Color

If you want to maintain your hair color or keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect the hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Identifying hair color can be a fun and useful skill to have. Whether you're trying to identify your own hair color or someone else's, these tips and tricks will help you do so with ease.
