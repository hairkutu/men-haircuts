Hairstyles With Layers And Side Bangs A Timeless Look

Written by Petter Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh and modern look? Look no further than hairstyles with layers and side bangs. This classic look has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to achieve this look, step by step, answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

How to Achieve Hairstyles with Layers and Side Bangs

Hairstyles with layers and side bangs are perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. This look can be achieved with any hair length, from short to long. The layers will add movement to your hair, while the side bangs will frame your face and give it a softer, more feminine look.

To achieve this look, you will need to start by consulting with your hairstylist. They will help you determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you have decided on the length and style, your hair will need to be cut into layers. Your stylist will use a razor or scissors to create different lengths of hair, which will add volume and texture to your hair.

After your hair has been cut into layers, your stylist will move on to cutting your side bangs. Side bangs can be cut at different lengths, depending on your preference. Your hairstylist will take into consideration your face shape and hair type when cutting your side bangs to ensure that they frame your face perfectly.

Step by Step Guide

If you want to try achieving this look at home, here is a step by step guide:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Using a comb, section off the top layer of your hair and clip it up.
  3. Cut the bottom layer of your hair into layers using scissors or a razor. Start by cutting the hair closest to your neck and work your way up.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the top layer of your hair.
  5. Using a comb, separate a section of hair that is about an inch wide from the front of your hairline.
  6. Cut the section of hair into side bangs. Start by cutting the hair longer than you want it to be, as you can always go back and cut it shorter if needed.
  7. Repeat step 6 with the other side of your hair.
  8. Using your fingers, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have achieved your desired look, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain your hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it is wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain your layers.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

Absolutely! This look can be achieved with any hair texture, including curly hair. Your hairstylist will need to cut your hair while it is dry and in its natural state to ensure that the layers and side bangs are cut correctly.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

We recommend visiting your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain your layers.

What face shapes are best suited for side bangs?

Side bangs can be flattering on any face shape, but they are especially suited for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as this is gentler on your hair than a regular towel.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Hairstyles with layers and side bangs are a timeless look that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. Whether you want a short or long hairstyle, this look is versatile and can be adapted to suit your needs. By following our tips and hair care advice, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. So, why not give this classic look a try?
