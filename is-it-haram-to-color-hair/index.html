Is It Haram To Color Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As Muslims, we are always conscious of following the rules and regulations set by Islam. One question that often arises is whether coloring hair is haram or not. In this article, we will explore the topic in depth to help you understand whether it is allowed or not.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Color Hair in Islam
  3. Step by Step Guide to Coloring Hair in Islam
  4. Tips for Coloring Hair in Islam
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair in Islam

Description

Hair coloring is a popular practice among women and men of all ages. It is a way of changing one's appearance and can be done for various reasons like covering gray hair, enhancing natural hair color, or simply for fashion. However, in Islam, everything we do has to be in line with the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah. Therefore, it is important to understand whether coloring hair is permissible or not.

How to Color Hair in Islam

Coloring hair in Islam is allowed as long as certain conditions are met. The color must be natural and not harmful to the hair or scalp. It should not be done to imitate non-Muslims or in a way that attracts unnecessary attention. Moreover, it is recommended to seek the advice of a knowledgeable Islamic scholar before coloring hair to ensure that it is permissible in Islam.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Hair in Islam

Here is a step by step guide to coloring hair in Islam:

  1. Choose a natural hair color that is allowed in Islam.
  2. Perform a patch test to ensure that the color is not harmful to the hair or scalp.
  3. Follow the instructions on the hair color package carefully.
  4. Apply the color to the hair, making sure to cover all strands evenly.
  5. Leave the color on for the recommended time and then rinse it off with water.
  6. Condition the hair to restore its moisture and shine.

Tips for Coloring Hair in Islam

Here are some tips to keep in mind when coloring hair in Islam:

  • Choose a natural hair color that complements your skin tone and features.
  • Avoid using hair colors that contain harmful chemicals like ammonia and bleach.
  • Do not color your hair to imitate non-Muslims or in a way that attracts unnecessary attention.
  • If you are unsure about whether a particular hair color is allowed in Islam, seek the advice of a knowledgeable Islamic scholar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about coloring hair in Islam:

Is it haram to color hair in Islam?

No, coloring hair in Islam is allowed as long as certain conditions are met. The color must be natural and not harmful to the hair or scalp. It should not be done to imitate non-Muslims or in a way that attracts unnecessary attention.

What hair colors are allowed in Islam?

Natural hair colors like black, brown, and red are allowed in Islam. However, it is recommended to seek the advice of a knowledgeable Islamic scholar before coloring hair to ensure that it is permissible in Islam.

Can men color their hair in Islam?

Yes, men can color their hair in Islam as long as they follow the same conditions as women. The color must be natural and not harmful to the hair or scalp. It should not be done to imitate non-Muslims or in a way that attracts unnecessary attention.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair in Islam

Here are some hair care tips for colored hair in Islam:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color and prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage the hair and fade the color.
  • Protect the hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Trim the ends of the hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, coloring hair in Islam is allowed as long as certain conditions are met. It is important to choose a natural hair color that is not harmful to the hair or scalp and to avoid imitating non-Muslims or attracting unnecessary attention. Seek the advice of a knowledgeable Islamic scholar if you are unsure about whether a particular hair color is allowed in Islam. With proper care, colored hair can look beautiful and healthy.
