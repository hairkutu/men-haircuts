How To Make Face Appear Rounder Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Apr 07, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have a square or angular face, you may want to soften your features and make your face appear rounder. While genetics play a major role in the shape of your face, there are ways to enhance your natural features with makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises. In this article, we will discuss various tips and tricks to make your face appear rounder.

How to Make Face Appear Rounder

If you want to make your face appear rounder, there are a few things you can do. First, you can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a rounder shape. You can also try different hairstyles that flatter your face shape. In addition, facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and create a more rounded appearance. Here are some tips to get started:

Makeup Tips

Makeup can be a powerful tool to enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some tips to try:

  • Use a matte bronzer or contour powder to create shadows on the sides of your face. Apply the product in a "3" shape, starting from your forehead, down to your cheeks, and then to your jawline. Blend well for a natural-looking effect.
  • Highlight the center of your face with a light-colored concealer or highlighter. Apply the product on your forehead, nose bridge, under-eye area, and chin. This will help create the illusion of a fuller, more rounded face.
  • Avoid using harsh lines or heavy products, as this can make your face look angular and harsh.

Hairstyle Tips

Your hairstyle can have a big impact on how your face looks. Here are some tips to try:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or severe, such as a blunt bob or a straight fringe. Instead, opt for softer, more rounded styles, such as loose waves or a layered haircut. This will help soften your features and create a more rounded appearance.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can make your face look more angular. Instead, try a loose ponytail or a messy bun.
  • Consider getting bangs or a side fringe, as this can help create the illusion of a rounder face.

Facial Exercise Tips

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and create a more rounded appearance. Here are some exercises to try:

  • Puff up your cheeks and hold for 10 seconds. Release and repeat 10 times.
  • Open your mouth wide and stick out your tongue as far as you can. Hold for 10 seconds and repeat 10 times.
  • Smile as wide as you can and hold for 10 seconds. Release and repeat 10 times.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to make your face appear rounder:

  1. Start by applying a light foundation or BB cream to even out your skin tone.
  2. Use a matte bronzer or contour powder to create shadows on the sides of your face. Apply the product in a "3" shape, starting from your forehead, down to your cheeks, and then to your jawline. Blend well for a natural-looking effect.
  3. Highlight the center of your face with a light-colored concealer or highlighter. Apply the product on your forehead, nose bridge, under-eye area, and chin.
  4. Blend well for a natural-looking effect.
  5. Avoid using harsh lines or heavy products, as this can make your face look angular and harsh.
  6. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or severe, and opt for softer, more rounded styles.
  7. Consider getting bangs or a side fringe, as this can help create the illusion of a rounder face.
  8. Try facial exercises to tone your facial muscles and create a more rounded appearance.
  9. Complete your look with a soft, natural makeup look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you've achieved the rounder face you desire, here are some tips to maintain your new look:

  • Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant.
  • Eat a healthy, well-balanced diet to maintain a healthy weight and keep your skin looking its best.
  • Get plenty of rest to reduce stress and promote healthy skin.
  • Protect your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen and avoiding prolonged exposure to UV rays.

FAQs

Q: Can facial exercises really make my face appear rounder?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and create a more rounded appearance. However, results may vary depending on your genetics and the amount of time and effort you put in.

Q: Do I have to use makeup to make my face appear rounder?

A: No, makeup is not necessary to make your face appear rounder. However, it can be a helpful tool if you want to enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a rounder face.

Q: How long will it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: Results may vary depending on your genetics and the amount of time and effort you put in. However, you should start to see some results within a few weeks of consistent practice.

Hair Care Tips

Good hair care practices can help keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to try:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as curling irons and straighteners, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye, as this can damage your hair and scalp.

By following these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural features and make your face appear rounder. Whether you choose to use makeup, hairstyles, or facial exercises, remember to embrace your unique features and love the skin you're in.
