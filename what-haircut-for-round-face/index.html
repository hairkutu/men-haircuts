What Haircut For Round Face A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Oliver Apr 11, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what haircut would best flatter your features. A round face is characterized by a circular shape with the same width and length, and often features soft, fuller cheeks. The right haircut can help elongate and slim the face, making you feel more confident and stylish. In this guide, we’ll explore the best haircuts for round faces, how to choose the right style, and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Round Face

When choosing a haircut for your round face, there are a few key factors to consider:

Length

The length of your hair can greatly impact the appearance of your face. Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can make it appear wider. Consider a longer layered cut or a shoulder-length bob to add depth and dimension to your face.

Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in the appearance of your face. Soft waves or curls can add volume to the top of your head, balancing out the fullness of your cheeks. Straight hair can elongate your face, while tight curls or kinks can make it appear rounder.

Layers

Layers can be a great way to add dimension and depth to your face. Consider a layered cut that starts at your cheekbones and falls below your shoulders. This can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut

Follow these steps to choose the right haircut for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a look in the mirror and determine the shape of your face. Is it circular with soft, fuller cheeks? If so, you likely have a round face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about the natural texture of your hair. Is it straight, wavy, or curly? This can impact the type of haircut that will flatter your features.

Step 3: Think About Length

Consider the length of your hair. Do you want to keep it long or go for a shorter cut? Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can make it appear wider.

Step 4: Look for Inspiration

Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or on social media. Search for haircuts that flatter round faces and save them to show your stylist.

Step 5: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your features. They can provide expert advice and help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your round face, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your new cut:

Tip 1: Visit Your Stylist Regularly

Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep it looking its best. For example, if you have curly hair, use products that enhance and define your curls.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

Tip 4: Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. This can help you maintain a healthy, natural look.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds dimension and elongates the face. Consider a layered cut, a shoulder-length bob, or long, layered hair.

Can short hair flatter a round face?

Short hair can flatter a round face, but it’s important to choose the right cut. Consider a short, layered cut or a pixie cut that adds volume to the top of your head.

What products should I use for my hair type?

Use products that are designed for your hair type, whether it’s straight, wavy, curly, or kinky. Look for products that enhance your natural texture and provide the right level of moisture and hydration.

How often should I trim my hair?

Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care for Round Faces

When caring for your hair, it’s important to choose products that are designed for your hair type and to visit your stylist regularly for trims. Embrace your natural texture and work with it to maintain a healthy, natural look.

Choosing the right haircut for your round face can help elongate and slim your features, making you feel more confident and stylish. Consider the length, texture, and layers of your hair to find a style that flatters your face shape and hair type. Consult with your stylist for expert advice and follow these tips to maintain your new look.
