Layered Bob Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. However, layered bob hairstyles are perfect for thick wavy hair as they add movement, texture, and dimension to your locks. In this article, we will guide you through the process of achieving the perfect layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair. Let's get started!

Description

Layered bob hairstyles for thick wavy hair are trendy and versatile. They are perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their locks. The hairstyle features layers that are cut into the hair, creating a textured and voluminous look. The layers can be long, short, or somewhere in between, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve Layered Bob Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

The process of achieving layered bob hairstyles for thick wavy hair involves several steps. Here's how you can achieve this look:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your hairstylist. They will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best layered bob hairstyle that suits you.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair, and then towel dry it. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the styling tools.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting from the roots to the ends. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair in layers, starting from the back and then moving to the sides. They may also use a razor to add texture to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or a texturizing spray. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair, starting from the mid-shaft to the ends. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Bob Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your layered bob hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your layered bob hairstyle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

FAQ

Q: Will layered bob hairstyles suit my face shape?

A: Layered bob hairstyles are versatile and can suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best layered bob hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Q: Can I achieve layered bob hairstyles for thick wavy hair at home?

A: We recommend that you consult with a hairstylist to achieve the perfect layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair. However, if you have experience styling your hair, you can try to achieve the look at home.

Hair Care for Layered Bob Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and beauty of your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your layered bob hairstyle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and hair products that can damage your hair.

In conclusion, layered bob hairstyles for thick wavy hair are trendy, versatile, and perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their locks. With proper hair care and maintenance, you can achieve the perfect layered bob hairstyle that suits your face shape and lifestyle. Consult with your hairstylist to achieve the perfect layered bob hairstyle for you. Happy styling!
