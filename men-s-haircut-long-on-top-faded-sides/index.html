Men s Haircut Long On Top Faded Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish men's haircut? Look no further than the long on top faded sides haircut. This versatile haircut is perfect for men of all ages and can be easily customized to suit your personal style. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about the men's haircut long on top faded sides, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The men's haircut long on top faded sides is a contemporary take on the classic short back and sides haircut. It features longer hair on the top of the head that gradually fades into shorter hair on the sides and back. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and modern look that can be easily dressed up or down.

How To

If you want to get the men's haircut long on top faded sides, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. Here are the steps they will typically follow:

Step 1: Consultation

The hairstylist will start by asking you about your personal style and the look you want to achieve. They will also assess the texture and thickness of your hair to determine the best approach for the cut.

Step 2: Clipper Cut

The hairstylist will use clippers to create a gradual fade on the sides and back of your head. They will start with a longer guard and gradually switch to shorter guards to create a seamless transition between the longer and shorter hair.

Step 3: Scissor Cut

The hairstylist will use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. They will typically leave the hair longer on top and gradually shorten it towards the back of your head.

Step 4: Styling

The hairstylist will style your hair using a combination of products and tools to create your desired look. They may use a blow dryer to add volume to the hair on top and a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your men's haircut long on top faded sides:

  • Visit a professional hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for maintenance.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Is the men's haircut long on top faded sides suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does the men's haircut long on top faded sides typically take to style?

A: The styling time can vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair, but it typically takes around 10-15 minutes to style.

Q: Can I wear the men's haircut long on top faded sides to a formal event?

A: Yes, this haircut can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To make it more formal, you can use a stronger hold product and comb your hair into a more polished style.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut long on top faded sides looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy and moisturized:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips, you can keep your men's haircut long on top faded sides looking stylish and healthy for months to come.
