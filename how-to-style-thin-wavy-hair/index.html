How To Style Thin Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter May 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thin wavy hair, you may feel like styling it is a challenge. But with the right techniques, you can create beautiful, voluminous hairstyles that will make you feel confident and stunning. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for styling thin wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thin Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Thin Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Wavy Hair

How to Style Thin Wavy Hair

The key to styling thin wavy hair is to create volume and texture. Here are some tips for achieving this:

  • Start with clean, washed hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your hair. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your natural curls and waves, and create volume.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Hold the iron vertically and wrap sections of hair around it, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  • Finish with a volumizing hairspray to add texture and hold to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling thin wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, scrunching your hair as you dry to enhance your natural curls and waves.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Hold the iron vertically and wrap sections of hair around it, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  6. Use your fingers to gently separate and tousle your curls for a more natural look.
  7. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to add texture and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips for Styling Thin Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling thin wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing, and avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Consider getting layers in your hair to add volume and movement.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids and updos, to add variety to your look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thin wavy hair:

Can I straighten my thin wavy hair?

Yes, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the flat iron on a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair.

What haircuts are best for thin wavy hair?

Layered haircuts can add volume and movement to thin wavy hair. Consider a long bob or shoulder-length cut with layers.

How often should I wash my thin wavy hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. Use a dry shampoo on the days between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

With these tips and tricks, you can create beautiful hairstyles for your thin wavy hair. Remember to be gentle with your hair and use products that add volume and texture, and you’ll have gorgeous, voluminous locks in no time!
