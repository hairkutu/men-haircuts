How To Check If A Haircut Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting a new haircut that doesn't suit you? It's frustrating to invest time and money into a new hairstyle only to realize that it doesn't look good on you. Luckily, there are some tricks you can use to check if a haircut suits you before committing to it. In this article, we'll go over some simple steps to help you determine if a new haircut is right for you.

How to Check if a Haircut Suits You: Step by Step

Step 1: Know Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if a haircut suits you is to know your face shape. Face shapes are divided into six categories: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has different features, which can help determine what type of haircut will look best on you. If you're not sure what your face shape is, take a quick online quiz or consult with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect how a haircut looks on you. If you have thick, curly hair, a short, layered cut might not be the best option for you. Similarly, if you have fine, straight hair, a long, blunt cut might not be the most flattering. Consider your hair texture when choosing a new haircut.

Step 3: Look at Photos

One of the best ways to determine if a new haircut will suit you is to look at photos of similar haircuts on people with similar face shapes and hair textures. You can find inspiration on social media platforms like Instagram or Pinterest, or you can browse through magazines. Bring these photos to your hairstylist to get their opinion on whether a particular haircut will suit you.

Step 4: Try on Wigs or Extensions

If you're still unsure whether a new haircut will suit you, try on wigs or extensions in a similar style to the haircut you're considering. This will give you a better idea of how the haircut will look on you without committing to it.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance cut might be best.
  • Consider your hair type. If you have thinning hair, a short cut might not be the best option.
  • Consult with your hairstylist. They can give you advice on what type of haircut will look best on you.
  • Take into consideration your personal style. If you prefer a more edgy look, a pixie cut might be a good option.

FAQs

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, don't panic. Most haircuts can be modified or adjusted to better suit your features. Talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like and they can make changes accordingly.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most people get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found a haircut that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, finding a haircut that suits you can be a challenge, but by following these simple tips and tricks, you can make the process a lot easier. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a new haircut, and don't be afraid to consult with your hairstylist for advice. With a little bit of patience and experimentation, you'll find the perfect haircut in no time!
