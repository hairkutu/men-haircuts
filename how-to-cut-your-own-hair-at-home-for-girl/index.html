How To Cut Your Own Hair At Home For Girls A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own hair at home can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it's possible to achieve a salon-worthy look. Whether you're looking to trim your ends or completely change your style, this step-by-step guide will show you how to cut your own hair at home for girls.

Table of Contents:

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Your Haircut

Tools You'll Need:

Before you get started, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A towel to drape over your shoulders

Prepping Your Hair:

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. It's important to cut your hair when it's dry so you can see the true length and texture. Once your hair is dry, brush it out to remove any tangles.


Sectioning Your Hair:

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by sectioning your hair horizontally at the back of your head, clipping the top section up and out of the way. Then, section the sides of your hair, clipping them up as well. You should have three sections: the top, left side, and right side.


Cutting Your Hair:

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it, and use your scissors to cut straight across. Repeat this process with small sections of hair until you've cut all of the back section.

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Comb the hair straight down and cut it at the desired length. Make sure to keep your cuts straight and even.

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. This is where you'll want to be the most careful, as any mistakes will be more noticeable. Comb the hair straight up and cut it at the desired length. Remember to take small sections and cut slowly to avoid any mistakes.


Tips for a Successful Haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as it's easier to trim more hair than to fix a mistake.
  • Make sure your hair is evenly sectioned and combed straight down.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to, and step away from the mirror to get a fresh perspective.

FAQs:

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.

How do I know how much hair to cut?

Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length. It's always better to cut less than to cut too much. You can always trim more later.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim more hair to fix the mistake. If it's a big mistake, consider going to a professional to fix it.


Hair Care After Your Haircut:

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a deep conditioner to nourish your hair, and avoid using heat tools for a few days to let your hair recover. Trim your hair regularly to maintain your new style.

Cutting your own hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful, salon-worthy haircut from the comfort of your own home.
