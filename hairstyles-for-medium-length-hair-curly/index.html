Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Curly - Tips, FAQs & Care

If you have medium length curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to find hairstyles that work for you. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips, step by step guides, and hair care tips for medium length curly hair, as well as some popular and trendy hairstyles that you can try out.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair
  • How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair
  • Popular Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about some tips for managing medium length curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair

Here's a step by step guide on how to style your medium length curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner and comb through with a wide-tooth comb.
  2. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Scrunch your hair using your hands to encourage the curls.
  4. Diffuse your hair using a blow dryer on low heat and low speed. Use a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Popular Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

Now that you know how to style your medium length curly hair, here are some popular and trendy hairstyles to try:

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down to show off your curls.

2. Side Braid

A side braid is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that works well with medium length curly hair. Braid your hair to one side, keeping it loose to show off your curls.

3. High Bun

A high bun is a chic and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for formal events. Gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.


Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I straighten my medium length curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

2. How often should I wash my medium length curly hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and frizz.

3. Can I dye my medium length curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair, but be sure to use a dye that's specifically formulated for curly hair and to follow the instructions carefully.


Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your medium length curly hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
