How To Do An Undercut Pixie Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a bold and trendy hairstyle, an undercut pixie might be the right choice for you. This style features a closely shaved undercut section with longer hair on top, creating a dramatic contrast of lengths. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to do an undercut pixie at home in 2023, including tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • What is an undercut pixie?
  • How to do an undercut pixie: Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for an undercut pixie

What is an undercut pixie?

An undercut pixie is a short hairstyle that features a buzzed or shaved section on one side or the back of the head. The remaining hair on top is kept longer and styled in a pixie cut, creating a contrast of lengths and textures. This style can be customized in many ways, from the length and shape of the undercut to the color and texture of the top hair.

How to do an undercut pixie: Step-by-step guide

Before you start, gather these tools and materials:

  • Clippers or trimmer
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hair dye (optional)

Here are the steps to do an undercut pixie:

Step 1: Decide on the length and shape of the undercut

The first step is to decide where and how much hair you want to shave or buzz off. This will depend on your personal preference, face shape, and hair texture. You can choose to shave the back or one side of your head, or both. You can also choose to keep the undercut short or long, or to create a specific pattern or design.

Step 2: Section off the hair on top

Use hair clips to section off the hair on top of your head, leaving out the hair that will be shaved or buzzed. This will make it easier to work on the undercut without cutting the top hair too short.

Step 3: Buzz or shave the undercut section

Use clippers or a trimmer to buzz or shave the undercut section. Start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter if needed. If you're not comfortable with using clippers, you can ask a friend or a professional stylist to do it for you. Make sure to follow the direction of the hair growth and to clean up any stray hairs.

Step 4: Blend the undercut with the top hair

Use scissors to blend the undercut with the top hair, creating a seamless transition. Take small sections of hair and cut them at a slight angle, following the shape of the head. You can also use thinning shears or a razor to add texture and soften the edges.

Step 5: Style the top hair

Now that the undercut is done, you can focus on styling the top hair into a pixie cut. Use a comb and scissors to trim the hair to the desired length and shape. You can choose to keep it messy and tousled, or sleek and polished. You can also add highlights, lowlights, or a bold color to make the style pop.

Tips for an undercut pixie

Here are some tips to make your undercut pixie look its best:

  • Choose the right length and shape for your face and hair type
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as pomade, wax, or mousse
  • Try different parting styles, such as a deep side part or a center part
  • Accessorize with headbands, barrettes, or scarves to add a personal touch
  • Maintain the undercut regularly by trimming or shaving it every few weeks

FAQ about undercut pixie

Here are some frequently asked questions about the undercut pixie:

1. Is an undercut pixie suitable for all hair types?

Yes, an undercut pixie can work for all hair types, from straight to curly, fine to thick. However, you may need to adjust the length and shape of the top hair to suit your hair type and texture.

2. How often do I need to maintain the undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep the undercut. Generally, you may need to trim or shave the undercut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

3. Can I color my hair with an undercut pixie?

Yes, you can add color to your hair with an undercut pixie. You can choose to dye the top hair only, or to add color to the undercut as well. However, keep in mind that bleaching or coloring the undercut may require more maintenance and touch-ups.

Hair care for undercut pixie

Here are some hair care tips for an undercut pixie:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling wands, too often
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of the top hair to keep them moisturized
  • Protect your hair and scalp from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV spray
  • Visit a stylist regularly to maintain the shape and health of your hair

With these steps, tips, and hair care advice, you can confidently do an undercut pixie at home in 2023. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or a subtle update, an undercut pixie can be a versatile and stylish choice for any hair type and face shape. Experiment with different lengths, colors, and styling techniques to make the look your own.
