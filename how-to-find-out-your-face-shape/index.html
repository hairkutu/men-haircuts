How To Find Out Your Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 07, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Have you ever wondered what your face shape is? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup looks, and even glasses frames that will flatter your features. In this guide, we'll show you how to find out your face shape, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Find Out Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Description

Before we dive into how to determine your face shape, let's first go over the different face shapes that exist. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and proportions. By knowing which face shape you have, you can highlight your best features and downplay any areas you're not as fond of.

How to Find Out Your Face Shape

There are a few different methods you can use to determine your face shape. Here are the most common ones:

Method #1: The Mirror Method

Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back and your face free of makeup. Look straight ahead and use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Step back and observe the shape you've traced.

If your face is longer than it is wide and your jaw is slightly narrower than your forehead, you likely have an oval face shape. If your face is as wide as it is long and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you likely have a round face shape. If your face is as wide as it is long and your jawline is sharp and angular, you likely have a square face shape.

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow jawline and chin. Diamond-shaped faces are similar to heart-shaped faces, but with a more pointed chin. Rectangle-shaped faces are longer than they are wide, with a strong, angular jawline.

Method #2: The Measurement Method

Grab a tape measure and measure the following areas of your face:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, typically above your eyebrows.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across the widest part of your cheekbones.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to below your ear, then double that measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, compare them to each other:

  • Oval face shape: Your face length measurement is larger than your cheekbones, and your forehead measurement is larger than your jawline measurement.
  • Round face shape: Your face length, cheekbone, and jawline measurements are all similar.
  • Square face shape: Your forehead, cheekbone, and jawline measurements are all similar.
  • Heart-shaped face: Your forehead measurement is larger than your cheekbone and jawline measurements.
  • Diamond-shaped face: Your cheekbone measurement is the largest, and your forehead and jawline measurements are smaller and similar.
  • Rectangle-shaped face: Your face length measurement is the largest, and your forehead, cheekbone, and jawline measurements are all similar.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding out your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back and your face free of makeup.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and observe the shape you've traced.
  4. If your face is longer than it is wide and your jaw is slightly narrower than your forehead, you likely have an oval face shape.
  5. If your face is as wide as it is long and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you likely have a round face shape.
  6. If your face is as wide as it is long and your jawline is sharp and angular, you likely have a square face shape.
  7. If your face is wide at the forehead and cheekbones, and tapers to a narrow jawline and chin, you likely have a heart-shaped face.
  8. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at both the forehead and jawline, you likely have a diamond-shaped face.
  9. If your face is longer than it is wide and has a strong, angular jawline, you likely have a rectangle-shaped face.
  10. Confirm your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length, and comparing the measurements to each other.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when trying to determine your face shape:

  • Make sure your hair is pulled back and your face is free of makeup, so you can see your natural features.
  • If you have trouble determining your face shape, try asking a friend or family member for their opinion.
  • Remember that everyone's face is unique, and you may have a combination of different face shapes.

FAQ

Q: Can my face shape change over time?

A: Your face shape is largely determined by your bone structure, so it is unlikely to change significantly over time.

Q: Can I have a combination of different face shapes?

A: Yes, it is possible to have a combination of different face shapes.

Q: Will knowing my face shape really make a difference in my appearance?

A: Yes! Knowing your face shape can help you choose hairstyles, makeup looks, and glasses frames that flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Oval face shape: Almost any hairstyle will flatter this face shape, so feel free to experiment!
  • Round face shape: Look for hairstyles that add height at the crown and lengthen the face.
  • Square face shape: Soft, layered styles and side-swept bangs can help soften a strong jawline.
  • Heart-shaped face: Side-parted hairstyles and chin-length bobs can help balance out a wide forehead.
  • Diamond-shaped face: Long, layered styles and side-swept bangs can help soften a pointed chin.
  • Rectangle-shaped face: Shorter, layered styles can help add fullness to the sides of the face.

By following these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance, no matter your face shape.
