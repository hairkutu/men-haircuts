Long Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 40 The Ultimate Guide

If you have a long face and are over 40, you might be wondering what hairstyles would look best on you. Fortunately, there are plenty of long hairstyles that can flatter your features and make you look youthful and stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to choose the perfect long hairstyle for your face shape and age, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

How to Choose the Perfect Long Hairstyle for Long Faces Over 40

When it comes to choosing a long hairstyle for a long face over 40, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a hairstyle that will balance out your face shape, rather than accentuating your length. Secondly, you want to choose a style that is age-appropriate, without looking too old-fashioned or matronly.

One of the best long hairstyles for long faces over 40 is the layered cut. This style adds volume to your hair and creates movement, which can help to balance out your face shape. You can also opt for a side-swept fringe or bangs, which can help to shorten the appearance of your face.

Another great option for long faces over 40 is the long bob, or "lob". This style is trendy yet sophisticated, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a blunt cut, while those with thick hair may prefer a layered lob.

Finally, consider adding some subtle highlights or lowlights to your hair. This can help to add dimension and depth, and can be a great way to update your look without making a drastic change.

Step-by-Step Tutorial: How to Style Long Hair for Long Faces Over 40

Now that you've chosen your perfect long hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step tutorial for styling long hair on long faces over 40:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  2. Using a 1-inch curling iron, curl small sections of hair away from your face. Hold each curl for 10-15 seconds before releasing.
  3. Once you've curled your entire head, run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural look.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Top Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Long Faces Over 40

Maintaining long hair can be a challenge, but it's worth it if you want to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some top tips for caring for long hair on long faces over 40:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb to prevent breakage and tangles.

FAQs About Long Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 40

What is the best long hairstyle for a long face over 40?

The best long hairstyle for a long face over 40 is the layered cut. This style adds volume and movement to your hair, which can help to balance out your face shape.

Should I get bangs if I have a long face?

Yes, getting bangs can be a great way to shorten the appearance of your face. Opt for a side-swept fringe or soft, wispy bangs for a flattering look.

How often should I trim my long hair?

You should trim your long hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair on Long Faces Over 40

In addition to the tips we mentioned earlier, there are a few more things you can do to keep your long hair looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and leave it looking dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot water when showering, as this can also dry out your hair.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage while you sleep.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to choose the perfect long hairstyle for your long face over 40 and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
