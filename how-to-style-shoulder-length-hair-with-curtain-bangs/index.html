How To Style Shoulder Length Hair With Curtain Bangs

Written by Oliver May 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Shoulder length hair with curtain bangs is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for some time now. It’s a versatile and chic look that can be worn by many women. If you have shoulder length hair and want to try this style, then you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll give you step-by-step instructions on how to style shoulder length hair with curtain bangs, along with some useful tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair with Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair with Curtain Bangs

The first step in styling shoulder length hair with curtain bangs is to ensure that your hair is clean and dry. You can blow dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference. Once your hair is dry, you can follow the steps below:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools. You can also apply some styling mousse to add volume to your hair.

Step 2: Create the Parting

To create the curtain bangs, you need to create a middle parting. Use a comb to create a straight line down the middle of your head. Then, comb your hair on either side of your head and let it fall naturally to create the curtain effect.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl your hair in small sections. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face to create a natural-looking curl. Once you’ve curled all your hair, comb your fingers through your curls to loosen them up.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

To style your curtain bangs, you can use a straightening iron to straighten them out or use a curling iron to curl them under. You can also leave them natural if you prefer. Use a hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style shoulder length hair with curtain bangs:

  1. Prep your hair with heat protectant spray and styling mousse.
  2. Create a middle parting with a comb.
  3. Curl your hair in small sections, starting from the bottom.
  4. Style your curtain bangs with a straightening or curling iron.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length hair with curtain bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Apply styling mousse to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron to curl your hair away from your face.
  • Use a straightening iron to straighten out your curtain bangs.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear curtain bangs with any face shape?

Yes, curtain bangs can be worn with any face shape. They’re a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit your face shape.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type.

Can I use a hairdryer to dry my hair?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer to dry your hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length hair with curtain bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to style shoulder length hair with curtain bangs, it’s time to try it out for yourself. With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a chic and trendy hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion.
