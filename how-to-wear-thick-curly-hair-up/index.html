How To Wear Thick Curly Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know that sometimes it can be a struggle to style it in a way that looks good and stays in place. One solution is to wear your hair up, but it can be tricky to do so without creating a frizzy mess. In this post, we'll show you how to wear thick curly hair up in a relaxed way that's easy to achieve and looks great.

How to Wear Thick Curly Hair Up

There are many ways to wear your thick curly hair up, but some styles work better than others. Here are a few options:

Bun

A bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for those with thick curly hair. You can either wear a high bun or a low bun, depending on your preference. To create a bun, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun shape. Secure it in place with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Ponytail

A ponytail is another easy and practical hairstyle for those with thick curly hair. You can wear a high ponytail or a low ponytail, depending on your preference. To create a ponytail, gather your hair into a ponytail and secure it in place with a hair tie. You can also use a scrunchie to avoid creating a crease in your hair.

Braid

A braid is a great option for those who want to wear their hair up but still show off their curls. You can either wear a single braid down your back or create multiple braids for a more intricate style. To create a braid, divide your hair into three sections and cross them over each other until you reach the end. Secure it in place with a hair tie.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the hairstyles you can wear with your thick curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to creating a relaxed updo:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent frizz.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, but don't rub it vigorously. Instead, gently squeeze the water out of your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and keep them frizz-free.
  4. Using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers, gently detangle your curls. Don't pull or tug on your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  5. Decide on the style you want to create (bun, ponytail, or braid) and gather your hair accordingly.
  6. Create your chosen hairstyle using bobby pins, hair ties, or scrunchies.
  7. Finish off your style with a hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tips for Wearing Thick Curly Hair Up

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your thick curly hair up:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and lead to more frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.
  • If you're in a rush, try a messy bun or a high ponytail.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories to your hairstyle, such as a headband or hair clip.

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent frizz when wearing my thick curly hair up?

A: To prevent frizz, make sure your hair is well-moisturized and use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream before styling. Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Q: Can I wear my thick curly hair up every day?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up every day if you want to. Just make sure to give your hair a break from tight hairstyles every once in a while to prevent breakage.

Q: What's the best hairstyle for thick curly hair?

A: The best hairstyle for thick curly hair is one that you feel confident and comfortable wearing. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

To maintain healthy and hydrated curls, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and lead to more frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your thick curly hair up in a relaxed and effortless way. Whether you prefer a bun, ponytail, or braid, there's a style out there that will work for you. Just remember to take care of your hair and give it the hydration and love it needs to look its best.
