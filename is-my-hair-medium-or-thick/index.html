Is My Hair Medium Or Thick

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post for 2023! Today, we will be discussing whether your hair is considered medium or thick. Many people struggle to identify their hair type, but it is important to know as it determines the best hair care routine for your tresses. So, let’s dive in and find out if your hair is medium or thick.

Description

Before we begin, let’s define what medium and thick hair mean. Medium hair is typically described as having a width of about the same size as a piece of thread. On the other hand, thick hair is usually wider than a thread and can be compared to a sewing needle. Knowing the difference between the two is crucial in determining the appropriate hair care routine.

How to Identify Your Hair Type

The easiest way to determine your hair type is to take a strand of your hair and compare it to a piece of thread or a sewing needle. If your hair is thinner than the thread, it is considered fine. If it is the same width as the thread, it is medium, and if it is wider than the thread, it is thick. Another way to identify your hair type is to pay attention to how it behaves. For instance, fine hair is often limp and flat, while thick hair is usually voluminous and bouncy.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Take a strand of your hair.

Step 2: Compare it to a piece of thread or a sewing needle.

Step 3: If it is thinner than the thread, it is fine. If it is the same width, it is medium, and if it is wider, it is thick.

Tips for Hair Care

Now that you have identified your hair type, it is time to take care of it properly. For medium hair, it is essential to use products that add volume and thickness to your hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for medium hair. For thick hair, use products that are lightweight and don’t weigh your hair down. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can my hair be both medium and thick?

A: Yes, it is possible to have hair that is both medium and thick. In this case, it is best to treat it as thick hair.

Q: Is it possible to change my hair type?

A: No, your hair type is determined by genetics and cannot be changed. However, you can make it appear thicker or thinner by using the appropriate hair care products.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your hair type, it is important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

2. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and perms.

3. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

4. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

5. Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

In conclusion, identifying your hair type is crucial in determining the appropriate hair care routine. By following the tips and guidelines provided in this blog post, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair properly, regardless of whether it is medium or thick.
