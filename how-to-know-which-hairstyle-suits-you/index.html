How To Know Which Hairstyle Suits You A Comprehensive Guide

In this article, we'll guide you on how to know which hairstyle suits you best. From determining your face shape to considering your hair type, we've got you covered.

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the basic things you need to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has its own ideal hairstyle that can enhance its features.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a good look at your face in a mirror. You may also use a measuring tape to get accurate measurements. Here's how to do it:

  1. Measure across your forehead at the widest point, just above your eyebrows.
  2. Measure across your cheekbones at the widest point, below your eyes.
  3. Measure from the tip of your chin to the bottom of your jawbone.
  4. Measure from the top of your head to the bottom of your chin.

Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: The length of your face is about one and a half times its width. Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Round: The length and width of your face are almost equal. Your jawline is soft and rounded.
  • Square: The length, width, and jawline of your face are almost equal. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: Your forehead and jawline are narrow, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Long: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.

Now that you know your face shape, let's move on to the next step.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Your hair type can affect how a certain hairstyle will look on you. Here are the most common hair types:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky or coily

Each hair type has its own unique texture and thickness, which can affect how it looks when styled. For example, a short pixie cut may look great on someone with straight hair, but may not work as well for someone with curly hair.

Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle, and look for styles that will work well with your hair's natural texture and thickness.

Taking Your Lifestyle into Account

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining which hairstyle suits you. If you have a busy schedule and don't have much time to style your hair, you may want to consider a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require much upkeep. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and have the time to style your hair every day, you may want to consider a more elaborate hairstyle.

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and look for styles that will fit in with your daily routine.

Matching Your Personality

Your hairstyle can also reflect your personality. If you're someone who likes to stand out and make a statement, you may want to consider a bold and edgy hairstyle. On the other hand, if you prefer a more classic and timeless look, you may want to consider a more traditional hairstyle.

Consider your personality when choosing a hairstyle, and look for styles that reflect who you are.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Bring pictures to your stylist to show them the exact style you're looking for.
  • Consider your hair's natural growth pattern when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I have multiple face shapes?

A: It's possible to have a combination of face shapes, in which case you should choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent part of your face.

Q: Should I choose a hairstyle based on current trends?

A: While it's okay to take inspiration from current trends, it's important to choose a hairstyle that suits your individual features and personality.

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear long hair if you have a round face, but it's important to choose a style that adds volume and height to the top of your head to elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, but by considering your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and personality, you can find a style that suits you best. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
