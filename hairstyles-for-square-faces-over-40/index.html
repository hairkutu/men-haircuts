Hairstyles for Square Faces over 40

If you have a square face and you're over 40, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that flatters your face shape and complements your age. Here are some hairstyles that work well for square faces over 40.

Table of Contents

  • How to determine if you have a square face
  • Hairstyles for square faces over 40
  • Tips for styling your hair
  • FAQs about hairstyles for square faces over 40
  • Hair care tips for square faces over 40

How to determine if you have a square face

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with straight sides. To determine if you have a square face, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Then measure across your cheekbones. If these measurements are equal, you have a square face.

Hairstyles for square faces over 40

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face over 40, you want to soften your features and create balance. Here are some styles to consider:

Short hair

A short, layered bob can be very flattering for square faces. It adds volume to the top of your head and draws attention away from your jawline. You can also add bangs to soften your forehead.

Medium hair

A shoulder-length cut with layers and soft waves can also work well for square faces. This style adds movement and texture to your hair, drawing attention away from your jawline. You can also add side-swept bangs to soften your features.

Long hair

If you prefer long hair, consider a layered cut with face-framing layers. This style softens your features and creates movement in your hair. You can also add loose waves to create texture.

Tips for styling your hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  • Blow dry your hair with a diffuser to create soft waves.
  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair.
  • Apply a styling product to add texture and hold.

FAQs about hairstyles for square faces over 40

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to add some volume to the top of your head to balance out your features.

Q: Should I avoid bangs?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs. In fact, side-swept bangs can be very flattering for square faces.

Hair care tips for square faces over 40

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to add moisture.
