How To Cut The Top Of An Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut, the undercut is a great option. This style involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top longer. But how do you cut the top of an undercut? In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step.

Description

The top of an undercut can be cut in a variety of ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Some people opt for a classic slicked-back look, while others prefer a messy, textured style. Whatever your preference, it's important to have a plan before you start cutting.

How to

Follow these steps to cut the top of your undercut:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut and prevent any unwanted snags or tangles.

Step 2: Section off the hair on top

Use a comb to section off the hair on top of your head, separating it from the shorter hair on the sides and back. You may want to use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 3: Decide on the length

Decide how much length you want to leave on top. You can use scissors or clippers to achieve your desired length. If you're not sure how short to go, start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.

Step 4: Cut the hair

Starting at the front of your head, use your scissors or clippers to cut the hair to your desired length. Work your way backwards, taking small sections at a time. Be sure to use the comb to keep the hair taut and straight as you cut.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Once you've cut the hair on top, you'll need to blend it with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use a comb and scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Check for symmetry

Check your work in the mirror to make sure both sides of your head are symmetrical. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a balanced look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut the top of your undercut:

  • Start with a longer length and cut more if needed
  • Use a comb to keep the hair taut and straight as you cut
  • Blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back for a seamless look
  • Check for symmetry as you go

FAQ

Can I cut the top of my undercut myself?

While it's possible to cut the top of your undercut yourself, we recommend having a friend or professional help you out. It can be difficult to achieve a balanced and symmetrical look on your own.

How often should I cut the top of my undercut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, you'll want to trim the top of your undercut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.

What products should I use to style my undercut?

The products you use will depend on your hair type and desired style. A pomade or wax can help you achieve a slicked-back look, while a texturizing spray or powder can add volume and texture.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important, especially if you're sporting an undercut. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance undercut haircut. Remember to always take your time and make sure you're happy with the results before you make any major changes.
