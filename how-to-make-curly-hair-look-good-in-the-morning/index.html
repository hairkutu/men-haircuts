How To Make Curly Hair Look Good In The Morning Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It looks beautiful when styled but can be a nightmare to manage, especially in the morning. If you have curly hair, you know the struggle of waking up with frizz and knots. However, with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls look good in the morning. In this article, we will discuss how to make curly hair look good in the morning and answer some frequently asked questions about curly hair.

Table of Content

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step to making your curly hair look good in the morning is to prepare your hair the night before. Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. After washing, apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair.

Next, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Be gentle to avoid breakage. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Once your hair is styled, wrap it in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet. These materials reduce friction and prevent frizz. If you don't have a scarf or bonnet, you can use a silk or satin pillowcase instead.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your curly hair look good in the morning:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair
  3. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers
  4. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel
  5. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation
  6. Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet
  7. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase if you don't have a scarf or bonnet
  8. In the morning, remove the scarf or bonnet and gently shake your hair to loosen the curls
  9. If needed, apply a small amount of styling product to refresh your curls
  10. Use a diffuser to dry your hair on low heat to avoid frizz

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your curly hair look good in the morning:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to reduce frizz
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt curl formation
  • If your hair is prone to tangling, sleep with your hair in a loose braid or pineapple to reduce knots
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair wash their hair once a week, while others wash it every other day. Generally, curly hair is more prone to dryness, so you don't want to wash it too often.

Should I use a diffuser to dry my curly hair?

Yes, a diffuser can help prevent frizz and enhance curl definition. Be sure to use it on low heat and avoid touching your hair too much while drying.

What styling products are best for curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Some popular products for curly hair include curl creams, gels, mousses, and oils. Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair and avoid products with sulfates, alcohols, and silicones.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for making your curly hair look good in the morning. Here are some basic hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair look good in the morning and enjoy your beautiful curls all day long.
