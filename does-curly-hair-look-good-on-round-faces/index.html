Does Curly Hair Look Good On Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby May 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and versatile hair type, but can it look good on round faces? If you have a round face and curly hair, you may be wondering if this combination is flattering or not. In this article, we will answer this question and give you some tips on how to style your curly hair to complement your round face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Curly Hair on Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair on Round Faces
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair on Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair on Round Faces

Description

Round faces have soft, curved features and are generally characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may want to create the illusion of more length and definition in your face shape. Hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head can be particularly flattering for round faces.

Curly hair can be a great option for round faces because the texture and volume can help elongate the face shape. However, not all curly hairstyles will suit round faces, so it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape.

How to Style Curly Hair on Round Faces

When it comes to styling curly hair on round faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid styles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and dimension.
  • Use products that enhance your natural curl pattern and add definition.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair on Round Faces

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair on round faces:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are formulated for curly hair. This will help enhance your natural curl pattern and add moisture to your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from roots to ends.
  3. Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add definition to any curls that need it. Be sure to wrap the hair around the barrel of the curling iron or wand away from your face.
  5. Add a volumizing product, such as a texturizing spray, to the crown of your head to add height and volume.
  6. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair on Round Faces

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair on round faces:

  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as these can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can disrupt your natural curl pattern.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair on round faces:

Q: Can I wear my curly hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?
A: Yes, you can wear your curly hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can make your face appear rounder. Q: What is the best haircut for curly hair on a round face?
A: The best haircut for curly hair on a round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. Consider a layered haircut with long layers that frame your face.

Hair Care for Curly Hair on Round Faces

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your curly hair looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair on a round face:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

In conclusion, curly hair can look great on round faces if you choose the right style and take good care of your hair. With the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can create a beautiful and flattering hairstyle that complements your round face shape.
