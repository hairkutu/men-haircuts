How To Cut Men s Medium Length Hair With Scissors

Written by Daniel Aug 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting men's hair can be intimidating, especially if you're not experienced. However, with some practice and patience, you can learn how to cut men's medium length hair with scissors. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to do it in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Description

Men's medium length hair can be styled in various ways, from slick back to messy and textured. To achieve these styles, you need to start with a proper haircut, and the best way to do it is by using scissors. Scissors give you more control over the length and texture of the hair, resulting in a more personalized and precise cut. In this article, we'll cover the basics of cutting men's medium length hair with scissors, including the tools you need, the techniques to use, and the common mistakes to avoid.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section the hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle with water to dampen the hair and make it easier to cut.

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Top Section

Starting with the top section, comb the hair straight up and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, using a point-cutting technique to create texture and movement. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair in the top section.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Next, move on to the back section. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to the desired length, using the point-cutting technique to create texture and blend the hair with the top section.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Finally, move on to the side sections. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to the desired length, using the point-cutting technique to create texture and blend the hair with the back and top sections. Repeat this process on both sides.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections, use your scissors to blend the hair together by point-cutting and texturizing the ends. This will create a more natural and seamless look.

Step 6: Check for Balance

After you've finished cutting, check the hair for balance and make any necessary adjustments. Use your comb to create the desired style, and you're done!

Tips

Cutting men's medium length hair with scissors can be challenging, but these tips can make the process easier:

  • Start with small cuts and gradually work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create texture and movement in the hair.
  • Hold the hair between your fingers to create a guide for cutting.
  • Use a comb to check for balance and symmetry.
  • Take breaks if you need to and don't rush the process.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's medium length hair with scissors:

Q: Can I cut men's medium length hair with regular scissors?

A: No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can damage the hair or create uneven cuts. You need to use sharp haircutting scissors to achieve a proper haircut.

Q: Do I need to dampen the hair before cutting?

A: It's recommended to dampen the hair with water to make it easier to cut and prevent flyaways. However, make sure the hair is not soaking wet, as this can affect the length and texture of the hair.

Q: How often should I cut men's medium length hair?

A: It depends on the individual's hair growth rate, but generally, men's medium length hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

After cutting men's medium length hair, it's essential to take care of the hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to tame frizz and add shine.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's medium length hair with scissors and achieve a professional-looking haircut. Remember to take your time, practice, and have fun!
