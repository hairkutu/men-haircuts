How To Trim Short Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Trimming short hair with clippers can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have any experience with them. However, it can be a lifesaver during a pandemic when it's not safe to go to the salon. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of trimming your short hair with clippers, from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Trim Short Hair with Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Trimming Short Hair with Clippers
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care after Trimming with Clippers

How to Trim Short Hair with Clippers

Before you start trimming your hair with clippers, you need to gather a few things:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Comb
  • Scissors
  • Mirror
  • Cape or towel

Once you have all of these things, it's time to get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start trimming. This will make it easier to get an even cut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose the clipper guard that you want to use. The size of the guard will determine how short your hair will be.

Step 3: Attach the Clipper Guard

Attach the clipper guard to your clippers. Make sure that it's securely in place.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Start trimming your hair with the clippers. Use the comb to guide the clippers through your hair. Work in small sections and move the clippers against the direction of your hair growth to get an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Use the shortest clipper guard to blend the sides and back of your hair. This will give you a more natural look.

Step 6: Trim the Top with Scissors

Switch to scissors to trim the top of your hair. Use the comb to lift your hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around your ears and neck.

Step 8: Check for Uneven Spots

Check your hair for any uneven spots. If you find any, go back and trim them with the clippers or scissors.

Step 9: Wash Your Hair Again

Wash your hair again to get rid of any loose hairs.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use hair products to keep it in place.

Tips for Trimming Short Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and work in small sections to get an even cut.
  • If you're not sure how short to go, start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use clippers to trim my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to trim your own hair. However, it can be difficult to get an even cut, especially if you don't have any experience with them.

How often should I trim my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you like to keep it. On average, you should trim your hair with clippers every 2-3 weeks.

What if I make a mistake while trimming my hair?

If you make a mistake while trimming your hair, don't panic. You can always go back and fix it. If you're not sure how to fix it, seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care after Trimming with Clippers

After trimming your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to trim short hair with clippers, you can save yourself a trip to the salon and do it yourself at home. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can get a great-looking cut that you'll be proud of.
