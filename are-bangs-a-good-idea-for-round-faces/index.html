Are Bangs A Good Idea For Round Faces

Written by Bardi May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your hairstyle and looking for a change? Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look, but are they a good idea for round faces? In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide tips on how to style your new bangs.

Description

If you have a round face, you know that it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. Bangs can be a great option, as they can help to balance out the proportions of your face. However, not all bangs are created equal, and it is important to choose the right style for your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

When choosing bangs for your round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept or angled bangs, which can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Second, consider the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose wispy bangs that are less heavy. If you have thick hair, you can go for more substantial bangs.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Bangs for Round Faces

Once you have chosen the right style of bangs for your face shape, it is important to know how to style them. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Part your hair in the desired location.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush, directing them to one side or the other.
  4. If necessary, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as hairspray or wax, to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Once you have styled your bangs, it is important to maintain them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can cause them to become greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs for round faces:

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Q: What kind of bangs are best for round faces?

A: Side-swept or angled bangs are generally the most flattering for round faces.

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Blunt, straight-across bangs can make your face look rounder, but side-swept or angled bangs can actually help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care for Bangs

It is important to take care of your hair, especially when you have bangs. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Overall, bangs can be a great option for round faces, as long as you choose the right style and take care of your hair. With these tips, you can rock your new look with confidence!
