How To Trim The Sides Of Your Hair With Scissors

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post about how to trim the sides of your hair with scissors. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve a fresh and clean haircut at home. Trimming the sides of your hair is essential for maintaining your hairstyle, and with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. We will cover everything from the equipment you need to the step-by-step process of trimming your hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Equipment Needed
  2. How to Trim the Sides of Your Hair with Scissors
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Trimming Your Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care After Trimming

Equipment Needed

Before we begin, let's talk about the equipment you need to trim the sides of your hair with scissors. Here are the things you should have:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Handheld mirror
  • Styling product (optional)

Make sure your scissors are sharp and specifically designed for cutting hair. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.

How to Trim the Sides of Your Hair with Scissors

Trimming the sides of your hair with scissors is a simple process that requires a bit of patience and attention to detail. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair by dividing it into two parts - the top and the sides. Use hair clips to keep the top part of your hair out of the way.
  4. Take a section of hair from one side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim the ends with your scissors.
  5. Repeat this process on the other side.
  6. Check for any uneven spots and trim if necessary.
  7. Release the top section of your hair and check if the sides match with the top.
  8. If needed, trim the sides a bit more to match the top.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Trimming Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when trimming your hair:

  • Start with small snips to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head for any uneven spots.
  • Don't rush the process - take your time and be patient.
  • Trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and may lead to uneven cuts.
  • If you're not comfortable trimming your hair yourself, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can cause damage and split ends. Use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.

How often should I trim the sides of my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and your hairstyle. Generally, it's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

It's best to trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and may lead to uneven cuts.

Hair Care After Trimming

After trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

And there you have it! Follow these simple steps and tips to trim the sides of your hair with scissors at home. With a bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve a fresh and clean haircut on your own.
