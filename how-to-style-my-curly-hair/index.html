How To Style My Curly Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Jun 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s a gorgeous feature that many people envy, it can also be a challenge to style and maintain. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can keep your curls looking healthy, defined, and beautiful. In this article, we’ll discuss how to style your curly hair, step by step, and answer some frequently asked questions about curly hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style My Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair
  • FAQ About Curly Hair Care
  • Curly Hair Care: Dos and Don’ts

How to Style My Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be overwhelming, especially if you’re not sure where to start. Here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Do not brush your hair when it’s dry. This can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when your hair is wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. They can damage your hair and cause it to lose its natural shape.
  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking healthy.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to keep it hydrated.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and make it easier to detangle.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while your hair is still wet.
  4. Apply a curl cream or gel to define your curls. Scrunch your hair while applying the product to encourage your curls to form.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Do not touch your hair while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, you can use your fingers to fluff your curls and add volume.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a trial and error process, but here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your curls. Some people find that they need a stronger hold gel, while others prefer a lightweight cream.
  • If you have frizz, try a serum or oil to tame flyaways.
  • Consider getting a haircut specifically designed for curly hair. A good stylist will know how to cut and shape your curls to make them look their best.
  • If you have trouble getting your curls to hold their shape, try sleeping with your hair in a pineapple or plop it overnight.
  • Don’t be afraid to embrace your natural curls! Curly hair is beautiful and unique, and there’s no need to try to straighten it.

FAQ About Curly Hair Care

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair care:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much product you use, but most people with curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and frizzy.

Should I use a diffuser attachment on my hair dryer?

Yes! A diffuser attachment will help distribute heat evenly and encourage your curls to form. Be sure to use a low heat setting and move the diffuser around your head to dry all of your hair.

Can I brush my curly hair?

It’s best to avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when your hair is wet.

Curly Hair Care: Dos and Don’ts

Here are some dos and don’ts for taking care of your curly hair:

Do:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture and make it easier to detangle
  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair
  • Get a haircut specifically designed for curly hair

Don’t:

  • Brush your hair when it’s dry
  • Use heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Use products with sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Touch your hair while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy, defined, and beautiful. Remember to embrace your natural curls and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
