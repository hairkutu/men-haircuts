Best Hair For Long Face Male Tips And Styles

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a long face, you may be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. The right haircut can help balance out your features and enhance your overall look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hair for long face male and provide tips for keeping your hair healthy and stylish.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face

Before we dive into hairstyles, let's first determine if you have a long face. To do this, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Then, measure the width of your face at the widest point (usually the cheekbones). If the length is significantly greater than the width, then you likely have a long face.

Best Haircuts for Long Face Male

When it comes to haircuts for long face males, the goal is to create the illusion of a wider face. This can be achieved by adding volume or width to the sides of the head. Here are some of the best haircuts for long face male:

1. Short Sides, Long Top

This popular style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, adding volume and width to the upper portion of the head.

2. Side-Parted Pompadour

The side-parted pompadour is a classic hairstyle that works well for long face males. The hair is styled with a deep side part and combed back, creating volume and width on one side of the head.

3. Messy Crop

The messy crop is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can add texture and volume to a long face. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Styling Long Face Male Hair

Once you've chosen a haircut, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips for styling long face male hair:

1. Use a High-Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Keeping your hair healthy is key to achieving a great style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure that your hair is clean, soft, and nourished.

2. Experiment with Products

Depending on your hair type and the style you're going for, different products may work better than others. Experiment with different gels, pomades, and waxes to find what works best for you.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help add volume and shape to your style. Use a round brush to lift the hair and create a fuller look.

FAQs About Best Hair for Long Face Male

1. What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, consider a shorter style that can make your hair appear thicker. Adding layers can also help create the illusion of volume.

2. Can I still have long hair if I have a long face?

Yes, but it's important to keep the hair from dragging down the face. Consider adding layers to create movement and texture, and avoid styles that are too flat or sleek.

3. Should I get a fringe with a long face?

A fringe can work well with a long face, but it's important to choose the right style. A side-swept fringe can help balance out the face, while a blunt fringe can make the face appear even longer.

Hair Care for Long Face Male

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling products, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, stylish hair:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling can damage your hair over time, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat tools too frequently.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, which can also benefit your hair.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, long face males can achieve a stylish, balanced look. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and a healthy diet.
