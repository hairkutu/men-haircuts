Naturally Curly Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Eveline Jul 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural shine and volume, making it difficult to style. But if you have naturally curly hair, you are in luck. Curly hair can be a blessing, and there are plenty of hairstyles that can make you look youthful and vibrant. In this article, we will share some of the best naturally curly hairstyles for women over 50.

How to Style Naturally Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls that last all day. Here are some tips to help you style your curly hair:

  • Start with clean, well-conditioned hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or curl cream.
  • Scrunch your hair to encourage your curls.
  • Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair on low heat.
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls for a more natural look.

Step by Step Guide for Curly Hair Over 50

Now that you know how to style your curly hair, let's take a look at some of the best naturally curly hairstyles for women over 50:

1. Short Curly Bob

A short curly bob is a great hairstyle for women over 50. It is easy to maintain and looks youthful and vibrant. To style, simply apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, scrunch, and let it air dry. If you want more volume, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

2. Shoulder-Length Curly Hair

Shoulder-length curly hair is another great option for women over 50. This hairstyle is easy to style and looks great with layers. To style, use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair. You can also add some volume by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

3. Curly Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, a curly pixie cut is a great option. This hairstyle is perfect for women over 50 who want a youthful and edgy look. To style, simply apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, scrunch, and let it air dry.

Tips for Maintaining Naturally Curly Hair

Curly hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining naturally curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to minimize damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I color my curly hair?

Yes, you can color your curly hair. However, it is important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the integrity of your hair.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so you may not need to wash it as often. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

3. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, and use a silk pillowcase. You can also use a styling product that contains anti-frizz properties.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair Over 50

Here is a simple hair care routine you can follow to keep your curly hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or curl cream.
  3. Scrunch your hair to encourage your curls.
  4. If necessary, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to add volume.
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  6. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  7. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry.
  8. Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Now that you know some of the best naturally curly hairstyles for women over 50, you can experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Remember to use the right products and techniques to keep your curly hair looking healthy and vibrant.
