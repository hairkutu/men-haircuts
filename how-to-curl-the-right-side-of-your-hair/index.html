How To Curl The Right Side Of Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 11, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, curling it is a great option. But if you're not sure how to curl the right side of your hair, it can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step and provide some helpful tips along the way. So grab your curling iron and let's get started!

Description

Curling your hair can be a great way to switch up your look and add some extra volume and texture to your locks. However, if you're not sure how to curl the right side of your hair, it can be a bit intimidating. In this guide, we'll break down the process into easy-to-follow steps and provide some helpful tips to make sure you get the perfect curl every time.

How to Curl the Right Side of Your Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let's first talk about what you'll need to get started. Here's a list of the tools and products you'll need:

  • Curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Clips or hair ties

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, it's important to prep your hair properly. This means washing and conditioning your hair, and then applying a heat protectant spray to protect your strands from damage. Once you've applied the heat protectant, blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll want to section your hair. This will make it easier to curl and ensure that you don't miss any pieces. Start by clipping or tying up the left side of your hair, so you're only working with the right side.

Step 3: Start Curling

Now it's time to start curling! Take a small section of hair (about 1-2 inches wide) and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 5-10 seconds (depending on the thickness of your hair) and then release the curl. Repeat this process until you've curled all the hair on the right side of your head.

Step 4: Repeat on the Left Side

Once you've finished curling the right side of your hair, unclip or untie the left side and repeat the same process. Take small sections of hair and curl them with the curling iron until you've curled all the hair on the left side of your head.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with some hairspray. This will help hold the curls in place and prevent them from falling out throughout the day. Spray the hairspray all over your hair, focusing on the curls.

Tips for Curling the Right Side of Your Hair

Here are a few extra tips to keep in mind when curling the right side of your hair:

  • Make sure your curling iron is hot enough. If it's not hot enough, your curls won't hold.
  • Don't curl too much hair at once. If you try to curl too much hair at once, the curl won't be as tight or defined.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls. This will help create a more natural look and prevent your curls from all blending together.

FAQ

How can I make my curls last longer?

To make your curls last longer, use a strong hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun to help preserve the curls overnight.

What if I don't have a curling iron?

If you don't have a curling iron, you can try using hot rollers or a flat iron to curl your hair. You can also try braiding your hair and then using a hair straightener to create waves.

How often should I curl my hair?

You should avoid curling your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Instead, try to limit your curling to once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl the right side of your hair like a pro. Whether you're going for a natural wave or tight curls, these steps will help you achieve the perfect look. Just remember to take care of your hair along the way to keep it healthy and strong.
