How To Style Mid Length Wavy Hair Male

Written by Oliver Apr 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you wondering how to style your mid length wavy hair as a male? Look no further! In this article, we'll break down the steps and provide some helpful tips to achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle.

Description

Mid length wavy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, it can look effortlessly cool and casual. Whether you're going for a messy, beachy look or a more polished style, there are a variety of ways to style your mid length wavy hair to suit your personal style.

How to

Before styling your mid length wavy hair, it's important to start with clean, damp hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it gently to remove excess water.

Step 1: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. A lightweight mousse or sea salt spray can add texture and hold to your hair without weighing it down. Rub the product between your palms and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using your fingers to scrunch it and create texture. For a more defined wave, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to distribute the heat more evenly. As you blow dry your hair, focus on lifting the roots and creating volume.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Use a comb or your fingers to create a part and shape your hair into the desired style. For a more polished look, use a wax or pomade to smooth down any flyaways and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your mid length wavy hair:

  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • For a more natural look, try air drying your hair instead of blow drying it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair without causing breakage.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my mid length wavy hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. If you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I style my hair without using heat?

Absolutely! You can achieve a tousled, natural look by air drying your hair and using a texturizing spray or wax to add definition and hold.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, vibrant hair. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to confidently style your mid length wavy hair and rock a cool, casual look. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
