Is Rose Gold Hair Still In Style 2021?

Rose gold hair has been a trendy hair color for a few years now. It's a beautiful and feminine shade that has been loved by many. We've seen it on celebrities, influencers, and everyday people. But is rose gold hair still in style in 2021? In this article, we'll answer that question and give you some tips on how to achieve and maintain this popular hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Rose Gold Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Rose Gold Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Rose Gold Hair

Description

Rose gold hair is a mixture of pink, blonde, and copper tones. It's a warm and soft shade that can be customized to suit different skin tones and hair types. This hair color has been popular for a few years now, and it's still in style in 2021. You can see it on Instagram, TikTok, and other social media platforms. It's a versatile shade that can be worn in different styles, from subtle highlights to all-over color.

How to Achieve Rose Gold Hair

If you want to achieve rose gold hair, you need to start with a light base color. This means that you need to bleach your hair if it's not already light. You can do this at home or at a salon, depending on your experience and budget. Once you have a light base color, you can apply a rose gold hair dye or toner. There are many brands and shades to choose from, so make sure to do your research before buying a product. You can also mix different shades to create a customized color.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve rose gold hair:

  1. Start with a light base color. You can bleach your hair if it's not already light.
  2. Choose a rose gold hair dye or toner. Make sure to read the instructions and do a patch test.
  3. Apply the dye or toner evenly to your hair. Use gloves and a brush to prevent staining.
  4. Leave the product on for the recommended time. This can vary depending on the brand and shade.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  6. Style your hair as usual. You can use heat tools and styling products, but make sure to protect your hair from damage.

Tips for Maintaining Rose Gold Hair

To maintain your rose gold hair, you need to follow some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a heat protectant and wearing a hat.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about rose gold hair:

  • Is rose gold hair still in style in 2021?

    • Yes, rose gold hair is still in style in 2021. It's a popular hair color that can be worn in different styles and shades.

  • Can I achieve rose gold hair on dark hair?

    • You can achieve rose gold hair on dark hair, but you need to bleach your hair first to lighten it. This can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to use a good quality bleach and follow the instructions carefully.

  • How long does rose gold hair last?

    • Rose gold hair can last for a few weeks to a few months, depending on how well you maintain it. You may need to touch up the color every few weeks or months to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care for Rose Gold Hair

Here are some hair care tips for rose gold hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, as this can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
