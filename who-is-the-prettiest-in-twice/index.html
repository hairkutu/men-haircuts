3. Regular Exercise

Tzuyu stays in shape by exercising regularly. She enjoys yoga, Pilates, and dance, which helps her maintain her toned figure.

Hair Care Tips from Twice

1. Sana

Sana keeps her hair looking shiny and healthy by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. She also avoids using heat styling tools too often, which can damage the hair.

2. Dahyun
