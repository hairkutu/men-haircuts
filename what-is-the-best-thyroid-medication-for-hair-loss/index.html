What Is The Best Thyroid Medication For Hair Loss

Written by William Apr 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Thyroid disorders can cause a variety of symptoms, including hair loss. In fact, hair loss is one of the most common symptoms of thyroid disorders. If you are experiencing hair loss due to a thyroid disorder, you may be wondering what the best thyroid medication for hair loss is.

What Causes Hair Loss in Thyroid Disorders?

Hair loss is a common symptom of thyroid disorders, including hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism. The thyroid gland produces hormones that regulate metabolism, which affects the growth and renewal of hair follicles. When the thyroid gland is not functioning properly, it can disrupt the growth and renewal of hair follicles, leading to hair loss.

How to Treat Hair Loss in Thyroid Disorders?

The best way to treat hair loss in thyroid disorders is to treat the underlying thyroid disorder. This may involve taking medication to regulate thyroid hormone levels. The most common medications used to treat thyroid disorders are levothyroxine and liothyronine.

Levothyroxine

Levothyroxine is the most commonly prescribed medication for hypothyroidism, which is an underactive thyroid gland. Levothyroxine is a synthetic thyroid hormone that replaces the hormone that the thyroid gland is not producing enough of. Levothyroxine can help regulate the thyroid hormone levels in the body, which can help improve hair loss.

Liothyronine

Liothyronine is a synthetic thyroid hormone that is used to treat hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism. Liothyronine is a more potent thyroid hormone than levothyroxine and is often used in combination with levothyroxine to treat hypothyroidism. Liothyronine can help regulate the thyroid hormone levels in the body, which can help improve hair loss.

Tips for Hair Care in Thyroid Disorders

While taking medication to treat thyroid disorders can help improve hair loss, there are also steps you can take to care for your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein and vitamins
  • Consider taking a hair supplement with biotin and other vitamins

FAQ

Can thyroid medication cause hair loss?

No, thyroid medication does not cause hair loss. In fact, taking thyroid medication can help improve hair loss caused by thyroid disorders.

How long does it take for thyroid medication to improve hair loss?

It can take several months of taking thyroid medication for hair loss to improve. However, some people may see improvement in hair loss within a few weeks of starting thyroid medication.

Can hair loss from thyroid disorders be reversed?

Yes, hair loss from thyroid disorders can be reversed with proper treatment. Taking medication to regulate thyroid hormone levels can help improve hair loss.

Conclusion

If you are experiencing hair loss due to a thyroid disorder, the best way to treat it is by treating the underlying thyroid disorder. Taking medication to regulate thyroid hormone levels can help improve hair loss. In addition, taking steps to care for your hair, such as using a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoiding heat styling tools, can also help improve hair loss.
