How To Handle Curly Hair In Humidity Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially if you live in a humid climate. The humidity can cause your curls to frizz and lose their shape, leaving you with a bad hair day. But fear not, there are ways to manage your curly hair in humidity. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you keep your curls looking their best, no matter the weather.

How to

The first step in managing curly hair in humidity is to understand how humidity affects your hair. Humidity causes curly hair to absorb moisture from the air, which can cause the hair shaft to swell and frizz. To combat this, you need to create a barrier between your hair and the humidity.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to managing curly hair in humidity is to start with clean hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. This will help your hair retain moisture and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and create a barrier between your hair and the humidity. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold your curls in place. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil, which can help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can cause your hair to frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair. Allow your hair to air dry as much as possible before styling.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your curly hair in humidity:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause your hair to frizz.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction on your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing curly hair in humidity:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others may need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Should I use a hair mask on my curly hair?

A: Yes, a hair mask can be a great way to nourish and hydrate your curly hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like avocado oil, shea butter, or coconut oil.

Hair Care

Managing curly hair in humidity requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it to keep your curls looking their best. Remember to start with clean hair, apply a leave-in conditioner, use a styling product, and dry your hair gently. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your curly hair, no matter the weather.
