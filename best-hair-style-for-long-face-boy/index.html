Best Hair Style For Long Face Boy The Ultimate Guide

Are you a boy with a long face and having trouble finding the best hairstyle suitable for you? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hair style for long face boy along with some tips and tricks to maintain and care for your hair.

Table of Content:

  2. How to
  3. Step by Step

Description

A long face can be tricky when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. You want a style that can balance out the length of your face and make it appear more proportional. The best hair style for long face boy is one that adds width to your face and doesn't make it look even longer.

How to

The first step in finding the best hair style for long face boy is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the length of your face from the hairline to the tip of the chin and the width across the cheekbones. If the length is significantly longer than the width, then you have a long face.

Step by Step

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the best hairstyle. Here are some of the best hair styles for long face boy:

  1. The Side Part: This classic hairstyle is perfect for long face boys. The side part adds volume and width to the top of the head, balancing out the length of the face.
  2. The Quiff: The quiff is another great option for long face boys. This hairstyle adds volume to the top of the head and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal style.
  3. The Buzz Cut: If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, the buzz cut is perfect for you. This hairstyle is short and easy to maintain, making it a great option for busy boys.
  4. The Pompadour: The pompadour is a stylish option for long face boys. This hairstyle features a high volume on the top of the head and shorter sides, creating a balanced look for the face.

Tips

When styling your hair, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face appear longer.
  • Experiment with different styles and find one that suits your personal style and face shape.
  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair style for long face boy:

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a long face?
A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have a long face. Just make sure to add some volume to the top of your head to balance out the length of your face. Q: Do I need to visit a hairstylist to get the best hairstyle for my long face?
A: It's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape. They can provide you with expert advice and recommend styles that will suit your personal style and face shape.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know the best hair style for long face boy, it's time to experiment with different styles and find one that suits your personal style and face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
