Medium Length Hair For Square Face A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a square face, choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging. While there are countless options available, not all of them will flatter your face shape. That's why medium length hair is an excellent choice for square faces. In this comprehensive guide, we'll share everything you need to know about medium length hair for square faces, including how to style it, tips for care, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Medium Length Hair for Square Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for Square Faces
  • Tips for Medium Length Hair Care for Square Faces
  • FAQs

Description

Medium length hair is defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This length is perfect for square faces because it softens the angular features that characterize this face shape. Additionally, medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways, making it an excellent option for those who like to switch up their looks.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Square Faces

There are several ways to style medium length hair for square faces. One popular option is to add layers, which can help soften the angles of the face. You can also add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face. Additionally, side-swept bangs can help to soften a strong jawline.

Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair and Square Faces

Here are some popular hairstyles for medium length hair and square faces:

  • Long bob
  • Wavy shoulder-length hair
  • Layered shag
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Messy updo

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for Square Faces

Follow these steps to style your medium length hair for square faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo to add body and texture to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  3. Part your hair to the side to create an asymmetrical look that softens your jawline.
  4. Add loose waves to your hair using a curling iron or wand.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to create a messy, undone look.

Tips for Medium Length Hair Care for Square Faces

To keep your medium length hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent dryness.
  • Choose hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

FAQs

What is the best hair length for a square face?

Medium length hair is the best hair length for square faces because it softens the angular features of the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face look even more angular, such as blunt bangs or a straight bob.

Can I still wear a ponytail if I have a square face?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail if you have a square face. However, consider adding some volume to the crown of your head to balance out the angles of your face.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently choose medium length hair as your next hairstyle for square faces. Remember to choose a style that flatters your unique features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
