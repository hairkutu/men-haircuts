Low Taper Fade Long Hair On Top A Stylish Haircut In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut in 2023, then the low taper fade long hair on top is a perfect choice. This haircut is perfect for men who want to have long hair on top while keeping the sides and back clean and short. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in several ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy and textured style.

Description

The low taper fade long hair on top is a modern haircut that combines different elements of classic and contemporary styles. This haircut features a short taper fade on the sides and back, while the hair on top is left long and textured. The hair on top can be styled in different ways, such as a slicked-back style, a messy and textured look, or a pompadour.

One of the best things about this haircut is that it's low-maintenance and easy to style. You don't have to spend a lot of time and effort to keep it looking good. All you need is a good hair product and a comb or brush to style it the way you want. The low taper fade also makes it a great choice for men who want to keep their hair short but still have some length on top.

How to Get a Low Taper Fade Long Hair on Top

The low taper fade long hair on top is a popular haircut that can be achieved by visiting a professional hairstylist. To get this haircut, you'll need to have some length on top, at least 2-3 inches. The hairstylist will then use clippers to create a taper fade on the sides and back, gradually blending the hair from short to long. The hair on top can be trimmed or styled according to your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a low taper fade long hair on top:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist and show them a picture of the low taper fade long hair on top that you want.
  3. Ask the hairstylist to create a taper fade on the sides and back, gradually blending the hair from short to long. The length of the hair on top should be at least 2-3 inches.
  4. The hairstylist can then trim or style the hair on top according to your preference.
  5. Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair using a good hair product and a comb or brush.

Tips for Maintaining the Low Taper Fade Long Hair on Top

Here are some tips for maintaining the low taper fade long hair on top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Apply a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair the way you want.
  • Get regular trims to keep the length on top and maintain the shape of the taper fade.
  • Experiment with different styling options to find the look that suits you best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the low taper fade long hair on top:

Q: Is the low taper fade long hair on top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes.

Q: What hair products are recommended for styling the low taper fade long hair on top?

A: Pomade, wax, and gel are good options for styling this haircut.

Q: How often do I need to trim my hair to maintain the low taper fade long hair on top?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length on top and keep the taper fade looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips for Low Taper Fade Long Hair on Top

Here are some hair care tips for the low taper fade long hair on top:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening, as it can damage the hair and make it brittle.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair spray with SPF.

The low taper fade long hair on top is a stylish and trendy haircut that's perfect for men who want to have long hair on top while keeping the sides and back clean and short. With the right hair product and styling techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks and maintain your hair in good condition. So, if you're looking for a new haircut in 2023, consider the low taper fade long hair on top.
