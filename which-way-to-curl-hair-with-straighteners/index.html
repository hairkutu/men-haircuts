Which Way To Curl Hair With Straighteners

Written by Frank Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a way to curl your hair without using a curling iron, then straighteners are a great option. Straighteners can be used to create a variety of curls, from loose waves to tight ringlets. But which way should you curl your hair with straighteners? In this article, we'll take a look at how to curl your hair with straighteners, step by step, and give you some tips to get the perfect curls every time.

Description

Straighteners, also known as flat irons, are typically used to straighten hair. However, they can also be used to create curls. By using a straightener to curl your hair, you'll get a more natural-looking curl than you would with a curling iron. This is because the curl will be less uniform and more relaxed, giving you a more effortless and laid-back look.

How to Curl Your Hair with Straighteners

Before you start curling your hair with straighteners, you'll need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Then, blow dry your hair until it's completely dry. Once your hair is dry, you can start curling.

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to create more sections. If you have thin hair, you may want to create fewer sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 2: Heat Up Your Straighteners

Turn on your straighteners and let them heat up. The ideal temperature for curling your hair with straighteners is around 350 degrees Fahrenheit. However, you may need to adjust the temperature depending on your hair type. If you have thin or damaged hair, you should use a lower temperature setting. If you have thick or coarse hair, you may need to use a higher temperature setting.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a section of hair and clamp the straighteners around it about an inch away from your scalp. Twist the straighteners away from your face, then slowly slide them down the length of your hair. The slower you slide the straighteners, the tighter the curl will be. If you want looser curls, slide the straighteners down your hair more quickly.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat this process with each section of hair. Remember to twist the straighteners away from your face each time to create a more natural-looking curl.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect curls:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair with straighteners.
  • Use a higher temperature setting for thicker hair and a lower temperature setting for thinner hair.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair. For looser curls, use larger sections of hair.
  • Don't clamp the straighteners too tightly around your hair or you may end up with kinks.
  • For a more natural-looking curl, alternate the direction in which you twist the straighteners.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair with straighteners:

Can you curl your hair with any type of straighteners?

Yes, you can curl your hair with any type of straighteners. However, straighteners with ceramic plates are the best option because they distribute heat more evenly and are less likely to damage your hair.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curls created with straighteners will last for one to two days.

Do you need to use hairspray?

Using hairspray can help your curls last longer. However, it's not necessary. If you prefer a more natural look, you can skip the hairspray.

Hair Care

Curling your hair with straighteners can cause damage if you're not careful. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair.
  • Don't use the straighteners on wet hair.
  • Don't clamp the straighteners too tightly around your hair or you may end up with kinks.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Curling your hair with straighteners is a great way to switch up your look. By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to create beautiful curls that will last for days. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
