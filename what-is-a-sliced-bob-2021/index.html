What Is A Sliced Bob 2021

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

A sliced bob is a trendy and stylish haircut that has been popular for many years. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. In 2021, the sliced bob has taken on a new twist, with a more relaxed and effortless look becoming the main trend.

Description

The sliced bob is a haircut that is created by cutting the hair into a bob shape and then slicing into the hair to create texture and movement. The hair is cut into different lengths, with the longest layer being at the bottom and the shortest at the top. This creates a layered effect that can be styled in many different ways.

How to

To create a sliced bob, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. The stylist will begin by cutting your hair into a bob shape, which is usually just above the shoulders. They will then use a slicing technique to create texture and movement in the hair. This involves cutting the hair at an angle, using scissors or a razor, to create different lengths and layers. The end result will be a chic and stylish haircut that is easy to manage.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair and towel dry it till it's damp.

2. Section your hair into four parts, two in the front and two in the back.

3. Cut the back section of your hair first, then move to the front part.

4. Start cutting your hair in a bob shape, making sure the length is just above your shoulders.

5. Once the bob shape is achieved, use a scissor or razor to slice into the hair.

6. Cut the hair at an angle to create different lengths and layers.

7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

- Bring a picture of the sliced bob you want to your hairstylist.

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

- Use a heat protectant before styling your hair with hot tools.

- Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the sliced bob.

FAQ

Q: Who can get a sliced bob?

A: A sliced bob can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It is a versatile haircut that can be worn by anyone.

Q: How do I style a sliced bob?

A: A sliced bob can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to beachy waves. Use a good quality styling product and hot tools to achieve your desired look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with a sliced bob?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To maintain the health and beauty of your sliced bob, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too often. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the sliced bob, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a trendy and stylish sliced bob haircut in 2021 and beyond!
