Best Figure Flattering Bikinis How To Choose The Perfect Swimsuit

Written by Christine Apr 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Summer is just around the corner, and it’s time to start thinking about swimsuits. Finding the perfect bikini can be challenging, especially when you want to find a swimsuit that flatters your figure. In this article, we’ll show you how to choose the best figure flattering bikinis that will make you feel confident and comfortable on the beach.

How to Choose the Best Figure Flattering Bikinis

Choosing the right bikini can be overwhelming, but it’s not impossible. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect swimsuit for your body type.

Determine Your Body Shape

The first step to finding the best figure flattering bikinis is to determine your body shape. There are five main body shapes: apple, pear, hourglass, rectangle, and inverted triangle. Each shape has different characteristics, and knowing your body shape will help you find a bikini that accentuates your best features.

Look for Supportive Tops

If you have a larger bust, look for bikinis with supportive tops. Underwire or padded tops will provide extra support and lift. High-neck tops are also a great option for larger busts because they provide coverage and support.

Choose the Right Bottoms

The right bottoms can make all the difference when it comes to finding the best figure flattering bikinis. If you have wider hips, look for bottoms with a higher cut to elongate your legs. High-waisted bottoms are also a great option for curvier figures because they provide coverage and support.

Experiment with Prints and Patterns

Prints and patterns can be your best friend when it comes to finding the best figure flattering bikinis. If you have a smaller bust, look for tops with bold prints or patterns to create the illusion of a larger chest. On the other hand, if you have a larger bust, look for tops with smaller prints or patterns to minimize your chest.

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can also play a role in finding the best figure flattering bikinis. If you have a lighter skin tone, look for bikinis in pastel colors or soft prints. If you have a darker skin tone, bold colors and bright prints will look great on you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bikini

Once you’ve found the best figure flattering bikinis, it’s important to maintain them properly. Here are some tips to help you keep your swimsuit looking great all summer long.

Rinse Your Bikini

After swimming, rinse your bikini with fresh water to remove any chlorine or saltwater that can damage the fabric.

Hand Wash Your Bikini

Hand washing your bikini is the best way to maintain its shape and color. Use a gentle detergent and cold water to wash your swimsuit.

Hang Dry Your Bikini

Avoid using a dryer to dry your bikini because the heat can damage the fabric. Instead, hang your bikini to dry in a shaded area.

FAQ

What is the best bikini for a curvy figure?

High-waisted bottoms and tops with underwire or padding are great options for curvy figures.

What is the best bikini for a smaller bust?

Tops with bold prints or patterns can create the illusion of a larger chest.

What is the best bikini color for darker skin tones?

Bold colors and bright prints look great on darker skin tones.

Hair Care Tips for the Beach

Don’t forget to take care of your hair when you’re at the beach. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair all summer long.

Wear a Hat

Wearing a hat can provide shade for your hair and protect it from the sun.

Apply Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to your hair before swimming to help protect it from the saltwater and chlorine.

Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove any saltwater or chlorine.

With these tips, you’re sure to find the best figure flattering bikinis and keep them looking great all summer long. Happy shopping!
