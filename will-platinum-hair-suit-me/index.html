Will Platinum Hair Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering going for the bold and beautiful platinum hair color? Before you make the decision, it's important to understand if this shade will suit your skin tone, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about whether platinum hair will suit you or not.

Description: What is Platinum Hair?

Platinum hair is a light, almost white shade of blonde. It's a bold and eye-catching color that can make a statement wherever you go. However, it's also one of the most high-maintenance hair colors out there, requiring regular salon visits and specialized hair care products.

How To: Can You Achieve Platinum Hair?

To achieve platinum hair, you'll need to go through a bleaching process that strips your hair of its natural color. This process can be harsh on your hair and scalp, so it's important to work with a professional stylist who can minimize the damage. After bleaching, your stylist will tone your hair to achieve the platinum shade.

Step By Step Guide: Will Platinum Hair Suit Me?

Step 1: Consider Your Skin Tone

Platinum hair looks best on cool-toned skin. If you have warm or olive skin, it may not be the best choice for you. Look at the veins on your wrist - if they appear blue, you have cool-toned skin. If they appear green, you have warm-toned skin.

Step 2: Think About Your Natural Hair Color

If you have naturally light hair, achieving platinum hair will be easier and less damaging. However, if you have dark hair, you'll need to go through multiple bleaching sessions to achieve the desired shade. This can be damaging to your hair and may not be worth the risk.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Platinum hair requires a lot of maintenance. You'll need to visit the salon every few weeks for touch-ups, and you'll need to use specialized hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Additionally, platinum hair can be high-maintenance when it comes to styling - it may require more heat tools, product, and time to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Hair

Once you've decided to go for platinum hair, it's important to take good care of your new shade. Here are some tips to keep your platinum hair looking its best:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your hair from turning brassy.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use hair masks and other deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can cause damage.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will platinum hair damage my hair?

A: Bleaching your hair can be damaging, but working with a professional stylist can minimize the risk. Additionally, taking good care of your hair with specialized products can help prevent damage.

Q: Can I achieve platinum hair at home?

A: It's not recommended to attempt to achieve platinum hair at home. The bleaching process can be harsh on your hair and scalp, and it's important to work with a professional stylist who can minimize the damage.

Q: Will platinum hair suit me if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Platinum hair looks best on cool-toned skin. If you have warm or olive skin, it may not be the best choice for you.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Platinum hair can be a bold and beautiful choice, but it's important to consider whether it will suit your skin tone, hair type, and lifestyle before making the decision. If you do decide to go for it, taking good care of your hair with specialized products and treatments is crucial to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
