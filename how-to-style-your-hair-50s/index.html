How To Style Your Hair 50S A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

The 1950s was a decade of glamour and elegance, and it's no wonder that many people are still inspired by the hairstyles of that era. Whether you're going to a vintage-themed party or just want to add a touch of retro to your everyday look, styling your hair 50s is easier than you think. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect 50s hairstyle.

How to Style Your Hair 50s: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to styling your hair 50s is to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the cuticles and add shine.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream evenly throughout your hair to prevent breakage and damage from hot tools.

Step 3: Separate Your Hair

Separate your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl each section of your hair towards your face. Hold the curling iron or rollers in place for a few seconds before releasing the hair. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 5: Brush Out Your Curls

Using a soft bristle brush, gently brush out your curls to create soft waves. Be careful not to brush too vigorously as this can cause your curls to fall out.

Step 6: Create a Side Part

Using a rat-tail comb, create a side part in your hair. The side part should be in line with the arch of your eyebrow.

Step 7: Pin Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the front of your head and twist it towards your face. Pin the twisted section to the side of your head using bobby pins. Repeat on the other side of your head.

Step 8: Add Volume

To add volume to your hair, gently tease the hair at the crown of your head using a teasing brush or comb. Be careful not to over-tease your hair as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 9: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your 50s hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place. Choose a hairspray that won't leave your hair feeling stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Styling Your Hair 50s

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair 50s:

  • Use hot rollers or a curling iron to create soft waves
  • Tease the hair at the crown of your head to add volume
  • Create a side part in your hair for an authentic 50s look
  • Pin your hair to the side for a glamorous touch
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place

FAQs about Styling Your Hair 50s

What kind of curling iron should I use to style my hair 50s?

For a 50s-inspired hairstyle, use a curling iron with a small barrel. This will create tight curls that can be brushed out for a softer look.

How can I add volume to my hair?

To add volume to your hair, gently tease the hair at the crown of your head using a teasing brush or comb. Be careful not to over-tease your hair as this can cause damage and breakage.

What kind of hairspray should I use to hold my 50s hairstyle in place?

Choose a light-hold hairspray that won't leave your hair feeling stiff or crunchy. Look for a hairspray that is designed to add shine and hold without weighing your hair down.

Hair Care for Styling Your Hair 50s

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage
  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth

Styling your hair 50s is a fun and glamorous way to add a touch of retro to your look. With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect 50s hairstyle in no time. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful for years to come.
