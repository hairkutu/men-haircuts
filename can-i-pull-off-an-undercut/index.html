Can I Pull Off An Undercut Tips For Rocking The Look

Written by Petter May 01, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're thinking about getting an undercut but you're not sure if you can pull it off, you're not alone. Many people wonder if this trendy hairstyle will suit their face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll answer the burning question: can I pull off an undercut?

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle that features shaved or closely cropped sides and back, with longer hair on top. This look has been popular for several years and can be seen on celebrities, fashion models, and everyday people. The undercut is versatile and can be adapted to suit a variety of hair types and face shapes.

How to

If you're considering getting an undercut, here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Research undercut styles that you like and that will suit your hair type and face shape. Look for inspiration online or in magazines.
  2. Find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with undercuts. Ask for recommendations or read reviews online.
  3. Book a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss the look you want and whether it will suit you. They can advise you on the best length, shape, and style for your hair.
  4. Prepare for the haircut by washing your hair and avoiding any styling products. Wear a shirt that you don't mind getting hair on.
  5. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist creates your new undercut. They may use clippers or scissors to create the desired shape.
  6. Style your new undercut as desired, using products such as pomade, wax, or gel to hold the hair in place.

Step by step

Let's break down the steps in more detail:

Step 1: Research undercut styles

Before you book your haircut, spend some time researching undercut styles that you like. Look for pictures of people with similar hair types and face shapes to yours. Consider whether you want a dramatic or subtle undercut, and whether you want the longer hair on top to be styled in a particular way.

Step 2: Find a skilled hairstylist

An undercut is not a simple haircut, so it's important to find a hairstylist who has experience with this style. Ask for recommendations from friends or family, or read reviews online. Look for a stylist who has a portfolio of undercut styles they have created.

Step 3: Book a consultation

Before you commit to the haircut, book a consultation with your hairstylist. This is a chance for you to discuss the look you want and for the stylist to advise you on whether it will suit you. They can also recommend the best length, shape, and style for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Prepare for the haircut

On the day of your appointment, wash your hair well and avoid using any styling products. Wear a button-up shirt that you don't mind getting hair on, as the hairstylist will need to access the back and sides of your head.

Step 5: Get the haircut

Sit back and relax while your hairstylist creates your new undercut. They may use clippers or scissors to create the desired shape. This process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the style.

Step 6: Style your new undercut

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your new undercut as desired. Use products such as pomade, wax, or gel to hold the hair in place. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you pull off an undercut:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different lengths and shapes to find what works best for you.
  • Consider styling the longer hair on top in different ways, such as slicking it back or creating a messy, textured look.
  • Use high-quality styling products to keep your hair in place.
  • Maintain your undercut with regular trims to keep the shape looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

Will an undercut suit my face shape?

There are undercut styles that can suit a variety of face shapes, from oval to square to round. Talk to your hairstylist about the best style for you.

What hair types can pull off an undercut?

Undercuts can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair. However, the length, shape, and styling of the longer hair on top may vary depending on your hair type.

Do I need to shave my head for an undercut?

No, you don't need to shave your head completely for an undercut. The sides and back can be shaved or closely cropped, while the longer hair on top can be left longer.

How do I maintain my undercut?

To maintain your undercut, you will need to get regular trims to keep the shape looking fresh. Use high-quality styling products to keep the hair in place, and wash and condition your hair regularly.

Hair Care

Undercuts require some maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Here are some tips for caring for your undercut:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply styling products to damp hair and work through with your fingers or a comb.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your undercut.

So, can you pull off an undercut? The answer is yes, as long as you choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care. Talk to your hairstylist about the best undercut style for you and rock this trendy look with confidence!
