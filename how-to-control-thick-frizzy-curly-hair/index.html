How To Control Thick Frizzy Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick, frizzy, curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage. However, with the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can tame your unruly locks and achieve a beautiful, healthy-looking mane. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to control thick frizzy curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To Control Thick Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

The first step in controlling thick frizzy curly hair is to establish a hair care routine that caters to your hair type. Here are some tips to get you started:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain moisturizing ingredients such as shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These ingredients will help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair no more than two to three times per week.

Apply a Hair Mask

Once a week, treat your hair to a deep conditioning hair mask. Look for a product that is formulated for curly hair and contains nourishing ingredients such as avocado oil or honey.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, gently combing through any tangles.

How to Control Thick Frizzy Curly Hair

Now that you have established a hair care routine, it's time to learn how to control your hair. Here are some tips and tricks:

Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your curls. Look for a product that is lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Apply a Hair Serum

Hair serums are great for controlling frizz and adding shine to your curls. Look for a product that is formulated for curly hair and contains ingredients such as argan oil or jojoba oil.

Use a Diffuser

When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to frizz. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to control thick frizzy curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Step 3: Apply a hair serum

Step 4: Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair

Step 5: Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you control your thick frizzy curly hair:

Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

Avoid Touching Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more frizz you will create. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day.

Embrace Your Curls

Instead of trying to fight your curls, embrace them! Curly hair is beautiful and unique.

FAQ

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to detangle your curly hair with a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to prevent breakage and damage.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my curly hair?

A: While you can use a hair straightener on curly hair, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low to prevent damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control your thick frizzy curly hair and achieve a gorgeous, healthy-looking mane. Remember to be patient and experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
