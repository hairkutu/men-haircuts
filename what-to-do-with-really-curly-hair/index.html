What To Do With Really Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Having really curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s unique and eye-catching. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you’re struggling with what to do with your curly locks, don’t worry! In this article, we’ll provide you with some tips and tricks to help you embrace and enhance your natural curls.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Really Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

The first step to managing really curly hair is to take care of it properly. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it’s important to keep it moisturized. Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz

By following these simple tips, you can keep your curly hair healthy and manageable.

How to Style Really Curly Hair

If you’re not sure how to style your curly hair, don’t worry! There are plenty of options. Here are some popular hairstyles for curly hair:

  • Wash and go: This is a simple and easy style that involves washing your hair and letting it air dry. You can use a styling gel or mousse to enhance your curls.
  • Braid-out: This style involves braiding your hair while it’s wet and then undoing the braids once your hair is dry. This creates a wavy texture.
  • Pineapple: This is a popular style for curly-haired people who want to keep their curls intact while they sleep. Simply gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie.
  • Bun: This is a classic style that’s great for formal occasions. Simply gather your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for styling really curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  2. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water.
  4. Squeeze out any excess water from your hair.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair.
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  7. Apply a styling gel or mousse to your hair to enhance your curls.
  8. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  9. Once your hair is dry, you can fluff it out with your fingers to create volume.

Remember to be gentle with your hair when styling it, as curly hair can be fragile.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling really curly hair:

  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your curly hair looking its best.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most people with curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, so it’s important not to wash your hair too often.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it’s important to use a heat protectant and not straighten your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Q: How can I combat frizz?

A: Frizz is a common problem for people with curly hair. To combat frizz, try using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can embrace and enhance your natural curls. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
