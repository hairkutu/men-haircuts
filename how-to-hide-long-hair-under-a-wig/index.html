How To Hide Long Hair Under A Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair and want to wear a wig, you might be wondering how to hide your hair underneath it. Fortunately, there are several ways to do this that will ensure your wig sits comfortably and looks natural. In this article, we'll go over some of the best techniques for hiding long hair under a wig, as well as some tips for caring for your hair and wig.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Hide Long Hair Under a Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Hide Long Hair Under a Wig

There are several ways to hide long hair under a wig, but the most effective methods include:

  • Braiding your hair
  • Twisting your hair into a bun
  • Using a wig cap
  • Using hairpins and bobby pins

Depending on your hair type and the style of your wig, you may need to use one or more of these methods to achieve the desired look. Keep in mind that the more hair you have, the more complicated it can be to hide it all under a wig.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to hiding long hair under a wig:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to create under your wig. You can braid your hair, twist it into a bun, or use a combination of these methods.
  3. If you're braiding your hair, start at the nape of your neck and braid all the way down to the ends. Secure the ends with an elastic band.
  4. If you're twisting your hair into a bun, gather your hair into a high or low ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun. Secure the bun with hairpins or bobby pins.
  5. Put on a wig cap to hold your hair in place. Tuck any loose strands of hair under the wig cap.
  6. Put on your wig and adjust it until it feels comfortable and secure.
  7. Use hairpins and bobby pins to secure the wig to your head. Insert the pins into the wig cap and then into your hair underneath. Be careful not to poke yourself with the pins.
  8. Style your wig as desired.

With these steps, you should be able to achieve a comfortable and natural-looking wig style that hides your long hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for hiding long hair under a wig:

  • Choose a wig cap that matches your skin tone to make it less noticeable under your wig.
  • Use a wig grip or adhesive to keep your wig in place and prevent it from slipping.
  • Experiment with different wig styles and colors to find the perfect look for you.
  • Be gentle when handling your wig and hair to avoid damaging either.
  • Use a wig stand to store your wig when you're not wearing it.

By following these tips and tricks, you can ensure that your wig looks great and your hair stays healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding long hair under a wig:

Do I need to cut my hair to wear a wig?

No, you don't need to cut your hair to wear a wig. You can hide your hair under a wig using the methods outlined in this article.

Can I wear a wig over curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a wig over curly hair. However, you may need to use more pins or a wig grip to keep the wig in place.

How can I prevent my wig from slipping?

You can prevent your wig from slipping by using a wig grip or adhesive, as well as by securing the wig with pins.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair and wig to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair and a beautiful wig:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig, and be gentle when handling it.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help it maintain its shape.

By taking good care of your hair and wig, you can ensure that they both look great for years to come.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks for hiding long hair under a wig, you can achieve a comfortable and natural-looking wig style that complements your personal style. Remember to take good care of your hair and wig to keep them both healthy and beautiful. With a little practice and patience, you can master the art of wig-wearing and enjoy all the benefits that come with it.
