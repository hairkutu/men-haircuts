How To Cover Hair Loss With Makeup

Written by Frank Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, hair loss becomes a common problem for both men and women. While there are several ways to combat hair loss, such as hair transplants and medication, covering hair loss with makeup can be a quick and easy solution. In this article, we will discuss how to cover hair loss with makeup in a few simple steps.

Description

Hair loss can occur due to various reasons, including genetics, age, stress, and hormonal changes. It can be a source of embarrassment and low self-esteem for many people. While wearing a wig or a hairpiece is a popular option, it may not be suitable for everyone. Makeup can be a great alternative as it can help to conceal bald spots and thinning hair.

How to Cover Hair Loss with Makeup

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

The first step in covering hair loss with makeup is to choose the right products. You will need a concealer that matches your hair color, a hairline powder or eyeshadow that matches your scalp color, and a setting spray.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair and Scalp

Before applying makeup, it's essential to prep your hair and scalp. Make sure your hair is clean and dry. Using a comb or a brush, style your hair in a way that covers the bald spots or thinning areas. If you have a receding hairline, pull your hair forward to create a fuller look.

Step 3: Apply the Concealer

Using a small brush, apply the concealer to the bald spots or thinning areas. Make sure to blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Apply the Hairline Powder or Eyeshadow

Using a small brush, apply the hairline powder or eyeshadow to your scalp. This will create an illusion of a fuller hairline. Make sure to blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Set the Makeup

Once you have applied the concealer and hairline powder, use a setting spray to set the makeup in place. This will ensure that the makeup stays put throughout the day.

Tips

  • Choose a concealer that matches your hair color.
  • Use a small brush to apply the concealer and hairline powder.
  • Blend the makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a setting spray to set the makeup in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair and scalp too much to prevent the makeup from rubbing off.

FAQ

Can I use regular makeup to cover hair loss?

Yes, you can use regular makeup to cover hair loss. However, it's essential to choose the right products that match your hair and scalp color.

How long will the makeup stay on?

The makeup can last for several hours if you use a setting spray to set it in place. However, it may start to wear off if you touch your hair and scalp too much.

Can I go swimming or exercise with the makeup on?

It's best to avoid swimming or exercising with the makeup on as it may rub off.

Hair Care

In addition to covering hair loss with makeup, it's essential to take care of your hair and scalp. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it hydrated. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair. A healthy diet, regular exercise, and stress management can also promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can easily cover hair loss with makeup and feel confident in your appearance.
