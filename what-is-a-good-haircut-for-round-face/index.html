What Is A Good Haircut For Round Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what kind of haircut suits you best. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we will help you find the perfect haircut for your round face shape.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, rounded cheeks, and a soft jawline. The goal of a good haircut for round face is to create an illusion of length and definition. This means avoiding styles that add width to your face and opting for ones that elongate your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, let's first determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, whether it's your forehead, cheeks, or jawline. If your cheeks are the widest part, you have a round face shape.

Step by Step Guide

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces because they add movement and volume without adding width. The layers should start at the cheekbones and go all the way down to the ends of your hair. This will create an illusion of length and definition.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces. They create an asymmetrical look that adds dimension to your face. The key is to keep the bangs long and angled towards the side of your face. This will draw attention away from the width of your cheeks.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be flattering for round faces, but it's important to choose the right style. A layered pixie cut with long bangs can create a slimming effect for your face. The layers should be longer on the top and shorter on the sides to create the illusion of length.

Tips

When choosing a haircut for your round face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose styles that add height and volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt cuts, chin-length bobs, and center parts.

Can short hair be flattering for round faces?

Yes, short hair can be flattering for round faces if the right style is chosen. Pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical cuts are all great options.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for your round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and guidance, you can find a style that flatters your features. Remember to consider your face shape, consult with a hairstylist, and take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful look.
