What Glasses Frame Suits A Round Face

Description

If you have a round face, choosing the right glasses frame can make a big difference in your overall appearance. Glasses frames can help to balance out the soft curves of a round face, creating a more defined and angular look. In this blog post, we will discuss how to choose the right glasses frame for a round face, and provide tips and tricks for finding the perfect pair of glasses.

How to Choose the Right Glasses Frame for a Round Face

When choosing glasses frames for a round face, the goal is to create balance by adding angles and definition to the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Look for Angular Frames

Angular frames, such as rectangular or square frames, can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Avoid round frames, which can make the face look even rounder.

2. Consider the Size of the Frames

Frames that are too small can make a round face look larger, while frames that are too large can overwhelm the face. Look for frames that are proportional to the size of your face, and make sure they fit comfortably without slipping or sliding.

3. Pay Attention to the Color

Dark frames can create a more defined look, while light frames can soften the face. Consider your skin tone when choosing a frame color, and look for shades that complement your complexion.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right glasses frame for a round face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right glasses frame, you need to know your face shape. To determine if you have a round face, look for the following characteristics:

  • Soft, curved jawline
  • Round cheeks
  • Equal width and length of the face

2. Look for Angular Frames

As mentioned earlier, angular frames can help to create more definition and balance for a round face. Look for frames that are rectangular, square, or have sharp angles.

3. Consider the Size of the Frames

Frames that are too small can make a round face look larger, while frames that are too large can overwhelm the face. Look for frames that are proportional to the size of your face, and make sure they fit comfortably without slipping or sliding.

4. Pay Attention to Frame Color

As mentioned earlier, frame color can make a big difference in how your glasses look on your face. Consider your skin tone when choosing a frame color, and look for shades that complement your complexion.

Tips for Choosing Glasses Frames for a Round Face

Here are some additional tips for choosing glasses frames for a round face:

  • Choose frames with a bridge that sits high on the nose to help elongate the face.
  • Avoid frames that are too round or curved, which can add more softness to the face.
  • Consider frames with a wider width to help balance out the roundness of the face.
  • Look for frames with bold or thick temples, which can add more definition to the face.

FAQs

What glasses frames look best on a round face?

Angular frames, such as rectangular or square frames, can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Avoid round frames, which can make the face look even rounder.

What color glasses frames should I choose for a round face?

Dark frames can create a more defined look, while light frames can soften the face. Consider your skin tone when choosing a frame color, and look for shades that complement your complexion.

How to Style Your Hair with Glasses for a Round Face

When wearing glasses with a round face, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. Here are some tips for styling your hair with glasses:

  • Choose a hairstyle with layers to add more definition to the face.
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on the sides, which can make the face look wider.
  • Consider a side-swept bang or a hairstyle with longer layers around the face to help elongate the face.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect glasses frame for your round face, and create a more defined and angular look.
