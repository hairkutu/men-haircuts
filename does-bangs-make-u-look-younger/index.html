Does Bangs Make You Look Younger Find Out The Truth

Written by Petter May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering if bangs can make you look younger? You're not alone. Many people believe that bangs are the secret to looking youthful and fresh, and there's some truth to that. In this article, we'll explore whether or not bangs can make you look younger, and what you need to know before taking the plunge.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Bangs are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. They can be worn in many different ways, from blunt to side-swept to wispy. But do bangs really make you look younger?

The answer is yes and no. Bangs can make you look younger, but it depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and the style of bangs you choose. Bangs can also make you look older if they're not done correctly, so it's important to do your research before making a decision.

How To

If you're thinking about getting bangs to look younger, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that will help elongate your face.

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy bangs, as they can be difficult to style and may fall flat. Instead, opt for wispy bangs that will add some texture and movement to your hair.

Finally, think about the style of bangs you want. Do you want blunt bangs that sit straight across your forehead, or do you want side-swept bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair? Take a look at some pictures online or in magazines to get an idea of what you like.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting bangs:

  1. Choose the style of bangs you want.
  2. Find a stylist who specializes in bangs.
  3. Bring a picture of the style you want to your appointment.
  4. Discuss your face shape and hair texture with your stylist.
  5. Let your stylist know if you want your bangs blunt or wispy.
  6. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic.
  7. Style your bangs at home using a blow dryer and round brush.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're thinking about getting bangs:

  • Start with side-swept bangs if you're not sure about getting blunt bangs.
  • Consider getting clip-in bangs to try out the look before committing.
  • Invest in a good round brush and blow dryer for styling your bangs at home.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Consider your lifestyle before getting bangs. Will they be easy to maintain?

FAQ

Q: Are bangs high maintenance?

A: It depends on the style of bangs you choose. Blunt bangs can be high maintenance, as they require regular trims to keep them looking fresh. Side-swept and wispy bangs are generally easier to maintain.

Q: Can bangs make you look older?

A: Yes, if they're not done correctly. Blunt bangs that are too heavy or sit too high on the forehead can make you look older. Side-swept and wispy bangs are generally more youthful.

Q: What face shapes look good with bangs?

A: Oval, heart, and square face shapes generally look good with bangs. Round faces may want to avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face look rounder.

Hair Care

If you do decide to get bangs, it's important to take good care of them. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny, and avoid using too much heat on your bangs when styling them. If your bangs get greasy between washes, use a dry shampoo to absorb the oil.

In conclusion, bangs can make you look younger, but it depends on several factors. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and the style of bangs you want before making a decision. If you do decide to get bangs, take good care of them to keep them looking fresh and youthful.
