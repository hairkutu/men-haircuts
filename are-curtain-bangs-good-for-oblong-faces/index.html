Are Curtain Bangs Good For Oblong Faces

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may be wondering if curtain bangs are a good choice for you. The good news is that curtain bangs can be a great option for oblong faces, as they can help soften and balance out the longer shape. In this article, we'll explore the benefits of curtain bangs for oblong faces and provide some tips for making the most of this trendy hairstyle.

Description

Oblong faces are characterized by their longer length and narrow width. If you have an oblong face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are likely to be about the same width, with a longer face length. This can make it challenging to find hairstyles that complement your features and balance out your face shape.

How to

Curtain bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can work with a variety of face shapes, including oblong faces. To get the look, ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs in a V-shape, with the longest pieces framing your cheekbones and the shortest pieces hitting just above your eyebrows. The middle of your bangs should be slightly longer than the sides, creating a soft, face-framing effect.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.

2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.

3. Section off your bangs, using a comb to create a V-shape.

4. Cut your bangs to the desired length, starting with the longest pieces first.

5. Use a razor or scissors to add texture and create a soft, wispy effect.

6. Style your bangs with a flat iron or curling iron, depending on your desired look.

Tips

When styling your curtain bangs, consider using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add body and movement. You can also experiment with different partings and angles to find the most flattering look for your face shape. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Can curtain bangs make my face look shorter?

Yes! Curtain bangs can help balance out the length of an oblong face shape, making it appear shorter and more proportional.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

For best results, we recommend trimming your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Can I wear curtain bangs with glasses?

Absolutely! Curtain bangs can work well with glasses, as they create a soft, face-framing effect that can complement your frames.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant products when styling, and avoiding excessive heat and chemical treatments. Be sure to also protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

In conclusion, if you have an oblong face shape, curtain bangs can be a great choice for adding softness and balance to your features. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence and style.
