How To Give Flat Straight Hair Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have flat, straight hair that lacks volume and body? If so, you're not alone. Many women struggle with this hair type, and it can be frustrating trying to figure out how to get the volume you desire. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and techniques on how to give flat straight hair volume.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Give Flat Straight Hair Volume
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Straight Hair

  5. Hair Care Tips for Flat Straight Hair

How to Give Flat Straight Hair Volume

There are several ways to add volume to flat straight hair, including:

  • Blow drying
  • Using volumizing products
  • Teasing
  • Using hot rollers or a curling iron
  • Getting a haircut with layers

By using one or more of these techniques, you can add volume and body to your flat straight hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to give flat straight hair volume:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for your hair type. Choose a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Products

Apply volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to your damp hair. These products will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you dry. This will help to add volume to your hair. Use a cool shot at the end to set the style.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take small sections of hair at the crown of your head and tease them with a comb. This will help to add height and volume to your hair.

Step 5: Use Hot Rollers or a Curling Iron

Use hot rollers or a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair. This will help to add volume and body to your hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light hairspray to hold the volume and body in place.

Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to your flat straight hair:

  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to add volume and texture.
  • Get regular haircuts with layers to add movement and volume to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to my flat straight hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can add volume to your hair by using volumizing products, such as mousse, and teasing your hair with a comb.

Q: How often should I wash my flat straight hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with flat straight hair can go a few days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair daily.

Hair Care Tips for Flat Straight Hair

If you have flat straight hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for flat straight hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can add volume and body to your flat straight hair and achieve the full, voluminous look you desire.
