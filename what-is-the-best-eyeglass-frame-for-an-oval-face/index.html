What Is The Best Eyeglass Frame For An Oval Face

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have an oval face, congratulations! You are one of the lucky ones who can pull off just about any eyeglass frame. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect one. In this article, we’ll explore what the best eyeglass frame for an oval face is and provide tips on how to choose the right one for you.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered the most versatile and can wear almost any frame style. However, it’s essential to choose a frame that complements your facial features and highlights your best features.

How to Choose the Best Eyeglass Frame for an Oval Face

When choosing an eyeglass frame, there are several factors to consider, including frame shape, size, color, and material. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect frame:

Frame Shape

For an oval face, rectangular, square, and cat-eye frames are excellent choices. These frames complement the oval face’s balanced proportions and add structure to soft features. Avoid round or oversized frames that can make your face appear unbalanced.

Frame Size

The frame size should be proportionate to your face size. Avoid frames that are too wide or too narrow. The ideal frame should sit comfortably on your nose bridge and not slip or rest on your cheeks.

Frame Color

The frame color should complement your skin tone, hair color, and eye color. Warm skin tones look best in earthy tones, while cool skin tones look great in black, silver, or blue frames. If you have light-colored eyes, try frames that match or complement your eye color.

Frame Material

Frames come in various materials, including plastic, metal, and acetate. For an oval face, lightweight frames are ideal. Avoid heavy or thick frames that can overwhelm your facial features.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the best eyeglass frame for an oval face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Identify your face shape to determine which frame styles will complement your facial features.

Step 2: Consider Your Facial Features

Consider which facial features you want to highlight. For example, if you have high cheekbones, choose a frame that sits high on your nose bridge.

Step 3: Choose the Right Frame Shape

Choose frame shapes that complement your facial features. Oval faces can rock rectangular, square, and cat-eye frames.

Step 4: Choose the Right Frame Size

Choose a frame size that is proportionate to your face size. The ideal frame should sit comfortably on your nose bridge and not slip or rest on your cheeks.

Step 5: Choose the Right Frame Color

Choose a frame color that complements your skin tone, hair color, and eye color.

Step 6: Choose the Right Frame Material

Choose a lightweight frame material that won’t overwhelm your facial features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best eyeglass frame for an oval face:

  • Consider your personal style and choose a frame that reflects it.
  • Don’t be afraid to try on different frame styles to see what looks best on you.
  • Choose a frame that is comfortable to wear for extended periods.
  • Consider the lens type and coating when choosing your frame.

FAQ

What are the Best Frame Shapes for an Oval Face?

Rectangular, square, and cat-eye frames are excellent choices for an oval face shape.

What Frame Size Should I Choose?

The frame size should be proportionate to your face size. Avoid frames that are too wide or too narrow.

What Frame Color Should I Choose?

Choose a frame color that complements your skin tone, hair color, and eye color.

Hair Care for Eyeglass Wearers

If you wear eyeglasses, you may be wondering how to care for your hair to prevent it from getting tangled or caught in your frames. Here are some tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that keep your hair away from your face, such as a high ponytail or bun.
  • Avoid using hair products that can leave a residue on your frames.
  • Clean your frames regularly to prevent oil and dirt buildup.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent tangles.

In conclusion, an oval face is a versatile face shape that can wear almost any frame style. When choosing the best eyeglass frame for an oval face, consider frame shape, size, color, and material. Follow these tips and steps to find the perfect frame that complements your facial features and highlights your best features. Don’t be afraid to try on different styles and have fun with your look!
