How To Dye Extensions To Match Your Hair

Written by William Jun 01, 2023 ¬∑ 8 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect shade of hair extensions to match your natural hair color? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of dyeing your extensions to match your hair color.

Description

Dyeing your hair extensions is a great option if you want to add length or volume to your hair without sacrificing a perfect color match. This method is also an excellent solution if you have hair that is difficult to match, such as ombre, balayage, or highlights. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily dye your extensions at home.

How to Dye Extensions to Match Your Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Color

The first step is to choose the right color of dye. You want to select a shade that matches your natural hair color as closely as possible. If you have highlights or lowlights, you may need to choose a few different shades to create a seamless blend.

Step 2: Wash Your Extensions

Before dyeing your extensions, you need to wash them thoroughly. Use a gentle shampoo to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Rinse the extensions well and let them air dry.

Step 3: Mix the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to mix the dye. It's important to use gloves to protect your hands and a plastic bowl to avoid staining any surfaces. Mix the dye until it's smooth and consistent.

Step 4: Test the Color

Before applying the dye to your extensions, it's essential to test the color on a small strand of hair. This will help you determine if the shade is a good match for your hair color. Apply the dye to the strand and let it sit for the recommended time. Rinse the strand with water and evaluate the color. If it's too light or too dark, adjust the dye accordingly.

Step 5: Apply the Dye

Once you're satisfied with the color, it's time to apply the dye to your extensions. Use a brush or comb to apply the dye evenly to each weft of hair. Make sure to saturate the hair completely and avoid clumping or tangling the strands. Cover the extensions with plastic wrap or a shower cap to keep them moist.

Step 6: Let the Dye Set

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to determine the set time. It's essential to let the dye sit for the recommended amount of time to achieve the best results. Check the extensions periodically to ensure that the color is developing evenly.

Step 7: Rinse the Extensions

After the set time has passed, rinse the extensions thoroughly with lukewarm water. Rinse until the water runs clear, and all the dye has been removed. Avoid using hot water as this can damage the extensions.

Step 8: Condition the Extensions

After rinsing the extensions, apply a deep conditioner to restore moisture and shine. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out. Pat the extensions dry with a towel, and let them air dry completely.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the dyeing process easier:

  • Use a dye that is specifically designed for human hair extensions.
  • Choose a dye that is ammonia-free to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Always wear gloves to protect your hands from staining.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can damage the extensions.
  • Use a deep conditioner after dyeing to restore moisture and shine.

FAQ

Can I dye synthetic hair extensions?

No, synthetic hair extensions cannot be dyed. Only human hair extensions can be dyed.

How often should I dye my extensions?

You should only dye your extensions when necessary. Over-dyeing can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle and dry. It's important to take good care of your extensions to ensure that they last as long as possible.

Can I dye my extensions a lighter color?

It's not recommended to dye your extensions a lighter color than their natural shade. Bleaching hair can cause damage and breakages. If you want to lighten your extensions, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After dyeing your extensions, it's essential to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to keep your extensions healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Comb the extensions regularly to prevent tangles and breakages.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet extensions to prevent tangling.

By following these tips, you can keep your extensions looking healthy and beautiful for months to come.
