Is Short Hair Better For Long Faces

Description

If you have a long face and you are looking for a new hairstyle, you may be wondering if short hair is better for you. The answer is yes! Short hair can help balance the proportions of your face and create a more flattering look. In this article, we will explore why short hair is better for long faces, how to choose the right short hairstyle, and how to care for your new haircut.

How to

Choosing the right short hairstyle for a long face can be challenging, but there are a few tips that can help. First, consider the shape of your face. If you have a longer face shape, you may want to avoid haircuts that are too short or too long. Instead, opt for a mid-length haircut that falls just below your chin.

You can also choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. These styles can help create the illusion of a wider face and balance out the length of your face.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the shape of your face.

Step 2: Choose a mid-length haircut that falls just below your chin.

Step 3: Consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a long face, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for a mid-length hairstyle that falls just below your chin.

Second, consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. These styles can help balance out the length of your face and create a more flattering look.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?

A: No, short hair can actually make your face look slimmer and more balanced. Just be sure to choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. You may need to use some hair accessories, such as bobby pins or hair clips, to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care

After getting a short haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for short hair. You may also want to use a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add volume and hold to your hair.

When styling your hair, be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair. You may also want to use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron.

In conclusion, short hair is better for long faces because it can help balance out the proportions of your face and create a more flattering look. When choosing a short hairstyle, consider a mid-length cut that falls just below your chin and adds volume to the sides of your face. With the right care and styling, your short haircut can look great and make you feel confident and stylish.
