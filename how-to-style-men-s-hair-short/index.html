How To Style Men s Hair Short

Written by Aprilia May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair on men has become a popular style in recent years. It's low maintenance, easy to style, and looks great on most face shapes. If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a shorter look, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over how to style men's hair short, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Men's Hair Short
  2. Step By Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Style Men's Hair Short

The first step in styling men's short hair is to determine the type of cut you want. There are many different styles to choose from, including buzz cuts, crew cuts, and fades. Once you've decided on the style you want, you'll need to decide on the length. Short hair can be anywhere from a few millimeters to a few inches in length.

Next, you'll need to choose a styling product that works best for your hair type and the look you're going for. Pomade, wax, and clay are all great options for short hair. If you're not sure which product to choose, ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step By Step

Now that you've chosen your cut and styling product, it's time to start styling. Here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips.
  3. Rub the product between your hands to warm it up.
  4. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  5. Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  6. Use a comb or brush to refine the style and remove any tangles.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling men's short hair:

  • Don't use too much product. A little goes a long way with short hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a comb or brush to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Try blow drying your hair for added volume and hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's short hair:

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on the style you have and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Absolutely! Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles for different occasions.

Q: How do I choose the right styling product?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Ask your hairstylist for recommendations or try a few different products to see what works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, manageable hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your men's short hair like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
