How Do Guys Get Fluffy Hair Tips Step By Step Guide And More

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Do you want to achieve a fluffy and voluminous hairstyle that will make heads turn? Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are many ways to boost your hair's volume and achieve a fluffy look. In this article, we'll show you how to get fluffy hair for guys by providing tips, a step-by-step guide, and more. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Tips for Getting Fluffy Hair
  2. How to Get Fluffy Hair Step-by-Step
  3. FAQ About Getting Fluffy Hair
  4. Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Hair

Tips for Getting Fluffy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's essential to understand the tips and tricks for getting fluffy hair. Here are some of the things you can do to achieve that fluffy and voluminous look:

Use the Right Hair Products

The key to achieving fluffy hair is to use the right hair products. Look for products that add volume, such as volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and mousses. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down, such as heavy conditioners and gels.

Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the best ways to add volume to your hair is to blow-dry it upside down. This technique helps lift your hair's roots and create a voluminous look. Use a round brush to help shape your hair and add volume.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and flat. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days. If your hair tends to get oily quickly, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume.

Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can weigh your hair down. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair to create a more voluminous look.

How to Get Fluffy Hair Step-by-Step

Now that you know the tips and tricks, let's dive into the step-by-step guide for getting fluffy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Massage the shampoo into your scalp to help lift your hair's roots.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. Use your fingers to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your hair upside down and use a round brush to blow-dry your hair. Focus on the roots to create volume. Once your hair is dry, flip it back over and brush it into place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hairspray to help hold your style in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh your hair down.

FAQ About Getting Fluffy Hair

Q: Can I get fluffy hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes! Curly hair can be voluminous and fluffy with the right hair products and styling techniques. Look for products that enhance curls and use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.

Q: Can I get fluffy hair without blow-drying?

A: Yes! While blow-drying can help create volume, you can also achieve a fluffy look by using a volumizing mousse and letting your hair air dry. Scrunch your hair with your fingers as it dries to help create volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to get fluffy hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and add volume.

Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Hair

To keep your hair looking fluffy and voluminous, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for fluffy hair:

Avoid Using Heat Tools Too Often

Using heat tools too often can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and flat. Limit your use of heat tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to help detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, too often. These hairstyles can pull on your hair and cause damage over time. Opt for looser hairstyles that allow your hair to move freely.

With these tips, you can achieve fluffy and voluminous hair that will turn heads. Remember to use the right hair products, blow-dry your hair upside down, and avoid over-washing your hair. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve the perfect fluffy hairstyle that suits your personal style.
