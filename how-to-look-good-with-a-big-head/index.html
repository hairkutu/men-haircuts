How To Look Good With A Big Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a big head can sometimes be a struggle, especially when it comes to finding the right hairstyle or hat that fits comfortably. But don't worry, there are plenty of things you can do to rock your big head and look good while doing it. In this article, we'll cover everything from hair care to fashion tips that will help you feel confident and stylish.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. Fashion Tips
  3. Hairstyle Ideas
  4. Hat Options
  5. Confidence Tips
  6. FAQ

Hair Care

One of the most important things you can do to look good with a big head is to take care of your hair. This means washing it regularly, using conditioner to keep it soft and manageable, and getting regular haircuts to keep it looking neat and tidy.

It's also important to choose the right hair products for your hair type. If you have thick hair, look for products that will help smooth and tame it. If you have thin hair, look for volumizing products that will give it some lift.

Finally, consider getting a hair transplant if you're experiencing hair loss. This can help fill in any bald spots and give you a fuller head of hair.

Fashion Tips

When it comes to fashion, there are a few things you can do to help balance out your big head. First, avoid wearing clothes that are too tight or form-fitting, as this can make your head look even larger in comparison.

Instead, opt for looser-fitting clothes that will create a more balanced silhouette. This can include things like flowy dresses, oversized sweaters, and wide-leg pants.

Another fashion tip is to wear clothing with vertical stripes or patterns. This will help elongate your body and create a more streamlined look.

Hairstyle Ideas

When it comes to hairstyles, there are plenty of options that will flatter a big head. One of the most popular styles is a layered cut, which will add volume and texture to your hair.

If you have curly hair, consider wearing it in a natural, voluminous style. This will help balance out the size of your head and create a more cohesive look.

Another option is to wear your hair in a sleek, pulled-back style. This will help elongate your neck and create a more elegant look.

Hat Options

If you're looking for a hat to wear with your big head, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for hats with a wider brim, as this will help balance out the size of your head.

You can also opt for hats with a lower crown, as this will help create a more proportional look. Avoid hats with a high crown, as this can make your head appear even larger.

Confidence Tips

At the end of the day, the most important thing when it comes to looking good with a big head is confidence. Remember that your head size does not define you, and there are plenty of things you can do to feel good about yourself.

Practice good posture, maintain eye contact, and speak with confidence. These little things can go a long way in helping you feel good about yourself and your appearance.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a beanie with a big head?

A: Absolutely! Look for beanies that are made from stretchy, comfortable materials that will conform to the size of your head.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid with a big head?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, as these can make your head appear even larger. Instead, opt for styles with volume and texture.

Q: Should I avoid wearing hats altogether?

A: No, hats can be a great way to accessorize and add some personality to your outfit. Just be sure to choose styles that flatter your head shape and size.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your big head with confidence and style. Remember, it's not about the size of your head, but how you carry yourself!
