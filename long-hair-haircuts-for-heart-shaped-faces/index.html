Long Hair Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, then you know how important it is to find the right haircut that will flatter your face shape. Long hair haircuts are a great option for heart-shaped faces because they help to balance out the forehead and the chin. In this article, we will discuss some of the best long hair haircuts for heart-shaped faces and provide you with tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Long hair haircuts can help to soften the angles of the face and provide balance to the overall shape. The best long hair haircuts for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the bottom of the hair, such as layers or waves.

How to

When choosing a long hair haircut for a heart-shaped face, it is important to consider the length of the hair. Hair that is too long can make the forehead appear even wider, while hair that is too short can make the chin look more prominent. The best length for long hair haircuts for heart-shaped faces is between the collarbone and the chest.

Step by step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the roots.

3. Part your hair down the middle or to one side, depending on your preference.

4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves throughout your hair.

5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

When styling long hair haircuts for heart-shaped faces, it is important to avoid adding volume to the top of the hair. This can make the forehead appear even wider. Instead, focus on adding volume to the bottom of the hair with layers or waves.

Another tip is to choose a haircut that frames the face, such as long side-swept bangs. This will help to balance out the forehead and the chin.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a long hair haircut for a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a long hair haircut for a heart-shaped face. However, it is important to add volume to the bottom of the hair to balance out the forehead and the chin.

Q: Should I avoid bangs with a heart-shaped face?

A: No, you can wear bangs with a heart-shaped face. However, it is important to choose a style that frames the face, such as long side-swept bangs.

Hair Care

To maintain your long hair haircut for a heart-shaped face, it is important to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to the roots and provide balance to the overall shape of the face.

It is also important to avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, try air-drying your hair and using heat tools sparingly.

In conclusion, long hair haircuts are a great option for heart-shaped faces. When choosing a long hair haircut, it is important to consider the length of the hair and to focus on adding volume to the bottom of the hair. With the right haircut and styling, you can flatter your heart-shaped face and feel confident in your new look.
