How To Sleep With Clip In Hair Extensions A Relaxing Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Clip in hair extensions are a game-changer when it comes to achieving that luscious, full head of hair we all dream of. However, many people struggle with the question of how to sleep with clip in hair extensions. The good news is that with the right techniques and a little bit of practice, sleeping with clip in hair extensions can be a breeze. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through how to sleep with clip in hair extensions step by step, as well as provide some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Sleep with Clip In Hair Extensions
  2. Step by Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Sleeping with Clip In Hair Extensions
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Clip In Hair Extensions

How to Sleep with Clip In Hair Extensions

The first step to sleeping with clip in hair extensions is to prepare your hair before bed. Start by brushing your extensions thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. Next, tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid to prevent your extensions from tangling or becoming matted while you sleep.

Once your hair is secured, it’s time to find a comfortable sleeping position. Sleeping on your back is the best option, as it will prevent your extensions from getting tangled or matted. If you prefer to sleep on your side or stomach, make sure to position your hair and extensions in a way that won’t put pressure on them.

Another important factor to consider when sleeping with clip in hair extensions is your pillowcase. Silk or satin pillowcases are the best options, as they are gentle on your hair and won’t cause friction or static.

Step by Step Instructions

Here’s a step by step guide on how to sleep with clip in hair extensions:

  1. Brush your extensions thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid.
  3. Find a comfortable sleeping position, preferably on your back.
  4. If you prefer to sleep on your side or stomach, position your hair and extensions in a way that won’t put pressure on them.
  5. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and static.

Tips for Sleeping with Clip In Hair Extensions

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when sleeping with clip in hair extensions:

  • Try to avoid tossing and turning too much while you sleep, as this can cause your extensions to tangle or become matted.
  • Consider investing in a silk or satin sleep cap, which will help keep your hair and extensions in place while you sleep.
  • If you have long extensions, consider tying them up in a bun or top knot to keep them from getting tangled or caught.
  • Be gentle when removing your extensions in the morning, and take the time to brush them thoroughly to prevent any tangles or knots.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about sleeping with clip in hair extensions:

  • Can I sleep with clip in hair extensions every night? While it’s possible to sleep with clip in hair extensions every night, it’s not recommended. It’s important to give your hair and scalp a break from the weight and tension of the extensions.
  • How long can I wear clip in hair extensions? Clip in hair extensions can be worn for up to 12 hours at a time, but it’s recommended to remove them before bed to prevent damage to your natural hair.
  • Can I wash my clip in hair extensions before bed? It’s not recommended to wash your clip in hair extensions before bed, as they need time to dry and set properly.

Hair Care Tips for Clip In Hair Extensions

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your clip in hair extensions looking and feeling their best:

  • Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, as this can cause damage and shorten their lifespan.
  • Wash your extensions every 6-8 wears, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent damage.

With these tips and techniques, sleeping with clip in hair extensions can be a stress-free experience. Remember to take the time to prepare your hair before bed, choose a comfortable sleeping position, and use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and static. Happy sleeping!
