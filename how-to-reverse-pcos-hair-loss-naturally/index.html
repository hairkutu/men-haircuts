How To Reverse Pcos Hair Loss Naturally

Written by Christine Jul 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) affects millions of women worldwide and is one of the leading causes of hair loss. PCOS is a hormonal disorder that causes an imbalance in the levels of estrogen and testosterone in the body. This imbalance can lead to hair loss, thinning hair, and baldness. In this article, we will discuss how to reverse PCOS hair loss naturally.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Reverse PCOS Hair Loss Naturally
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

PCOS hair loss is caused by an excess of androgens, which are male hormones that are present in women too. These hormones cause the hair follicles to shrink, resulting in hair loss. The hair loss can occur on the scalp, face, chest, and back. PCOS hair loss can be distressing and can lead to a loss of confidence and self-esteem.

How to Reverse PCOS Hair Loss Naturally

There are several natural ways to reverse PCOS hair loss. These include:

Diet

Eating a healthy diet is important for overall health and can help to reduce the symptoms of PCOS. A diet that is low in carbohydrates and high in protein and healthy fats can help to regulate insulin levels and reduce androgens. Foods that are rich in biotin, such as eggs, nuts, and leafy greens, can also help to promote healthy hair growth.

Exercise

Regular exercise can help to reduce insulin resistance and improve the body's ability to use insulin. This can help to reduce androgens and promote healthy hair growth. Exercise can also help to reduce stress, which can contribute to hair loss.

Supplements

Some supplements can help to promote healthy hair growth in women with PCOS. These include biotin, iron, vitamin D, and omega-3 fatty acids. These supplements can be taken in pill form or obtained through the diet.

Herbs

Herbs such as saw palmetto, spearmint, and licorice root can help to reduce androgens and promote healthy hair growth. These herbs can be taken in supplement form or added to tea.

Stress Reduction

Reducing stress can help to promote healthy hair growth by reducing the levels of cortisol, a stress hormone that can contribute to hair loss. Practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, and deep breathing can help to reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to reverse PCOS hair loss naturally:

  1. Eat a healthy diet that is low in carbohydrates and high in protein and healthy fats.
  2. Exercise regularly to reduce insulin resistance and improve the body's ability to use insulin.
  3. Take supplements such as biotin, iron, vitamin D, and omega-3 fatty acids to promote healthy hair growth.
  4. Add herbs such as saw palmetto, spearmint, and licorice root to your diet to reduce androgens and promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Practice relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, and deep breathing to reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you reverse PCOS hair loss naturally:

  • Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new supplements or herbs.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage the hair and contribute to hair loss.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair and contribute to hair loss.
  • Be patient, as it may take several months to see results.

FAQ

Can PCOS hair loss be reversed naturally?

Yes, PCOS hair loss can be reversed naturally through diet, exercise, supplements, herbs, and stress reduction techniques.

How long does it take to see results?

It may take several months to see results, as healthy hair growth takes time.

What supplements are good for PCOS hair loss?

Supplements that are good for PCOS hair loss include biotin, iron, vitamin D, and omega-3 fatty acids.

Hair Care

In addition to the natural remedies mentioned above, it is important to take good care of your hair to promote healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage the hair and contribute to hair loss.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair and contribute to hair loss.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, and avoid brushing wet hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

By following these natural remedies and hair care tips, you can reverse PCOS hair loss naturally and promote healthy hair growth.
