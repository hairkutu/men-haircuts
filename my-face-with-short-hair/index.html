My Face With Short Hair A New Look In 2023

Written by Luffy May 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice among women. It can be chic, edgy, and versatile. In 2023, I decided to take the plunge and chop off my long locks. I was nervous, but I couldn't be happier with the results. In this blog post, I'll share my experience with my face with short hair, including how to style it, tips, and hair care.

Description

My face with short hair is a dramatic change from my previous look. My hair is now cut to just above my shoulders in a blunt bob. The hair is parted in the middle with slight layers, giving it a soft and effortless look. This style frames my face beautifully and accentuates my features, especially my cheekbones and jawline. It's a low-maintenance style that is perfect for busy women.

How to

The first step in achieving my face with short hair is to find a good hair stylist. I went to a stylist who specializes in short haircuts and brought in pictures of the style I wanted. It's important to communicate with your stylist to ensure that you both have the same vision in mind.

Once you have your new cut, it's time to style it. My go-to style is a messy, beachy wave. To achieve this look, I use a curling wand on sections of my hair, leaving the ends out for a more relaxed look. I then use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Another style I love is a sleek and straight look. I use a flat iron to straighten my hair and finish with a shine serum.

Step by Step

1. Find a good stylist who specializes in short haircuts.

2. Communicate with your stylist to ensure that you both have the same vision in mind.

3. Style your hair in a messy, beachy wave or a sleek and straight look.

4. Use the right hair products to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Tips

1. Invest in good hair products that are designed for short hair.

2. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.

3. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks.

4. Get regular trims to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair work for my face shape?

A: Short hair can work for any face shape, but it's important to find a style that complements your features.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a half-up style or a messy bun.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it's still important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for short hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Avoid over-styling your hair with hot tools, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Get regular trims to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Overall, my face with short hair has been a great change for me. It's given me a fresh new look and has been easy to maintain. If you're considering a short haircut, go for it! With the right style and care, you'll love your new look.
