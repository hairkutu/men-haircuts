Which Hair Cut Is Best For Round Face

Written by William May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what hair cut will flatter your face shape the most. In this article, we will discuss the different hair cuts that are best for round faces and how to style them. By the end of this article, you will have a better idea of what hair cut will suit you best.

Description

A round face has soft, curved lines with the width and length in the same proportions. The chin and forehead are usually rounded, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. The goal of a hair cut for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face shape.

How to

When choosing a hair cut for a round face, it is important to consider the length, texture, and style. Here are some tips to keep in mind when deciding:

Length

  • Long hair can elongate the face, but avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.
  • Short hair can also be flattering, but avoid pixie cuts that are too short.
  • Medium length hair is a safe choice and can be styled in many ways.

Texture

  • Straight hair can make the face appear longer, but add layers for movement.
  • Curly or wavy hair can add volume to the top of the head, but avoid adding volume to the sides.

Style

  • Side-swept bangs can help lengthen the face.
  • Layers can add texture and volume.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.

Step by Step

Here are some hair cuts that are best for round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can elongate the face and add movement to the hair. Ask your stylist for layers that start at the collarbone and angle downward. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help lengthen the face and add dimension. Ask your stylist for long, wispy bangs that are swept to the side.

3. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers can add volume and texture to the hair. Ask your stylist for a bob that ends just below the chin and has layers throughout.

4. Pixie Cut with Side-Swept Bangs

A pixie cut can be flattering for round faces if it has side-swept bangs. Ask your stylist for a short cut with long, wispy bangs that are swept to the side.

Tips

  • Avoid adding volume to the sides of the hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying hair to add volume to the roots.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What hair cut should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin. They can make the face appear wider.

What length is best for a hair cut for a round face?

Long hair can elongate the face, but avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin. Short hair can also be flattering, but avoid pixie cuts that are too short. Medium length hair is a safe choice and can be styled in many ways.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Overall, the best hair cut for a round face is one that elongates the face and adds dimension. Consider the length, texture, and style when deciding on a hair cut. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
