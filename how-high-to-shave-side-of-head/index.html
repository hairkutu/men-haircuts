How High To Shave Side Of Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Shaving the side of your head is a bold and edgy hairstyle that has been popular for years. But, before you grab a razor and start shaving, it's important to understand how high to shave the side of your head. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to shave the side of your head, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shave the Side of Your Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaving the Side of Your Head
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Shaving the Side of Your Head

How to Shave the Side of Your Head

Shaving the side of your head is a simple process that can be done at home with the right tools and technique. But before you start, you need to decide how high you want to shave. Here are some factors to consider:

  • Your hair type: If you have thick or curly hair, shaving too high may result in an uneven look.
  • Your face shape: Shaving too high can elongate your face or make your forehead appear larger.
  • Your personal style: Shaving higher up on the side of your head will create a more dramatic look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to shave the side of your head:

  1. Start by washing your hair to remove any product buildup.
  2. Decide how high you want to shave and use a comb to section off the hair above that point. Secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Use clippers or a razor to shave the hair below the sectioned-off area. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter one to achieve the desired length.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Blend the shaved area with the rest of your hair using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Shaving the Side of Your Head

Follow these tips to ensure the best results:

  • Invest in high-quality clippers or a razor to avoid irritation and uneven cuts.
  • Have a friend or professional help you for a more precise cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any missed spots.
  • Consider using a shaving cream or gel to reduce irritation and make the process smoother.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about shaving the side of your head:

How high should I shave the side of my head?

It depends on your personal preference and other factors, such as your hair type and face shape. Generally, shaving about halfway up the side of your head is a good starting point.

Can I shave the side of my head with a razor?

Yes, you can use a razor to shave the side of your head, but it may take longer and require more precision than using clippers.

Will shaving the side of my head damage my hair?

No, shaving the side of your head will not damage your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair after shaving to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care After Shaving the Side of Your Head

After shaving the side of your head, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp with a lightweight oil or serum to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Be patient as your hair grows back and avoid re-shaving too soon to prevent irritation.

By following these tips and techniques, you can successfully shave the side of your head and maintain a stylish and edgy look.
