How To Cut Men s Curly Hair With Scissors For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to give your curly-haired clients a fresh new cut? Cutting curly hair can be intimidating, especially if you're a beginner. But fear not! With the right tools and techniques, you can give your clients a stylish and professional haircut. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's curly hair with scissors for beginners.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing the Hair
  • Sectioning the Hair
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Blending the Hair
  • Clean Up
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather the right tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or rubber bands
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Preparing the Hair

Before you start cutting, you'll need to prep the hair:

  1. Wash and condition the hair.
  2. Towel-dry the hair until it's damp.
  3. Detangle the hair using a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling product to help define the curls.
  5. Part the hair where you want the final part to be.

Sectioning the Hair

Sectioning the hair will help you achieve an even cut:

  1. Start by creating a horizontal part at the nape of the neck.
  2. Clip the top section of the hair out of the way.
  3. Divide the bottom section of the hair into smaller sections.
  4. Starting with the bottom section, take a small subsection of hair and comb it straight down.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the desired length with your scissors.
  6. Move on to the next subsection of hair, using the previous subsection as a guide.
  7. Once you've finished the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the process.

Cutting the Hair

Now that you've sectioned the hair, it's time to start cutting:

  1. Starting with the bottom section, take a small subsection of hair and comb it straight down.
  2. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the desired length with your scissors.
  3. Move on to the next subsection of hair, using the previous subsection as a guide.
  4. Once you've finished the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the process.

Blending the Hair

After you've cut all the sections, it's time to blend the hair:

  1. Take a small subsection of hair from the top and bottom sections.
  2. Comb the hair straight down.
  3. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the desired length with your scissors.
  4. Repeat the process until you've blended all the sections.

Clean Up

After you've finished cutting, it's time to clean up:

  1. Use a comb to check for any uneven sections.
  2. Trim any stray hairs or uneven sections.
  3. Blow-dry the hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance the curls.

Tips for Cutting Men's Curly Hair

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a comb to check for any uneven sections.
  • Be conservative with your cutting. It's better to cut less hair and make multiple passes than to cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

FAQs

How often should men with curly hair get a haircut?

Men with curly hair should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain their style and prevent split ends.

Can men with curly hair use the same scissors as women?

Yes, men with curly hair can use the same scissors as women. The most important thing is to use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

Is it better to cut curly hair wet or dry?

It's generally better to cut curly hair when it's dry. This allows you to see how the curls fall naturally and make more precise cuts.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

After cutting, it's important to take care of your client's curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or styling product to define the curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

Cutting men's curly hair with scissors can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut. Remember to start with small sections, be conservative with your cutting, and use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying to enhance the curls. With practice, you'll become a pro at cutting men's curly hair!
