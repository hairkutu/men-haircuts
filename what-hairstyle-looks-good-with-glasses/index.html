What Hairstyle Looks Good With Glasses A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you someone who wears glasses and is always confused about what hairstyle to opt for? Well, you are not alone. Glasses can be a statement accessory that adds charm to your overall look. However, finding the right hairstyle can sometimes be challenging. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about what hairstyle looks good with glasses.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose The Right Hairstyle
  3. Step By Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for People with Glasses

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle when you wear glasses can be tricky. The hairstyle you choose should complement the shape of your face, the type of glasses you wear, and your lifestyle. A good hairstyle can enhance your facial features, while the wrong one can make you look awkward or unbalanced. In this article, we will help you choose the perfect hairstyle that looks good with your glasses.

How To Choose The Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for you:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape. There are different face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Each face shape requires a different hairstyle. For instance, if you have a heart-shaped face, you should opt for a hairstyle that adds volume to the top and balances out your jawline.

Consider Your Glasses Frames

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your glasses frames. If you have thick frames, you should opt for a hairstyle that doesn't compete with your glasses. For instance, you can go for a sleek hairstyle that doesn't add volume to your face. On the other hand, if you have thin frames, you can go for a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also influence your hairstyle choice. If you are always on the go, you should opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require much upkeep. On the other hand, if you have a lot of free time, you can opt for a more elaborate hairstyle.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is determining your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at the shape of your face in the mirror.

Step 2: Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or celebrities who have a similar face shape to yours. You can also consult with your hairstylist for suggestions.

Step 3: Consider Your Glasses Frames

Consider the shape and size of your glasses frames. You don't want your hairstyle to compete with your glasses.

Step 4: Test the Look

Before committing to a new hairstyle, try it out first. You can use a virtual hairstyle app or try on wigs to see how the hairstyle looks with your glasses.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Tip 1: Go for Volume

If you have thin frames, you can opt for a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair. This will balance out your look and make your glasses look more prominent.

Tip 2: Avoid Bangs

Avoid bangs if you have thick frames. Bangs can make your face look crowded and compete with your glasses.

Tip 3: Keep it Simple

If you have thick frames, go for a simple hairstyle that doesn't add volume to your face. A sleek ponytail or a low bun is an excellent option.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Hairstyle Looks Good with Round Glasses?

If you have round glasses, you should opt for a hairstyle that adds height to your face. A high ponytail or an updo will look good with round glasses.

What Hairstyle Looks Good with Square Glasses?

If you have square glasses, you should opt for a hairstyle that softens your face. Soft waves or a layered cut will look good with square glasses.

What Hairstyle Looks Good with Oval Glasses?

If you have oval glasses, you can opt for any hairstyle. Oval glasses are versatile and can complement any hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for People with Glasses

If you wear glasses, you should take extra care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for people with glasses:

Tip 1: Avoid Hair Products that Can Damage your Glasses

Avoid hair products that can damage your glasses, such as hair spray. Hair spray can leave a residue on your glasses, making them difficult to clean.

Tip 2: Clean Your Glasses Regularly

Clean your glasses regularly to avoid smudges and fingerprints. Use a microfiber cloth to clean your glasses to avoid scratches.

Tip 3: Keep Your Glasses in Place

To avoid your glasses from slipping down your nose, use a hairband or clips to keep them in place.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, it can be easy. Consider your face shape, glasses frames, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. Remember to keep it simple and avoid hairstyles that compete with your glasses. With these tips, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that looks good with your glasses.
