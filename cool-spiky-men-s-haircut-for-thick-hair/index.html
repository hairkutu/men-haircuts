Cool Spiky Men s Haircut For Thick Hair A Guide To Achieving A Stylish Look

Written by Robby Jun 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your plain and boring hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and exciting? If you have thick hair, a cool spiky haircut might just be what you need. Not only does it give you a trendy and edgy look, but it also helps to manage your thick hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve a cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair.

Description

A cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair is a style that is characterized by short, spiky hair on the top of the head and faded or tapered sides. This style is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types. It is also low maintenance and easy to style, making it a popular choice among men.

How to Achieve a Cool Spiky Men's Haircut for Thick Hair

The first step in achieving a cool spiky haircut is to choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. If you have a round face, a spiky haircut with longer hair on top and shorter sides can help to elongate your face. For those with a square face, a shorter spiky haircut with a high fade can help to soften your features.

After choosing the right haircut, the next step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can execute the style. A professional hairstylist can help to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head seamlessly with the spiky hair on top. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your spiky haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair:

  1. Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a skilled hairstylist who can execute the style.
  3. Clean your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as wax or pomade.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair upwards into spikes.
  6. Set your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Spiky Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your spiky haircut.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to set your spikes in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause your spikes to flatten.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair:

1. Is a spiky haircut suitable for all hair types?

A spiky haircut can work for most hair types, but it is best suited for thick hair, as it provides more volume and texture.

2. How often should I trim my spiky haircut?

You should trim your spiky haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

3. Can I style my spiky haircut differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to achieve different looks, such as a messy or slicked-back style.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips for men with thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, a cool spiky men's haircut for thick hair is a stylish and low maintenance hairstyle that can suit different face shapes and hair types. By following our guide and tips, you can achieve and maintain this trendy haircut and look your best every day.
