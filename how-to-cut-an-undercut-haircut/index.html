How To Cut An Undercut Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut, an undercut might be the perfect choice for you. An undercut is a style where the hair on the top of the head is left longer, while the sides and back are shaved or cut very short. This style has been popular for several years now, and it doesn't seem to be going anywhere anytime soon.

How to Cut an Undercut Haircut

Cutting an undercut haircut is not as difficult as it may seem, but it does require some practice and patience. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by sectioning the hair. Clip the hair on the top of the head out of the way, leaving only the sides and back.
  2. Choose the length you want for the undercut. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your head. Use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back to the desired length.
  3. Use a comb and scissors to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.
  4. Repeat this process on the other side of the head.
  5. If desired, you can use clippers or scissors to create a fade effect between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  6. Once the undercut is complete, you can style the longer hair on top however you like. This can be done with a comb, brush, or your fingers.

Tips for Cutting an Undercut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting an undercut:

  • Take your time and work slowly. It's better to take small sections of hair at a time rather than trying to cut too much at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles. This will help you make sure the undercut is even on both sides.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider visiting a professional stylist for help.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

Q: Is an undercut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes! Undercuts work for all hair types, from straight and fine to thick and curly.

Q: How often do I need to get an undercut trimmed?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, you'll need to get it trimmed every four to six weeks.

Q: Can I still tie my hair back with an undercut?

A: Yes! While the sides and back of your hair will be shorter, the longer hair on top can still be tied back into a ponytail or bun.

Hair Care Tips for an Undercut

Once you've got your undercut, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a small amount of styling product to keep the longer hair on top looking neat and tidy.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

Now that you know how to cut an undercut haircut, you can try this trendy style for yourself. With a little practice and patience, you'll be rocking the undercut look in no time!
