What Face Shape Best Suits Curtain Bangs Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try, curtain bangs might just be what you need. They're versatile, easy to style, and can complement any face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing what face shape best suits curtain bangs and how you can rock this trendy hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curtain Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that frame the face and are parted down the middle, resembling a set of curtains. This hairstyle is very versatile and can add volume and texture to any hair length or style. The best part is that curtain bangs can complement any face shape, as long as they are styled correctly.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs, it's important to consult with your hairstylist to make sure they're the right fit for you. Once you've decided on the style, your hairdresser will likely cut your bangs while your hair is dry, to ensure the perfect length and shape.

What to Consider

When deciding on the length and shape of your curtain bangs, there are a few factors to consider:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • The amount of maintenance you're willing to do

It's important to keep in mind that curtain bangs require regular trimming and styling, so be prepared to visit your hairstylist every few weeks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curtain Bangs

Styling curtain bangs is relatively easy, and there are a few different ways to wear them depending on your preference. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect curtain bangs:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Take a section of hair from your forehead and comb it forward
  4. Trim your bangs to the desired length using hair cutting scissors
  5. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, curling them away from your face
  6. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as hair oil or serum, to add shine and control any flyaways
  7. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to keep them looking fresh
  • Avoid using heated styling tools on your bangs, as this can cause damage
  • Use a small amount of hairspray to keep your bangs in place
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become oily
  • Wear a headband or hair clip to keep your bangs out of your face when exercising or doing other activities

FAQs

What face shape suits curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can complement any face shape, as long as they are styled correctly. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for longer bangs that are swept to the sides, while those with a heart-shaped face may prefer shorter, more blunt bangs.

How do I style curtain bangs?

To style curtain bangs, simply blow-dry them with a round brush, curling them away from your face. You can also add a small amount of hair product, such as hair oil or serum, to add shine and control any flyaways.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs require regular trimming, so you should plan on visiting your hairstylist every few weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every few days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

Overall, curtain bangs are a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can complement any face shape. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock this look with confidence.
