Is It Haram To Get Box Braids

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Box braids have been a popular hairstyle for many years, but for some Muslims, there is a question of whether it is haram (forbidden) to get them. This article will explore that question and provide information on how to care for box braids once you have them.

Description

Box braids are a protective hairstyle that involves dividing the hair into small, even sections and braiding each section from the root to the tip. The end result is a series of small, box-shaped braids that can be styled in many different ways.

How to Get Box Braids

If you are interested in getting box braids, you should first find a reputable hairstylist who is experienced in this type of hairstyle. They will be able to advise you on the best length and thickness for your hair, as well as the type of hair extensions to use if you want to add length or volume to your hair.

Once you have chosen your stylist, they will begin by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure that it is free of any dirt or oil that could interfere with the braiding process. They will then divide your hair into sections and begin braiding each section from the root to the tip.

Depending on the length and thickness of your hair, the braiding process can take several hours to complete. Once the braids are finished, your stylist will trim any loose ends and style the braids in a way that is flattering to your face shape and personal style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get box braids:

Step 1: Choose a Hairstylist

Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in box braids. Ask for recommendations from friends, family members, or online reviews.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before the braiding process begins, your hairstylist will wash and condition your hair to ensure that it is clean and free of any dirt or oil.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Your hairstylist will divide your hair into small, even sections using a comb or a parting tool.

Step 4: Begin Braiding

Your hairstylist will begin braiding each section from the root to the tip. They may add hair extensions to each braid if you want to add length or volume to your hair.

Step 5: Trim Loose Ends

After the braiding process is complete, your hairstylist will trim any loose ends to ensure that your braids are neat and tidy.

Step 6: Style Your Braids

Your hairstylist will style your braids in a way that is flattering to your face shape and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids

Once you have box braids, it is important to take good care of them to ensure that they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your box braids:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids or cause buildup on your scalp.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase to prevent frizzing and tangling.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling or pulling on your braids, as this can cause them to unravel or break.

FAQs

Is it haram to get box braids?

There is no consensus among Muslims on whether box braids are haram or not. Some believe that they are permissible as a form of cultural expression, while others believe that they are imitating non-Muslim hairstyles and therefore forbidden. Ultimately, the decision to get box braids is a personal one that should be based on your own beliefs and values.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from four to eight weeks, depending on how well you take care of them and how quickly your hair grows. It is important to avoid excessive heat styling or pulling on your braids, as this can cause them to unravel or break.

Can I wash my box braids?

Yes, you can wash your box braids, but it is important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids or cause buildup on your scalp.

Hair Care

Box braids are a great protective hairstyle that can help to keep your natural hair healthy and strong. However, it is important to take good care of your hair while it is in braids to ensure that it stays healthy and doesn't break or become damaged. This includes keeping your scalp clean and moisturized, avoiding excessive heat styling or pulling on your braids, and using gentle products that are suitable for your hair type.
