Best Hairstyles For Small Thin Faces In 2023

Are you tired of trying out hairstyles that don't flatter your small, thin face? Well, fret no more! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for small thin faces in 2023. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for small thin faces, it's important to determine your face shape. There are 5 main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead
  • Cheekbones
  • Jawline
  • Face length

Once you have these measurements, compare them to determine your face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your forehead and jawline are rounded, you have a round face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrow at the forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face.

Best Hairstyles for Small Thin Faces

Now that you know your face shape, let's talk about the best hairstyles for small thin faces:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! You can pull off just about any hairstyle. However, shoulder-length hair with layers is a great choice. This adds volume and dimension to your hair without overwhelming your face.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for a long, layered cut that adds volume and elongates your face. A side-swept bang also works well to break up the roundness of your face.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you want to soften your features. Long, wavy hair with layers that frame your face works well. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your features. Shoulder-length hair with layers and side-swept bangs works well. Avoid hairstyles that are too top-heavy, such as a high bun or top knot.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face, you want to soften your features and add volume to the sides of your face. A layered bob or shoulder-length cut with waves works well. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you have an idea of the best hairstyles for small thin faces, here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying.
  • When using a curling iron, curl your hair away from your face to create a more open look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still rock these hairstyles! Just make sure to use volumizing products and avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

What if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, opt for a layered cut that adds volume and dimension to your hair. A pixie cut can also work well for small thin faces.

Can I still wear bangs?

Yes! Bangs can work well for small thin faces. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Small Thin Faces

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for small thin faces:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Limit your use of hair products, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that work well for small thin faces. By choosing the right cut and styling your hair properly, you can accentuate your features and feel confident in your appearance. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
