How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Only At Home

Written by Eveline Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time on haircuts, learning how to cut men's hair with scissors only at home can be a great option. With a few tools and some patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your house. In this article, we'll go over how to cut men's hair with scissors only at home step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • Tools Needed
  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Only at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Better Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After a Haircut

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting hair, you'll need to gather a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Large mirror and smaller handheld mirror (or two mirrors facing each other)
  • Towel or cape to protect clothing

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Only at Home

Cutting men's hair with scissors only at home can seem intimidating, but with some basic knowledge and the right tools, you can achieve a great haircut. Here's how:

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly.

Step 2: Decide on the length you want to cut. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut straight across.

Step 3: Use hair clips to section off the hair to make cutting easier.

Step 4: Cut the hair in sections, starting from the back of the head and working your way to the front.

Step 5: Blend the hair between sections by combing the hair and cutting at an angle.

Step 6: Use the smaller scissors to trim around the ears and neckline.

Step 7: Double-check for any uneven spots and make necessary adjustments.

Tips for a Better Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair with scissors only at home:

  • Start with longer hair and make small cuts to avoid taking off too much at once.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid fatigue and mistakes.
  • Use the larger mirror to check the back of the head and the smaller handheld mirror to see the sides and front.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from a friend or family member.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with scissors only at home:

Q: Can I use regular household scissors to cut hair?

A: No, it's best to use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair to achieve a clean cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Q: What if I mess up my haircut?

A: Don't worry, hair grows back! You can always go to a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After a Haircut

After cutting men's hair with scissors only at home, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain the new style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Style hair with a hair product that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Trim hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.

Cutting men's hair with scissors only at home can be a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully, and don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from a friend or family member. Happy cutting!
