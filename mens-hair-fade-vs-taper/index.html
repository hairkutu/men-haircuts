Mens Hair Fade Vs Taper Which One Suits You Better

Written by Eveline Jul 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you planning on getting a new haircut soon? Then you might have come across these two terms - mens hair fade vs taper. Both the haircuts are quite popular and have their own unique style. But, which one is better for you? In this article, we'll help you understand the differences between the two and help you choose the one that suits you better.

Description

The men's hair fade and taper are both short haircuts that are popular among men. The primary difference between the two is the way the hair is cut. A fade is a gradual decrease in hair length from the top to the sides and back. On the other hand, a taper is a haircut where the hair length gradually decreases from the top to the bottom.

How To

If you're planning on getting a hair fade or taper, it's important to choose a good barber or hair stylist who can give you the desired look. Here's how you can get a hair fade or taper:

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Fade:

  1. Start by choosing the length of hair you want on the top of your head.
  2. Ask your barber to use clippers to gradually reduce the hair length on the sides and back of your head.
  3. Your barber will start with a longer clipper guard size and gradually switch to smaller sizes to create the fade effect.
  4. Repeat the process until the hair is the desired length.
  5. Finally, the barber will use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Taper:

  1. Start by choosing the length of hair you want on the top of your head.
  2. Ask your barber to use scissors or clippers to gradually reduce the hair length from the top of your head to the bottom.
  3. Your barber will use a comb and scissors to blend the hair length from the top to the bottom.
  4. Repeat the process until the hair is the desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips that you should keep in mind while getting a hair fade or taper:

  • Choose a barber or hair stylist who has experience in doing hair fades or tapers.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber about the length and style you want.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Maintain your hair regularly by getting a trim every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hair fade vs taper:

1. Which is better, hair fade or taper?

Both haircuts are quite popular and have their own unique style. It depends on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve.

2. How often should I get a hair fade or taper?

You should get a hair fade or taper every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

3. Can I style my hair after getting a hair fade or taper?

Yes, you can style your hair after getting a hair fade or taper. You can use hair gel, wax or pomade to style your hair as per your preference.

Hair Care

After getting a hair fade or taper, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

So, now that you understand the difference between mens hair fade vs taper, you can choose the one that suits you best. Don't forget to follow the tips and take care of your hair to maintain the style.
