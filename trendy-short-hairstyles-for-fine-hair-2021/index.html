Trendy Short Hairstyles For Fine Hair 2021

Written by Daniel Apr 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hairstyles are a great way to refresh your look and create a bold statement. However, if you have fine hair, finding the right short hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will add volume and texture to your hair, without making it look flat or lifeless. In this blog post, we'll explore some trendy short hairstyles for fine hair in 2021.

Description

When it comes to short hairstyles for fine hair, it's all about finding a style that will add volume and texture. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves. Short hairstyles also work well with fine hair, as they make it easier to create volume and add texture.

How to

If you're considering a short hairstyle for your fine hair, here are some things to keep in mind:

Consider your face shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape. Some styles work better with certain face shapes than others. For example, a bob with side-swept bangs works well with a heart-shaped face, while a pixie cut looks great on an oval-shaped face.

Choose a style with layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to fine hair. A layered bob or a shaggy pixie cut are both great options for adding volume to your hair.

Opt for curls or waves

Curly or wavy hair can add volume and texture to fine hair. Consider a short hairstyle with loose curls or waves to add some movement to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are some trendy short hairstyles for fine hair in 2021:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for fine hair. This style is low maintenance and easy to style. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short cut with layers.

The Bob

A bob is another great short hairstyle for fine hair. This style can be worn straight or wavy and works well with layers. To add some volume to your hair, consider adding some texture with a beach wave spray.

The Shaggy Pixie Cut

A shaggy pixie cut is a great option for adding volume and texture to fine hair. This style features choppy layers and works well with a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

Use the right products

Use products that are designed for fine hair to add volume and texture. Look for products that contain volumizing ingredients like rice protein or keratin.

Style your hair when it's wet

When styling your hair, start with damp hair. This will make it easier to add volume and texture to your hair.

Get regular trims

To maintain your short hairstyle, get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What short hairstyles work best for fine hair?

Short hairstyles with layers, curls, or waves work best for fine hair. A bob, pixie cut, or shaggy pixie cut are all great options for adding volume and texture to fine hair.

What products should I use for fine hair?

Look for products that are designed for fine hair and contain volumizing ingredients like rice protein or keratin.

How often should I get a trim?

To maintain your short hairstyle, get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

Caring for your fine hair is important to keep it looking healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle and formulated for fine hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain nourishing ingredients like argan oil or avocado oil.

Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look flat or lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

Get regular hair treatments

Get regular hair treatments to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Look for treatments that are designed for fine hair and contain ingredients like keratin or collagen.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are a great way to refresh your look and create a bold statement. With these trendy short hairstyles for fine hair in 2021, you can add volume and texture to your hair and create a look that is both stylish and low maintenance.
