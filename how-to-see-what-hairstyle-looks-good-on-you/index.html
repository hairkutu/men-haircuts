How To See What Hairstyle Looks Good On You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up? Or maybe you're looking for a new look altogether, but you're not sure what would suit you? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to see what hairstyle looks good on you in just a few easy steps. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture
  • Step 3: Look for Inspiration
  • Step 4: Try Virtual Makeover Tools
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that compliments your features and enhances your natural beauty. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

The next step is to consider your hair type and texture. Do you have thick or thin hair? Is it curly or straight? These factors will impact the types of hairstyles that will suit you best. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can weigh down your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to opt for a style that embraces your natural texture.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you know your face shape and hair type, it's time to start looking for inspiration. Browse through magazines, Pinterest, or Instagram to find hairstyles that catch your eye. Look for styles that are similar to your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Try Virtual Makeover Tools

If you're still unsure, try using virtual makeover tools to see what different hairstyles would look like on you. There are many free online tools that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles. This is a great way to see what a new hairstyle would look like without committing to a cut or color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style.
  • Think about your hair texture. Some styles work better with certain hair textures than others.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for advice. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that suits you best.
  • Try something new. If you've always had long hair, why not try a short bob?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and lengthens your face. Long layers or a side-swept fringe can help achieve this.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

People with oval faces can rock just about any hairstyle. Consider a long bob or a sleek updo.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyle for a square face is one that softens your features. Consider a layered bob or long, loose waves.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools.
  • Wash your hair regularly but not too often.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality hair products.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to find the perfect hairstyle that compliments your features and enhances your natural beauty. So go ahead, try something new and embrace your new look!
