How To Make Straight Hair Thick And Wavy

Written by Luffy May 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having thin, lifeless hair? Do you dream of having thick, voluminous waves? Well, you're in luck because we have some tips and tricks to help you achieve that look. In this article, we will show you how to make straight hair thick and wavy.

Description

When it comes to hair, there are two main types of people: those who have naturally thick and wavy hair, and those who don't. If you fall into the latter category, don't worry, you can still achieve that look. By following the steps below, you can make your straight hair thick and wavy.

How to

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to achieving thick and wavy hair is to start with the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to hair. These products will help to create a good foundation for the rest of your styling routine.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Once you have washed your hair, it's time to start styling. Begin by blow-drying your hair upside down. This will help to create volume at the roots and give your hair a lift. Use a round brush to help create waves and curls.

Step 3: Use a Curling Wand

If you want more defined waves, use a curling wand to create curls throughout your hair. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, wrapping small sections of hair around the wand. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Use Hair Spray

To help hold your waves in place, use a good quality hairspray. Spray your hair lightly, holding the can about 10 inches away from your head. Be sure to use a hairspray that won't make your hair stiff and crunchy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve thick and wavy hair:

- Use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying your hair
- Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help create waves
- Sleep with your hair in braids to create natural waves in the morning
- Use a sea salt spray to create a beachy, textured look
- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve this look if I have short hair?
A: Yes, you can still achieve this look if you have short hair. Use a smaller curling wand and focus on creating waves at the top of your head.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make straight hair thick and wavy, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve the voluminous, textured look you've been dreaming of.
