Men s Haircut Longer On Top With Blended Short Sides The Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a trendy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd, the men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides is the perfect choice. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is expected to be a top trend for 2023. The combination of longer hair on top and shorter sides creates a stylish and modern look that can be customized to suit any face shape or personal style. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides is a versatile hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head that gradually blends into shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair is usually cut to a length of 2-4 inches on the top and tapered down to a shorter length on the sides and back. This creates a clean and polished look that can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

How to Achieve the Look

The men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides can be achieved by following these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

It's important to choose a barber who has experience cutting this type of hairstyle. Look for a barber who specializes in men's haircuts and has a portfolio of work that includes this style.

Step 2: Discuss Your Desired Look

Before your barber starts cutting, make sure you discuss your desired look. Show them pictures or examples of the style you want, and be clear about how much length you want on the top and how short you want the sides.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The barber will start by cutting the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. They may use scissors or clippers, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Next, the barber will use clippers to gradually blend the hair on the sides and back. They will start with a longer guard and work their way down to a shorter guard to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 5: Style as Desired

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair as desired using products like pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

Tip 1: Get Regular Trims

To maintain the shape and length of your haircut, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

Tip 2: Use Quality Products

Invest in quality hair products like shampoo, conditioner, and styling products to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find the one that suits you best. Try slicking your hair back with pomade for a classic look, or use wax to create a messy, textured style.

FAQ

Q: Is the men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape. Talk to your barber about the best way to tailor the style to your unique features.

Q: Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

A: Yes, this hairstyle works well with curly hair. However, you may need to use some additional products to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

Q: How long does it take to style this haircut?

A: The time it takes to style this haircut depends on the look you're going for and the length of your hair. On average, it takes about 5-10 minutes to style this haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein in your diet.

Overall, the men's haircut longer on top with blended short sides is a stylish and modern look that is perfect for 2023. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
