Best Long Bangs For Oval Face A Complete Guide

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky. Your face shape is considered the most versatile as you can pull off different hairstyles and bangs. However, with so many options out there, it can be tough to find the right bangs that complement your face shape.

Description

Long bangs can be a game-changer for oval face shapes. They can soften your features, frame your face, and add a touch of glamour to your look. However, not all long bangs are created equal. Some types of bangs can make your face shape appear longer, wider, or more angular. Therefore, it's essential to choose the right type of long bangs that flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Long Bangs for Oval Face

The best long bangs for oval face shape are those that add width to your forehead, balance your facial features, and highlight your eyes. Here are some tips to help you choose the right long bangs:

  • Opt for side-swept bangs that start from the temple and fall diagonally towards the cheekbone. This will create an illusion of width and balance your face shape.
  • Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make your face look longer and sharper.
  • Consider layered bangs that blend with the rest of your hair and add movement to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with fringe bangs that start from the middle of your forehead and gradually get longer towards the sides. This can add dimension to your face and accentuate your eyes.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Long Bangs for Oval Face

Cutting long bangs can be intimidating, but with the right technique, you can do it at home. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting long bangs for oval face shape:

  1. Start with dry hair and comb it straight down.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the crown of your head, and the point should be at the middle of your forehead.
  3. Twist the hair to create tension and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  4. Cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the middle of the forehead and getting longer towards the sides.
  5. Release the hair and check the length. If needed, trim the ends to make them even.

Tips to Maintain Long Bangs for Oval Face

Long bangs require regular maintenance to keep them in shape. Here are some tips to help you maintain your long bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if they become frizzy or curly.
  • Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them at the desired length.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too often or using too much product as it can make them greasy or heavy.

FAQs

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still rock long bangs. However, you may need to use a flat iron to straighten them or opt for layered bangs that blend with your curls.

Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your bangs at home, but it's important to use proper tools and techniques to avoid any mishaps. If you're not confident, it's best to visit a salon.

What if I don't like my bangs?

If you don't like your bangs, don't worry. They will grow out eventually. In the meantime, you can pin them back or try different hairstyles to conceal them.

Hair Care Tips for Long Bangs

Long bangs require extra care to keep them healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for long bangs:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

With the right bangs and hair care routine, you can enhance your oval face shape and look fabulous. Try out different long bangs styles and see what works best for you.
