Best Haircut For Oblong Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a man with an oblong face shape and wondering what haircut would suit you best? Look no further! We have compiled a comprehensive guide to the best haircuts for oblong face males. Whether you have short hair, long hair, or somewhere in between, we have got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions about the best haircut for oblong face male.

Description of Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is characterized by a longer, narrow face with a strong jawline and cheekbones. This face shape is longer than it is wide and can sometimes appear more oval than oblong. The key to finding the right haircut for an oblong face shape is to add width to the forehead and temples while minimizing the length of the face.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oblong Face Male

When choosing a haircut for an oblong face male, it is essential to consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • Your hair type (curly, straight, thick, or thin)
  • Your personal style and preferences

Once you have taken these factors into consideration, you can start to look for haircuts that will flatter your oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Best Haircut for Oblong Face Male

Follow these simple steps to find the best haircut for your oblong face shape:

  1. First, determine the length of hair you want. Short haircuts work well for oblong face shapes, as they help to widen the forehead and temples. Long hair can also work well, but it is important to add layers to create volume and width.
  2. Next, decide on the style of cut. A side-swept fringe or a messy quiff can help to create the illusion of width on the forehead. Avoid center parts or slicked-back hairstyles, as these can make the face appear even longer.
  3. Consider adding layers to your haircut. Layers can help to create volume and movement, giving the illusion of a wider face shape. This is particularly important for those with longer hair.
  4. Finally, consult with your hairstylist. They can help you to choose a haircut that will flatter your oblong face shape and work with your hair type and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have found the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape, it is important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for oblong face males with short hair?

Short haircuts that work well for oblong face males include the buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop.

What are the best haircuts for oblong face males with long hair?

Long haircuts that work well for oblong face males include the layered cut, messy or textured style, and side-swept fringe.

Can I pull off a center part with an oblong face shape?

No, it is best to avoid center parts with an oblong face shape, as they can make the face appear even longer.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for my oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or too long, as these can elongate the face. Also, avoid center parts and flat hairstyles that lack volume.

In Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for oblong face male can be challenging, but with the help of our guide, you can find a haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. Remember to consider the length of your hair, your hair type, and your personal preferences when choosing a haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape, and maintain it properly with high-quality hair care products and regular trims.
