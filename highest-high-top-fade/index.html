The Highest High Top Fade A Timeless Haircut That Never Goes Out Of Style

Written by William May 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a fan of classic 90s hip hop and R&B, then you’ve probably seen the highest high top fade hairstyle. This iconic haircut has been around for decades and is still one of the most popular among men who want to stand out from the crowd.

Description

The highest high top fade is a hairstyle where the hair is cut short on the sides and back, while the top is left long and styled upwards. The hair on the top is usually flat at the base and gradually gets longer towards the top, forming a pyramid shape. This style is also known as the box fade or the hi-top fade.

How to Get a High Top Fade

Getting a high top fade is not difficult, but it does require some skill and patience. Here’s how to get the perfect high top fade:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Decide on the type of high top fade you want. Do you want a short fade or a longer one? Do you want a boxy shape or a rounded one? Look at pictures online or ask your barber for advice.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

Find a barber who is experienced in cutting high top fades. Look for someone who has a good reputation and has worked on similar hair types.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and let it air dry. Don’t use any products or blow dry your hair as this can affect the final result.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Your barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with clippers. They will then use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Shape the Hair

Once the hair is cut to the right length, your barber will shape it into a pyramid shape. They will use a combination of scissors and clippers to create the right shape.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Apply some styling product to the top of your hair and use a comb to style it upwards. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume.

Tips for Maintaining a High Top Fade

Once you have your high top fade, you’ll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair to keep it in place.
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims.

FAQ

What hair types are best for a high top fade?

A high top fade works best on thick, curly or coily hair. If you have straight hair, you may need to use styling products to create the right shape.

How long does a high top fade last?

A high top fade can last for several weeks, but you’ll need to visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims.

Can I style my high top fade in different ways?

Yes, you can style your high top fade in different ways. You can try adding a part, creating a mohawk, or even adding some color.

Hair Care for High Top Fade

Keeping your high top fade in good condition requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat.

The highest high top fade is a classic hairstyle that is still popular today. Whether you’re into hip hop or just want to stand out from the crowd, this haircut is a great choice for men who want to look their best. Follow these tips and you’ll have a high top fade that turns heads wherever you go.
