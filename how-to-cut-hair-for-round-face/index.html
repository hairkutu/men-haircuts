How To Cut Hair For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting hair for a round face can be quite challenging, but with the right techniques and styles, you can enhance your facial features and look your best. In this guide, we will go through everything you need to know about how to cut hair for a round face, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Hair for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Cutting Hair for Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The key to cutting hair for a round face is to create angles and volume to balance out the roundness of the face.

There are several hairstyles that work well for a round face, including layered cuts, long bobs, and side-swept bangs. These styles help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

How to Cut Hair for Round Face

The first step in cutting hair for a round face is to determine the face shape. Once you have identified a round face, you can proceed with the following steps:

1. Choose the Right Length

The length of the hair is crucial when it comes to cutting hair for a round face. Long hair can elongate the face, while short hair can make it appear wider. A medium-length haircut, such as a long bob, is an excellent choice for a round face.

2. Create Layers

Layered haircuts help to create volume and movement, which can balance out the roundness of the face. Layers can be added to any length of hair and can be customized to suit the individual's face shape.

3. Add Angles

Angles can be created by cutting the hair at an angle or adding side-swept bangs. These techniques help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape.

4. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Blunt cuts can make a round face appear wider and more circular. It is best to avoid blunt cuts and opt for layered cuts instead.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair for a round face:

1. Section the Hair

Section the hair into four parts, starting at the top of the head and working your way down. Use hair clips to secure each section.

2. Cut the Bottom Layer

Start with the bottom layer of the hair and cut it to the desired length. Use a blending technique to create a seamless transition from the bottom layer to the next layer.

3. Add Layers

Add layers to the hair, starting at the back and working your way to the front. Use scissors to create angled cuts that help to elongate the face.

4. Create Bangs

If desired, create side-swept bangs that help to frame the face and create angles.

5. Style the Hair

Style the hair using a blow dryer, a curling iron, or a straightener. Use products that add volume and texture to the hair.

Tips for Cutting Hair for Round Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting hair for a round face:

  • Opt for a middle part instead of a side part, as a side part can make the face appear wider.
  • Use thinning shears to create texture and movement in the hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What hairstyles work best for a round face?

Layered cuts, long bobs, and side-swept bangs work well for a round face.

Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

Short haircuts can make a round face appear wider, so it is best to opt for medium-length haircuts instead.

Hair Care Advice

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut hair for a round face with confidence and create a hairstyle that enhances your natural beauty.
