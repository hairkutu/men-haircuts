How To Cut And Style Medium Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you have medium curly hair and are looking for ways to style and cut it? Look no further, as this guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect look for your hair type. From tips to frequently asked questions and hair care, you'll learn everything you need to know to master your curls.

Description

Medium curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right styling and cutting techniques, it can look stunning. This hair type has loose curls that fall between the chin and the shoulders. It can be thick or thin, and the curls can range from loose waves to tight coils. In this article, we will discuss the best ways to cut and style medium curly hair.

How to Cut Medium Curly Hair

Cutting medium curly hair is an art form. It is important to cut the hair in a way that enhances the natural curls and flatters the face shape. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before cutting the hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Detangle the hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. This will make it easier to cut and prevent any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Part the hair down the middle and separate it into four sections - two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Starting at the back, take a small section of hair and twist it. Use sharp scissors to cut the ends of the twisted section. Repeat this process for all sections of the hair.

Step 4: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them with scissors. Take small sections of the bangs and cut them at an angle. This will give them a soft and natural look.

How to Style Medium Curly Hair

Styling medium curly hair can be fun and creative. Here are some tips on how to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Apply Styling Products

Start by applying a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to damp hair. This will help define the curls and reduce frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the curls.

Step 2: Use Heat Tools

If you want to add more volume or definition to your curls, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Styles

There are many different ways to style medium curly hair. You can wear it down, half-up, or in a ponytail. You can also add accessories like headbands or clips to enhance your look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Cutting and Styling Medium Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting and styling medium curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Cut the hair when it is dry to see how the curls will fall.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair.
  • Apply styling products to damp hair to enhance the curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the curls.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting and styling medium curly hair:

Can I cut my own hair?

You can cut your own hair, but it is recommended to go to a professional stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should get your hair cut every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your curls.

What styling products should I use?

You can use a leave-in conditioner, curl-defining cream, or mousse to enhance your curls. It is best to choose products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Hair Care for Medium Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curls. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut and style your medium curly hair to perfection. Remember to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and looking their best.
