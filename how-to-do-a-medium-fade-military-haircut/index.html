How To Do A Medium Fade Military Haircut

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a classic and stylish haircut, then the medium fade military haircut is a great option. This haircut is easy to maintain and looks great on all face shapes. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a medium fade military haircut in easy to understand steps.

Description

The medium fade military haircut is a type of crew cut that features a gradual decrease in hair length from the top of the head to the sides and back. This haircut is often seen in the military and is a popular choice among men who want a low-maintenance and stylish look. It is also known as the regulation cut.

How to do a Medium Fade Military Haircut

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect medium fade military haircut:

Step 1: Prepare the hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting the haircut. This will make it easier to cut and style. Comb your hair in the direction of growth to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Determine the length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and face shape. Use a clipper with a #4 guard to cut the top of the hair evenly.

Step 3: Create the fade

Use a #2 guard to create a fade on the sides and back of your head. Start by trimming the hair around the ears and then move towards the nape of the neck. Use a comb to blend the hair in a smooth manner as you go along.

Step 4: Blend the top with the sides

Use a clipper with a #3 guard to blend the top of the hair with the sides. Work on the hairline gradually, using a comb to ensure an even and smooth transition.

Step 5: Trim the neckline

Use a trimmer to clean up the neckline and create a defined edge. Start from the center and move towards the sides, making sure to create a straight line.

Step 6: Style the hair

Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to the top of the hair and style it as desired. Use a comb to create a natural and clean look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a medium fade military haircut:

  • Use a guard that is appropriate for your hair length.
  • Take your time and work slowly to ensure an even and smooth cut.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair and create a natural look.
  • Trim the hairline carefully to create a defined edge.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to keep the hair in place.

FAQ

What is the difference between a medium fade and a high fade?

A medium fade features a gradual decrease in hair length from the top of the head to the sides and back, while a high fade starts above the temples and creates a dramatic transition to the top of the head.

How often do I need to get a medium fade military haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the haircut. On average, you should get a medium fade military haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking clean and stylish.

Can I do a medium fade military haircut at home?

Yes, you can do a medium fade military haircut at home with the right tools and techniques. However, it is always recommended to visit a professional barber for the best results.

Hair Care

To maintain a medium fade military haircut, it is important to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. You can also apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun and other environmental factors.
