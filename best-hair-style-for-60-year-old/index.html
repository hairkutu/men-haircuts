Best Hair Style For 60 Year Old A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Christine May 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and drier, making it challenging to find a hairstyle that looks great and flatters our features. However, there are plenty of stylish and age-appropriate hairstyles that can enhance your look and make you feel confident and fabulous at 60. In this guide, we will share with you some of the best hair styles for 60 year old women that are easy to maintain and will make you look and feel your best.

Description

Whether you prefer short or long hair, there are plenty of hairstyles that can work for you at 60. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A flattering hairstyle can take years off your look and enhance your natural beauty. In this guide, we will show you some of the most popular hair styles for 60 year old women that are easy to style and maintain.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for 60 Year Old

When choosing a hairstyle at 60, there are several factors to consider, including your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for your age:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. If you have a round face, consider a layered haircut that adds volume and dimension to your hair. If you have a square face, go for a soft and wavy hairstyle that softens your jawline. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle.

2. Choose a Low-Maintenance Style

As you age, your hair tends to become drier and thinner, making it more challenging to style. Choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and maintain. A short bob or pixie cut is an excellent choice for women who prefer a wash-and-go style.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that matches your lifestyle. If you are active and on-the-go, a short and sleek haircut is an excellent choice. If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, consider a long and layered hairstyle that you can style in different ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Best Hair Style for 60 Year Old

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a flattering hairstyle at 60:

1. Short Bob

A short bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that is perfect for women over 60. It is easy to maintain, and it can be styled in different ways to suit your mood and occasion. Here's how to achieve a short bob:

Step 1: Wash and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add texture and volume.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek and polished look.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that is perfect for women who want to make a statement. It is low-maintenance and easy to style, making it an excellent choice for busy women over 60. Here's how to achieve a pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling gel to your hair to add texture and hold.

Step 3: Use a comb to create a deep side part.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to style your hair and create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair style and keep your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for aging hair. A mild shampoo will cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils, leaving it soft and hydrated.

2. Condition Your Hair Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and shiny. Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and prevent dryness and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for 60 year old women with thin hair?

A short bob or pixie cut is an excellent choice for women with thin hair. These styles add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Can women over 60 wear long hair?

Yes, women over 60 can wear long hair, but it should be styled in a way that flatters their features and complements their lifestyle. A long and layered hairstyle is an excellent choice for women who prefer a relaxed and casual look.

What is the best hair color for women over 60?

For women over 60, it is best to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Shades of blonde, brown, and red are excellent choices, as they add warmth and dimension to your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair style for 60 year old can be challenging, but with the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age. Whether you prefer a short bob, pixie cut, or long and layered hairstyle, there are plenty of options to choose from. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and don't forget to maintain your hair with regular conditioning and styling.
