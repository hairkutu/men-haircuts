Medium Length Haircuts With Bangs For Long Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that flatters your long face shape? Look no further than medium length haircuts with bangs! This versatile style is perfect for those with longer faces, as it helps to balance out the proportions of your face. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting and maintaining medium length haircuts with bangs for long faces.

Description

Medium length haircuts with bangs are a popular choice among those with longer faces. This style typically falls between the chin and shoulders and features bangs that frame the face. It helps to add volume and balance to the face, making it a great choice for those with longer face shapes.

How to Get Medium Length Haircuts with Bangs for Long Faces

If you're interested in getting a medium length haircut with bangs for your long face, the first step is to find a reputable hairstylist who has experience with this style. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can discuss your options and preferences.

During your consultation, your hairstylist will consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle to help determine the best type of medium length haircut with bangs for you. They may suggest a layered cut, face-framing layers, or blunt bangs, depending on your individual needs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've decided on the perfect medium length haircut with bangs, here's what you can expect during your appointment:

  1. Your hairstylist will start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. They will then section your hair and begin cutting, taking care to create the desired length and shape.
  3. Your stylist will then add layers or bangs as needed to create the desired look.
  4. Once the cut is complete, your hairstylist will style your hair using heat tools or styling products as needed.
  5. Finally, they will give you tips on how to maintain your new haircut and style it at home.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Haircuts with Bangs

To keep your medium length haircut with bangs looking great, try these tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them from getting too long.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Can medium length haircuts with bangs work for all hair types?

Yes! Medium length haircuts with bangs can be customized to work with any hair type, whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair.

How often should I get my medium length haircut with bangs trimmed?

It's best to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

What face shapes work best with medium length haircuts with bangs?

Medium length haircuts with bangs work particularly well for those with longer face shapes, but they can be customized to flatter any face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length haircut with bangs looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to love your new medium length haircut with bangs for your long face shape.
