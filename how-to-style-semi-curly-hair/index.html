How To Style Semi Curly Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine Jun 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Semi curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it has natural texture and volume that can be difficult to achieve with straight hair. On the other hand, it can be hard to tame and style, especially if you don't know how to work with your natural texture. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and hair care advice on how to style semi curly hair.

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs

How to Style Semi Curly Hair

The first step to styling semi curly hair is to work with your natural texture. This means embracing your curls and waves, rather than trying to straighten them out. Here are some tips on how to style semi curly hair:

  • Start with clean, conditioned hair. Semi curly hair tends to be dry, so make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a styling product to damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a curl cream, mousse, or gel, depending on your hair type and texture.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help dry your hair without disrupting your curls. Hold the diffuser close to your head and scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your curls. Don't brush your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Finish with a lightweight hairspray or serum to add shine and hold.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still not sure how to style semi curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of curl cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Flip your head upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your head and scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your curls.
  5. Apply a pea-sized amount of lightweight serum or oil to your hair to add shine.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling semi curly hair:

  • Don't wash your hair too often. Semi curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it every day can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • If you want to straighten your hair, use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with ceramic plates.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling semi curly hair:

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on semi curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener on semi curly hair, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with ceramic plates. Straightening your hair too often can damage it and cause frizz.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in semi curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in semi curly hair, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a styling product to damp hair, and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Q: How can I add volume to semi curly hair?

A: To add volume to semi curly hair, flip your head upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it to create natural volume and definition.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some additional tips for taking care of semi curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, styling semi curly hair doesn't have to be difficult. With the right products, tools, and techniques, you can enhance your natural texture and create beautiful, defined curls. Remember to take care of your hair by using moisturizing products, avoiding heat styling tools, and trimming it regularly. Happy styling!
