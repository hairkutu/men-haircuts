If you have a square face shape, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that flatters your features. Luckily, there are plenty of hairstyles that can enhance your strong jawline and angular face shape. Description

Square faces have a strong, angular jawline, and the width of the forehead and jawline are roughly equal. The goal of a good haircut for a square face is to soften the sharp angles and create balance. Look for hairstyles that add volume and movement around the temples and cheekbones, while avoiding styles that emphasize the jawline.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a square face, consider the length, layers, and texture. Long layers can help soften the face and create movement, while shorter layers can add volume around the crown. Textured cuts, such as shaggy or choppy styles, can also help break up the angular lines of a square face.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

Step 2: Choose a style that adds volume and movement around the temples and cheekbones.

Step 3: Consider adding layers or texture to soften the sharp angles of your face.

Step 4: Ask your stylist to create a cut that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a haircut for a square face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the jawline.
  • Consider side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.
  • Try a middle part to elongate the face.
  • Experiment with curls or waves to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Q: What hairstyles should square-faced men avoid?
A: Men with square faces should avoid buzz cuts or shaved heads, as they can emphasize the angular jawline. Instead, opt for styles with texture or length on top, such as a messy fringe or layered cut.

Q: Can a bob flatter a square face?
A: Yes, a bob can flatter a square face if it is cut with layers and texture to soften the sharp angles. Consider a chin-length bob with wispy ends or a layered bob with side-swept bangs.

Hair Care

Regardless of your haircut, itâ€™s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.
