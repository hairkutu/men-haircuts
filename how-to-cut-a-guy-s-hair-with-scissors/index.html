How To Cut A Guy s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying for haircuts? Or maybe you just want to try something new and cut your partner's hair yourself. Whatever your reason may be, cutting a guy's hair with scissors is easier than you might think. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step and provide tips to ensure that your guy's haircut turns out great.

How to Cut a Guy's Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers if you plan to use them.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel dry it. It's important to start with clean hair so that you can see the natural texture and shape of the hair.

Step 3: Section Off the Hair

Using the comb, section off the hair into four equal parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start with the top section and comb the hair straight up. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid making any mistakes.

After you finish the top, move on to the sides and back, cutting the hair to your desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle if you want a layered look.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you finish cutting, use the clippers to trim any hair around the neckline and ears. Then, use the comb to check for any uneven spots and trim them with the scissors.

Tips for Cutting a Guy's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle if you want a layered look.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's better to take it slow and make small adjustments as you go.
  • Use the clippers to trim any hair around the neckline and ears.
  • Check for any uneven spots and trim them with the scissors.

FAQs

Can I cut my guy's hair with regular household scissors?

It's best to use hair cutting scissors because they are sharper and designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors may result in uneven cuts or split ends.

How often should I cut my guy's hair?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. Generally, hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the cut.

Hair Care

To keep your guy's hair looking great, be sure to wash and condition it regularly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for his hair type. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized.

Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage the hair. If he must use a blow dryer or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Conclusion

With the right tools and technique, cutting a guy's hair with scissors is easy and cost-effective. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve a great haircut every time.
