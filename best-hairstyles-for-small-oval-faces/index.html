Best Hairstyles For Small Oval Faces How To Choose The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a small oval face, you’re in luck! This face shape is versatile and works well with many different hairstyles. However, with so many options, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect look. In this article, we’ll go over the best hairstyles for small oval faces and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Small Oval Faces

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for a small oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work better with thick, straight hair, while others work better with fine, curly hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. Are you looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle or something that requires more upkeep?
  • Consider your personal style. Do you prefer a classic, elegant look or something more edgy and modern?

By taking these factors into account, you’ll be able to narrow down your options and choose the perfect hairstyle for your small oval face.

Bob Hairstyles for Small Oval Faces

Bob hairstyles are a classic look that work well with small oval faces. Here are a few options:

  • The blunt bob: This look features a straight cut that falls just above the shoulders. It works well with thick, straight hair.
  • The asymmetrical bob: This look features a longer length on one side and a shorter length on the other. It works well with fine, curly hair.
  • The layered bob: This look features layers throughout the hair, which adds volume and movement. It works well with any hair texture.

Pixie Cuts for Small Oval Faces

Pixie cuts are a bold and edgy look that work well with small oval faces. Here are a few options:

  • The classic pixie: This look features short, cropped hair that is styled with a bit of texture. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The layered pixie: This look features layers throughout the hair, which adds volume and movement. It works well with fine, straight hair.
  • The undercut pixie: This look features shaved sides and longer hair on top. It works well with thick, straight hair.

Bangs for Small Oval Faces

Bangs are a versatile look that work well with small oval faces. Here are a few options:

  • The blunt bangs: This look features straight-cut bangs that fall just above the eyebrows. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The side-swept bangs: This look features bangs that are swept to one side. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The curtain bangs: This look features longer bangs that are parted down the middle. It works well with fine, wavy hair.

Updos for Small Oval Faces

Updos are a classic and elegant look that work well with small oval faces. Here are a few options:

  • The low bun: This look features a bun that is styled at the nape of the neck. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The braided updo: This look features braids that are styled into an updo. It works well with thick, curly hair.
  • The twisted updo: This look features twists that are styled into an updo. It works well with fine, straight hair.

Long Hairstyles for Small Oval Faces

Long hairstyles are a classic look that work well with small oval faces. Here are a few options:

  • The beachy waves: This look features loose, tousled waves that are styled with texture. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The layered cut: This look features layers throughout the hair, which adds volume and movement. It works well with any hair texture.
  • The half-up, half-down style: This look features half of the hair pulled back into a ponytail or bun. It works well with any hair texture.

Hair Care Tips for Small Oval Faces

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQs

What is a small oval face?

A small oval face is a face shape that is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and high cheekbones.

What hairstyles work best with small oval faces?

Hairstyles that work well with small oval faces include bob hairstyles, pixie cuts, bangs, updos, and long hairstyles.

How do I choose the best hairstyle for my small oval face?

When choosing a hairstyle for a small oval face, consider your hair texture and thickness, your lifestyle, and your personal style.

How can I care for my hair?

To care for your hair, use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, avoid using heat tools too often, get regular trims, and use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week.
