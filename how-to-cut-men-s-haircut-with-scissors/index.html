How To Cut Men s Haircut With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a simple haircut? Or maybe you're just looking to learn a new skill? Whatever the reason, cutting men's hair with scissors is easier than you might think. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step, from choosing the right tools to finishing touches.

How to Cut Men's Haircut with Scissors

Tools You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Spray bottle (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb through it to remove any tangles or knots. If the hair is particularly thick or long, you may want to use clippers to trim it down to a more manageable length.

Step 2: Choose Your Cut

Decide what kind of cut you want to give. You can choose from a variety of styles, from a simple buzz cut to a more complex layered cut. Look up pictures of the style you want to get an idea of what it should look like.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections, using clips or hair ties to keep them separate. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair in the bottom section. Use your comb to guide the scissors and make sure you're cutting evenly. Always cut small sections at a time, and don't be afraid to go slow.

Step 5: Move on to the Next Section

Once you've finished the bottom section, move on to the next section and repeat the process. Make sure the hair is still damp and use your comb to keep it in place.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Every few sections, stop and check the hair for evenness. Comb it straight up and look for any uneven spots. If you find any, go back and trim them down.

Step 7: Finish the Cut

Once you've finished all the sections, it's time to do any finishing touches. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs and make sure the cut is even all around.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

  • Always cut small sections at a time.
  • Go slow and be patient.
  • Use your comb to guide the scissors.
  • Check for evenness every few sections.
  • Don't cut too much off at once.

FAQ

Can I cut men's hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically use regular scissors, it's not recommended. Haircutting scissors are designed specifically for cutting hair and will give you a better result.

How often should I cut men's hair?

It depends on the style and how fast the hair grows. Generally, hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting men's hair, it's important to care for it properly. Make sure to shampoo and condition the hair regularly, and use a leave-in conditioner if necessary. Avoid using too much heat on the hair, and use a heat protectant if you do use heat styling tools. Finally, get regular trims to keep the hair looking its best.

Cutting men's hair with scissors is a skill that anyone can learn. With the right tools and a little patience, you can give a great haircut at home. Just remember to take it slow and go easy on the scissors!
