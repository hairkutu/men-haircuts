How To Make Your Straight Hair Naturally Curly Permanently

Written by William Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls to your life, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over how to make your straight hair naturally curly permanently. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair for the process to how to care for your newly curly hair. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. Preparing Your Hair
  2. Getting a Perm
  3. Styling Your New Curls
  4. Caring for Your Curly Hair
  5. FAQs

Preparing Your Hair

Before you get a perm to make your hair curly, it's important to prepare your hair. First, stop using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers. These tools can damage your hair and make it harder for the perm to take hold. Second, start using a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Finally, make sure your hair is in good overall condition before getting a perm. If your hair is damaged or over-processed, it may not be able to handle the chemicals in the perm solution.

Getting a Perm

Once your hair is prepared, it's time to get a perm. A perm is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly. There are two types of perms: alkaline and acid. Alkaline perms are stronger and create tighter curls, while acid perms are gentler and create looser curls. Talk to your stylist about which type of perm is best for your hair.

The perm process can take a few hours, so be prepared to spend some time at the salon. Your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, then wrap your hair around rods to create the curls. After a set amount of time, the rods will be removed and your hair will be rinsed and neutralized to stop the perming process.

Styling Your New Curls

Once your hair is curly, it's time to start styling it. There are a variety of ways to style curly hair, from wearing it down to putting it up in a bun or ponytail. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You may also want to invest in some new hair products specifically designed for curly hair, like curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, styling cream, and hair spray.

Caring for Your Curly Hair

Curly hair requires different care than straight hair. First, avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy. Instead, wash your hair every other day or every few days. Second, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Third, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Finally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I make my straight hair curly without getting a perm?

Yes, there are a variety of ways to add curls to straight hair, like using a curling iron or hot rollers. However, these methods are temporary and will require daily styling to maintain the curls.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical process of the perm.

We hope this article has helped you learn how to make your straight hair naturally curly permanently. With a little preparation and the right care, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls every day!
