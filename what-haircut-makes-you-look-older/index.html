What Haircut Makes You Look Older

Written by Oliver Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair changes just like the rest of our body. We start to notice gray hairs, thinning hair, and changes in texture. However, did you know that the wrong haircut can actually make you look older than you are? In this blog post, we will discuss what haircut makes you look older and how you can avoid it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to haircuts that make you look older, there are certain styles that you should avoid. These include:

Bowl Cut

The bowl cut is a hairstyle where the hair is cut straight around the head, creating a bowl-like shape. This style can make you look like a child or even older than you actually are.

Short, Blunt Bangs

Short, blunt bangs can be harsh and create a severe look. They can also make your face appear shorter and wider, which can add years to your appearance.

Choppy Layers

Choppy layers can be trendy and edgy, but they can also make you look older. The uneven layers can create a messy look that is not flattering for all face shapes and hair textures.

How to

If you want to avoid a haircut that makes you look older, consider these tips:

Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist can help you determine what hairstyle will work best for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They can also help you avoid haircuts that will make you look older.

Consider Your Face Shape

Not all haircuts are flattering for all face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.

Choose a Classic Style

Classic hairstyles are timeless and can be flattering for all ages. Consider a bob, a pixie cut, or long layers for a more classic look.

Step by Step

If you are unsure of what haircut will work best for you, follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Determine which of the following face shapes you have:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Look online or in magazines for hairstyles that are flattering for your face shape. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Bring pictures of hairstyles that you like to your stylist. They can help you determine if the hairstyle will work for you and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance if you have a busy lifestyle.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also be open to your stylist's suggestions.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut?

The most flattering haircut depends on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A classic bob or long layers can be flattering for many people.

Can a bad haircut make you look older?

Yes, a bad haircut can make you look older. Hairstyles that are too short, too choppy, or too severe can add years to your appearance.

Hair Care

Regardless of your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and youthful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know what haircut makes you look older, use these tips to choose a hairstyle that is flattering and youthful. Remember to consult with your stylist and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
