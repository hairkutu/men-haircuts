Short Hair For Oblong Face 2021 How To Choose The Best Style

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to choose the best short hair for oblong face 2021, including how to style it, tips, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Description: What is an Oblong Face Shape?

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's first understand what an oblong face shape is. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a straight jawline and forehead. This face shape has a length that is more than its width, and it is often compared to an oval shape.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair for Oblong Face 2021?

Choosing the best short hair for an oblong face shape can be tricky, but the key is to create volume and width to balance out the length of your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Opt for Layers

Layered haircuts are perfect for oblong face shapes as they add volume and body to your hair. You can opt for short layers or long layers, depending on your preference, but make sure to ask your stylist to create layers that frame your face and add width.

2. Consider Bangs

Bangs are another great way to balance out an oblong face shape. Opt for thick, side-swept bangs that hit at or above your eyebrows. This will create the illusion of a shorter forehead and add width to your face.

3. Try a Bob

A bob is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on oblong face shapes. Choose a chin-length bob with layers or soft waves to add volume and width to your hair. You can also try an asymmetrical bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, as this will help to balance out your face shape.

4. Experiment with Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy hairstyle. If you have an oblong face shape, opt for a textured pixie cut with layers and volume on top. This will create the illusion of a shorter face and add width to your hair.

Step by Step: How to Style Short Hair for Oblong Face 2021

Now that you know which short hairstyles work best for oblong face shapes, let's talk about how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair to create volume and body. Focus on lifting the roots and creating volume at the crown of your head.

3. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired. You can add curls or waves for a more textured look, or keep it straight for a sleek finish.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Oblong Face 2021

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy, make sure to trim it every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are specifically designed for short hair to keep it looking its best. Look for products that add volume, texture, and shine to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair if not used properly. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoid using them on wet hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions about Short Hair for Oblong Face 2021

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oblong face shape. However, make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to balance out the length of your face.

2. Can I wear a pixie cut if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have an oblong face shape. Opt for a textured pixie cut with volume on top to add width to your hair.

3. What hair color looks best on oblong face shapes?

Warm hair colors such as caramel, honey, and golden blonde look best on oblong face shapes as they add warmth and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts on Short Hair for Oblong Face 2021

Choosing the best short hair for an oblong face shape can be challenging, but with the right hairstyle and styling tips, you can create a flattering look that accentuates your features. Remember to opt for layered haircuts, consider bangs, try a bob, and experiment with pixie cuts. Also, make sure to maintain your short hair with regular trims, use the right hair products, and protect your hair from heat damage. With these tips, you'll have the perfect short hair for oblong face 2021 in no time!
