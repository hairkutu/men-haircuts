How To Stop Hair Loss From Thyroid Medication

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Contents:

  • How to Stop Hair Loss from Thyroid Medication
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Thyroid medication is a common treatment for an underactive thyroid gland or hypothyroidism. While it can be effective in regulating thyroid hormone levels, one common side effect is hair loss. This can be distressing for many people, but there are steps you can take to stop or minimize hair loss caused by thyroid medication.

How to Stop Hair Loss from Thyroid Medication

The following steps can help stop hair loss from thyroid medication:

Step 1: Speak to your doctor

If you are experiencing hair loss from thyroid medication, it is important to speak to your doctor. They may be able to adjust your dosage or prescribe a different medication that has fewer side effects.

Step 2: Ensure proper nutrition

A balanced diet with sufficient vitamins and minerals is important for healthy hair growth. Make sure your diet includes protein, iron, zinc, and vitamins A, C, and D. You can also consider taking supplements if your diet is lacking in any of these nutrients.

Step 3: Reduce stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss, so it is important to find ways to manage stress. This can include exercise, meditation, or relaxing activities like reading or listening to music.

Step 4: Use gentle hair care products

Harsh chemicals and styling products can damage hair and contribute to hair loss. Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help stop hair loss from thyroid medication:

  • Be patient – it may take several months for hair to grow back.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more fragile and prone to breakage.
  • Consider using a hair growth serum or oil to stimulate hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Is hair loss from thyroid medication permanent?

A: No, hair loss from thyroid medication is usually temporary and will stop once medication is adjusted or discontinued.

Q: Can changing my diet help stop hair loss from thyroid medication?

A: Yes, a balanced diet with sufficient vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care

While hair loss from thyroid medication can be distressing, there are steps you can take to stop or minimize it. Speak to your doctor, ensure proper nutrition, reduce stress, and use gentle hair care products. Be patient, and consider using a hair growth serum or oil to stimulate hair growth. With time and care, your hair can return to its healthy state.
