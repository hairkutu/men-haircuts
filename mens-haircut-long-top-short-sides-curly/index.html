Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly A Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men, then the long top short sides curly style might be just what you need. This style has been popular for quite some time and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for maintenance, and more. So, let's dive in!

Description

The mens haircut long top short sides curly is a versatile style that works well for those with curly or wavy hair. The hair on top is left long, while the sides and back are kept short. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it usually falls somewhere between 3 and 6 inches.

This style is popular among men of all ages and can be modified to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance but stylish look.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly

The mens haircut long top short sides curly is a relatively easy style to get. Here's how:

  1. Find a reputable barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want or describe it in detail.
  3. They will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, usually with clippers.
  4. The hair on top will be left longer and can be cut with scissors or a razor to create texture.
  5. The stylist will then add layers to the top to create volume and movement.
  6. Finally, they will style your hair using a product of your choice.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircut long top short sides curly:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product to your damp hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on low heat to help enhance your curls.
  4. Section your hair into three parts: the top, the back, and the sides.
  5. Starting with the sides, use clippers to cut your hair short, leaving about 1/4 inch of hair.
  6. Move to the back of your head and repeat the process, making sure to blend the sides and back together.
  7. Now it's time to work on the top. Using scissors or a razor, cut your hair to the desired length, leaving it longer on top than on the sides.
  8. Add layers to the top to create volume and movement.
  9. Style your hair using a product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircut long top short sides curly:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back short.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls defined and hydrated.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a styling product to your hair when it's damp to help define your curls and add texture.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause frizz.

FAQs About the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly

Q. Will this style suit me if I have straight hair?

A. This style is best suited for those with curly or wavy hair. However, it can be modified to suit straight hair by adding texture and volume.

Q. Can I style my hair differently with this cut?

A. Yes, this style is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can use a pomade for a slicked-back look or a texturizing product for a messy, tousled look.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean without stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Tips for the Mens Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens haircut long top short sides curly looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

With the mens haircut long top short sides curly, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that is easy to maintain. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your curls looking their best for years to come.
