How To Pin A Low Cut Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Low cut tops can be tricky to wear, especially if you want to maintain a modest look. Fortunately, there is a simple solution to this problem – pinning the top. In this article, we will guide you on how to pin a low cut top to avoid any wardrobe malfunctions. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pin a Low Cut Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Pinning a Low Cut Top
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips When Wearing a Low Cut Top

How to Pin a Low Cut Top

Pinning a low cut top is a simple process that requires a few materials. You will need a safety pin or a fashion tape and a low cut top. Both these items can be found in fashion stores or online shops. You can also use a brooch or a scarf to pin the top if you don't have a safety pin or fashion tape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to pin a low cut top:

  1. Wear the low cut top and look in the mirror to determine the area that needs to be pinned.
  2. Place the safety pin or fashion tape on the inside of the top where you want to pin it.
  3. Hold the top with one hand while you pin it with the other hand. Be careful not to prick yourself with the safety pin.
  4. Pin the top in a way that it doesn't show on the outside. You can also use a brooch or a scarf to cover the safety pin or fashion tape.
  5. Adjust the pin to your desired level of coverage. You can make the pin tighter or looser depending on your preference.
  6. Double-check the pin to ensure it is secure and doesn't come off easily. You don't want to have a wardrobe malfunction in public.
  7. Enjoy your low cut top with confidence, knowing that you are well-covered.

Tips for Pinning a Low Cut Top

Here are some tips to keep in mind when pinning a low cut top:

  • Choose a safety pin or fashion tape that matches the color of your top to avoid attracting attention to the pin.
  • Make sure the pin or tape is strong enough to hold the top in place without causing any damage to the fabric.
  • Test the pin or tape on a small area of the fabric before pinning the entire top to avoid any mishaps.
  • Pin the top in a way that it doesn't show on the outside to maintain a clean and polished look.
  • Wear a camisole or a tank top under the low cut top to provide an extra layer of coverage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about pinning a low cut top:

Can I use a regular safety pin to pin my low cut top?

Yes, you can use a regular safety pin to pin your low cut top. However, make sure the pin is strong enough to hold the top in place without causing any damage to the fabric. You can also use a fashion tape or a brooch as an alternative.

Can I wear a low cut top to work?

It depends on your workplace's dress code. If your workplace allows low cut tops, make sure to wear a camisole or a tank top underneath to avoid any wardrobe malfunctions.

Hair Care Tips When Wearing a Low Cut Top

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing a low cut top:

  • Wear your hair up in a bun or a ponytail to avoid hair getting in the way of the top.
  • Use hair gel or hairspray to keep stray hairs in place.
  • Avoid wearing heavy earrings that can get tangled in your hair or the top.

Now that you know how to pin a low cut top, you can confidently wear your favorite low cut tops without worrying about any wardrobe malfunctions. Remember to choose a safety pin or fashion tape that matches the color of your top and test it on a small area before pinning the entire top. Happy pinning!
