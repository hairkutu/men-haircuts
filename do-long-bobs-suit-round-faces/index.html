Do Long Bobs Suit Round Faces Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face and you're thinking of getting a haircut, you might be wondering if long bobs are the right choice for you. Long bobs, or "lobs," have been a popular haircut for many years now, and for good reason. They're versatile, easy to style, and they flatter many face shapes. But do long bobs suit round faces? In this article, we'll explore this question and give you tips and tricks for the perfect look.

  • Step by Step: Cutting Your Long Bob

Description

A long bob is a haircut that falls somewhere between a bob and shoulder-length hair. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from straight and sleek to wavy and tousled. Long bobs are popular because they flatter many face shapes and hair types. But do they suit round faces?

The answer is yes! Long bobs can look great on round faces, as long as they're cut and styled properly. The key is to create a haircut that elongates the face and draws attention to the cheekbones and jawline.

How To: Get the Perfect Long Bob for Round Faces

Getting the perfect long bob for a round face involves a few key steps:

  1. Find a style that suits your face shape. Look for long bobs with layers and angles that create movement and elongate the face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as these can make the face appear rounder.
  2. Consult with your stylist. Your stylist can help you choose a cut that suits your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style your long bob at home.
  3. Consider adding bangs. Bangs can help balance a round face and draw attention to the eyes. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can be a great option for long bobs.

Step by Step: Cutting Your Long Bob

If you're planning to cut your long bob at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Starting at the back, use a razor or scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating layers that frame the face.
  4. Move to the front sections and repeat the process, cutting the hair at an angle so that it frames the face.
  5. Finish by trimming any uneven ends and adjusting the length as needed.

Tips for Styling Your Long Bob

Once you have your long bob, there are many ways to style it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • Try a side part or a deep side part to create asymmetry and elongate the face.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and movement to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your look.

FAQ: Common Questions About Long Bobs and Round Faces

Here are answers to some common questions about long bobs and round faces:

Q: Can I wear a long bob if I have a double chin?

A: Yes! A long bob can actually help draw attention away from a double chin and highlight the jawline.

Q: Can I wear a long bob if I have thick hair?

A: Absolutely! In fact, long bobs can look great on thick hair because they help to lighten the weight of the hair and make it more manageable.

Q: How often should I get my long bob trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your long bob looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Long Bob Healthy and Beautiful

To keep your long bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a long bob no matter what your face shape is. Whether you're looking for a sleek and sophisticated look or a relaxed and tousled style, a long bob can be a great choice. So go ahead and book that haircut – you won't regret it!
