How To Cut Men s Hair Fade Long On Top A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that's both stylish and low maintenance, the long on top fade is a great option for men. This hairstyle features longer hair on the top of the head that gradually fades into shorter hair on the sides and back. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut men's hair fade long on top.

How to Cut Men's Hair Fade Long on Top

The long on top fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. You can choose to keep the top hair long and messy, slicked back, or even styled into a pompadour. The key to a great fade is to make sure that the transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides is seamless.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair fade long on top:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section the hair. Use hair clippers to create a line around the head where the fade will begin. This line should be about an inch above the ears and should follow the natural curve of the head.
  3. Choose a guard size. Select a guard size that will give you the desired length on the sides of the head. A #2 guard is a good starting point for most fades.
  4. Begin cutting. Use the hair clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using smooth, even strokes.
  5. Blend the hair. Once you've reached the line you created in step 2, switch to a shorter guard size (such as a #1 or #0.5) and use the clippers to blend the hair between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.
  6. Cut the top. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Be sure to leave enough length to style the hair.
  7. Style the hair. Use a comb and hair product to style the hair on top of the head. You can choose to slick it back, create a messy look, or even style it into a pompadour.
  8. Trim the edges. Use scissors or clippers to clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline.
  9. Final touches. Check the haircut for any uneven spots or missed areas. Make any necessary adjustments for a clean, polished look.
  10. Clean up. Sweep up any hair clippings and wipe down the clippers and scissors.

Tips for a Perfect Fade

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a perfect long on top fade:

  • Choose the right guard size for your hair type and the desired length.
  • Use smooth, even strokes when cutting the hair.
  • Blend the hair gradually to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.
  • Leave enough length on top to style the hair.
  • Use a comb and hair product to style the hair on top of the head.
  • Check the haircut for any uneven spots or missed areas and make necessary adjustments.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a long on top fade:

What hair types work best for a long on top fade?

The long on top fade can work well for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to style for those with very thick or coarse hair.

How often should I get my hair cut for a long on top fade?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the shape of the fade.

Can I cut a long on top fade at home?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can cut a long on top fade at home. However, if you're unsure or inexperienced, it's best to leave it to a professional barber.

Hair Care for Men's Fades

Proper hair care is important to maintain the look of a long on top fade. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition the hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair.
  • Apply hair product sparingly to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.
  • Protect the hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Visit a barber regularly to maintain the shape of the fade.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve a stylish long on top fade haircut that's perfect for any occasion. Remember to take your time and be patient, as practice makes perfect!
