Can You Do Faux Locs With Kanekalon Hair

Written by Daniel Mar 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking about getting faux locs but aren't sure if you can use Kanekalon hair? Well, the good news is that you can! Kanekalon hair is a popular choice for faux locs because it is affordable, easy to find, and versatile. In this article, we will discuss how to do faux locs with Kanekalon hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Description

Faux locs are a protective hairstyle that imitates the look of real locs. This hairstyle is achieved by wrapping synthetic hair around your natural hair, creating a dreadlock-like style. Faux locs can be done with various types of synthetic hair, including Kanekalon hair.

How to Do Faux Locs With Kanekalon Hair

Here are the steps to follow to do faux locs with Kanekalon hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before installing the faux locs, make sure your hair is washed, moisturized, and detangled. You can also stretch your hair using the African threading method or blow-drying it to make it more manageable.

Step 2: Choose Your Kanekalon Hair

Choose the color and length of Kanekalon hair you want for your faux locs. Make sure the hair is high-quality and tangle-free.

Step 3: Install the Faux Locs

Divide your hair into small sections and braid each section to the desired length. Take a piece of Kanekalon hair and wrap it around the braided section, securing it with a crochet needle. Continue wrapping the Kanekalon hair around the braid until it forms a dreadlock-like style. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are done.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your faux locs with Kanekalon hair look and feel great:

  • Use a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your faux locs while sleeping.
  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products that can weigh down your faux locs.
  • Trim any frizzy or loose ends to keep your faux locs neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing faux locs with Kanekalon hair:

Can I wash my faux locs with Kanekalon hair?

Yes, you can wash your faux locs with Kanekalon hair. However, be gentle when washing and avoid using hot water or harsh shampoos that can damage the hair.

How long do faux locs with Kanekalon hair last?

Faux locs with Kanekalon hair can last up to 8 weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Can I swim with faux locs with Kanekalon hair?

Yes, you can swim with faux locs with Kanekalon hair. However, make sure to wear a swimming cap and rinse your hair thoroughly with clean water after swimming to remove any chlorine or saltwater.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy faux locs with Kanekalon hair. Here are some tips to care for your faux locs:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly using a light oil or moisturizer.
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent tangles and frizz.
  • Trim any loose or frizzy ends to keep your faux locs neat and tidy.
  • Visit a professional stylist for maintenance and retouching every 4-6 weeks.

So, there you have it! You can do faux locs with Kanekalon hair and achieve a beautiful, protective style. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care instructions to keep your faux locs looking great for weeks to come.
