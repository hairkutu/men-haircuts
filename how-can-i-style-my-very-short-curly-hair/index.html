How To Style Very Short Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have very short curly hair, you might be wondering how to style it. Don’t worry, there are many ways you can style your hair to make it look amazing. In this article, we’ll show you how to style your very short curly hair.

Table of Content

  • How to Style Very Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Very Short Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Very Short Curly Hair

How to Style Very Short Curly Hair

When it comes to styling very short curly hair, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. The first thing is to make sure your hair is clean and well-moisturized. This will help to keep your curls defined and prevent frizz.

The second thing you need to keep in mind is that you don’t want to use too much product. Very short hair can quickly become weighed down and greasy if you use too much product. A small amount of product goes a long way with short hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps you can follow to style your very short curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair. This will help to keep your curls defined and prevent frizz.
  2. Apply a small amount of curl defining cream or gel to your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls. You can also use a small amount of pomade to define and shape your curls.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Very Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your very short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply product to your hair in small sections, working from the roots to the ends.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling very short curly hair:

  • Can I straighten my very short curly hair?
    Yes, you can straighten your very short curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant and don’t use high heat.
  • How often should I wash my very short curly hair?
    It depends on your hair type, but most people with very short curly hair can wash their hair every 2-3 days.
  • How can I prevent frizz in my very short curly hair?
    To prevent frizz, make sure your hair is well-moisturized and avoid touching it too much throughout the day.

Hair Care for Very Short Curly Hair

To keep your very short curly hair looking great, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to style your very short curly hair, you can experiment with different looks and find what works best for you. Remember to keep your hair well-moisturized and use a small amount of product to avoid weighing your hair down. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create amazing looks with your very short curly hair!
