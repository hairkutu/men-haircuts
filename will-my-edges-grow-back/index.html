Will My Edges Grow Back How To Regain Your Hairline

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling with hair loss around your hairline? Losing your edges can be a frustrating experience, but the good news is that it is possible to regrow your hairline. In this blog post, we will discuss how to regrow your edges and answer some frequently asked questions about hair growth.

How to Regrow Your Edges

Regrowing your edges will require patience, consistency, and the right hair care routine. Here are some steps you can take to encourage hair growth around your hairline:

Step 1: Identify the Cause of Hair Loss

The first step in regrowing your edges is to determine what is causing your hair loss. Common causes of hair loss around the hairline include:

  • Tight hairstyles (e.g. braids, weaves, and ponytails)
  • Chemical damage from relaxers or hair dyes
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Hormonal imbalances

Once you identify the cause of your hair loss, you can take steps to address it. For example, if tight hairstyles are the culprit, you can switch to looser styles that put less tension on your hairline.

Step 2: Practice Good Hair Care Habits

In addition to addressing the underlying cause of hair loss, you should also practice good hair care habits to encourage growth. This includes:

  • Gently massaging your scalp to increase blood flow
  • Using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoiding heat styling tools
  • Protecting your hair with a satin or silk scarf at night

Step 3: Use Hair Growth Products

There are many hair growth products on the market that can help stimulate hair growth. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and castor oil, which are known for their hair growth benefits. You can also try using essential oils like peppermint, rosemary, and lavender, which have been shown to promote hair growth.

Tips for Regrowing Your Edges

Here are some additional tips for regrowing your edges:

  • Avoid using tight hair accessories
  • Minimize your use of heat styling tools
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils

FAQ About Regrowing Your Edges

Q: How long does it take for edges to grow back?

A: Hair growth varies from person to person, but it typically takes around 3-6 months to see noticeable hair growth.

Q: Can heat damage cause hair loss around the hairline?

A: Yes, heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair, including hair loss around the hairline. To minimize damage, try to limit your use of heat styling tools or use a heat protectant spray.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss around the hairline?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss, including around the hairline. To reduce stress, try practicing relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.

Hair Care Routine for Regrowing Your Edges

Here is a sample hair care routine for regrowing your edges:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair
  • Apply hair growth products to your edges daily
  • Avoid tight hairstyles and hair accessories

Final Thoughts

Regrowing your edges will require patience and consistency, but with the right hair care routine and some TLC, you can regain your hairline. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid tight hairstyles, and use hair growth products to stimulate growth.
