How To Style Short Choppy Layers A Relaxed Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Short choppy layers are a popular hairstyle choice for those looking to add texture and volume to their hair. They can be styled in many different ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and polished. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style short choppy layers in a relaxed, easy-going way.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Short Choppy Layers
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Short Choppy Layers
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Short Choppy Layers

Description

Short choppy layers are typically cut to frame the face and add texture to the hair. They can be cut in a variety of lengths, from just below the chin to a pixie cut. Short choppy layers can be styled in many different ways, depending on your personal style and the occasion. Whether you prefer a messy, tousled look or a sleek, polished style, short choppy layers can be versatile and fun to style.

How to Style Short Choppy Layers

There are many different ways to style short choppy layers, but here are a few of our favorite relaxed styles:

  • Messy Waves - To achieve this look, apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Blow dry with a diffuser, then use a curling wand to add loose waves.
  • Half-Up Top Knot - Gather the top half of your hair into a messy bun, leaving the bottom half loose. Tease the crown of your hair for added volume.
  • Sleek and Straight - Apply a heat protectant to dry hair, then use a straightener to smooth out any kinks or frizz. Finish with a shine spray for a polished look.
  • Side Braid - Create a loose braid on one side of your head, leaving some pieces out to frame your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for achieving a relaxed, messy waves look with short choppy layers:

  1. Start with damp hair and apply a texturizing spray.
  2. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create loose waves.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment, using a low heat setting.
  4. Use a curling wand to add more defined waves if desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Choppy Layers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling short choppy layers:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality curling wand or straightener to achieve different styles.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works for you.
  • Use a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short choppy layers:

  • Q: Are short choppy layers easy to style?
  • A: Yes, short choppy layers can be easy to style once you find the right technique and products for your hair type.
  • Q: Can short choppy layers work for any hair type?
  • A: Yes, short choppy layers can work for many different hair types, from fine to thick and curly to straight.
  • Q: How often should I get my short choppy layers trimmed?
  • A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your layers.

Hair Care for Short Choppy Layers

To keep your short choppy layers looking their best, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your layers.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment every few weeks to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a relaxed, effortless look with short choppy layers. Whether you’re heading to work or out for a night on the town, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
