How To Get Thick Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having thin and lifeless short hair? Do you want to achieve a fuller and thicker look without having to resort to hair extensions? If so, you’ve come to the right place! This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to get thick short hair. We’ll also share some tips and tricks on how to maintain your new look and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Thick Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Thick Short Hair

How to Get Thick Short Hair

There are a few things you can do to achieve a fuller and thicker look for your short hair:

  • Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner: These products are specifically designed to add volume to your hair. Look for formulas with ingredients like keratin, biotin, and wheat protein.
  • Avoid heavy products: Heavy products like oils and waxes can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products like mousses and sprays.
  • Add layers: Layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, which can make it look fuller. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best layering technique for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims: Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look thinner.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get thick short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift your roots as you go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a round brush to smooth and shape your hair.
  5. Add layers to your hair, focusing on the crown and sides.
  6. Use a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect thick short hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it’s wet. This can help prevent breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush, like a boar bristle brush. This can help distribute your scalp’s natural oils throughout your hair, which can promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. These tools can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals. Your hair needs nutrients like biotin, iron, and zinc to grow strong and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get thick short hair if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can! The tips and tricks we’ve shared in this guide are designed to add volume and thickness to all hair types.

Do I need to use special products to get thick short hair?

While using volumizing products can help, they’re not absolutely necessary. You can still achieve a fuller and thicker look with the right styling techniques.

How long does it take to get thick short hair?

It depends on your hair type and the techniques you use. Some people may see results in just a few weeks, while others may take several months to achieve their desired look.

Hair Care for Thick Short Hair

Once you’ve achieved your desired look, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too oily.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect thick short hair in no time. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you’ll be rewarded with healthy and beautiful hair.
