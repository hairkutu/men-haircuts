How To Cut Side Swept Bangs For Boys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to give your little boy a new haircut, consider adding side swept bangs to their look. Not only do they add a touch of style, but they can also help keep hair out of their eyes. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut side swept bangs for boys.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs

  5. Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs for Boys

Before you begin cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also helpful to have a second pair of hands to help hold the hair in place.

Start by determining how long you want the bangs to be. The hair should be long enough to be swept to the side, but not too long that it covers the eyes. Once you have an idea of the length, begin by sectioning off the hair you want to cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wet the Hair

Use the spray bottle to wet the hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see any uneven areas.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Use the comb to section off the hair you want to cut. This should be a triangle-shaped section at the front of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut straight across. Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the new bangs into the rest of the hair. This will create a natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting side swept bangs:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place as you cut.
  • Blend the new bangs into the rest of the hair for a natural look.
  • Don't cut too much at once. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Consider taking your child to a professional stylist if you're unsure about cutting their hair yourself.

FAQs

Can I cut side swept bangs on curly hair?

Yes, but keep in mind that curly hair will appear shorter when it's dry. Start with a longer length than you think you need to avoid cutting too much off.

How often should I trim my child's side swept bangs?

It depends on how fast their hair grows, but generally every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

To maintain your child's side swept bangs, use a comb to style them to the side after washing. You can also use a small amount of styling product to help keep them in place.

Now that you know how to cut side swept bangs for boys, give it a try and see how it looks. Remember to start with small snips and blend the new bangs into the rest of the hair for a natural look. And if you're unsure, consider taking your child to a professional stylist for a trim.
