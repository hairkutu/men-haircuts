How To Style Skirts Plus Size

Written by Robby Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a plus size woman, you may have been told that skirts are not for you. But that is not true! Skirts can be a great addition to any plus size wardrobe. They are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. In this article, we will discuss how to style skirts plus size in a relaxed English language.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Skirts Plus Size
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Skirts Plus Size
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Skirts Plus Size

When it comes to styling skirts plus size, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a skirt that fits well. It should not be too tight or too loose. Second, you want to choose the right length. Midi skirts are a great option for plus size women as they hit at the calf, which is a flattering length for most body types. Third, you want to choose the right fabric. A heavier fabric will help to smooth out any lumps or bumps.

Choose the Right Top

When it comes to choosing a top to wear with your skirt, you want to choose something that complements your body shape. For example, if you have an apple-shaped body, you may want to choose a top that has a V-neckline to elongate your torso. If you have a pear-shaped body, you may want to choose a top that is fitted at the waist to balance out your proportions.

Accessorize

Accessories can really make an outfit. When it comes to styling skirts plus size, you want to choose accessories that complement the outfit rather than overpower it. For example, if you are wearing a statement skirt, you may want to choose simple accessories such as stud earrings and a bracelet. If you are wearing a simple skirt, you may want to choose a statement necklace or earrings to add some interest to the outfit.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style skirts plus size:

  1. Choose a skirt that fits well.
  2. Choose the right length.
  3. Choose the right fabric.
  4. Choose a top that complements your body shape.
  5. Accessorize with simple or statement pieces depending on the outfit.
  6. Choose the right shoes.

Tips for Styling Skirts Plus Size

Here are some additional tips for styling skirts plus size:

  • Choose a skirt with a pattern to add some interest to the outfit.
  • Choose a skirt with pockets to add some functionality.
  • Choose a skirt with a high waist to elongate your legs.
  • Choose a skirt with a slit to show off some skin without revealing too much.
  • Choose a skirt with a matching top to create a cohesive outfit.

FAQs

Q: Can plus size women wear mini skirts?

A: Yes, plus size women can wear mini skirts. However, you want to choose a mini skirt that is not too short and pair it with the right top and accessories.

Q: Can plus size women wear pencil skirts?

A: Yes, plus size women can wear pencil skirts. However, you want to choose a pencil skirt that fits well and pair it with the right top and accessories.

Hair Care Tips

When it comes to styling skirts plus size, you also want to consider your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your outfit.
  • Consider investing in a good quality wig or hair extensions if you want to change up your look.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Styling skirts plus size can be fun and easy with the right tips and tricks. Remember to choose a skirt that fits well, choose the right top, accessorize with simple or statement pieces, and choose the right shoes. With these tips, you can create a stylish and flattering outfit that you will feel confident in.
