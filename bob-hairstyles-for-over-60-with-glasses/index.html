Bob Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses Look Fabulous And Confident

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Don't worry, you can still rock a fabulous hairstyle that complements your glasses and makes you look confident. One of the most popular hairstyles for women over 60 is the bob. In this article, we'll guide you on how to choose the right bob hairstyle for you, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The bob hairstyle is a classic and versatile cut that suits women of all ages. It's a short to medium-length haircut that can be styled in various ways, from sleek and straight to messy and wavy. For women over 60 who wear glasses, a bob hairstyle can be an excellent choice as it frames the face and highlights the eyes.

How to Choose the Right Bob Hairstyle for You

Choosing the right bob hairstyle depends on several factors, such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose:

Consider your face shape

If you have a round face, opt for a longer bob that falls below the chin. If you have a square face, go for a shorter bob that hits above the chin. If you have an oval face, you can pull off any bob length.

Think about your hair texture

If you have thin hair, a shorter bob with layers can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer bob with blunt ends can tame the volume and create a sleek look.

Find inspiration from celebrities

Look for celebrities with a similar face shape and hair texture as yours and see how they style their bob. You can also consult with your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step by Step: How to Style a Bob Hairstyle

Styling a bob hairstyle is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help create volume and hold your style.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair while pulling it away from your face. This will create a sleek and smooth look.

Step 4: Style your hair

Using a flat iron or curling iron, style your hair according to your preference. You can create soft waves or straighten your hair for a sleek look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Hairstyle

To keep your bob hairstyle looking fabulous, follow these tips:

Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your bob.

Protect your hair from heat

If you use heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using them.

Use the right hair products

Choose hair products that suit your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, use a volumizing spray if you want to add volume to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear glasses with a bob hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still wear glasses with a bob hairstyle. In fact, glasses can complement your bob and highlight your eyes.

Q: Can I style my bob hairstyle differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your bob hairstyle differently for different occasions. For example, you can create soft waves for a casual look, or you can straighten your hair for a formal event.

Q: Can I color my bob hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can color your bob hairstyle. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations on the best hair color for your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care for Bob Hairstyles

To keep your bob hairstyle healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, so it's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Avoid over-washing your hair.

Washing your hair too often can dry it out and make it brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

A deep conditioning treatment can nourish your hair and keep it soft and shiny. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

In conclusion, bob hairstyles for over 60 with glasses can make you look fabulous and confident. Choose the right bob hairstyle for you, style it according to your preference, and take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. With these tips, you'll be able to rock a bob hairstyle that suits your personal style and complements your glasses.
