How To Fix Full Lace Frontal Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a step-by-step guide on how to fix full lace frontal, you've come to the right place. In this blog post, we'll go over everything you need to know about fixing your full lace frontal, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Whether you're new to wearing wigs or you're a seasoned pro, this guide will help you achieve a flawless look.

Description

Full lace frontals are a popular choice for those who want a natural-looking wig that can be styled in many different ways. However, fixing a full lace frontal can be challenging for those who are new to the process. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to fix your full lace frontal with ease.

How to Fix Full Lace Frontal

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Wig Cap

Before you begin fixing your full lace frontal, you'll need to prepare your hair and wig cap. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Then, put on your wig cap and adjust it to fit your head size.

Step 2: Apply Adhesive

Next, you'll need to apply adhesive to your full lace frontal. There are several types of adhesive you can use, including wig glue, tape, and adhesive spray. Choose the one that works best for you and apply it to your scalp and the front of your wig cap.

Step 3: Apply Your Full Lace Frontal

Once you've applied your adhesive, carefully place your full lace frontal on your head. Make sure it's aligned properly with your hairline and adjust it as needed. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your full lace frontal is in place, you can style your hair as desired. You can use heat tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, to create the look you want. Be careful not to apply too much heat, as this can damage your wig.

Tips for Fixing Full Lace Frontal

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look when fixing your full lace frontal:

  • Use high-quality adhesive to ensure your wig stays in place.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid damaging your wig.
  • Use a wig stand to store your wig when not in use.
  • Clean your wig regularly to keep it looking its best.

FAQs About Fixing Full Lace Frontal

Q: How long can I wear my full lace frontal?

A: You can wear your full lace frontal for up to six weeks at a time, although this can vary depending on the type of adhesive you use and your hair care routine.

Q: Can I swim or shower with my full lace frontal?

A: It's best to avoid swimming or showering with your full lace frontal, as this can damage the adhesive and cause your wig to come loose. If you do need to get your wig wet, use a water-resistant adhesive and be sure to dry it thoroughly afterwards.

Q: How do I clean my full lace frontal?

A: To clean your full lace frontal, use a mild shampoo and conditioner and gently massage it into your wig. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water and pat dry with a towel. Allow your wig to air dry completely before styling.

Hair Care Tips for Full Lace Frontal

Caring for your full lace frontal is important to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your wig.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help maintain its shape.
  • Clean your wig regularly to prevent buildup of oils and dirt.

With these tips and step-by-step guide, you'll be able to fix your full lace frontal with ease and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle that you'll love.
