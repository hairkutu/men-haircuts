How To Wear A Hat With Medium Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Hats are an excellent accessory to add to any outfit, but sometimes it can be challenging to figure out how to wear a hat with medium hair. In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks that will help you rock any hat with confidence. Whether you have a bob, lob, or shoulder-length hair, we've got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Hat with Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Hat with Medium Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips when Wearing a Hat

How to Wear a Hat with Medium Hair

Wearing a hat with medium hair can be easy if you know how to style your hair correctly. Here are some tips to help you wear a hat with medium hair.

1. Choose the Right Hat

Choosing the right hat is crucial when it comes to styling your medium hair. You want to pick a hat that complements your face shape and hair type. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a hat with a brim that is wider than your face to balance out your features. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a hat that is deeper to accommodate your curls.

2. Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right hat, it's time to style your hair. You want to keep your hair simple and low-key to avoid any awkward bumps or lumps under your hat. If you have straight hair, you can leave it down or pull it back into a low ponytail or bun. If you have curly hair, you may want to pull it back into a loose braid or bun.

3. Position the Hat Correctly

When wearing a hat, it's essential to position it correctly. You want to make sure that the hat sits comfortably on your head and doesn't slide around. The hat should sit on the crown of your head, not too far back or too far forward.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to wear a hat with medium hair let's go over a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look.

Step 1: Choose the Right Hat

Choose a hat that complements your face shape and hair type. Try on different hats to find the one that fits you best.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in a simple and low-key way to avoid any awkward bumps or lumps under your hat.

Step 3: Position the Hat Correctly

Position the hat on the crown of your head, making sure that it sits comfortably and doesn't slide around.

Tips for Wearing a Hat with Medium Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a hat with medium hair:

  • Choose a hat color that complements your skin tone and outfit.
  • Avoid wearing hats that are too tight or too loose.
  • Experiment with different hat styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your hat with a scarf or brooch.

FAQs

Can I wear a hat with bangs?

Yes, you can wear a hat with bangs. To avoid any awkward bumps, you may want to sweep your bangs to the side or pin them back.

What hat styles work best with medium hair?

Beanies, fedoras, and berets are all great hat styles that work well with medium hair.

Can I wear a hat with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a hat with curly hair. To avoid flattening your curls, you may want to choose a hat that is deeper to accommodate your curls.

Hair Care Tips when Wearing a Hat

Wearing a hat can cause your hair to become oily and flat. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking fresh and clean when wearing a hat:

  • Wash your hair before wearing a hat.
  • Avoid wearing a hat for extended periods.
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil.
  • Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a hat with medium hair. Remember, the key is to choose the right hat, style your hair correctly, and position the hat correctly. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hat styles to find the one that suits you best. Happy hat-wearing!
