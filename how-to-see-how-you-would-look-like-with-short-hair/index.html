How To See How You Would Look Like With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 09, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you thinking about cutting your hair short but not sure if it will suit you? It can be a daunting decision to make, especially if you’ve had long hair for a while. However, there are ways to see how you would look like with short hair before taking the plunge. In this article, we will give you tips on how to see how you would look like with short hair and provide you with some hair care advice.

Table of Content

  • How to See How You Would Look Like with Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to See How You Would Look Like with Short Hair

There are a few ways you can see how you would look like with short hair:

1. Virtual Try-Ons

Many hair salons and beauty websites offer virtual try-ons where you can upload a photo of yourself and see how you would look like with different hairstyles. Some apps also allow you to see how you would look like with different hair colors and makeup. Virtual try-ons are a great way to experiment with different hairstyles and colors without committing to a permanent change.

2. Wigs and Hairpieces

Wigs and hairpieces are another way to see how you would look like with short hair. You can purchase a wig or hairpiece that is similar to the hairstyle you want and try it on at home. Wigs and hairpieces are also a great way to change up your look for a special occasion without committing to a permanent change.

3. Photoshop

If you have some basic Photoshop skills, you can also try to edit a photo of yourself to see how you would look like with short hair. You can use the lasso tool to select your hair and then use the crop tool to remove it. You can then use the brush tool to fill in the gaps and create a short hair look.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to see how you would look like with short hair using virtual try-ons:

Step 1: Choose a Virtual Try-On App or Website

There are many virtual try-on apps and websites available, such as Hairstyle Try-On, ModiFace, and Virtual Makeover. Choose one that suits your needs.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Upload a clear photo of yourself that shows your face and hair. Make sure the photo is well-lit and that your hair is pulled back or away from your face.

Step 3: Choose a Short Hair Style

Browse through the different short hair styles available on the app or website and choose one that you like.

Step 4: Try on the Short Hair Style

Follow the instructions on the app or website to try on the short hair style. You may need to adjust the size and placement of the hairstyle to fit your face and head shape.

Step 5: Save and Share

If you like the short hair style, save the photo and share it with your friends and family to get their opinion.

Tips

  • Choose a short hair style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider your lifestyle and daily routine when choosing a short hair style.
  • Bring a photo of the short hair style you want to your hairdresser to ensure that they understand what you want.

FAQ

Will short hair suit me?

Short hair can suit anyone, but it’s important to choose a short hair style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Virtual try-ons, wigs, and hairpieces are great ways to see how you would look like with short hair before committing to a permanent change.

How often do I need to cut short hair?

Short hair needs to be cut more frequently than long hair. It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape of the short hair style.

How do I style short hair?

There are many ways to style short hair, such as using a straightener or curling iron, using hair products like mousse or gel, and blow-drying your hair with a diffuser. Your hairdresser can recommend the best styling products and techniques for your short hair style.

Hair Care

Short hair requires different hair care than long hair. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your short hair style.

Now that you know how to see how you would look like with short hair, you can confidently make the decision to cut your hair. Remember to choose a short hair style that suits your face shape and hair texture and to take care of your short hair with the right hair care products and techniques.
