How To Fix Missed Spots When Dying Hair

Written by Christine Apr 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of seeing missed spots after dying your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people face the same issue when dying their hair. In this article, we will discuss how to fix missed spots when dying hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Missed Spots When Dying Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Avoid Missed Spots When Dying Hair

How to Fix Missed Spots When Dying Hair

There are several ways to fix missed spots when dying hair. Here are some of the methods:

  • Use a tint brush or toothbrush to apply the hair dye to the missed spots. Make sure to apply the dye evenly to avoid further missed spots.
  • If you have a darker hair color, you can use a mascara wand to apply the hair dye to the missed spots.
  • If you have lighter hair color, use a cotton swab to apply the hair dye to the missed spots.
  • If the missed spots are on the hairline or around the face, use a q-tip to apply the hair dye. Be careful not to get the dye on your skin.
  • If you have a lot of missed spots, you may need to reapply the hair dye to your entire head. This will ensure that the color is even.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to fixing missed spots when dying hair:

  1. Section your hair to expose the missed spots.
  2. Prepare the hair dye and apply it to a tint brush or toothbrush.
  3. Starting with the missed spots, apply the hair dye to the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to apply the dye evenly.
  4. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended time on the packaging.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.
  6. Apply conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair with water and dry it with a towel.

Tips to Avoid Missed Spots When Dying Hair

Here are some tips to avoid missed spots when dying hair:

  • Divide your hair into sections before applying the hair dye.
  • Use a mirror to check for missed spots after applying the hair dye.
  • Apply the hair dye in a well-lit area to avoid missing any spots.
  • Wear gloves to avoid getting the hair dye on your skin.
  • Follow the instructions on the packaging carefully.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing missed spots when dying hair:

  • Can I use a different hair dye to fix missed spots?
    Yes, you can use a different hair dye to fix missed spots. Just make sure that the color is similar to the original color.
  • How long should I leave the hair dye on?
    You should leave the hair dye on for the recommended time on the packaging.
  • Can I dye my hair again if there are still missed spots?
    Yes, you can dye your hair again if there are still missed spots. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the packaging.

Hair Care After Dying

After dying your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain the color and health of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid fading the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as it can damage the hair cuticles and cause the color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Follow these tips to fix missed spots when dying hair and maintain the health and color of your hair.
