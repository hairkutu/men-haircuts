How To Style Skirts For Work In 2023

Written by Oliver Jul 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old pantsuit to work every day? Switch things up by incorporating skirts into your work wardrobe! Skirts can be just as professional and stylish as pants, and they can add a feminine touch to your outfit. Read on for tips on how to style skirts for work in 2023.

How to:

1. Choose the Right Skirt

When selecting a skirt for work, it’s important to choose one that is appropriate for your workplace. If you work in a conservative environment, opt for a knee-length or longer skirt in a neutral color like black, navy, or gray. If your workplace is more relaxed, you can experiment with different colors and prints.

2. Pair with a Blouse or Top

The key to styling a skirt for work is to pair it with a blouse or top that is professional and polished. A button-down shirt or a simple blouse in a solid color is a safe bet. If you want to add some personality to your outfit, you can choose a blouse with a print or a pop of color.

3. Accessorize with the Right Shoes

The shoes you wear with your skirt can make or break your outfit. Closed-toe pumps or flats in a neutral color like black or nude are a classic choice. If you want to add some interest to your outfit, you can choose shoes with a pattern or texture. Just make sure they are still professional and appropriate for your workplace.

4. Add a Blazer or Cardigan

If your workplace requires a more formal dress code, you may want to add a blazer or cardigan to your outfit. A blazer can add structure and polish to your outfit, while a cardigan can add some warmth and comfort.

Step by Step:

1. Start with a Basic Skirt

Choose a knee-length or longer skirt in a neutral color like black, navy, or gray.

2. Pair with a Simple Blouse or Top

Select a button-down shirt or a simple blouse in a solid color.

3. Choose Neutral Shoes

Opt for closed-toe pumps or flats in a neutral color like black or nude.

4. Add a Blazer or Cardigan

If necessary, add a blazer or cardigan in a coordinating color.

5. Accessorize with Simple Jewelry

Complete your outfit with simple jewelry like stud earrings and a watch.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different skirt lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Stick to simple, classic pieces to ensure your outfit remains professional.
  • Don’t be afraid to add some personality to your outfit with accessories or a fun print.
  • Always make sure your shoes are clean and in good condition.

FAQ:

Q: Can I wear a shorter skirt to work?

A: It depends on your workplace’s dress code. If you work in a conservative environment, it’s best to stick to knee-length or longer skirts.

Q: Can I wear a skirt with a pattern?

A: Yes, you can wear a skirt with a pattern as long as it’s not too bold or distracting. Stick to subtle prints like pinstripes or polka dots.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a skirt to work?

A: It depends on your workplace’s dress code. If sandals are allowed, make sure they are closed-toe and professional-looking.

Hair Care:

When styling a skirt for work, it’s important to consider your hair as well. Make sure your hair is clean and styled in a professional manner. If you have long hair, consider pulling it back in a low bun or ponytail to keep it out of your face.

By following these tips, you can style skirts for work in 2023 and beyond. Remember to always consider your workplace’s dress code and to choose pieces that are both professional and stylish.
