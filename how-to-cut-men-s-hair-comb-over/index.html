How To Cut Men s Hair Comb Over

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the barbershop? Or maybe you just want to learn how to cut your own hair? Whatever the reason, learning how to cut men's hair comb over is a great place to start. In this guide, we will take you through the steps to achieve the perfect comb over haircut at home.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Prepare
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Cut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Prepare

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to catch any hair that falls.

Once you have your tools, it's time to prepare your hair. Wash your hair and make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay, so you can see the natural part.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Create a Guide

Using your comb, create a part on the side of your head where you want your hair to lay. This will be your guide for the rest of the haircut.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Using your clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you have the desired length. Be careful not to cut too high up on your head, as this can ruin the look of the comb over.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of your hair to the desired length. Make sure you're cutting in the direction you want your hair to lay, so it falls naturally into place.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Using your clippers, blend the sides and the top of your hair together. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Using your comb, style your hair into the comb over look you desire. You can use a little bit of product, like pomade or wax, to help hold your hair in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Cut

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down, to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to take small snips than to cut off too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head, so you can make sure everything is even.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, start with a small trim and work your way up to a full cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair if it's wet?

No, it's best to cut your hair when it's completely dry. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly and can lead to mistakes.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Hair grows back and mistakes can be fixed. If you're unsure about fixing a mistake, it's best to seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've mastered the art of cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can lead to dryness and damage.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting your own hair can be a bit nerve-wracking at first, but with practice and patience, you can achieve a great look. Follow these steps and tips to get the perfect comb over haircut every time.
