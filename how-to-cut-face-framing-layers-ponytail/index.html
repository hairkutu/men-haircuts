How To Cut Face Framing Layers Ponytail

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Cutting face framing layers ponytail is a great way to achieve a fresh and trendy look. This hairstyle is perfect for people who want to add more dimension to their hair and frame their face with layers. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut face framing layers ponytail step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Face Framing Layers Ponytail
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A face framing layers ponytail is a hairstyle where the hair is pulled back into a ponytail, and layers are cut around the face to frame it. This hairstyle is perfect for people with long hair who want to add more volume and dimension to their hair. It's also great for people who want to highlight their facial features.

How to Cut Face Framing Layers Ponytail

Cutting face framing layers ponytail is easy to do. Here are the steps:

Step by Step

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, leaving a small section of hair in the front that you want to cut into layers.
  3. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.
  4. Take the small section of hair in the front and comb it straight down.
  5. Cut the hair straight across, making sure to leave it longer than you want it to be. You can always trim it later.
  6. Take the same section of hair and comb it straight up towards the ceiling.
  7. Using your scissors, cut into the hair at a slight angle to create a layer.
  8. Repeat steps 4 to 7 on the other side of your face.
  9. Trim any excess hair that you want to remove from the layers.
  10. Release the ponytail and style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting face framing layers ponytail:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean and precise cuts.
  • Leave the hair longer than you want it to be so that you can trim it later if needed.
  • Be patient and take your time when cutting the layers to ensure that they are even on both sides.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut less and check your progress frequently.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting face framing layers ponytail:

Can I cut face framing layers ponytail on my own?

Yes, you can cut face framing layers ponytail on your own. However, it's recommended that you seek the help of a professional stylist if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

What kind of face shapes look good with face framing layers ponytail?

Face framing layers ponytail looks good on all face shapes. The layers are cut to enhance your facial features, so it's a versatile hairstyle that can work for everyone.

How often do I need to trim my face framing layers ponytail?

You should trim your face framing layers ponytail every 4 to 6 weeks to keep the layers looking fresh and to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair after cutting face framing layers ponytail:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the health of your hair.

Now that you know how to cut face framing layers ponytail, you can try this hairstyle at home. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a trendy and stylish new look in no time!
