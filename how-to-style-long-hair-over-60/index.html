How To Style Long Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 03, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our hair texture and thickness change, and it’s common for many women over 60 to experience thinning hair. However, this doesn't mean you have to cut your hair short. Long hair can still be stylish and elegant, regardless of age. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style long hair over 60.

How to Style Long Hair Over 60

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it instead of trying to straighten it out. Use a styling product that enhances your natural texture and adds volume to your hair. A lightweight mousse or curl-enhancing cream can work wonders.

2. Use a Good Quality Hair Dryer

A good quality hair dryer can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Invest in a hair dryer that has a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural texture and add volume to your hair.

3. Avoid Overstyling Your Hair

Overstyling your hair can lead to breakage and damage. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons every day. Instead, try to air dry your hair as much as possible and use hot tools sparingly. When you do use hot tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Try a Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for long hair over 60. It’s a simple yet elegant style that can be dressed up or down. To create this style, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie at the back of your head.

5. Experiment with Different Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves can add a touch of style to any hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to find what works best for you. A simple headband can help to keep your hair out of your face, while a scarf can add a pop of color to your outfit.

6. Try a Classic Updo

A classic updo is a timeless hairstyle that can be worn by women of all ages. To create an updo, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with bobby pins or a hair tie. You can leave some hair loose to frame your face or create a sleek, polished look by pulling all of your hair back.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair Over 60

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair over 60:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual and let it air dry or blow dry it with a diffuser attachment.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a lightweight mousse or curl-enhancing cream to your hair to enhance your natural texture and add volume.

Step 3: Try a Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie at the back of your head.

Step 4: Add a Hair Accessory

Add a hair accessory like a headband or clip to add some style to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Over 60

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair over 60:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in good quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and movement.

FAQ About Styling Long Hair Over 60

Q: Can I still wear my hair long over 60?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can be stylish and elegant at any age. Just make sure to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained with regular trims and good quality hair care products.

Q: What are some good hairstyles for long hair over 60?

A: Half-up half-down hairstyles, classic updos, and loose waves are all great options for long hair over 60.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days between washes. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Over 60

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock long hair over 60 with confidence and style!
