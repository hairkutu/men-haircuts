How to Know if You Would Look Good with Short Hair

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair is a bold and trendy choice for any woman. It can make you feel liberated, confident and stylish. However, chopping off your locks is not a decision that should be taken lightly. Before you take the plunge, it's important to determine whether or not short hair will suit you. This article will guide you through the steps to figure out if you would look good with short hair.

How to

The first step to figuring out if you would look good with short hair is to determine your face shape. Short hair can look great on any face shape, but some styles are more flattering than others. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can research which short haircuts would work best for you.

Step by step

Step 1: Determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and examining your face in a mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, the length from your forehead to your chin, and the shape of your jawline.

Step 2: Research short haircuts that would flatter your face shape. Look for inspiration online or in magazines. You can also consult with a stylist to determine which styles would work best for you.

Step 3: Consider your hair texture. Short hair can look great on any hair type, but some styles may require more maintenance than others. If you have curly or thick hair, you may need to get frequent trims to keep the style looking fresh.

Tips

Tip 1: Use virtual makeover tools. There are many websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles. This can give you a good idea of what you would look like with short hair.

Tip 2: Consult with a stylist. A professional stylist can give you personalized advice on which short haircuts would work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?

A: Short hair can actually make your face look slimmer, especially if you choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with short hair?

A: Depending on the length of your hair, you may still be able to wear it in a ponytail or other updo styles.

Hair Care

Short hair may require more frequent trims to maintain the style. You may also need to invest in styling products, such as mousse, gel or hairspray, to keep your hair looking its best. Be sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
