How Long Will It Take To Get A Man Bun

Written by William Jul 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're thinking about growing out your hair into a man bun, the first question on your mind is probably "how long will it take?" There are a few factors that will determine how quickly you can achieve your desired look, but with a little patience and some hair care tips, you'll be rocking a man bun in no time. In this article, we'll dive into the details of how long it will take to get a man bun, how to achieve the look, and how to take care of your hair along the way.

How to Get a Man Bun

Before we dive into how long it will take to get a man bun, let's talk about what exactly a man bun is and how to achieve the look. A man bun is a hairstyle where the hair is pulled back into a bun at the back of the head. The length of the hair can vary, but generally, you'll need at least 6 inches of hair to achieve a man bun.

To get a man bun, you'll need to grow out your hair to the desired length. This can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your man bun to be. Once your hair is long enough, you can tie it back into a bun using a hair tie or elastic band.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're not sure how to grow out your hair and style a man bun, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by growing out your hair. You'll need at least 6 inches of hair to achieve a man bun, but you can grow it longer if you prefer.
  2. Keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  3. As your hair grows, use a comb or brush to keep it neat and tidy. You can also use styling products like hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  4. When your hair is long enough, pull it back into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure it in place.
  5. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with another hair tie or elastic band.
  6. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your man bun in place.

Tips for Growing a Man Bun

Here are a few tips to help you grow out your hair and achieve a man bun:

  • Be patient. Growing out your hair can take time, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't happen overnight.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can damage your hair and slow down growth.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles as you grow out your hair. You might find that you like a different look better than a man bun.

FAQ about Man Buns

Here are a few frequently asked questions about man buns:

  • Can anyone pull off a man bun? Yes, anyone with long enough hair can wear a man bun. However, it might not be the right look for everyone.
  • Is it bad for your hair to wear a man bun? Wearing a man bun can put some strain on your hair, but as long as you're gentle when tying it back and don't wear it too tight, it shouldn't cause any damage.
  • How long should my hair be before I can get a man bun? You'll need at least 6 inches of hair to achieve a man bun, but you can grow it longer if you prefer.
  • Can I wear a man bun to work? It depends on your workplace dress code, but in general, a man bun is a casual hairstyle that might not be appropriate for more formal settings.

Hair Care for Man Buns

Once you've achieved your man bun, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.

In conclusion, growing out your hair to achieve a man bun can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more, depending on how long you want your hair to be. With a little patience and some hair care tips, you'll be able to achieve the look you want and keep your hair healthy along the way.
