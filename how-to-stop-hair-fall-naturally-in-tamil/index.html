How To Stop Hair Fall Naturally In Tamil

Written by Oliver May 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of losing hair and want to know how to stop hair fall naturally in Tamil? Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. There are several reasons for hair fall such as stress, hormonal imbalance, nutritional deficiencies, and genetics. However, you can prevent hair fall naturally by following some simple tips and remedies. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall naturally in Tamil.

How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Tamil - Tips

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall naturally:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals is essential to prevent hair fall. Include foods like eggs, fish, nuts, green vegetables, and fruits in your diet.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water will keep your scalp hydrated and prevent hair fall. It will also help in flushing out toxins from your body.

3. Massage Your Scalp Regularly

Massaging your scalp regularly with coconut oil or any other hair oil will improve blood circulation, nourish your hair follicles and prevent hair fall.

4. Use a Mild Shampoo

Using a mild shampoo will prevent hair fall and keep your hair clean. Avoid using harsh shampoos that contain chemicals.

5. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curlers as they can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Tamil - Remedies

Here are some natural remedies to prevent hair fall:

1. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural remedy that can prevent hair fall. Apply onion juice on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural remedy that can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Apply aloe vera gel on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

3. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight. Grind the soaked seeds and apply the paste on your scalp. Leave it for 30 minutes and wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

4. Coconut Milk

Coconut milk is a natural remedy that can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Apply coconut milk on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

FAQ - How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Tamil

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you are stressed, your body releases cortisol hormone which can cause hair fall.

Q: Can nutritional deficiencies cause hair fall?

A: Yes, nutritional deficiencies such as iron deficiency, vitamin D deficiency, and protein deficiency can cause hair fall.

Q: How often should I massage my scalp?

A: You can massage your scalp 2-3 times a week.

Hair Care

Following a proper hair care routine is essential to prevent hair fall. Here are some tips:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will prevent split ends and breakage.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb will prevent hair breakage and reduce hair fall.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause hair breakage and hair fall.

4. Protect Your Hair from Sunlight

Protect your hair from sunlight by wearing a hat or using a scarf. Sunlight can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

By following these tips and remedies, you can prevent hair fall naturally in Tamil. Remember to follow a healthy lifestyle and take care of your hair regularly.
