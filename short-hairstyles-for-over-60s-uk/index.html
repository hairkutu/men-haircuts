If you're over 60 and thinking about getting a new short hairstyle, you're on the right track. Short hair is low maintenance, easy to style, and can make you look younger and more vibrant. But with so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle for your age and face shape.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner, drier, and more brittle. That's why it's important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type, while also accentuating your best features. Whether you have fine, straight hair or thick, curly locks, there's a short hairstyle out there that will work for you.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a longer bob or a layered cut that falls just below your chin. If you have a square jawline, choose a short pixie cut or a bob that's shorter in the back and longer in the front. If you have an oval face, you're lucky â almost any short hairstyle will suit you!

Next, consider your hair type. If you have fine, straight hair, go for a blunt bob or a layered cut with lots of volume. If you have thick, curly hair, a short pixie cut or a shaggy bob with choppy layers will look great.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen your ideal short hairstyle, it's time to get it cut. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the look:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts â front, back, and two sides.
  3. Starting at the back, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way up to the top of your head.
  4. Repeat on the sides and front, being careful not to cut too much off.
  5. Use a comb to make sure your hair is even and straight.
  6. Style your hair using your favorite products, such as mousse or hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims â short hair grows out quickly, so it's important to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Invest in good quality products â using the right products can help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styles â short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.
  • Accessorize â add a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your short hairstyle to give it some extra flair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for over 60s:

Q. Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

No, quite the opposite! A short hairstyle can make you look younger and more vibrant. It's all about choosing the right style for your face shape and hair type.

Q. Can I still color my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

Yes, absolutely! Short hair can be colored just like long hair. Just make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking fresh.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair looking its best is important at any age, but especially as we get older. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than every other day â washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner â look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.
  • Avoid heat styling â excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Eat a healthy diet â a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

With these tips in mind, you'll be well on your way to choosing the perfect short hairstyle for your over 60s look. Whether you go for a chic pixie cut or a sassy bob, you're sure to turn heads and feel confident and stylish.
