Women's Haircuts Shaved Sides Long on Top | A Complete Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the women's haircut shaved sides long on top. This unique hairstyle combines the best of both worlds, with a sleek and daring undercut on the sides and a longer, more feminine look on top. Keep reading for a complete guide on how to achieve this bold and beautiful hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Shaved Sides Long on Top Look
  • Step by Step Instructions

Description

The women's haircut shaved sides long on top is a bold and edgy hairstyle that has become increasingly popular in recent years. The haircut features shaved or closely cropped sides, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. This versatile hairstyle can be worn by women of all ages and hair types, making it a great choice for anyone who wants to make a statement with their hair.

How to Achieve the Shaved Sides Long on Top Look

If you're interested in getting a shaved sides long on top haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can work together to create the perfect customized look for you.

Step by Step Instructions

Here are the basic steps involved in getting a shaved sides long on top haircut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Using clippers or scissors, your hairstylist will shave or closely crop the sides of your head.
  3. The hair on top of your head will be left longer, usually between 2 and 4 inches, depending on your preference.
  4. You can choose to style the longer hair on top in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.
  5. Depending on the length of your hair, you may need to come back for regular trims to maintain the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shaved Sides Long on Top Haircut

To keep your shaved sides long on top haircut looking fresh and stylish, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in high-quality hair care products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  • Regularly visit your hairstylist for trims to keep the sides of your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find a look that works for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth to the style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will a shaved sides long on top haircut work for my hair type?

Yes! This versatile haircut can work for women with all different hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

How often will I need to get my hair trimmed?

How often you need to get your hair trimmed will depend on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. Your hairstylist can recommend a schedule that works best for you.

Can I still wear my hair up with a shaved sides long on top haircut?

Yes! You can still wear your hair up in a ponytail, bun, or other updo style with a shaved sides long on top haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Shaved Sides Long on Top Haircuts

To keep your shaved sides long on top haircut looking its best, here are a few hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a shaved sides long on top haircut with confidence and style. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or just want to try something new with your hair, this trendy haircut is sure to turn heads.
