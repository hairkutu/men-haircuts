How To Cut Long Men s Hair With Scissors At Home

Written by Eveline Aug 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money and get a fresh haircut without leaving your house, cutting your own hair is a great option. While cutting long men's hair with scissors can be intimidating, it's not impossible. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut long men's hair with scissors at home.

How to Cut Long Men's Hair with Scissors at Home

Cutting long men's hair with scissors requires a bit of preparation beforehand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Large mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut long men's hair with scissors at home:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.
  2. Section your hair using hair clips. Divide your hair into four sections: the top, back, and two sides.
  3. Start with the back section. Comb your hair straight down and trim off any split ends. Work your way up the back of your head, cutting small sections at a time.
  4. Move onto the side sections. Comb your hair straight down and cut the ends at a slight angle, following the shape of your head.
  5. Finally, move onto the top section. Comb your hair straight up and trim off any split ends. Be careful not to cut too much off the top.
  6. Once you've finished cutting your hair, comb it out and check for any uneven spots. Trim off any remaining split ends or uneven spots.

Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long men's hair with scissors:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair off than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors and keep your cuts straight.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting hair can be time-consuming, but rushing through it can lead to mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're struggling with a certain section, ask a friend or family member for assistance.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long men's hair with scissors:

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair scissors are specifically designed for cutting hair and will give you a cleaner, more precise cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Can cutting my own hair damage it?

If done incorrectly, cutting your own hair can lead to damage and uneven spots. Be sure to follow the steps outlined in this article and take your time.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Men's Hair with Scissors at Home

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can lead to damage and split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

By following these tips and techniques, you can successfully cut long men's hair with scissors at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and ask for help if needed. With practice, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
