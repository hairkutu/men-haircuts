What Natural Products Can I Use To Thicken My Hair

Written by Frank Jul 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Thinning hair can be a frustrating and challenging issue to deal with. While there are many products on the market aimed at thickening hair, many of them are filled with harsh chemicals that can do more harm than good in the long run. Thankfully, there are plenty of natural products that you can use to thicken your hair that are both safe and effective.

Oil

One natural product that can help thicken your hair is oil. Coconut oil, olive oil, and castor oil are all excellent options. These oils work by moisturizing your hair and scalp, which can help prevent breakage and promote hair growth. To use, simply massage the oil into your hair and scalp and leave it on for at least an hour before washing it out.

Egg

Egg is another natural product that can help thicken your hair. Eggs are rich in protein, which can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage. To use, beat two eggs and apply the mixture to your hair, making sure to cover all of your strands. Leave the mixture on for 30 minutes before washing it out with shampoo.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural product that can help thicken your hair and soothe your scalp. Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help promote hair growth and reduce inflammation. To use, apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it out with shampoo.

Avocado

Avocado is a natural product that is rich in vitamins and minerals that can help thicken your hair. Avocado contains vitamins B and E, which can help prevent hair loss and promote hair growth. To use, mash one ripe avocado and apply it to your hair, making sure to cover all of your strands. Leave the avocado on for 30 minutes before washing it out with shampoo.

Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural product that can help thicken your hair and prevent hair loss. Onion juice contains sulfur, which can help promote collagen production and improve blood circulation to the scalp. To use, blend one onion and strain the mixture to get the juice. Apply the juice to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it out with shampoo.

Rosemary Oil

Rosemary oil is a natural product that can help thicken your hair and promote hair growth. Rosemary oil contains antioxidants that can help prevent hair loss and improve hair thickness. To use, mix a few drops of rosemary oil with a carrier oil, such as coconut oil or olive oil, and massage the mixture into your scalp and hair. Leave the mixture on for at least an hour before washing it out with shampoo.

Tips for Thicker Hair

While using natural products can be helpful in thickening your hair, there are also other things you can do to promote hair growth and prevent breakage:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as much as possible.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, as they can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as vitamin A, vitamin C, and biotin.

FAQs

Can natural products really thicken my hair?

Yes, natural products can be effective in thickening your hair. Many natural products contain vitamins and minerals that can promote hair growth and prevent breakage.

How often should I use natural products to thicken my hair?

You can use natural products to thicken your hair as often as you like, but it's best to start with once a week and see how your hair responds. You can gradually increase the frequency if you find that your hair is responding well.

Can I use multiple natural products to thicken my hair?

Yes, you can use multiple natural products to thicken your hair. In fact, using a combination of natural products can be even more effective than using one product alone.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Once you have successfully thickened your hair using natural products, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and promote continued growth. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using hot tools as much as possible.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and overall health.

Thickening your hair doesn't have to involve harsh chemicals or expensive treatments. With the right natural products and care, you can achieve thicker, healthier hair that you can be proud of.
