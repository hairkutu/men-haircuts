Short Hairstyles For Straight Fine Hair 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 13, 2023 Â· 12 min read

If you have straight and fine hair, you may find it challenging to style and maintain your locks. However, this shouldn't stop you from trying different haircuts that can enhance your hair's natural texture and volume. In this blog post, we will share some of the best short hairstyles for straight fine hair in 2019, along with tips on how to style and take care of your hair.

  • Pixie Cut

1. Short Bob

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that can look great on straight fine hair. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A bob that ends just above the chin or at the jawline can add volume and movement to your hair. Pairing it with side-swept bangs can also create a more youthful and edgy look.

How to Style:

To style a short bob, you can use a volumizing mousse or hair spray to add texture and hold. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and curl the ends inward or outward for a more polished look. You can also use a flat iron to create subtle waves or curls.

Tips:

When cutting your hair into a short bob, ask your stylist to add layers to create movement and texture. This can also help to prevent your hair from looking too flat or limp. Use a lightweight and non-greasy hair product to avoid weighing down your hair.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and bold haircut that can be a great option for those with straight fine hair. It can help to elongate your neck and highlight your facial features. A pixie cut that is slightly longer in the front can create a more feminine and delicate look.

How to Style:

To style a pixie cut, you can use a root-lifting spray or powder to add volume to your roots. Use your fingers to style your hair and create a messy and textured look. You can also use a pomade or wax to define your hair and create a more polished finish.

Tips:

A pixie cut can be a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut, but it does require regular trimming to maintain its shape. You can also experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your look.

3. Lob

A lob, or a long bob, is a trendy and versatile haircut that can work well for those with straight fine hair. It is a flattering hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A lob that is slightly longer in the front can help to create an illusion of volume and texture.

How to Style:

To style a lob, you can use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add some texture and beachy waves to your hair. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.

Tips:

A lob can be a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut, but it does require regular trimming to maintain its shape. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

4. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a modern and chic haircut that can add volume and texture to straight fine hair. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob that is slightly longer in the front can create a more flattering and elongating look.

How to Style:

To style a layered bob, you can use a volumizing mousse or hair spray to add texture and hold. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and curl the ends inward or outward for a more polished look. You can also use a flat iron to create subtle waves or curls.

Tips:

When cutting your hair into a layered bob, ask your stylist to add choppy layers to create movement and texture. This can also help to prevent your hair from looking too flat or limp. Use a lightweight and non-greasy hair product to avoid weighing down your hair.

How to Style Short Hairstyles for Straight Fine Hair

When styling short hairstyles for straight fine hair, it's important to use the right hair products and tools to enhance your hair's natural texture and volume. Here are some tips on how to style different short haircuts:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or hair spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Curl the ends of your hair inward or outward for a more polished look.
  • Use a flat iron to create subtle waves or curls.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Straight Fine Hair

Maintaining short hairstyles for straight fine hair requires some effort and care. Here are some tips on how to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy and oily hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my straight fine hair?

Yes, you can dye your straight fine hair. However, it's important to use hair dyes that are gentle and formulated for your hair type. Talk to your stylist about the best hair dye options for your hair.

2. How often should I wash my straight fine hair?

You should wash your straight fine hair every other day or every three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

3. Can I use a hair mask on my straight fine hair?

Yes, you can use a hair mask on your straight fine hair to nourish and hydrate your locks. Use a hair mask once a week or every two weeks, depending on your hair's needs.

Hair Care Tips for Straight Fine Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for straight fine hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause heat damage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or clips that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can transform your straight fine hair into a stylish and chic look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right hair products and styling tools, and by following a healthy hair care routine. Happy styling!
