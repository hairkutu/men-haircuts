Round Face Short Haircuts For Men

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a guy with a round face and looking for a new haircut? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss some of the best short haircuts for men with round faces. We will also provide tips on how to choose the right haircut and how to take care of your hair after getting the cut.

Description

Round face shape is characterized by having a wide forehead, rounded cheeks, and a rounded jawline. Men with this face shape often struggle to find the right haircut that can balance out their features. Short haircuts are often recommended for men with round faces, as they can help elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's essential to consider the length and style that will complement your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Type

Before choosing a haircut, consider your hair type. Men with straight hair can go for a shorter, more textured cut, while men with curly hair should opt for a longer, layered cut.

Focus on the Top

When getting a haircut for a round face, focus on the top of your head. A textured, layered cut can help add height to your hair and elongate your face.

Avoid Full Fringes

Avoid getting a full fringe cut as it can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for a side swept fringe or a quiff.

Step by Step Guide to Round Face Short Haircuts

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short haircut for your round face:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Choose a style that works best for your hair type and face shape. Some popular short haircuts for men with round faces include buzz cuts, crew cuts, and textured crops.

Step 2: Consult with Your Barber

Consult with your barber or hairstylist. They can help you choose the right haircut and give you advice on how to style it.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair before going to the salon. This will make it easier for your barber to cut your hair and ensure that your hair looks clean and fresh after the cut.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Get the cut. Make sure to tell your barber exactly what you want and ask for their advice on how to style it.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair. Use a hair product that works well for your hair type and style to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Men with Round Faces

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. This will also help prevent split ends and breakage.

Style Your Hair Regularly

Style your hair regularly to maintain the look you want. Use a hair product that works well for your hair type and style.

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for men with round faces?

Some of the best short haircuts for men with round faces include buzz cuts, crew cuts, and textured crops.

Can men with round faces get a fringe?

Men with round faces can get a fringe, but it's important to avoid a full fringe as it can make the face look rounder. Instead, opt for a side swept fringe or a quiff.

How often should men with short haircuts get a trim?

Men with short haircuts should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep their hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Men with Round Face Short Haircuts

Men with round face short haircuts should take care of their hair by using the right shampoo and conditioner, getting regular trims, and styling their hair regularly. It's also important to choose a haircut that complements your features and face shape. Consult with your barber or hairstylist for advice on the best haircut for you.
