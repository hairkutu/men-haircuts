Best Short Hair For Long Oval Face

Written by Oliver Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having long hair that always seems to be in the way? Perhaps you're ready for a new look, but aren't sure what style would suit you best. If you have a long oval face, there are plenty of short haircuts that will flatter your features. In this article, we'll explore the best short hair for long oval face, how to style it, and how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long oval faces are longer than they are wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. The best short haircuts for this face shape are those that add volume and width to the sides of the face, while softening the forehead and chin. These cuts can be playful, chic, or sophisticated, depending on your personal style. The key is to find a cut that complements your features and makes you feel confident.

How to

The first step in finding the best short hair for long oval face is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine which cuts will suit your face shape and hair type, and can offer styling tips to help you maintain your new look. When choosing a cut, consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling each day. Some cuts require more maintenance than others.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen your cut, it's important to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long oval faces:

What are the best short haircuts for long oval faces?

The best short haircuts for long oval faces are those that add volume to the sides of the face while softening the forehead and chin. Some popular styles include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob.

Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with a short haircut. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape. Side-swept bangs are a great option for long oval faces.

How can I make my short haircut look more sophisticated?

To make your short haircut look more sophisticated, try adding texture and volume to your hair. You can also experiment with different styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create a polished look.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using harsh chemical treatments, such as perms or relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can be a chic and flattering option for long oval faces. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best short hair for your face shape and hair type, and be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
