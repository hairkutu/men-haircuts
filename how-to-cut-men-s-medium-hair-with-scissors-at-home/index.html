How To Cut Men s Medium Hair With Scissors At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a way to save some money on haircuts or simply want to try your hand at cutting hair, you might be wondering how to cut men's medium hair with scissors at home. While it might seem daunting at first, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut without leaving your house. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cutting men's medium hair with scissors at home, as well as provide some tips and tricks for success.

Description

Medium-length hair is a popular choice for men, as it offers versatility and can work for a variety of styles. However, heading to the salon or barbershop every few weeks for a trim can add up over time. By learning how to cut men's medium hair with scissors at home, you can save money and have more control over your hairstyle.

How to Cut Men's Medium Hair with Scissors at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips or elastics to section off the hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as needed.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly. This will remove any product buildup or dirt and make the hair easier to cut. Once the hair is dry, use a comb to part it into sections.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by trimming the ends of the hair to remove any split or damaged ends. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across, using the comb as a guide. Work your way around the head, trimming the ends of each section.

Step 4: Layer the Hair

If you want to add some layers to the hair, use the same technique as above, but hold the hair at an angle instead of straight across. This will create a more textured, layered look.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair, use the scissors to cut small sections of hair at an angle, working from the bottom up. This will create a more natural, blended look.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the scissors or a trimmer to clean up the neckline and around the ears. This will give the haircut a polished, finished look.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, comb through the hair and check for any unevenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure the hair is even and balanced.

Step 8: Style as Desired

Now that the haircut is complete, style the hair as desired using products like pomade or gel.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's medium hair with scissors at home:

  • Start with small snips, as it's easier to take off more hair than to add it back.
  • Use the comb as a guide to ensure evenness.
  • Don't forget to clean up the neckline and around the ears for a polished look.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid fatigue or frustration.

FAQ

Can I cut men's medium hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to use regular scissors, it's best to use hair cutting scissors for the best results. Hair cutting scissors are sharper and designed for cutting hair, which will make the process easier and produce a better outcome.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Just take a step back and assess the situation. Depending on the severity of the mistake, you may be able to fix it by making small adjustments or trimming a bit more hair. If the mistake is more significant, you may need to seek professional help to correct it.

How often should I cut men's medium hair?

The frequency of haircuts will depend on the individual's hair type and style. Generally, men's medium hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting men's medium hair with scissors at home, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and appearance. This includes regular washing and conditioning, using products designed for your hair type, and avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.

Learning how to cut men's medium hair with scissors at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
