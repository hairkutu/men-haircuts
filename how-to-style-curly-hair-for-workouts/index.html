How To Style Curly Hair For Workouts

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to working out, curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, your curls can add volume and bounce to your workout look. On the other hand, they can also become frizzy and unmanageable during a sweaty workout session. So, how can you style your curly hair for workouts to ensure it stays intact and looks good? Here are some tips and tricks to help you out.

Description

Curly hair is naturally prone to frizz and can be difficult to manage during a workout. Sweat and humidity can make your curls look unruly and unmanageable. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can keep your curls looking great while you exercise.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean hair
Before you start styling your hair for a workout, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to help keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner
To help keep your curls moisturized during your workout, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking bouncy and defined.

Step 3: Use a hair tie
When it comes to styling your hair for a workout, it's important to use a hair tie that won't damage your hair. Look for hair ties made from soft materials like silk or satin to help prevent breakage and damage to your curls.

Step 4: Try a protective style
If you're looking for a more low-maintenance option, try a protective style like braids or twists. These styles will help keep your curls contained and prevent frizz during your workout.

Step 5: Use a refresher spray
After your workout, use a refresher spray specifically designed for curly hair to help revive your curls and add moisture back into your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean hair.
Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner.
Step 3: Use a hair tie.
Step 4: Try a protective style.
Step 5: Use a refresher spray after your workout.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls before styling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curls before a workout.
  • Use a sweatband to help absorb sweat and prevent it from getting into your hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and sweat after your workout.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair down for a workout?
A: While it's possible to wear your hair down for a workout, it's not recommended for curly hair. Sweating and humidity can cause your curls to become frizzy and unmanageable. It's best to wear your hair up in a protective style or use a sweatband to help keep your curls contained.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair after a workout?
A: It's recommended to wash your hair after every workout to help remove sweat and prevent buildup. However, if you're short on time, you can also use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and sweat.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include using a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair, as well as a leave-in conditioner and refresher spray. It's also important to avoid using heat styling tools on your curls and to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls gently.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your curly hair looking great during your next workout. Whether you prefer a protective style or a simple ponytail, these styling techniques will help keep your curls defined and frizz-free.
