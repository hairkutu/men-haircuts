How To Use Thinning Shears For Curly Hair A Guide For Beautiful Curls

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to achieve a stunning hairstyle that will make your curly hair look amazing? Thinning shears are one of the best tools that you can use to achieve this goal. However, using thinning shears for curly hair can be tricky if you're not familiar with the process. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to use thinning shears for curly hair. We'll also give you tips on how to take care of your hair after using thinning shears.

Description

Thinning shears are specialized scissors that have teeth on one blade and a smooth blade on the other. They are designed to remove some of the bulk from your hair without changing the length of your hair. When used correctly, thinning shears can create a more natural look for your curls, reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable.

How to Use Thinning Shears for Curly Hair

Thinning shears are a great tool for curly hair, but you need to know how to use them correctly to get the best results. To use thinning shears for curly hair, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Never use thinning shears on wet hair because it can cause damage to the hair.

2. Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to work with your hair using thinning shears.

3. Hold the thinning shears with the teeth facing upwards and the smooth blade facing your hair.

4. Start at the mid-length of your hair and work your way towards the ends. Close the thinning shears gently on your hair and then open them again. Continue this process until you've cut through the entire section of hair.

5. Repeat this process for each section of your hair. Make sure to work slowly and carefully, so you don't remove too much hair at once.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears for Curly Hair

1. Don't use thinning shears on the top layer of your hair. This will create thin, wispy strands that can look frizzy and unattractive.

2. Use thinning shears sparingly. Overuse can lead to uneven, choppy haircuts.

3. Always start with less hair and work your way up. You can always remove more hair if needed, but you can't add it back if you've removed too much.

FAQs

Q: Can thinning shears damage my hair?

A: Thinning shears can damage your hair if used improperly. Always use them on dry hair and avoid using them too often.

Q: Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

A: Yes, thinning shears can be used on all hair types, including curly hair.

Hair Care After Using Thinning Shears

After using thinning shears on your curly hair, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to prevent frizz and keep your curls defined.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers. These can damage your hair and make your curls look frizzy.

4. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Using thinning shears for curly hair can be a great way to achieve a natural, beautiful hairstyle. By following these tips and using thinning shears correctly, you can create gorgeous curls that you'll love.
