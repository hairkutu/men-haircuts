Does A Pixie Cut Make You Look Older

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically short on the sides and back and longer on the top. It's a popular choice for women of all ages due to its low maintenance and stylish look. However, there is a common misconception that a pixie cut can make you look older. This article will explore whether or not this is true and provide tips on how to make your pixie cut look youthful and fresh.

How To Get a Pixie Cut

If you're considering a pixie cut, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. You can bring pictures of the style you want to help your stylist understand your vision. It's also important to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a pixie cut style. Your stylist can help you determine what will work best for you.

Step-By-Step Guide to Pixie Cut

1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. It's important to have clean, dry hair before cutting.

2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.

3. Cut the sides and back with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference.

4. Cut the top section to the desired length, leaving some length for styling.

5. Use texturizing scissors to add texture and movement to the hair.

6. Style your pixie cut using a styling product, such as wax or gel.

Tips for Styling Pixie Cut

1. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.

2. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as slicking your hair back or adding volume at the crown.

3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

4. Accessorize your pixie cut with headbands or hair clips to add interest to your look.

FAQ About Pixie Cut and Age

Q: Does a pixie cut make you look older?

A: There is no definitive answer to this question as it depends on the individual's face shape, hair texture, and styling techniques. However, some people believe that a pixie cut can make you look older if it's styled in a way that is severe or matronly.

Q: Can a pixie cut be styled in a youthful way?

A: Yes! There are many ways to style a pixie cut to make it look youthful, such as adding volume at the crown, using a texturizing product, or accessorizing with headbands or hair clips.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut

1. Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut shape.

Overall, a pixie cut can be a stylish and youthful choice for women of all ages. By choosing the right style and using the right styling techniques and hair care products, you can make your pixie cut look fresh and modern.
