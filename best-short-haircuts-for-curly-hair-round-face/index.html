Best Short Haircuts For Curly Hair Round Face

Written by Christine Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the best short haircuts for curly hair round face? If you have a round face and curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a bit of a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and highlight your gorgeous curls.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to short haircuts for curly hair round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that will elongate your face and provide some height on top. This will help balance out the fullness of your face shape.

Another important factor to consider is the texture of your curls. Some curly hair types are more prone to frizz or require more maintenance than others. You want to choose a style that works with your natural curl pattern and doesn't require too much styling.

How to

When choosing the best short haircut for your curly hair round face, you should start by looking for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and other online resources to find styles that you love and that will flatter your face shape.

Once you've found a few styles that you like, it's time to talk to your stylist. They can help you determine which cuts will work best with your curly hair and round face. They may also be able to recommend styling products and techniques that will help you achieve your desired look.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve one of the best short haircuts for curly hair round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rough up the curls too much.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel.
  4. Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose or frizzy curls.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for choosing and maintaining the best short haircut for your curly hair round face:

  • Choose a cut that provides some height on top to balance out the fullness of your face shape.
  • Avoid cuts that are too blunt, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your cut to help define your curls and add movement.
  • Use products designed for curly hair, such as mousse, gel, and hairspray.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircuts for curly hair round face:

What is the best short haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best short haircut for a round face with curly hair is one that provides some height on top and elongates the face. Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with lots of texture.

How do I style my curly hair for a short haircut?

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Use a curling iron to define any loose or frizzy curls, and finish with a lightweight hairspray.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use products designed for curly hair, such as sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, mousse, and gel.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural curl pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best short haircuts for curly hair round face and rock your natural curls with confidence!
