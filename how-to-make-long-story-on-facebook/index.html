How To Make Long Story On Facebook

Written by Eveline Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are an avid Facebook user, you might have seen long stories being shared by your friends. These stories are a great way to share your thoughts, experiences, and feelings in a more detailed manner. In this article, we will guide you on how to make long story on Facebook.

Description

A long story on Facebook is a post that is longer than the usual status update. It can be a personal experience, a travelogue, a review, or anything that you want to share with your friends in more detail. Facebook allows you to write up to 63,206 characters in a post, which is more than enough to write a long story.

How to Make Long Story on Facebook

Follow these simple steps to make a long story on Facebook:

Step 1: Open Facebook App

Open the Facebook app on your smartphone or tablet. If you don't have the app, you can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Create a New Post

Tap on the "Create Post" button located at the bottom of the screen. This will open a new post window where you can write your long story.

Step 3: Write Your Story

Start writing your story in the post window. You can use the formatting options to make your post more readable. You can also add photos, videos, and GIFs to make your post more engaging.

Step 4: Preview Your Post

Once you have finished writing your post, preview it to make sure everything looks good. You can also edit your post if you find any mistakes or want to make any changes.

Step 5: Publish Your Post

When you are satisfied with your post, click on the "Post" button to publish it. Your long story will now be visible to your friends and followers.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your long story more engaging:

  • Use a catchy headline to grab the attention of your readers.
  • Use short paragraphs to make your post more readable.
  • Use images and videos to make your post more engaging.
  • Use emojis to express your emotions.
  • Add a call-to-action at the end of your post to encourage your readers to engage with your post.

FAQ

Q: Can I edit my long story after publishing it?

A: Yes, you can edit your long story after publishing it. Just click on the three dots on the top right corner of your post and select "Edit Post".

Q: Can I delete my long story after publishing it?

A: Yes, you can delete your long story after publishing it. Just click on the three dots on the top right corner of your post and select "Delete Post".

Q: Can I see how many people have read my long story?

A: No, Facebook does not provide any statistics on how many people have read your post.

Hair Care Blog Example

As a hair care blogger, you can use long stories on Facebook to share your hair care journey with your followers. You can write about your hair care routine, product reviews, and hair care tips. Here are some tips to make your long story more engaging:

  • Add before and after photos to show the difference in your hair.
  • Share your favorite hair care products and how they have helped you.
  • Share your hair care mistakes and what you have learned from them.
  • Ask your followers to share their hair care tips and experiences.

By following these tips, you can create engaging long stories on Facebook that will help you connect with your followers and grow your hair care blog.
