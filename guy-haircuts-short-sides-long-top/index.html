Guy Haircuts Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, the guy haircuts short sides long top trend may be just what you need. This versatile style works for all hair types and face shapes, and can be customized to suit your personal taste. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about this popular haircut.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The guy haircuts short sides long top style features shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This contrast in length creates a bold and edgy look, while still being professional enough for the workplace. It's a great option for guys who want to add some personality to their hairstyle without going too extreme.

How To

If you're interested in getting a guy haircuts short sides long top style, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with this cut. Bring in a few pictures of the style you like, and talk to them about the length and texture you want. They'll be able to give you advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a guy haircuts short sides long top style:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any buildup or dirt, and make your hair easier to work with.
  2. Next, section off the top of your hair with clips or hair ties. This will help you focus on the sides and back first.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. A #2 or #3 guard is a good starting point, but you can go shorter or longer depending on your preference.
  4. Blend the sides and back into the top using scissors or clippers. You want a smooth transition from short to long hair.
  5. Unclip the top section of your hair and trim it to your desired length. You can leave it long and messy, or slick it back for a more polished look.
  6. Style your hair using a good quality pomade or wax. This will help keep it in place and add some texture.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your guy haircuts short sides long top style:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to maintain your new haircut at home.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Try out different styling techniques, such as slicking it back or parting it to the side, to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are answers to some common questions about guy haircuts short sides long top:

What face shapes work best with this style?

This style works well with most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on guys with square or oval faces.

What hair types work best with this style?

This style works well with all hair types, but it's particularly easy to style if you have thick or wavy hair.

How often do I need to get it trimmed?

You'll want to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your guy haircuts short sides long top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the guy haircuts short sides long top style with confidence. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle or something that's a bit more daring, this cut is a great option.
