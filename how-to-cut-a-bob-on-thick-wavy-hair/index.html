How To Cut A Bob On Thick Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you may be wondering how to achieve the perfect bob haircut. A bob is a classic hairstyle that can work for any face shape and hair type, including thick wavy hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a bob on thick wavy hair, from preparation to finishing touches.

  2. Sectioning the Hair
  3. Cutting the Hair

  8. FAQs
  9. Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, it is important to prepare the tools and materials you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A fine-tooth comb
  • Hair clips or ties to section the hair
  • A spray bottle with water
  • A mirror, preferably a handheld one

It is also helpful to have a picture or an idea of the bob haircut you want to achieve. You can search for inspiration online or in magazines to find the right style for you.

Sectioning the Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb through it to remove any tangles, and part it in the middle or on one side, depending on your preference. Then, divide the hair into four sections:

  1. The top section, from the hairline to the crown of the head
  2. The left side section, from the top of the ear to the back of the head
  3. The right side section, from the top of the ear to the back of the head
  4. The back section, from the nape of the neck to the crown of the head

Secure each section with hair clips or ties to keep them separate.

Cutting the Hair

Start by cutting the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the bottom, comb it straight down, and cut it at the desired length. Repeat this process, working your way up to the crown of the head.

Then, move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from the back of the section, comb it straight down, and cut it at the same length as the back section. Repeat this process, working your way forward to the front of the section.

Finally, move on to the top section. Comb it straight up, and cut it at the desired length. To create a layered effect, you can cut the top section slightly shorter than the back and side sections.

Texturizing the Hair

If you have thick wavy hair, you may want to add some texture to your bob haircut. To do this, take small sections of hair and slide the scissors down the length of the hair, cutting into it at a slight angle. This will create a textured, layered effect.

Shaping the Hair

After you have cut and textured your hair, it is time to shape it. Start by combing through your hair to remove any tangles. Then, use your scissors to shape the hair around your face, cutting it at an angle to create a flattering frame.

Finishing Touches

Once you have shaped your hair, it is time to add some finishing touches. Check your hair in the mirror to ensure that the length and shape are even. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them with your scissors.

Tips for Cutting a Bob on Thick Wavy Hair

  • Start with a longer length, as you can always cut more off later
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Keep your hair damp while cutting, as this will make it easier to work with
  • Take your time, and don't rush the process

FAQs

How often should I cut my thick wavy hair?

You should aim to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair?

While it is possible to cut your own hair, it is recommended that you seek the help of a professional stylist, especially if you are cutting your hair for the first time.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

To keep your thick wavy hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for thick, wavy hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray

By following these tips and using this step-by-step guide, you can achieve a beautiful bob haircut on your thick wavy hair. Good luck!
