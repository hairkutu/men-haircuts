How To Pick Wedding Hair And Makeup

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Planning a wedding can be stressful, but one of the most fun parts is choosing your hair and makeup for the big day. Your wedding hair and makeup will be in all your photos, so it's important to choose something that you love and that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Here's everything you need to know about how to pick wedding hair and makeup.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

The first step in picking wedding hair and makeup is to consider your overall wedding style. Are you having a formal black-tie event, or a casual beach wedding? Your hair and makeup should complement your dress and the overall theme of your wedding.

Next, think about your personal style. Do you typically wear your hair up or down? Do you prefer bold makeup or a more natural look? You want to choose something that reflects your personality and makes you feel comfortable.

Consider the time of year and location of your wedding. If you're getting married in the summer, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will hold up in the heat. If you're getting married in a humid location, you may want to choose makeup that is sweat-proof.

Step by Step:

Here are the steps to follow when picking your wedding hair and makeup:

  1. Consider your wedding style.
  2. Think about your personal style.
  3. Consider the time of year and location of your wedding.
  4. Look through bridal magazines and websites for inspiration.
  5. Book a trial with a hair and makeup artist.
  6. Bring photos of hairstyles and makeup looks you like to your trial.
  7. Try on your wedding dress and accessories before your trial.
  8. Take photos of your hair and makeup during your trial.
  9. Make any necessary changes to your hair and makeup.
  10. Book your hair and makeup artist for your wedding day.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when picking your wedding hair and makeup:

  • Choose a hairstyle and makeup look that will flatter your face shape and skin tone.
  • Consider adding hair accessories or a veil to your hairstyle.
  • Don't make drastic changes to your hair or makeup right before your wedding day.
  • Remember to consider your bridesmaids' hair and makeup looks as well.
  • Book your hair and makeup artist early to ensure availability.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking wedding hair and makeup:

Should I do my own hair and makeup for my wedding?

It's up to you, but it's recommended to hire a professional hair and makeup artist. They have the expertise and products to ensure your hair and makeup will look flawless all day long.

How far in advance should I book my hair and makeup artist?

It's best to book your hair and makeup artist at least 6 months before your wedding date to ensure availability.

What should I bring to my hair and makeup trial?

Bring photos of hairstyles and makeup looks you like, as well as your wedding dress and accessories. This will give your hair and makeup artist a good idea of your overall bridal look.

Hair Care:

In the months leading up to your wedding, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means getting regular trims, using a deep conditioning mask once a week, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. You want your hair to be healthy and strong for your wedding day.

When it comes to picking your wedding hair and makeup, the most important thing is to choose something that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Follow these tips and you'll be sure to have a bridal look that you'll love for years to come.
