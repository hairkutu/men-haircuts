How To Stop Male Pattern Baldness

Written by Petter Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Male pattern baldness, also known as androgenic alopecia, is a common condition that affects men of all ages. It is characterized by a receding hairline and thinning of hair on the crown of the head. While genetics play a big role in male pattern baldness, there are ways to slow down or even stop the process. In this article, we will discuss how to stop male pattern baldness using natural remedies, medications, and lifestyle changes.

  2. Step by step guide
  3. Tips to prevent male pattern baldness

How to stop male pattern baldness

Male pattern baldness is caused by a hormone called dihydrotestosterone (DHT), which is a byproduct of testosterone. DHT shrinks hair follicles, making it difficult for hair to grow. Here are some ways to stop male pattern baldness:

1. Natural remedies

There are several natural remedies that you can try to stop male pattern baldness. One of the most popular remedies is saw palmetto, which is an herb that blocks the production of DHT. You can take saw palmetto supplements or use saw palmetto oil on your scalp. Other natural remedies include onion juice, aloe vera, and coconut oil.

2. Medications

There are two FDA-approved medications for male pattern baldness: minoxidil and finasteride. Minoxidil is a topical solution that you apply to your scalp twice a day. It helps to stimulate hair growth and prolong the growth phase of hair follicles. Finasteride is a prescription pill that works by blocking the production of DHT. It is only available by prescription and should only be taken under the supervision of a doctor.

3. Lifestyle changes

Your lifestyle can also play a big role in male pattern baldness. Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote hair growth. You should also avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can damage hair follicles. Stress can also contribute to male pattern baldness, so finding ways to manage stress, such as through exercise or meditation, can be helpful.

Step by step guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to stop male pattern baldness:

  1. Consult with a doctor to determine the cause of your male pattern baldness
  2. Try natural remedies such as saw palmetto, onion juice, aloe vera, and coconut oil
  3. Consider using FDA-approved medications such as minoxidil and finasteride
  4. Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  5. Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  6. Manage stress through exercise or meditation

Tips to prevent male pattern baldness

Here are some tips to help prevent male pattern baldness:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Avoid wearing hats or helmets for extended periods of time
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair

Frequently asked questions

Q: Can male pattern baldness be reversed?
A: While there is no cure for male pattern baldness, it can be reversed or slowed down with the use of medications or natural remedies.

Q: Can stress cause male pattern baldness?
A: Stress can contribute to male pattern baldness, as it can cause hair follicles to enter the resting phase, leading to hair loss.

Hair care for male pattern baldness

If you have male pattern baldness, it is important to take good care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of harsh chemicals
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow to hair follicles
  • Consider using a hair growth serum or supplement

By following these tips and treatments, you can help to stop male pattern baldness and promote hair growth. Remember to consult with a doctor before starting any new treatments or medications.
