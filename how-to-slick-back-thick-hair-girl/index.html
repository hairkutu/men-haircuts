How To Slick Back Thick Hair Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have thick hair that's difficult to manage? Slicking it back is a great solution for a sleek, polished look. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back thick hair girl in a few easy steps. From choosing the perfect product to maintaining your style, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Product
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Slicking Back Your Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Success
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Choosing the Right Product

The first step to slicking back your thick hair is choosing the right product. Look for a strong-hold gel or pomade that won't weigh down your hair. Avoid products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Some great options include:

  • Hair gel
  • Pomade
  • Hair wax
  • Hair spray

Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

Prepping Your Hair

Before you slick back your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Then, blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting until it's completely dry. If your hair is prone to frizz, apply a small amount of anti-frizz serum to your hair.

Slicking Back Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to slick it back. Follow these steps:

  1. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and rub them together.
  2. Starting at the crown of your head, work the product through your hair in a downward motion.
  3. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting at your hairline and working your way back.
  4. Continue to comb your hair back until you achieve your desired style.

Finishing Touches

Once your hair is slicked back, use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to set your style in place. Then, apply a small amount of product to any flyaways or frizz. Finally, use a hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb for a sleek, polished look.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent frizz.
  • Try different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair. Use a strong-hold gel or pomade and a fine-tooth comb to tame your curls and achieve a sleek look.

How often should I wash my hair if I'm slicking it back?

You can wash your hair as often as you normally would. However, using a clarifying shampoo once a week can help remove any buildup from styling products.

What should I do if my hair is too thick to slick back?

If your hair is extremely thick, consider getting a layered haircut to make it more manageable. You can also thin out your hair with thinning shears or a razor.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients.

Now that you know how to slick back thick hair girl, you can rock this stylish look with confidence. With the right product and technique, you can achieve a sleek, polished style that will turn heads wherever you go.
