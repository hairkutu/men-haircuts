How To Activate My Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 16, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you may be wondering how to activate your curls and achieve your best hair day yet. Luckily, there are a few things you can do to bring out your natural curls and keep them looking their best. Here’s everything you need to know.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

How to Activate Your Curls

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of how to activate your curls, it’s important to understand what causes curly hair to lose its shape in the first place. Often, curly hair becomes frizzy or limp due to damage or lack of moisture. This can be caused by heat styling, chemical treatments, or using the wrong hair products.

To activate your curls, you need to address these underlying issues and give your hair the TLC it needs. Here are a few steps you can take to bring out your natural curls:

1. Start with a Clarifying Shampoo

Over time, hair products and natural oils can build up on your hair, leaving it looking dull and lifeless. To give your curls a fresh start, use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

2. Use a Moisturizing Conditioner

Curly hair needs moisture to stay bouncy and defined. Look for a conditioner with hydrating ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil to help nourish your curls.

3. Apply a Curl-Activating Product

There are many products on the market designed to activate and define curls. Look for a product that works with your hair type and texture, whether you have loose waves or tight coils.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once you’ve applied your curl-activating product, use your hands to scrunch your hair upward from the ends. This will help encourage your curls to form and create more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to activate your curls, let’s walk through the process step-by-step:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Rinse thoroughly and follow up with a moisturizing conditioner.

Step 2: Apply Your Curl-Activating Product

While your hair is still damp, apply your curl-activating product of choice. Work the product through your hair from root to tip, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair upward from the ends, encouraging your curls to form. Continue scrunching until your hair is dry.

Step 4: Fluff and Go

Once your hair is dry, fluff it gently with your fingers to create more volume. Then, style as desired and enjoy your bouncy, defined curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Once you’ve activated your curls, it’s important to take steps to maintain them. Here are a few tips to help keep your curls looking their best:

1. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your curls and cause them to lose their shape. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and breakage, which can lead to frizz and limp curls. Switch to a silk pillowcase to help keep your curls looking smooth and defined.

3. Refresh Your Curls

As the day goes on, your curls may start to lose their shape. Keep a curl-refreshing spray on hand to give your curls a boost throughout the day.

FAQs

Q: Can I activate my curls without using products?

A: While products can be helpful in activating and defining curls, you may be able to achieve similar results using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Experiment with different techniques to see what works best for your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and texture, but in general, it’s best to wash curly hair no more than once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage.

Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

Choosing the right hair care products is key to maintaining healthy, defined curls. Here are a few products to consider:

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

This curl-enhancing cream is made with natural ingredients like coconut oil and hibiscus flower extract to help define and hydrate curls.

2. DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler

This lightweight styling cream is designed to fight frizz and enhance curls without weighing them down.

3. Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Moisturizing Curl Activator Cream

Formulated with shea butter and natural oils, this curl activator cream can help define and moisturize curls for a soft, bouncy finish.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can activate your curls and keep them looking their best. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair, and enjoy your beautiful, defined curls!
