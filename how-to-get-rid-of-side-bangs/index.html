How To Get Rid Of Side Bangs A Step By Step Guide

If you're tired of your side bangs and want to try a new look, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting rid of side bangs, including how to do it, tips on styling your hair after, and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and happy.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Rid of Side Bangs

There are a few different ways to get rid of side bangs, depending on how drastic of a change you want to make. Here are some options:

Cut Them Off

The most straightforward option is to simply cut off your side bangs. This can be done at home with a pair of scissors, but we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure an even cut. If you're not ready to commit to a full chop, you can gradually trim your bangs shorter over time until they blend in with the rest of your hair.

Sweep Them to the Side

If you're not quite ready to part with your side bangs, you can try sweeping them to the side instead. This will give you a different look without completely getting rid of your bangs. To do this, use a round brush and blow-dry your bangs to the side. Then, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks and finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Pull Them Back

If you want to completely hide your side bangs, you can pull them back with bobby pins or a headband. This is a great option if you're growing out your bangs or just want to change up your look for a day.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to cut off your side bangs, here's a step-by-step guide to help you do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs from the rest of your hair.
  3. Use sharp scissors to trim your bangs to the desired length. Start with a little bit at a time and gradually cut more if needed.
  4. Blend your bangs into the rest of your hair by cutting at an angle.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Your Hair After

Now that you've gotten rid of your side bangs, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Experiment with different parts to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Try using a volumizing spray or mousse to add body to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair or add some waves with a curling iron.
  • Consider getting a new haircut to complement your new look.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow out side bangs?

It can take anywhere from a few months to a year to grow out side bangs, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long your bangs are.

Can I cut my bangs myself?

While it's possible to cut your bangs at home, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure an even cut.

Will cutting my side bangs damage my hair?

Cutting your hair, including your side bangs, will not damage your hair as long as you use sharp scissors and do not cut too much at once.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you've changed your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and happy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can say goodbye to your side bangs and hello to a new hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
