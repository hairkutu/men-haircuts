How Long Is An 8 Guard Haircut

Written by Daniel Jul 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you wondering how long an 8 guard haircut is? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll discuss what an 8 guard haircut is, how to achieve it, and some tips on how to care for your hair after the cut.

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of an 8 guard haircut, let’s define what a guard is. A guard is a clipper attachment that determines the length of the hair being cut. The number on the guard corresponds to the length of the hair in eighths of an inch. So, an 8 guard will cut hair to a length of one inch.

How to Achieve an 8 Guard Haircut

If you’re looking to get an 8 guard haircut, you’ll need to have clippers with an 8 guard attachment. Here are the steps to achieve the cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Attach the 8 guard to your clippers.
  3. Begin at the sides and work your way up to the top of the head, using the clippers to cut the hair to a uniform length.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.
  5. Blend the top of the hair into the sides with scissors or clippers, depending on your preference.
  6. Finish the cut with a neckline trim and clean up any stray hairs.

Tips for an 8 Guard Haircut

Now that you know how to achieve an 8 guard haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your clippers are well-maintained and oiled before using them.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help or consult a professional if you’re unsure.
  • Consider using a higher guard number if you want a longer cut.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure a uniform cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about an 8 guard haircut:

How long is an 8 guard haircut?

An 8 guard haircut is one inch in length.

What other guard sizes are available?

Guard sizes range from 0 (1/16 inch) to 8 (1 inch), with many sizes in between.

Can I achieve an 8 guard haircut on my own?

Yes, you can achieve an 8 guard haircut at home with the proper tools and techniques.

Hair Care

After getting an 8 guard haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a good quality hair brush or comb to style your hair.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how long an 8 guard haircut is, how to achieve it, and how to care for your hair afterwards, you can confidently rock this classic hairstyle. Remember to take your time, use the proper tools, and consult a professional if needed. Happy clipping!
