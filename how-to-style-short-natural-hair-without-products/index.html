How To Style Short Natural Hair Without Products

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short natural hair, you may be wondering how to style it without using any products. While products can be helpful for achieving certain styles, there are many ways to style your hair without them. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for styling short natural hair without products.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Natural Hair Without Products
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair Without Products
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

How to Style Short Natural Hair Without Products

There are many ways to style short natural hair without using any products. Here are some ideas:

Braids or Twists

Braids or twists are a great way to style short natural hair without products. You can do small or large braids or twists, and you can do them all over your head or just in certain sections. To do this, section off your hair and braid or twist each section. You can leave them in for a few days or take them out right away for a crimped look.

Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another great way to style short natural hair without products. To do this, section off your hair and twist each section into a small knot. You can do this all over your head or just in certain sections. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight, then take them out for a cute curly style.

Pineapple Updo

The pineapple updo is a great way to style short natural hair without products. To do this, gather all of your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave the ends out or tuck them in for a sleeker look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide for styling short natural hair without products:

  1. Decide on the style you want to do.
  2. Section off your hair if necessary.
  3. Braid, twist, or knot each section as desired.
  4. Leave in for a few hours or overnight.
  5. Take out braids, twists, or knots for a curly style.
  6. Style as desired.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair Without Products

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short natural hair without products:

  • Start with clean, moisturized hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Section off your hair to make styling easier.
  • Don’t pull your hair too tight when braiding, twisting, or knotting.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I style short natural hair without products?

Yes, you can definitely style short natural hair without using any products. Braids, twists, knots, and updos are all great options.

How do I keep my short natural hair moisturized?

Keeping your hair moisturized is important for maintaining healthy hair. You can use a leave-in conditioner, oil, or butter to keep your hair moisturized. You can also try the LOC method (liquid, oil, cream) to seal in moisture.

How often should I wash my short natural hair?

Everyone’s hair is different, so there’s no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. Some people wash their hair once a week, while others wash it every other week. Pay attention to how your hair feels and adjust your washing routine accordingly.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of short natural hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Styling short natural hair without products is easy once you know what styles work best for you. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair. Don’t forget to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and keeping it moisturized.
