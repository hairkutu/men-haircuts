Short Hair For Round Face 2022 How To Choose The Perfect Style

Are you ready to change up your hairstyle in 2023? If you have a round face and are considering a short haircut, there are several things to keep in mind. In this article, we will explore the best short haircuts for round faces in 2022, as well as tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut for a Round Face

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are a few things to consider:

1. Face Shape

Consider the shape of your face. Round faces are typically wider in the middle and have soft, curved edges. The goal of a short haircut is to create angles and add height to your face. Look for styles that have layers, spikes or textured ends to add dimension.

2. Hair Texture

Consider the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement. If you have thick hair, consider a blunt cut with textured ends to thin out your hair and create a more flattering shape.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle. Are you low-maintenance or do you enjoy styling your hair every day? Choose a style that suits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Once you have chosen the perfect short haircut for your round face, follow these steps to style it:

1. Wash and Condition

Start with clean, damp hair. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to protect it from damage caused by heat styling tools.

3. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a smooth, polished look.

4. Style

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron, curling iron or texturizing spray to add texture and shape to your hair. Play around with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Follow these tips to keep your short haircut looking great:

1. Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray to add body.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant and avoiding excessive heat styling.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Round Faces

What short haircuts are best for round faces?

The best short haircuts for round faces are those that add height and angles to your face. Look for styles with layers, spikes or textured ends.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a round face?

A pixie cut can be flattering for a round face if it is styled correctly. Look for a pixie cut with longer bangs and layers at the crown to add height to your face.

How do I know if a short haircut will suit my face?

Consult with your stylist to determine which short haircut will flatter your face shape and hair texture. Bring in pictures of styles you like to help guide the conversation.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Follow these tips to keep your short haircut looking great:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut that flatters your round face in 2023. Remember to choose a style that suits your face shape, hair texture and lifestyle, and maintain your new look with regular trims and the right products.
