Does Hair Grow Back After Weight Loss Surgery

Written by Frank May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Weight loss surgery has become a popular solution for people who want to lose weight quickly and effectively. However, one of the side effects of weight loss surgery that many people don't know about is hair loss. This can be a distressing experience for individuals who have already undergone a significant physical transformation. In this article, we will explore the question "does hair grow back after weight loss surgery?" and provide tips for hair care after surgery.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prevent Hair Loss After Weight Loss Surgery
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Hair Care After Surgery
  • Tips for Hair Care After Weight Loss Surgery
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Weight Loss Surgery

The first step in preventing hair loss after weight loss surgery is to ensure that you are getting enough nutrients in your diet. A diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals will promote healthy hair growth. You can also take supplements that are specifically designed to promote hair growth, such as biotin and iron.

Another way to prevent hair loss is to avoid rapid weight loss. Losing weight too quickly can put stress on your body, which can lead to hair loss. It's important to work with your doctor to develop a weight loss plan that is safe and sustainable.

Types of Weight Loss Surgery

There are several types of weight loss surgery, including gastric bypass, gastric sleeve, and laparoscopic adjustable gastric banding. Each type of surgery has its own risks and benefits, and your doctor can help you determine which type of surgery is best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hair Care After Surgery

After weight loss surgery, it's important to take care of your hair to promote healthy growth. Here is a step-by-step guide for hair care after surgery:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid pulling or tugging.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  4. Eat a diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Consider using hair growth supplements, such as biotin and iron.

Tips for Hair Care After Weight Loss Surgery

Here are some additional tips for hair care after weight loss surgery:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, which can pull on your hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes and perms, which can also cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair.
  • If you are experiencing significant hair loss, talk to your doctor about potential treatments, such as hair transplants or medications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does hair grow back after weight loss surgery?

A: Yes, hair can grow back after weight loss surgery. However, it may take several months for your hair to return to its normal growth cycle.

Q: How long does hair loss last after weight loss surgery?

A: Hair loss after weight loss surgery can last for several months to a year. However, with proper hair care and nutrition, you can promote healthy hair growth.

Q: Can hair loss after weight loss surgery be prevented?

A: Yes, hair loss after weight loss surgery can be prevented by maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding rapid weight loss, and taking hair growth supplements.


In conclusion, hair loss after weight loss surgery is a common side effect that can be distressing for many individuals. However, with proper hair care and nutrition, hair can grow back after surgery. If you are experiencing significant hair loss, talk to your doctor about potential treatments and solutions.
