Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair Over 50 Tips How To And Hair Care

If you have medium length curly hair and are over 50, you may be wondering what hairstyles are best for you. The good news is that there are plenty of options that can flatter your face shape, enhance your natural curls, and make you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll explore some popular hairstyles for medium length curly hair over 50, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for your locks.

How to Style Medium Length Curly Hair Over 50

Styling medium length curly hair over 50 can be a challenge, but there are ways to make it work. Here are some tips:

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your locks soft and healthy.
  2. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while your hair is wet.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream, to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their natural shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to set your style and add shine.

Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair Over 50

Now that you know how to style your curly hair, let's explore some popular hairstyles for medium length hair over 50:

Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers can be a great choice for medium length curly hair over 50. This style adds volume and movement to your curls, while still keeping them manageable.

Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs can be a stylish option for medium length curly hair. This look can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and can be worn straight or curly.

Side-Swept Bob

A side-swept bob can be a sophisticated choice for medium length curly hair over 50. This look features a deep side part and a sleek, angled bob that frames your face and accentuates your curls.

Layered Lob

A layered lob, or long bob, can be a trendy and youthful option for medium length curly hair over 50. This style features longer layers in the front and shorter layers in the back, creating a flattering shape that enhances your natural curls.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling and maintaining your medium length curly hair:

  1. Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  2. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles while you sleep.
  3. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  4. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  5. Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair texture and curl pattern.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your curly hair. However, be sure to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and consider using a semi-permanent or demi-permanent dye to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair texture and how oily your scalp is. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to shampoo more frequently. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair Over 50

In addition to styling tips, here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length curly hair healthy:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can dry out your curls.
  3. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls soft and moisturized.
  4. Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  5. Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as relaxers or perms, which can damage your hair.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can rock your medium length curly hair over 50 with confidence and style.
