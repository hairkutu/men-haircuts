Low Drop Fade Haircut Curly Hair How To Get The Look

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair looking unkempt and unruly? Do you want a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great? Look no further than the low drop fade haircut for curly hair. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for those with curly hair who want a clean and stylish look. In this article, we'll go over how to get the look, step by step, and provide some tips and tricks for maintaining your curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get the Look

The low drop fade haircut is a popular hairstyle for men with curly hair. It features a short fade on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled in loose curls. To get the look, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Here are some tips for getting the perfect low drop fade haircut:

Choose the Right Barber or Stylist

When it comes to cutting curly hair, not all barbers or stylists are created equal. Look for someone who has experience working with curly hair and can provide you with the right cut and style. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or do some research online to find a stylist in your area.

Know What You Want

Before you sit down in the barber chair, it's important to have a clear idea of what you want. Bring in a picture of the low drop fade haircut you like and discuss it with your stylist. They can help you determine if the style is right for you and make any necessary adjustments to fit your face shape and hair type.

Communicate with Your Barber or Stylist

Effective communication is key to getting the perfect haircut. Make sure you communicate your needs and preferences to your stylist. Let them know if you prefer a shorter or longer style, and how much you want them to take off the sides and back.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieving the low drop fade haircut for curly hair:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Before getting your haircut, it's important to cleanse your hair thoroughly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to help maintain its shape and texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess moisture. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage to your curls.

Step 3: Get Your Hair Cut

Visit your barber or stylist and get your hair cut to your desired length and style. Make sure to communicate your needs and preferences to your stylist to ensure you get the perfect cut.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your haircut, it's time to style your hair. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a curl defining cream or gel, and work it through your hair with your fingers. Style your hair in loose curls on top, and use a wide-tooth comb to shape and define your curls.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining your low drop fade haircut for curly hair:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to maintaining your curly hair. Look for products specifically designed for curly hair that will help define and enhance your curls without weighing them down.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on your curly hair. These tools can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to frizz and breakage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important for maintaining the shape and health of your hair. Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

What is a low drop fade haircut?

A low drop fade haircut features a short fade on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled in loose curls.

Is the low drop fade haircut suitable for curly hair?

Yes, the low drop fade haircut is a great option for those with curly hair. It helps to define and enhance your curls, while still maintaining a clean and stylish look.

What products should I use to maintain my curly hair?

Look for products specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl defining creams or gels. These products will help define and enhance your curls without weighing them down.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy curly hair requires a little extra care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair healthy and looking great:

Avoid Over-Shampooing

Shampooing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Try to limit your shampooing to once or twice a week, and use a gentle shampoo specifically designed for curly hair.

Moisturize Regularly

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to moisturize regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your curly hair can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and shape your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect low drop fade haircut for curly hair and maintain healthy, beautiful curls. Remember to communicate with your barber or stylist, use the right products, and take care of your hair regularly to keep it looking great.
