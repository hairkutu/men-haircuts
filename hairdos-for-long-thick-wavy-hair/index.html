Hairdos For Long Thick Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Mar 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long, thick, and wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. Fortunately, we've compiled a list of the best hairdos for long, thick, wavy hair that will help you achieve the look you want. Whether you're going for a casual look or a formal one, these hairstyles are sure to impress.

Description

Long, thick, wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it's beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. That's why it's important to find the right hairstyle that works for you. Whether you have a special occasion coming up or just want to switch up your everyday look, these hairdos for long, thick, wavy hair are perfect for any occasion.

How to

Before diving into the hairdos, it's important to know how to properly care for long, thick, wavy hair. This type of hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz, so it's important to use hydrating and nourishing hair products. Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

When it comes to styling, it's best to work with your natural texture. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair over time. Instead, embrace your natural waves and curls by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer or scrunching your hair with a styling product.

Step by Step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

Start by separating the top half of your hair from the bottom. Secure the top section with a hair tie or clip. Take a small section of hair from the top and twist it, then secure it with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side. Release the rest of your hair and tousle it for a casual look.

2. Braided Crown

Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it. Secure the braid with a bobby pin at the back of your head. Repeat on the other side. Tuck the ends of the braids under each other to create a crown-like effect.

3. Messy Bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail, leaving a few pieces out in the front. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins. Tousle the front pieces for a messy, effortless look.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

- Invest in a quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated throughout the day.

FAQ

What products should I use for long, thick, wavy hair?

It's important to use hydrating and nourishing hair products, such as a deep conditioning treatment, a leave-in conditioner, and a styling cream or gel. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter, avocado oil, and coconut oil.

How often should I wash my long, thick, wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and how often you style your hair. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and damage. However, if your hair tends to get oily, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for long, thick, wavy hair. In addition to using hydrating and nourishing hair products, it's important to avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools. Instead, embrace your natural texture and use styling products that enhance your waves and curls. Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
