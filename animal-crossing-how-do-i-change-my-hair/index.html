Animal Crossing How Do I Change My Hair

Written by Bardi May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle in Animal Crossing? Want to switch things up and try out a new look? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be walking you through the steps to change your hair in Animal Crossing. From how to access the hair salon to tips on maintaining your new 'do, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Accessing the Hair Salon
  2. Choosing a Hairstyle
  3. Hair Colors
  4. Accessories
  5. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  6. FAQ

Accessing the Hair Salon

The first step to changing your hair in Animal Crossing is to visit Harriet the poodle at Shampoodle's salon. You'll need to have played for at least seven days and have at least one other human character living on your island before Harriet will set up shop. Once she does, head on over to Main Street and enter the salon.

Harriet will greet you and ask if you'd like to change your hair. Select yes, and she'll take you to the back room where the magic happens.

Choosing a Hairstyle

Now that you're in the back room, it's time to choose a new hairstyle. Harriet will ask you a series of questions to help narrow down your options. These questions include:

  • What kind of look are you going for?
  • Do you want your hair short or long?
  • Do you want bangs?
  • Do you want your hair to be curly or straight?

Answer these questions honestly, and Harriet will present you with a selection of hairstyles that fit your criteria. Take your time browsing through them and choose the one that speaks to you.

Hair Colors

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to pick a hair color. Harriet will present you with a selection of colors to choose from. If you don't see the color you want, don't worry. You can unlock more hair colors by spending 10,000 bells at the salon.

Accessories

If you want to take your new hairstyle to the next level, you can also purchase accessories at the salon. These include hats, glasses, and hairpins. These accessories are purely cosmetic and won't affect your character's abilities in any way.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you have a fabulous new hairstyle, you'll want to make sure it stays looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Don't overuse the hair salon. You can only change your hair once per day, so make sure you're happy with your new look before leaving the salon.
  • Avoid getting your hair wet. If you go swimming or get caught in the rain, your hair will revert to its previous style.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head. Sometimes, hairstyles can look different from the back than they do from the front.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hair as many times as I want?

No, you can only change your hair once per day. Make sure you're happy with your new look before leaving the salon.

Can I change my hair color without changing my hairstyle?

Yes, you can change your hair color without changing your hairstyle.

Do I have to pay to change my hair?

Yes, changing your hair costs 3,000 bells.

With these steps, you'll be on your way to a fabulous new look in Animal Crossing. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and colors!
