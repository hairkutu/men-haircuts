How To Make Thick Hair Soft For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you a guy with thick hair that's difficult to manage and feels coarse to the touch? If so, you're not alone. Many men struggle with making their hair softer, but luckily, there are a few things you can do to achieve the soft, silky hair you desire. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make thick hair soft for guys.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can give you a full head of hair and a stylish look, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Thick hair tends to be more coarse and less pliable than other hair types, which can make it feel rough and prickly. However, with a few simple changes to your hair care routine, you can soften your hair and make it more manageable.

How to Make Thick Hair Soft for Guys

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

One of the most important things you can do to make your hair softer is to use a gentle shampoo. Shampoos that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Look for a shampoo that's specifically designed for thick hair and is sulfate-free. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair is another important step in making it softer. After shampooing, apply a conditioner that's designed for thick hair. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it more manageable.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

In addition to using a regular conditioner, you can also use a leave-in conditioner to make your hair softer. Leave-in conditioners are designed to be left in your hair after washing, and they can help to moisturize and soften your hair throughout the day.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

If you want to make your hair softer, it's important to avoid heat styling as much as possible. Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

5. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brushing your hair regularly is another important step in making it softer. Brushing helps to distribute your hair's natural oils, which can help to moisturize and soften your hair. Use a brush with natural bristles and start at the ends of your hair, working your way up to the roots.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose a gentle shampoo

Look for a shampoo that's specifically designed for thick hair and is sulfate-free. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

Step 2: Condition your hair

Apply a conditioner that's designed for thick hair. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it more manageable.

Step 3: Use a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing. This will help to moisturize and soften your hair throughout the day.

Step 4: Avoid heat styling

Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

Step 5: Brush your hair regularly

Use a brush with natural bristles and start at the ends of your hair, working your way up to the roots.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your thick hair softer:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair mask to make my hair softer?

A: Yes, hair masks can be a great way to moisturize and soften your hair. Look for a hair mask that's specifically designed for thick hair and use it once a week.

Q: Can I use a hair oil to make my hair softer?

A: Yes, hair oils can be a great way to moisturize and soften your hair. Look for a hair oil that's designed for thick hair and apply a small amount to the ends of your hair.

Q: How long will it take to make my hair softer?

A: It may take a few weeks to notice a difference in the texture of your hair. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you should start to see results.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it soft and healthy. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause damage.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that are too tight, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thick hair softer and more manageable. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you should start to see results in no time!
