Does Middle Parting Suit Oval Faces

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to switch things up? One of the most popular hairstyles that have been trending lately is the middle parting. However, you might be wondering if this would suit your oval face shape. Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will be discussing whether middle parting suits oval faces or not.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Oval face shapes are said to be the most versatile as they can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, when it comes to middle parting, it can be a hit or miss. Some oval-shaped faces can look great with a middle parting, while others may not. It all depends on the individual's hair texture, length, and personal preference.

How to

To determine if a middle parting suits your oval face, you need to consider a few things:

  • Hair Texture: Middle parting suits best for those who have straight or slightly wavy hair.
  • Hair Length: The length of your hair also matters when it comes to middle parting. If you have long hair, a middle parting can look great. However, if you have short hair, you might want to consider other options.
  • Personal Preference: Ultimately, it comes down to what you feel comfortable with. If you like the way you look with a middle parting, then go for it!

Step by Step

If you've decided that a middle parting suits your oval face, here's how you can achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Dry your hair with a hairdryer or let it air-dry naturally.
  3. Apply some heat protectant and straighten your hair with a flat iron.
  4. Part your hair in the middle using a comb.
  5. Apply some hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're planning to try a middle parting:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, clips, and barrettes to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • If you have bangs, you can still try a middle parting. Just make sure to style your bangs to the side instead of parting it down the middle.
  • If you have curly hair, you can still try a middle parting. Just make sure to define your curls with a curling cream or gel.

FAQ

Q: Can a middle parting make my face look longer?

A: It's possible. If you have an oval-shaped face, a middle parting can make your face appear longer. However, this varies from person to person, and it ultimately depends on your hair length and texture.

Q: Can middle parting cause hair damage?

A: Middle parting doesn't necessarily cause hair damage. However, excessive heat styling and using hair products can damage your hair over time. Make sure to use a heat protectant and limit the use of hair products.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and luscious look. Make sure to wash your hair regularly, use a conditioner, and avoid excessive heat styling. Additionally, incorporate a hair mask into your hair care routine once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, whether a middle parting suits your oval face or not depends on your hair texture, length, and personal preference. If you feel confident and comfortable with a middle parting, then go for it! Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles.
