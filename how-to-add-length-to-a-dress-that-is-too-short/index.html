How To Add Length To A Dress That Is Too Short

Written by Luffy Sep 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a favorite dress that you love to wear but it's just too short? Don't worry, you don't have to give up on it just yet. Here's a guide on how to add length to a dress that is too short.

Description

Adding length to a dress can be done in various ways. Some methods are easy, while others require a bit of sewing skills. The goal is to make your dress look longer without compromising its style and shape.

How to Add Length to a Dress That is Too Short

Here are some ways to add length to your dress:

1. Add a Hemline

The easiest way to add length to a dress is to add a hemline. You can do this by sewing a strip of fabric to the bottom of your dress. Make sure to choose a fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress.

2. Add a Lace Trim

If you're feeling creative, you can add a lace trim to the bottom of your dress. This will not only add length to your dress but also give it a new look. You can use a contrasting color or a matching color lace trim.

3. Add a Tiered Skirt

You can also add a tiered skirt to your dress. This will give your dress a bohemian look and add length to it. You can choose to make the tiers out of the same fabric or a contrasting fabric.

4. Add a Ruffle Hem

If you're into the romantic look, you can add a ruffle hem to your dress. This will not only add length but also give your dress a feminine touch.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to add length to your dress:

Step 1: Measure the Length You Need to Add

Measure the length you need to add to your dress. Make sure to add an extra inch for the hemline or the trim you'll be adding.

Step 2: Choose Your Fabric or Trim

Choose the fabric or trim you want to add to your dress. Make sure to choose a fabric or trim that matches the color and texture of your dress.

Step 3: Cut Your Fabric or Trim

Cut your fabric or trim according to the length you need to add. Make sure to add an extra inch for the hemline or the seam allowance.

Step 4: Sew Your Fabric or Trim

Sew your fabric or trim to the bottom of your dress. Make sure to sew it evenly and straight.

Step 5: Hem Your Dress

Hem your dress by folding the fabric or trim under and sewing it in place. Make sure to do this evenly and straight.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when adding length to your dress:

  • Choose a fabric or trim that matches the color and texture of your dress.
  • Measure the length you need to add carefully.
  • Sew your fabric or trim evenly and straight.
  • Hem your dress evenly and straight.
  • Try on your dress before hemming to make sure you're happy with the length.

FAQ

Q: Can I add length to a dress that is too short without sewing?

A: Yes, you can add length to a dress without sewing by wearing leggings or tights underneath it. You can also wear a long cardigan or a vest over it.

Q: Can I add length to a dress that is too short by cutting it?

A: It's not recommended to cut your dress to add length as it can alter its shape and style.

Hair Care

When adding length to your dress, don't forget about your hair. Try a new hairstyle that complements your dress. You can also accessorize with a hair clip or a headband.

Now that you know how to add length to a dress that is too short, you can save your favorite dress from being retired. With a little creativity and sewing skills, you can give your dress a new look and make it work for you.
