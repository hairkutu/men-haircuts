Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by gently brushing the edges with a soft-bristled brush or a toothbrush.
  2. Apply a small amount of edge control or gel to the edges and smooth down with your fingers or a brush.
  3. Use a scarf or headband to hold the edges down while the product dries.
  4. Once the edges are dry, remove the scarf or headband and style as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help maintain edges with crochet braids:

  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on the edges, as this can weigh down the hair and cause breakage.

  • Protect the edges at night by wearing a satin bonnet or using a satin pillowcase.
  • If you notice any signs of breakage or thinning, take a break from crochet braids and focus on hair care and growth.

Hair Care for Crochet Braids

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy edges with crochet braids. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize regularly with a lightweight leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on the scalp, as this can cause buildup and clog pores

  • Take breaks between styles to allow the hair to rest and recover
