Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle? The hairstyle short sides long top male is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been in trend for a long time and is still popular among men of all ages. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips to maintain it, and much more. So, let's get started.

Description

The hairstyle short sides long top male is a haircut where the sides are trimmed short or shaved, while the top is left long. The length of the top hair can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. It is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance but stylish haircut.

How to Get the Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top Male?

To get this hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Length

The first step is to choose the length of your top hair. You can go for a short or medium length, or you can opt for a longer length if you want a more dramatic look. Discuss your preferences with your barber or stylist, and they will guide you through the process.

Step 2: Trim the Sides

The next step is to trim the sides. You can either shave them or trim them short with clippers. The length of the sides can vary depending on your preference, but usually, it is around 1-2 inches.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the sides are trimmed, it's time to style the top hair. You can use pomade, wax, or gel to style it in any way you like. Slick it back for a classic look, or tousle it for a more textured appearance.

Tips to Maintain the Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top Male?

Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking great:

Tip 1: Trim the Sides Regularly

As the sides are trimmed short, they tend to grow faster than the top. Trim them regularly to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use the right products to style your hair. Pomade, wax, or gel can be used to create different looks, but make sure to choose the one that suits your hair type.

Tip 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair in good condition.

FAQ

Q1: Is the Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top Male Suitable for All Face Shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length of the top hair may vary depending on your face shape. Consult with your barber or stylist to find the right length for you.

Q2: Can I Style the Top Hair in Different Ways?

A: Yes, you can style the top hair in different ways. You can slick it back, comb it over, or create a messy textured look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Choose products that are suitable for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons. They can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. Regular trims also promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the hairstyle short sides long top male is a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for men of all ages. With the right styling products and proper hair care, you can maintain this hairstyle and keep it looking great. Consult with your barber or stylist to find the right length and style for you.
