Is Permed Hair In Style For 2021

Written by Bardi Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle this year, you may be wondering if permed hair is still in style. The answer is yes! Permed hair is definitely making a comeback in 2021, and there are a variety of ways to rock this look. In this article, we'll take a closer look at permed hair, how to get it, and some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls.

Description

Permed hair, also known as a permanent wave, is a chemical process that adds curls or waves to straight hair. This process involves applying a perm solution to the hair, which breaks down the hair's protein structure and reshapes it into curls or waves. The result is a long-lasting, low-maintenance hairstyle that can add volume and texture to your hair.

How to Perm Your Hair

If you're interested in perming your hair, it's important to find a reputable stylist who has experience with this process. The stylist will start by washing your hair to remove any product buildup, then section your hair and wrap it with perming solution and rods. The solution will then be left on your hair for a set amount of time, typically 15-30 minutes, before being rinsed out and neutralized. Once the neutralizer is applied, the rods will be removed, and your hair will be washed and styled.

Step by Step Guide to Perming Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to perming your hair:

  1. Find a reputable stylist who has experience with perming.
  2. Wash your hair to remove any product buildup.
  3. Section your hair and wrap it with perming solution and rods.
  4. Leave the solution on your hair for 15-30 minutes.
  5. Rinse out the solution and apply neutralizer.
  6. Remove the rods and wash your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Permed Hair

Once you've permed your hair, there are some tips to keep in mind to make sure your curls stay healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after perming to give your curls time to set.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your curly shape.

FAQ

Is permed hair suitable for all hair types?

Permed hair can work for most hair types, but it's essential to work with a stylist who has experience with your hair type. Fine or thin hair may not hold a perm as well as thicker hair, so your stylist may recommend a different technique or solution.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you take care of your curls.

Can you color permed hair?

Yes, you can color permed hair, but it's essential to wait at least two weeks after perming to allow your hair to recover. You should also work with a stylist who has experience coloring permed hair to ensure the best results.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

It's essential to take care of your permed hair to keep your curls healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products with alcohol, as they can dry out your curls.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

Conclusion

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, long-lasting hairstyle, permed hair may be the perfect choice for you. With the right stylist and proper care, your curls can stay healthy and beautiful for months to come. Whether you opt for loose waves or tight curls, permed hair is definitely in style for 2021.
