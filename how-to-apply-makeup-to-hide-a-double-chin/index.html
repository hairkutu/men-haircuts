How To Apply Makeup To Hide A Double Chin

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a double chin that you want to hide? Makeup can be a great solution! In this article, we will show you how to apply makeup to hide a double chin. We will cover everything from how to choose the right products to how to apply them.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to hiding a double chin with makeup is to choose the right products. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone
  • Use a concealer that is one shade lighter than your foundation
  • Choose a contour shade that is a few shades darker than your foundation

Once you have your products, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Apply your foundation all over your face, including your neck. Blend it well with a makeup sponge or brush.
  2. Apply your concealer under your chin and blend it well.
  3. Apply your contour shade under your chin, along your jawline, and on the sides of your neck. Blend it well.
  4. Apply a highlighter on top of your cheekbones, down the center of your nose, and on your cupid's bow to draw attention away from your chin.
  5. Set your makeup with a setting spray or powder.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Make sure to blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a powder contour instead of a cream contour if you have oily skin.
  • Apply your makeup in natural light to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don't forget to blend your makeup down your neck to avoid any harsh lines.

FAQ

What causes a double chin?

A double chin can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, weight gain, and aging.

Can makeup really hide a double chin?

Yes, makeup can be a great way to hide a double chin. It won't make it disappear completely, but it can help to make it less noticeable.

What other products can I use to hide a double chin?

Other products that can help to hide a double chin include neck creams, face masks, and facial exercises.

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in the appearance of your double chin. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Try a side-swept bang to draw attention away from your chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight, as they can create a double chin.
  • Consider getting layers to create movement and volume around your face.

By following these tips and using the right makeup products, you can hide your double chin and feel more confident in your appearance.
