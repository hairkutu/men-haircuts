Would I Look Good With Blue Black Hair

Written by Robby Jul 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're considering changing your hair color to blue black, you might be wondering if it will suit you. This article will help you answer that question and provide you with tips and advice on how to achieve and maintain the look.

Description

Blue black hair is a trendy and bold hair color that combines blue and black hues. The result is a deep, rich, and striking shade that can give you a unique and edgy look. Blue black hair is a popular choice among celebrities like Katy Perry, Selena Gomez, and Rihanna.

How to

If you're ready to try blue black hair, there are a few steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shade

There are different shades of blue black hair, so it's essential to choose the one that suits your skin tone and features. If you have fair skin, you can opt for a brighter shade of blue black. If you have a deeper skin tone, you can choose a darker shade.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before coloring your hair, you need to prepare it by washing and conditioning it. You should also avoid using any styling products, as they can affect the color result.

Step 3: Apply the Hair Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye kit and apply it to your hair. Make sure to wear gloves and protect your clothing and skin from the dye. Leave the dye on for the recommended time and rinse it off thoroughly.

Step 4: Maintain Your Hair Color

To maintain your blue black hair color, you should use color-safe shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight, as it can cause the color to fade.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your blue black hair:

  • Pair your hair color with bold makeup and accessories for a dramatic look.
  • Wear clothes that complement your hair color, such as blue or black outfits.
  • Consider getting a haircut or hairstyle that flatters your new hair color.

FAQ

Is blue black hair easy to maintain?

Blue black hair requires some maintenance to keep the color looking fresh and vibrant. You should use color-safe hair products and avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight.

Will blue black hair suit me?

Blue black hair can suit different skin tones and features, but it's essential to choose the right shade. Consult with a hairstylist or use virtual hair color try-on tools to see how the color will look on you.

Can I dye my hair blue black at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair blue black at home using a hair dye kit. Follow the instructions carefully and take the necessary precautions to avoid damaging your hair or skin.

Hair Care

To keep your blue black hair healthy and shiny, you should follow a hair care routine that includes:

  • Washing your hair with a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week.
  • Avoiding heat styling tools or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Trimming your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Overall, blue black hair can be a bold and trendy hair color choice that can give you a unique and edgy look. Follow the steps and tips in this article to achieve and maintain the look.
