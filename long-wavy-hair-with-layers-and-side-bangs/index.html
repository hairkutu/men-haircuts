Long Wavy Hair With Layers And Side Bangs A Guide To Styling

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both effortlessly chic and low maintenance? Look no further than long wavy hair with layers and side bangs. This timeless style is perfect for those with naturally wavy or curly hair, and can be easily achieved with a few simple styling tips.

Description

Long wavy hair with layers and side bangs is a versatile and flattering hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. The layers help to add movement and dimension to the hair, while the side bangs frame the face and add a touch of edginess.

How to Achieve Long Wavy Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a small amount of styling mousse or cream to enhance your natural waves or curls.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections and begin blow-drying with a diffuser attachment. Use your fingers to scrunch and shape the hair as you dry it.

Step 3: Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or iron to define any sections that need extra help. Wrap the hair around the wand, hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step 4: To add layers, use a pair of sharp hair cutting shears and cut small sections of hair at an angle. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up, cutting slightly shorter sections as you go.

Step 5: Finally, add side bangs by sectioning off a small triangle of hair at the front of your head. Cut the hair at an angle so that it falls just above your eyebrows. Use a razor or scissors to create a textured, wispy effect.

Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet, as brushing can cause frizz and breakage.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can I still achieve this style if my hair is straight?

A: Yes! Simply use a curling wand or iron to create waves or curls, then follow the steps for adding layers and side bangs.

Q: How often should I trim my hair to maintain this style?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layers and bangs.

Hair Care for Long Wavy Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

- Apply a heat protectant spray or serum before using any hot styling tools.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

With these simple tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect long wavy hair with layers and side bangs. This effortlessly chic style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
