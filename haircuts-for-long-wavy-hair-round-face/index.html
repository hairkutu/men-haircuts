Haircuts For Long Wavy Hair Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Long wavy hair is one of the most coveted hair types. But having a round face can sometimes be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. In this post, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for long wavy hair round face. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right haircut to tips on how to care for your hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Haircuts for Long Wavy Hair Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut for your long wavy hair round face can be a daunting task. But it doesn't have to be! Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect haircut:

  • Consider your face shape: As mentioned earlier, having a round face can sometimes limit your options. But don't worry, there are still plenty of haircuts that will look great on you. The key is to choose a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more height.
  • Think about your hair texture: Long wavy hair can look great with a variety of haircuts. But if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid styles that are too heavy or layered. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to help reduce bulk.
  • Consider your lifestyle: Do you have time to spend styling your hair every day? If not, you may want to choose a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled quickly and easily.

Haircuts for Long Wavy Hair Round Face

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for long wavy hair round face:

The Long Layered Cut

The long layered cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on almost everyone. This style involves adding layers throughout your hair to create texture and movement. The layers can be cut in a variety of lengths, depending on your preference.

The Side-Swept Bangs

If you're looking for a way to add some interest to your haircut, side-swept bangs are a great option. These bangs are cut at an angle, starting from one side of your forehead and sweeping across to the other side. They can add a touch of glamour to your look and help to elongate your face.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a popular haircut that looks great on almost everyone. This style involves cutting your hair to just above your shoulders, with some subtle layers to add texture. It's a great option if you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Wavy Hair

Now that you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's time to learn how to style your long wavy hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. This will help to enhance your natural waves.
  4. If you want to add some extra volume, you can use a round brush to lift your roots while blow-drying.
  5. When your hair is dry, you can use a curling wand to add some loose waves.
  6. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair

Long wavy hair can be stunning, but it does require some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about haircuts for long wavy hair round face:

What is the best haircut for long wavy hair round face?

The best haircut for long wavy hair round face is one that will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of more height. Some great options include the long layered cut, side-swept bangs, and the lob.

How do I style my long wavy hair?

To style your long wavy hair, start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray, then blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. You can add some extra volume with a round brush, then use a curling wand to add loose waves. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

How do I care for my long wavy hair?

To care for your long wavy hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb, and avoid using hot tools too often. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your long wavy hair round face and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. So, go ahead and try out some new styles – you might just find your new signature look!
