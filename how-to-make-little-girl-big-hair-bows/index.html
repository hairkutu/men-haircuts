How To Make Little Girl Big Hair Bows

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on hair accessories for your little girl? Why not try making your own big hair bows? They're easy to create and can be customized to suit your child's personality and style. In this article, we'll show you how to make little girl big hair bows in simple steps.

Description

Big hair bows are a fun and stylish accessory that can add a pop of color and personality to any outfit. They are easy to make and can be customized to match any outfit or occasion. Making your own hair bows is also a great way to save money and have fun with your child. With a few simple tools and some creativity, you can make little girl big hair bows that are sure to impress.

How to Make Little Girl Big Hair Bows

Step 1: Gather Supplies

The first step in making big hair bows is to gather your supplies. You'll need:

  • Grosgrain ribbon
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun
  • Clip or elastic hairband
  • Embellishments (optional)

Step 2: Cut the Ribbon

Cut a piece of grosgrain ribbon to your desired length. For a big hair bow, we recommend using a ribbon that is at least 2 inches in width and 18 inches in length.

Step 3: Create the Bow Loops

Take the ribbon and fold it in half to create a loop. Pinch the center of the loop to create a crease, then fold the loop in half again to create two loops. Pinch the center of the two loops together to create a crease.

Step 4: Secure the Loops

Use a hot glue gun to secure the center of the two loops together. Hold the loops in place until the glue dries.

Step 5: Create the Bow Tails

Take the remaining ribbon and cut it in half. Fold each half in half again to create a loop. Pinch the center of each loop to create a crease.

Step 6: Attach the Bow Tails

Use a hot glue gun to attach the bow tails to the back of the bow loops. Hold the tails in place until the glue dries.

Step 7: Add Embellishments (Optional)

If you'd like to add embellishments to your big hair bow, now is the time to do it. You can add rhinestones, sequins, or any other decorations that your child loves.

Step 8: Attach the Clip or Elastic Hairband

Use a hot glue gun to attach a clip or elastic hairband to the back of the bow. Hold the clip or hairband in place until the glue dries.

Tips

  • Experiment with different ribbon widths and lengths to create different sized bows.
  • Try using different types of ribbon, such as satin or velvet, to create different textures.
  • Use a lighter or candle to heat-seal the edges of the ribbon to prevent fraying.
  • Get creative with embellishments! You can use buttons, feathers, or even small toys to add some personality to your big hair bow.

FAQ

Can I use a different type of glue?

We recommend using a hot glue gun for the best results. Other types of glue may not hold as well.

How do I attach the bow to my child's hair?

You can attach the bow to your child's hair using a clip or elastic hairband. If using a clip, simply clip the bow onto your child's hair. If using an elastic hairband, stretch the band around your child's head and position the bow where you'd like it to sit.

How do I care for my big hair bow?

To care for your big hair bow, store it in a safe place when not in use. If the bow becomes dirty, spot clean it with a damp cloth and mild soap.

Hair Care

When creating big hair bows for your child, it's important to take care of their hair. Be sure to use gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools on their hair. Regular trims can also help keep their hair healthy and prevent split ends. Finally, be sure to brush their hair gently to avoid damaging their delicate strands.
