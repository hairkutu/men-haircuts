How To Dry Curly Hair In Winter Tips For Healthy Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Winter can be a difficult season for curly hair. The cold weather and lack of humidity can leave your hair dry and brittle. And, if you’re not careful, you may be damaging your hair when trying to dry it. In this article, we’ll share some tips on how to dry curly hair in winter without causing damage.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dry Curly Hair in Winter
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Drying Curly Hair in Winter
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair in Winter

How to Dry Curly Hair in Winter

Drying curly hair in winter requires a different approach than in other seasons. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Don’t use a hair dryer on high heat
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to drying your curly hair in winter:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add moisture to your hair and make it easier to manage.
  2. After washing, gently squeeze out excess water from your hair.
  3. Take a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt and gently scrunch your hair. This will help to remove excess water without causing frizz.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and reduce frizz.
  5. Flip your head upside down and gently scrunch your hair again with the towel or t-shirt. This will help to add volume and bounce to your curls.
  6. Let your hair air dry. Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat as this can cause damage to your hair.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair in Winter

Here are some additional tips for drying your curly hair in winter:

  • Use a humidifier in your home to add moisture to the air.
  • Avoid going outside with wet hair. The cold air can cause damage to your hair and scalp.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the cold.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to dry my curly hair in winter?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer. However, make sure to use it on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair in winter?

A: It’s best to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week in winter. This will help to prevent your hair from getting too dry.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair in Winter

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind during the winter:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help to reduce breakage and damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful in winter. Remember to avoid using high heat on your hair, use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair, and keep your hair moisturized with leave-in conditioner and deep conditioning treatments.
