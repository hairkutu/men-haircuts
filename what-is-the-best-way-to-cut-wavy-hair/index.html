What Is The Best Way To Cut Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting wavy hair can be a challenge for both the stylist and the client. However, with the right techniques and tools, it can be done successfully. In this article, we will explore the best way to cut wavy hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care recommendations.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To Cut Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs

Description

Wavy hair can be challenging to cut because of its natural texture and the potential for unevenness. The best way to cut wavy hair is to use a technique that takes into account the wave pattern and the desired length. This may involve cutting the hair while it is wet, dry, or a combination of the two.

How to Cut Wavy Hair

The best way to cut wavy hair is to use a technique that takes into account the wave pattern and the desired length. This may involve cutting the hair while it is wet, dry, or a combination of the two. The following steps provide a general guide for cutting wavy hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing the hair and detangling it with a wide-tooth comb.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Starting at the nape of the neck, take a small section of hair and comb it straight.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers at the desired length and cut straight across.
  5. Continue this process, working your way up the head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them to the desired length.
  6. Once you have finished cutting the back sections, move on to the front sections, using the same technique.
  7. When you have finished cutting all the sections, check for any unevenness and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Style the hair as desired.

Remember to always use sharp scissors and to cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes. You can also use thinning shears to remove bulk and create movement in the hair.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for cutting wavy hair:

  • Consider the natural wave pattern of the hair when cutting it.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Use thinning shears to remove bulk and create movement in the hair.
  • Consider cutting the hair while it is dry to see the natural wave pattern and avoid any surprises once the hair dries.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting wavy hair:

Can you cut wavy hair with a razor?
Yes, you can cut wavy hair with a razor, but it is not recommended as it can cause frizz and breakage.
Should you cut wavy hair wet or dry?
It depends on the desired outcome. Cutting wavy hair while it is wet can result in a more precise cut, while cutting it dry can help you see the natural wave pattern and avoid any surprises once the hair dries.
How often should you cut wavy hair?
It is recommended to cut wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of the hair to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing the hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can successfully cut and care for wavy hair. Remember to always use sharp scissors, cut small sections of hair at a time, and consider the natural wave pattern of the hair when cutting it.
