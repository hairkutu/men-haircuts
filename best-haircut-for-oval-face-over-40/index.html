Best Haircut For Oval Face Over 40 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Bardi Mar 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best haircut for oval face over 40. As we age, our hair changes, and so do our faces. An oval face is considered the most versatile shape, but getting the right haircut can make all the difference. We’ve put together a comprehensive guide to help you find the perfect style for your face shape and age.

Description

An oval face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered the most versatile because it can pull off almost any haircut. However, as we age, our skin loses elasticity, and our features become less defined. Choosing the right haircut can help to enhance your features and give you a more youthful appearance.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oval Face Over 40

When choosing a haircut, it’s essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. For oval faces over 40, you want to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for oval face over 40:

Tip 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is the most important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. Oval faces can pull off almost any style, but as we age, our features become less defined. You want to choose a style that enhances your features and gives you a more youthful appearance.

Tip 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have fine hair, you want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you want to choose a style that thins out your hair and makes it more manageable.

Tip 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you’re a busy mom or working professional, you want a style that’s easy to maintain and doesn’t require a lot of styling time. If you have more time to style your hair, you can choose a more elaborate style.

Step by Step Guide to the Best Haircut for Oval Face Over 40

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, here’s a step-by-step guide to finding the best haircut for oval face over 40:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you choose a haircut, you need to determine your face shape. One way to do this is to measure your face with a tape measure. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and the length of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and high cheekbones, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The next step is to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you want to choose a style that thins out your hair and makes it more manageable.

Step 3: Choose a Style That Flatters Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, you can choose a style that flatters your face shape. Oval faces can pull off almost any style, but as we age, our features become less defined. You want to choose a style that enhances your features and gives you a more youthful appearance.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional Stylist

The final step is to consult with a professional stylist. A stylist can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture. They can also give you tips on how to style your hair and maintain your haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve chosen the best haircut for oval face over 40, it’s essential to maintain your style. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

Tip 1: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products will help to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy. Choose products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it look frizzy and dull. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
