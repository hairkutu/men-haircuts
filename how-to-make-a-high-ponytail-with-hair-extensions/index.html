How To Make A High Ponytail With Hair Extensions

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch it up with a high ponytail? Adding hair extensions can take your ponytail to the next level. In this article, we’ll show you how to make a high ponytail with hair extensions step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a High Ponytail with Hair Extensions
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make a High Ponytail with Hair Extensions

Before we get started, you’ll need to gather a few materials:

  • Hair extensions
  • Hair elastic
  • Bobby pins
  • Comb
  • Hairspray

Choose the Right Hair Extensions

When selecting hair extensions for your high ponytail, you want to choose ones that match your hair color and texture. Clip-in extensions are the easiest to use for a high ponytail, but you can use other types such as tape-in or sew-in extensions.

Prep Your Hair

Before adding the hair extensions, you want to make sure your hair is clean and brushed out. Use a comb to create a part in the middle or to the side of your head, whichever you prefer.

Step by Step

Now that you’ve prepped your hair and have your materials ready, let’s get started on how to make a high ponytail with hair extensions:

Step 1: Clip in Hair Extensions

Start by clipping in your hair extensions. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head. Make sure to evenly distribute the extensions for a natural look.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Use a comb to gather your hair into a high ponytail. Make sure it’s smooth and free of bumps or tangles.

Step 3: Secure the Ponytail

Using a hair elastic, secure the ponytail in place. Make sure it’s tight enough to hold the hair in place, but not too tight to cause discomfort.

Step 4: Conceal the Elastic

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic. Use a bobby pin to secure it in place.

Step 5: Add Hairspray

Finally, add hairspray to keep your high ponytail in place all day. Use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making a high ponytail with hair extensions:

  • Use a high-quality hair elastic to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to tease your hair at the crown to add volume.
  • Experiment with different ponytail heights to see what looks best on you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair.

FAQ

Can I use hair extensions on short hair?

Yes, you can use hair extensions on short hair. You’ll just need to make sure the extensions are properly blended and hidden within your hair.

How long should my hair extensions be for a high ponytail?

Your hair extensions should be at least the length of your natural hair, but longer extensions can add more volume and length to your ponytail.

How do I care for my hair extensions?

Hair Care

Caring for your hair extensions is important to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Brush your extensions daily with a soft-bristle brush.
  • Wash your extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions as much as possible.
  • Store your extensions properly in a cool, dry place.

Now that you know how to make a high ponytail with hair extensions, it’s time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create a flawless high ponytail that is sure to turn heads.
