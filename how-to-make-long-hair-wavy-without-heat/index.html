How To Make Long Hair Wavy Without Heat

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 · 10 min read

Do you want to achieve beautiful beachy waves without having to use heat on your long hair? There are several ways to do this without causing damage to your hair. In this article, we will go through how to make long hair wavy without heat, step by step.

Description

If you have long hair, you may be hesitant to use heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners, as they can cause damage to your hair over time. However, there are many ways to achieve wavy hair without heat that are safe and easy to do at home. Whether you prefer loose, beachy waves or tighter curls, there is a method that will work for you.

How To

There are several methods you can use to get wavy hair without heat. Here are some of the most popular techniques:

Braids

Braiding your hair is a simple and effective way to get beautiful waves without using heat. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp.
  3. Divide your hair into three or four sections, depending on how thick it is.
  4. Braid each section tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Secure the ends of each braid with a hair tie.
  6. Leave the braids in overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Take out the braids and use your fingers to gently separate the waves.

Twists

Twisting your hair is another easy way to create natural-looking waves. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp.
  3. Divide your hair into two sections.
  4. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it tightly.
  5. Wrap the twisted section around your finger to create a coil, then secure it with a hair tie.
  6. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair.
  7. Leave the twists in overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  8. Remove the hair ties and use your fingers to separate the waves.

Headband Curls

If you want tighter curls, you can try using a headband to create heatless curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.
  2. Put on a stretchy headband around your head, so that it sits just above your forehead.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and tuck it over the headband.
  4. Take another small section of hair and wrap it over the headband, in the opposite direction.
  5. Continue wrapping sections of hair over the headband, alternating directions, until all of your hair is wrapped.
  6. Leave the headband in overnight, or until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Remove the headband and use your fingers to separate the curls.

Step by Step

Now that you know some of the best methods for creating wavy hair without heat, let’s go through the steps in more detail:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Use a moisturizing conditioner to help prevent frizz and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair until it is slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Choose Your Method

Decide which method you want to use to create your waves. Braids, twists, and headband curls are all great options.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, depending on the method you are using. For braids and twists, you will need to divide your hair into three or four sections. For headband curls, you can leave your hair in one section.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Start braiding, twisting, or wrapping your hair, depending on the method you have chosen. Make sure to secure your hair with a hair tie or headband to keep it in place.

Step 6: Leave Your Hair Overnight

Once you have styled your hair, leave it in overnight or until it is completely dry. This will ensure that your waves set properly.

Step 7: Separate Your Waves

Use your fingers to gently separate your waves, starting at the ends and working your way up. This will give you a natural and effortless look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when creating wavy hair without heat:

  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance your waves and add volume.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your waves.
  • For tighter curls, you can use smaller sections of hair and wrap them more tightly around your headband.
  • To keep your waves looking fresh, sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid.

FAQ

Can I use these methods on any hair type?

Yes, these methods can be used on any hair type, from straight to curly. However, the results may vary depending on your hair texture and thickness.

How long will the waves last?

The waves should last for several days, as long as you take care not to brush your hair too much or use too much product.

Can I use these methods on short hair?

Yes, these methods can be used on short hair, although the results may be less dramatic.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Now that you know how to make long hair wavy without heat, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking waves without causing damage to your hair. Try out these methods and see which one works best for you.
