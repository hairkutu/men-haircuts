How To Wear A Denim Skirt Over 60 A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Denim skirts are a timeless fashion staple that can be worn by women of all ages. However, as we age, it can be challenging to find the right balance between fashion and comfort. If you're over 60 and wondering how to wear a denim skirt, this guide is for you. From how to style it to what to pair it with, we've got you covered.

Description

Denim skirts are versatile and can be worn in many different ways. They come in various styles, including pencil, A-line, and maxi, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. If you're over 60 and looking for a comfortable and stylish outfit, a denim skirt is an excellent option.

How to Wear a Denim Skirt Over 60: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Skirt

When selecting a denim skirt, consider the length, fit, and style. A midi or maxi skirt is a great option for those who prefer more coverage, while a pencil skirt is perfect for those who want a more fitted look. Make sure the skirt fits well and is comfortable to move around in.

Step 2: Pair with a Blouse

A denim skirt looks great when paired with a blouse. Choose a blouse in a color that complements the skirt, such as white or pastel. Opt for a loose-fitting blouse that is comfortable to wear and doesn't cling to your body.

Step 3: Add Accessories

Accessories can elevate any outfit, and a denim skirt is no exception. Consider adding a statement necklace, scarf, or bracelet to your outfit. Keep in mind that less is more, so don't go overboard with accessories.

Step 4: Choose the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make or break an outfit. For a denim skirt, consider wearing a pair of comfortable flats or sandals. If you prefer heels, choose a low to mid-heel that is easy to walk in.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing a denim skirt over 60:

  • Choose a skirt with a high waist to flatter your figure
  • Avoid skirts with large pockets or embellishments as they can add bulk to your frame
  • Pair your denim skirt with a blazer for a more polished look
  • Consider wearing a denim skirt with a denim jacket for a trendy denim-on-denim look
  • Choose a skirt in a dark wash for a more slimming effect

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a denim skirt to a formal event?

A: While denim skirts are versatile, they are not appropriate for formal events. Opt for a dressy skirt or dress instead.

Q: Can I wear a denim skirt in the winter?

A: Yes, you can wear a denim skirt in the winter. Pair it with a sweater or turtleneck, tights, and boots for a cozy and stylish look.

Q: Can I wear a denim skirt to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace is business casual, a denim skirt paired with a blouse and blazer can be appropriate. However, if your workplace is more formal, a denim skirt may not be appropriate.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair may become thinner and more fragile. To keep your hair healthy and strong, consider using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using hot styling tools frequently and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to wear a denim skirt over 60, it's time to embrace this timeless fashion staple. Remember, the key to looking stylish and comfortable is to choose the right skirt, pair it with the right top and accessories, and most importantly, feel confident in your outfit.
