What Length Is A Number 0 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you're looking for a buzz cut or a shaved head, you may have heard the term "number 0 haircut." But what exactly does that mean? In this blog post, we'll go over the basics of a number 0 haircut, including what length it is, how to achieve it, and some tips for taking care of your hair afterward.

Description

A number 0 haircut is the shortest possible haircut you can get with clippers. It is achieved by using a clipper guard that leaves the hair at a length of 1/16 of an inch or 1.5 millimeters. This is basically the same length as a clean shave, but with a little bit of stubble left behind.

How to

If you're looking to get a number 0 haircut, the first thing you'll need is a good set of clippers. You'll also need a clipper guard that is labeled as a "0" or "1/16". Once you have your tools, follow these steps:
  1. Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly.
  2. Attach the "0" or "1/16" guard to your clippers.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, run the clippers over your scalp in short, even strokes.
  4. Continue working your way around your head until all the hair is the same length.
  5. If you want to clean up the edges, use a razor or trimmer to shave around your ears and neck.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair:
Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your clippers don't get clogged with hair. 2. Attach the "0" guard to your clippers:
Next, attach the "0" guard to your clippers. This will ensure that your hair is cut to the correct length. 3. Start at the back of your head:
Starting at the back of your head, run the clippers over your scalp in short, even strokes. Be sure to move the clippers in all directions to ensure that all the hair is cut evenly. 4. Work your way around your head:
Continue working your way around your head until all the hair is the same length. Be sure to take your time and go slowly to avoid any mistakes. 5. Clean up the edges:
If you want to clean up the edges, use a razor or trimmer to shave around your ears and neck. This will give you a clean, polished look.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you haven't missed any spots.
- If you're not sure if you want to go all the way with a number 0 haircut, you can always start with a longer guard and work your way down.
- Remember to use sunscreen or wear a hat if you're going to be out in the sun for an extended period of time, as a shaved head can be more sensitive to sunburn.

FAQ

Q: Will a number 0 haircut look good on me?
A: It depends on your face shape and personal style. It's always a good idea to consult with a stylist or barber before making a dramatic change to your hair. Q: How often do I need to get a number 0 haircut?
A: That depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the look. Generally, a number 0 haircut will need to be touched up every two to four weeks.

Hair Care

After getting a number 0 haircut, it's important to take care of your scalp and hair to keep it healthy. Here are a few tips: - Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp and hair hydrated.
- Avoid using styling products that can clog your hair follicles.
- Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to your scalp to prevent dryness and irritation.
- Protect your head from the sun with sunscreen or a hat.

Conclusion

A number 0 haircut is a dramatic and bold look that can be achieved with clippers and a "0" or "1/16" guard. Whether you're looking to try something new or simply want a low-maintenance hairstyle, a number 0 haircut can be a great choice. Just be sure to take care of your scalp and hair afterward to keep it healthy and looking great.
