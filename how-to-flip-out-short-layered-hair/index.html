How To Flip Out Short Layered Hair

Written by Frank May 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to try something new? Flipping out short layered hair can add volume and texture, giving you a fresh look. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this hairstyle step by step, along with tips and tricks to make it last. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Flip Out Short Layered Hair
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

Step by Step

Before we begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. It's also best to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from hot tools.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into manageable pieces. Clip the top layer up and away from the bottom layer.

Step 2: Curl the Bottom Layer

Take a small section of the bottom layer and curl it away from your face using a curling iron. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Continue curling small sections until you've curled all of the bottom layer.

Step 3: Flip Out the Layers

Take the top layer of your hair and curl it away from your face. Once you've curled the entire top layer, use a brush to flip the ends outwards. This will create the "flipped out" look. Repeat this process for each section of the top layer until you've flipped out all of the layers.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Once you've flipped out all of your layers, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

  • If you have thick hair, use a smaller curling iron to create tighter curls.
  • If you have thin hair, use a larger curling iron to create bigger curls.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Don't over-curl your hair or it will look too stiff and unnatural.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to gently brush out the curls and create a softer look.

FAQ

Can I flip out short layered hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a straightening iron to achieve the same effect. Simply curl the ends of your hair outwards using the straightening iron.

How long will this hairstyle last?

This hairstyle can last all day with the right products and techniques. Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place, and avoid touching it too much throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily flip out your short layered hair for a fun and fresh look. Experiment with different products and styles to find the perfect look for you. Good luck!
