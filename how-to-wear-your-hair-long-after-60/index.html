How To Wear Your Hair Long After 60

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Long hair is often associated with youthfulness, but that doesn’t mean you have to cut your hair short once you reach a certain age. As we age, our hair naturally loses some of its elasticity and shine, but with the right care and styling, you can wear long hair well into your 60s and beyond. In this article, we’ll share some tips on how to wear your hair long after 60.

Table of Content

  • How to Wear Your Hair Long After 60
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Long Hair After 60
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Hair After 60

How to Wear Your Hair Long After 60

The first step to wearing long hair after 60 is to find a style that works for you. While you may have loved your long hair in your younger years, it may not be the best fit for your current lifestyle or hair type. Consider factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day.

Once you’ve found a style that works for you, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using the right products can help keep your hair looking its best.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when wearing long hair after 60:

  1. Choose a style that works for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Maintain healthy hair by getting regular trims and using the right products.
  3. Style your hair based on your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on your hair each day.
  4. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, updos, and ponytails, to keep your look fresh and versatile.
  5. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and interest to your hair.

Tips for Long Hair After 60

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing long hair after 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as scarves or headbands, to add interest to your look.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and well-maintained.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best products and styles for your hair type and face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing long hair after 60:

Is it appropriate to wear long hair after 60?

Yes, it’s perfectly appropriate to wear long hair after 60. It’s all about finding a style that works for you and keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained.

What hairstyles are best for long hair after 60?

The best hairstyles for long hair after 60 depend on your face shape and hair texture. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best styles for you.

How can I keep my long hair looking healthy?

Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using the right products can help keep your long hair looking healthy and shiny.

Hair Care for Long Hair After 60

Here are some tips for caring for long hair after 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best products and styles for your hair type and face shape.

If you follow these tips and take good care of your hair, you can wear long hair well into your 60s and beyond. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
