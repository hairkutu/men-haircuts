Is Shoulder Length Hair Long Or Short A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Shoulder length hair is a popular hairstyle among women. It's versatile, easy to manage, and can suit different face shapes and hair textures. However, one question that often comes up is whether shoulder length hair is considered long or short. In this article, we'll explore this topic in-depth and provide you with all the information you need to know.

  • Is Shoulder Length Hair Long or Short?

  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Definition of Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair is hair that falls just below the shoulders and above the collarbone. It's typically around 12 to 14 inches in length, but this can vary depending on the individual's height, body shape, and hair texture. Shoulder length hair can be straight, wavy, or curly, and can be cut in different styles, such as blunt, layered, or asymmetrical.

Is Shoulder Length Hair Long or Short?

The answer to this question depends on your perspective. Compared to short haircuts, such as pixie cuts or bobs, shoulder length hair can be considered long. However, compared to longer hairstyles, such as waist-length hair, shoulder length hair can be considered short. Ultimately, whether you consider shoulder length hair long or short is subjective and depends on your personal preference and the context.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some popular styling options:

1. Straight and Sleek

For a classic and polished look, straighten your shoulder length hair with a flat iron and apply a smoothing serum to tame frizz and add shine.

2. Beachy Waves

For a more relaxed and effortless look, create beachy waves using a curling iron or sea salt spray. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to add texture.

3. Half Up, Half Down

For a cute and easy hairstyle, gather the top section of your hair and tie it into a half-up ponytail. Leave the rest of your hair down and add loose waves or curls for extra volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your shoulder length hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type and texture. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness. Divide your hair into sections and focus on one section at a time. Use a cool shot setting to set your hair in place.

4. Style Your Hair

Choose a styling option from the list above or come up with your own. Use a curling iron, flat iron, or hair accessories to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful shoulder length hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hair's shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible or use them on a low heat setting.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

FAQs

Q: Can shoulder length hair be considered medium length?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair can be considered a medium length hairstyle.

Q: Is shoulder length hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Consult with a hairstylist to find a style that flatters your features.

Q: Can you put shoulder length hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can put shoulder length hair in a ponytail. Use a hair tie or scrunchie to secure your hair at the desired height.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for shoulder length hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner that suit your hair type and texture
  • Leave-in conditioner to detangle and moisturize your hair
  • Heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage from heat styling tools
  • Deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair
  • Hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness

In conclusion, whether you consider shoulder length hair long or short is up to you. What matters most is that you feel confident and beautiful in your hairstyle. Experiment with different styles and hair care products to find what works best for you.
