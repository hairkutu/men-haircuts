What Is The Best Haircut For My Face Shape

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to getting a new haircut, one of the most important factors to consider is your face shape. The right haircut can accentuate your features and enhance your overall appearance, while the wrong one can leave you feeling less than satisfied with your new look. In this blog post, we’ll discuss how to determine your face shape and what the best haircuts are for each shape.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Each Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your New Cut

Determining Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the best haircut for your face shape, you need to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and take note of the following:

  • Forehead – is it wider or narrower than your cheekbones?
  • Cheekbones – are they the widest part of your face?
  • Jawline – is it rounded or squared?
  • Face length – is your face longer or shorter than it is wide?

Once you’ve assessed these features, you can determine your face shape. If you’re still unsure, there are also online tools and quizzes that can help you determine your face shape.

Best Haircuts for Each Face Shape

Round Face

If you have a round face, the best haircuts will add angles and length to your face. This can be achieved with layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical styles. Avoid blunt cuts and middle parts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Oval Face

An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off a variety of haircuts. However, the best haircuts will showcase your facial symmetry, such as long layers or a bob. Avoid styles that cover your face, as they can detract from your natural beauty.

Square Face

If you have a square face, the best haircuts will soften your jawline and add length to your face. This can be achieved with layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and textured styles. Avoid blunt cuts and straight styles, as they can accentuate your square features.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The best haircuts will balance out these features, such as long layers or a side-swept bob. Avoid styles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, the best haircuts will balance out your cheekbones and jawline. This can be achieved with chin-length bobs or layered cuts with volume at the crown. Avoid styles that add volume to your cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Rectangle Face

A rectangle face is characterized by a longer face length and a squared jawline. The best haircuts will add width to your face, such as layered cuts with volume at the sides. Avoid styles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Now that you know what the best haircuts are for your face shape, here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair type – certain haircuts may work better with straight or curly hair
  • Think about your lifestyle – choose a style that is easy to maintain and fits with your daily routine
  • Consult with a stylist – they can provide personalized recommendations based on your face shape and hair type

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still get a haircut that isn’t recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These recommendations are simply guidelines – ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and happy.

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It’s not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a haircut that complements the most prominent features of your face.

Hair Care for Your New Cut

After getting a new haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools excessively
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny

Remember, the best haircut for your face shape is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Use these guidelines as a starting point, but don’t be afraid to experiment and try new styles!
