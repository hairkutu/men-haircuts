How To Shave Sides Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, one option to consider is shaving the sides of your head. This edgy look has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's a great way to add some personality to your hair. But before you grab your razor and start shaving away, it's important to know how to do it properly. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for shaving the sides of your head, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shave Sides of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaving Sides of Head
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Shaving Sides of Head

How to Shave Sides of Head

Shaving the sides of your head can be a bit intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools and technique, it's a fairly straightforward process. Here's what you'll need:

  • Electric razor or clippers
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to shave the sides of your head:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and make it easier to shave.
  2. Use the comb to separate the hair on the top of your head from the hair on the sides. Clip the top section up and out of the way.
  3. Turn on your electric razor or clippers and begin shaving the sides of your head, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to guide the razor and ensure an even cut.
  4. Continue shaving until you've reached your desired length. If you're unsure, start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you're happy with the look.
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  6. Use the towel to wipe away any loose hair.
  7. Take down the top section of your hair and style as desired.

Tips for Shaving Sides of Head

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaving the sides of your head:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you're happy with the look. It's easier to take more hair off than to put it back on!
  • Use a mirror to ensure you're getting an even cut on both sides.
  • Go slowly and be patient. It's better to take your time and get it right than rush and end up with a botched job.
  • If you're using an electric razor, make sure it's fully charged before you start. A dead battery can lead to uneven cutting.
  • Consider having a professional barber or stylist do it for you if you're unsure or nervous about doing it yourself.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving the sides of your head:

Will shaving the sides of my head make my hair grow back thicker?

No, shaving your hair does not affect the thickness or texture of your hair. This is a common myth.

How often do I need to shave the sides of my head?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, you'll need to shave every two to four weeks.

Can I still style my hair after shaving the sides?

Absolutely! Shaving the sides of your head doesn't affect the length or texture of the hair on top, so you can still style it as you normally would.

Hair Care After Shaving Sides of Head

Once you've shaved the sides of your head, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid irritating your scalp.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can dry it out and cause damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently shave the sides of your head and rock a bold new look. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with it!
