How To Know What Hair Color Suits You A Guide

Are you tired of having the same hair color for years? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, but don't worry, we're here to help. In this guide, we'll show you how to know what hair color suits you.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Know What Hair Color Suits You

Before you start browsing hair color options, it's important to determine what hair color will suit you best. There are a few factors to consider:

  • Your skin tone
  • Your eye color
  • Your natural hair color
  • Your lifestyle and personality

By taking these factors into account, you can narrow down your choices and choose a hair color that will complement your features and style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a big role in what hair color will suit you best. There are three main skin tones:

  • Cool
  • Warm
  • Neutral

If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color with cool tones as well. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color with warm tones. If you have neutral undertones, you can choose a hair color with either cool or warm tones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine what hair color will suit you best. Typically, hair colors that are opposite your eye color on the color wheel will complement your eyes the most. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde or copper will look great on you.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

If you're not looking for a drastic change, you may want to consider your natural hair color when choosing a new shade. Opt for a hair color that's similar to your natural color but with subtle highlights or lowlights to add dimension.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle and Personality

Your lifestyle and personality can also play a role in what hair color will suit you best. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have a lot of time for upkeep, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color. If you're more adventurous and like to make a statement, you may want to choose a bold hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Ask your stylist for their opinion
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colors will look on you
  • Consider the season - lighter hair colors are great for summer, while darker hair colors are perfect for fall and winter
  • Don't be afraid to experiment - it's just hair and it will grow back

FAQs

What hair color looks good on everyone?

It's hard to say what hair color looks good on everyone since everyone's features and skin tones are different. However, natural-looking hair colors like warm browns and blondes tend to look good on a wide range of people.

What hair color makes you look younger?

Lighter hair colors like blonde and light brown can make you look younger since they brighten up your complexion. However, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to know what hair color suits you, it's time to book that appointment with your stylist and try something new!
