Best Haircut For Round Face Female Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our face shape changes and it becomes more difficult to find the perfect haircut that suits our face shape. For women over 50 with a round face, it's important to choose a haircut that accentuates the features of the face while hiding some of the flaws. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for round face female over 50, how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Women with round faces have a circular face shape with soft features. The best haircut for round face female over 50 should have layers that add volume and movement to the hair. The layers should be around the face and chin-length to elongate the face. A side-swept bang is also a great option to add asymmetry, which helps to balance the roundness of the face. The right haircut can make a world of difference and make you look and feel younger.

How To

The first step to getting the best haircut for round face female over 50 is to find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for older women. They will be able to guide you on what style will suit your face shape and hair texture. You can also do some research and bring pictures of haircuts you like to your appointment.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to get the best haircut for round face female over 50:

  1. Find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for older women.
  2. Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your appointment.
  3. Discuss with your hairstylist what style will suit your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to add layers around your face and chin-length to elongate your face.
  5. Consider adding a side-swept bang to add asymmetry to your face.
  6. Ask your hairstylist to add volume and movement to your hair with layers.
  7. Choose a low-maintenance haircut that works with your lifestyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the best haircut for round face female over 50:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long as they can make your face look wider.
  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Opt for soft, layered haircuts that add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Choose a low-maintenance haircut that works with your lifestyle.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for round face female over 50:

1. Should I go for a short or long haircut if I have a round face?

Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long as they can make your face look wider. Opt for a medium-length haircut that falls around your chin.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

No, you can still have bangs if you have a round face. Opt for a side-swept bang that adds asymmetry to your face.

3. What hair texture works best for the best haircut for round face female over 50?

The best hair texture for the best haircut for round face female over 50 is fine to medium hair. If you have thick or coarse hair, ask your hairstylist to add layers to reduce the volume.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Getting the best haircut for round face female over 50 can be a game-changer. It can make you feel more confident and youthful. Follow these tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
