How To Style Short Coarse Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Mar 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short coarse hair, you know how challenging it can be to style. But fear not, we have some tips and tricks for you to achieve a fabulous look. Whether you want to straighten your hair or add some curls, these techniques will help you get the most out of your hair type.

Description

Short coarse hair is typically thicker and coarser than other hair types. This can make it difficult to style and manage. However, with the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks.

How to Style Short Coarse Hair

Step 1: Start with the Right Products

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to use the right products. Look for products designed for coarse hair, such as a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or oil to help tame frizz and add shine.

Step 2: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, be sure to use a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from damage and keep it looking healthy.

Step 3: Straightening Short Coarse Hair

To straighten short coarse hair, start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it in small sections. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to avoid damage.

Step 4: Adding Curls to Short Coarse Hair

If you want to add some curls to your short coarse hair, start by applying a curl-enhancing product. Use a curling wand or curling iron to create loose curls, and then use your fingers to separate and define the curls.

Tips for Styling Short Coarse Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short coarse hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Can I dye my short coarse hair?

Yes, you can dye your short coarse hair. However, be sure to use a dye that is specifically designed for coarse hair, and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damage.

How often should I wash my short coarse hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have very oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. However, if your hair is dry, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Short Coarse Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your short coarse hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short coarse hair with confidence. Remember to use the right products, protect your hair from heat damage, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
