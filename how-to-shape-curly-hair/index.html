How To Shape Curly Hair Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Daniel Apr 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have curly hair and want to know how to shape it properly? Look no further! In this post, we will go over all the tips and tricks you need to know about shaping curly hair. From the types of products to use to the different techniques available, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Shape Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaping Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Curly Hair Care

How to Shape Curly Hair

Shaping curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, it can be done. The first step is to determine what type of curls you have. Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils, and each type requires different styling methods. Once you determine your curl type, you can choose the products and techniques that will work best for you.

There are several products that can help shape curly hair, including:

  • Styling creams
  • Gels
  • Mousses
  • Hair sprays

Each of these products has a different level of hold and can be used to achieve different styles. For example, if you want to define your curls and reduce frizz, a styling cream or gel may be the best option. If you want more volume, a mousse may work better. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your products, it’s time to start shaping your curls. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply your chosen product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and distribute the product evenly.
  4. Flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting, scrunching as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate and shape your curls.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Shaping Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips for shaping curly hair:

  • Don’t over-manipulate your curls. Too much touching and styling can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can break up the curl pattern.
  • Consider getting a curly haircut to help shape your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about shaping curly hair:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Can I blow dry my curly hair?

Yes, you can blow dry your curly hair, but it’s important to use a diffuser attachment and a low heat setting to prevent frizz and damage.

What’s the best way to preserve my curls overnight?

You can preserve your curls overnight by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase and using a hair bonnet or pineapple technique to keep your curls in place.

Curly Hair Care

Shaping curly hair is just one part of curly hair care. To keep your curls healthy and beautiful, you should also:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips and tricks, you can shape your curly hair and keep it looking its best. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
