How To Thin Out A Thick Curly Wig

Written by Robby Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a thick curly wig, you might want to thin it out to make it look more natural and less bulky. Thinning out a wig is a simple process that can be done at home, without the need for professional help. In this article, we will show you how to thin out a thick curly wig step-by-step. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier, as well as answer some frequently asked questions about wig thinning.

How to Thin Out A Thick Curly Wig

First and foremost, you will need a pair of thinning shears. These are special scissors designed to remove hair in a natural-looking way. You can find them in most beauty supply stores or online. Once you have your thinning shears, follow these steps:

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

Before you start thinning out the wig, you need to make sure it is free of tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle the hair. Be careful not to pull or yank the hair, as this can damage the wig.

Step 2: Section the Wig

Separate the wig into sections using clips. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will make it easier to thin out the hair evenly.

Step 3: Thin Out the Hair

Using your thinning shears, start cutting into the hair at a diagonal angle. Be sure to only cut a few strands at a time, and start at the bottom of the section. Work your way up, cutting in small sections until you reach the top. Be sure to keep the scissors moving in a diagonal direction to avoid creating harsh lines.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After you have thinned out the hair, use a comb to blend the hair together. This will ensure that the hair looks natural and not choppy or uneven.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once you have thinned out the hair, style the wig as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.

Tips for Thinning Out A Wig

Thinning out a wig can be a bit tricky, especially if you have never done it before. Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use thinning shears that are specifically designed for wigs.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to uneven or choppy results.
  • Blend the hair together after thinning to create a natural look.

FAQs About Wig Thinning

Can I Thin Out A Synthetic Wig?

Yes, you can thin out a synthetic wig. However, synthetic hair is often more delicate than human hair, so you need to be extra careful when using thinning shears.

How Often Should I Thin Out My Wig?

This depends on how often you wear the wig and how much styling you do. If you wear the wig daily and style it often, you may need to thin it out more frequently.

Can I Thin Out My Wig Too Much?

Yes, it is possible to thin out a wig too much. This can lead to uneven or choppy results. Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid thinning out too much hair.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

Now that you know how to thin out a thick curly wig, itâs important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for your wig:

  • Always use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the hair.
  • Wash the wig with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or in a hair net to prevent tangling.

Thinning out a thick curly wig is a simple process that can be done at home. With the right tools and a bit of patience, you can achieve a natural-looking wig that is perfect for any occasion.
