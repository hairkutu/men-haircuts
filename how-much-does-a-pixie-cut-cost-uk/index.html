How Much Does A Pixie Cut Cost Uk

Written by Frank Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking of getting a pixie cut but not sure how much it will cost you? Look no further as we break down the prices for a pixie cut in the UK.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut short at the back and sides and longer on top. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those looking for a simple yet chic look. A pixie cut can be personalized to suit individual face shapes and hair textures.

How To

To get a pixie cut, you need to find a reputable hairdresser who specializes in short haircuts. You can bring a picture of the desired style or ask the hairdresser for advice on what would suit your face shape and hair type. The hairdresser will then proceed to cut your hair to the desired length and style.

Step by Step

Step 1: Research

Research different hairdressers in your area who specialize in short haircuts. Look at their reviews and portfolio to ensure they can provide the look you want.

Step 2: Book an Appointment

Book an appointment with the hairdresser of your choice. Make sure to mention that you want a pixie cut so they can allow enough time for the appointment.

Step 3: Consultation

During the consultation, discuss with the hairdresser the style you want and what would suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Cut and Style

The hairdresser will proceed to cut your hair to the desired length and style. They will then style your hair using products and tools to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a pixie cut:

  • Choose a hairdresser who specializes in short haircuts
  • Bring a picture of the desired style or ask for advice from the hairdresser
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style
  • Invest in quality styling products to maintain the look

FAQ

How much does a pixie cut cost in the UK?

The cost of a pixie cut in the UK can vary depending on the location, salon, and experience of the hairdresser. On average, a pixie cut can cost anywhere from Â£30 to Â£100.

Is a pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a pixie cut can be tailored to suit different face shapes. It is important to consult with a hairdresser who can advise on the best style for your face shape.

How often do I need to get a pixie cut?

A pixie cut requires regular maintenance to keep the shape and length. It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, invest in quality hair care products. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and nourished. Avoid using too much heat on the hair and protect it with a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

So there you have it, the complete guide on how much a pixie cut costs in the UK. Remember to choose a reputable hairdresser and invest in quality hair care products to maintain your stylish new look.
