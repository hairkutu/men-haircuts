Which Hairstyle Suits My Round Face

Written by Robby Sep 08, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that do not suit your round face shape? Don't worry, we are here to help you find the perfect hairstyle that will enhance your features and make you look amazing. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the right hairstyle for your round face shape. Read on to find out more!

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are typically about as wide as they are long. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is rounded. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and slimness to balance out the natural shape of the face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face?

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it is essential to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you should look for hairstyles that create the illusion of length and slimness.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is also an essential factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle for a round face. If you have fine hair, you should opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you should choose hairstyles that keep your hair sleek and smooth.

Step 3: Determine the Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair is also crucial when choosing a hairstyle for a round face. You should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that fall below the chin or longer.

Step 4: Consider the Volume of Your Hair

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, you should also consider the volume of your hair. Hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head can make your face look longer and slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face look wider.

Top Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for round faces because they create the illusion of length and slimness. This hairstyle works best for long hair, and the layers should start at the chin or below.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces. They can help to create the illusion of length and slimness and draw attention away from the widest part of the face.

3. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for round faces. The key is to make sure that the length falls below the chin and that the hair is cut at an angle to create the illusion of length.

4. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that works well for round faces. The high ponytail can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of length.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help to keep your hairstyle looking great. Choose products that are designed for your hair type and use them as directed.

2. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and prevent split ends. Aim to get your hair cut every six to eight weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Use heat protectant products before using hot tools and avoid using them too often.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs and straight-across bangs.

Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. However, you should choose a pixie cut that has longer layers on top to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help to prevent tangles and breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

2. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help to keep it clean and healthy. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a daunting task. However, by considering your face shape, hair texture, length, and volume, you can find the perfect hairstyle that will enhance your features and make you look amazing. Follow our tips and tricks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
