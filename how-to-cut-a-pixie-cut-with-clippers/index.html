Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is popular among women. It is a great way to change your look and can be easily maintained. If you are looking to cut a pixie cut yourself, using clippers can be a good option. Clippers are easy to use and can give you a clean and precise cut. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a pixie cut with clippers.

How to Cut a Pixie Cut with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a clipper, a comb, hair-cutting scissors, and a mirror. Once you have all the tools, follow the steps below:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by cutting the sides of your hair. Use the clipper with the lowest guard setting and start by trimming the sides of your hair. Move the clipper from the bottom of your ear to the top of your head. Repeat this on the other side.

Step 2: Move to the back of your head. Use the clipper to trim the hair at the back of your head. Start at the nape of your neck and move up towards the crown of your head. Use a higher guard setting for the back of your head.

Step 3: Cut the top of your hair. Use the hair-cutting scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front of your head and work your way to the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 4: Blend the sides and the back of your hair. Use the clipper without a guard to blend the sides and the back of your hair. Make sure the transitions are smooth and there are no visible lines.

Step 5: Finish up with the details. Use the scissors to trim the hair around your ears and the nape of your neck. Check your hair in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Cut with Clippers

Tip 1: Start with clean and dry hair. This will make it easier to cut your hair and ensure a more precise cut.

Tip 2: Use a mirror to check the back of your head. This will help you see any uneven spots and make adjustments accordingly.

Tip 3: Take your time. Cutting your hair with clippers can be tricky, so take your time and be patient. It is better to cut your hair in small sections rather than trying to do it all at once.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut a pixie cut with regular scissors?

A: It is possible to cut a pixie cut with regular scissors, but it can be more difficult to achieve a precise cut.

Q: How often should I cut my pixie cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut.

Hair Care after Cutting a Pixie Cut

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.

Tip 2: Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.

Tip 3: Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.

Tip 4: Trim your hair regularly to maintain your pixie cut.

Cutting a pixie cut with clippers may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be easily done. Just remember to take your time and be patient. Good luck!
