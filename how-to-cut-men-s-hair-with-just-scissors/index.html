How To Cut Men s Hair With Just Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money on haircuts or simply prefer to cut your hair at home, you might be wondering how to cut men's hair with just scissors. While it might seem daunting at first, with a little practice and patience, you can achieve a clean, professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step, including tips and tricks for achieving the perfect cut every time.

Description

Cutting men's hair with just scissors can be a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your hairstyle without having to visit a barber or hair salon. With the right tools, techniques, and a bit of practice, you can achieve a clean, polished look that rivals that of a professional haircut.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Just Scissors

Before you get started, it's important to gather all the necessary tools and materials. You'll need a pair of high-quality hair cutting scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will help you to see any uneven areas or split ends that need to be trimmed.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the desired style. This will help you to see where to make your cuts.

Step 3: Wet Your Hair

Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will help to prevent split ends.

Step 4: Start Cutting Your Hair

Using your scissors, begin cutting your hair in small sections. Start at the back and work your way forward, making sure to cut evenly and avoid any uneven lines or choppy areas.

Step 5: Check Your Progress in the Mirror

After each section, check your hair in the mirror to make sure you're making progress and that your cut is even. Adjust as necessary.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots. You can also use the scissors to shape your sideburns or create a more defined hairline.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Just Scissors

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Invest in a high-quality pair of hair cutting scissors. Cheap, dull scissors can lead to uneven or choppy cuts.
  • Start with small sections and work your way up. This will help you to avoid cutting too much hair at once and making mistakes.
  • Use your comb to help guide your scissors and create a more even cut.
  • Take breaks as needed. Cutting hair can be tiring, so be sure to rest your arms and hands as necessary.

FAQ About Cutting Men's Hair with Just Scissors

Can I cut my own hair with just regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can lead to uneven or choppy cuts. Invest in a pair of high-quality hair cutting scissors for best results.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you should cut your hair depends on your individual hair type and style. As a general rule, most people should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain a healthy, polished look.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your style and prevent damage. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments, which can damage your hair.
  • Regularly trim any split ends to prevent them from traveling up the hair shaft.

With a bit of practice and patience, cutting men's hair with just scissors can be a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your hairstyle from home. Just be sure to invest in high-quality tools, take your time, and follow our step-by-step guide for best results.
