What Is The Most Popular Hairstyle For Guys 2021

Written by Oliver Mar 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy who's looking to switch up your hairstyle, you're probably wondering what's currently in style. Well, look no further! In this article, we'll be discussing the most popular hairstyle for guys in 2021.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The most popular hairstyle for guys in 2021 is the textured crop. This look is characterized by short, layered hair on top and tapered sides and back. The hair on top is styled up and forward, creating a messy, textured look.

This hairstyle is popular because it's versatile and easy to style. It works well for both casual and formal occasions and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

To achieve the textured crop, you'll need to have around 2-3 inches of hair on top and have the sides and back tapered. You can ask your barber or stylist for this cut or do it yourself if you have the right tools and skills.

Once you have the right cut, you can style your hair by applying a small amount of texturizing product (like pomade or wax) to your hair and working it through with your fingers. You can then use a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the textured crop:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing product to your hair.
  3. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, focusing on the top and front sections.
  4. Blow dry your hair, using your fingers to shape it as you go.
  5. Finish with a small amount of product to add definition and hold.

Tips

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on how to style your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your hair.

FAQ

Is the textured crop suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the textured crop can work for all hair types, but it may require different products and styling techniques depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How often should I get a trim to maintain the textured crop?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. This includes regular washing and conditioning, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding over-styling or using too much product.

Additionally, consider incorporating a hair mask or deep conditioner into your routine once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Overall, the textured crop is a popular and stylish choice for guys in 2021. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a messy, textured look that's both versatile and easy to maintain.
