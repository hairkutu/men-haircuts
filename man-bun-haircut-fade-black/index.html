Man Bun Haircut Fade Black A Complete Guide To The Latest Trend

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Man bun haircut fade black is one of the most popular hairstyles for men in 2023. This hairstyle is a combination of the classic man bun and a fade haircut. If you are looking to try out this haircut, then this guide is for you. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about the man bun haircut fade black, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Man Bun Haircut Fade Black
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get Man Bun Haircut Fade Black

The man bun haircut fade black is a combination of a fade haircut and a man bun. The fade haircut is a short haircut that gradually fades from the top of the head to the sides and back. The man bun is a long hairstyle where the hair is tied up into a bun at the back of the head. To get this haircut, you will need to have long hair and be comfortable with the man bun style.

Choose a Good Barber

The first step to getting the man bun haircut fade black is to choose a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience cutting long hair and can give you a fade haircut. You can ask for recommendations from friends or check online reviews to find a good barber.

Decide on the Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair will determine how high or low your man bun will be. If you have shorter hair, your man bun will be higher up on your head. If you have longer hair, your man bun will be lower down on your head. Decide on the length of your hair before getting the haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the man bun haircut fade black:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting the haircut, make sure to wash your hair. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair and will ensure that your hair is clean and fresh.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Decide on the length of your hair before getting the haircut. This will help the barber to know how high or low to cut the fade.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides and Back

The barber will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. This will be a short haircut that gradually fades from the top of the head to the sides and back.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair into a Man Bun

Once the fade is complete, the barber will tie your hair into a man bun at the back of your head. The man bun can be high or low depending on the length of your hair.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your man bun haircut fade black:

Use Hair Products

Use hair products like wax or pomade to style your hair and keep it in place. These products will also give your hair a shiny and polished look.

Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair regularly to keep it neat and tidy. This will also help to prevent tangles and knots in your hair.

Accessorize Your Hair

Accessorize your hair with headbands or hair ties to keep your hair in place and add a stylish touch to your look.

FAQ

Can I Get a Man Bun Haircut Fade Black with Short Hair?

No, the man bun haircut fade black requires long hair. If you have short hair, you will need to grow it out before getting this haircut.

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and fresh. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your man bun haircut fade black:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. This will also help to maintain the shape and style of your man bun.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers and straighteners as much as possible. These tools can damage your hair and lead to dryness and breakage.

Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. This will help to prevent dryness and damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Now that you have a complete guide to the man bun haircut fade black, you can confidently rock this stylish and trendy hairstyle. With the right barber, styling products, and hair care, you can maintain your man bun and keep it looking great all year round.
