How To Trim Your Own Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Trimming your own head can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and efficiently. In this guide, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to trim your own head, as well as tips and hair care advice to ensure you achieve the best results possible.

How to Trim Your Own Head

Before we dive in, it's important to make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a good pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror to help you see the back of your head. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in trimming your own head is to wash and dry your hair. This will help you get a better idea of your hair's natural texture and shape, as well as make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Start with the Clippers

Next, use your clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length. Take your time and work in small sections, making sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 3: Move on to the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, use your scissors to trim the top of your head. Again, work in small sections and take your time to ensure even and balanced results.

Step 4: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, use your scissors to trim them to the desired length. Start with small snips and work your way up until you achieve the desired look.

Step 5: Clean up the Edges

Finally, use your clippers and scissors to clean up the edges around your ears and neck. Take your time and use small, precise movements to achieve a polished and professional look.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Head

Trimming your own head can be a challenging task, but these tips will help make the process easier and more efficient:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one
  • Work in small sections to ensure even results
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Use a handheld mirror to see the back of your head
  • Invest in quality tools to ensure the best results

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming your own head:

Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?

No, it's best to use hair cutting scissors as they are specifically designed for cutting hair and will give you better results.

How often should I trim my own head?

This will depend on your hair type and desired length. As a general rule, it's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained.

Can I trim my own head if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just be sure to work in small sections and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.

Hair Care After Trimming Your Own Head

Once you've finished trimming your own head, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to trim your own head with confidence and achieve professional-looking results every time.
