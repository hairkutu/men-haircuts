How To Style Hair With Short Layers On Top

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to refresh your hairstyle, short layers on top can give you a chic and trendy look. However, figuring out how to style hair with short layers on top can be a bit tricky. Fortunately, we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide that will help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Short layers on top can be a great way to add texture and volume to your hair. They work especially well for those with thin or fine hair, as they can make your hair look fuller and thicker. Short layers on top can also add movement and dimension to your hair, making it look more lively and dynamic.

How to Style Hair with Short Layers on Top

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair with short layers on top:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add texture and volume to your hair, making it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style. Be sure to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the back and working your way forward. Be sure to lift the hair at the roots to add volume. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any frizzy or unruly sections.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Take a small amount of styling wax or pomade and rub it between your palms. Apply it to the ends of your hair, working your way up to the roots. This will help define the layers and add texture to your hair. You can also use a curling iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish by spraying your hair with a light hold hairspray. This will help hold your style in place and add some shine to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect style with short layers on top:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten any frizzy or unruly sections of your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to the ends of your hair to define the layers and add texture.
  • Use a curling iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to hold your style in place and add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Can short layers on top work for all hair types?

Short layers on top work especially well for those with thin or fine hair, as they can make your hair look fuller and thicker. However, they can also work for other hair types with a little bit of tweaking.

How often should I wash my hair if I have short layers on top?

It's generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have short layers on top. This will help maintain the texture and volume of your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair in great shape:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time. Use heat protectant products before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style your hair with short layers on top and achieve a chic and trendy look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques!
