How To Get Thicker Hair Over 40 Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Apr 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its volume. This can be especially frustrating for women over 40 who want to maintain a youthful appearance. However, there are ways to get thicker hair even as you age. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve thicker, more voluminous hair.

How to Get Thicker Hair Over 40: Step by Step

Step 1: Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a significant impact on the health of your hair. Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and thickness. Foods that are high in protein, such as eggs, lean meat, and fish, are particularly beneficial. Additionally, foods that are high in biotin, such as nuts and seeds, can help strengthen your hair.

Step 2: Use Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a thickening shampoo and conditioner can help add volume to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which are known to help strengthen and thicken hair.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look thin and flat. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use these tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and only use them on low heat.

Step 4: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thin and frizzy. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Step 5: Try Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a quick way to add volume to your hair, hair extensions can be a great option. There are many different types of extensions available, from clip-ins to sew-ins, so you're sure to find one that works for you.

Tips for Getting Thicker Hair Over 40

In addition to the steps outlined above, here are some additional tips for getting thicker hair over 40:

Tip 1: Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth.

Tip 2: Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

Tip 3: Consider taking a hair growth supplement, such as biotin or collagen.

Tip 4: Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as perms and relaxers, which can damage your hair.

FAQs About Getting Thicker Hair Over 40

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're stressed, your body releases hormones that can disrupt the hair growth cycle.

Q: Will cutting my hair make it thicker?

A: No, cutting your hair won't make it thicker. However, getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I use hair extensions if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume to thinning hair. Just be sure to choose extensions that are gentle on your hair and scalp.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker Hair Over 40

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, thick hair as you age:

Tip 1: Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

Tip 3: Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

Tip 4: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, more voluminous hair even as you age. Remember to eat a healthy diet, use thickening products, avoid excessive heat styling, get regular trims, and consider hair extensions if you're looking for a quick fix. With a little extra care and attention, you can have the beautiful, healthy hair you've always wanted.
