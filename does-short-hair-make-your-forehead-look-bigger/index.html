Does Short Hair Make Your Forehead Look Bigger

Written by Petter Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a popular hairstyle choice among women, especially during the summer months. However, some women are hesitant to try it because they fear that it will make their forehead look bigger. In this article, we will explore whether short hair really makes your forehead look bigger or not.

Description

Many women believe that short hair makes their forehead look bigger because it exposes more of their forehead. In reality, the length of your hair has little to do with the size of your forehead. The appearance of a larger forehead is often due to the way the hair is styled, the shape of your face, and the proportion of your features.

How to

If you have a larger forehead and want to try a short hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to balance out your features:

1. Add Bangs:

Adding bangs can help to visually shorten your forehead and balance out your features. Blunt bangs or side-swept bangs are both great options.

2. Opt for Volume:

Adding volume to your hair can also help to balance out your features. Use volumizing products or try backcombing the roots of your hair to add height.

3. Consider Your Face Shape:

The shape of your face can also have an impact on how short hair looks on you. If you have a round face, a shorter bob may not be the most flattering option. Instead, opt for a longer bob or a lob (long bob) to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shorter, layered haircut may be more flattering.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow if you want to try a short hairstyle:

1. Research:

Look for inspiration images online or in magazines to get an idea of what type of short hairstyle you want to try.

2. Consult with a Stylist:

Visit a hairstylist and discuss your options. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and features.

3. Try it Out:

When you go in for your haircut, be sure to communicate with your stylist and let them know if you're unsure about anything. Once your hair is cut, style it at home and see how you feel about the new look.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when trying a short hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Lifestyle:

Short hair can be more high maintenance than longer hair, so consider your lifestyle before making the change.

2. Experiment with Styling:

Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, so don't be afraid to experiment with different looks.

3. Be Confident:

Ultimately, the most important thing when trying a new hairstyle is to be confident and own the look.

FAQ

1. Does short hair make your forehead look bigger?

No, the length of your hair has little to do with the size of your forehead. The appearance of a larger forehead is often due to the way the hair is styled, the shape of your face, and the proportion of your features.

2. What type of short hairstyle is best for a larger forehead?

Adding bangs can help to visually shorten your forehead and balance out your features. Blunt bangs or side-swept bangs are both great options.

3. How can I make my short hair look good?

Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you. Consider your face shape and features when choosing a short hairstyle.

Hair Care

When you have short hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are a few tips:

1. Shampoo and Condition Regularly:

Short hair can get greasy quickly, so be sure to shampoo and condition regularly.

2. Use Heat Protectant:

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

3. Get Regular Trims:

To maintain the shape of your short hairstyle, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

In conclusion, short hair does not necessarily make your forehead look bigger. The appearance of a larger forehead is often due to the way the hair is styled, the shape of your face, and the proportion of your features. With the right styling techniques and confidence, anyone can rock a short hairstyle.
