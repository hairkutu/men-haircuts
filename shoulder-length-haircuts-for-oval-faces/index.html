Shoulder Length Haircuts For Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you an oval-faced beauty struggling to find the right haircut that accentuates your natural features? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide on the best shoulder length haircuts for oval faces. From how to choose the right cut to hair care tips, we have got you covered.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off a wide range of haircuts. Shoulder length haircuts are the perfect length for oval faces, as they highlight the cheekbones and frame the face perfectly.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing the right shoulder length haircut for an oval face, it's important to consider the hair texture and thickness as well. Here are some of the best shoulder length haircuts for oval faces:

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a perfect option for those who want to keep their hair length around the shoulders. The layers add texture and dimension to the hair, drawing attention to the cheekbones and jawline. This cut works well with both straight and wavy hair textures.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a chic and sophisticated option for oval faces. This haircut is perfect for those with thicker hair as it adds volume and weight to the hair. It works best with straight hair textures and can be styled sleek or with a slight wave.

Side-Swept Bangs

For those who want to add a little extra to their shoulder length haircut, side-swept bangs are a great option. This cut adds softness and frames the face perfectly. It works best with straight to wavy hair textures.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect shoulder length haircut for an oval face:

Step 1: Consultation

Before anything else, consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture. Bring inspiration photos and discuss any concerns or preferences you may have.

Step 2: Hair Preparation

Wash and dry your hair prior to your haircut appointment. This will help your hairstylist assess your hair texture and make the necessary adjustments to the cut.

Step 3: Cut

Your hairstylist will begin cutting your hair according to the chosen style. They may use various techniques such as layering, texturizing, or blunt cutting to achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

After the cut is complete, your hairstylist will style your hair to perfection. They may use hot tools or styling products to achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a shoulder length haircut for an oval face:

- Choose a haircut that complements your natural features. - Consider your hair texture and thickness when selecting a cut. - Regular trims will help maintain the shape of your haircut. - Use hair care products that are appropriate for your hair type to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best shoulder length haircut for an oval face?

There are several great options for shoulder length haircuts for oval faces. Some of the best include layered lobs, blunt bobs, and side-swept bangs.

Can oval faces pull off shorter haircuts?

Yes, oval faces can pull off shorter haircuts such as pixie cuts or bobs. However, it's important to consider the hair texture and thickness before getting a shorter cut.

Do shoulder length haircuts require a lot of maintenance?

Shoulder length haircuts require moderate maintenance, including regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut and using appropriate hair care products for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best, use hair care products that are appropriate for your hair type. Regular trims will also help maintain the shape of your cut. Additionally, avoid using hot tools too often and protect your hair from the sun and heat damage.

With this guide, you're now equipped to choose the perfect shoulder length haircut for your oval face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take into account your hair texture and thickness when selecting a cut. With the right care and maintenance, your shoulder length haircut will look fabulous for months to come.
