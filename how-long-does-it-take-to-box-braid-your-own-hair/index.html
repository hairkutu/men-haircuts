How Long Does It Take To Box Braid Your Own Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that is both versatile and low maintenance. They are a great way to add length and style to your hair while protecting your natural hair from damage caused by heat and styling products. But how long does it take to box braid your own hair? In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to box braid your own hair, as well as tips and tricks to make the process easier and faster.

Description

Box braids are individual braids that are sectioned off into small squares, or "boxes," and then braided from the root to the tip. They can be done using either synthetic or natural hair extensions, depending on the desired length and thickness of the braids. Box braids are a great protective style for those with natural hair, as they allow the hair to rest and grow without the need for daily styling and manipulation.

How to Box Braid Your Own Hair

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before starting the braiding process, it is important to prep your hair by washing and conditioning it. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any dirt or product buildup that could interfere with the braiding process. It is also important to detangle your hair and trim any split ends to prevent breakage and make the braids easier to manage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small squares using a rat-tail comb. The size of the sections will depend on the desired thickness of the braids. Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair out of the way while you work on each section.

Step 3: Start Braiding

Take a small section of hair from the first box and divide it into three equal parts. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue braiding in this way, adding in more hair from each box as you go, until you reach the end of the section. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat the braiding process on each section of hair until all of your hair is braided. This may take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair and the size of the braids.

Tips for Faster Braiding

Box braiding your own hair can be a time-consuming process, but there are a few tips and tricks that can help make the process faster and more efficient:

  • Use pre-stretched braiding hair to speed up the braiding process.
  • Braid your hair in smaller sections to make the braids more manageable.
  • Take breaks as needed to prevent hand and wrist fatigue.
  • Listen to music or watch a movie to make the time go by faster.

FAQ

How long does it take to box braid your own hair?

The length of time it takes to box braid your own hair will depend on the length and thickness of your hair and the size of the braids. On average, it can take anywhere from 4 to 8 hours to complete the braiding process.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on how well they are maintained. To prolong the life of your braids, it is important to keep them clean and moisturized and to avoid excessive pulling or tugging on the braids.

Hair Care

While box braids are a great protective style, it is important to take care of your natural hair underneath. This includes keeping your scalp clean and moisturized, avoiding excessive pulling or tugging on the braids, and using a satin or silk scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while sleeping.

Overall, box braiding your own hair can be a time-consuming process, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Whether you choose to use synthetic or natural hair extensions, box braids are a stylish and low-maintenance way to protect and grow your natural hair.
