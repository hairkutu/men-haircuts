What Is The Best Shampoo And Conditioner For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 17, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know that finding the right shampoo and conditioner can be a challenge. You need products that will help tame frizz, define your curls, and moisturize your hair without weighing it down. In this article, we’ll explore what is the best shampoo and conditioner for thick curly hair and provide some tips for taking care of your hair.

How to Choose the Best Shampoo and Conditioner for Thick Curly Hair

When shopping for shampoo and conditioner for thick curly hair, look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Instead, look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil, which can help moisturize your hair and define your curls.

It’s also important to choose a shampoo and conditioner that are pH-balanced. Your hair has a natural pH level of around 4.5 to 5.5, and using products that are too alkaline or too acidic can disrupt this balance and cause damage to your hair. Look for products that have a pH level of around 5.5 to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Top Shampoo for Thick Curly Hair

One of the best shampoos for thick curly hair is the Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo. This sulfate-free shampoo is infused with coconut oil, silk protein, and neem oil, which work together to moisturize your hair and enhance your natural curls. It also has a delicious tropical scent that will leave your hair smelling amazing.

Another great option is the DevaCurl No-Poo Original Zero Lather Conditioning Cleanser. This gentle cleanser is formulated without sulfates and is designed to cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils. It’s also enriched with peppermint and grapeseed oil, which help to stimulate your scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

Top Conditioner for Thick Curly Hair

The best conditioner for thick curly hair is the OGX Argan Oil of Morocco Conditioner. This conditioner is infused with argan oil, which is rich in antioxidants and vitamin E, and can help to nourish and moisturize your hair. It also has a delicious vanilla and musk scent that will leave your hair smelling amazing.

Another great option is the TRESemmé Flawless Curls Conditioner. This conditioner is formulated with vitamin B1 and keratin, which help to strengthen and nourish your hair. It’s also designed to detangle your hair and enhance your natural curls, leaving your hair looking shiny and healthy.

How to Use Shampoo and Conditioner for Thick Curly Hair

When using shampoo and conditioner for thick curly hair, it’s important to use the right technique. Start by wetting your hair thoroughly, then apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your scalp. Massage the shampoo into your scalp, then work it through the rest of your hair, focusing on the roots. Rinse your hair thoroughly, then apply a quarter-sized amount of conditioner to the ends of your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly, then leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Tips for Taking Care of Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of thick curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Don’t wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my thick curly hair?

A: While you can technically use any shampoo and conditioner on your thick curly hair, it’s best to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Regular shampoo and conditioner can contain harsh sulfates that can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your thick curly hair no more than two or three times per week. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: Can I use a leave-in conditioner on my thick curly hair?

A: Yes, using a leave-in conditioner can be a great way to moisturize and define your curls. Look for a leave-in conditioner that is specifically designed for thick curly hair, and apply it to damp hair after washing and conditioning.

Conclusion

If you have thick curly hair, choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is key to keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products that are sulfate-free, pH-balanced, and enriched with natural ingredients like coconut oil and argan oil. And don't forget to follow our tips for taking care of your hair!
