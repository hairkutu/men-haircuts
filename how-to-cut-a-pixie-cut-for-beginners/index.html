How To Cut A Pixie Cut For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that is both stylish and practical, then the pixie cut might be just what you need. While it might seem intimidating to cut your hair short, especially if you have never done it before, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can easily achieve a pixie cut at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a pixie cut for beginners, including tips and tricks to make the process as smooth as possible.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically has short layers on top and longer layers on the sides and back. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways, from sleek and sophisticated to messy and tousled. Pixie cuts are particularly popular among women who want to add some edge to their look, as well as those who are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling time.

How to Cut a Pixie Cut for Beginners

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting a pixie cut is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It is best to use professional-grade scissors, as they will give you clean, precise cuts.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you need to section your hair into manageable parts. Start by dividing your hair into two sections: the top and the bottom. Use hair clips to secure the top section, leaving the bottom section loose. Then, divide the bottom section into smaller sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start by cutting the back of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the bottom, and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut it straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair, moving up towards the crown of your head. Be sure to cut each section to the same length, to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you have finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Comb a small section of hair straight down, and hold it between your fingers. Use the hair in the back as a guide, and cut the side section to the same length. Repeat this process for each side, making sure that both sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top. Unclip the top section of your hair, and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your fingers, and cut it to the desired length. You can choose to leave the top longer or cut it short, depending on your preference. Be sure to cut the hair evenly, and blend it into the sides and back.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Cut

Cutting a pixie cut can be a bit tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. Here are some tips to help make the process easier:

  • Start with clean, dry hair, so that you can see the natural texture and shape of your hair.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors, and make sure to cut in small sections.
  • Take your time, and don't rush the process. Cutting hair too quickly can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.
  • Before you start cutting, decide on the length and style that you want. Use pictures for inspiration, and communicate your ideas clearly.

FAQ about Pixie Cut

1. How often do I need to cut my pixie cut?

Pixie cuts require regular maintenance, as they tend to grow out quickly. You may need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks, depending on your hair growth rate.

2. Can I style my pixie cut?

Yes, pixie cuts can be styled in many ways, from slicked back to tousled and messy. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that works for you.

3. Is a pixie cut suitable for all hair types?

Pixie cuts can work for most hair types, but they may require different styling techniques. If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to use a diffuser to enhance your natural texture. If you have fine hair, you may need to use volumizing products to add body and fullness.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut

Once you have cut your pixie cut, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush, which can damage the hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut a pixie cut at home, even if you're a beginner. Whether you want a sleek and sophisticated look or a messy and edgy style, a pixie cut is a versatile and low-maintenance option that is perfect for any occasion.
