How To Do Pin Up Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Robby Mar 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Looking for a retro-inspired hairstyle that’s perfect for short hair? Pin up hairstyles are a great choice! These styles are classic and stylish, and can be done with a variety of hair lengths and textures. In this article, we’ll show you how to do pin up hairstyles for short hair, step by step.

Description

Pin up hairstyles are a vintage style that has become popular again in recent years. These hairstyles are characterized by their voluminous curls, sleek waves, and dramatic hair accessories like bows and flowers. Pin up hairstyles are perfect for formal occasions or just for adding some retro flair to your everyday look.

How to

Here’s how to do pin up hairstyles for short hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling. If your hair is naturally curly, you may want to straighten it first for a sleeker look.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into 1-inch sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl and style your hair.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl each section of hair. Start at the roots and wrap the hair around the curling iron or roller, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Pin your curls

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use bobby pins to pin each curl in place. Pin the curls up and back, creating a voluminous look.

Step 5: Add accessories

Pin up hairstyles often involve adding accessories like bows, flowers, or headbands. Choose an accessory that complements your outfit and pin it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pin up hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Tease the hair at the crown of your head for extra volume.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Can I do pin up hairstyles with short hair?

Yes! Pin up hairstyles can be done with hair of any length. For short hair, you may need to use smaller rollers or curling irons to achieve the desired look.

How long does it take to do a pin up hairstyle?

The time it takes to do a pin up hairstyle will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the complexity of the style. On average, it can take anywhere from 20 minutes to an hour to do a pin up hairstyle.

Hair Care

When styling your hair in a pin up hairstyle, it’s important to use heat protectant products to prevent damage from curling irons and hot rollers. You should also avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends. To keep your hair healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and get regular trims to remove dead ends.

With these tips, you’ll be able to create beautiful pin up hairstyles for short hair in no time. Remember to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you!
