What Does A Number 4 Haircut Look Like

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you planning to get a haircut soon and wondering what a number 4 haircut looks like? Look no further! In this article, we’ll discuss the details of a number 4 haircut, including the length, styling options, and how to maintain it.

Description of a Number 4 Haircut

A number 4 haircut is a medium-length cut that leaves about ½ inch of hair on your scalp. It’s a popular choice for those who want a shorter haircut without going too close to the scalp. The sides and back of the head are typically cut with clippers, while the top is left slightly longer and can be styled in various ways.

One of the best things about a number 4 haircut is its versatility. You can style it in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part. It’s also a great option if you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn’t require too much styling.

How to Get a Number 4 Haircut

If you’re interested in getting a number 4 haircut, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose a barber or stylist

The first step is to find a barber or stylist who has experience with clipper cuts. Look for someone who has good reviews and a portfolio of previous haircuts.

Step 2: Communicate your desired length

When you arrive at the salon, tell the barber or stylist that you want a number 4 haircut. They will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to the desired length.

Step 3: Style the top

The top of your hair will be left longer than the sides and back. You can choose to style it in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 4 Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your number 4 haircut looking fresh:

Tip 1: Use the right products

Use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you want a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel. If you prefer a more natural look, use a matte finish product.

Tip 2: Get regular trims

To keep your haircut looking sharp, it’s important to get regular trims. Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat damage

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a number 4 haircut?

A number 4 haircut looks great on a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Can I style a number 4 haircut in different ways?

Yes! The top of a number 4 haircut is long enough to be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Is a number 4 haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, a number 4 haircut is relatively low-maintenance and doesn’t require too much styling. However, it’s important to get regular trims to keep it looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips for a Number 4 Haircut

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

It’s important to wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Tip 2: Use a hair mask

Once a week, use a hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair. This will help keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when you’re outside for extended periods.

In conclusion, a number 4 haircut is a versatile and stylish option for those who want a shorter haircut without going too close to the scalp. With the right styling products and regular trims, you can keep your number 4 haircut looking fresh and stylish.
