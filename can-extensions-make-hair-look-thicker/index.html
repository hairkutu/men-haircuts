Can Extensions Make Hair Look Thicker

Description

If you have thin hair, you might feel like your hair is impossible to style or maintain. However, hair extensions can help you achieve the thicker hair look you’ve always wanted. Hair extensions are an excellent way to add volume and length to your hair. They come in different colors, lengths, and textures, so you can choose the ones that match your hair perfectly. In this article, we will discuss how extensions can make hair look thicker and guide you on how to apply them.

How to Make Hair Look Thicker with Extensions

Extensions can make hair look thicker because they add volume and density to your hair. They can also cover up bald patches or areas where your hair is thinning. To make your hair look thicker with extensions, you need to choose the right type of extensions.

The best extensions for thin hair are clip-in extensions. They are easy to apply and remove, and you can choose the number of wefts you want to use depending on how much volume you want to add. Clip-in extensions come in different lengths, so you can choose the ones that match your hair perfectly.

You can also opt for tape-in extensions, which are more permanent than clip-ins. Tape-in extensions are attached to your natural hair using a special tape that is designed to last for several weeks. They are also easy to maintain and style, and you can wash them and style them just like your natural hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Apply Extensions for Thicker Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply clip-in extensions to make your hair look thicker:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting about an inch above the nape of your neck. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  2. Take one of the clip-in wefts and attach it to the root of your hair. Make sure to clip it as close to your scalp as possible.
  3. Repeat the same process with the other wefts. Use a mirror to check that the wefts are evenly spaced and that there are no gaps.
  4. Once you have attached all the wefts, blend your natural hair with the extensions. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  5. Style your hair as desired. You can curl or straighten your hair, or create a half-up, half-down style to show off your new thicker-looking hair.

Tips to Make Extensions Look Natural and Thicker

Here are some tips to make your extensions look natural and thicker:

  • Choose extensions that match your hair color and texture perfectly. You can even buy extensions that are a shade lighter or darker than your natural hair to create dimension.
  • Trim your extensions to blend with your natural hair. You can take them to a professional stylist or do it yourself at home.
  • Avoid using too many wefts, as this can make your hair look bulky and unnatural.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your natural hair and extensions a boost.
  • Try to avoid heat styling your extensions too often, as this can damage them and make them look thinner.

FAQs about Hair Extensions and Thick Hair Look

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair extensions and making hair look thicker:

Q: Can extensions damage my natural hair?

A: Extensions can damage your natural hair if they are not applied or maintained properly. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully, and avoid sleeping or showering with your extensions in.

Q: Can I dye my extensions?

A: It depends on the type of extensions you have. Clip-in extensions can be dyed, but tape-in extensions cannot. It’s best to consult with a professional stylist before dyeing your extensions.

Q: How long do extensions last?

A: Clip-in extensions can last for up to a year if they are well-maintained. Tape-in extensions, on the other hand, last for about six to eight weeks.

Hair Care for Thicker Looking Hair with Extensions

To maintain your thicker-looking hair with extensions, you need to take good care of them. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Brush your hair and extensions gently to avoid tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and extensions.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Wash your extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep them looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid sleeping or showering with your extensions in, as this can damage them and make them look thinner.

Overall, extensions can make hair look thicker and give you the confidence to try new hairstyles. With the right type of extensions and proper care, you can have the thicker-looking hair you’ve always wanted.
