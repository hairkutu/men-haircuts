How To Curl Naturally Straight Hair Without Heat

Written by Frank May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have naturally straight hair and want to add some curls without using heat, you're in luck. There are several methods you can use to achieve those curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we'll share with you some tips and techniques on how to curl naturally straight hair without heat.

Description

Curling your hair without heat is a great way to achieve a natural look without damaging your hair. It's an easy and affordable way to add some volume, texture, and bounce to your hair. There are several methods you can use to curl your hair without heat, and we'll discuss them in this article.

How To

Braiding Method

One of the easiest ways to curl your hair without heat is the braiding method. This method works best on damp hair, so start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's slightly damp. Then, divide your hair into small sections and braid each section tightly. Once you've braided all your hair, leave it in for a few hours or overnight. When you take out the braids, you'll have beautiful, natural-looking curls.

Twisting Method

The twisting method is another great way to curl your hair without heat. Start by taking a small section of your hair and twisting it tightly. Then, wrap the twisted section around your finger and hold it in place for a few seconds. Release the section and repeat the process on the rest of your hair. Once you've twisted all your hair, leave it in for a few hours or overnight. When you take out the twists, you'll have soft and bouncy curls.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's slightly damp. It's important to start with damp hair to ensure that your curls hold.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Choose your curling method

Choose your preferred curling method, either braiding or twisting, and work on each section of your hair.

Step 4: Leave your hair in overnight

Once you've curled all your hair, leave it in overnight or for a few hours. This will give your curls time to set and ensure that they hold.

Step 5: Take out the braids or twists

When you wake up or after a few hours, take out the braids or twists from your hair. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair without heat:

  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it's not naturally damp.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after you've curled it. Use your fingers to separate the curls instead.

FAQ

Can I curl my hair without heat if it's naturally curly?

If your hair is naturally curly, you don't need to curl it without heat. However, you can use these methods to enhance your curls or create a different look.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls will last for a few days, depending on how well you take care of them. Use a hair spray or mousse to help your curls hold and avoid touching your hair too much.

Hair Care

Curling your hair without heat is a great way to avoid damaging your hair. However, it's still important to take care of your hair by using a good shampoo and conditioner and avoiding harsh chemicals. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to curl naturally.

Now that you know how to curl naturally straight hair without heat, it's time to try it out for yourself. Experiment with different methods and find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without damaging your hair.
