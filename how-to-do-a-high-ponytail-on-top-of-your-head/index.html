How To Do A High Ponytail On Top Of Your Head

Written by Bardi May 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a chic and easy hairstyle that can be done in just a few minutes, a high ponytail on top of your head is the perfect choice. Not only is it a great way to keep your hair out of your face, but it's also a versatile style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Whether you're heading to work or going out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads. In this blog post, we'll show you how to do a high ponytail on top of your head in just a few simple steps.

How to:

Before we get started, you'll need a few things:

  • A hairbrush
  • A hair tie
  • Hairspray (optional)

Now that you have everything you need, let's get started:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Using a hairbrush, brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to create a smooth ponytail.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your hands, gather all of your hair at the top of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or kinks.

Step 3: Secure Your Ponytail

Using a hair tie, secure your ponytail at the top of your head. Make sure to pull the hair tight to create a sleek and polished look.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

If you want to add a little extra polish to your high ponytail, you can use hairspray to tame any flyaways or add a little extra shine. And that's it! You now have a chic and easy high ponytail on top of your head.

Tips:

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your high ponytail:

  • If you have thick hair, you may want to use two hair ties to keep your ponytail in place.
  • If you want to add a little extra volume to your ponytail, try teasing the hair at the crown of your head before pulling it back.
  • If you want to make your ponytail look more polished, try wrapping a piece of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

FAQ:

Here are a few frequently asked questions about high ponytails:

Can I Wear a High Ponytail on Top of My Head Every Day?

Yes, a high ponytail on top of your head is a versatile style that can be worn every day. Just make sure to give your hair a break every once in a while to prevent damage.

Can I Wear a High Ponytail on Top of My Head to Work?

Yes, a high ponytail on top of your head is a great hairstyle for work. Just make sure to keep it polished and professional.

How Do I Prevent Headaches from Wearing a High Ponytail?

To prevent headaches from wearing a high ponytail, make sure to use a hair tie that is not too tight. You can also try adjusting the height of your ponytail to see if that helps.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair, especially when wearing it in a high ponytail on top of your head. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Give your hair a break from high ponytails every once in a while to prevent damage.

Now that you know how to do a high ponytail on top of your head, you can rock this chic and easy hairstyle anytime, anywhere. Whether you're heading to work or going out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads. So grab your hair tie and get ready to look fabulous!
