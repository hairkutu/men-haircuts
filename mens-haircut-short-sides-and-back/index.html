Mens Haircut Short Sides And Back A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut, then the mens haircut short sides and back is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this popular haircut.

Description

The mens haircut short sides and back is a simple yet stylish haircut that features short hair on the sides and back, and a slightly longer length on the top. This haircut is also known as the "fade" haircut, as the hair gradually fades from short to long. The short sides and back provide a clean and polished look, while the longer hair on top can be styled in various ways to suit your personality and style.

How to get a Mens Haircut Short Sides and Back

To get a mens haircut short sides and back, you can either visit a professional barber or do it yourself at home. If you're getting it done by a professional, make sure to communicate your desired length and style clearly. If you're doing it at home, make sure to have the right tools, such as clippers, scissors, and a comb.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a mens haircut short sides and back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter one.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  4. Style the hair on top as desired using a comb and/or hair product.
  5. Clean up any stray hairs and make sure the cut is even.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Haircut Short Sides and Back

To keep your mens haircut short sides and back looking sharp, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit a professional barber every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair using a high-quality hair product that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides and back:

1. Is the mens haircut short sides and back suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens haircut short sides and back suits all face shapes. However, the length of the hair on top can be adjusted according to your face shape to create a more flattering look.

2. Can I style my hair differently with the mens haircut short sides and back?

Yes, you can style your hair on top in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.

3. How do I maintain the fade on the sides and back?

Visit a professional barber every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up to maintain the fade on the sides and back. You can also use clippers at home to trim any stray hairs.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Short Sides and Back

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens haircut short sides and back looking its best:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the mens haircut short sides and back is a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut that is perfect for any occasion. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can keep your hair looking sharp and stylish all year round.
