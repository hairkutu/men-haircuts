  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

How often do I need to get a trim with a shaved sides long top haircut?

We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Maintaining a shaved sides long top haircut requires some extra hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
