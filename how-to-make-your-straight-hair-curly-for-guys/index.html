How To Make Your Straight Hair Curly For Guys

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing how to make your straight hair curly for guys. Whether you want tight curls or loose waves, we have got you covered.

Description

Straight hair can be difficult to style, especially if you want to add some curls. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve curly hair that looks natural and effortless. In this article, we will be discussing how to make your straight hair curly for guys, including step-by-step instructions and helpful tips.

How To

There are several methods you can use to make your straight hair curly:

Method 1: Using a Curling Iron

The easiest way to add curls to your straight hair is by using a curling iron. Here's how:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant product.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  3. Take one section and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  4. Repeat with each section until you have curled all of your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold the curls in place.

Tip: Use a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of curls you want. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel size, and for looser waves, use a larger barrel size.

Method 2: Using Rollers

You can also use rollers to add curls to your hair. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant product.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  3. Take one section and wrap it around a roller, securing it in place.
  4. Repeat with each section until you have rolled all of your hair.
  5. Leave the rollers in your hair for several hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the rollers and style your hair with your fingers or a comb.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold the curls in place.

Tip: Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and larger rollers for looser waves.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and create a clean base for styling.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant Product

Before applying heat to your hair, it is essential to protect it from damage. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair, focusing on the ends and lengths. This will help to prevent split ends and breakage.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you curl every section evenly.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Rollers

Choose your preferred method of adding curls to your hair. Use a curling iron or rollers to create curls or waves in each section of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have curled your hair, style it with your fingers or a comb. You can also apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to hold the curls in place.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold the curls in place. Choose a hairspray that matches your hair type and the level of hold you prefer.

Tips

  • Don't forget to apply a heat protectant product before using heat styling tools.
  • Choose a curling iron or rollers that match the size of curls you want.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to help hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Can guys with straight hair get curly hair?

Yes, guys with straight hair can get curly hair by using heat styling tools or rollers.

What size curling iron should I use?

The size of curling iron you should use depends on the size of curls you want. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel size, and for looser waves, use a larger barrel size.

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type and the styling products you use. Generally, curls created with heat styling tools will last for a day or two.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy locks. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant product before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid overusing heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your straight hair curly for guys. Whether you want tight curls or loose waves, there is a method that will work for you. Don't be afraid to experiment and find the best method for your hair type and desired style.
