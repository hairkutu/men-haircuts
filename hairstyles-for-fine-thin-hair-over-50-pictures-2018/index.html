If you are over 50 and have fine, thin hair, you may be struggling to find a hairstyle that suits you. But don't worry, there are plenty of options out there that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for fine thin hair over 50 pictures 2018, with tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for your fine, thin hair can make all the difference. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape. Some hairstyles look better on certain face shapes than others.
  • Think about the texture of your hair. Fine, thin hair will require a different cut than thick, curly hair.
  • Consider your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need something low maintenance?
  • Look for inspiration. Browse magazines and websites to find hairstyles you like.

Once you have a good idea of what you want, it's time to talk to your stylist. They can help you choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Hairstyling Tips

Now that you have a hairstyle in mind, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some step-by-step hairstyling tips:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add body.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add texture and body.
  5. Tease the crown of your hair to add height.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling fine, thin hair. Over-styling can weigh your hair down and make it look flat and lifeless.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have your perfect hairstyle, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair over time.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great between salon visits.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for fine thin hair over 50 pictures 2018?

Some of the best hairstyles for fine thin hair over 50 pictures 2018 include short pixie cuts, layered bobs, and textured crops. These styles are low maintenance and can add volume and texture to your hair.

Can I color my fine thin hair?

Yes, you can color your fine thin hair. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and doesn't damage your hair. Talk to your stylist about the best color options for your hair type.

How often should I wash my fine thin hair?

It's best to wash fine thin hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils. However, if your hair gets oily quickly, you may need to wash it more often.

Hair Care for Fine Thin Hair Over 50

Caring for your fine thin hair is essential for keeping it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise to reduce stress, which can cause hair loss.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your fine thin hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right hairstyle for fine thin hair over 50 can be challenging, but with the right cut and styling tips, you can achieve a look that suits you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and talk to your stylist for personalized recommendations.
