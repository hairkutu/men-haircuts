Best Hairspray For Volume And Humidity In 2023

Written by Christine Mar 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling flat and lifeless, especially during humid weather? Luckily, there are many hairsprays designed to add volume and protect your hair from humidity. In this article, we will discuss the best hairspray for volume and humidity, how to use it, and tips for keeping your hair looking its best.

Description

Humidity can wreak havoc on your hair, causing it to frizz and lose volume. To combat this, you need a hairspray that not only provides hold but also protects your hair from moisture. The best hairspray for volume and humidity will keep your hair in place while preventing frizz and maintaining volume.

How to

When using hairspray for volume and humidity, it's important to apply it correctly. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairspray

Look for a hairspray that is designed for volume and humidity. It should provide a flexible hold without leaving your hair stiff or sticky.

Step 2: Apply to Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is completely dry before applying hairspray. This will ensure that the product can provide a strong hold without weighing your hair down.

Step 3: Hold the Can at Arm's Length

Hold the can of hairspray about 10 to 12 inches away from your head. This will ensure a light, even mist that won't leave your hair feeling heavy or crunchy.

Step 4: Spray in Sections

Divide your hair into sections and spray each section individually. This will ensure that every part of your hair is coated with the product, providing maximum hold and protection from humidity.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hairspray for volume and humidity:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add even more volume to your hair. - Apply hairspray to the roots of your hair to create lift and volume. - Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. - Don't overdo it with hairspray. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. - Avoid touching your hair too much after applying hairspray, as this can cause it to lose volume.

How often should I use hairspray?

It's best to use hairspray sparingly, as overuse can leave your hair feeling stiff and crunchy. Use just enough to achieve the hold and volume you want, and avoid touching your hair too much after applying.

Hair Care

Using the right hairspray for volume and humidity is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some other tips to help keep your hair looking its best:

- Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools. - Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. - Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using the best hairspray for volume and humidity, you can keep your hair looking voluminous and frizz-free even on the most humid days.
