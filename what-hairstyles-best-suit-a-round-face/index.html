What Hairstyles Best Suit A Round Face In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on what hairstyles best suit a round face, including how to style them, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Content

  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Round faces typically have a soft, circular shape with wider cheekbones and a shorter chin. The goal is to elongate the face and create more definition in the jawline. Hairstyles that offer height and volume on top, while keeping the sides and back of the hair closer to the head, are the perfect solution. Below are some of the hairstyles that best suit a round face shape.

How to

First, determine your face shape. If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and straight hairstyles that fall at the jawline. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add length and create angles in the face.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles can be a great option for those with a round face shape. A layered pixie cut, for example, will add height and volume on top, elongating the face shape. Adding texture to the hair with a choppy cut or tousled waves will also create more dimension and definition.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium-length hairstyles that fall just below the shoulders can also work well with a round face shape. A long bob with layers can create the illusion of a longer face, while adding volume to the crown of the head. Beachy waves or loose curls can also add texture and movement to the hair.

Long Hairstyles

For those with long hair, layers and face-framing highlights can add dimension and definition to a round face shape. Opt for a side part and long, loose waves to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. A high ponytail or updo with height at the crown can also add length to the face.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some of the best hairstyles for a round face:

Layered Pixie Cut

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush.
  2. Use a flat iron to create texture and definition in the hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of texturizing paste to the ends of the hair for added texture and hold.

Long Bob with Layers

  1. Apply a heat protectant to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush.
  2. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  3. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray to the ends of the hair for added texture and movement.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid blunt bangs and straight hairstyles that fall at the jawline.
  • Opt for hairstyles with height and volume on top.
  • Add texture and movement to the hair with layers, waves, or curls.
  • Try a side part to create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  • Consider face-framing highlights to add definition to the jawline.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for a round face:

Can I still wear a bob with a round face shape?

A chin-length bob can still work with a round face shape, but make sure to add layers and texture to create more definition in the hair.

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

A layered haircut with long, loose curls can work well with a round face shape. Consider adding face-framing layers to create more definition.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Consider getting regular trims to remove split ends and keep the hair looking healthy and full of life.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face shape can be challenging, but with the right cut and styling techniques, anyone can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Use these tips and tricks to create the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks to find the one that best suits you.
