Men s Hair Short Back And Sides Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style, the "short back and sides long on top" is the perfect choice. This versatile hairstyle has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, style it, and take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The short back and sides long on top hairstyle is characterized by short, tapered hair on the back and sides of the head, and longer hair on the top. This style is sometimes referred to as a "modern pompadour" or a "quiff" and can be worn in a variety of ways. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference.

How to

The short back and sides long on top hairstyle can be achieved by visiting a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating this style. When discussing the style with your stylist, be sure to mention the length you want on the top and the type of taper you prefer on the back and sides. You can also show them a picture of the style you want to achieve.

Step by Step

If you want to achieve the short back and sides long on top hairstyle at home, you can follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a side part, if desired.
  5. Use a styling product to create texture and hold on the top of your hair.
  6. Use a trimmer or scissors to taper the back and sides of your hair, leaving the top longer.

Tips

To get the most out of your short back and sides long on top hairstyle, follow these tips:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to achieve the desired texture and hold.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the style and keep the back and sides tapered.
  • Experiment with different lengths on the top to find the perfect look for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.
  • Use a hairdryer and round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides long on top hairstyle:

How long should the hair on top be?

The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference. Generally, the hair on top should be at least a few inches long.

What type of face shape does this hairstyle suit?

The short back and sides long on top hairstyle suits a variety of face shapes, including square, oval, and round.

What type of hair works best for this hairstyle?

This hairstyle works best for thick, straight, and wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to use a straightening iron to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides long on top hairstyle looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short back and sides long on top hairstyle and keep it looking great for weeks to come. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
