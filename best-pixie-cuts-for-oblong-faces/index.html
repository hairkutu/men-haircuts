How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best pixie cuts for oblong faces, it's important to determine if you actually have an oblong face shape. To do this, take a measuring tape and measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead and jawline are roughly the same width and your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oblong face shape.

Best Pixie Cuts for Oblong Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's talk about the best pixie cuts for oblong faces.

The Classic Pixie

The classic pixie cut is a timeless style that looks great on oblong faces. This cut features short, cropped sides and back with longer, layered hair on top. The longer hair on top helps to balance out the length of the face.

The Textured Pixie

For those who want a little more edge to their pixie cut, the textured pixie is the perfect option. This cut features choppy layers and a messy, tousled look. The added texture helps to create the illusion of width to the face.

The Asymmetric Pixie

If you're feeling daring, the asymmetric pixie is a great option for oblong faces. This cut features longer hair on one side and shorter hair on the other. The asymmetry helps to break up the length of the face and create a more balanced look.

The Long Pixie

For those who aren't quite ready to commit to a super short pixie cut, the long pixie is a great option. This cut features longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The longer hair on top helps to create the illusion of width to the face.

The Pixie Bob

If you want a pixie cut with a little more length, the pixie bob is the perfect option for oblong faces. This cut features longer hair in the front with shorter hair in the back. The added length in the front helps to create the illusion of width to the face.

How to Achieve the Perfect Pixie Cut

Now that you've chosen your perfect pixie cut, it's time to achieve it! Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie cut:
  1. Find a reputable stylist who has experience with pixie cuts.
  2. Bring in pictures of the pixie cut you want to help your stylist understand your vision.
  3. Be open to suggestions from your stylist. They may have ideas to help you achieve your desired look.
  4. Communicate with your stylist throughout the process to ensure you're happy with the result.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining a pixie cut requires a little more upkeep than longer hairstyles. Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:
  1. Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh.
  2. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and definition to your hair.
  3. Consider investing in a quality hair dryer and styling tools to make styling your pixie cut easier.
  4. Embrace your natural texture and work with it instead of against it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look longer?

A: It depends on the cut. Some pixie cuts, like the classic pixie or long pixie, can actually help to balance out the length of an oblong face.

Q: Can I still wear accessories with a pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! In fact, accessories like bold earrings or headbands can help to draw attention to your face and balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Maintaining healthy hair is important, no matter what your hair length is. Here are some hair care tips for pixie cuts:
  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Consider using a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair.
  4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to rocking the perfect pixie cut for your oblong face shape. Remember to communicate with your stylist and experiment with different styles to find the perfect cut for you. Happy styling!
