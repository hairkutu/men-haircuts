How To Get The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By determining your face shape and finding a haircut that complements it, you can achieve your perfect look. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Haircuts for Different Face Shapes
  • Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step to getting the perfect haircut is determining your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and look at your face in the mirror. Pay attention to the shape of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. There are six common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will complement it.

Haircuts for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that will flatter it. Here are some guidelines:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds some angles and definition. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Angled bobs
  • Pixie cuts with volume on top

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes are versatile and can pull off many different haircuts. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Blunt bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cuts

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your jawline with your haircut. Some great options include:

  • Soft layers
  • Textured bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long, loose waves

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you'll want to balance your forehead and chin with your haircut. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Pixie cuts with volume on top

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to balance your cheekbones with your haircut. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Short bobs
  • Pixie cuts

Rectangle Face Shape

If you have a rectangle face shape, you'll want to add some width to your face with your haircut. Some great options include:

  • Soft layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Short bobs
  • Pixie cuts

Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know which haircuts work best for your face shape, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect haircut:

  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your appointment
  • Be honest with your stylist about your hair texture and lifestyle
  • Consider getting a consultation before your appointment
  • Ask your stylist for styling tips and product recommendations

FAQs

What if my face shape is a combination of two shapes?

It's common for people to have a combination of two face shapes. In this case, choose a haircut that flatters the shape that is most prominent.

What if I don't like any of the haircuts recommended for my face shape?

At the end of the day, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable. These recommendations are just guidelines, so feel free to deviate from them if you prefer a different style.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's generally recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've gotten the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape, you'll be well on your way to achieving your perfect look. Happy styling!
