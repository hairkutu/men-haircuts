Best Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Female

Written by Aprilia Jun 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that flatters your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oblong faces female. We’ll provide you with a how-to guide, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice that you need to know. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a high forehead and defined jawline. The best hairstyles for oblong faces female are those that add width and volume to the face, making it appear more balanced. These hairstyles should also draw attention away from the forehead and elongated chin.

How To

To find the best hairstyle for your oblong face shape, you need to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. You can consult with a hairstylist to help you choose the best cut and style that flatters your face shape. Some of the best hairstyles for oblong faces female include:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great choice for oblong faces. The layers add volume and dimension to the hair, making the face appear wider. This hairstyle can be styled straight or with soft waves for a more feminine look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for oblong faces, as they draw attention away from the forehead and elongated chin. These bangs can be paired with a variety of hairstyles, including long layers or a bob.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy choice that works well for oblong faces. This short hairstyle adds volume to the crown of the head, making the face appear more balanced. It’s also low-maintenance and easy to style.

Step-by-Step

To achieve these hairstyles, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Layered Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  5. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add soft waves.
  6. Apply a hair spray to set the style in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Part your hair on the side.
  5. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add soft waves.
  6. Brush your bangs to the side and apply a hair spray to set them in place.

Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling cream to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to add volume to the crown of your head.
  5. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add texture.
  6. Apply a hair spray to set the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for your oblong face:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Choose hairstyles with soft waves or curls to add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short.
  • Avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs or center parts, as they emphasize the length of your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyles for oblong faces female:

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is a layered bob, as it adds volume and dimension to the hair, making the face appear wider.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Also, avoid blunt bangs or center parts, as they emphasize the length of your face.

Hair Care

To keep your oblong face looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and promote healthy growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

We hope this article has helped you find the best hairstyles for oblong faces female. Remember, the key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty. With the right cut and style, you can look and feel your best!
