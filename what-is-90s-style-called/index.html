What Is 90S Style Called

Written by Robby May 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on understanding what is 90s style called! The 90s was a decade that marked a significant change in fashion, music, and culture. The style of the 90s was unique and diverse, and it had something for everyone. From grunge to hip hop, the 90s had it all. In this article, we will go over what is 90s style called, how to achieve the look, step by step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

What is 90s style called?

The 90s style is called "retro" or "vintage." It is a term used to describe the fashion trends of the decade. The style was influenced by various subcultures like grunge, hip hop, and rave culture. It was a time when oversized clothing, bright colors, and bold prints were in fashion. Accessories like chokers, snap bracelets, and platform shoes were also popular.

How to achieve the 90s look?

To achieve the 90s look, you need to incorporate key elements of the decade. Here are some steps to help you achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the right clothing

The 90s were all about oversized clothing. So, choose baggy jeans, oversized t-shirts, and hoodies. You can also go for plaid shirts, denim jackets, and leather jackets.

Step 2: Accessorize

Accessories played a significant role in the 90s look. So, add a choker necklace, snap bracelets, and a fanny pack to your outfit. You can also add sunglasses, a beanie, and a bucket hat.

Step 3: Hair and Makeup

Hair and makeup were also an essential part of the 90s look. For hair, go for a messy bun or a high ponytail. You can also add some hair clips and scrunchies. For makeup, go for dark lipstick, brown lipstick, or a nude lipstick. You can also add some glitter to your eyes.

Tips for achieving the 90s look

Here are some tips to help you achieve the 90s look:

  • Choose baggy clothing
  • Accessorize with chokers, snap bracelets, and fanny packs
  • Go for dark lipstick or a nude lipstick
  • Add some glitter to your eyes
  • Style your hair in a messy bun or a high ponytail

FAQ

What were the popular 90s fashion trends?

The popular 90s fashion trends were oversized clothing, denim jackets, leather jackets, plaid shirts, chokers, snap bracelets, and platform shoes.

What was the 90s grunge look?

The 90s grunge look was all about baggy clothing, ripped jeans, flannel shirts, and combat boots.

What was the 90s hip hop look?

The 90s hip hop look was all about oversized clothing, tracksuits, and bucket hats.

Hair Care

Styling your hair in a messy bun or a high ponytail can be damaging to your hair. So, make sure you use a good hair care routine to keep your hair healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

So, that's it for our guide on understanding what is 90s style called. We hope you found this article helpful, and you can now achieve the 90s look with ease.
