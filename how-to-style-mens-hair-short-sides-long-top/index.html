How To Style Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the short sides long top hairstyle might be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle has been popular for several years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. This haircut features closely cropped sides and a more extended top section that can be styled in various ways. How to Style Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

The short sides long top haircut is incredibly versatile and can be styled in various ways, depending on your preferences. Here are some of the most popular ways to style the hair:

1. Textured Quiff

The textured quiff is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, apply a styling product to your hair and comb the top section back. Then, use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also add a side part to the haircut for a more polished look.

2. Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is perfect for those who want a relaxed and effortless look. To style this haircut, apply a texturizing product to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy fringe. You can also add some texture to the sides using a hair clipper.

3. Slicked-Back Hair

The slicked-back hairstyle is perfect for those who want a more polished and formal look. To achieve this look, apply a styling product to your hair and comb it back. You can also use a hairdryer to add volume and texture to the top section.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style men's hair short sides long top:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in styling your hair is to choose the right haircut. Make sure to communicate with your barber or stylist to get the exact look you want.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash and dry it thoroughly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Make sure to use an appropriate amount depending on your hair type and length.

Step 4: Style the Top Section

Use your fingers or a comb to style the top section of your hair. You can create texture, volume, or a slicked-back look, depending on your preferences.

Step 5: Style the Sides

Use a hair clipper to style the sides of your hair. You can go for a fade, undercut, or even a buzz cut, depending on your preferences.

Step 6: Add Finishing Touches

Finally, add some finishing touches to your hair, such as hairspray or a matte finish product, to keep it in place and looking great all day long.

Tips for Styling Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling men's hair short sides long top:

  • Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Use high-quality styling products that are suitable for your hair type
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh

FAQs

1. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 3-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

2. Can I style my short sides long top haircut with curly hair?

Absolutely! The short sides long top haircut works well with curly hair, and you can style it in various ways, such as a curly fringe or textured quiff.

3. What styling product should I use for my short sides long top haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the hairstyle you want to achieve. For a textured look, use a wax or clay. For a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel.

Hair Care for Men's Short Sides Long Top Haircut

To keep your short sides long top haircut looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers or straighteners, too often
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

Styling men's hair short sides long top can be a fun and creative process. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to choose the right haircut, use high-quality styling products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking great all year round.
