How To Fix Hair Thicker Tips Steps And Care

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're struggling with thinning hair, there are steps you can take to help your hair look thicker and fuller. In this post, we'll go over tips, steps, and hair care practices to help you achieve the hair you want.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Fix Hair Thicker
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thicker Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thicker Hair

How to Fix Hair Thicker

There are a few things you can do to help your hair look thicker:

  • Use volumizing products
  • Get regular haircuts
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Take care of your scalp
  • Consider hair extensions or a hairpiece

By following these steps, you can help your hair look thicker and fuller.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you fix your hair and make it look thicker:

Step 1: Start with a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add thickness to your hair. Look for a shampoo that's specifically designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Be sure to massage the shampoo into your scalp to help stimulate blood flow.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Conditioner

After shampooing, use a volumizing conditioner to help add thickness to your hair. Be sure to apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding your scalp.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help add volume and thickness. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Use a Volumizing Mousse or Spray

After blow drying, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add even more thickness and volume to your hair. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

Tips for Thicker Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve thicker hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Consider using a hair growth supplement
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to brush your hair

FAQs

Q: Can hair dye make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair dye can help add thickness and volume to your hair. However, be sure to use high-quality dye and avoid over-processing your hair.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair extensions can help add thickness and volume to your hair. Just be sure to use high-quality extensions and have them installed by a professional.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

To keep your hair looking thick and healthy, be sure to take care of it properly:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

By following these hair care practices, you can help keep your hair looking thick and healthy.

Conclusion

Thinning hair can be frustrating, but there are steps you can take to help your hair look thicker and fuller. By using volumizing products, getting regular haircuts, eating a healthy diet, and taking care of your scalp, you can help your hair look its best. If you're still struggling with thinning hair, consider talking to a dermatologist or hairstylist for more personalized advice.
