How To Manage Thick Curly Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick curly frizzy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have voluminous and bouncy curls that can make heads turn. On the other hand, you have to deal with frizz, tangles, and a lot of maintenance. If you have thick curly frizzy hair, you know the struggle. But fear not! With the right techniques and products, you can manage your hair and make it look fabulous. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to manage thick curly frizzy hair.

How to Manage Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

The first step to managing your thick curly frizzy hair is to understand your hair type. Thick curly hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz because the natural oils from the scalp do not travel down the hair shaft easily. This means that you need to moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

The second step is to find the right hair products for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for curly hair. They should be sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals like alcohol and sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more prone to frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage thick curly frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your damp hair to seal in moisture.
  3. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots.
  4. Apply a styling product like mousse, gel, or cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  5. Scrunch your hair with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  6. Air dry your hair or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a hair oil to smooth down any frizz and add shine.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for managing thick curly frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it even more prone to frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick curly frizzy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair with a flat iron. However, you should use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the flat iron too often, as this can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you exercise or sweat. If you have thick curly frizzy hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more prone to frizz.

Hair Care Products

Here are some hair care products that are great for managing thick curly frizzy hair:

  • Moroccanoil Intense Hydrating Mask
  • OGX Coconut Curls Milk
  • Marc Anthony Strictly Curls Enhancing Mousse
  • OGX Renewing + Argan Oil of Morocco Penetrating Oil

Managing thick curly frizzy hair requires patience and a lot of trial and error. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful and healthy curls that will make you feel confident and gorgeous. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid harsh chemicals, and moisturize regularly. Your hair will thank you!
