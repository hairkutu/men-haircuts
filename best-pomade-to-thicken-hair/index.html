The Best Pomade To Thicken Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair? Do you want to add volume and thickness to your locks? Look no further than the best pomade to thicken hair. In this guide, we will explore the top pomades on the market designed to give you fuller, more voluminous hair. From how to use it to tips on hair care, we've got you covered.

What is Pomade?

Pomade is a hair styling product that has been around since the 19th century. It is a greasy, waxy substance that is used to style hair and give it shine. Pomade is typically thicker and heavier than other hair styling products like gel or mousse.

How Does Pomade Thicken Hair?

The best pomade to thicken hair contains ingredients like beeswax and lanolin that add volume and texture to your hair. These ingredients coat each hair shaft, making it thicker and more voluminous. Pomade also helps to keep your hair in place, preventing it from falling flat.

How to Use Pomade

Using pomade to thicken hair is simple. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips.
  3. Rub the pomade between your palms to warm it up.
  4. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Remember, a little goes a long way when it comes to pomade. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Tips for Using Pomade

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your pomade:

  • Choose a pomade that is specifically designed to thicken hair.
  • Don't use too much pomade. A little goes a long way.
  • Apply the pomade to your hair when it is damp for best results.
  • Comb your hair after applying the pomade to distribute it evenly.
  • Avoid applying pomade to your scalp, as it can clog your pores and cause breakouts.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using pomade to thicken hair:

Can Pomade Cause Hair Loss?

No, pomade does not cause hair loss. However, using too much pomade or not washing it out properly can lead to buildup on your scalp, which can contribute to hair loss.

Is Pomade Safe for Colored Hair?

Yes, pomade is safe for colored hair. However, be sure to choose a pomade that is specifically formulated for your hair type and color.

Can I Use Pomade Every Day?

Yes, you can use pomade every day. However, be sure to wash it out thoroughly at the end of the day to avoid buildup on your scalp.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using the best pomade to thicken hair, there are other things you can do to keep your locks healthy and voluminous:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

The best pomade to thicken hair is a game-changer for anyone looking to add volume and texture to their locks. By choosing the right pomade and using it correctly, you can achieve a fuller, more voluminous mane in no time. Remember to follow our tips for using pomade and taking care of your hair to get the best results.
