How To Cut A Pixie Haircut With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Pixie haircuts have been a popular choice for women for decades. They are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in many different ways. If you’re looking to cut your hair into a pixie, you may be wondering how to do it with clippers. In this post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut a pixie haircut with clippers.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut to a length of one to two inches. It is characterized by its short layers and tapered sides, which create a soft, feminine look. Pixie haircuts are perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and looks great on all face shapes.

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin cutting your hair, you will need to gather the necessary supplies. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, hair cutting scissors, and a mirror.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots. If you have long hair, you may want to use hair ties to section off your hair.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for your pixie haircut. Use your clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. You may want to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use your hair cutting scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use a comb to lift sections of hair and cut them to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use your clippers to blend the sides and back of your hair with the top. This will create a seamless look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any necessary adjustments to the length or shape of your hair.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired look.
  • Use hair ties to section off your hair if you have long hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your hair with clippers can be tricky, especially if you’re new to it.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair into a pixie haircut with clippers by myself?

Yes, you can cut your hair into a pixie haircut with clippers by yourself. However, it may be easier to have someone else help you, especially if you’re new to cutting your hair with clippers.

What length should I use for my clippers?

The length you use for your clippers will depend on the desired length of your pixie haircut. Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

To maintain your pixie haircut, you will need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You may also want to use a hair styling product, such as a pomade or hair wax, to style your hair and add texture.

Cutting a pixie haircut with clippers can be a great way to achieve a low-maintenance, stylish look. With the right tools and a little patience, you can create a flattering and feminine hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion.
