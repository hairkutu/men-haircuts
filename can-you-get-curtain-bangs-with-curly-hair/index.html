Can You Get Curtain Bangs With Curly Hair

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle trend that originated in the 60s and 70s. This style is characterized by long, face-framing bangs that are parted down the middle, similar to curtains. Many people with straight hair have embraced this trend, but what about those with curly hair? Can you get curtain bangs with curly hair? The answer is yes! With the right technique and maintenance, you can rock the curtain bangs with curly hair look.

How to Get Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

The first step in getting curtain bangs with curly hair is to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, so it's important to find someone who understands the unique needs of your hair.

When getting your curtain bangs cut, it's important to communicate with your stylist about the length and shape you want. Be sure to let them know that you have curly hair and that you want your bangs to work with your curls. They may suggest cutting your bangs a bit longer than you normally would to account for the way your curls will spring up.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting curtain bangs with curly hair:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair.
  2. Communicate with your stylist about the length and shape you want.
  3. Your stylist will likely cut your bangs a bit longer than you normally would to account for your curls.
  4. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs while they are dry to ensure that they work well with your curl pattern.
  5. Part your bangs down the middle and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

Once you've got your curtain bangs with curly hair, here are some tips for maintaining the look:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Apply a curling cream or mousse to your bangs to help enhance your curl pattern.
  • Regularly trim your bangs to keep them looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as pinning your bangs back or adding a headband.

FAQ

Q: Will curtain bangs work with all types of curly hair?

A: Yes! Curtain bangs can work with all types of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils. The key is to find a stylist who understands your hair and can cut your bangs to work with your unique curl pattern.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with curtain bangs?

A: Yes! Curtain bangs look great when worn down, but they can also be incorporated into updos and half-up hairstyles.

Curly Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right care and styling, anyone can rock the curtain bangs with curly hair look. So go ahead and book that appointment with your stylist â your new look is just a few snips away!
