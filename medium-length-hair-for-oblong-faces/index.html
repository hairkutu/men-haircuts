Medium Length Hair For Oblong Faces The Perfect Hairstyle

Description

Medium length hair is the perfect hairstyle for oblong faces. Oblong faces are longer than they are wide, with a narrow jawline and forehead. Medium length hair adds volume to the sides of the face, making it appear wider and more balanced. This hairstyle is versatile and works for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Oblong Faces

The first step in styling medium length hair for oblong faces is choosing the right cut. A layered haircut that falls just below the shoulders is ideal. Layers add volume and movement to the hair, creating a more balanced look. Bangs can also help to shorten the face and make it appear wider.

When it comes to styling, there are many options for medium length hair. Loose waves or curls add volume and movement to the hair, creating a more balanced look. A side part can also help to break up the length of the face. Half-up hairstyles are another great option, as they add volume to the top of the head and balance out the face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling medium length hair for oblong faces:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to create a more tousled look.
  6. Create a side part and tuck one side behind your ear.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length hair for oblong faces:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling and tugging on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hair for oblong faces:

What face shape is best for medium length hair?

Medium length hair works well for a variety of face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for oblong faces. The length and volume of the hair help to balance out the longer length of the face.

What kind of bangs are best for oblong faces?

Side-swept bangs are ideal for oblong faces, as they help to shorten the face and create a more balanced look. Blunt bangs can also work, but they should be cut above the eyebrows to avoid making the face appear even longer.

Can I still wear my hair up with medium length hair?

Absolutely! There are many updo options for medium length hair, including braids, buns, and half-up hairstyles. Just be sure to add volume to the top of your head to balance out the length of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your medium length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

With the right cut and styling, medium length hair can be the perfect hairstyle for oblong faces. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best and create a more balanced look.
