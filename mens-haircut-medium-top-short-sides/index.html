Mens Haircut Medium Top Short Sides A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that is not too high maintenance, then the mens haircut medium top short sides is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is versatile and easy to style, making it ideal for men who are always on the go. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to help you maintain your new haircut.

Description

The mens haircut medium top short sides is a style that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. The sides and back are typically cut short, but the length can also be adjusted based on the desired look.

How To

To achieve the mens haircut medium top short sides, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it is clean and ready to be cut.
  2. Sit down in a comfortable chair and have a mirror in front of you so that you can see what you're doing.
  3. Decide on the length that you want for the top of your hair and use scissors or clippers to cut it to that length.
  4. Next, use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length, making sure to blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on the top of your head.
  5. Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a comb to style it into the desired shape.
  6. If you want to add some texture to your hair, you can use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create a tousled or messy look.

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide to achieving the mens haircut medium top short sides:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it is clean and ready to be cut. Use warm water to wet your hair, then apply a small amount of shampoo and massage it into your scalp. Rinse your hair thoroughly, then apply conditioner to the ends of your hair. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip the top section out of the way so that you can focus on cutting the sides and back.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a tapered look. Make sure to blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on the top of your head.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Unclip the top section of your hair and use scissors or clippers to cut it to the desired length. You can leave the hair longer on top or opt for a shorter, more textured look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. If you want to add some texture to your hair, you can use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create a tousled or messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mens haircut medium top short sides:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a mirror to ensure that you are cutting your hair evenly on both sides.
  • Keep your hair well-maintained by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to help you style your hair and keep it looking healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut medium top short sides:

What face shape does this haircut suit?

This haircut suits most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering on men with oval or square-shaped faces.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and well-maintained.

What hair products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a range of hair products to style your hair, including hair wax, pomade, and styling cream. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut medium top short sides looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mens haircut medium top short sides and keep it looking great for weeks to come. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual, tousled style, this haircut is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
