How To Make Your Hair Flip Out At The Ends A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to add a little bit of flair to your hairstyle? One way to do so is by making your hair flip out at the ends. This simple but stylish look can be achieved with a few hair styling tools and products. In this article, we will walk you through the steps on how to make your hair flip out at the ends.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Flip Out at the Ends
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Flip Out at the Ends
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Flipping Out Your Hair

How to Make Your Hair Flip Out at the Ends

The key to achieving this look is to start with a haircut that has layers and ends that are slightly longer than the rest of your hair. This will give you the foundation for flipping out your hair at the ends. Here are the tools and products you will need:

  • Blow dryer
  • Round brush
  • Hair straightener or curling iron
  • Hair spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Blow-dry your hair

Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. Lift your hair up at the roots and blow-dry them to create volume. Make sure to dry your hair completely before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts, left and right. Use hair clips to secure the sections and prevent them from getting in the way.

Step 3: Straighten your hair

Take a small section of hair from the left side and straighten it with a hair straightener or a curling iron. Twist the straightener or curling iron away from your face to create a flip at the ends. Repeat this step on the right side.

Step 4: Spray your hair

Finish off the look by spraying your hair with some hair spray. This will help hold the style and prevent frizz.

Tips for Making Your Hair Flip Out at the Ends

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect flipped-out hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair straightener or curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Choose a round brush with natural bristles to prevent static and frizz.
  • Experiment with different sizes of hair straighteners or curling irons to find the one that works best for your hair length and type.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase the temperature to avoid burning your hair.

FAQs

Can I flip out my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve a similar look by using rollers or braids to create waves or curls at the ends of your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. On the other hand, if you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Flipping Out Your Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy-looking hairstyle.

By following these steps and tips, you can easily make your hair flip out at the ends and add a touch of style and personality to your look. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
