What Was The Style In The 60 S

Welcome to our blog post about the style in the 60's! In this article, we'll take a look at the fashion trends and styles that were popular during this era. From the mod look to hippie fashion, we'll cover everything you need to know about what was in vogue in the 60's.

Table of Contents

  • The Mod Look
  • Hippie Fashion
  • Hair Styles
  • FAQ

The Mod Look

The mod look was one of the most popular styles in the 60's. It was characterized by bold, geometric patterns, bright colors, and short hemlines. Women wore mini skirts and dresses, while men wore tailored suits with narrow lapels and skinny ties. The mod look was influenced by the fashion of London's Carnaby Street and the music of The Beatles.

If you want to recreate the mod look, opt for bold prints and bright colors. Look for mini dresses and skirts, and pair them with knee-high boots or flats. For men, wear slim-fit suits in bold colors like red or blue. Add a skinny tie and some Chelsea boots to complete the look.

Hippie Fashion

Hippie fashion was all about embracing a natural, laid-back style. It was influenced by the counterculture movement and the desire to break free from traditional norms. Women wore long, flowing dresses and skirts, peasant blouses, and bell-bottom jeans. Men also wore bell-bottoms, along with tie-dye shirts and vests.

To achieve the hippie look, focus on natural fabrics like cotton and linen. Opt for loose-fitting garments in earthy tones like brown, green, and orange. Add some fringe or embroidery to your outfit for a bohemian touch. Accessorize with beads, headbands, and sandals to complete the look.

Hair Styles

Hair was also an important part of fashion in the 60's. Women often wore their hair in bouffants, beehives, and other elaborate updos. Men had shorter hair with sideburns and often styled it with products like Brylcreem.

To achieve a 60's hairstyle, invest in some hair products like mousse, hairspray, and gel. Women can try teasing their hair to create volume, or create a sleek updo with a headband. Men can use pomade or wax to style their hair into a side part or slicked-back look.

FAQ

What were popular colors in the 60's?

Bright colors like pink, orange, and blue were popular in the 60's, along with earthy tones like brown and green.

What were popular fabrics in the 60's?

Natural fabrics like cotton and linen were popular in the 60's, along with synthetic fabrics like polyester.

What were some popular accessories in the 60's?

Beads, headbands, and sandals were popular accessories in the 60's, along with hats and sunglasses.

What famous people influenced 60's fashion?

Icons like Twiggy, Jackie Kennedy, and The Beatles all influenced 60's fashion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best in a 60's style, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products that can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, and let your hair air-dry whenever possible.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to recreate the fashion of the 60's and channel your inner mod or hippie. Have fun experimenting with different styles and don't be afraid to mix and match to create your own unique look!
