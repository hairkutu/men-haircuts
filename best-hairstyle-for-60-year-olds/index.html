Best Hairstyles For 60 Year Olds A Guide To Flattering And Easy To Manage Hair

Written by Christine Jul 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair also changes in texture, volume, and color. Many women over 60 may find that their hair is thinner, coarser, and grayer than it used to be. However, this doesn't mean that you can't have a stylish and youthful-looking hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. In this guide, we will share some of the best hairstyles for 60 year olds that are low-maintenance, flattering, and versatile.

Description: Understanding Your Hair Type and Face Shape

Before you decide on a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and face shape. For example, if you have thin or fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds volume and movement. If you have thick or curly hair, you may prefer a longer cut that allows you to showcase your natural texture.

Similarly, your face shape can also influence the type of hairstyle that looks best on you. For instance, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a layered bob or a shoulder-length cut with side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length. If you have a square or angular face, you may prefer a soft and wispy pixie cut that softens your features.

How to: Choosing the Right Hairstyle for You

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to work with a trusted hairstylist who can give you personalized advice based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Here are some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for you:

  • Bring photos of hairstyles that you like and that flatter your face shape.
  • Be open to trying new styles, but also be honest about your preferences and comfort level.
  • Consider your daily routine and how much time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on how to maintain and care for your new hairstyle.

Step-by-Step: Styling Tips for Easy-to-Manage Hair

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, you'll want to know how to style it at home. Here are some step-by-step tips for easy-to-manage hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that suits your hair type, such as mousse, gel, or serum.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curl pattern.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture, waves, or curls as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your style in place.

Tips: Maintaining Healthy and Vibrant Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling products, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy, shiny, and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are gentle on your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.

FAQ: Common Questions about Hairstyles for 60 Year Olds

Here are some common questions and answers about hairstyles for 60 year olds:

What is the best haircut for thinning hair?
A layered bob or a short pixie cut with side-swept bangs can add volume and texture to thinning hair.
Can older women wear long hair?
Yes, older women can wear long hair as long as it suits their hair type, face shape, and personal style.
Should I choose a hair color that matches my skin tone?
It's recommended to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone, rather than matches it exactly. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right shade.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts on Best Hairstyles for 60 Year Olds

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and empowering experience, especially when you find a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful. By understanding your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and is easy to manage. Remember to take good care of your hair by using gentle products, protecting it from damage, and eating a healthy diet. With these tips and tricks, you can rock the best hairstyle for 60 year olds and enjoy your golden years with style and grace.
