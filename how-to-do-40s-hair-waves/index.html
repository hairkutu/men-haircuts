How To Do 40S Hair Waves Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a fan of vintage hairstyles? Do you want to learn how to do 40s hair waves? Look no further! In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to achieve the perfect 40s hair waves in no time. From prepping your hair to styling it with the right tools and products, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description

The 40s hair waves were popular during the World War II era and have made a comeback in recent years. This vintage hairstyle is all about soft, bouncy waves that frame your face and add volume to your hair. It’s a classic look that’s perfect for any occasion, from weddings to proms to everyday wear. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve this timeless hairstyle in no time.

How to

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Section your hair into four parts, using clips to secure them. This will make it easier to style your hair.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Take a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel and wrap a section of hair around it, starting from the roots to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 3: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you’ve curled all sections of your hair, use a paddle brush to brush out your curls. This will create soft, bouncy waves.

Step 4: Pin Your Waves

Use bobby pins to secure your waves in place. Pin the waves horizontally along your head, starting from the front and working your way back. This will create a wave pattern that frames your face.

Step 5: Set Your Waves

Once you’ve pinned all your waves, spritz some hairspray over your hair to set the waves in place. Leave the pins in for at least 20 minutes to ensure that the waves set properly.

Tips

- Use a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel to create the perfect-sized waves.

- Brush out your curls gently to avoid creating frizz.

- Use bobby pins that match your hair color to avoid them being visible.

- Experiment with different wave patterns to find the one that suits your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve 40s hair waves on short hair?

A: Yes, you can! Use a smaller curling iron and create tighter curls to achieve the same effect.

Q: Do I need to use hairspray?

A: Yes, hairspray will help set your waves in place and make them last longer.

Q: How long will my 40s hair waves last?

A: Your waves should last for at least a day, but you can make them last longer by using a silk pillowcase and avoiding touching your hair too much.

Hair Care

To maintain your 40s hair waves, avoid washing your hair too often. Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes. Also, use a heat protectant spray whenever you use heat styling tools on your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect 40s hair waves that will make you feel like a vintage goddess. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
