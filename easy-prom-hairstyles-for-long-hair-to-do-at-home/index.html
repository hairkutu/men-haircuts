Easy Prom Hairstyles For Long Hair To Do At Home

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a high school or college student, prom is probably one of the most anticipated events of the year. However, getting ready for prom can be quite stressful, especially when it comes to deciding on a hairstyle. Fortunately, you don't have to spend a fortune at a salon to look stunning on prom night. In this article, we'll be sharing some easy prom hairstyles for long hair that you can do at home.

Description

Long hair is versatile, and you can create various hairstyles that suit your style and personality. Prom hairstyles for long hair can range from elegant updos to loose waves, depending on your preference. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and enhances your facial features.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's washed and dried. Apply some heat protectant spray to avoid damage from hot styling tools. You'll also need some bobby pins, hair elastics, and hairspray to hold the style in place.

Loose Waves

Loose waves are perfect for a relaxed and effortless look. To achieve this hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle or on one side, depending on your preference.
  2. Use a curling iron to curl your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  3. Once you've curled all your hair, run your fingers through it to create loose waves.
  4. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Low Bun

A low bun is elegant and sophisticated, perfect for a formal event like prom. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Use your fingers to loosen the bun for a relaxed look.
  5. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a half-up half-down hairstyle:

  1. Divide your hair into two sections: top and bottom.
  2. Gather the top section of your hair and twist it into a loose bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Leave the bottom section of your hair loose.
  5. Curl the bottom section of your hair for a wavy effect.
  6. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect prom hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles before prom night to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your hair with a headband, tiara, or flowers.
  • If you're using hot styling tools, make sure you don't leave them on your hair for too long to avoid damage.
  • Make sure you have all the necessary tools and products before you start styling your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about prom hairstyles for long hair:

What if my hair is too short for these hairstyles?

If your hair is too short, you can consider using hair extensions to add length and volume.

How long should I spend on my hair on prom night?

You should give yourself enough time to wash, dry, and style your hair. Ideally, you should start getting ready a few hours before the event.

What if I mess up my hairstyle?

If you mess up your hairstyle, don't panic. You can always start over, or opt for a simpler hairstyle.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using hot styling tools frequently. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage from hot styling tools.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.

With these easy prom hairstyles for long hair to do at home, you'll be able to achieve a stunning look without breaking the bank. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
