Are Short Layers Good For Curly Hair

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you considering getting a new haircut, but not sure if short layers are good for curly hair? Short layers can add volume and movement to curly hair, but it's important to consider your hair type, face shape, and styling routine when deciding on a haircut. In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits and drawbacks of short layers for curly hair, and provide tips on how to care for your hair after getting a new haircut.

  • The Drawbacks of Short Layers for Curly Hair

  • FAQs About Short Layers for Curly Hair

The Benefits of Short Layers for Curly Hair

Short layers can add volume and movement to curly hair, which can make it look more lively and bouncy. The layers can also enhance the natural curl pattern, especially if you have looser curls that tend to fall flat. Additionally, short layers can make it easier to style your hair, as they can create more texture and hold.

Short layers are also a great option if you have thin or fine curly hair, as they can make it look thicker and fuller. By removing some of the weight from the ends of your hair, short layers can create the illusion of more volume and body.

The Drawbacks of Short Layers for Curly Hair

While short layers can be beneficial for curly hair, they may not be the best option for everyone. If you have very tight or coily curls, short layers may cause your hair to become too voluminous or frizzy. Additionally, if you have a long face shape, short layers may make your face appear even longer.

Short layers can also require more maintenance than longer haircuts, as they can require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. If you're not willing to commit to regular salon visits, a short layered haircut may not be the best choice for you.

Choosing the Right Short Layered Haircut for Your Face Shape

When choosing a short layered haircut for curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape. A layered bob or lob can be a great option if you have a round or heart-shaped face, as it can create balance and frame your face. If you have a square or angular face shape, a soft, wispy layered haircut can soften your features and add dimension.

If you're not sure what haircut would be best for your face shape, consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They can recommend a haircut that will flatter your features and work with your natural texture.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Curly Hair

After getting a short layered haircut for curly hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short layered curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Short Layers for Curly Hair

Q: Will short layers make my curly hair look too puffy?

A: It depends on your hair type and the type of short layered haircut you get. If you have very tight or coily curls, short layers may make your hair look too voluminous or frizzy. However, if you have looser curls, short layers can enhance your natural curl pattern and add movement.

Q: How often do I need to trim my short layered curly hair?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or updo with short layers?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or updo with short layers. However, you may need to use bobby pins or hair clips to secure any shorter layers that may stick out.

Q: Can I color my short layered curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short layered curly hair. However, it's important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your color and prevent fading. It's also recommended to get a deep conditioning treatment after coloring your hair to keep it healthy and moisturized.

In conclusion, short layers can be a great option for curly hair, as they can add volume, movement, and texture. However, it's important to consider your hair type, face shape, and styling routine when deciding on a haircut. By following these tips for caring for short layered curly hair, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
