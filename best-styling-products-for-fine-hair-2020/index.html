Best Styling Products For Fine Hair 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver May 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your fine hair falling flat after just a few hours of styling? Do you struggle to find products that add volume and hold without weighing your hair down? Look no further! In this article, we'll be exploring the best styling products for fine hair in 2020. From mousses to hairsprays, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

When it comes to fine hair, finding the right styling products can make all the difference. Products that are too heavy or greasy can weigh your hair down, leaving it looking limp and lifeless. On the other hand, products that are too light may not provide enough hold or volume. That's why it's important to choose products that are specifically formulated for fine hair.

How-To: Choosing the Right Styling Products for Fine Hair

Before we dive into our top product picks, let's talk about how to choose the right styling products for your fine hair.

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you start shopping for styling products, it's important to determine your hair type. Is your hair straight, wavy, or curly? Is it oily or dry? Does it tend to frizz or fall flat? These factors will help you choose the right products for your hair.

Step 2: Look for Lightweight Formulas

When it comes to styling products for fine hair, lightweight formulas are key. Look for products that are labeled "lightweight" or "volumizing." These products will add volume and hold without weighing your hair down.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hold

The type of hold you need will depend on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. If you have straight or wavy hair, you may want a product with a light hold. If you have curly hair, you may want a product with a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.

Top Styling Products for Fine Hair 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right styling products for your fine hair, let's take a look at our top picks for 2020.

1. Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse

Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse is a lightweight mousse that provides volume and hold without weighing down fine hair. It contains patented Thickening Molecule (PBAE) technology that creates a micro-pattern of thickening dots on each strand of hair, making it look and feel fuller.

2. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray

Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray is a lightweight spray that adds volume and texture to fine hair. It contains polymers that coat each strand of hair, making it look thicker and fuller. This spray is perfect for adding body to fine, limp hair.

3. Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray

Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray is a lightweight spray that adds volume and texture to fine hair. It contains a blend of polymers and zeolite cristals that absorb oil and add texture to hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

4. Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray

Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray is a lightweight hairspray that provides strong hold without weighing down fine hair. It contains argan oil, which nourishes and protects hair while providing hold and shine. This hairspray is perfect for keeping your style in place all day.

Tips for Styling Fine Hair

Now that you have the right products, here are some tips for styling fine hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Start by using a volumizing shampoo to add body to your hair. Look for a shampoo that is specifically formulated for fine hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can add volume and lift to your roots. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow dry.

3. Use a Root Lifter

A root lifter can add volume and lift to your roots. Apply it to your roots before blow drying for extra volume.

4. Don't Overdo It

When it comes to styling fine hair, less is more. Use just enough product to achieve the desired hold and volume, but don't overdo it.

FAQ: Styling Fine Hair

Q: Can I use hair oil on fine hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair oil on fine hair, but be careful not to use too much. A small amount of oil can help nourish and protect your hair, but too much can weigh it down.

Q: How often should I wash my fine hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with fine hair may need to wash it every day, while others can go a few days between washes.

Q: Can I use hairspray on fine hair?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray on fine hair, but choose a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your fine hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips for caring for fine hair:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products and hard water.

2. Don't Overuse Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage fine hair, so use them sparingly. When you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make fine hair look thinner.

With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can achieve voluminous, healthy-looking fine hair. Give these products and tips a try and see the difference for yourself!
