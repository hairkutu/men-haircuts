Which Hair Parting Suits Oval Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we all know, hair plays an important role in enhancing our appearance. It is important to style your hair in a way that complements your face shape. If you have an oval face, you are in luck as this face shape is considered to be the most versatile. You can experiment with different hairstyles and hair partings. However, choosing the right hair parting can make a huge difference in how you look. In this article, we will discuss which hair parting suits oval face and provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to style your hair to enhance your features.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into which hair parting suits oval face, let's first understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. This face shape is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical, with no dominant feature. This makes it easy to style your hair in different ways.

How to

The first step to choosing the right hair parting for your oval face shape is to determine your hair texture and length. Depending on your hair type, some hair partings might not suit you. For example, if you have fine hair, a center parting might make your hair look flat. Similarly, if you have curly hair, a side parting might not work for you.

Step by step

Here are the steps to determine which hair parting suits oval face:

  1. Start by identifying the widest part of your face. This is usually at the temples.
  2. Determine your hair texture and length.
  3. If you have fine hair, consider a side parting to add volume to your hair.
  4. If you have thick hair, a center parting can help balance out your features.
  5. If you have curly hair, a deep side parting can help showcase your curls.
  6. If you have a long face, a center parting can help shorten the appearance of your face.
  7. If you have a wide forehead, a side parting can help balance out your features.
  8. If you have a narrow forehead, a center parting can help create the illusion of width.
  9. Experiment with different hair partings to find the one that suits you best.
  10. Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair grows back, so have fun with it!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair parting:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance hair parting might be best for you.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice. They can help you determine which hair parting suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Accessorize your hair. Adding a headband, hair clip, or scarf can help enhance your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles. Don't be afraid to try a new hairstyle to complement your hair parting.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which hair parting suits oval face:

  • Can an oval face shape pull off any hair parting? Yes, an oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile, which means you can experiment with different hair partings.
  • What if I have a combination of face shapes? If you have a combination of face shapes, consider choosing a hair parting that complements your dominant feature.
  • Can I change my hair parting every day? Yes, changing your hair parting can help keep your hairstyle fresh and interesting.

Hair Care

Whichever hair parting you choose, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips on how to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair parting can make a huge difference in how you look. If you have an oval face, you are in luck as you can experiment with different hairstyles and hair partings. Use the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to determine which hair parting suits oval face and enhance your features. Remember, your hair is your crowning glory, so have fun with it!
