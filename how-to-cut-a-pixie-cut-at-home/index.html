How To Cut A Pixie Cut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut might be just what you need. However, going to a salon to get a pixie cut can be expensive, and not everyone has the time or money to do so. Fortunately, you can cut a pixie cut at home with the right tools and technique. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting a pixie cut step by step.

How to Cut a Pixie Cut at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

To cut a pixie cut at home, you'll need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties

Make sure your scissors are sharp and your clippers are fully charged.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the back, the sides, and the top. Use hair ties to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Use your clippers to create a guide line. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head. Make sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check for evenness. Use your scissors to trim any uneven bits.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Cut at Home

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Take your time and don't rush.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider asking a friend or family member for help.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.

FAQ

How long does a pixie cut last?

A pixie cut can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

How often do you need to trim a pixie cut?

It's recommended to trim a pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I color my hair after cutting a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair after cutting a pixie cut. However, it's recommended to wait a few days after cutting your hair to allow your scalp to heal.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut a pixie cut at home and keep it looking great. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment and try new things with your hair.
