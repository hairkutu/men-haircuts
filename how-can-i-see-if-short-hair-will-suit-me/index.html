How Can I See If Short Hair Will Suit Me

Written by Eveline May 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about chopping off your locks, you might be wondering if short hair will suit you. It's a big decision to make, and you don't want to end up with a cut that doesn't flatter your face shape or hair texture. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you determine if short hair is the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Trying On Short Wigs
  • Consulting with a Stylist
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Conclusion

Determining Your Face Shape

The first thing you should consider when thinking about getting a short haircut is your face shape. Different face shapes look better with different haircuts, so it's important to determine what your face shape is before making any decisions.

There are a few different ways to determine your face shape. One method is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Then, step back and take a look at the shape you've drawn. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that will complement your features.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Short haircuts can look drastically different depending on your hair type, so it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

For example, if you have naturally curly hair, you might want to opt for a short, layered cut that will enhance your curls. On the other hand, if you have straight hair, a sleek, angled bob might be more flattering.

Trying On Short Wigs

If you're still not sure if short hair will suit you, you can always try on some short wigs to get a better idea of what you'll look like. Wigs are a great way to experiment with different styles without committing to a haircut.

You can either visit a wig shop in person or order a few wigs online. Make sure to choose wigs that are similar in style and color to the haircut you're considering.

Consulting with a Stylist

When in doubt, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They'll be able to offer personalized advice based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Bring in pictures of short haircuts you like, and be open to their suggestions. They might recommend a slightly longer or shorter cut than you originally had in mind.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

If you do decide to go for a short haircut, there are a few tips you should keep in mind to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for short hair.
  • Visit your stylist for regular trims to keep your cut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair texture.

FAQs

Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. It's all about choosing the right cut for your face shape.

What if I don't like my short haircut?

Hair grows back! If you're not happy with your short haircut, you can always grow it out or try a different style.

Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style short hair, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. Experiment with different styling products and tools to find what works best for you.

Conclusion

Determining whether short hair will suit you can be a tricky decision, but by considering your face shape and hair texture, trying on some wigs, consulting with a stylist, and keeping up with proper hair care, you can confidently make the leap to a shorter cut. Remember, hair grows back, so don't be afraid to experiment and have fun!
