Does Fringes Suit Curly Hair

Written by William Apr 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair has always been a topic of discussion when it comes to hairstyles. While there are many options for styling curly hair, fringes have been a popular trend. However, the question arises, does fringes suit curly hair? In this article, we will explore this topic in detail and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Table of Content:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description:

Fringes are a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some excitement to your look. They come in various lengths and styles, making it easy for you to find one that suits your face shape and hair type. However, when it comes to curly hair, fringes can be a bit tricky. The natural curls can make it difficult to style fringes and maintain their shape. In this article, we will explore whether fringes suit curly hair and provide you with some tips on how to style them.

How to:

If you have curly hair and are considering getting fringes, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Firstly, it is important to choose a fringe that complements your face shape. Secondly, you should consider the length of your curls and choose a fringe that works well with your hair type. Lastly, you should be prepared to put in some effort to style your fringes regularly.

Choose a Fringe that Complements Your Face Shape:

Just like any other hairstyle, fringes should be chosen based on your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for longer fringes that fall below your eyebrows. This will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. If you have a heart-shaped face, choose wispy fringes that are shorter on the sides and longer in the middle. This will soften your features and balance out your face shape. If you have an oval face, almost any fringe style will suit you.

Consider the Length of Your Curls:

The length of your curls can also play a role in whether fringes suit you or not. If you have short curls, choose a fringe that is shorter in length and works well with your curls. If you have long curls, opt for longer fringes that complement your hair length.

Be Prepared to Style Your Fringes Regularly:

Styling fringes can be challenging, especially if you have curly hair. You will need to invest in some good quality styling products such as mousse, hair spray, and a diffuser. You should also be prepared to style your fringes regularly to maintain their shape and keep them looking good.

Step by step:

If you have curly hair and are considering getting fringes, here are some steps you can follow to style them:

  1. Wash your hair and apply some leave-in conditioner to define your curls.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and add volume.
  3. Section off your hair and use a curling iron to define your fringes.
  4. Apply some hair spray to keep your fringes in place.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling fringes on curly hair:

  • Invest in good quality styling products.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Choose a fringe that complements your face shape.
  • Consider the length of your curls when choosing a fringe.
  • Style your fringes regularly to maintain their shape.

FAQ:

Q. Can fringes make curly hair look shorter?

A. Yes, fringes can make curly hair look shorter. If you have short curls, it is best to choose a shorter fringe to avoid making your hair appear too short.

Q. How often should I style my fringes?

A. It is recommended to style your fringes every 2-3 days to maintain their shape and keep them looking good.

Hair Care:

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair.

Overall, fringes can look great on curly hair if styled properly. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that complements your curly hair.
