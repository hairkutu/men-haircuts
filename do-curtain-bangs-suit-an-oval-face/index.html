Do Curtain Bangs Suit An Oval Face

Written by William Jul 14, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face and are considering a new hairstyle, you may be wondering if curtain bangs are a good choice for you. Curtain bangs have become a popular choice among many women in recent years, but not every hairstyle is ideal for every face shape. In this article, we’ll explore whether curtain bangs suit an oval face and provide you with some tips for pulling off this stylish look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of long, wispy fringe that frame the face and are parted down the middle, creating a curtain-like effect. This hairstyle has gained popularity in recent years due to its versatility and ability to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. However, not every hairstyle is ideal for every face shape, so it’s important to consider whether curtain bangs will suit your oval face before taking the plunge.

How to

If you’re considering getting curtain bangs and have an oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider the length: Curtain bangs that are too short or too long can make your face appear wider than it actually is. Opt for bangs that fall just above your eyebrows or mid-cheek.
  • Think about the volume: If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid curtain bangs that are too thick or heavy, as they can make your hair look flat. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for slightly thicker curtain bangs to balance out your hair volume.
  • Consider your forehead: If you have a large forehead, curtain bangs can help to balance out your face and make it appear smaller. However, if you have a small forehead, you may want to avoid curtain bangs that are too heavy, as they can make your forehead appear even smaller.

Step by Step

If you’ve decided to go ahead with curtain bangs, here’s how to style them:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip them away from the rest of your hair.
  3. Brush your bangs forward and trim them to the desired length.
  4. Part your bangs down the middle and style them using a round brush and a blow dryer. Alternatively, you can let them air dry for a more relaxed look.
  5. Finish off with some hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for pulling off curtain bangs with an oval face:

  • Experiment with different parting styles: While a middle part is the classic curtain bangs look, you may want to try a side part or a zig-zag part to see what suits you best.
  • Don’t be afraid to play with the length: If you’re not sure what length to go for, start with longer bangs and gradually trim them shorter until you find the perfect length.
  • Consider your hair texture: If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to opt for longer bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair to avoid creating a triangular shape.

FAQ

Are curtain bangs easy to maintain?

Curtain bangs require regular trims to maintain their shape, so they may not be the best choice if you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle. However, if you’re willing to put in the effort, they can be a stylish and versatile choice.

Can I still wear my hair up with curtain bangs?

Absolutely! Curtain bangs can be incorporated into a variety of hairstyles, including ponytails and buns. You may need to use some bobby pins to keep your bangs in place, but this shouldn’t be too difficult.

Will curtain bangs suit my face if I have a round face?

Curtain bangs can actually be a great choice for round faces, as they help to elongate the face and create a slimming effect. However, it’s important to consider the length and volume of your bangs to ensure they don’t make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a nourishing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your curtain bangs.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

Overall, if you have an oval face, curtain bangs can be a stylish and versatile choice. By considering the length, volume, and parting style of your bangs, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style.
