How To Do Heart Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Heart hair is a cute and trendy hairstyle that has been making rounds on social media platforms like Instagram and Tiktok. This hairstyle involves creating heart-shaped sections on the top of your head, giving the illusion of a heart-shaped hairdo. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of creating heart hair, as well as provide you with some tips, FAQ, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Heart Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Creating Heart Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Heart Hair

How to Do Heart Hair

Before we jump into the step-by-step guide, let's discuss the materials you will need to create heart hair. You will need:

  • A comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • A hair tie
  • Hairspray

Once you have these materials, you can start creating your heart-shaped hairdo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create the perfect heart hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles or knots.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair, from the crown of your head to your ears.
  3. Clip the bottom section of your hair away, so it doesn't get in the way while you're working on the top section.
  4. Take the top section of your hair and brush it upwards.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the center of your head and tie it into a ponytail.
  6. Divide the ponytail into two equal parts and flip them over to create a small gap in the hair.
  7. Take two small sections of hair from the sides of your head and bring them up to the center ponytail.
  8. Secure the two small sections of hair with bobby pins to create the top of the heart.
  9. Let go of the center ponytail and brush out any tangles or knots.
  10. Use hairspray to set the heart shape in place.

And voila! You now have the perfect heart-shaped hair.

Tips for Creating Heart Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating heart hair:

  • Make sure your hair is straight and smooth before starting. This will make it easier to create the heart shape.
  • Use sectioning clips to keep your hair organized and prevent any stray hairs from getting in the way.
  • If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins to secure the sections in place instead of tying them in a ponytail.
  • Practice makes perfect! It may take a few tries to get the heart shape just right, so don't get discouraged.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about heart hair:

  1. How long does heart hair take to create?

    2. Heart hair can take anywhere from 10-30 minutes to create, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

  2. Can I create heart hair on curly hair?

    3. Yes, you can create heart hair on curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to get the heart shape just right.

  3. Can I create heart hair on myself?

    4. Yes, you can create heart hair on yourself. It may be easier to use a mirror to help you see what you're doing.

Hair Care for Heart Hair

After creating heart hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent any damage or breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips, you can keep your heart hair looking healthy and beautiful.

In conclusion, creating heart hair is a fun and trendy way to switch up your hairstyle. With our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to create the perfect heart-shaped hairdo in no time. So, give it a try and show off your new heart hair on social media!
