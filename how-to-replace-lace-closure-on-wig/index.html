How To Replace Lace Closure On Wig A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your old lace closure on your wig? Or do you want to replace it with a new one? Whatever your reason may be, replacing a lace closure on a wig can be an easy process if you know how to do it. In this article, we will guide you through the process of replacing a lace closure on a wig step-by-step. We will also provide you with some tips and answer some frequently asked questions about wig care.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Replace Lace Closure on Wig

Before we get started, make sure that you have all the tools and materials that you need. You will need a wig cap, a new lace closure, scissors, a needle, and thread.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to replace the lace closure on your wig:

  1. Put on the wig cap to protect your natural hair.
  2. Cut off the old lace closure as close to the hairline as possible using scissors. Be careful not to cut the hair on the wig.
  3. Place the new lace closure on the wig and adjust it to fit the hairline. Use hairpins to hold the closure in place.
  4. Thread the needle and knot the end of the thread.
  5. Sew the new lace closure onto the wig. Make sure to sew it tightly so that it does not come off easily.
  6. Remove the hairpins and adjust the lace closure if necessary.
  7. Cut off any excess lace around the closure using scissors.
  8. Style the wig as desired.

That's it! You have successfully replaced the lace closure on your wig.

Tips for Replacing Lace Closure on Wig

Here are some tips to make the process of replacing a lace closure on a wig easier:

  • Choose a lace closure that matches the color and texture of your wig.
  • Make sure that the new lace closure is the same size as the old one.
  • Be careful not to cut the hair on the wig when cutting off the old lace closure.
  • Sew the new lace closure onto the wig tightly to prevent it from coming off easily.
  • Trim the excess lace carefully to avoid cutting the hair on the wig.

FAQs about Wig Care

Here are some frequently asked questions about wig care:

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears or when it starts to look dirty or oily.

Can I use regular shampoo on my wig?

No, you should use a shampoo that is specifically designed for wigs. Regular shampoo can damage the hair on the wig.

How do I store my wig?

You should store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to prevent it from getting tangled or damaged.

Hair Care Tips for Wigs

To keep your wig looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Brush your wig gently with a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat on your wig, such as blow dryers, flat irons, or curling irons.
  • Protect your wig from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Store your wig properly when you are not wearing it.

By following these tips, you can keep your wig looking beautiful and in great condition for a long time.

Now that you know how to replace lace closure on a wig, you can keep your wig looking fresh and new. Remember to take good care of your wig by following the hair care tips we provided.
