Haircut For Round Face And Wide Forehead A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face and wide forehead? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with this face shape struggle to find a hairstyle that flatters their features. In this article, we'll go over the best haircuts for round faces and wide foreheads, how to achieve them, and some tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face with a wide forehead is characterized by a circular face shape and a broad forehead. The goal when choosing a haircut for this face shape is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the crown of the head and keeping the sides of the hair close to the face.

How to

The first step in choosing a haircut for a round face and wide forehead is to consider the length of your hair. Short haircuts are often the most flattering for this face shape, as they create the illusion of length. However, if you prefer longer hair, there are still plenty of options.

The next step is to consider the style of your hair. Layered haircuts are great for adding volume to the crown of the head, which can help elongate your face. Bangs are also a great option for this face shape, as they draw attention away from the width of the forehead.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieving the perfect haircut for a round face and wide forehead:

  1. Start by choosing a length that flatters your face shape. Short haircuts are often the most flattering.
  2. Consider adding layers to your hair to create volume at the crown of your head.
  3. Choose a style that flatters your face shape, such as side-swept bangs or a deep side part.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and features.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as braids or updos, to change up your look.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face and wide forehead?

The best haircut for a round face and wide forehead is one that adds volume to the crown of the head and keeps the sides of the hair close to the face. Short haircuts are often the most flattering for this face shape.

What hairstyles should be avoided for a round face and wide forehead?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the hair, such as wide curls or a blunt bob, should be avoided for a round face and wide forehead. These styles can make the face appear even rounder.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving a flattering haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your round face and wide forehead. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and features, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
