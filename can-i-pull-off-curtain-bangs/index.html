Can I Pull Off Curtain Bangs Everything You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Apr 22, 2023 · 6 min read

Curtain bangs have been trending for a while now and for good reason. They’re chic, stylish, and can transform any hairstyle. However, the big question remains, can I pull off curtain bangs?

Description

Curtain bangs are essentially parted in the center and frame your face on both sides, just like curtains. They’re versatile and work with any hair length and texture. Curtain bangs can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, and create a soft, feminine look. However, not everyone can pull them off.

How to

The first step in getting curtain bangs is to assess if they’ll suit your face shape. Curtain bangs look great on heart-shaped or oval faces, but may not be suitable for round or square faces. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if curtain bangs will suit you.

Once you’ve decided to go for it, the next step is to choose the right length. Curtain bangs can be long or short, depending on how much hair you want to cut. Longer bangs are easier to style and can be swept to the side, while shorter bangs frame your face better.

When getting your curtain bangs cut, make sure your stylist cuts them dry. This ensures that the bangs are cut to the right length and shape.

Step by step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if curtain bangs are right for you.

2. Choose the right length for your curtain bangs.

3. Get your curtain bangs cut dry to ensure the right length and shape.

4. Style your curtain bangs with a round brush and blow dryer for volume and movement.

Tips

If you’re unsure about committing to curtain bangs, try faux curtain bangs first. Pin your hair back and use hair extensions to create the illusion of bangs.

Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your curtain bangs.

When styling your curtain bangs, use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and movement. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls.

FAQ

1. Can curtain bangs be styled differently?

Yes, curtain bangs can be styled in different ways, such as swept to the side or pinned back. You can also add waves or curls to your curtain bangs for a different look.

2. How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the right length and shape.

3. Can curtain bangs be worn with glasses?

Yes, curtain bangs can be worn with glasses. Choose a length that doesn’t interfere with your glasses and style them to the side.

Hair Care

Caring for your curtain bangs is important to maintain their shape and texture. Use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to keep your bangs looking fresh and voluminous. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause your bangs to look greasy.

When blow-drying your hair, use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair and bangs healthy and nourished.

In conclusion, yes, you can pull off curtain bangs as long as you choose the right length and style them to suit your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if curtain bangs are right for you and follow these tips to keep them looking great.

So, what are you waiting for? Give curtain bangs a try and transform your look today!
