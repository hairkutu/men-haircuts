How To Cut Your Own Long Wavy Hair At Home

Written by Bardi Mar 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon? Do you have long wavy hair that needs a trim but don't want to leave your house? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut your own long wavy hair at home. With our step-by-step instructions and tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect cut in no time.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the steps on how to cut your own long wavy hair, let's talk about how to prepare for the cut. Make sure you have the right tools such as hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You can purchase these at your local drugstore or online. It's also important to have a clear space to work in and a mirror to see what you're doing. Lastly, make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting the cut.

How to

The first step to cutting your own long wavy hair is to determine how much hair you want to cut. If you're just looking for a trim, start with cutting 1/4 inch at a time. If you're feeling adventurous and want to cut more, start with 1/2 inch at a time. Remember, you can always cut more but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and brush it out.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the front and twist it tightly.

Step 3: Cut the ends of the twisted hair at an angle. This will help create layers and texture.

Step 4: Repeat steps 2 and 3 on the other side of your head. Make sure the sections of hair are even.

Step 5: Take a small section of hair from the back and twist it tightly.

Step 6: Cut the ends at an angle. This will help create layers and texture in the back.

Step 7: Repeat steps 5 and 6 on the other side of your head. Make sure the sections of hair are even.

Step 8: Finish by cutting any uneven ends or flyaways.

Tips

1. Don't cut your hair when it's wet. It's harder to see how much you're cutting and your hair shrinks when it dries.

2. Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and it's important to not rush the process.

3. Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more but can't put hair back once it's cut.

4. Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. This will help ensure a clean cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's curly. Follow the same steps as cutting wavy hair but make sure to cut less at a time since curly hair shrinks more when it dries.

How often should I cut my own long wavy hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends. However, if you're just looking for a trim, you can do it as often as you'd like.

What should I do if I mess up my cut?

If you mess up your cut, don't panic! You can always go to a professional to fix it or try cutting it shorter. Remember, hair grows back.

Hair Care

After cutting your own long wavy hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible and let your hair air dry. Trimming your hair regularly will also help maintain healthy ends.

That's it! You now know how to cut your own long wavy hair at home. Remember to take your time and start with small cuts. With practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect cut every time.
