Haircut For Oval Face Big Forehead Male

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a male with an oval face and a big forehead? Do you struggle to find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape and forehead size? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for oval face big forehead male and provide you with some tips and tricks to maintain your hairstyle.

Description

Having an oval face with a big forehead can be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right haircut. You want a style that complements your face shape while minimizing the appearance of your forehead. The ideal haircut for oval face big forehead male is one that adds volume to the sides and back of the head while keeping the hair on top shorter.

How to

When you visit your barber or hairdresser, make sure to communicate your concerns about your forehead and the shape of your face. They will be able to recommend the best haircut for you based on your hair type, face shape, and personal style.

The most popular haircuts for oval face big forehead male include the classic crew cut, side-swept bangs, messy fringe, and textured quiff. These styles are easy to maintain and can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect haircut for oval face big forehead male:

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. 2. Blow-dry your hair to add volume to the sides and back of your head. 3. Use a comb to create a side part or a messy fringe, depending on your preference. 4. Trim the hair on top to a length that suits your face shape and forehead size. 5. Use a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair. 6. Finish off with a hairspray or hair gel to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for oval face big forehead male:

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short on top, as they can make your forehead appear larger. - Opt for styles that add volume and texture to the sides and back of your head. - Consider getting bangs or a fringe to cover your forehead. - Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval face big forehead male:

Q: Can I still have a buzz cut if I have an oval face with a big forehead?
A: Yes, you can still have a buzz cut if you have an oval face with a big forehead. However, it is recommended to keep some hair on top to balance out the proportions of your face. Q: How often should I get my hair cut?
A: It is recommended to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy. Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?
A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles and products to suit different occasions and events. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and forehead size.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners. - Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair. - Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know the best haircut for oval face big forehead male and how to maintain it, you can confidently rock your new hairstyle with pride!
