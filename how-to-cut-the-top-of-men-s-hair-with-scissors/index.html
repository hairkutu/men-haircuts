How To Cut The Top Of Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to cut your hair at home, it's important to know how to cut the top of men's hair with scissors. This is a great way to save money and time, but it can be tricky if you don't know what you're doing. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut the top of men's hair with scissors in a relaxed, easy-to-follow language. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hair cutter, these tips and tricks will help you achieve a salon-worthy cut at home.

How to Cut the Top of Men's Hair with Scissors

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the tools you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Hair clips (optional)

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of your hair, which will make it easier to cut. Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly before you start.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair by creating a part down the middle of your head. Clip one side of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length of your hair. If you're unsure, start with a longer length and work your way down. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't add length back once you've cut it.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at the front of your head, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Repeat this process, working your way back toward the crown of your head.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair on the top of your head, it's important to blend the hair so that it looks natural. To do this, take a comb and comb the hair upward. Use your scissors to trim any longer hairs that stick out.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Before you move on to the other side of your head, check your work in the mirror. Make sure the hair is even and the length is what you want. If you need to make any adjustments, do so before moving on.

Step 7: Repeat on the Other Side

Repeat the same steps on the other side of your head.

Step 8: Blend the Sides

To blend the sides of your hair with the top, use your scissors to trim any longer hairs that stick out. Start at the front of your head and work your way back, using your comb to guide you.

Step 9: Clean Up

Once you're finished cutting your hair, use your clippers to trim the hair around your ears and the back of your neck. Clean up any loose hairs with a brush or towel.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a hair product that works for your hair type and style it as desired.

Tips for Cutting the Top of Men's Hair with Scissors

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down
  • Use a comb to guide you when cutting
  • Check your work in the mirror often
  • Blend the hair for a more natural look
  • Use clippers to trim the hair around your ears and the back of your neck

FAQs

Is it hard to cut the top of men's hair with scissors?

It can be tricky if you don't know what you're doing, but with practice and the right tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut at home.

Do I need to use clippers?

No, clippers are optional, but they can be helpful when trimming the hair around your ears and the back of your neck.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim more hair to even it out, or you can go to a professional for help.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape, and consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
