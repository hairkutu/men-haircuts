How Long Is A Blade 3 Haircut

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to get a new haircut, you might be wondering how long a blade 3 haircut is. A blade 3 is a popular length for haircuts, and it can vary depending on the type of hair you have and your personal preferences. In this article, we'll discuss how long a blade 3 haircut is, how to get one, and some tips for taking care of your hair after the cut.

Description

A blade 3 haircut is a style that uses a clipper guard that is 3/8 of an inch long. This is the length of the hair that is left on your head after the cut. The blade 3 is a popular length because it is long enough to provide some volume and texture to your hair, but short enough to be easy to maintain. It's a versatile length that can work for a variety of hair types and styles.

How To Get a Blade 3 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a blade 3 haircut, you can either go to a barber or stylist or do it yourself at home. If you're going to a professional, you can bring a picture of the style you want or describe it to them. They will use clippers with a blade 3 guard to cut your hair to the desired length. If you're doing it yourself, you'll need to purchase clippers with a blade 3 guard and follow the instructions that come with them.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to getting a blade 3 haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Decide on the length you want
  3. Attach the blade 3 guard to your clippers
  4. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, using the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length
  5. Blend the hair on the sides and back with scissors or another guard
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after a blade 3 haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean
  • Use a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair

FAQ

What other guard lengths are available?

There are many different guard lengths available, ranging from 1/16 of an inch to 1 inch or more. The length you choose will depend on your personal preferences and the style you're going for.

Is a blade 3 haircut good for thick hair?

Yes, a blade 3 haircut can be a good option for thick hair. It will help to remove some of the bulk and provide a more manageable length.

Can I get a blade 3 haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, a blade 3 haircut can work for curly hair. However, it's important to work with a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair to ensure that the cut is even and doesn't create frizz.

Hair Care

Overall, a blade 3 haircut is a versatile and easy-to-maintain style that can work for a variety of hair types and styles. By following the tips for hair care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish long after the cut.
