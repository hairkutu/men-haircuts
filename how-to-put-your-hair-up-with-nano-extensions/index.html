How To Put Your Hair Up With Nano Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 08, 2023 · 6 min read
Are you looking for an easy way to put your hair up with nano extensions? Look no further! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to put your hair up with nano extensions. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process as easy as possible. Plus, we’ll answer some frequently asked questions and give you some hair care tips to keep your extensions looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Your Hair Up with Nano Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Put Your Hair Up with Nano Extensions

Nano extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair. They are also great for styling your hair in various ways, including putting your hair up. Here’s what you’ll need to put your hair up with nano extensions:
  • Nano extensions
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair tie
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let’s get started with the step-by-step guide on how to put your hair up with nano extensions:
  1. Start by brushing your hair and your extensions to remove any tangles.

  2. Gather your hair and your extensions into a ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.

  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.

  4. Divide the ponytail into two sections.

  5. Take one section and twist it tightly.

  6. Wrap the twisted section around the hair tie and secure the end with a bobby pin.

  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 with the other section of the ponytail.

  8. Use bobby pins to secure any loose sections or flyaways.

  9. Finish with hairspray (optional).

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make putting your hair up with nano extensions easier:
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to conceal them better.
  • If you have fine hair, backcomb the sections of your hair before twisting them to add more volume.
  • Experiment with different styles and variations to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting your hair up with nano extensions: Q: Can I put my hair up with nano extensions if I have short hair?
A: Yes, you can still put your hair up with nano extensions if you have short hair. Just make sure to gather all of your hair, including the extensions, into a ponytail before starting. Q: How long will my hair stay up with nano extensions?
A: Your hair should stay up all day with nano extensions, as long as you secure it properly with bobby pins. Q: Can I sleep with my hair up with nano extensions?
A: We don’t recommend sleeping with your hair up with nano extensions. It can cause damage to your hair and your extensions.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your extensions looking great:
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the extensions of their natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the extensions as much as possible.
  • Brush your hair and your extensions gently to avoid tangles and breakage.
  • Get your extensions maintained regularly by a professional to keep them in the best condition.
Now that you know how to put your hair up with nano extensions, you’re ready to try it out for yourself! With these simple steps, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will showcase your new extensions. Don’t forget to follow our tips and tricks and take care of your extensions to keep them looking great for as long as possible.
