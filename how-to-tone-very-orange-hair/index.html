How To Tone Very Orange Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to tone very orange hair. If you're reading this, chances are you've recently dyed your hair and it turned out much more orange than you had hoped. Don't worry, it happens to the best of us. In this guide, we'll walk you through step-by-step on how to tone down that orange and achieve the hair color you desire.

Description

Toning orange hair can be a tricky process, but with the right products and techniques, it's definitely achievable. Whether you want to go back to your natural hair color or try something completely new, toning your hair is an essential step to achieving your desired look.

How to Tone Very Orange Hair

Step 1: Assess your hair

The first step in toning very orange hair is to assess the current condition of your hair. If your hair is damaged or over-processed, it's important to take the necessary steps to repair it before attempting to tone it. This will ensure that your hair is healthy enough to withstand the toning process.

Step 2: Choose the right toner

Choosing the right toner is crucial in achieving the desired results. Look for a toner that is specifically designed to neutralize orange tones. Popular toners for orange hair include purple or blue-based toners. These colors will counteract the orange and give your hair a more natural look.

Step 3: Mix the toner

Once you've chosen the right toner, it's time to mix it. Follow the instructions on the box carefully and mix the toner with the developer. The developer will activate the toner and help it penetrate your hair.

Step 4: Apply the toner

Apply the toner to your hair using a tint brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate your hair evenly and avoid missing any spots. Leave the toner on for the recommended amount of time, usually around 20-30 minutes.

Step 5: Rinse and condition

Once the toner has been on for the recommended amount of time, rinse it out thoroughly with cool water. Follow up with a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent further damage.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when toning very orange hair:

  • Use a toner that is specifically designed for orange hair
  • Follow the instructions on the box carefully
  • Wear gloves to avoid staining your hands
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after toning to restore moisture to your hair

FAQ

Can I tone very orange hair at home?

Yes, you can tone very orange hair at home. However, it's important to choose the right toner and follow the instructions carefully to achieve the best results.

How long does toner last on orange hair?

The length of time toner lasts on orange hair varies depending on a number of factors, including your hair type and how often you wash your hair. Generally, toner will last for several weeks to a few months.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair after toning to keep it healthy and prevent further damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat styling
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

And there you have it, a comprehensive guide on how to tone very orange hair. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve the hair color you desire and keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Happy toning!
