How To Decide Hair Up Or Down For Wedding Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing how to wear your hair for your wedding day can be a daunting task. Your hairstyle can completely change your look and you want to make sure you get it right. One of the biggest decisions you’ll have to make is whether to wear your hair up or down. In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to decide which hairstyle is best for your big day.

How to Decide

Deciding whether to wear your hair up or down can be a challenge, but there are a few things you can consider to help make the decision easier.

Look at Your Dress

Your wedding dress can give you some hints about what hairstyle would be best. If your dress has a high neckline or intricate detailing on the back, an updo may be the way to go. If your dress is simple and elegant, wearing your hair down may be a better option.

Consider Your Venue

The location of your wedding can also play a role in your hairstyle decision. If you’re getting married on a beach or outdoor location, wearing your hair down may not be practical. An updo can help keep your hair out of your face and prevent it from getting ruined by the wind.

Think About Your Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and style. If you typically wear your hair down and feel uncomfortable with an updo, it may not be the best choice for your wedding day.

Step by Step

Once you’ve decided whether to wear your hair up or down, there are a few steps you can take to ensure your hairstyle looks perfect on your wedding day.

Book a Trial

Book a hair trial with your stylist to try out different hairstyles and see what works best for you. This will give you a chance to make any necessary adjustments before the big day.

Choose Accessories

Choose any hair accessories you want to wear on your wedding day, such as a veil or hairpiece. This can help determine the final hairstyle and ensure everything looks cohesive.

Practice Good Hair Care

To ensure your hair looks its best on your wedding day, make sure you’re taking good care of it leading up to the event. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, getting regular trims, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you decide whether to wear your hair up or down for your wedding day:

  • Consider the weather and time of year when making your decision.
  • Look at photos of hairstyles you like to get inspiration.
  • Ask your bridal party for their opinions.

FAQ

Should I wear my hair up or down for an indoor wedding?

This depends on your personal preference and the style of your dress. If your dress has intricate detailing on the back, an updo may be the way to go. If your dress is simple and elegant, wearing your hair down can look beautiful.

Can I wear a veil with my hair down?

Yes, you can definitely wear a veil with your hair down. Just make sure your stylist knows you plan to wear a veil so they can create a style that works with it.

Should I wear my hair up or down if I have short hair?

Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, so it really depends on your personal preference and the style of your dress. Talk to your stylist about different options and try out a few styles at your hair trial to see what works best for you.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, make sure you’re taking good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, getting regular trims, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

On your wedding day, use a hairspray that will hold your style in place without making your hair feel crunchy or stiff. Bring a small bottle of hairspray with you for touch-ups throughout the day.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to decide whether to wear your hair up or down for your wedding day with confidence. Remember to book a hair trial, choose accessories, and practice good hair care leading up to the event.
