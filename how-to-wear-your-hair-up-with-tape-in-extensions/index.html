How To Wear Your Hair Up With Tape In Extensions

Are you looking for a way to wear your hair up with tape in extensions? Whether you're going to a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday look, wearing your hair up with tape in extensions can add volume and length to your style. In this article, we'll discuss how to wear your hair up with tape in extensions, step-by-step instructions, useful tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips. Let's get started!

How to Wear Your Hair Up with Tape In Extensions

First, it's important to choose the right type of tape in extensions for your hair. Make sure the extensions match your hair color and texture. Once you have the right extensions, you can follow these steps to wear your hair up:

1. Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles or knots. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for extensions.

2. Section your hair

Next, section your hair into two parts: the top half and the bottom half. Use a clip to hold the top half of your hair in place.

3. Apply the tape in extensions

Take one of the tape in extensions and remove the backing. Place the extension underneath a small section of your hair, close to the roots. Press the extension firmly onto your hair to secure it in place. Repeat this process with the remaining extensions, spacing them out evenly.

4. Release the top half of your hair

Once you've applied all of the extensions, release the top half of your hair and use a comb to blend your natural hair with the extensions.

5. Style your hair up

Now that your extensions are in place, you can style your hair up as desired. There are many different updo styles to choose from, including buns, braids, and ponytails. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you need more detailed instructions, here's a step-by-step guide to wearing your hair up with tape in extensions:

  1. Brush your hair to remove tangles and knots.
  2. Section your hair into two parts: the top half and the bottom half.
  3. Take one tape in extension and remove the backing.
  4. Place the extension underneath a small section of your hair, close to the roots.
  5. Press the extension firmly onto your hair to secure it in place.
  6. Repeat this process with the remaining extensions, spacing them out evenly.
  7. Release the top half of your hair and use a comb to blend your natural hair with the extensions.
  8. Style your hair up as desired.

Useful Tips

Here are some useful tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up with tape in extensions:

  • Choose the right type of tape in extensions for your hair.
  • Make sure the extensions match your hair color and texture.
  • Apply the extensions close to the roots for a more natural look.
  • Use a comb to blend your natural hair with the extensions.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up with tape in extensions:

What are tape in extensions?

Tape in extensions are a type of hair extension that is attached to your natural hair using adhesive tape. They are easy to apply and can add volume and length to your hair.

Can I wear my hair up with tape in extensions?

Yes, you can wear your hair up with tape in extensions. Just make sure to apply the extensions close to the roots and use a comb to blend your natural hair with the extensions.

How long do tape in extensions last?

Tape in extensions can last up to 8 weeks with proper care and maintenance. However, you may need to have them re-applied sooner if they start to slip or lose their hold.

How do I remove tape in extensions?

To remove tape in extensions, use a hair extension removal solution or oil to dissolve the adhesive. Gently pull the extensions out of your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing tape in extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling on the extensions.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose braid to prevent tangling.
  • Have the extensions removed and re-applied by a professional stylist.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear your hair up with tape in extensions. Whether you're going for a sleek bun or a messy braid, your extensions will add volume and length to your style. Just remember to take good care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible.
