Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Wavy A Guide To Get The Look

Written by Aprilia Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, low-maintenance, and suits your wavy hair? Look no further than the mens haircut short sides long top wavy. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to get the look, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. So, let's dive in.

  • How to Get the Look

Description

The mens haircut short sides long top wavy is a versatile hairstyle that suits different face shapes, hair types, and ages. The style features short sides and back, with longer hair on the top that can be styled in waves, curls, or spikes. This haircut is low-maintenance, easy to style, and looks great on both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get the Look

To get the mens haircut short sides long top wavy, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience in cutting men's hair. You can bring a photo of the style to show the hairstylist what you want. The stylist will then cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving about 1 to 2 inches of hair on the top. The top hair can be layered or thinned out to create volume and movement.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your mens haircut short sides long top wavy:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a sea salt spray or a hair wax, to the top hair.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to create waves or curls on the top hair.
  5. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, using your fingers to create volume and movement.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a styling cream to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut short sides long top wavy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and is sulfate-free to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip away the natural oils and make your hair frizzy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I get the mens haircut short sides long top wavy if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a curling iron or a hair product to create waves or curls on the top hair.

2. How long does the mens haircut short sides long top wavy last?

The style can last for a few weeks with proper maintenance and regular trims.

3. Is the mens haircut short sides long top wavy suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the style can be adapted to suit different face shapes by adjusting the length and the volume of the top hair.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips for men with wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or a curling iron, as they can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when blow-drying your hair, as it can dry out your hair and make it brittle.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments, such as hair relaxers or perms, as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the wind by wearing a hat or a scarf.

With these tips and advice, you can rock the mens haircut short sides long top wavy with confidence and style.
