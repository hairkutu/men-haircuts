How To Make Thin Hair Look Good In A Ponytail

Written by Petter Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thin hair, you may feel like ponytails are not an option for you. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can make your ponytail look fuller and more voluminous. Here's how to make thin hair look good in a ponytail.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra body to your locks.

Step 2: Use a teasing brush

To add volume to your ponytail, use a teasing brush to gently backcomb your hair at the crown of your head. This will create a bit of lift and make your ponytail look fuller.

Step 3: Secure your ponytail

Once you've teased your hair, gather it into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure the elastic is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort or breakage.

Step 4: Add some texture

To give your ponytail some texture and dimension, use a curling iron or wand to add some loose waves or curls. This will make your hair look thicker and more voluminous.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To keep your ponytail in place all day, finish with a spritz of hairspray. Use a flexible hold hairspray to avoid any stiffness or crunchiness.

Tips

  • Try using a hair accessory, like a scrunchie or ribbon, to add some extra volume and interest to your ponytail.
  • If you're really struggling with thin hair, consider getting some extensions or a hairpiece to add some extra thickness and length to your ponytail.
  • Avoid pulling your ponytail too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQ

How can I make my ponytail look less flat?

To add some volume to your ponytail, use a teasing brush to gently backcomb your hair at the crown of your head. You can also try using a volumizing powder or spray to add some extra lift.

Can I still wear a high ponytail with thin hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to use a teasing brush to add some volume at the crown of your head before securing your ponytail.

How often should I wash my thin hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your scalp of its natural oils. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra body to your locks.

Hair Care

To keep your thin hair looking its best, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down. Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
