How To Fix Short Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia May 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling with short layered hair that just won't behave? Whether you've recently gotten a haircut or you're trying to grow out your layers, there are plenty of ways to fix short layered hair and make it look amazing. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for fixing short layered hair that you can use right now.

How to Fix Short Layered Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to fixing short layered hair is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help remove any product buildup, excess oil, and dirt from your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and protect it from heat damage. This will also help keep your hair moisturized, which is essential for healthy, shiny hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Next, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your short layered hair. Use the brush to pull your hair up and away from your scalp, which will help create more fullness at the roots.

Step 4: Add Some Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, add a small amount of hair oil to help smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair. This will also help protect your hair from heat damage if you plan on using hot tools to style your hair.

Step 5: Use Hot Tools

If you want to add some curls or waves to your short layered hair, use hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Fixing Short Layered Hair

Here are some additional tips and tricks for fixing short layered hair:

Tip 1: Use Dry Shampoo

If you're struggling with oily roots or flat hair, use dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair. Apply it to your roots and massage it in with your fingers for instant volume.

Tip 2: Experiment with Hair Accessories

Try using hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and barrettes to add some interest to your short layered hair. You can use them to pull your hair back or to add some sparkle to your hairstyle.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your short layered hair looking its best, it's essential to get regular trims. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and damaged.

FAQ

Q: Can you fix short layered hair without cutting it?

Yes, there are plenty of ways to fix short layered hair without cutting it. You can use hot tools, hair accessories, and styling products to create the look you want.

Q: How often should I wash my short layered hair?

How often you wash your short layered hair will depend on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How can I add volume to my short layered hair?

To add volume to your short layered hair, use a round brush when blow-drying your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray before styling. You can also use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your roots.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

To keep your short layered hair looking its best, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care for short layered hair:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any hot tools on your short layered hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing through your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.

Tip 3: Avoid Using Too Much Product

Using too much product on your short layered hair can weigh it down and make it look greasy. Use just enough product to achieve the look you want.

Conclusion

Fixing short layered hair doesn't have to be difficult. By following these tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair properly and get regular trims to keep it looking its best.
