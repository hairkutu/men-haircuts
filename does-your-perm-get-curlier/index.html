Does Your Perm Get Curlier

Written by Bardi Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you might be considering getting a perm. However, you might be wondering if your perm will get curlier over time. In this blog post, we will answer the question "does your perm get curlier?" and provide you with useful information about perming your hair.

Description

A perm is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair. It involves wrapping your hair around rods and applying a chemical solution that breaks the bonds in your hair. Once the bonds are broken, the hair can be molded into a new shape. The hair is then rinsed and neutralized to reform the bonds and set the new shape.

How to

If you want to get a perm, you should go to a professional hair stylist who is experienced in perming hair. They will be able to assess the condition of your hair and recommend the best type of perm for you.

Before getting a perm, you should also consider the maintenance involved. Permed hair requires regular conditioning and styling to maintain the shape and prevent damage.

Step by Step

Here are the steps involved in getting a perm:

  1. Consult with a professional hair stylist to determine the best type of perm for your hair.
  2. Shampoo your hair and towel-dry it.
  3. Section your hair and wrap it around perm rods.
  4. Apply a chemical solution to your hair and let it process for the recommended time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly and neutralize it with a neutralizer solution.
  6. Remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining permed hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for permed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage permed hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your perm.

FAQ

Q: Does your perm get curlier over time?

A: No, your perm does not get curlier over time. Once the chemical process is complete, your hair will maintain its shape until it grows out or is cut off.

Q: Can you straighten permed hair?

A: It is possible to straighten permed hair, but it is not recommended as it can cause damage to the hair. If you want to straighten your hair, it's best to wait until the perm has grown out or get it professionally relaxed.

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on the type of perm and how well you maintain it.

Hair Care

Taking care of permed hair requires some extra effort, but it's worth it to maintain the shape and prevent damage. Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, a perm does not get curlier over time, but it requires regular maintenance to maintain the shape and prevent damage. If you're considering getting a perm, make sure to consult with a professional hair stylist and follow their recommendations for caring for your permed hair.
