How To Cut Hair Behind Head Step By Step Guide

Cutting your hair at home can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to cutting the hair at the back of your head. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. How to Cut Hair Behind Head

Cutting hair at the back of your head can be challenging, but with the right technique and tools, it can be done easily. Here are some simple steps on how to cut hair behind head:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and tangle-free. Comb your hair thoroughly, and part it in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Choose Your Tools

You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to use hair clippers to trim the sides and back of your head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Stand in front of a mirror and hold the comb vertically against the back of your head. Use the comb as a guide and cut your hair in a straight line. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair behind head:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry. Make sure your hair is dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into two parts, left and right. Tie the left section with a hair tie to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Right Side

Using your comb as a guide, start cutting the right side of your head. Cut small sections at a time, and make sure the hair is even. Use the mirror to check your progress.

Step 4: Cut the Left Side

Untie the left section of your hair and repeat the same process on the left side of your head. Use the mirror to check for any unevenness.

Step 5: Trim the Back

Use the mirror to check the back of your head, and trim any uneven hair using your scissors. Use the comb to guide you.

Tips for Cutting Hair Behind Head

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect haircut:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Sharp scissors will make the cutting process easier and more precise.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Start cutting small sections at a time, and trim slowly.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at the back of my head?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at the back of your head with the right tools and techniques.

What kind of scissors should I use?

Use a pair of sharp, professional-grade scissors. Avoid using regular household scissors, as they can damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting Hair Behind Head

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it requires patience, practice, and the right tools. By following these simple steps, you can easily cut hair behind head and achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
