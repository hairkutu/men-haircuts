Long Layered Haircut With Side Bangs A Guide For Gorgeous Hair In 2023

Written by Frank Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long layered haircuts with side bangs are a popular hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. This versatile haircut can give you a fresh new look and transform your appearance entirely. In this article, we will take you through the step-by-step process of getting a long layered haircut with side bangs, tips, and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Description

A long layered haircut with side bangs involves cutting long hair into layers of different lengths, creating a textured, voluminous look. The side bangs are cut to frame the face and add dimension to the style. This haircut works well with all hair types and can be customized to suit individual preferences.

How to Get a Long Layered Haircut with Side Bangs

Getting a long layered haircut with side bangs can be done at a salon or at home. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it using a towel. Your hair should be damp but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle or to one side, depending on your preference. Use a comb to create a straight parting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back.

Step 4: Cut the Layers

Starting from the back, use hair cutting scissors to cut your hair into layers. Cut each section of hair at a slight angle, working your way up to the top of your head. For longer layers, cut your hair at a steeper angle. For shorter layers, cut your hair at a shallower angle.

Step 5: Cut the Side Bangs

Once you have cut the layers, it is time to cut the side bangs. Use a comb to create a diagonal parting and cut the hair at an angle to create a soft, layered look. Repeat on the other side.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener.

Tips for Long Layered Haircut with Side Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your long layered haircut with side bangs looking gorgeous:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers and shape
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to avoid tangles and breakage
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools

FAQ

Can I get a long layered haircut with side bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Long layered haircuts with side bangs work well with curly hair and can enhance your natural curls.

How often should I wash my hair after getting a long layered haircut with side bangs?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out and losing its natural oils.

Hair Care for Long Layered Haircut with Side Bangs

To keep your long layered haircut with side bangs looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause damage to your hair

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stunning long layered haircut with side bangs in 2023 and beyond. Whether you choose to get it done professionally or do it yourself at home, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
