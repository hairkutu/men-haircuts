How To Make Your Hair Look Thicker When You Straighten It

Written by William May 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thin hair, you may feel like there's nothing you can do to make it look thicker. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of thicker hair when you straighten it. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair look thicker when you straighten it.

Description

Straightening your hair can make it look sleek and shiny, but it can also make it look flatter and thinner. If you have thin hair, you may feel like there's nothing you can do to make it look thicker. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of thicker hair when you straighten it. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair look thicker when you straighten it.

How to Make Your Hair Look Thicker When You Straighten It

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body to your hair and make it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help add volume and texture to your hair. Use a comb to distribute the mousse evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help add volume and lift to your hair. As you blow dry, use the brush to lift your hair at the roots.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to straighten it. Use a high-quality straightening iron with ceramic plates to help protect your hair from damage. Straighten your hair in sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Use a Root Lifter

Use a root lifter to add extra volume to your hair. Apply the root lifter to the roots of your hair and use your fingers to massage it in. This will help lift your hair and add extra body.

Step 6: Finish with a Texturizing Spray

Finish by applying a texturizing spray to your hair. This will help add texture and hold to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller. Spray the texturizing spray evenly through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift.
  • Use a high-quality straightening iron with ceramic plates to protect your hair from damage.
  • Apply a root lifter to add extra volume to your hair.
  • Finish with a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQ

What is a root lifter?

A root lifter is a product that is designed to add volume and lift to the roots of your hair. It typically comes in a spray or mousse form and can be applied directly to the roots of your hair.

What is a texturizing spray?

A texturizing spray is a product that is designed to add texture and hold to your hair. It typically comes in a spray form and can be applied to your hair after you have straightened it.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking thick and healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make your hair look thicker when you straighten it. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
