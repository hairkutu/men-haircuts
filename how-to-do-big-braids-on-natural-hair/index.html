How To Do Big Braids On Natural Hair

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Big braids on natural hair have been a popular hairstyle for many years. They can be worn in different styles and sizes, and are perfect for protecting your hair from damage caused by heat and harsh chemicals. In this article, we will discuss how to do big braids on natural hair, step by step. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair while it's in braids, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

How to do Big Braids on Natural Hair

Before we get started, make sure that your hair is clean, detangled, and moisturized. You'll also need some hair extensions (optional), a comb, hair ties, and hair oil.

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small parts. The size of the parts will depend on the size of the braids you want. Use hair ties to secure each section.

Step 2: Add hair extensions (optional)

If you want your braids to be longer or thicker, you can add hair extensions. To do this, take a small portion of the extension hair and attach it to your natural hair using a braiding technique called "feed-in" braids.

Step 3: Start braiding

Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts. Cross the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the new middle strand. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of the section.

Step 4: Seal the ends

Once you've braided the entire section, use a hair tie to secure the end. Apply hair oil to the ends to prevent split ends.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section until your entire head is braided.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results with your big braids:

  • Do not braid your hair too tightly, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent dryness.
  • Cover your hair with a satin scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair while it's in braids, as this can cause buildup and make it difficult to wash.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do big braids last?

Big braids can last for up to six weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

How do I wash my hair while it's in braids?

To wash your hair while it's in braids, dilute shampoo with water and apply it to your scalp using a spray bottle. Massage your scalp gently, then rinse with water. Do not rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Can I wear my big braids in different styles?

Yes, you can wear your big braids in different styles such as a ponytail, bun, or half-up half-down style.

Hair Care

While your hair is in braids, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips for big braids:

  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly using a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your hairline.
  • Cover your hair with a satin scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Take breaks between braiding styles to give your hair a rest.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve beautiful big braids on natural hair while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
