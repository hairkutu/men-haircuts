How To Cut Men s Top Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting men's hair can seem like a daunting task, especially if you're not a professional barber. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily trim your own hair or that of a friend or family member. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut men's top hair with scissors in a relaxed and easy-to-understand manner.

  2. How to Cut Men's Top Hair with Scissors
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Cutting men's top hair with scissors involves trimming the hair on the top of the head to a desired length. This is typically done with a pair of hair cutting scissors, although you can use regular scissors in a pinch. It's important to use sharp scissors and to take your time to ensure an even cut.

How to Cut Men's Top Hair with Scissors

To cut men's top hair with scissors, you will need the following tools:

  • Hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle of water
  • A mirror or someone to help you see the back of the head

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by dampening the hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and help to prevent flyaways.
  2. Comb the hair straight up from the scalp. Use your fingers to hold the hair in place and to gauge the length you want to cut.
  3. Starting at the front of the head, use your scissors to make small snips in the hair. Work your way back towards the crown of the head, taking small sections of hair at a time.
  4. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head. You can check this by holding a section of hair between your fingers on each side and comparing the length.
  5. Continue to work your way back towards the crown of the head, taking small sections of hair at a time. Be sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines or unevenness.
  6. Once you reach the crown of the head, blend the hair on the sides and back of the head to create a seamless transition.
  7. Check the hair in the mirror to ensure an even and balanced cut. Make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Trim the hair around the ears and neckline as needed.
  9. Finish by styling the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length. This will help you avoid cutting off too much hair at once.
  • Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head to avoid any asymmetry.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair can be time-consuming, but rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors and keep the hair in place.
  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They will make the job much easier and give you a better result.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's top hair with scissors:

Is it better to cut hair wet or dry?

It's typically easier to cut hair when it's damp, as this helps to prevent flyaways and makes it easier to see the hair you're cutting.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always try to blend the hair to hide the mistake or cut the hair shorter to even it out. If you're really unhappy with the result, you can always go to a professional barber to fix it.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and desired length. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired style.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the look and prevent damage. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Regularly trim the ends of your hair to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's top hair with scissors at home. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and be patient. Happy cutting!
