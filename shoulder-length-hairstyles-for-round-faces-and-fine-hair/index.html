Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Round Faces And Fine Hair

Written by Robby May 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that flatters your round face and fine hair? Look no further than shoulder length hairstyles! This length of hair provides versatility, volume, and a flattering frame for your face shape. In this article, we'll dive into some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for round faces and fine hair, as well as tips for caring for and styling your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair
  • FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Faces and Fine Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle for round faces and fine hair, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you want to create volume and texture to add dimension to your hair. Layers and waves are great options for achieving this. You also want to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Round faces look best with hairstyles that create angles and lengthen the face.

Some great shoulder length hairstyles for round faces and fine hair include:

Long Bob

A long bob, or "lob," is a classic shoulder length hairstyle that works well for round faces and fine hair. The length hits just below the shoulders, creating a flattering frame for the face. Layers and waves add volume and texture, while angled cuts create angles that flatter the face shape.

Shoulder Length Shag

A shoulder length shag is another great option for round faces and fine hair. This style features layers throughout the hair, creating volume and texture. The length hits just at the shoulders, creating a flattering frame for the face. The shag cut also adds angles and dimension to the hair, which flatters round faces.

Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to any shoulder length hairstyle can create a flattering frame for round faces. Bangs also add texture and dimension to fine hair. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs that graze the cheekbones to create angles and lengthen the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Once you've chosen the right shoulder length hairstyle for your round face and fine hair, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair to create lift.
  2. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to create volume. Use the brush to curl the ends of your hair under.
  3. Once your hair is dry, add waves or curls using a curling iron or wand. Alternate the direction of the curls to create volume and dimension.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Maintaining shoulder length hair requires some effort, but the results are worth it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools every day to prevent damage. Instead, opt for air drying or using heatless styling methods.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create lift and volume in your hair.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Faces and Fine Hair

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face and fine hair?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear rounder. Also avoid styles that are too flat, as they can make your hair look limp and lifeless. Instead, opt for styles with volume and texture.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face and fine hair?

Absolutely! Straight hair can be flattering on round faces, especially if you add volume and texture with layers or waves.

Do I need to use heat tools to style my shoulder length hair?

No! There are plenty of heatless styling methods, such as braids or buns, that can create beautiful, voluminous hair without the use of heat tools.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

In addition to maintaining your shoulder length hair, it's important to care for your fine hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair. Look for lightweight, volumizing products instead.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Use lukewarm or cool water instead.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your round face and fine hair. Remember to care for and style your hair regularly to keep it looking its best!
