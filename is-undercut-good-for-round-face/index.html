Is Undercut Good For Round Face

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking of getting an undercut but are unsure if it will suit your round face shape? Well, you're not alone, as many people are hesitant to try out this trendy hairstyle. However, the answer to whether or not an undercut is good for a round face is not as simple as a yes or no. In this blog post, we will delve into the details of this hairstyle and how it can work for those with round faces.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving the hair on the sides and/or back of the head while leaving the top longer. This style has gained popularity in recent years and has been seen on both men and women. It's a versatile and edgy look that can be customized to fit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you're interested in getting an undercut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose the right length for your hair. The length of your top hair will determine how dramatic your undercut will look.
  2. Decide on the location of your undercut. You can have it on one side, both sides, or at the back of your head.
  3. Visit a salon or barber who has experience with undercut hairstyles.
  4. Maintain your undercut by regularly trimming the longer hair on top and shaving the sides.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your undercut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply some styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair on top, creating volume and texture.
  4. Use a hair clipper to shave the sides and/or back of your head. You can choose the length you prefer.
  5. Blend the shaved area with the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  6. Use a trimmer to clean up the edges and create a defined look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing an undercut hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid going too short on the sides, as that can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Keep the top hair longer to create a more balanced look.
  • Choose a side part or a messy, textured style on top to add volume and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts for round faces:

1. Will an undercut make my face look rounder?

No, not necessarily. If done correctly, an undercut can actually help to balance out the proportions of your face.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

Yes, you can. You can style your hair in a bun, ponytail, or any other updo you prefer.

3. How often do I need to maintain my undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how neat you want your undercut to look. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Maintaining an undercut requires some hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons.
  • Apply some hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and neat.

Conclusion

So, is an undercut good for a round face? The answer is yes, as long as you choose the right length and style for your hair. With the right cut and care, an undercut can be a stylish and edgy look that flatters your face shape. So, go ahead and give it a try!
