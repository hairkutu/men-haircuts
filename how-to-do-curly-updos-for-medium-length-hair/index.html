How To Do Curly Updos For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly updos are a great way to add some style to your hair for special occasions. If you have medium length hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, that's not the case! With the right techniques and tools, you can create beautiful curly updos for medium length hair. In this article, we'll show you how to do just that.

How to Do Curly Updos for Medium Length Hair

The first step in creating a curly updo for medium length hair is to prepare your hair. Start by washing it with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is important when using heat styling tools.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the curling iron or other heat styling tools you'll be using to create your curly updo.

Once your hair is prepped, you can start styling. Begin by curling your hair with a curling iron. You can use a curling wand or a traditional curling iron, depending on your preference. Be sure to curl small sections of hair at a time, and hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl.

After you've curled your entire head of hair, it's time to create your updo. There are many different types of curly updos you can try, from messy buns to braided updos. Here's one simple updo you can try:

Simple Curly Updo

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull a few strands of hair out around your face for a more relaxed look.
  5. Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep the updo in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a curly updo for medium length hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Curl small sections of hair with a curling iron or wand.
  4. Create your updo using your preferred technique.
  5. Secure your updo with bobby pins.
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair for a more relaxed look.
  7. Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep your updo in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating the perfect curly updo:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a small barrel to create tight curls.
  • For a more relaxed look, use a larger barrel curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different updo styles until you find one you love.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Apply hairspray to your hair before curling to give it extra hold.

FAQ

Can I create a curly updo with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can! Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then apply a curl-enhancing product. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, then style it into an updo using the same techniques outlined above.

How long will my curly updo last?

This depends on a number of factors, including how well you've curled your hair and how tightly you've secured your updo. With proper styling and use of hairspray, your curly updo should last all day or night.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great, even when creating curly updos for medium length hair.
