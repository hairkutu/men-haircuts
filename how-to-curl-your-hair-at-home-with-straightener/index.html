How To Curl Your Hair At Home With A Straightener

Written by Eveline Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are someone who loves trying out different hairstyles but does not want to spend a lot of money at the salon, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to curl your hair at home with a straightener. Not only is it a convenient way to get beautiful curls, but it is also cost-effective. So, let's get started!

How to

To curl your hair at home with a straightener, you will need:

  • A hair straightener
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Clips
  • Hairspray

Now, let's follow the below steps to get those beautiful curls:

Step by Step

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid any damage.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up. Make sure the sections are not too thick as it may result in uneven curls.
  3. Take one section of hair and clamp the straightener at the roots.
  4. Rotate the straightener 180 degrees away from your face and slowly glide it down till the end of the hair.
  5. Release the hair and repeat the same process for the rest of the sections.
  6. Once done, let your hair cool down for a couple of minutes.
  7. Use your fingers to run through the curls to loosen them up.
  8. Finish it off with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that will help you achieve better results:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray to avoid any damage to your hair.
  • Do not rush through the process. Take your time with each section to ensure that the curls are even.
  • Use clips to hold the sections in place while curling to avoid tangling of hair.
  • For more defined curls, hold the straightener for a longer time.
  • For loose curls, run your fingers through the curls after they have cooled down.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

  • Can I use a hair straightener to curl my hair if it is already curly?
    Yes, you can. It will give you more defined curls.
  • How long will the curls last?
    It depends on your hair type and the weather conditions. However, it can last up to 2-3 days with the use of hairspray.
  • Can I curl my hair with a straightener without using a heat protectant spray?
    No, it is not recommended as it can damage your hair.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to avoid any damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heating tools on a daily basis as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to curl your hair at home with a straightener, go ahead and try it out. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
