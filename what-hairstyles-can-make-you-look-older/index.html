What Hairstyles Can Make You Look Older

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you want to look younger, your hairstyle plays a significant role in your appearance. However, some hairstyles can make you look older than your actual age. In this post, we’ll explore what hairstyles can make you look older and what you can do to avoid them.

Table of Content

  • Short Hairstyles
  • Long Hairstyles
  • Updos
  • Bangs
  • Hair Color
  • Hair Care

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles can make you look older if they’re too severe or harsh. For example, a short bob that’s cut straight across can make your face look wider and more angular, which can add years to your appearance. Instead, opt for a layered bob or a pixie cut that’s soft and feminine.

If you have fine hair, avoid short hairstyles that are too spiky or choppy. These styles can make your hair look thin and lifeless, which can age you. Instead, choose a style with soft layers that add volume and movement to your hair.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can also make you look older if they’re too heavy or flat. For example, long hair that’s all one length can drag your face down, making you look tired and worn out. To avoid this, consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and movement.

If you have curly hair, avoid long hairstyles that are too tight or structured. These styles can make your hair look stiff and unnatural, which can age you. Instead, choose a style that’s loose and flowing, with soft waves and curls that frame your face.

Updos

Updos can make you look older if they’re too severe or tight. For example, a tight bun or chignon can pull your face back, making you look older and less approachable. To avoid this, choose an updo that’s loose and relaxed, with soft tendrils and loose curls framing your face.

If you’re attending a formal event, consider a low chignon or messy bun that’s soft and romantic. This style will add elegance to your appearance without making you look older.

Bangs

Bangs can make you look older if they’re too heavy or blunt. For example, thick, straight across bangs can make your face look wider and more angular, which can add years to your appearance. Instead, opt for soft, wispy bangs that are swept to the side, which will add softness and femininity to your look.

If you have a high forehead, consider a longer, side-swept bang that will frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Hair Color

Your hair color can also make you look older if it’s too harsh or unnatural. For example, a solid, dark color can make your skin look pale and lifeless, which can age you. Instead, choose a hair color that’s soft and natural, with highlights or lowlights that add depth and dimension to your hair.

If you’re going gray, consider embracing your natural hair color with a few subtle highlights. This will add brightness and dimension to your hair, which can make you look younger.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential if you want to look younger. Dry, damaged hair can make you look older and less vibrant. To keep your hair looking healthy and youthful, make sure to:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

FAQ

Can short hairstyles make you look older?

Yes, short hairstyles can make you look older if they’re too severe or angular. Opt for soft, feminine styles that flatter your face shape.

Can long hairstyles make you look older?

Yes, long hairstyles can make you look older if they’re too heavy or flat. Add layers and volume to your hair to keep it looking youthful and vibrant.

What hair colors can make you look younger?

Natural hair colors with subtle highlights or lowlights can make you look younger. Avoid solid, dark colors that can make you look pale and lifeless.

How can I keep my hair looking healthy and youthful?

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, get regular trims, and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather.

By avoiding hairstyles that can make you look older and taking care of your hair, you can look youthful and vibrant at any age.
