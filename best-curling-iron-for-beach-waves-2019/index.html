Best Curling Iron For Beach Waves 2019 Create Perfect Waves With Ease

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to create those perfect beachy waves to rock this summer? Look no further than the best curling iron for beach waves 2019! Whether you have fine or thick hair, short or long, there is a curling iron out there that is perfect for you. In this blog post, we will discuss how to choose the right curling iron, steps to create perfect beach waves, tips for maintaining your waves, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Curling Iron

Before diving into the steps of creating beach waves, it is important to choose the right curling iron. Here are some factors to consider:

Barrel Size

The size of the curling iron barrel will determine the size of your waves. For beach waves, a larger barrel size is recommended, typically between 1.25-2 inches.

Material

The material of the curling iron can affect the quality of your waves. Ceramic and tourmaline are popular choices as they distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Temperature Settings

Look for a curling iron with adjustable temperature settings to ensure you can use it on any hair type. Fine hair requires less heat than thick hair.

Steps to Create Perfect Beach Waves

Now that you have the right curling iron, follow these steps to create perfect beach waves:

Step 1: Protect Your Hair

Before using any heat styling tool, it is important to protect your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, starting at the top. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Shake Out Your Waves

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, shake out your waves to create a natural, beachy look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Here are some tips for maintaining your beach waves:

Tip 1: Use a Texturizing Spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your waves to add volume and hold.

Tip 2: Don't Over Brush

Brushing too much can cause your waves to fall flat. Instead, use your fingers to separate and define your waves.

Tip 3: Sleep in Braids

If you want to wake up with beach waves, sleep in braids overnight.

FAQ

Q: Can I create beach waves with a straightener?

Yes, you can create beach waves with a straightener. Follow the same steps as you would with a curling iron, but instead of wrapping your hair around the barrel, twist it around the straightener.

Q: How long will my beach waves last?

Beach waves can last anywhere from 1-3 days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Hair Care

Lastly, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With the best curling iron for beach waves 2019 and these tips, you will have perfect beachy waves all summer long!
