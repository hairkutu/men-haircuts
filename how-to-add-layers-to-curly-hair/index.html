How To Add Layers To Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair looking flat and lifeless? Adding layers to your curly hair can give it more volume and movement. In this guide, we’ll show you how to add layers to curly hair in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Add Layers to Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Layers to Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Description

Adding layers to curly hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create depth and dimension. This technique can make curly hair appear fuller and bouncier. Layers can also help to define curls and reduce frizz.

How to Add Layers to Curly Hair

Adding layers to curly hair is a job best left to a professional hairstylist. However, if you feel confident in your hair cutting abilities, you can attempt to add layers at home. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to add layers to your curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and let it air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections, using hair clips to secure each section. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use a comb to separate a small section of hair at the nape of your neck. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle. Repeat this process for each section of hair at the bottom layer.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Once you’ve finished cutting the bottom layer, move on to the middle layer. Use the same technique as before, cutting each section of hair at a slightly steeper angle than the bottom layer.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

The top layer of your hair should be the shortest. Use the same technique as before, cutting each section of hair at a steep angle to create the most volume.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check for balance. Stand in front of a mirror and make sure the layers are even. If necessary, make small adjustments to even out the layers.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would, using a diffuser to enhance your curls. You can also use a curling iron to add more definition to your curls.

Tips for Adding Layers to Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when adding layers to curly hair:

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t cut too much off at once. It’s better to make small adjustments and trim a little bit at a time.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist if you’re unsure about how to add layers to your curly hair.

FAQ

Can I add layers to my curly hair if it’s short?

Yes, you can add layers to short curly hair. However, keep in mind that the length of your hair will affect how many layers you can add.

Will adding layers make my curly hair look thinner?

No, adding layers can actually make your curly hair look thicker and fuller.

How often should I add layers to my curly hair?

It’s not necessary to add layers to curly hair on a regular basis. However, you may want to consider adding layers if you feel like your hair is lacking volume or movement.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to add layers to curly hair, you can give your hair a new look and more volume. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you’ll have beautiful, bouncy curls in no time!
