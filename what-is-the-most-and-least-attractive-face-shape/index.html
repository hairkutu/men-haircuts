What Is The Most And Least Attractive Face Shape

Most Attractive Face Shape

According to a study conducted by the University of Toronto, the most attractive face shape for both men and women is the oval face shape. This face shape is characterized by features that are proportional and balanced, with a gently rounded forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The oval face shape is also versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles and makeup looks.

Other attractive face shapes for women include the heart-shaped face, which is characterized by a wider forehead and narrow jawline, and the square face shape, which features a strong jawline and angular features. For men, the square face shape is also considered attractive, as well as the triangular face shape, which features a broad forehead and narrow chin.

Least Attractive Face Shape

On the other hand, the least attractive face shape for both men and women is the diamond face shape. This face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. The diamond face shape is often associated with aging and may appear harsh or masculine.

Other less attractive face shapes for women include the round face shape, which features full cheeks and a rounded chin, and the rectangular face shape, which features a long face with a prominent forehead and chin. For men, the oblong face shape, which is elongated with a long forehead and chin, is also considered less attractive.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

To determine your face shape, there are a few simple steps you can follow:

  1. Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, just above your eyebrows.
  3. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point, just below your ears.
  5. Compare your measurements to the following face shapes:
  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the chin, with a pointy chin.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, with a narrow forehead and chin.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length are about the same, with a rounded chin.
  • Rectangular: Face length is the longest measurement, with a pointed chin.
  • Triangular: Forehead is the widest part of the face, with a narrow chin.
  • Oblong: Face length is the longest measurement, with a long, narrow shape.

Tips for Flattering Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, there are a few tips you can follow to flatter your features:

  • Oval: Experiment with a variety of hairstyles and makeup looks, as this face shape can pull off many different styles.
  • Heart: Try a side-swept bang to balance out a wider forehead, and avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head.
  • Square: Soften angular features with layered hairstyles and rounded makeup looks.
  • Diamond: Add volume to the forehead and chin to balance out wide cheekbones.
  • Round: Add height to the crown of the head with hairstyles and contour the cheeks to create a more defined look.
  • Rectangular: Add volume to the sides of the face with layered hairstyles and avoid elongating styles.
  • Triangular: Add volume to the chin and avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead.
  • Oblong: Create width with hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my face shape?

A: While you can't change your bone structure, you can create the illusion of a different face shape with hairstyles and makeup.

Q: Is the most attractive face shape the same for everyone?

A: While the oval face shape is generally considered the most attractive, beauty is subjective and can vary based on cultural and personal preferences.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

When it comes to hair care, your face shape can also play a role in the styles and products that work best for you. Here are a few tips:

  • Oval: Experiment with a variety of hairstyles and products, as this face shape is versatile.
  • Heart: Avoid styles that add volume to the crown of the head, and use products that add shine and definition.
  • Square: Soften angular features with layered styles and use products that add volume and texture.
  • Diamond: Add volume to the forehead and chin with styles that create height, and use products that add volume and texture.
  • Round: Add height to the crown of the head with styles that create volume, and use products that add texture and definition.
  • Rectangular: Add volume to the sides of the face with layered styles, and use products that add shine and smoothness.
  • Triangular: Add volume to the chin with styles that create width, and use products that add volume and texture.
  • Oblong: Add volume to the sides of the face with layered styles, and use products that add texture and definition.
