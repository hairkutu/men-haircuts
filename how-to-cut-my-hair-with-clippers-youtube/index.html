How To Cut My Hair With Clippers Youtube A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or simply want to learn how to cut your own hair, using clippers can be a great option. But where do you start? With so many tutorials and videos out there, it can be overwhelming to know which ones to follow. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your hair with clippers using YouTube tutorials, offering tips and advice along the way.

How to Cut Your Hair with Clippers YouTube

Before diving into YouTube tutorials, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to purchase a hair cutting cape or towel to protect your clothing from hair clippings. Once you have your tools ready, it's time to search for tutorials on YouTube.

Choose the Right Tutorial

There are a lot of tutorials out there on cutting hair with clippers, so it's important to choose one that suits your needs. Look for tutorials that are aimed at beginners or those with similar hair types to yours. You may also want to search for tutorials specifically for men's or women's haircuts. Check the comments section of the videos to see if other viewers found the tutorial helpful.

Watch the Tutorial First

Before diving in and actually cutting your hair, watch the tutorial all the way through. Make sure you understand the steps involved and what tools you'll need. You may also want to take notes or bookmark certain parts of the video to refer back to later.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've found a tutorial and know what tools you need, it's time to start cutting your hair with clippers. Follow these steps:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before starting to cut.

2. Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure you don't miss any spots.

3. Cut the Sides and Back

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a shorter guard on your clippers and work your way up from the bottom. Start with the sides and work your way towards the back.

4. Blend the Top

Next, blend the top of your hair with the sides and back. Use a longer guard on your clippers and blend the hair together using a sweeping motion.

5. Trim the Top

Using scissors, trim the top of your hair to your desired length. Use a comb to guide you and make sure the hair is even.

6. Clean Up the Neckline

Use your clippers to clean up the neckline and any stray hairs around your ears.

7. Check Your Work

Using a mirror, check your work to make sure everything looks even and there are no missed spots.

8. Clean Up

Clean up any hair clippings and wash your hair to remove any loose hairs.

Tips and Advice

Here are some tips and advice to help you get the most out of cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Invest in a good pair of clippers. Cheaper models may not cut as well and can be more difficult to use.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always go shorter, but it's hard to fix a mistake if you go too short.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't worry if your first few attempts aren't perfect. The more you practice, the better you'll get.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It may take some practice, but it's a great way to save money on haircuts.

What guard should I use on my clippers?

The guard you use will depend on how short you want your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and your desired length. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut your hair with clippers using YouTube tutorials, you can save money on haircuts and have more control over your hairstyle. With some practice and patience, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.
