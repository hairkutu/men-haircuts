The Best Figure Flattering Bathing Suits Of 2023

Written by Petter Jul 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Looking for a bathing suit that flatters your figure? Look no further than our list of the best figure flattering bathing suits of 2023! From one-piece swimsuits to high-waisted bikinis, we’ve got you covered. Read on for our top picks.

How to Choose a Figure Flattering Bathing Suit

Before we dive into our list, let’s talk about how to choose a figure flattering bathing suit. Here are some tips:

Know Your Body Type

When shopping for a bathing suit, it’s important to know your body type. Are you hourglass-shaped? Pear-shaped? Rectangle-shaped? Knowing your body type will help you choose a bathing suit that flatters your figure.

Highlight Your Best Features

Do you love your legs? Look for a bathing suit that shows them off. Do you have a small waist? Choose a bathing suit with a belt or cinched waist to accentuate it.

Choose the Right Fabric

Fabric can make a big difference in how a bathing suit looks on you. Look for a bathing suit with a supportive fabric that smooths and contours your body.

Top Figure Flattering Bathing Suits of 2023

Now that you know how to choose a figure flattering bathing suit, let’s get into our top picks:

1. One-Piece Swimsuits

One-piece swimsuits are a classic and flattering choice for any body type. Look for a one-piece with ruching or a deep V-neckline to accentuate your figure.

2. High-Waisted Bikinis

High-waisted bikinis are a retro-inspired choice that flatters many body types. Look for a high-waisted bikini with a halter top to highlight your shoulders and create a balanced silhouette.

3. Monokinis

Monokinis are a unique and stylish choice for a figure flattering bathing suit. Look for a monokini with cutouts that highlight your waist and create an hourglass shape.

4. Tankinis

Tankinis are a versatile choice that offer more coverage than a bikini but still have a flattering silhouette. Look for a tankini with a ruched top to create a slimming effect.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bathing Suit

Now that you’ve found the perfect figure flattering bathing suit, it’s important to take care of it so it lasts. Here are some tips:

Rinse Your Bathing Suit After Every Use

Chlorine and salt can damage your bathing suit over time. Rinse your bathing suit with cool water after every use to remove any chlorine or salt residue.

Avoid Wrinkling

Don’t throw your bathing suit in a ball at the bottom of your beach bag! Lay it flat to avoid wrinkles that can be difficult to remove.

Wash with Care

When it’s time to wash your bathing suit, hand wash it with a gentle detergent and cool water. Don’t wring it out – gently squeeze out the water instead.

FAQ

What bathing suit is best for a pear-shaped body?

A high-waisted bikini or a one-piece swimsuit with ruching is a great choice for a pear-shaped body.

What bathing suit is best for an hourglass-shaped body?

A one-piece swimsuit with a deep V-neckline or a monokini with cutouts is a great choice for an hourglass-shaped body.

What bathing suit is best for a rectangle-shaped body?

A one-piece swimsuit or a tankini with a ruched top is a great choice for a rectangle-shaped body.

Hair Care for Your Beach Day

Don’t forget about your hair when you’re heading to the beach! Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Apply Sunscreen to Your Scalp

Your scalp can get sunburned too! Apply sunscreen to your scalp or wear a hat to protect it from the sun.

Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

Chlorine and salt can damage your hair. Rinse your hair with cool water after swimming to remove any chlorine or salt residue.

Deep Condition Your Hair

After a day at the beach, treat your hair to a deep conditioning treatment to keep it healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and our top picks for figure flattering bathing suits, you’ll be ready for a day of fun in the sun!
