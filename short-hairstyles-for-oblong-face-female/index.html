Short Hairstyles For Oblong Face Female The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Luckily, there are plenty of short hairstyles for oblong face females that can do just that. In this post, we'll break down the best short hairstyles for oblong face females, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oblong Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oblong Face Females

  5. FAQs

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow width and high forehead. The goal of a short hairstyle for oblong face females is to create balance and proportion by adding width and volume to the sides of the face while minimizing the length. There are many short hairstyles that can accomplish this, from pixie cuts to bobs to shags.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oblong Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for your oblong face, consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Some factors to keep in mind include:

  • Texture: Fine hair may benefit from shorter styles that add volume, while thick hair can handle more layers and dimension.
  • Face shape: Oblong faces can benefit from hairstyles that create width and volume on the sides, such as bobs, shags, and layered cuts.
  • Personal style: Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oblong Face Females

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for oblong face females:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse, gel, or wax.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. If desired, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Oblong Face Females

To keep your short hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your style and prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about short hairstyles for oblong face females:

What is the best short hairstyle for an oblong face?
There are many great short hairstyles for oblong face females, including bobs, pixie cuts, and shags. The key is to add width and volume to the sides of the face while minimizing the length.
Can I still have bangs with an oblong face?
Yes, you can still have bangs with an oblong face. Consider side-swept bangs or a curtain fringe to add dimension to your face.
How often should I get my short hair cut?
It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Oblong Face Females

Here are some additional hair care tips for short hair for oblong face females:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip natural oils and make your hair look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your oblong face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
