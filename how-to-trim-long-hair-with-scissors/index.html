How To Trim Long Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair is beautiful, but it can be difficult to manage. One way to keep your long locks looking their best is to trim them regularly. Trimming your hair can help prevent split ends, reduce breakage, and promote healthy growth. But if you don't want to go to a salon, you can do it yourself at home with a pair of scissors. In this article, we'll show you how to trim long hair with scissors in a few easy steps.

How to Trim Long Hair with Scissors

Before you start trimming your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Clips or hair ties to section your hair

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. This will help you see any split ends or damaged hair that needs to be trimmed.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using clips or hair ties, section your hair into manageable pieces. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.

Step 3: Trim the Ends

Take one section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the ends between your fingers and cut off any split ends or damaged hair. Be careful not to cut too much at once. It's better to trim a little bit at a time until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, working from the back to the front. Remember to comb each section straight before cutting and to trim a little bit at a time.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished trimming your hair, check your work in a mirror. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Trimming Long Hair with Scissors

Trimming your own hair can be intimidating, but with a little practice, you can master the technique. Here are a few tips to help you get started:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Sharp scissors will make the process easier and prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to trim a little bit at a time until you achieve the desired length.
  • Work in a well-lit area. Natural light is best.
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired. Trimming your hair can be a workout!

FAQ

Can I trim my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can trim curly hair with scissors. Just make sure to cut it dry and avoid cutting the curls too short.

How often should I trim my long hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?

No, regular scissors are not sharp enough and can damage your hair. Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors.

Hair Care

Trimming your hair is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your long locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to trim your long hair with scissors like a pro. Happy trimming!
