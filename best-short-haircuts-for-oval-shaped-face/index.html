Best Short Haircuts For Oval Shaped Face

Written by Oliver Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A perfect hairstyle can change your entire look and make you look more confident and beautiful. But choosing the right hairstyle according to your face shape is essential. If you have an oval-shaped face, then consider yourself lucky as this face shape is considered the most versatile face shape. So, if you want to try a short haircut, here are some of the best short haircuts for oval shaped face that you can consider.

Description

An oval-shaped face is considered the most symmetrical face shape, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. This face shape has balanced proportions, and any hairstyle goes well with it. You can experiment with different haircuts and styles without worrying about it not suiting your face shape. Short haircuts are great for oval-shaped faces as they accentuate the features and make you look more stunning.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Oval Shaped Face

While choosing a short haircut, you need to consider your hair texture and your face shape. Here are some tips to help you choose the best short haircut for oval-shaped face:

Tips

  • Consider your hair texture â If you have thin hair, then opt for a layered haircut, and if you have thick hair, then go for a blunt haircut.
  • Consider your face shape â An oval-shaped face can pull off any hairstyle, but you can choose a haircut that accentuates your features.
  • Consult with a hairstylist â A hairstylist can guide you to choose the best short haircut according to your face shape and hair texture.

Best Short Haircuts for Oval Shaped Face

1. Pixie Cut

Pixie cut is a short haircut that is perfect for oval-shaped faces. This cut is low maintenance and looks great on all hair textures. You can keep it messy or slicked back for a more polished look. It accentuates the cheekbones and jawline and makes you look stunning.

2. Bob Cut

Bob cut is a classic short haircut that looks great on oval-shaped faces. This cut can be customized according to your hair texture and face shape. You can go for a blunt bob or a layered bob, depending on your preferences. It accentuates the jawline and makes your face look more defined.

3. Lob Cut

Lob cut is a longer version of the bob cut, and it looks great on oval-shaped faces. This cut falls just above the shoulder and accentuates the collarbone. It looks great on all hair textures, and you can add some layers to add more volume and texture.

4. Side Swept Bangs

If you want to add some drama to your short haircut, then go for side-swept bangs. It looks great on oval-shaped faces and accentuates the cheekbones. You can keep it short or long, depending on your preferences.

FAQ

1. Can oval-shaped faces pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval-shaped faces can pull off any hairstyle, but you need to consider your hair texture and face shape.

2. Which short haircut is the most popular for oval-shaped faces?

Pixie cut and bob cut are the most popular short haircuts for oval-shaped faces.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

You can get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require low maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your short hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Choosing the right short haircut for your oval-shaped face can enhance your features and make you look more stunning. Consider your hair texture and face shape while choosing a haircut and consult with a hairstylist if you are unsure. Take care of your hair by following a hair care routine, and enjoy your new short haircut.
