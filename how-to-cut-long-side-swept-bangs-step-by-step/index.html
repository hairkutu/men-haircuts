How To Cut Long Side Swept Bangs Step By Step

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Want to add some style to your hair without losing the length? Long side swept bangs are a perfect addition to any hairstyle. They frame your face and give you a chic look. However, going to a salon every time you need a trim can be expensive. This step-by-step guide will show you how to cut long side swept bangs at home.

Description

Long side swept bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be sported by any hair type or length. They add a soft and feminine touch to any look, and you can style them in many different ways. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, long side swept bangs can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to Cut Long Side Swept Bangs Step by Step

The following steps will guide you through the process of cutting long side swept bangs:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb it well to ensure there are no tangles.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut. You can use a comb to create a V-shape section starting from the middle of your head.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair away from the section you are cutting. This will prevent you from accidentally cutting the wrong hair.
  4. Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. Remember that you can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back.
  5. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and comb it downwards. Make sure the hair is taut and straight.
  6. Using a sharp pair of scissors, cut the hair at an angle. Start from the outer part of the section and move towards the center.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until you have achieved the desired length.
  8. Check both sides to ensure they are even. You can make small adjustments if necessary.
  9. Release the rest of your hair and style your bangs as desired.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting long side swept bangs:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it can shrink when it dries.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair to avoid making mistakes.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short as they may take longer to grow back.
  • If you have curly hair, cut your bangs when they are dry to prevent shrinkage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long side swept bangs:

  • Can I cut my bangs with regular scissors?

    • You can, but it's better to use sharp hair cutting scissors to prevent split ends.

  • How often should I trim my bangs?

    • You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them in shape.

  • What face shapes suit long side swept bangs?

    • Long side swept bangs suit oval, heart, and square face shapes.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to style your bangs.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent oil buildup.
  • Don't use too much product on your bangs as it can weigh them down.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent split ends.

By following this step-by-step guide, you can easily cut long side swept bangs at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a chic new hairstyle in no time!
