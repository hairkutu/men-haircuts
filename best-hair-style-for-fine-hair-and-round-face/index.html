Best Hair Style For Fine Hair And Round Face

Written by Eveline Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you have fine hair and a round face? Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyle for fine hair and round face. We will also provide you with tips on how to style and take care of your hair. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyle for fine hair and round face, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

If your face is widest at the cheeks and you have a round chin, then you have a round face shape.

Best Hair Style for Fine Hair and Round Face

The best hairstyle for fine hair and round face is a layered bob. This hairstyle adds volume and dimension to fine hair, while also slimming down a round face. The layers create movement and texture, making your hair look fuller and thicker. The bob length should be around chin-length, with the layers starting at the cheekbones. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Other Hairstyles to Consider

If you're not a fan of the layered bob, there are other hairstyles you can consider:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Pixie cut

These hairstyles also add volume and texture to fine hair, while slimming down a round face. However, make sure to consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will best suit your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyle for fine hair and round face, let's talk about how to style it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add soft waves or texture to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to adding product to your hair. Too much product can weigh down fine hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry out fine hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQs

Can I wear my hair straight?

Absolutely! While soft waves and texture add volume and dimension to fine hair, you can also wear your hair straight. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Can I still wear a side part?

Yes, you can still wear a side part with a layered bob. Just make sure to add volume to the roots and use a round brush when blow drying your hair to create movement and texture.

Do I have to add bangs?

No, you don't have to add bangs to your hairstyle. However, side-swept bangs can help frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some hair care tips for fine hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and thickness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and serums, as they can weigh down fine hair.
  • Invest in a good quality dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry out fine hair.

By following these tips and styling your hair with the best hairstyle for fine hair and round face, you can flaunt your features with confidence!
