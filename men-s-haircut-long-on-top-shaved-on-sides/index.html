Men s Haircut Long On Top Shaved On Sides The Trendy Style For 2023

Written by Christine Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Looking for a stylish and modern men's haircut? The long on top shaved on sides style is a popular choice that has been trending for years. This haircut is perfect for guys who want to keep their hair long on the top while keeping the sides short and tapered. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get this trendy haircut and how to take care of it for the best look.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Shaved on Sides

The first step in getting this trendy haircut is to find a skilled barber who can deliver the look you want. When you go to the barber, you should bring a picture of the style you want to achieve. This will help your barber understand exactly what you're looking for.

Before your barber starts cutting, they will need to determine the length of your hair on the top and the sides. They will then use clippers to shave the sides of your hair to your desired length. The top of your hair will be left longer and can be styled with scissors to achieve the desired length and shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you get the men's haircut long on top shaved on sides:

  1. Find a skilled barber who can deliver the look you want.
  2. Bring a picture of the style you want to achieve.
  3. Determine the length of your hair on the top and the sides.
  4. Use clippers to shave the sides of your hair to your desired length.
  5. Leave the top of your hair longer and style it with scissors to achieve the desired length and shape.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Haircut Long on Top Shaved on Sides

Once you've got the men's haircut long on top shaved on sides, it's important to take proper care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Trim the sides of your hair every two weeks to maintain the length.
  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair looking neat and in place.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair in shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should the hair on top be?

The length of the hair on top depends on your personal preference. Typically, the hair on top is left long enough to be styled in a variety of ways, ranging from a messy, tousled look to a sleek, combed-back style.

Do I need to use a styling product?

Yes, using a styling product is recommended to keep your hair looking neat and in place. Depending on your hair type and desired style, you can use a variety of products, such as pomade, wax, or gel.

Can I still get this haircut if I have thinning hair?

A men's haircut long on top shaved on sides can still work for guys with thinning hair. Your barber can adjust the length and style of your hair to make it look fuller and thicker.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock the men's haircut long on top shaved on sides and keep your hair looking great. Book your appointment with a skilled barber today!
