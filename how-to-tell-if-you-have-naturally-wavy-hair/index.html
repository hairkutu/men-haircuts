How To Tell If You Have Naturally Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi May 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you've always wondered whether your hair is naturally wavy or not, this guide is for you. In this article, we will discuss how to tell if you have naturally wavy hair and provide you with tips and tricks to take care of your waves. Read on to find out more!

Description

Wavy hair is a beautiful and versatile hair type that can be styled in many ways. It is characterized by loose, flowing curls that are not as tight as curly hair but not as straight as straight hair. If you have naturally wavy hair, you may notice that your hair has a natural bounce and movement to it. Wavy hair is also prone to frizz and can be difficult to manage if not properly cared for.

How to Tell If You Have Naturally Wavy Hair

Here are some ways to tell if you have naturally wavy hair:

1. Look at Your Hair's Texture

One of the easiest ways to tell if you have naturally wavy hair is to look at your hair's texture. Wavy hair is often thicker and coarser than straight hair, but not as thick or coarse as curly hair. If you have wavy hair, you may notice that your hair has a natural wave pattern that is visible even when your hair is wet.

2. Observe Your Hair's Natural Movement

Another way to tell if you have naturally wavy hair is to observe your hair's natural movement. Wavy hair has a natural bounce and movement to it that is not present in straight hair. If you notice that your hair has a natural wave or curl to it, you may have naturally wavy hair.

3. Take a Strand Test

If you're still not sure whether you have naturally wavy hair, you can take a strand test. Take a strand of hair and gently pull it straight. If your hair has a natural wave or curl to it, it will bounce back into a wave or curl when you release it. This is a good indication that you have naturally wavy hair.

Step by Step Guide: How to Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to tell if you have naturally wavy hair, it's important to know how to take care of it. Here is a step by step guide on how to care for wavy hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for wavy hair. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil, jojoba oil, and shea butter.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

After shampooing and conditioning, use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots to prevent breakage and damage.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Look for a lightweight leave-in conditioner that won't weigh your hair down.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

If you're using a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your hair's natural waves. Set your dryer to a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. Wavy hair can be styled in many ways, including braids, buns, and loose waves. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Taking Care of Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of wavy hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important not to overwash your hair. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day to help maintain its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage wavy hair and cause it to lose its natural wave pattern. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible or use them sparingly.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. Silk is also gentle on your hair and won't cause as much friction as cotton or other materials.

FAQs

1. Can I Have Naturally Wavy Hair Even If My Hair Is Thin?

Yes, you can have naturally wavy hair even if your hair is thin. Wavy hair can come in many different textures and thicknesses.

2. Can I Brush My Wavy Hair?

You can brush your wavy hair, but it's important to use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and damage.

3. How Can I Enhance My Hair's Natural Waves?

You can enhance your hair's natural waves by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, scrunching your hair with your hands, or using a curl-enhancing product.

Hair Care Products for Wavy Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for wavy hair:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo
  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Conditioner
  • Cantu Leave-In Conditioning Repair Cream
  • DevaCurl Wave Maker Touchable Texture Whip

Now that you know how to tell if you have naturally wavy hair and how to take care of it, you can embrace your beautiful waves with confidence. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and it will reward you with beautiful, bouncy waves!
