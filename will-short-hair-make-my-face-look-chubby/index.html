Will Short Hair Make My Face Look Chubby

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has been a trend for a while now, and many of us are considering cutting our long tresses. However, one question that keeps popping up in our minds is, "Will short hair make my face look chubby?" The answer is not straightforward, but we will explore this topic in detail in this article.

Description

If you have a round face, you might want to avoid short hairstyles that can make your face appear even chubbier. However, this does not mean that you cannot have short hair. There are plenty of short hairstyles that can complement your face shape and enhance your features. We will discuss these hairstyles in detail later in this article.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all these measurements are almost equal, you have a round face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead, you have a square-shaped face. And if your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval-shaped face.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for short hairstyles that can complement your features. Here are some tips:

Round faces

If you have a round face, you can opt for short hairstyles that add height to your crown and elongate your face. You can also go for hairstyles with side-swept bangs or layers that frame your face. Avoid blunt cuts and hairstyles that end at your chin.

Heart-shaped faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you can go for short hairstyles that add volume to your chin area and balance your forehead. You can also opt for hairstyles with layers or choppy ends that soften your features. Avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs and volume at the crown.

Square-shaped faces

If you have a square-shaped face, you can go for short hairstyles that add volume to your crown and soften your jawline. You can also opt for hairstyles with side-swept bangs or layers that frame your face. Avoid blunt cuts and hairstyles that end at your jawline.

Oval-shaped faces

If you have an oval-shaped face, you can opt for almost any short hairstyle. You can go for hairstyles with bangs, layers, or choppy ends. However, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to your crown.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to choose the right short hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research short hairstyles that complement your features
  3. Consult with a hairstylist
  4. Choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle
  5. Experiment with different styles and hair products

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain
  • Avoid hairstyles that require too much styling
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves
  • Take care of your hair by using the right products and getting regular trims

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look chubby?

A: It depends on your face shape and the short hairstyle you choose. Some short hairstyles can complement your features and make your face appear slimmer. However, some hairstyles can make your face appear rounder. It is best to consult with a hairstylist and choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Q: Can I still have short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still have short hair if you have a round face. You just need to choose a hairstyle that adds height to your crown and elongates your face. Avoid blunt cuts and hairstyles that end at your chin.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution

In conclusion, short hair can complement any face shape if you choose the right hairstyle. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a short hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
