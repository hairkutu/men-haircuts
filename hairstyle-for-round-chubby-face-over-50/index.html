Hairstyle For Round Chubby Face Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Apr 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round and chubby face, you may feel limited when it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle. But don't worry, there are plenty of options that can flatter your features and make you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for finding the perfect hairstyle for round chubby face over 50.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for round chubby face over 50, it's important to consider a few factors:

Face Shape

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are approximately the same width and length. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and definition. You can do this by adding layers, angles, and volume to your hair.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact which hairstyles will suit you best. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer cut with blunt ends to help weigh your hair down.

Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's also important to consider your lifestyle. If you're a busy mom or work full-time, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to consider a more intricate style that requires more upkeep.

Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face over 50

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best options for round chubby face over 50:

Short Bob

The short bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a great option for round faces because it creates the illusion of length and definition. You can add layers and volume to your bob to create a more textured look.

Shoulder-Length Layers

If you prefer longer hair, shoulder-length layers are a great option for round chubby face over 50. The layers will add volume and movement to your hair, while the length will help elongate your face.

Side-Swept Bangs

If you have a round face, bangs can be a tricky hairstyle to pull off. However, side-swept bangs are a great option because they create the illusion of length and definition. Just make sure to keep your bangs long enough to sweep to the side.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your round chubby face over 50, it's important to know how to style it to perfection. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

Choosing the right products for your hair type is crucial when it comes to styling your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a smoothing serum to help tame frizz.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help add volume and texture to your hairstyle. Use a round brush to help create volume at the roots, and blow dry your hair in sections for best results.

Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to add some personality to your hairstyle. Consider trying out some hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add some extra flair to your look.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo can help prevent damage to your hair. Look for a shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens, and avoid washing your hair every day.

Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair is crucial for keeping it healthy and hydrated. Use a conditioner that is suited for your hair type, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so try to avoid using hot tools as much as possible. If you must use a hair dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for round chubby face over 50?

The best hairstyle for round chubby face over 50 is one that creates the illusion of length and definition. Short bobs, shoulder-length layers, and side-swept bangs are all great options.

How do I style my hair for a round chubby face?

To style your hair for a round chubby face, use the right products, blow dry your hair, and experiment with accessories. You can also consider adding layers and volume to your hairstyle.

How do I take care of my hair?

To take care of your hair, use a gentle shampoo, condition your hair, and avoid heat styling as much as possible. You can also consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round chubby face over 50 and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
