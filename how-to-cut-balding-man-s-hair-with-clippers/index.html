How To Cut Balding Man s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a balding man who wants to keep your hair neat and tidy, using clippers is a great way to do it. Not only is it easy and convenient, but it's also cost-effective. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to cut balding man's hair with clippers.

How to Cut Balding Man's Hair with Clippers

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need. Here's what you'll need to cut your hair with clippers:

  • Clippers with guards
  • A mirror
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A towel
  • A cape or a large shirt

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have all the necessary tools, let's begin with the step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash your hair thoroughly. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, dry it with a towel.

Step 3: Put on a Cape or a Large Shirt

To avoid getting hair all over your clothes, put on a cape or a large shirt.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair in the direction it naturally falls. This will help you to see where your hair is thinnest.

Step 5: Choose a Guard

Clippers come with guards of different sizes. Choose a guard that is one size longer than the length you want your hair to be.

Step 6: Start Cutting

Turn on your clippers and start cutting your hair. Begin at the back of your head and move forward. Use the comb as a guide to ensure that you cut your hair evenly.

Step 7: Cut the Sides

After you've cut the back, move on to the sides. Use the same guard that you used for the back.

Step 8: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Cut it at an angle, so that it blends in with the sides.

Step 9: Clean Up

Use the clippers to clean up the edges around your ears and the back of your neck.

Step 10: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller guard if necessary.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers.
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • If you're unsure about anything, consult a professional barber.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting balding man's hair with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. However, it's important to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

What guard should I use?

Use a guard that is one size longer than the length you want your hair to be.

Do I need to use scissors?

Yes, you'll need scissors to trim the top of your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling out hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-blocking product.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can save you time and money. Follow these simple steps and you'll have a neat and tidy haircut in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and it will look great!
