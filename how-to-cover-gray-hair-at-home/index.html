How To Cover Gray Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to cover gray hair at home, you're not alone. Many people choose to color their hair to hide gray strands, and with the right tools and techniques, it's easy to do at home. In this article, we'll show you how to cover gray hair at home in a few easy steps. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you get the best results possible. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Gray Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Gray Hair at Home
  • FAQs

How to Cover Gray Hair at Home

Covering gray hair at home is easy and affordable. You'll need a few tools to get started, including:

  • Hair color kit
  • Applicator brush
  • Bowl
  • Gloves
  • Old towel or cape

Before you begin, be sure to choose a hair color that matches your natural shade. You can also opt for a shade that's one or two shades lighter or darker, depending on your preference. It's best to avoid drastic color changes, as these can be difficult to achieve at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools and hair color, it's time to get started. Follow these steps to cover gray hair at home:

Step 1: Prepare

Before you begin, put on your gloves and old towel or cape to protect your skin and clothing from any hair color drips. Mix the hair color according to the instructions on the box, and pour it into a bowl.

Step 2: Section your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Apply the Hair Color

Starting with the back section, use the applicator brush to apply the hair color to your roots. Work your way towards the front, making sure to cover all the gray hair. Be sure to apply the hair color evenly, and avoid applying too much color, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 4: Wait

Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time. This will vary depending on the brand of hair color you're using, but it's usually around 30 minutes.

Step 5: Rinse

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that came with the hair color kit, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 6: Dry and Style

Dry your hair with a towel, and style it as usual. You'll now have beautiful, shiny hair without any gray strands!

Tips for Covering Gray Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when covering gray hair at home:

  • Choose a hair color that matches your natural shade or one to two shades lighter or darker.
  • Section your hair into four parts to ensure even coverage.
  • Avoid applying too much hair color, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time to achieve the best results.

FAQs

Can you cover gray hair without coloring it?

No, the only way to cover gray hair is to color it.

How often do I need to color my hair to cover gray roots?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your gray roots become visible. As a general rule, you should color your hair every four to six weeks to cover gray roots.

Can I use a semi-permanent hair color to cover gray hair?

Yes, you can use a semi-permanent hair color to cover gray hair. However, keep in mind that the color will fade over time and you'll need to reapply the color more frequently than with a permanent hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've covered your gray hair at home, it's important to take care of your newly colored hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Now that you know how to cover gray hair at home, you can achieve beautiful, shiny hair without any gray strands. Follow our step-by-step guide, and use our tips to get the best results possible. Happy coloring!
