How To Spike Hair Up The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a hairstyle that screams confidence and style, spiking your hair is a great option. Spiked hair has been a popular trend for decades, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll show you how to spike hair up in a relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair for spiking, to the best products to use, to how to maintain your spiked hairstyle. So, let's get started!

How to Spike Hair Up

Before we dive into the steps on how to spike your hair up, it's important to prepare your hair. Make sure your hair is clean and dry, and you have all the necessary products on hand. You'll need a strong hold hair gel or pomade, a comb, and a blow dryer.

Step 1: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Start by applying a dime-sized amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Rub the gel or pomade between your hands and run your fingers through your hair to distribute the product evenly. Make sure you focus on the roots and tips of your hair, as these are the areas that need the most hold.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while running your fingers through it. This will help to create volume and texture in your hair, making it easier to spike up. Make sure you blow dry your hair in an upward direction, as this will help create the height you need for a spiked hairstyle.

Step 3: Spike Your Hair Up

Using a comb, start spiking your hair up. Work in small sections, starting from the back of your head and moving forward. Use the comb to lift your hair up and away from your scalp, creating the desired height and shape. Once you have your desired look, use your hands to mold your hair into place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to spike hair up:

  1. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it.
  2. Apply a dime-sized amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair in an upward direction.
  4. Using a comb, start spiking your hair up in small sections.
  5. Mold your hair into place using your hands.

Tips for Spiking Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when spiking your hair up:

  • Use a strong hold hair gel or pomade for maximum hold.
  • Blow dry your hair in an upward direction to create volume.
  • Work in small sections when spiking your hair up.
  • Use your hands to mold your hair into place for a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

How long will my spiked hairstyle last?

Your spiked hairstyle should last all day with the right products and technique. However, if you're going to be outside in hot or humid weather, you may need to touch up your hairstyle throughout the day.

What products should I use to spike my hair up?

You'll need a strong hold hair gel or pomade to spike your hair up. Look for products that are specifically designed for spiking hair and provide maximum hold.

Can I spike my hair up without blow drying it?

While blow drying your hair can help create volume and texture, it's not necessary to spike your hair up. If you have naturally thick or curly hair, you may be able to skip the blow dryer and go straight to spiking your hair up.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

Spiked hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your spiked hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can disrupt the shape of your spikes.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools.

Now that you know how to spike hair up, give it a try and see how it looks on you. With a little practice and the right products, you can achieve a stylish and confident spiked hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.
