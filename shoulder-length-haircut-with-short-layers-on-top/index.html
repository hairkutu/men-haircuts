Shoulder Length Haircut With Short Layers On Top

Written by Robby Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, you might want to consider the shoulder length haircut with short layers on top. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair without sacrificing length. In this article, we'll discuss how to get this haircut, some tips for styling it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The shoulder length haircut with short layers on top is a haircut that features longer hair on the sides and back, with shorter layers on top. This haircut is great for those who want to keep some length but also want to add some volume and texture to their hair. The layers on top can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile and trendy haircut.

How to

If you want to get a shoulder length haircut with short layers on top, the first step is to find a hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut. Once you've found a hairstylist, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

During the consultation, you should discuss with your hairstylist the length and style you want. They will also evaluate your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best haircut for you.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Before the haircut, your hairstylist will shampoo and condition your hair to make it easier to cut and style.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Your hairstylist will use scissors or a razor to cut your hair to the desired length and add the short layers on top. They will also blend the layers to make the haircut look more natural.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is done, your hairstylist will style your hair using a blow-dryer, round brush, and other styling tools to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your shoulder length haircut with short layers on top:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shoulder length haircut with short layers on top:

Q: Will this haircut work for all hair types?

A: This haircut can work for most hair types, but it may not look as dramatic on very fine or thin hair.

Q: How long does it take to style this haircut?

A: It can take anywhere from 10-30 minutes to style this haircut, depending on the desired look and the tools used.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this haircut. However, you may need to use some bobby pins or hair clips to keep the shorter layers in place.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length haircut with short layers on top looking its best, you should follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Overall, the shoulder length haircut with short layers on top is a trendy and versatile haircut that can work for many different hair types and lifestyles. By following the tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep this haircut looking its best for weeks to come.
