How To Tell If You Have A Diamond Shaped Face

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you curious about your face shape? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories to flatter your features. One of the less common face shapes is the diamond shape. In this article, we'll show you how to tell if you have a diamond shaped face and give you tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Diamond Shaped Face?
  • How to Tell If You Have a Diamond Shaped Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Determine Your Face Shape

  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Faces

What is a Diamond Shaped Face?

A diamond shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This face shape is often compared to an inverted triangle or a diamond shape. Celebrities with diamond shaped faces include Rihanna, Jennifer Lopez, and Keira Knightley.

If you have a diamond shaped face, you may also have a slightly angular jawline and wide cheekbones that are the widest part of your face. Your forehead may be slightly narrower than your cheekbones, and your chin may be pointed or slightly rounded.

How to Tell If You Have a Diamond Shaped Face

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few things you can look for to determine if you have a diamond shaped face:

  • Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Your forehead is slightly narrower than your cheekbones.
  • Your chin is pointed or slightly rounded.
  • Your face may be slightly longer than it is wide.

If you have these features, you likely have a diamond shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're still not sure what your face shape is, you can follow these steps:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo or use your phone or computer to draw lines on the photo.
  3. Draw a line from the top of your forehead to the tip of your chin.
  4. Draw a line from the widest point of your cheekbones on one side of your face to the widest point on the other side.
  5. Draw a line from the bottom of your nose to the tip of your chin.
  6. Measure the length and width of your face.
  7. Compare your measurements to the descriptions of different face shapes.

If your measurements match the description of a diamond shaped face, then you have a diamond shaped face.

Tips for Enhancing a Diamond Shaped Face

Now that you know your face shape, you can use these tips to enhance your natural beauty:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and balance out your cheekbones. Avoid hairstyles that are too narrow or flat.
  • Try makeup techniques that highlight your cheekbones and soften your jawline. Use contouring to create a more oval shape on your face.
  • Wear accessories that draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, such as statement earrings or a bold necklace.

Remember, these are just guidelines. The most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about diamond shaped faces:

Can diamond shaped faces wear bangs?

Yes, diamond shaped faces can wear bangs. However, it's best to choose bangs that are swept to the side or have soft, rounded edges to balance out your features.

What are the best hairstyles for diamond shaped faces?

The best hairstyles for diamond shaped faces are those that add volume to the top of the head and balance out the cheekbones. This can include layered cuts, pixie cuts, and long hairstyles with curls or waves.

What are the worst hairstyles for diamond shaped faces?

The worst hairstyles for diamond shaped faces are those that are too narrow or flat, such as slicked-back styles or straight bobs that end at the chin.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care, diamond shaped faces should focus on adding volume to the top of the head and avoiding styles that are too narrow or flat. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lifter, to add height to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, straight styles that can make your face look longer.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering style for your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create movement and balance.

By following these tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your diamond shaped face.
