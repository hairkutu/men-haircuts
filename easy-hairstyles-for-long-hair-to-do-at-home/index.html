Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair To Do At Home

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of versatility when it comes to styling your hair. On the other hand, it can be difficult to come up with new styles to keep your long locks looking fresh and polished. In this article, we'll provide you with easy hairstyles for long hair to do at home that will help you switch up your look without too much fuss.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to easy hairstyles for long hair to do at home, the key is to keep it simple. You don't need to be a professional stylist to create a beautiful look that will turn heads. With just a few simple tools and some styling products, you can transform your long locks into a gorgeous hairstyle that will last all day.

How to

The first thing you need to do when creating an easy hairstyle for long hair is to choose the right tools. You'll need a good quality brush, a hair tie or two, some bobby pins, and some styling products like hairspray or mousse. Once you have your tools ready, you can start experimenting with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step

Here are some easy hairstyles for long hair to do at home:

1. Messy Bun

Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face to give it a messy, effortless look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Take the top section of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie. Leave the bottom section of your hair down for a simple yet elegant look.

3. Side Braid

Sweep your hair to one side and begin braiding it. Secure the end with a hair tie and pull out some pieces of hair to give it a boho vibe.

4. Beachy Waves

Spritz some sea salt spray onto your damp hair and scrunch it up with your hands. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser to enhance the waves, and you're done!

Tips

When creating easy hairstyles for long hair to do at home, it's important to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair strong and shiny, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

FAQ

Q: Can these hairstyles be done on any hair type?
A: Yes! These hairstyles can be done on any hair type, whether your hair is curly, straight, or wavy.

Q: How long do these hairstyles take to do?
A: Each hairstyle should take no more than 10-15 minutes to do.

Hair Care

When it comes to keeping your long hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these easy hairstyles for long hair to do at home, you can switch up your look without too much fuss. Whether you're going for a casual, boho vibe or a sleek and polished look, there's a hairstyle that will work for you. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your long locks!
