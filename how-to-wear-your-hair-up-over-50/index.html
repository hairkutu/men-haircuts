How To Wear Your Hair Up Over 50 A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle, wearing your hair up can be a stylish and practical option. Not only does it keep your hair out of your face, but it also gives you a polished and put-together look. In this guide, we'll show you how to wear your hair up over 50 with our step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQ. Let's get started!

How to Wear Your Hair Up Over 50

There are many ways to wear your hair up over 50, but we'll focus on three classic styles: the bun, the ponytail, and the updo.

The Bun

To create a bun, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair back into a ponytail at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base of the elastic band to create a bun.
  3. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

The bun is a classic and elegant hairstyle that works well for any occasion. You can make the bun sleek and polished or loose and messy depending on your preference.

The Ponytail

To create a ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair back into a high ponytail at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume and height.
  3. Smooth the hair on top with a brush or comb to create a polished look.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

The ponytail is a simple and versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. You can make the ponytail sleek and sophisticated or add some volume and texture for a more casual look.

The Updo

To create an updo, follow these steps:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail at the crown of your head and securing it with an elastic band.
  2. Divide the ponytail into sections and twist each section around your fingers to create loops.
  3. Pin the loops in place with bobby pins, forming a messy bun.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

The updo is a sophisticated and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for dressy occasions. You can make the updo sleek and polished or add some texture and volume for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Wearing Your Hair Up Over 50

Here are some tips to help you wear your hair up over 50:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features.
  • Use hair accessories like clips, pins, and headbands to add interest and detail to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different textures and finishes, like smooth and sleek or messy and tousled.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on what hairstyles would work best for you.

FAQ About Wearing Your Hair Up Over 50

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up over 50:

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have thinning hair. Just be gentle when securing your hair in a ponytail or bun, and use hair accessories like clips and pins to add volume and texture.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face, like a high ponytail or a top knot.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have short hair. Just use bobby pins and hair ties to create a mini bun or ponytail at the back of your head.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Your Hair Up Over 50

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up over 50:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and smooth.

Now that you know how to wear your hair up over 50, it's time to try out some new hairstyles! Whether you prefer a classic bun, a sleek ponytail, or an elegant updo, there's a hairstyle that's perfect for you. Just remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and features, and have fun experimenting with different textures and finishes.
