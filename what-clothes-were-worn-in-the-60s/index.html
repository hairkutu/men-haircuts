What Clothes Were Worn In The 60S

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read
What Clothes Were Worn in the 60s? | A Fashion Guide

The 1960s was a decade of change, experimentation, and rebellion. The fashion of the era reflected this, with bold colors, daring designs, and a rejection of traditional gender roles. In this article, we will take a look at the clothes that were popular in the 60s and how you can incorporate them into your wardrobe today.

What Were the Key Fashion Trends of the 60s?

The 60s was a time of tremendous change, both politically and socially. The fashion of the era reflected this, with many new styles and trends emerging. Some of the key fashion trends of the 60s included:

  • Mini skirts and dresses
  • Bell-bottom pants
  • Tie-dye shirts
  • Hippie fashion
  • Mod fashion
  • Go-go boots
  • Hot pants

How to Incorporate 60s Fashion into Your Wardrobe Today

If you want to add a touch of 60s style to your wardrobe, there are a few key pieces you can look for. Here are some tips:

Mini Skirts and Dresses

Mini skirts and dresses were a staple of 60s fashion. Look for dresses that are short and fitted, and pair them with knee-high boots for a retro look. You can also opt for a mini skirt and pair it with a colorful top for a fun, 60s-inspired outfit.

Bell-Bottom Pants

Bell-bottom pants were another popular item in the 60s. Look for a pair of high-waisted bell-bottoms and pair them with a tucked-in blouse for a classic 60s look. You can also add a pair of platform shoes to complete the outfit.

Tie-Dye Shirts

Tie-dye shirts were a staple of hippie fashion in the 60s. Look for a colorful tie-dye shirt and pair it with jeans or a denim skirt for a fun, retro look.

Hippie Fashion

Hippie fashion was all about expressing yourself through clothing. Look for loose, flowy dresses and tops with colorful patterns. You can also add a pair of round sunglasses and a flower crown to complete the look.

Mod Fashion

Mod fashion was all about clean lines and bold colors. Look for a shift dress in a bright color, and pair it with a pair of chunky heels. Add some statement jewelry to complete the look.

Go-Go Boots

Go-go boots were a staple of 60s fashion. Look for a pair of knee-high boots with a chunky heel, and pair them with a mini skirt or dress for a fun, retro look.

Hot Pants

Hot pants were a daring new trend in the 60s. Look for a pair of high-waisted hot pants and pair them with a crop top or a tucked-in blouse for a fun, retro look.

FAQs

What Colors Were Popular in the 60s?

Bold, bright colors were popular in the 60s. Colors like pink, orange, and yellow were particularly popular.

What Fabrics Were Popular in the 60s?

Fabrics like polyester, nylon, and rayon were popular in the 60s. These fabrics were often used for bold, colorful clothing.

Hair Care in the 60s

Hair was a big part of 60s fashion. Women often wore their hair in bouffant or beehive styles, while men often had longer hair. To achieve a 60s-inspired hairstyle, you can try teasing your hair at the crown and adding a headband or ribbon for a retro look.

Overall, the 60s was a decade of change and experimentation in fashion. By incorporating some of these key trends into your wardrobe today, you can add a touch of retro flair to your style.
