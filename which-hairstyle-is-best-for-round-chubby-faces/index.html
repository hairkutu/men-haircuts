Which Hairstyle Is Best For Round Chubby Faces

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round chubby face? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round chubby faces, as well as provide tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Chubby Faces
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions for Hairstyles
  4. Tips for Styling Round Chubby Faces
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Advice for Round Chubby Faces

Description

Round chubby faces can be challenging to style, but there are many hairstyles that can flatter your face shape. The key is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds volume to the crown of your head. Some of the best hairstyles for round chubby faces include layered cuts, angled bobs, and pixie cuts.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Chubby Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for a round chubby face, consider the following factors:

  • Face shape: Round faces have soft curves and equal length and width.
  • Hair texture: Fine hair is best suited for layered cuts, while thick hair can handle more volume and length.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your daily routine and how much time you can devote to styling your hair.

Once you have considered these factors, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Hairstyles

Here are step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for round chubby faces:

Layered Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  3. Part your hair to one side.
  4. Cut layers into your hair, starting at your cheekbones and working your way down. Make sure the layers are angled towards your face.
  5. Style your hair using a curling iron to create soft waves.

Angled Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Bow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  3. Part your hair to one side.
  4. Cut your hair into an angled bob, with the front of your hair longer than the back.
  5. Style your hair using a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair.

Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Cut your hair into a pixie cut, leaving the sides and back short and the top longer.
  4. Style your hair using a texturizing product to create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Styling Round Chubby Faces

Here are some tips for styling round chubby faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to draw attention to your eyes.
  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and create a messy, tousled look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round chubby faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round chubby face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and blunt cuts.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round chubby face. Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Should I add layers to my hair if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, adding layers to your hair can help elongate your face and add volume to the crown of your head.

Hair Care Advice for Round Chubby Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round chubby faces:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good texturizing product to add volume and create a messy, tousled look.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that can flatter round chubby faces. The key is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds volume to the crown of your head. Follow our tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to achieve your desired look!
