Best Wedding Hairstyles For Square Faces

Written by Bardi Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a bride with a square face and looking for the perfect wedding hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with the best hairstyles for square faces that will make you look stunning on your wedding day.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and an equally wide forehead. The goal of a hairstyle for square faces is to soften these features and balance the face shape. For a wedding hairstyle, you want something that is elegant, timeless, and complements your overall bridal look. We have compiled a list of hairstyles that will flatter your square face and make you feel beautiful on your big day.

How to

The first step in choosing a wedding hairstyle for a square face is to determine your face shape. Once you have identified your face shape, consider your hair texture, length, and overall bridal look. From there, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your wedding dress.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape: Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face, and compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

2. Consider your hair texture and length: Choose a hairstyle that works well with your hair texture and length. If you have long hair, consider an updo or half-up, half-down style. If you have short hair, consider a pixie cut or bob.

3. Think about your overall bridal look: Your wedding hairstyle should complement your wedding dress and overall bridal look. If you have a classic wedding dress, consider a timeless updo. If you have a bohemian wedding dress, consider loose waves or braids.

Tips

1. Soften your features: Soften your strong jawline with loose waves or a side-swept fringe.

2. Accessorize: Consider adding a hair accessory such as a sparkly hairpin or floral crown to add interest to your hairstyle.

3. Consult with a hairstylist: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best wedding hairstyle for your square face. They can provide you with recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and overall bridal look.

FAQ

Q: What are the best wedding hairstyles for square faces?

A: The best wedding hairstyles for square faces include soft waves, loose curls, updos, and braids.

Q: How can I soften my strong jawline with a wedding hairstyle?

A: Soften your strong jawline with loose waves, a side-swept fringe, or a hairstyle that has volume at the crown.

Hair Care

To ensure your wedding hairstyle looks its best, it is important to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day. This includes regular trims to prevent split ends, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, consider using a hair mask to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, there are many beautiful and flattering wedding hairstyles for square faces. By following the tips and recommendations in this article, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and overall bridal look. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day for the best results.
