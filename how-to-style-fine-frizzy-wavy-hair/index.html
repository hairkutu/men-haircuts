How To Style Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline May 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your hair always looking frizzy and unmanageable? Styling fine frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek and polished look. In this article, we will guide you through how to style fine frizzy wavy hair step by step, along with tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

  • Hair Care for Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

How to Style Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

The key to styling fine frizzy wavy hair is finding the right products that work for your hair type. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo designed for wavy or curly hair. Follow up with a hydrating conditioner to help smooth out any frizz. Rinse your hair with cool water to help seal the cuticles and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help detangle and add moisture. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Avoid applying the product to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair.

Step 4: Diffuse Dry

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and reduce frizz. Use a low-heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Oil

Finish off your hairstyle by applying a small amount of hair oil to your ends. This will help add shine and control any flyaways.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step by step guide on how to style fine frizzy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo designed for wavy or curly hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, being careful not to tangle your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a hydrating conditioner to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse with cool water.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help add moisture and detangle your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Avoid applying the product to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair.

Step 5: Diffuse Dry

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to a low-heat setting. Tilt your head to the side and place your hair into the diffuser. Use a scrunching motion to dry your hair, working your way around your head. Repeat until your hair is dry.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Oil

Finish off your hairstyle by applying a small amount of hair oil to your ends. Use your fingers to distribute the oil evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help add shine and control any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling fine frizzy wavy hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and prevent hair breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons, as this can cause damage and frizz.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash your hair more often to prevent it from looking greasy. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less frequently.

What is the best hairstyle for fine frizzy wavy hair?

A layered haircut can help add volume and movement to fine frizzy wavy hair. Talk to your hairstylist about the best cut for your hair type.

Hair Care for Fine Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for fine frizzy wavy hair:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your fine frizzy wavy hair with ease. Remember to use the right products for your hair type and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
