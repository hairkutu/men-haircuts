Can You Change Hairstyle In Animal Crossing

Written by Oliver Apr 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Animal Crossing is a popular life simulation game that has captured the hearts of many players. One of the exciting features of the game is the ability to customize your character's appearance, including their hairstyle. However, many players wonder if they can change their hairstyle once they have chosen it. In this blog post, we will answer the question, "Can you change hairstyle in Animal Crossing?" and provide you with all the information you need to know about this topic.

Description

Animal Crossing is a game where you can create and customize your own character, including their hairstyle. There are various hairstyles available in the game, ranging from short to long, curly to straight, and everything in between. Once you have chosen your hairstyle, you might wonder if you can change it later on.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Yes, you can change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing. To do so, you will need to visit Harriet's salon. Harriet is a poodle who runs the salon in the game. She can change your hairstyle, hair color, and eye color. To find Harriet's salon, you will need to unlock it first. To unlock the salon, you will need to spend at least 10,000 Bells at the Able Sisters' shop. Once you have spent enough money, Harriet's salon will be available to you.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  1. Visit the Able Sisters' shop and spend at least 10,000 Bells.
  2. Once you have spent enough money, Harriet's salon will be unlocked.
  3. Visit Harriet's salon and sit in the chair.
  4. Choose the hairstyle you want and confirm your selection.
  5. Pay the fee to change your hairstyle.

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Changing your hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience in Animal Crossing. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Before changing your hairstyle, make sure you have enough Bells to pay for the fee.
  • Try out different hairstyles before settling on one. You can always change it again later.
  • Consider changing your hair color and eye color as well to create a completely new look.
  • Take a screenshot of your new hairstyle to share with your friends on social media.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

Can you change your hairstyle for free?

No, you will need to pay a fee to change your hairstyle at Harriet's salon.

Can you change your hairstyle as many times as you want?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want, but you will need to pay the fee each time.

Can you change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons by visiting Harriet's salon.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Just like in real life, taking care of your hair in Animal Crossing is important. You can't have a good hairstyle if your hair is not healthy. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair in the game:

  • Make sure to brush your hair every day to prevent tangles.
  • Wear a hat or umbrella when it's raining to protect your hair.
  • Visit Harriet's salon regularly to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Overall, changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is easy and fun. Whether you want to try out a new look or just switch things up, Harriet's salon is the place to go. Just remember to have enough Bells to pay for the fee and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
