Does Hair Shed When It Grows

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have ever wondered why you find hair strands on your brush or comb, you may have asked yourself, "does hair shed when it grows?" The answer is yes, hair does shed when it grows, but it's a natural process that occurs in all individuals. In this post, we will discuss the science behind hair shedding, how to minimize hair loss, and tips on how to take care of your hair.

The Science Behind Hair Shedding

Hair shedding is a natural process that occurs when the hair follicles go through the growth cycle. Each hair strand has a lifespan of 2-7 years, and the growth cycle consists of three phases: the anagen phase, the catagen phase, and the telogen phase.

The anagen phase is the active growth phase, and it lasts for 2-7 years, depending on the individual. During this phase, the hair grows about 1cm per month. Once the anagen phase is complete, the hair follicle enters the catagen phase, which is the transitional phase. This phase lasts for 2-3 weeks, and the hair follicle shrinks, disconnecting the hair strand from the blood supply.

The final phase is the telogen phase, which is the resting phase. During this phase, the hair follicle remains inactive for about 3 months, and the hair strand falls out, making room for new hair growth. The average person sheds about 50-100 hairs per day, and this is considered normal.

How to Minimize Hair Loss

While hair shedding is a natural process, there are ways to minimize hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid hair breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as braids or ponytails.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which are essential for hair growth.
  • Manage stress, as stress can contribute to hair loss.

Tips on How to Take Care of Your Hair

To maintain healthy hair, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

1. Is hair shedding normal?

Yes, hair shedding is normal, and the average person sheds about 50-100 hairs per day.

2. How can I tell if I'm shedding more hair than usual?

You may notice more hair on your brush or comb, or you may see more hair strands in the shower drain. If you're concerned, speak to your doctor or a dermatologist.

3. Can hair shedding be a sign of a health problem?

In some cases, hair shedding can be a sign of an underlying health problem, such as a thyroid disorder or iron deficiency. If you're concerned, speak to your doctor or a dermatologist.

Hair Care Products That Help Minimize Hair Loss

There are many hair care products that claim to help minimize hair loss. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Castor oil: Castor oil is rich in ricinoleic acid, which is believed to help promote hair growth.
  • Biotin supplements: Biotin is an essential vitamin for hair growth, and taking biotin supplements may help promote hair growth.
  • Scalp massage: Massaging your scalp can help improve blood flow to your hair follicles, which may promote hair growth.
  • Essential oils: Essential oils, such as lavender or peppermint oil, may help promote hair growth.
  • DHT-blocking shampoos: DHT is a hormone that can contribute to hair loss, and using a DHT-blocking shampoo may help minimize hair loss.

In conclusion, hair shedding is a natural process that occurs when the hair follicles go through the growth cycle. While shedding is normal, there are ways to minimize hair loss, such as using a wide-tooth comb, avoiding tight hairstyles, and eating a healthy diet. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can promote healthy hair growth and minimize hair loss.
