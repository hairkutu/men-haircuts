Best Matte Pomade For Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair, finding the perfect hair styling product can be a challenge. You want something that will hold your hair in place without making it look greasy or weighed down. That's where matte pomade comes in. In this article, we will discuss the best matte pomade for long hair and provide tips on how to use it.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Use Matte Pomade on Long Hair

Description

Matte pomade is a hair styling product that provides a natural-looking, matte finish. It is perfect for guys with longer hair who want a messy, textured look without the shine. Unlike other hair products, matte pomade won't make your hair look greasy or oily. It also won't weigh your hair down, allowing it to move naturally.

The best matte pomade for long hair should provide a strong hold that lasts all day. It should also be easy to wash out and not leave any residue behind. The following are some of the best matte pomades for long hair on the market:

1. Baxter of California Clay Pomade

Baxter of California Clay Pomade is a popular choice among men with longer hair. It provides a strong hold and matte finish, making it perfect for achieving a messy, textured look. It is also easy to apply and wash out, making it a convenient choice for daily use.

2. Hanz de Fuko Claymation

Hanz de Fuko Claymation is another great option for guys with long hair. It provides a strong hold and matte finish, but also has a bit of shine to it. This makes it perfect for those who want a natural-looking finish without the dullness of a completely matte product.

3. American Crew Fiber

American Crew Fiber is a classic choice for men's hair styling. It provides a strong hold and matte finish, but is also pliable and easy to rework throughout the day. This makes it perfect for guys with longer hair who want a product that can be reshaped as needed.

How to Use Matte Pomade on Long Hair

Using matte pomade on long hair is easy, but it does require some technique to get the best results. The following steps will guide you through the process:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Matte pomade works best on hair that is free of any other products or oils.
  2. Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips. A little goes a long way, so start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  3. Rub the pomade between your palms to warm it up and make it easier to apply.
  4. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Style your hair as desired. Matte pomade allows for a messy, textured look, so don't worry about making it perfect.
  6. Use a comb or brush to help distribute the product and create the desired style.

Tips for Using Matte Pomade on Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your matte pomade:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed. Overusing the product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Use a comb or brush to help distribute the product and create the desired style.
  • Experiment with different amounts of product to find the perfect balance between hold and texture.
  • Wash your hair thoroughly to remove any pomade residue.

FAQ

Q. Can matte pomade be used on all hair types?

A. Yes, matte pomade can be used on all hair types, but it works best on hair that is medium to thick in texture.

Q. How often should I use matte pomade?

A. Matte pomade can be used daily, but it's best to give your hair a break every few days to prevent buildup.

Q. Can I restyle my hair after applying matte pomade?

A. Yes, matte pomade is pliable and can be reshaped throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you keep your long hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends.

By following these tips and using the best matte pomade for long hair, you can achieve a stylish, textured look that lasts all day. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect style for your hair type and personal preferences.
