How To Know If Side Bangs Suit You

Written by Christine Mar 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Side bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some dimension to your look. However, not everyone can pull off this style. If you're considering getting side bangs, it's important to know if they'll suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll go over how to know if side bangs suit you and provide some tips for maintaining and styling them.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Assessing Your Lifestyle
  • Highlighting Your Face Features
  • Tips for Hair Care and Styling
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if side bangs suit you is to figure out your face shape. Common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has certain features that can be accentuated or minimized with different hairstyles.

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape tends to look good with most hairstyles, including side bangs. Round face shapes can also benefit from side bangs, as they can help create the illusion of a longer, more balanced face shape. Square face shapes may want to avoid side bangs, as they can emphasize the angular features of the face. Heart and diamond face shapes can also benefit from side bangs, but it's important to make sure they're not too heavy or short.

Considering Your Hair Type

In addition to your face shape, it's important to consider your hair type when deciding if side bangs are right for you. If you have thin or fine hair, side bangs can help add volume and texture to your hair. However, if you have thick or curly hair, side bangs may be more difficult to manage and style. It's also important to consider the length of your hair - if your hair is very short, side bangs may not be possible.

Assessing Your Lifestyle

Another factor to consider when deciding if side bangs suit you is your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule or don't have a lot of time to style your hair, side bangs may not be the best choice for you. Side bangs require regular trimming and styling to maintain their shape and can be high maintenance. Additionally, if you engage in activities that require you to wear a helmet or headband, side bangs may not be practical.

Highlighting Your Face Features

Side bangs can be a great way to highlight certain features of your face, such as your eyes or cheekbones. However, it's important to make sure the length and thickness of your side bangs complement your face shape and features. If you have small eyes or a short forehead, shorter side bangs may be more flattering. If you have a prominent nose or high forehead, longer side bangs can help balance out your features.

Tips for Hair Care and Styling

If you've decided that side bangs are right for you, it's important to take proper care of them to keep them looking their best. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can help maintain the shape of your side bangs and prevent them from becoming too long or heavy. When styling your side bangs, use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and shape. Avoid using too much heat or product, as this can damage your hair and make your side bangs look greasy or flat.

FAQs

Can I get side bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still get side bangs if you have curly hair! However, it may require more maintenance and styling to keep them looking their best. Consider using a curl-defining product and diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz.

How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your side bangs and prevent them from becoming too long or heavy.

What if I don't like my side bangs?

If you're not happy with your side bangs, don't worry - they'll grow out eventually! In the meantime, you can try pinning them back or styling them in a different way. It's important to communicate with your stylist if you're unhappy with your side bangs, as they may be able to adjust the length or thickness to better suit your preferences.

Can I wear my side bangs with a ponytail?

Yes, you can definitely wear your side bangs with a ponytail! Experiment with different styles, such as a high ponytail or low side ponytail, to find a look that works for you.

What if I have a small forehead?

If you have a small forehead, shorter side bangs can help create the illusion of a longer forehead. Avoid side bangs that are too thick or heavy, as this can make your forehead appear even smaller.

Overall, side bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some dimension to your look. By considering your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and face features, you can determine if side bangs are right for you. Remember to take proper care of your side bangs with regular trims and styling, and don't be afraid to communicate with your stylist if you're unhappy with the results.
