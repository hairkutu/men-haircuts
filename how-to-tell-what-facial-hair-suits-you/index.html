How To Tell What Facial Hair Suits You

Do you want to grow a beard, mustache, or goatee, but don't know which style suits you best? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to tell what facial hair suits you. We'll also provide you with some frequently asked questions about facial hair and hair care tips to keep your facial hair looking great.

How to Determine Your Facial Hair Style

Choosing the right facial hair style can be a daunting task. There are so many options to choose from, and not all styles suit every face shape or hair type. The first step in determining your facial hair style is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes require different styles to look their best. Here are some tips to help you determine your face shape and the best style for you.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you should go for a style that will elongate your face. A goatee or a chin strap beard will help you achieve this look.

Oval Face

With an oval face, you have the most flexibility in terms of facial hair styles. You can pretty much pull off any style, from a full beard to a mustache.

Square Face

A square face requires a style that will soften the jawline. A short beard or a mustache can help achieve this look.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention away from your forehead. A goatee or a soul patch can help achieve this look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Facial Hair Style

Now that you know your face shape and the styles that suit you best, it's time to find your perfect facial hair style. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the right style:

  1. Start by growing out your facial hair for at least a week. This will give you an idea of what hair you have to work with.
  2. Consider the styles that suit your face shape, as discussed above.
  3. Look at pictures of men with similar face shapes and hair types to yours who are rocking the styles you're interested in.
  4. Ask for feedback from friends or a trusted barber. They can give you an honest opinion on what looks best.
  5. Experiment with different styles until you find the right one. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

Once you've found your perfect facial hair style, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your facial hair:

  • Invest in a good quality beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and healthy.
  • Trim your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your facial hair regularly with a beard shampoo to keep it clean and free from debris.
  • Use a comb or brush to keep your facial hair looking neat and untangled.

Frequently Asked Questions about Facial Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about facial hair:

Should I shave my facial hair before trying a new style?

It's best to start with a clean slate when trying a new style. Shave your facial hair before trying a new style to get the best results.

What if I can't grow a full beard?

Don't worry if you can't grow a full beard. There are plenty of other styles to choose from, such as a mustache or a goatee.

How often should I trim my facial hair?

You should trim your facial hair every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Your Facial Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your facial hair looking great:

  • Avoid using regular shampoo on your facial hair. It can dry out the hair and make it brittle.
  • Invest in a good quality beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and healthy.
  • Trim your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a comb or brush to keep your facial hair looking neat and untangled.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect facial hair style for you and keep it looking great. Remember to take care of your facial hair with regular maintenance and hair care products to keep it soft and healthy.
