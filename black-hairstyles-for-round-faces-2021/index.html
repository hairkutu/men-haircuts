Black Hairstyles For Round Faces 2021 A Complete Guide

If you have a round face, finding a hairstyle that flatters your features can be challenging. But don't worry, there are plenty of black hairstyles for round faces 2021 that will make you look stunning. In this post, we'll show you how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for Your Round Face

When it comes to hairstyles for round faces, the goal is to create the illusion of length and definition. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even more round.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height at the crown, as this can elongate your face.
  • Layered styles can also help to create the illusion of length and definition.
  • Avoid bangs that are straight across, as these can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces, as they can help to create the illusion of length and definition. Here are some of our favorite short hairstyles for round faces:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that is perfect for round faces. This style adds height at the crown, which can help to elongate your face. It's also a great low-maintenance option.

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. For round faces, opt for a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back. This will help to create the illusion of length.

Medium Hairstyles for Round Faces

Medium hairstyles are a great option for round faces, as they can help to create the illusion of length and definition while still being versatile. Here are some of our favorite medium hairstyles for round faces:

Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) is a great option for round faces. This style adds height at the crown, which can help to elongate your face. The layers also add definition and movement.

Side-Swept Waves

Side-swept waves are a great option for medium-length hair. This style adds height at the crown and the side-swept waves help to create the illusion of length.

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

Long hairstyles can be tricky for round faces, as they can sometimes make your face appear even rounder. But don't worry, there are still plenty of options. Here are some of our favorite long hairstyles for round faces:

Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is a great option for long hair. This style adds definition and movement, which can help to elongate your face. Opt for layers that start at your chin or below.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option for long hair. This style adds height at the crown, which can help to elongate your face. Plus, it's a great way to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Black Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, as this can prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a round face. Just make sure to opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even more round. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height at the crown.

Hair Care Tips for Black Hair

Black hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know all about black hairstyles for round faces 2021, it's time to choose the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to keep in mind the tips we've shared and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
