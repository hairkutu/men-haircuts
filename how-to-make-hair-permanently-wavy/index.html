How To Make Hair Permanently Wavy A Guide To Perfect Beachy Waves

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of constantly curling your hair to achieve a beachy wave, then you might be interested in learning how to make your hair permanently wavy. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve beautiful, natural-looking waves that will last for months.

Description

Permanently wavy hair is achieved through a chemical treatment called a perm. This process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds in your hair shaft, allowing it to be reshaped into a wave pattern. The perm solution is then neutralized, and your hair is rinsed and styled into the desired wave pattern.

While perms were popular in the '80s and '90s, they fell out of fashion in the 2000s. However, they've recently made a comeback as people seek out low-maintenance styling options.

How to Make Hair Permanently Wavy

If you're interested in getting a perm, the first step is to find a reputable salon that specializes in this type of treatment. You'll want to look for a stylist who has experience working with your hair type and who can guide you through the process.

Before getting a perm, it's important to know that the process can be damaging to your hair. The chemicals used in the treatment can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage. It's also important to note that once you get a perm, you won't be able to straighten your hair without damaging the wave pattern.

Here's what you can expect during the perm process:

Step 1: Consultation

Your stylist will start by examining your hair and discussing your desired wave pattern. They'll also ask you about your hair history and any previous chemical treatments you've had.

Step 2: Prep

Your stylist will apply a protective cream to your scalp and ears to prevent the perm solution from irritating your skin. They'll also section your hair and wrap it around perm rods.

Step 3: Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply a perm solution to your hair, making sure to saturate each section evenly. The solution will break down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into a wave pattern.

Step 4: Neutralizer

After the perm solution has been applied, your stylist will rinse your hair and apply a neutralizing solution. This solution will stop the chemical process and lock in your wave pattern.

Step 5: Styling

Your stylist will remove the perm rods and style your hair into the desired wave pattern. They may use a diffuser to enhance your hair's natural texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Perm

After you've gotten your perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and disrupt the wave pattern.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can weaken your hair and cause it to break.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushing can disrupt the wave pattern.

FAQ

Is it possible to make hair permanently wavy without a perm?

No, a perm is the only way to achieve permanent waves in your hair.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical treatment.

Hair Care

If you're interested in getting a perm, it's important to take good care of your hair before and after the treatment. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.
