Short Hairstyles For Round Faces With Double Chin

Are you tired of trying to hide your double chin with your hair? If you have a round face with a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and minimize the appearance of your double chin. In this article, weâll show you some of the best short hairstyles for round faces with double chin, and give you tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Round faces have soft, curved features and a wider, circular shape. Double chins are caused by excess fat around the neck, which can make the area below the chin appear fuller. When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin, the goal is to create the illusion of a slimmer face and neck. This can be achieved by choosing a hairstyle that adds height or volume to the crown of the head, and keeping the sides and back of the hair tapered or layered to create a more angular shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin, consider the following factors:

  • Length: Short hairstyles that fall just below the chin or above the ears are the most flattering for round faces with double chins. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Shape: Look for hairstyles that add height or volume to the crown of the head, and keep the sides and back of the hair tapered or layered to create a more angular shape.
  • Texture: Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Consider getting a perm or adding layers to your hair to add volume and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Follow these steps to create a simple, flattering hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Get regular trims: Short hair grows quickly, so itâs important to get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and styling needs.
  • Protect your hair: Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools, and avoid over-styling your hair with heat.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, bangs can be a flattering addition to a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin. Consider side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that are longer on the sides.

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face with a double chin?

A: The best haircut for a round face with a double chin is a short, layered bob or pixie cut. These styles add height to the crown of the head and create a more angular shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Short hair can get greasy quickly, so itâs important to wash it every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling: Over-styling your hair with heat can damage it and make it look dull. Use heat tools sparingly, and always use a heat protectant spray.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can flatter your round face and minimize the appearance of your double chin. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds height or volume to the crown of your head, and keep the sides and back of your hair tapered or layered to create a more angular shape. With regular maintenance and care, your short hair will look healthy and vibrant.
