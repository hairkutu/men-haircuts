How To Make Men s Hair Pomade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of buying hair pomade that doesn't work for your hair type? Or are you looking for a more natural alternative to store-bought pomades? Well, look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to make men's hair pomade using simple ingredients that you can find in your kitchen or at your local health food store.

Table of Contents

  • Ingredients
  • How to Make Men's Hair Pomade
  • Tips for Using Hair Pomade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Ingredients

Before we get started, here are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 1/2 cup beeswax
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 2-3 drops of essential oil (optional)

You can find beeswax and coconut oil at your local health food store or online. Essential oils are optional, but they can add a nice scent to your hair pomade.

How to Make Men's Hair Pomade

Follow these simple steps to make your own hair pomade:

  1. Melt the beeswax and coconut oil in a double boiler over low heat. If you don't have a double boiler, you can use a heatproof bowl over a pot of boiling water.
  2. Stir the mixture until everything is melted and well combined.
  3. If using essential oils, add them to the mixture and stir well.
  4. Pour the mixture into a glass jar or tin and let it cool for a few hours until it solidifies.

That's it! Your homemade hair pomade is ready to use.

Tips for Using Hair Pomade

Here are some tips for using your homemade hair pomade:

  • Start with a small amount and add more as needed. You can always add more pomade, but it's harder to remove excess pomade.
  • Apply the pomade to dry or slightly damp hair for best results.
  • Distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair with your fingers or a comb.
  • Style your hair as desired.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair pomade:

What is hair pomade?

Hair pomade is a styling product that is used to add shine and hold to hair. It's typically made from wax and oil, and it can be used to create a variety of hairstyles.

Is hair pomade bad for your hair?

Hair pomade is not necessarily bad for your hair, but using too much can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down. It's important to use the right amount of pomade for your hair type and to wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup.

Can I use hair pomade on wet hair?

It's best to use hair pomade on dry or slightly damp hair for best results. Applying pomade to wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips and using your homemade hair pomade, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
