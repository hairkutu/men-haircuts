How To Make Bikini Bottoms Fit Better

Written by William May 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly adjusting your bikini bottoms when you're at the beach or pool? It can be frustrating when your bikini bottoms won't stay in place or fit properly. But don't worry, there are a few simple tricks you can use to make your bikini bottoms fit better and stay in place throughout the day. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect fit for your bikini bottoms.

How to

1. Choose the Right Size

One of the most important things you can do to make sure your bikini bottoms fit properly is to choose the right size. Make sure you measure yourself accurately and consult the sizing chart provided by the brand you're buying from. Keep in mind that sizes can vary between brands, so don't assume that your usual size will fit perfectly. If you're in between sizes, it's usually best to go up a size rather than down.

2. Adjust the Straps

If your bikini bottoms have adjustable straps, take advantage of this feature to achieve a better fit. You can tighten or loosen the straps to fit your body shape and prevent the bottoms from slipping down. Make sure the straps aren't too tight, as this can cause discomfort and digging into your skin.

3. Try Different Styles

Not all bikini bottoms are created equal, and some styles might fit better than others depending on your body shape. If you're having trouble finding the perfect fit, try different styles such as high-waisted, Brazilian, or cheeky bottoms. You might be surprised to find that a style you didn't think would suit you actually fits perfectly.

4. Use Bikini Tape

If you're still struggling to keep your bikini bottoms in place, consider using bikini tape. This is a special tape that's designed to stick to your skin and keep your bikini in place. It's perfect for preventing slip-ups and keeping your bikini bottoms where they should be.

Step by Step

Step 1: Measure Yourself

Before buying a new pair of bikini bottoms, make sure you measure yourself accurately to determine your size. Use a tape measure to measure your hips and consult the sizing chart provided by the brand you're buying from.

Step 2: Try on Different Styles

Not all bikini bottoms are created equal, and some styles might fit better than others depending on your body shape. Try on different styles such as high-waisted, Brazilian, or cheeky bottoms to see which one suits you best.

Step 3: Adjust the Straps

If your bikini bottoms have adjustable straps, take advantage of this feature to achieve a better fit. You can tighten or loosen the straps to fit your body shape and prevent the bottoms from slipping down.

Step 4: Use Bikini Tape

If you're still struggling to keep your bikini bottoms in place, consider using bikini tape. This is a special tape that's designed to stick to your skin and keep your bikini in place.

Tips

  • Choose the right size
  • Adjust the straps
  • Try different styles
  • Use bikini tape
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match tops and bottoms from different brands
  • Make sure you're comfortable in your bikini
  • Don't forget to apply sunscreen regularly

FAQ

What if I'm in between sizes?

If you're in between sizes, it's usually best to go up a size rather than down. A bikini that's too tight can cause discomfort and digging into your skin.

What if I can't find the right size or style?

If you can't find the right size or style, consider getting your bikini bottoms custom-made. This way, you can ensure a perfect fit that's tailored to your body shape.

Can I wear bikini tape for an extended period?

You can wear bikini tape for an extended period, but make sure you remove it gently to avoid irritation or discomfort.

Hair Care

Don't forget to take care of your hair while you're at the beach or pool. Saltwater and chlorine can be damaging to your hair, so make sure you rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from the sun and keep it hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make sure your bikini bottoms fit better and stay in place throughout the day. Remember to choose the right size, adjust the straps, try different styles, and use bikini tape if necessary. With the perfect fit, you can feel confident and comfortable in your bikini all day long.
