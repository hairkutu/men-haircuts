How To Do Undercut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, the undercut is a great option. It's a versatile cut that can be customized to suit your personal style, and it's relatively easy to do yourself. In this step-by-step guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to do undercut yourself. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the best techniques for achieving the perfect cut. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Prepping Your Hair
  3. Sectioning Your Hair
  4. Clipping Your Hair
  5. Cutting Your Hair
  6. Styling Your Hair

  8. FAQ
  9. Hair Care

Tools You'll Need

To do an undercut yourself, you'll need a few basic tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or ties
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle filled with water

You may also want to invest in a cape or towel to protect your clothing from hair clippings.

Prepping Your Hair

Before you begin cutting, you'll need to prep your hair. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. It's important that your hair is clean and free of any styling products or oils. Once your hair is dry, comb it straight down so it's easy to section.

Sectioning Your Hair

Next, you'll need to section your hair. Use your comb to create a horizontal part from ear to ear, separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Clipping Your Hair

Clip the sides and back of your hair to your desired length, using your clippers. For a classic undercut, you'll want to use a shorter guard on the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length. Use your comb to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Cutting Your Hair

Using your scissors, trim the top section of your hair to your desired length. You can choose to keep the top section longer or shorter, depending on your personal style. If you're unsure how much to cut off, start with a little and work your way up. You can always trim more later.

Styling Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create your desired look. You can choose to style your hair up, to the side, or slicked back. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Success

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down gradually.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I do an undercut myself?

Yes, you can do an undercut yourself. With the right tools and techniques, it's a relatively easy cut to achieve.

What length should I cut my hair?

The length of your hair will depend on your personal style. For a classic undercut, use a shorter guard on the sides and back, and trim the top to your desired length.

What styling products should I use?

There are many different styling products you can use, depending on your hair type and desired look. Pomade, wax, and gel are all popular choices.

Hair Care

After you've done your undercut, it's important to take proper care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. And don't forget to get regular trims to maintain your style.

Now that you know how to do undercut yourself, you can experiment with different styles and create a look that's uniquely you. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at doing undercuts in no time!
