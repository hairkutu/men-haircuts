Is Wearing Box Braids Haram

Written by Bardi May 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Box braids have been a popular hairstyle among women for decades. It is a protective hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped boxes and braiding it from the roots to the ends. However, some people have raised concerns about whether box braids are haram or not. In this article, we will explore this topic in detail and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Description

Box braids are a form of protective hairstyle that originated in Africa. It involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped boxes and braiding it from the roots to the ends. This hairstyle is popular because it is low maintenance and can last for weeks if properly cared for. Box braids can be styled in various ways and can be worn by women of all ages and hair textures.

How to

If you are interested in getting box braids, you can follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Section your hair into small, square-shaped boxes.
  3. Braid your hair from the roots to the ends.
  4. Repeat the process until all the sections are braided.
  5. Trim the ends of the braids to your desired length.

It is recommended that you get your box braids done by a professional hairstylist to ensure that they are done correctly and last longer.

Step by Step

If you are interested in getting box braids, you can follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Section your hair into small, square-shaped boxes using a rat-tail comb.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to each section to keep it moisturized.
  5. Braid each section from the roots to the ends using three strands of hair.
  6. Repeat the process until all the sections are braided.
  7. Dip the ends of the braids in hot water to seal them.
  8. Trim the ends of the braids to your desired length.

It is important to note that box braids can take several hours to complete depending on the length and thickness of your hair. It is also important to take breaks and stretch your arms and fingers to avoid fatigue and strain.

Tips

If you are considering getting box braids, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and moisturized before getting box braids.
  • Choose a professional hairstylist with experience in box braids.
  • Avoid pulling the braids too tight to avoid hair breakage and scalp damage.
  • Wear a satin bonnet or scarf at night to keep the braids from frizzing and tangling.
  • Apply a moisturizing hair oil or leave-in conditioner to your scalp and braids to keep them moisturized.

FAQ

Is wearing box braids haram?

There is no clear answer to this question as it depends on the interpretation of Islamic laws and traditions. Some Muslims believe that it is haram to imitate the hairstyles of non-Muslims, while others believe that it is permissible as long as it does not lead to harm or sin. Ultimately, it is up to the individual to make their own decision based on their beliefs and values.

Can I wear box braids during Ramadan?

Yes, you can wear box braids during Ramadan as long as you follow the rules and customs of the holy month. It is important to maintain good hygiene and avoid excessive hair styling during Ramadan.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last for several weeks to months depending on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as how well you care for them. It is recommended that you get your box braids redone every 6-8 weeks to prevent hair breakage and scalp damage.

Hair Care

Box braids require low maintenance but still need proper care to prevent hair breakage and scalp damage. Here are some hair care tips for box braids:

  • Wash your braids every 2-3 weeks to keep them clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your braids and cause buildup.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your scalp and braids to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and tight hairstyles that can cause hair breakage and scalp damage.
  • Take breaks and stretch your arms and fingers to avoid fatigue and strain.

In conclusion, whether or not wearing box braids is haram depends on personal interpretation and beliefs. However, box braids are a popular and versatile hairstyle that can be worn by women of all ages and hair textures. If you are considering getting box braids, make sure to follow the proper steps and care for them to ensure they last longer and maintain their quality.
