How To Look Beautiful With Square Face

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face, you may sometimes feel like it's difficult to find hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your features. But with the right tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your skin. In this article, we'll show you how to look beautiful with square face by providing step-by-step guides, expert tips, and answers to frequently asked questions. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Beautiful with Square Face
  • Understanding Your Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Face
  • Makeup Tips for Square Face
  • Fashion Tips for Square Face
  • FAQ

Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's important to understand your face shape. A square face shape is one that has a broad forehead, a strong jawline, and a square chin. If you're not sure if you have a square face shape, here are some characteristics to look for:

  • Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width
  • Your jawline is sharp and angular
  • Your chin is square and slightly protruding

If these characteristics sound like you, then you likely have a square face shape. Now, let's dive into some tips and tricks for looking beautiful with this face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face

Your hairstyle can make a big difference in the overall appearance of your face. Here are some hair care tips for square face:

  • Go for soft, layered haircuts that add volume and movement to your hair
  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face appear more angular
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to soften your forehead and draw attention to your eyes
  • Experiment with curls and waves, which can add softness to your face shape

Remember, the key is to add volume and movement to your hair to offset the angular features of your face shape.

Makeup Tips for Square Face

Makeup can also be used to enhance your natural beauty and balance out your face shape. Here are some makeup tips for square face:

  • Use a contouring kit to create a more defined jawline and soften your features
  • Avoid harsh, angular lines and instead opt for soft, rounded shapes
  • Focus on drawing attention to your eyes with mascara, eyeliner, and eyeshadow
  • Experiment with bold lip colors to draw attention to your lips and balance out your face shape

Remember, the goal is to create balance and softness with your makeup.

Fashion Tips for Square Face

Your fashion choices can also play a role in how your face shape is perceived. Here are some fashion tips for square face:

  • Choose tops and dresses with soft, rounded necklines to draw attention to your face
  • Avoid boxy, square-shaped clothing that can make your face appear more angular
  • Experiment with statement jewelry to draw attention to your face and add interest to your outfits
  • Consider layering clothing to add volume and movement to your outfits

Remember, the key is to create balance and softness with your clothing choices.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a square face shape?

Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face appear more angular. Also, avoid center parts as they can make your face appear more boxy.

What makeup should I avoid with a square face shape?

Avoid harsh, angular lines and instead opt for soft, rounded shapes. Also, avoid heavy contouring as it can make your face appear more angular.

Can I wear glasses with a square face shape?

Absolutely! The key is to choose glasses that balance out your features. Look for glasses with round or oval frames to soften your face shape.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your skin. Remember, the key is to create balance and softness with your hair, makeup, and fashion choices. Experiment with different looks and have fun!
