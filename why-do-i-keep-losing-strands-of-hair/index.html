Why Do I Keep Losing Strands Of Hair

Written by Daniel May 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

It's not uncommon to find a few strands of hair on your brush or in the shower drain every now and then. However, if you have been losing more hair than usual, you may be wondering why this is happening.

There are several reasons why you may be losing hair. It could be due to genetics, hormonal changes, stress, or even certain medications. However, if you have relaxed hair, there may be some specific reasons why you are losing strands of hair.

How to

If you want to prevent hair loss, the first step is to figure out why it's happening. Here are some things you can do:

  • Visit a dermatologist or trichologist to get a proper diagnosis.
  • Take a look at your diet and make sure you're getting enough nutrients, such as iron and protein.
  • Manage your stress levels through exercise, meditation, or therapy.
  • Be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh chemicals or treatments.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can take to prevent hair loss:

  1. Start by identifying the root cause of your hair loss.
  2. Make changes to your diet and lifestyle to promote hair growth.
  3. Be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh chemicals or treatments.
  4. Consider using hair growth supplements or topical treatments.
  5. Consult with a professional if you're still experiencing hair loss.

Tips

Here are some tips to help prevent hair loss:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote circulation.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss:

Q: Is it normal to lose hair?

A: Yes, it's normal to lose up to 100 strands of hair per day.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss.

Q: Can medications cause hair loss?

A: Yes, certain medications can cause hair loss as a side effect.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair loss:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind.

Overall, losing strands of hair can be a frustrating experience. However, by identifying the root cause of your hair loss and taking steps to prevent it, you can promote healthy hair growth and enjoy a full head of hair once again.
