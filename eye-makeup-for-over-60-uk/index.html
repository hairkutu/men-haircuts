Eye Makeup For Over 60 Uk Tips How To And Faq

Written by William May 25, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our skin and hair change, and so does our makeup routine. If you're over 60 and looking for advice on how to enhance your eyes with makeup, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right eye makeup for over 60 UK, step-by-step tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK
  2. Step-by-Step Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK
  3. Tips for Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK
  4. FAQ about Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK
  5. Hair Care for Over 60 UK

How to Choose Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK

The first step in choosing eye makeup for over 60 UK is to consider your skin tone and eye color. If you have fair skin, you may want to opt for lighter, more natural shades, while those with darker skin can experiment with bolder colors. When it comes to eye color, there are certain shades that will make your eyes pop:

  • Brown eyes: purples, greens, and blues
  • Blue eyes: browns, coppers, and oranges
  • Green eyes: purples, pinks, and browns

Another important factor to consider is the texture of your skin. As we age, our skin loses elasticity and can become dry, so it's important to choose eye makeup that won't settle into fine lines or dry out your skin. Look for creamy, moisturizing formulas that will hydrate your skin, and avoid powdery or matte products.

Step-by-Step Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK

Now that you know how to choose the right eye makeup for over 60 UK, let's walk through a step-by-step tutorial:

  1. Start by applying a moisturizing eye cream to hydrate your skin and prep it for makeup.
  2. Use a primer to create a smooth, even base for your eye shadow to adhere to.
  3. Apply a light-colored eye shadow to your lid and brow bone to create a bright, wide-eyed look.
  4. Use a darker shade in the crease of your eye to create depth and dimension.
  5. Apply eyeliner to your upper lash line, using a pencil or gel formula for a softer look.
  6. Curl your lashes and apply mascara, focusing on the outer corners of your eyes to create a lifted effect.
  7. Finish with a touch of highlighter on the inner corners of your eyes to make them appear brighter.

Tips for Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when applying eye makeup for over 60 UK:

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to avoid a heavy, cakey look.
  • Choose a mascara that is lengthening and volumizing to create a youthful, lifted effect.
  • Avoid using shimmery or glittery eye shadows, which can draw attention to fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Consider using a brow pencil or powder to fill in and shape your eyebrows, which can thin and fade with age.
  • Always remove your makeup before bed to avoid irritation and damage to your skin.

FAQ about Eye Makeup for Over 60 UK

What is the best eye shadow color for over 60 UK?

The best eye shadow color for over 60 UK depends on your skin tone and eye color. Neutral shades like taupe, beige, and brown are universally flattering, while those with fair skin can experiment with lighter shades like pink and lavender, and those with darker skin can try deeper shades like burgundy and navy.

How can I make my eyes look bigger?

To make your eyes look bigger, use a light-colored eye shadow on your lid and brow bone, and a darker shade in the crease of your eye. Apply eyeliner to your upper lash line, and curl your lashes before applying mascara. You can also use a white or nude eyeliner on your lower waterline to create the illusion of bigger eyes.

Hair Care for Over 60 UK

In addition to eye makeup, hair care is also important as we age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair.
  • Consider a shorter haircut or layers to add volume and texture to thinning hair.
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments like perms and relaxers, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can enhance your eyes with makeup and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant at any age. Remember to choose products that work for you and make you feel confident and beautiful.
