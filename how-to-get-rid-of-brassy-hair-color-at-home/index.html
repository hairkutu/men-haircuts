How To Get Rid Of Brassy Hair Color At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you have colored your hair blonde, you may have experienced the dreaded brassy tones that can sometimes appear. Brassy hair color occurs when the warm undertones in your hair become more prominent, giving your hair an orange or yellow tone. While it may seem like a nightmare, the good news is that you can fix it at home. In this article, we will show you how to get rid of brassy hair color at home with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Color at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Color at Home

One of the easiest ways to get rid of brassy hair color at home is to use a toner. A toner is a product that helps to neutralize the unwanted warm tones in your hair. There are several toners available in the market, but you can also make your own toner at home using natural ingredients such as vinegar, baking soda, and lemon juice.

When to Use a Toner

You can use a toner immediately after coloring your hair to prevent brassy tones from showing up. If you have already colored your hair and notice brassy tones, you can use a toner to get rid of them. It is recommended to wait at least 24 hours after coloring your hair before using a toner.

Choosing the Right Toner

When choosing a toner, it is important to select the right shade for your hair color. If you have light blonde hair, you should use a purple toner to neutralize the yellow tones. If you have dark blonde hair, you should use a blue toner to neutralize the orange tones. You can also mix toners to achieve the desired shade.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when using a toner to get rid of brassy hair color at home:
  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.
  3. Mix your toner according to the instructions on the package or make your own toner using natural ingredients.
  4. Apply the toner to your hair using a wide-tooth comb, starting from the roots to the ends.
  5. Leave the toner on for the recommended time, usually between 5 to 30 minutes.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water until the water runs clear.
  7. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 5 to 10 minutes.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  9. Towel-dry your hair and style as usual.

Tips for Preventing Brassy Hair Color

Here are some tips to prevent brassy hair color:
  • Use a purple or blue shampoo once a week to maintain the cool tones in your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water as it can open up the cuticles and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun to prevent the color from fading.

FAQs

How often should I use a toner?

It depends on how often you wash your hair and the color of your hair. If you have light blonde hair, you may need to use a toner once a week. If you have dark blonde hair, you may only need to use a toner once a month.

Can I use a toner on colored hair?

Yes, you can use a toner on colored hair. In fact, it is recommended to use a toner after coloring your hair to prevent brassy tones from showing up.

Can I make my own toner at home?

Yes, you can make your own toner at home using natural ingredients such as vinegar, baking soda, and lemon juice. However, it is important to do a patch test before using any homemade toner to avoid any allergic reactions.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Here are some tips to care for colored hair:
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
In conclusion, getting rid of brassy hair color at home is easy with the use of a toner. Follow the steps in this guide and use the tips and hair care advice to prevent brassy tones from showing up. With a little bit of effort, you can have beautiful, vibrant hair that you can be proud of.
