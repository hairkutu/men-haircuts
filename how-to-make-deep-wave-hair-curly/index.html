How To Make Deep Wave Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your deep wave hair but don't want to go through the hassle of getting a new hairstyle? Good news! You can turn your deep wave hair into curly hair with just a few steps. In this article, we will show you how to make deep wave hair curly with a step-by-step guide, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Deep Wave Hair Curly
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Making Deep Wave Hair Curly
  4. FAQs

How to Make Deep Wave Hair Curly

Making deep wave hair curly is easy and can be done with just a few simple steps. Here's what you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Curling cream or mousse
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer

With these items, you can start creating curls in your deep wave hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Deep wave hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to keep it moisturized.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

After washing, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Make sure to do this gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and use hair clips to secure each section. This will make it easier to work with your hair.

Step 4: Apply Curling Cream or Mousse

Apply curling cream or mousse to each section of your hair, starting from the root to the end. Make sure to distribute the product evenly to ensure that your curls are uniform.

Step 5: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to a low heat setting. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, starting from the roots to the end. Make sure to scrunch your hair with the diffuser to create curls.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Making Deep Wave Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a curling cream or mousse that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Don't apply too much product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Scrunch your hair with the diffuser to create curls.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

FAQs

Can I make deep wave hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can create curls without using heat. One way to do this is by using flexi rods. Apply curling cream or mousse to your hair, wrap each section around the flexi rod, and let it air dry overnight. In the morning, remove the flexi rods, and you'll have beautiful curls.

Can I use a curling iron to make deep wave hair curly?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to make deep wave hair curly. However, using heat on your hair regularly can cause damage, so make sure to apply heat protectant before using a curling iron.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires more care than straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your curls looking healthy:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to avoid dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Now that you know how to make deep wave hair curly, you can switch up your hairstyle without having to make any drastic changes. Follow these steps, and you'll have beautiful curls in no time!
