How To Give Yourself A Straight Across Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts or waiting for hours at the salon? Giving yourself a straight across haircut is easier than you think. In this guide, we will show you how to do it yourself at home.

How to Give Yourself a Straight Across Haircut

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts- two front sections and two back sections. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Cut the Hair in the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Comb the hair straight down and cut it straight across. Make sure the hair is even on both sides.

Step 5: Cut the Hair in the Front

Once you have cut the back of your hair, move on to the front sections. Comb the hair straight down and cut it straight across. Make sure the hair is even on both sides.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Check the length of your hair by combing it straight down. If you notice any uneven sections, trim them until the hair is even.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you have given yourself a straight across haircut, style your hair as desired. You can blow-dry it or let it air dry.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Straight Across Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving yourself a straight across haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Trim a little at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Do not cut your hair when it is wet as it tends to shrink when dry.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.

FAQs About Giving Yourself a Straight Across Haircut

Can I cut my hair without sectioning it?

No, sectioning your hair makes it easier to cut and ensures that the hair is even on both sides.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, do not panic. Trim the hair slowly until it is even. If the mistake is too big, you can always go to a salon to get it fixed.

How often should I give myself a straight across haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care After Giving Yourself a Straight Across Haircut

After giving yourself a straight across haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to give yourself a straight across haircut, you can save money and time by doing it yourself at home. Follow these steps and tips to get a perfect cut every time!
