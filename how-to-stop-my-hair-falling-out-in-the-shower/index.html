How To Stop My Hair Falling Out In The Shower Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of seeing clumps of hair in your shower drain, it's time to take action. Hair loss can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, and hormonal changes. While you can't control everything, there are steps you can take to prevent hair loss in the shower. Here are some tips and tricks to help you stop your hair falling out in the shower.

How to

1. Use a gentle shampoo. Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to breakage and hair loss. Look for a shampoo that is sulfate-free and designed for your hair type.

2. Avoid hot water. Hot water can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Instead, use warm or cool water when you wash your hair.

3. Be gentle when washing your hair. Don't scrub too hard or use too much pressure when shampooing your hair. Instead, use a gentle circular motion with your fingertips.

4. Use a wide-toothed comb. After you shampoo your hair, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and hair loss.

5. Don't tie your hair up too tightly. Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or braids, can cause hair loss over time. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that don't put stress on your hair.

Step by Step

1. Wet your hair with warm or cool water.

2. Apply a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to your hair.

3. Use a gentle circular motion with your fingertips to shampoo your hair.

4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm or cool water.

5. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

6. Rinse your hair with cold water to help seal the cuticle and prevent breakage.

7. Apply a conditioner to the ends of your hair.

8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

9. Gently pat your hair dry with a towel.

10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

- Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron.

- Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause it to fall out.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

- Don't brush your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and hair loss.

- Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're under stress, your body releases hormones that can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause hair loss.

Q: Is it normal to lose hair in the shower?

A: Yes, it is normal to lose some hair in the shower. However, if you're losing excessive amounts of hair, it's important to talk to your doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions.

Q: Can smoking cause hair loss?

A: Yes, smoking can cause hair loss. Smoking constricts blood vessels, which can reduce blood flow to the scalp and cause hair loss.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for preventing hair loss in the shower. In addition to using gentle shampoos and avoiding hot water, make sure to eat a healthy diet, avoid heat styling tools, and use a silk or satin pillowcase. By following these tips and tricks, you can stop your hair falling out in the shower and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
