How To Curl Your Hair Without A Curling Iron

Written by Petter Jun 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Have you ever wanted to add some curls to your hair, but didn't have a curling iron on hand? Don't worry, there are plenty of other ways to achieve those bouncy, beautiful curls you're looking for. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to curl your hair without a curling iron.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Curl Your Hair Without a Curling Iron

There are several ways to curl your hair without using a curling iron, including:

  • Using a straightening iron
  • Using a hair dryer and round brush
  • Using braids or twists
  • Using foam rollers

Each method has its own benefits and drawbacks, so it's important to find the one that works best for you and your hair type.

Using a Straightening Iron

One way to curl your hair without a curling iron is to use a straightening iron. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair and clamp it between the straightening iron plates.
  3. Twist the straightening iron away from your face, then glide it down the length of the hair.
  4. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

This method works best for those with straight or slightly wavy hair.

Using a Hair Dryer and Round Brush

If you have a hair dryer and round brush, you can also use these tools to create curls. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the round brush.
  3. Blow dry the hair while holding the brush in place.
  4. Release the hair from the brush and repeat until all sections of hair are curled.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

This method works best for those with medium to long hair.

Using Braids or Twists

Braids and twists are another great way to create curls without a curling iron. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly.
  3. Secure the twisted hair with a hair tie or bobby pin.
  4. Repeat until all sections of hair are twisted.
  5. Let hair dry completely, then release the twists and separate the curls with your fingers.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

This method works best for those with medium to long hair.

Using Foam Rollers

Finally, foam rollers are another option for those who want to curl their hair without a curling iron. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Wrap a small section of hair around a foam roller.
  3. Secure the roller in place with the attached clip.
  4. Repeat until all sections of hair are rolled.
  5. Let hair dry completely, then remove the rollers and separate the curls with your fingers.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

This method works best for those with medium to long hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the ways to curl your hair without a curling iron, let's go over a step-by-step guide for how to achieve those curls:

  1. Start with clean, dry or damp hair.
  2. Select the method you want to use to curl your hair (straightening iron, hair dryer and round brush, braids or twists, foam rollers).
  3. Follow the steps for your chosen method.
  4. Once all sections of hair are curled, let your hair cool completely before separating the curls with your fingers.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Your Hair Without a Curling Iron

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curls without a curling iron:

  • Start with clean, dry or damp hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair with hot tools.
  • Let your hair cool completely before separating the curls to avoid frizz.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray – too much can weigh down your curls.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Can I curl my hair without any tools?

Yes, you can curl your hair without any tools by using braids or twists. Simply twist small sections of damp hair and secure them with bobby pins or hair ties. Let your hair dry completely, then release the twists and separate the curls with your fingers.

How long will my curls last?

This depends on your hair type and the method you use to curl your hair. Generally, curls created without a curling iron will last for a day or two.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry – this can cause frizz and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often – this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without a curling iron. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you and your hair type.
