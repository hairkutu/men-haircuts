How To Style Your Hair When Growing Out Layers

Are you growing out layers and struggling to style your hair? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you style your hair while it's in the awkward growing out phase.

How to Style Your Hair When Growing Out Layers

When growing out layers, it's important to have patience and a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you style your hair during this phase:

1. Embrace Updos

Updos are your best friend when growing out layers. They can help you hide those awkward layers and give your hair a polished look. Try a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo to switch up your look.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies can help add some personality to your hair while you're growing out layers. They can also help you keep your hair out of your face and prevent breakage.

3. Experiment with Different Partings

Changing up your parting can help you hide those awkward layers and give your hair a new look. Try a middle part, a deep side part, or a zigzag part to switch things up.

4. Opt for a Textured Look

Textured hairstyles like beachy waves and messy curls can help you embrace your natural hair texture and make the growing out phase more bearable. Use a curling wand or a texturizing spray to achieve this look.

5. Trim Your Hair Regularly

While you're growing out layers, it's important to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Ask your stylist to give you a blunt cut or a long bob to help you transition to your desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair when growing out layers:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. This will help you achieve the best results.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.

3. Choose your hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that works for you, whether it's an updo or a textured look.

4. Style your hair

Use styling tools like a curling wand, straightener, or texturizing spray to achieve your desired look.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your hair when growing out layers:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

A deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

2. Invest in a good quality brush

A good quality brush can help prevent breakage and make styling your hair easier.

3. Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles

Trying new hairstyles can help you find the perfect look for your growing out phase.

FAQs

Q: How often should I trim my hair when growing out layers?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks when growing out layers to prevent split ends and breakage.

Q: What are some good hairstyles for growing out layers?

A: Updos, textured hairstyles, and hairstyles with different partings are all good options for growing out layers.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you grow out layers:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfate can strip your hair of its natural oils, so it's important to use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and detangle your hair without causing damage.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible when growing out layers.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your hair with confidence while growing out layers. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you'll be rocking your new look in no time!
