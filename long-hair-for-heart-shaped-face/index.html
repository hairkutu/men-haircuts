Long Hair For Heart Shaped Face A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you’re in luck! Long hair is perfect for your face shape, as it helps balance out your forehead and chin. But how exactly should you style your long hair for a heart-shaped face? In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about long hair for heart-shaped faces, from how to style it to how to care for it.

Description: What is a Heart Shaped Face?

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It’s often considered one of the most feminine face shapes, as it resembles a heart or an inverted triangle. If you’re unsure whether you have a heart-shaped face or not, stand in front of a mirror and draw an imaginary line from your temples to your chin. If the line creates a heart shape, then you have a heart-shaped face!

How to Style Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The key to styling long hair for a heart-shaped face is to choose the right length. You want to aim for hair that falls past your shoulders, as this will help balance out your forehead and chin. Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Step 2: Add Layers

Adding layers to your long hair can help soften your face and create a more balanced look. Ask your hairstylist to add long layers that start around your chin or cheekbones. This will help draw attention away from your forehead and towards your cheekbones.

Step 3: Create Soft Waves

Soft, loose waves are perfect for heart-shaped faces, as they help create a more rounded look. Use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose waves that start around your cheekbones. Avoid curling your hair too close to your forehead, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.

Step 4: Add Volume at the Crown

Adding volume at the crown of your head can help balance out your forehead and create a more symmetrical look. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your roots. You can also tease your hair at the crown to create even more volume.

Tips for Long Hair with Heart Shaped Face

When styling long hair for a heart-shaped face, there are a few tips you should keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt
  • Opt for long layers to soften your face
  • Create soft waves to add volume
  • Add volume at the crown to balance out your forehead
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as they can make your forehead appear even wider

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart-shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your forehead appear even wider. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as this can make your forehead appear even more prominent.

Should I add bangs to my long hair?

Bangs can be tricky for heart-shaped faces, as they can make your forehead appear even wider. However, if you’re determined to add bangs to your long hair, opt for side-swept bangs that start around your cheekbones. This will help draw attention away from your forehead and towards your cheekbones.

How often should I trim my long hair?

You should trim your long hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. Regular trims can also help your hair look more voluminous and bouncy.

Hair Care for Long Hair with Heart Shaped Face

When it comes to caring for your long hair, there are a few tips you should keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair healthy and beautiful, while also flattering your heart-shaped face.

Conclusion

If you have a heart-shaped face, long hair is perfect for you! By choosing the right length, adding layers, creating soft waves, and adding volume at the crown, you can create a balanced and symmetrical look. And by following our tips for hair care, you can keep your long hair healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and embrace your heart-shaped face with long, gorgeous locks!
