What Shampoo Is Best For Hair Fall A Complete Guide

Written by Christine May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of seeing clumps of hair on your brush and pillow every day? Do you feel like you've tried every shampoo out there but nothing seems to work? Don't worry, you're not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. However, with the right shampoo, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we'll be discussing what shampoo is best for hair fall and how you can choose the right one for your hair type.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes Hair Fall?
  • How to Choose the Right Shampoo
  • Best Shampoos for Hair Fall
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Final Thoughts

What Causes Hair Fall?

Hair fall can be caused by a variety of factors such as hormonal changes, stress, genetics, nutritional deficiencies, and harsh chemicals. Using the wrong shampoo can also contribute to hair fall. Shampoos that contain sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals can strip the hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. This can result in breakage and hair fall.

How to Choose the Right Shampoo

Choosing the right shampoo is crucial when it comes to preventing hair fall. Here are some tips to help you choose the right shampoo:

  • Determine your hair type: Is your hair oily, dry, or normal? Choose a shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Look for natural ingredients: Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals such as sulfates, parabens, and silicones. Look for shampoos that contain natural ingredients such as aloe vera, coconut oil, and tea tree oil.
  • Read reviews: Read reviews from other customers to see if the shampoo has helped them with their hair fall problem.

Best Shampoos for Hair Fall

Now that you know how to choose the right shampoo, let's take a look at some of the best shampoos for hair fall:

  1. Biotin Shampoo: Biotin is a vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth. This shampoo contains biotin and other natural ingredients that can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.
  2. Nizoral Shampoo: Nizoral shampoo contains ketoconazole, an antifungal ingredient that can help reduce inflammation and prevent hair fall.
  3. Pura D'or Original Gold Label Anti-Thinning Shampoo: This shampoo contains natural ingredients such as argan oil, biotin, and nettle extract that can help reduce hair fall and promote thicker, healthier hair.
  4. OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo: This shampoo contains biotin and collagen, two ingredients that can help strengthen the hair and prevent breakage.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

In addition to using the right shampoo, there are other things you can do to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy diet: Make sure you're getting enough vitamins and minerals in your diet, especially vitamin B12, iron, and zinc.
  • Reduce stress: Stress can contribute to hair fall, so make sure you're taking time to relax and unwind.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles such as braids and ponytails can pull on the hair and cause breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about shampoo for hair fall:

  • Can shampoo alone stop hair fall? Shampoo alone cannot stop hair fall. You need to take a holistic approach to hair care, which includes a healthy diet, regular exercise, and stress management.
  • How often should I use shampoo for hair fall? It's best to use shampoo for hair fall every other day or every two days. Overwashing can strip the hair of its natural oils, which can lead to breakage and hair fall.
  • Can hair fall be reversed? In some cases, hair fall can be reversed with the right treatment. However, it's important to see a dermatologist if you're experiencing severe hair fall.

Final Thoughts

Hair fall can be a frustrating problem, but with the right shampoo and hair care routine, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to choose a shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type and contains natural ingredients. In addition, make sure you're eating a healthy diet, reducing stress, and avoiding tight hairstyles. With these tips, you'll be on your way to healthier, fuller hair.
