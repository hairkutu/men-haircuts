How To Style Thick Wavy Hair Naturally In 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look voluminous and glamorous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to style thick wavy hair naturally, without resorting to heat styling tools or harsh chemicals.

Description

Thick wavy hair is characterized by its volume, body, and natural waves. It can be difficult to manage, especially in humid weather or when exposed to moisture. However, with the right care and styling techniques, you can enhance the natural beauty of your hair and make it work for you.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Naturally

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair naturally, there are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and strip it of its natural oils.
  • Opt for natural styling products that are gentle on your hair and scalp.
  • Embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than trying to force it into a different style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style thick wavy hair naturally:

  1. Start with clean, freshly washed hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub or pull on it too much.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and avoiding the roots.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to enhance your natural waves.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low or cool setting.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair and separate any tangles.
  7. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and tame any frizz.
  8. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, or half-up styles, to find what works best for your hair.

Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Hair Naturally

Here are some additional tips for styling thick wavy hair naturally:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair, as these materials are gentler on your hair than regular towels.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to enhance your natural waves and prevent tangling.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick wavy hair naturally:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with thick wavy hair can benefit from washing their hair 1-2 times per week.

Q: Can I use hair gel to style my hair?

A: Yes, but be careful not to use too much or it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my hair?

A: Use a hair oil or serum to add moisture and shine to your hair, and avoid brushing or touching your hair too much.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to caring for thick wavy hair, there are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring products, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can embrace your natural waves and style your thick wavy hair naturally, without resorting to harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture and beauty.
