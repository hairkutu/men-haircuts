How To Determine Hair Type For Men A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 · 9 min read

Knowing your hair type is essential when it comes to choosing the right products and styling techniques. However, determining your hair type can be a challenge, especially for men. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to determine your hair type and provide tips on how to care for each type of hair.

Table of Contents

  • What is Hair Type?
  • How to Determine Hair Type for Men
  • Hair Type Classifications
  • Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Types for Men

What is Hair Type?

Hair type refers to the texture and shape of your hair strands. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has different characteristics that affect how it looks and behaves.

Aside from the four main hair types, hair is also classified based on its thickness, density, and porosity. These factors determine how much moisture your hair can retain and how it responds to different products.

How to Determine Hair Type for Men

The easiest way to determine your hair type is by examining your hair while it's clean and dry. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using any styling products or oils, as they can alter the appearance of your hair.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Air-Dry

After washing your hair, let it air-dry without using a towel or blow-dryer. This will help you see your hair's natural texture and shape.

Step 3: Examine Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, examine it in a well-lit area. Look at the texture, shape, and overall appearance of your hair. Here are some things to consider:

  • Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or coily?
  • How tightly coiled are your curls?
  • Is your hair fine, medium, or thick?
  • How dense is your hair?
  • Is your hair shiny or dull?

Make note of your hair's characteristics, as they will help you determine your hair type.

Hair Type Classifications

Now that you've determined your hair's characteristics, it's time to classify your hair type. Here are the main hair type classifications:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is characterized by hair strands that have no curl or wave pattern. Straight hair can be fine, medium, or thick and can be shiny or dull.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair has a slight curl pattern that creates an "S" shape. Wavy hair can be fine, medium, or thick and can be shiny or dull.

Curly Hair

Curly hair has a distinct curl pattern that ranges from loose to tight. Curly hair can be fine, medium, or thick and can be shiny or dull.

Coily Hair

Coily hair has a tight curl pattern that ranges from "Z" shaped to tight coils. Coily hair can be fine, medium, or thick and can be shiny or dull.

Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types

Now that you know your hair type, it's important to care for it properly. Here are some tips for caring for each hair type:

Straight Hair Care Tips

  • Use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

Wavy Hair Care Tips

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves.

Curly Hair Care Tips

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.

Coily Hair Care Tips

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using a brush, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and defined.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Types for Men

What factors determine hair type?

Hair type is determined by the texture and shape of your hair strands, as well as factors such as thickness, density, and porosity.

How can I tell if my hair is fine or thick?

Fine hair strands are thinner and more delicate, while thick hair strands are thicker and more robust. You can tell if your hair is fine or thick by examining the thickness of a single strand of hair.

Can my hair type change over time?

Yes, your hair type can change over time due to factors such as aging, hormonal changes, and environmental factors.

Should I use different products for different hair types?

Yes, using products specifically formulated for your hair type will help you achieve the best results. For example, if you have curly hair, you should use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Conclusion

Determining your hair type is essential for choosing the right products and styling techniques. By following the steps outlined in this guide, you can determine your hair type and learn how to care for it properly. Remember to use products specifically formulated for your hair type and to avoid using heat styling tools too often to keep your hair healthy and strong.
