How To Angle Long Hair Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a challenge, especially if you have a round face. While long hair can look stunning on a round face, it can also make your face look wider and rounder if not styled correctly. One way to combat this is by angling your long hair to create a slimming effect. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to angle long hair round face, including tips, step-by-step guide, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Angle Long Hair Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Angling your long hair can create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. This technique involves cutting your hair at an angle to create layers that frame your face. The layers should be longer towards the front and shorter towards the back. The angle created by the layers gives your hair a natural flow, creating a slimming effect.

How to Angle Long Hair Round Face

Before you start angling your long hair, it is important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. If you have a round face, you may want to add layers that fall at your cheekbones to create an illusion of a slimmer face. Here are the steps to angle long hair round face:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Once done, towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair completely, but avoid using too much heat as it can damage your hair. If possible, let your hair air dry.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using a comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle, then divide it into two sections. Create more sections by parting your hair from ear to ear. This will make it easier to cut your hair at an angle.

Step 3: Angle Your Hair

Start by cutting your hair at an angle, making sure that the layers are longer towards the front and shorter towards the back. You can start by cutting small sections of your hair, then gradually cut more as you get comfortable. Be careful not to cut too much, as it can affect the overall look of your hair.

Step 4: Blend Your Layers

Once you have angled your hair, use a pair of scissors to blend your layers. This will create a natural look and prevent your hair from looking choppy. You can also use a razor to blend your layers, but be careful not to use too much pressure as it can damage your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have angled your hair, style it to suit your personal preference. You can use a curling iron to create loose waves or a straightener to create a sleek look. Use a hair spray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when angling long hair round face:

  • Consider your face shape when angling your hair
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to prevent your hair from looking choppy
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of the layers to create a natural flow
  • Use a hair mask to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about angling long hair round face:

Q: How often should I angle my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair growth and personal preference. You can angle your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Will angling my long hair damage my hair?

A: If done correctly, angling your long hair should not damage your hair. However, using too much heat or cutting too much hair can cause damage.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Angling long hair round face can create a stunning look that complements your face shape. By following the steps and tips outlined in this blog post, you can angle your long hair like a pro. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
