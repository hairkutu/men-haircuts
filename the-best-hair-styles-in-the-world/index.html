The Best Hair Styles In The World In 2023

Written by Robby Mar 18, 2023 · 11 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle or just need some inspiration, look no further! We've rounded up the best hair styles from around the world for 2023. From sleek and sophisticated to edgy and bold, there's something for everyone. Read on for how to achieve these looks, tips, and hair care advice.

Sleek Ponytail

The sleek ponytail is a classic look that never goes out of style. It's perfect for both formal events and casual outings. To achieve this look, start by brushing your hair back and securing it in a ponytail with an elastic. Then, use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tip:

If you want to add some extra volume, tease the crown of your head before pulling your hair back into a ponytail.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for achieving a relaxed, effortless look. To get this style, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a curling wand to create loose waves. Once you've curled your hair, use your fingers to separate the waves and create a tousled look. Finish off with a spritz of salt spray to enhance the beachy texture.

Tip:

For an even more natural look, try alternating the direction you curl your hair in.

French Bob

The French bob is a chic and sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for a blunt bob that falls just below your chin. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair straight, and finish off with a spritz of shine spray for a glossy finish.

Tip:

For a more dramatic look, add some choppy layers to your bob.

Slick Back

The slick back is a bold and edgy look that's perfect for making a statement. To achieve this style, use a comb to slick back your hair with some gel or pomade. Then, use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tip:

If you want to add some extra shine, try using a hair oil before slicking your hair back.

Braided Crown

The braided crown is a romantic and feminine look that's perfect for weddings or other formal events. To achieve this style, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, create two braids on either side of your head, starting at your temples. Once you've braided your hair, cross the braids over the top of your head and secure them in place with bobby pins.

Tip:

If you have long hair, try wrapping the braids around the back of your head for a more intricate look.

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a casual and effortless look that's perfect for running errands or going to the gym. To achieve this style, start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Then, twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Tip:

If you have thin hair, try adding some texture by using a texturizing spray before creating your bun.

Curly Fro

The curly fro is a bold and playful look that's perfect for those with naturally curly hair. To achieve this style, start by using a curl defining cream to enhance your curls. Then, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tip:

If you want to add some extra volume, try using a pick to fluff out your curls.

Side Part

The side part is a classic and timeless look that's perfect for any occasion. To achieve this style, start by parting your hair to one side. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look. Finish off with a spritz of shine spray for a glossy finish.

Tip:

If you have long hair, try adding some loose waves to the ends for a more romantic look.

Ombre Hair

Ombre hair is a trendy and eye-catching look that's perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their hair. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for a gradual color change from your natural hair color to a lighter shade. Then, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Tip:

If you're not ready to commit to a full ombre, try adding some subtle highlights to your hair instead.

Accessories

Accessories can take any hairstyle to the next level. Try adding a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your look for some extra flair. Just make sure to choose accessories that complement your outfit and hairstyle.

Tip:

If you have short hair, try adding some statement earrings to draw attention to your face.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat on your hair too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

FAQ

What's the best hairstyle for thin hair?

The best hairstyle for thin hair is one that adds volume and texture. Try a layered bob or a tousled pixie cut.

What's the best hairstyle for curly hair?

The best hairstyle for curly hair is one that enhances your natural curls. Try a curly fro or beachy waves.

What's the best way to keep my hair healthy?

The best way to keep your hair healthy is to use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoid using heat on your hair too often, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

What's the best way to add volume to my hair?

The best way to add volume to your hair is to tease the crown of your head before styling. You can also use a texturizing spray or volumizing mousse.

What's the best way to keep my hair in place?

The best way to keep your hair in place is to use a hairspray or gel. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.
