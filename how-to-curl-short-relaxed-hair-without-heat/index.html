How To Curl Short Relaxed Hair Without Heat A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of using heat to curl your short relaxed hair? Heat can cause damage and breakage to your hair, so it’s important to find alternative methods to achieve those bouncy curls. In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to curl your short relaxed hair without heat.

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curling Short Relaxed Hair Without Heat
  • FAQs

How to Curl Short Relaxed Hair Without Heat

Curling short relaxed hair without heat can seem like a daunting task, but it’s actually quite simple. There are many methods to achieve beautiful curls without using heat. Here are some popular methods:

Method 1: Twist-Out

A twist-out is a popular method for curling short relaxed hair without heat. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice (e.g. curl custard, mousse, or gel)
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Twist each section tightly from the root to the end
  5. Allow your hair to dry completely (this can take several hours or overnight)
  6. Untwist each section and fluff your curls

This method will give you defined, bouncy curls that can last for several days.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another popular method for curling short relaxed hair without heat. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Twist each section tightly from the root to the end
  5. Wrap each twist around itself to create a knot
  6. Allow your hair to dry completely (this can take several hours or overnight)
  7. Untwist each knot and fluff your curls

This method will give you smaller, tighter curls that can be easily manipulated into different styles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to curl short relaxed hair without heat:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Choose a curling method (e.g. twist-out, bantu knots)
  5. Follow the steps for your chosen method
  6. Allow your hair to dry completely (this can take several hours or overnight)
  7. Untwist or un-knot each section and fluff your curls
  8. Style your curls as desired

Tips for Curling Short Relaxed Hair Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when curling short relaxed hair without heat:

  • Start with clean, damp hair
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type
  • Section your hair into small sections for best results
  • Choose a method that works best for your hair type and desired outcome
  • Allow your hair to dry completely before un-twisting or un-knotting
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to fluff your curls
  • Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to maintain your curls overnight

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type, the method you used, and how well you maintain them. Generally, curls can last from several days to a week.

Q: Will my curls look natural?

A: Yes! The methods we’ve outlined in this article will give you natural-looking curls without the use of heat.

Q: Can I use these methods on all hair types?

A: Yes! These methods can be used on all hair types, but the results may vary depending on your hair’s texture and length.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

Here are some tips to keep your relaxed hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf or bonnet
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or relaxers too often

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, strong hair that’s perfect for curling without heat.

Conclusion

Curling short relaxed hair without heat is easy and can give you beautiful, natural-looking curls. By following the methods and tips we’ve outlined in this article, you can achieve the curls you’ve always wanted without damaging your hair. Remember to keep your hair healthy and strong by following our hair care tips, and you’ll be on your way to gorgeous curls in no time!
