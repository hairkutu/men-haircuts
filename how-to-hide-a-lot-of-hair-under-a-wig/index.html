How To Hide A Lot Of Hair Under A Wig

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to wear a wig, you may be wondering how to hide a lot of hair underneath it. Whether you have long or thick hair, it can be a challenge to fit it all under a wig without it looking bulky or feeling uncomfortable. In this guide, we'll go over some tips and tricks for hiding a lot of hair under a wig so you can achieve a natural and comfortable look.

Description

Wearing a wig can be a great way to change up your look or add some extra volume to your hair. However, if you have a lot of hair, it can be difficult to fit it all under a wig without it looking unnatural or feeling uncomfortable. In this guide, we'll cover some techniques for hiding a lot of hair under a wig so you can achieve a seamless and comfortable look.

How to

Step 1: Braid your hair

The first step in hiding a lot of hair under a wig is to braid your hair. This will help to keep your hair flat and compact so you can fit it all under the wig. You can either do a regular braid or a French braid, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Create a wig cap

Next, you'll want to create a wig cap to help keep your hair in place. You can either use a pre-made wig cap or make one yourself using a pair of pantyhose. Simply cut off the legs of the pantyhose and tie a knot in the end to create a cap. Then, place the cap over your head and tuck any loose hair inside.

Step 3: Put on the wig

Once you have your braids and wig cap in place, it's time to put on the wig. Start by placing the wig on your head and adjusting it so it sits securely. Then, use small hairpins to attach the wig to your wig cap and secure any loose hair underneath.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you hide a lot of hair under a wig:

  • Use a wig cap that matches your skin tone to help it blend in.
  • Try using a wig grip to help keep your wig in place.
  • If you have long hair, consider cutting it or using a shorter wig to make it easier to manage.
  • Experiment with different wig styles to find one that works best for your hair type and volume.

FAQ

Can I wear a wig if I have a lot of hair?

Yes, you can still wear a wig even if you have a lot of hair. By braiding your hair and using a wig cap, you can create a smooth and flat surface for your wig to sit on.

Will wearing a wig damage my hair?

No, wearing a wig should not damage your hair as long as you are careful when putting it on and taking it off. Be sure to use gentle hairpins and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your natural hair even when wearing a wig. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can cause damage. Also, be sure to give your hair a break from wigs and other hair accessories from time to time to allow it to breathe and stay healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide a lot of hair under a wig and achieve a natural and comfortable look. Whether you're looking to change up your style or add some extra volume to your hair, a wig can be a great option.
