What Is The Best Haircut For My Face Shape Quiz Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're wondering what haircut suits your face shape best, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters their unique features. Luckily, there's a simple solution: take a quiz! In this article, we'll show you how to take the best haircut for my face shape quiz and give you some tips on how to make the most of your new look.

Description: What Is a Face Shape?

Before you take the quiz, it's essential to understand what a face shape is. Your face shape is determined by the proportions of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin. There are six main types of face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Knowing your face shape is crucial in determining what haircut will suit you best.

How to Take the Best Haircut for My Face Shape Quiz

There are many face shape quizzes online, but not all of them are created equal. To get the most accurate result, look for a quiz that asks you specific questions about the shape of your face. Also, make sure that the quiz takes into account other factors such as hair texture, thickness, and length.

One of the best quizzes available is the one offered by Birchbox. It asks you to upload a photo of your face, and then it analyzes your features to determine your face shape. It also gives you personalized haircut recommendations based on your answers.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Take the Birchbox Face Shape Quiz

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to take the Birchbox face shape quiz:

Step 1: Take a Photo of Your Face

Take a photo of your face with your hair pulled back or out of your face. Make sure the lighting is good, and your face is centered in the frame.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Go to the Birchbox website and click on the "Take the Quiz" button. Upload your photo and wait for the site to analyze your face.

Step 3: Answer the Quiz Questions

Next, answer the quiz questions about your hair texture, thickness, and length. The quiz will also ask you about your preferred style and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day.

Step 4: Get Your Results

Once you've answered all the questions, the site will give you personalized haircut recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. You can also browse through different hairstyles and save your favorites for later.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape and taken the quiz, it's time to choose a haircut. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape, not one that tries to change it.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a haircut. Curly hair, for example, will look different than straight hair in the same cut.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day. A low-maintenance haircut may be better for you if you're always on the go.
  • Ask your hair stylist for their opinion. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people ask about choosing a haircut based on their face shape:

What is the best haircut for an oval face?

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile, so you can pull off many different hairstyles. Some of the best haircuts for an oval face include long layers, a blunt bob, and a pixie cut.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

A round face shape benefits from haircuts that create angles and lengthen the face. Some of the best haircuts for a round face include a long layered cut, an angled bob, and a side-swept fringe.

What is the best haircut for a square face?

A square face shape is defined by a strong jawline, so haircuts that soften the edges are ideal. Some of the best haircuts for a square face include a layered bob, a side-swept fringe, and a long layered cut with waves.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Haircut

Once you've chosen your new haircut, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Visit your hair stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

Conclusion

Taking a quiz to determine the best haircut for your face shape is an easy and fun way to try out a new look. Just remember to consider your hair texture, thickness, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a new hairstyle in no time!
