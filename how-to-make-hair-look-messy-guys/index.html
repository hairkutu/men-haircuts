How To Make Hair Look Messy Guys A Guide To Effortless Style

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some edge to your hairstyle, the messy look is the way to go. Messy hairstyles give you that effortless, carefree vibe that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to make hair look messy guys, so you can rock this style with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Look Messy Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Messy Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Make Hair Look Messy Guys

The messy look is all about creating the illusion that you didn't put any effort into your hair. Of course, achieving this style does require some work, but it's worth it for the end result. Here are some ways to make your hair look messy:

  • Use a texturizing product: A texturizing product like sea salt spray or hair wax can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.
  • Use your hands: Instead of using a comb or brush, use your hands to style your hair. This will create a more natural, messy look.
  • Don't be too neat: The key to the messy look is to embrace imperfection. Don't worry if your hair isn't perfectly styled or if there are some flyaways.
  • Consider a messy fringe: If you have bangs or a fringe, consider styling it in a messy, tousled way.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of creating a messy hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing product like sea salt spray or hair wax to add volume and texture.
  3. Use your hands to tousle your hair, creating a messy, undone look.
  4. Focus on the top and front of your hair, as these are the areas that are most visible.
  5. If you have bangs or a fringe, use your fingers to tousle them as well.
  6. Don't worry about making your hair too neat or perfect. Embrace the imperfections.

Tips for Maintaining the Messy Look

Once you've achieved the messy look, you'll want to make sure it lasts throughout the day. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a hairspray or styling spray to help hold your hair in place.
  • Carry a texturizing product with you to touch up your hair throughout the day.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair too much, as this can ruin the messy look.
  • Don't be afraid to add some more product to your hair if it starts to fall flat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making hair look messy guys:

Can this style work for all hair types?

Yes, the messy look can work for all hair types. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you may need to use more product to add volume and texture.

Can I achieve this look without using any products?

It's possible to create a messy look without using any products, but it may be more difficult to achieve. Texturizing products can help add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style in a messy way.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain this style?

You should wash your hair as often as you normally do. However, using too much shampoo can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it more difficult to style in a messy way. Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to help maintain the texture and volume of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, strong hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make your hair look messy guys and achieve that effortless, cool style that's perfect for any occasion.
