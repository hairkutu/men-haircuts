Is Diamond Face Shape Rare Everything You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're curious about your face shape, you've probably heard of the diamond face shape. While it's not as common as other face shapes like round, oval, or heart-shaped, it's still a beautiful and unique facial structure. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about the diamond face shape, including its rarity, how to identify it, and how to style your hair to flatter your features.

Table of Contents

  • Is Diamond Face Shape Rare?
  • How to Identify a Diamond Face Shape
  • How to Style Hair for Diamond Face Shapes
  • Tips for Flattering Diamond Face Shapes

Is Diamond Face Shape Rare?

While the diamond face shape is not as common as other shapes, it's not necessarily rare. In fact, it's estimated that around 5% of the population has a diamond face shape. This means that while it may not be as prevalent as other shapes, it's still a beautiful and unique feature that should be celebrated.

How to Identify a Diamond Face Shape

The diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with wider cheekbones. It's similar to the heart-shaped face, but without the widows peak hairline. To identify a diamond face shape, look for the following features:

  • High, prominent cheekbones
  • Narrow forehead
  • Narrow chin
  • Pointed jawline

If you're still unsure if you have a diamond face shape, try taking a photo of yourself with your hair pulled back and your face in a neutral expression. Then, draw an imaginary line from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin. If the line creates a diamond shape, you likely have a diamond face shape.

How to Style Hair for Diamond Face Shapes

When it comes to styling hair for diamond face shapes, the goal is to balance out the prominent cheekbones and narrow forehead and chin. Here are some tips:

  • Try a side-swept bang to create the illusion of a wider forehead
  • Opt for long layers to soften the jawline
  • Avoid styles that add height to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear longer and narrower
  • Consider a bob or lob that hits just below the chin to create balance

Tips for Flattering Diamond Face Shapes

In addition to hairstyles, there are other ways to flatter a diamond face shape:

  • Wear earrings that dangle or are wider at the bottom to balance out the cheekbones
  • Try glasses or sunglasses with rounded edges to soften the face
  • Avoid wearing hats that are too tall or narrow, as they can make the face appear longer

FAQs About Diamond Face Shapes

Can a diamond face shape have a widow's peak?

While it's not common, it is possible for a diamond face shape to have a widow's peak hairline. This can make the shape appear more heart-shaped.

Is a diamond face shape good for short haircuts?

Yes! A diamond face shape is well-suited for short haircuts, especially those that hit just below the chin. This can help balance out the prominent cheekbones and narrow chin.

What celebrities have diamond face shapes?

Some celebrities with diamond face shapes include Rihanna, Keira Knightley, and Jennifer Lopez.

Hair Care Tips for Diamond Face Shapes

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Condition regularly to keep hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair
  • Consider regular trims to keep split ends at bay

Now that you know more about the diamond face shape, you can embrace your unique features and style your hair to flatter your face. Whether you opt for a side-swept bang or a lob, the goal is to create balance and enhance your natural beauty.
