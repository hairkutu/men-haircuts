How To Style Wet Hair So It s Curly In The Morning

Written by Bardi May 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you struggle with getting your hair to look curly when you wake up in the morning? You're not alone. Many people find that their hair falls flat overnight, leaving them with a lackluster look in the morning. Fortunately, there are some simple steps you can take to style your wet hair so it's curly when you wake up. In this post, we'll go over how to achieve this look and provide some tips for maintaining it.

How to Style Wet Hair for Curly Results

Styling wet hair for curls requires a bit of preparation and patience, but the results are worth it. Here are the steps you can take to achieve curly hair in the morning:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Rinse your hair thoroughly and then gently towel dry, being careful not to rub your hair too hard.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Products

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. There are many options to choose from, including curl creams, gels, and mousses. Work the product through your hair evenly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into even sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to apply heat evenly and ensure that your curls are uniform.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain your curls. Start by drying the roots of your hair, then work your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Sleep with Your Hair in a Bun

Once your hair is dry, twist it into a loose bun and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie. This will help to maintain your curls overnight and prevent them from falling flat.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Now that you know how to style your wet hair for curly results, here are some tips for maintaining your curls throughout the day:

  • Don't touch your hair too much. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate your curls.
  • Apply a small amount of curl-enhancing product to your hair throughout the day to maintain your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

FAQs

Can I achieve curly hair without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve curly hair without using heat. Try braiding your damp hair and leaving it overnight. In the morning, take out the braids and you should have curly hair.

What should I do if my curls fall flat overnight?

If your curls fall flat overnight, try refreshing them with a curl-enhancing spray or cream. Apply the product to your hair and scrunch your curls to revive them.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve curly hair that lasts all day and night. With a little patience and practice, you'll be able to wake up with beautiful, bouncy curls every morning.
