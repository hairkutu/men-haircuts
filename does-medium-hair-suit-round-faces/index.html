Does Medium Hair Suit Round Faces

Written by Daniel Jun 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Description | How to | Step by step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

For those with round faces, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. Many people believe that short hair is the only option for those with round faces, but that's not always the case. Medium-length hair can actually be quite flattering on those with round faces, as it helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

How to

The key to making medium-length hair work for a round face is choosing the right cut. One of the best options is a layered cut, which helps to create the illusion of length and volume. Another great option is a long bob, which is typically shorter in the back and longer in the front, creating a flattering angle that helps to elongate the face.

Step by step

When getting a layered cut, make sure to ask your stylist to cut the layers so that they fall below the chin. This length will help to create a slimming effect on the face, while still allowing for plenty of movement and volume. If you opt for a long bob, make sure to ask for a slight angle that's shorter in the back and longer in the front.

Tips

When styling medium-length hair for a round face, it's important to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of the face. Instead, focus on creating volume at the crown of the head, which will help to elongate the face. You can achieve this by using a volumizing mousse or spray, and blow-drying your hair with a round brush.

FAQ

Q: Can people with round faces wear bangs?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides can be quite flattering on those with round faces, as they help to create the illusion of length.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that people with round faces should avoid?

A: Yes, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider. It's also best to avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can make the face appear even rounder.

Hair Care

To keep medium-length hair looking its best, it's important to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. You should also get regular trims to keep your ends looking healthy and prevent split ends. Finally, be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.

Overall, medium-length hair can be a great option for those with round faces, as long as you choose the right cut and style. With the right care and attention, your medium-length hair can look great and help to create a more balanced, flattering look.
