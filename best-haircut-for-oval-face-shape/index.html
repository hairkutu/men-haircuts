Best Haircut For Oval Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face shape, count yourself lucky! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best haircut that flatters your features. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut for your oval face shape.

Description

An oval face shape is defined by balanced features with a gently curved jawline that is slightly narrower than the forehead. The cheekbones are usually the widest part of the face. Celebrities with oval face shapes include Beyonce, Jessica Alba, and Emma Watson. This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut.

How to Find the Best Haircut for Oval Face Shape

When choosing the best haircut for oval face shape, consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • Your hair texture (straight, wavy, or curly)
  • Your personal style
  • Your face shape features (such as your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline)

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps to guide you in finding the best haircut for oval face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape: Before choosing a haircut, identify your face shape to ensure that it is an oval face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture: Choose a haircut that suits your hair texture.
  3. Look for inspiration: Browse through magazines or online for haircut inspiration.
  4. Consult a professional hairstylist: A hairstylist can help you choose the best haircut that flatters your features.
  5. Take care of your hair: Maintain your haircut by taking care of your hair with proper hair care products and techniques.

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut for Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for your oval face shape:

  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the top of your head, as this will make your face appear longer.
  • Choose a haircut that frames your face and accentuates your features.
  • Consider adding layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short, as this can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider a side-swept bang to soften your features.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for oval face shape?

The best haircut for oval face shape is a haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Some popular haircuts for oval face shape include the long bob, layered haircut, and pixie cut.

What hair length is best for oval face shape?

Oval face shapes can pull off almost any hair length, but medium to long hair lengths are the most flattering.

What hair texture is best for oval face shape?

Oval face shapes can pull off any hair texture, but a haircut that suits your natural hair texture is the most flattering.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

After finding the best haircut for oval face shape, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

Choosing the best haircut for oval face shape is all about finding a haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style. With the tips and advice in this article, you can confidently choose the best haircut for your oval face shape and maintain its health and appearance with proper hair care.
