Cutting Men s Hair Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle? Look no further than the short back and sides cut! This style has been popular for decades and is still a favorite today. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve this style, along with tips and FAQs to ensure you get the perfect cut.

Description

The short back and sides cut is a classic men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. This style is versatile, low maintenance, and can be worn in a variety of ways. It's a great option for men who want a neat and tidy look without sacrificing style.

How To

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with different guard sizes, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle of water. Here's how to achieve the short back and sides cut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even finish.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using a comb, section the hair into three parts: the top, sides, and back. Clip the top section out of the way for now.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Attach a guard to your clippers and start at the bottom of the hairline on one side. Use upward motions to cut the hair, moving the clippers up the head as you go. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Back

Remove the guard from your clippers and use them to blend the back of the hair into the sides. Use short, upward strokes to create a seamless transition.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Unclip the top section of hair and comb it straight up. Use scissors to trim the hair to your desired length. You can leave the hair longer for a more modern look or shorter for a more traditional cut.

Step 6: Blend the Top

Using scissors, blend the top section of hair into the sides and back. Use short, snipping motions to create a natural-looking finish.

Step 7: Finish and Style

Use a comb to style the hair into your desired look. You can use a hair product like gel or wax to add texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short back and sides cut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to ensure you don't cut too much off at once.
  • Use a spray bottle of water to keep the hair damp while cutting. This will make it easier to control and cut evenly.
  • Take breaks and step back to check your progress. This will help you avoid any mistakes and ensure a more even cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides cut:

Is the short back and sides cut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this cut can work for all hair types, from straight to curly to thick. The key is to adjust the length and texture to suit your hair type.

How often should I get a short back and sides cut?

This cut is low maintenance and can last up to four weeks. However, if you want to keep the look sharp, it's best to get a trim every two to three weeks.

Can I style the short back and sides cut in different ways?

Yes, this cut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can comb it to the side, slick it back, or leave it messy for a more casual look.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Now that you know how to achieve the short back and sides cut, it's time to give it a try! Follow these steps and tips, and you'll have a classic and stylish look in no time.
