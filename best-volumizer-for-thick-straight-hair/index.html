The Best Volumizer For Thick Straight Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick straight hair, you know how difficult it can be to add volume to your locks. Fortunately, there are volumizers out there that can help you achieve the perfect amount of lift and bounce. In this guide, we'll introduce you to some of the best volumizers for thick straight hair, and provide you with tips on how to use them effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use a Volumizer for Thick Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Volumizer for Thick Straight Hair
  • Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Volumizer
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

Description

When it comes to volumizers for thick straight hair, you want to look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. You also want to look for products that provide a long-lasting hold, without making your hair stiff or crunchy. Some of the best volumizers for thick straight hair include:

Hair Mousse

Hair mousse is a popular choice for adding volume to thick straight hair. Look for mousses that are lightweight and provide a flexible hold. Apply the mousse evenly throughout your damp hair, and then blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the roots.

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is another great option for adding volume to your hair. Spray the dry shampoo at the roots of your hair, and then massage it in with your fingers. The dry shampoo will absorb any excess oil, which can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

Root Lifter

A root lifter is a product that is specifically designed to add volume to the roots of your hair. Apply the root lifter directly to your roots, and then blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift and volume.

How to Use a Volumizer for Thick Straight Hair

Before you start using a volumizer for thick straight hair, it's important to start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo, or just wet your hair in the shower. Here are some tips on how to use a volumizer:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift and volume
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create a more natural, tousled look

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Volumizer for Thick Straight Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to using a volumizer for thick straight hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a small amount of volumizer to your hair, focusing on the roots
  3. Use a round brush to lift and separate your hair as you blow dry it
  4. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to help hold your style in place

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Volumizer

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your volumizer:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift and volume
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create a more natural, tousled look
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Experiment with different types of volumizers to find the one that works best for your hair type

FAQ

What is the best volumizer for thick straight hair?

The best volumizer for thick straight hair depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some popular options include hair mousse, dry shampoo, and root lifters.

Can I use a volumizer on dry hair?

Yes, you can use a volumizer on dry hair. However, it's generally more effective to use a volumizer on clean, damp hair.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy after using a volumizer?

To prevent your hair from looking greasy after using a volumizer, be sure to start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Avoid applying the product directly to your roots, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

In addition to using a volumizer, there are several other hair care tips you can follow to keep your thick straight hair looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips and using a volumizer, you can achieve the perfect amount of volume and bounce for your thick straight hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you, and enjoy your beautiful, voluminous locks!
