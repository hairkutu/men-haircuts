What Hairstyle Would Suit Me App Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Petter Sep 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through Pinterest and Instagram, trying to find the perfect hairstyle that will suit your face shape and hair type? Look no further than the "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app, your personal hair consultant at your fingertips. In this article, we'll show you how to use the app, give you some tips for finding the perfect hairstyle, answer some frequently asked questions, and provide some hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

How to Use the App

The "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app is easy to use and available for both iOS and Android devices. Simply download the app from your app store, and follow these steps:

  1. Take a clear photo of your face, with your hair pulled back or off your face.
  2. Select your gender.
  3. Select your face shape from the options provided (oval, round, square, heart, or long).
  4. Select your hair type from the options provided (straight, wavy, curly, or kinky).
  5. Choose from the selection of hairstyles provided, and try them on virtually to see how they would look on you.

It's that simple! The app uses advanced facial recognition technology to analyze your face shape and hair type, and recommends hairstyles that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some more detailed instructions for using the app:

Step 1: Take a Clear Photo

Make sure your face is well-lit and there are no shadows or obstructions on your face. Pull your hair back or off your face so that your features are clearly visible.

Step 2: Select Your Gender

Choose whether you are male or female. This will help the app recommend hairstyles that are appropriate for your gender.

Step 3: Select Your Face Shape

Choose your face shape from the options provided. If you're not sure what your face shape is, the app provides a guide to help you determine it.

Step 4: Select Your Hair Type

Choose your hair type from the options provided. If you're not sure what your hair type is, the app provides a guide to help you determine it.

Step 5: Try on Hairstyles

Choose from the selection of hairstyles provided, and try them on virtually to see how they would look on you. You can adjust the size and position of the hairstyle to fit your face, and even change the hair color to see how you would look with a different shade.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using the "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day. Choose a hairstyle that fits your busy schedule.
  • Think about your hair texture and how the hairstyle will work with it. If you have thick hair, a layered cut will help to thin it out. If you have thin hair, a blunt cut will help to give it more volume.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. The app allows you to try on as many hairstyles as you want, so have fun and find the look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app:

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the app is free to download and use.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

Yes, you will need an internet connection to download the app and use it for the first time. After that, you can use the app offline.

Can I save my favorite hairstyles?

Yes, you can save your favorite hairstyles to your phone's camera roll or share them on social media.

Can I use the app to try on different hair colors?

Yes, the app allows you to change the hair color of the hairstyles you try on.

Hair Care Advice

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle using the "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to help detangle your hair and keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With the "What Hairstyle Would Suit Me" app and these hair care tips, you'll be able to find and maintain the perfect hairstyle for you. Happy styling!
