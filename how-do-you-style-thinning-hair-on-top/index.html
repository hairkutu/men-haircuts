How Do You Style Thinning Hair On Top

Written by Robby Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Thinning hair on top can be a frustrating experience for many people, but there are ways to style and manage this type of hair. In this article, we will discuss how to style thinning hair on top, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice. Read on to discover how to make the most of your thinning hair and feel confident each day.

Description

Thinning hair on top is a common issue for both men and women, and it can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, aging, hormonal changes, and more. This type of hair can be difficult to style, as it may lack volume and thickness, but there are ways to make it look its best. Whether you have a receding hairline, bald spots, or just thinning hair on top, these tips and tricks will help you style your hair with confidence.

How to Style Thinning Hair on Top

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in styling thinning hair on top is to choose a hairstyle that works for you. Avoid styles that require a lot of volume or thickness, as these can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for shorter hairstyles that are easy to manage and don't require a lot of styling. A buzz cut, crew cut, or a short textured crop are all great options for thinning hair on top.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a huge difference in the appearance of thinning hair on top. Look for products that add volume and texture, such as volumizing shampoos, thickening sprays, and dry shampoos. Avoid heavy products like gels and waxes, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help add volume and lift to thinning hair on top. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots, and aim the blow dryer at the roots as well. This will help create volume and lift, giving your hair a fuller appearance.

Step 4: Consider Hair Fibers or Hair Pieces

If you're looking for a more dramatic solution, hair fibers or hair pieces can help cover up bald spots or thinning areas. Hair fibers are small, microscopic fibers that attach to your existing hair, creating the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. Hair pieces, such as toupees or wigs, can also be an option for more severe cases of hair loss.

Tips for Styling Thinning Hair on Top

Here are a few additional tips to help you style thinning hair on top:

  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Try cutting your hair shorter, as shorter hair can create the appearance of thickness and fullness.
  • Consider getting regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals or treatments, as these can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Consider using a scalp treatment or hair growth serum to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles are best for thinning hair on top?

Shorter hairstyles that don't require a lot of volume or thickness are best for thinning hair on top. A buzz cut, crew cut, or short textured crop are all great options.

How can I add volume to thinning hair on top?

Using volumizing shampoos, thickening sprays, and blow-drying your hair with a round brush can all help add volume and lift to thinning hair on top.

Can hair fibers or hair pieces help with thinning hair on top?

Yes, hair fibers or hair pieces can help cover up bald spots or thinning areas, creating the appearance of thicker, fuller hair.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on Top

Caring for thinning hair on top is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid harsh chemicals or treatments.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles and lead to more hair loss.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Thinning hair on top can be a challenge, but with the right hairstyle, products, and care, you can make the most of your hair and feel confident each day. Whether you choose a shorter hairstyle, use hair fibers or hair pieces, or just focus on healthy hair care, these tips will help you style your hair with ease.
