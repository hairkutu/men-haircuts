How To Do A 0 Degree Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a haircut that's both modern and versatile, then a 0 degree haircut might be just what you need. This type of haircut features a uniform length all around the head, creating a sleek and polished look that's perfect for both men and women. In this article, we'll take you through the process of how to do a 0 degree haircut step by step, as well as provide some tips for taking care of your hair afterward.

What is a 0 Degree Haircut?

A 0 degree haircut is a haircut where the hair is cut to the same length all around the head, creating a blunt and uniform look. This type of haircut is also known as a "one-length" cut, as there are no layers or varying lengths throughout the hair. The term "0 degree" refers to the angle at which the hair is cut, which is perpendicular to the scalp.

How to Do a 0 Degree Haircut

What You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and conditioning the hair, then towel-dry it until it's damp. Comb through any tangles or knots, and divide the hair into four sections using hair clips.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair at the nape of the neck, using your scissors to cut straight across the hair. Move slowly and carefully, making sure to keep the hair taut and straight as you cut. Continue working your way up the head, cutting each section to the same length.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the hair to the same length, it's time to blend the hair to create a more natural look. Take small sections of hair and use your scissors to cut into the ends, creating soft, feathered edges. This will help to blend the hair and create a more natural look.

Step 4: Finish the Cut

Once you've blended the hair, it's time to finish the cut. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven sections, and make sure that the hair is even all around the head.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair After a 0 Degree Haircut

Now that you've mastered how to do a 0 degree haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use them
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy

FAQ

Is a 0 degree haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a 0 degree haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to consider your hair's texture and thickness when deciding on a haircut, as some styles may require more maintenance than others.

How often should I get a 0 degree haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair after a 0 degree haircut to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut. With a little bit of care, you can keep your 0 degree haircut looking sleek and polished for weeks to come.
