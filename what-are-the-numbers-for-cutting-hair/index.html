What Are The Numbers For Cutting Hair

Written by Petter Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you wondering what those numbers on your hair clipper mean? Well, you’re not alone. Many people do not understand the significance of the numbers on their hair clippers. If you’re looking to give yourself a haircut or thinking about cutting someone else's hair, understanding the numbers is crucial. In this article, we’ll explain what the numbers for cutting hair mean, how to use them and some tips to get the perfect haircut.

Description

The numbers on hair clippers indicate the length of the hair that will be left after the cut. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. For example, if you use a number 1 clipper guard, it will leave 1/8 of an inch of hair. On the other hand, if you use a number 8 clipper guard, it will leave 1 inch of hair. Each number has a specific length associated with it.

How to

To use the hair clipper, you need to attach the clipper guard to the clipper blade. The clipper guard will determine the length of the hair that is cut. Once you have attached the clipper guard, turn on the clipper and start cutting the hair. Make sure to start with a larger number guard and work your way down to the desired length.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut hair with clippers:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair.
  2. Choose the right clipper guard for the desired length
  3. Attach the clipper guard to the clipper blade
  4. Turn on the clipper and start cutting the hair from the bottom, moving upwards
  5. Repeat until the desired length is achieved
  6. Use scissors to trim and shape the hair
  7. Finish with a comb and hair styling product

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect haircut:

  • Always start with a larger clipper guard and work your way down to the desired length.
  • Use scissors to trim and shape the hair for a more polished look.
  • Don't rush the process, take your time to ensure you get an even cut.
  • Keep your hair clippers clean and lubricated to ensure they work properly.

FAQ

What is the difference between hair clippers and hair trimmers?

Hair clippers are designed to cut hair in bulk, while hair trimmers are designed for more precise trimming, such as around the ears and neckline.

Can I use hair clippers on wet hair?

It is not recommended to use hair clippers on wet hair. Wet hair can clog the blades and cause the clippers to malfunction.

How often should I replace my clipper blades?

Clipper blades should be replaced every 6 to 12 months, depending on how often they are used. Dull blades can cause uneven cuts and pull hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair. Regular haircuts, using quality hair products, and protecting your hair from heat damage are some of the ways to keep your hair looking great.

Now that you know what the numbers for cutting hair mean, you can confidently give yourself or someone else a haircut. Remember to start with a larger clipper guard and work your way down to the desired length. Take your time, and don't rush the process. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect haircut every time.
