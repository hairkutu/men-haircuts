Why Is My Hair Wavy Underneath And Straight On Top

Written by William Apr 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Have you ever looked in the mirror and wondered why your hair seems to have different textures? Why is it wavy underneath and straight on top? This can be a frustrating issue for many people, especially those who are trying to style their hair or achieve a certain look. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this phenomenon and offer some tips on how to manage your hair effectively.

  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Wavy Underneath and Straight on Top Hair

Reasons for Wavy Underneath and Straight on Top Hair

There are several reasons why your hair may be wavy underneath and straight on top. One of the primary reasons is genetics. Your hair type and texture are determined by your genes, and some people are simply predisposed to have hair that is wavy or curly underneath and straight on top.

Another reason is the way that you style your hair. If you frequently use heat styling tools like flat irons or blow dryers, this can cause damage to your hair and lead to a change in texture. Over time, the heat can break down the proteins in your hair, leading to frizz and waves.

Lastly, the way that you sleep can also play a role in the texture of your hair. If you sleep with your hair in a bun or ponytail, this can cause your hair to become wavy or curly underneath and straight on top.

How to Manage Wavy Underneath and Straight on Top Hair

If you're struggling with wavy underneath and straight on top hair, there are several things that you can do to manage it effectively. First, consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for your hair type. A layered cut can help to blend the different textures of your hair and create a more uniform look.

You can also experiment with different styling techniques to help manage your hair. For example, if you want to create more volume in your hair, try blow drying your hair upside down. This can help to create lift and add volume to the roots of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your wavy underneath and straight on top hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to help prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  5. Use a flat iron to straighten the top layer of your hair.
  6. Curl the underneath layer of your hair using a curling iron or wand.
  7. Apply a finishing spray or serum to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy and Manageable

Here are some tips for keeping your wavy underneath and straight on top hair healthy and manageable:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. Try to limit your use to once or twice a week.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I permanently straighten the underneath layer of my hair?

A: Yes, you can permanently straighten your hair using a chemical straightening treatment. However, this can be damaging to your hair and should only be done by a professional stylist.

Q: Can I curl the top layer of my hair?

A: Yes, you can curl the top layer of your hair using a curling iron or wand. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Products for Wavy Underneath and Straight on Top Hair

Here are some hair care products that are specifically designed for wavy underneath and straight on top hair:

  • Matrix Biolage HydraSource Detangling Solution
  • Briogeo Curl Charisma Rice Amino + Avocado Leave-In Defining Crème
  • Oribe Curl Control Silkening Crème

If you're struggling with wavy underneath and straight on top hair, don't give up hope. With the right styling techniques and hair care products, you can achieve a look that you love. Just be patient and experiment until you find the techniques that work best for you!
