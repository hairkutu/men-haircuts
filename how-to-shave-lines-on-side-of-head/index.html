How To Shave Lines On Side Of Head Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to add some edge to your style, shaving lines on the side of your head can be a great option. This look is popular with both men and women and can be customized to your personal preference. In this guide, we'll go over how to shave lines on the side of your head in a few simple steps.

Description

Shaving lines on the side of your head is a bold and trendy hairstyle that involves creating sharp, clean lines along the hairline. This style is often paired with a fade or undercut, but can also be worn with longer hair. The lines can be straight or curved, and can be placed in various patterns depending on your preference.

How to Shave Lines on Side of Head

Step 1: Choose the Placement

The first step is to decide where you want the lines to be placed. This will depend on your hair length and the overall style you're going for. Some popular options include:

  • One or two lines along the side of the head
  • A zig-zag pattern along the hairline
  • Multiple lines along the side and back of the head

Once you've decided on the placement, use a comb to section off the hair where you want the lines to be.

Step 2: Use a Razor or Clippers

Next, you'll need to use a razor or clippers to shave the lines. If you're using a razor, be sure to use a sharp, clean blade. Start by lightly shaving the outline of the line, then go over it again to create a deeper, more defined line.

If you're using clippers, attach a blade guard that matches the length of your hair. Use the clippers to shave along the outline of the line, then go over it again to create a sharper edge.

Step 3: Clean Up the Lines

After you've shaved the lines, it's important to clean up any stray hairs or uneven edges. Use a pair of scissors or trimmers to carefully cut away any excess hair around the lines. You can also use a small brush or comb to brush away any loose hairs.

Step 4: Style as Desired

Once you've finished shaving the lines, you can style your hair as desired. This look pairs well with a fade, undercut, or buzz cut, but can also be worn with longer hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect style for you.

Tips for Shaving Lines on Side of Head

  • Use a mirror to check the placement and symmetry of the lines
  • Start with a light touch and gradually shave deeper to avoid mistakes
  • Clean your razor or clippers before and after use to prevent infection
  • Consider consulting a professional barber or stylist for best results

FAQs

How long do shaved lines last?

Shaved lines can last for several weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows. It's important to maintain the lines by trimming away any stray hairs as they grow back.

Can I shave lines on my own?

Yes, you can shave lines on your own, but it's important to use caution and take your time. If you're unsure about the process, consider consulting a professional barber or stylist for guidance.

Can women shave lines on the side of their head?

Absolutely! Shaved lines on the side of the head can be a great option for women who want to add some edge to their style.

Hair Care for Shaved Lines

After shaving lines on the side of your head, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and irritation. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently shave lines on the side of your head and rock a bold, trendy hairdo.
