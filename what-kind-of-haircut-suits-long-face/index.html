What Kind Of Haircut Suits Long Face

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face, you might be wondering what kind of haircut suits you best. Having a long face can be challenging when it comes to choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features. But worry not, as we have some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect haircut for your face shape. In this article, we'll give you some ideas on haircuts that suit long faces, how to style them, and some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Description

A long face is characterized as having a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheekbones. If you have this face shape, you might want to choose a haircut that adds width to your face and balances your features. The right haircut can make your face appear shorter and more proportional.

How to Choose the Right Haircut?

When choosing a haircut for a long face, keep in mind that you want to add width to your face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of your head. Haircuts that work best for long faces are those that are layered, wavy, or have bangs. Avoid haircuts that are too long or straight as they can elongate your face even more.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for long faces as they add volume to the sides of the head. Pixie cuts, choppy bobs, and layered haircuts are great options. You can also add bangs to your short haircut to balance your features.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts are perfect for long faces as they add volume and texture. Layered cuts, beachy waves, and side-swept bangs are great options.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer long hair, go for layered cuts that add volume to the sides of your head. You can also add waves or curls to your long hair to add texture and volume.

Tips for Styling Haircuts for Long Faces

Now that you have an idea of what kind of haircut suits long faces, here are some tips for styling them:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a round brush to curl the ends of your hair and add volume.
  • Avoid parting your hair in the middle as it can elongate your face.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands or clips to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Can I have straight hair if I have a long face?

Straight hair can elongate your face even more, so it's best to add some texture and volume to your hair. If you prefer straight hair, add some layers or waves to your hair to add volume and texture.

Can I have bangs if I have a long face?

Yes, you can have bangs if you have a long face. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options as they add width to your face and balance your features.

What kind of hair color suits long faces?

Warm hair colors like caramel, honey, or chestnut flatter long faces as they add warmth to the skin and soften the features. Avoid hair colors that are too cool or ashy as they can make your face appear more elongated.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, finding the right haircut for a long face can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a haircut that flatters your features and adds volume to your hair. Remember to add texture and volume to your hair, avoid haircuts that are too long or straight, and use hair care products that keep your hair healthy and shiny.
