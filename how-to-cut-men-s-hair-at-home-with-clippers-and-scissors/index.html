How To Cut Men s Hair At Home With Clippers And Scissors

Written by Frank May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's hair at home with clippers and scissors. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve a professional-looking haircut.

Description

With the ongoing pandemic, many people are avoiding going to salons and choosing to cut their own hair at home. Cutting men's hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair at home using clippers and scissors.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the necessary tools: a pair of scissors, clippers, comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Start by washing and drying the hair. Using the comb, comb the hair to remove any tangles and create a part where you want to start the haircut.

Next, use the clippers with the desired guard length to trim the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.

Once you've trimmed the sides and back, move on to the top of the head. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it in small sections to ensure an even cut.

After you've finished cutting, use the clippers to trim the neckline and around the ears.

Finally, use the comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Step-by-step

Step 1: Wash and dry the hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry.

Step 2: Comb the hair

Using the comb, remove any tangles and create a part where you want to start the haircut.

Step 3: Trim the sides and back with clippers

Use the clippers with the desired guard length to trim the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the top with scissors

Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Trim the neckline and around the ears with clippers

Use the clippers to trim the hair around the neckline and ears.

Step 6: Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed

Use the comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Tips

-Start with a longer guard length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

-Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair before cutting. This will make it easier to cut and ensure an even cut.

-Cut the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.

FAQ

What tools do I need to cut men's hair at home?

You'll need a pair of scissors, clippers, comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

What guard length should I start with?

Start with a longer guard length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't worry. You can always go back and trim the hair as needed.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and shiny appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling.

Cutting men's hair at home can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.
