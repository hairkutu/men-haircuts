Can You Turn Curly Hair Into Straight Hair Permanently

Written by Bardi Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be beautiful, but some people prefer the sleek look of straight hair. If you are someone who wants to turn their curly hair into straight hair permanently, you may be wondering if it is possible. The short answer is yes, but it is important to understand the process and consider the potential risks.

Description

Before we dive into the details of turning curly hair into straight hair permanently, let's define what we mean by "permanently." Permanently straightening your hair means that the results will last until your hair grows out or until you cut it off. This is different from temporary straightening methods, such as using a flat iron or blow drying your hair straight, which only last until your next shampoo.

How To

There are a few different methods you can use to permanently straighten your curly hair. One popular method is a chemical relaxer, also known as a perm. This involves applying a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its curly shape. After the solution is applied, your hair is washed and conditioned, and then a neutralizer is applied to set the new shape. This process can take a few hours and should be done by a professional stylist.

Another option is a keratin treatment, which is a smoothing treatment that can make your hair straighter and more manageable. This involves applying a keratin solution to your hair and then sealing it in with heat. The treatment can take a few hours and should also be done by a professional stylist.

Step by Step

If you decide to go with a chemical relaxer or keratin treatment, here are the basic steps you can expect:

Chemical Relaxer:

  1. Your hair will be washed and sectioned.
  2. The relaxer solution will be applied to each section.
  3. Your hair will be combed through to ensure even distribution.
  4. The solution will be left on for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and texture.
  5. The solution will be rinsed out and a neutralizer will be applied.
  6. Your hair will be washed and conditioned.

Keratin Treatment:

  1. Your hair will be washed and dried.
  2. The keratin solution will be applied to your hair.
  3. Your hair will be flat ironed to seal in the solution.
  4. Your hair will be washed and conditioned.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you decide to permanently straighten your hair:

  • Choose a reputable stylist who has experience with the method you choose.
  • Make sure to follow any aftercare instructions provided by your stylist.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Is it safe to permanently straighten curly hair?

Both chemical relaxers and keratin treatments can be safe if done correctly by a professional stylist. However, there is always a risk of damage to your hair, so it is important to choose a reputable stylist and follow any aftercare instructions provided.

Will my hair be completely straight after a chemical relaxer or keratin treatment?

Your hair may not be completely straight after a chemical relaxer or keratin treatment, especially if you have very curly hair. However, these treatments can make your hair much more manageable and easier to straighten with heat styling tools.

How long will the results last?

The results of a chemical relaxer or keratin treatment can last several months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your hair.

Hair Care

If you decide to permanently straighten your curly hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for chemically treated hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, if you want to turn your curly hair into straight hair permanently, it is possible with the right method and a skilled stylist. However, it is important to understand the potential risks and take good care of your hair after the process is complete.
