How To Style Shoulder Length Thick Layered Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for those who want to keep their hair manageable and easy to style. However, when you add thickness and layers to the mix, it can be challenging to know how to style your hair. In this guide, we will walk you through how to style shoulder length thick layered hair in a way that is easy and effortless.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for many women because it is versatile and easy to maintain. When you add layers to the mix, it can add dimension and movement to your hair. However, styling this type of hair can be challenging, especially if you have thick hair. The good news is that with the right techniques and tools, you can style your hair in a way that is both effortless and chic.

How to Style Shoulder Length Thick Layered Hair

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to styling your shoulder length thick layered hair is to start with clean, dry hair. This will ensure that your hair is easy to work with and that your style will last throughout the day. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons.

Step 3: Choose your styling tool

When it comes to styling shoulder length thick layered hair, there are many tools you can use. A straightener can give you sleek, smooth hair, while a curling iron can add waves or curls. An air-dry cream or mousse can also help you achieve a natural, tousled look.

Step 4: Section your hair

Once you have chosen your styling tool, it's time to section your hair. Divide your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Style your hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Depending on the look you are going for, you can use your straightener or curling iron to create waves or curls. If you prefer a more natural look, use an air-dry cream or mousse to create texture and volume. Don't forget to use a hairspray or finishing spray to keep your style in place.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
- Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
- Experiment with different styling tools to find what works best for your hair.
- Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length thick layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it healthy.

Q: What kind of products should I use on my shoulder length thick layered hair?

A: Use products that are designed for your hair type, such as a volumizing mousse or a smoothing serum. It's also important to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage from hot tools.

Q: How can I add volume to my shoulder length thick layered hair?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair. You can also flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair to add volume.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too often. It's also important to protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you swim regularly. If you have any concerns about your hair, don't hesitate to ask your stylist for advice.
