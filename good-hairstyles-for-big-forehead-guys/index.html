Good Hairstyles For Big Forehead Guys Tips How To And More

Written by Robby Jul 21, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a guy with a big forehead and looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we’ll go over some great hairstyles for big forehead guys, including how to achieve them and tips for maintaining your new ‘do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Styles for Big Forehead Guys
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQ About Hairstyles for Big Forehead Guys
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a big forehead is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes require different hairstyles to balance out features and create a harmonious look. If you’re not sure what your face shape is, ask your hairstylist or use a face shape guide online.

Once you know your face shape, look for hairstyles that work with your forehead size. For example, a fringe or bangs can help to cover a larger forehead, while a side part can create the illusion of a smaller forehead. Be sure to also consider your hair type and texture, as some styles may not work well with certain hair types.

Styles for Big Forehead Guys

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let’s dive into some great styles for big forehead guys:

  • The Fringe: A fringe or bangs can be a great option for guys with big foreheads. This style adds volume to the hairline and covers up some of the forehead, creating a more balanced look.
  • The Quiff: The quiff is a popular hairstyle that works well for guys with big foreheads. This style involves pushing the hair up and back, creating volume and height at the top of the head.
  • The Side Part: If you prefer a more classic look, a side part can be a great option. This style involves parting the hair on one side and combing it over to cover up part of the forehead.
  • The Buzzcut: If you’re looking for a low-maintenance option, a buzzcut can work well for guys with big foreheads. This style involves cutting the hair very short all over, creating a clean and simple look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new ‘do:

  • Regular trims: Even if you have a longer hairstyle, regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture, such as a lightweight styling cream for fine hair or a heavier pomade for thicker hair.
  • Don’t over-wash: Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

FAQ About Hairstyles for Big Forehead Guys

Q: Can I still wear a short hairstyle if I have a big forehead?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles can work well for guys with big foreheads, especially if you go for a textured or layered look.

Q: Will a fringe make my forehead look smaller?

A: Yes, a fringe or bangs can help to cover up some of your forehead and create a more balanced look.

Q: What if I don’t like how my hairstyle looks?

A: If you’re not happy with your hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist about making changes or trying a different style.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair over time. Use a heat protectant spray and try to limit your use of these tools.
  • Get enough sleep: Getting enough sleep can help to reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you’re sure to find the perfect look to flatter your big forehead. Remember to maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and the right products, and take care of your hair with a healthy diet and protective measures against heat damage. Happy styling!
