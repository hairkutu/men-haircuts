Can You Get Layers With Curly Hair

Written by Bardi May 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s voluminous and full of texture. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you’re considering getting layers in your curly hair, you may be wondering if it’s even possible. The good news is that yes, you can get layers with curly hair. In this article, we’ll explore how to get layers in curly hair, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Getting layers in curly hair can enhance the natural texture and volume of your hair. Layers can also add movement and shape to your curls, making them look more defined and bouncy. However, it’s important to consider your hair type and face shape before getting layers. Layers may not be suitable for everyone, and it’s best to consult with a hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

How to Get Layers in Curly Hair

The process of getting layers in curly hair is similar to getting layers in straight hair. However, there are a few key differences to keep in mind. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

It’s important to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with curly hair. They can assess your hair type and face shape to determine the best layering technique for you.

Step 2: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Once you’ve washed your hair, detangle it using a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair in layers, starting from the bottom and working their way up. They may use a combination of scissors and a razor to create different layers and textures.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance your curls, or you can straighten your hair using a flat iron for a different look.

Tips for Getting Layers in Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting layers in curly hair:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience working with curly hair.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type before getting layers.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to wash and condition your hair.
  • Detangle your hair before cutting it to ensure an even cut.
  • Style your hair as desired to enhance your curls or create a different look.

FAQs

Q: Will layers make my curls look better?

A: Yes, layers can enhance the natural texture and volume of your curls, making them look more defined and bouncy.

Q: Will layers make my hair frizzier?

A: It depends on your hair type and the layering technique used. If your hair tends to frizz easily, your hairstylist may use a razor to create softer layers that blend together.

Q: Can I get layers if I have short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get layers in short curly hair. Your hairstylist will use a different layering technique to create volume and shape.

Hair Care for Curly Hair with Layers

Once you’ve gotten layers in your curly hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are a few tips:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

Getting layers in curly hair can be a great way to enhance your natural texture and volume. With the right hairstylist and hair care routine, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy head of hair.
