Short Spiky Haircuts For Women Edgy And Stylish

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and daring way to revamp your hairstyle, short spiky haircuts for women may be just what you need. This haircut style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's not difficult to see why. Short spiky haircuts are edgy, stylish, and low-maintenance, making them perfect for women who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about short spiky haircuts for women, including how to get the look, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Tips for Styling
  • Hair Care Advice
  • FAQ

How to Get the Look

Getting a short spiky haircut is a bold move, but it's also quite easy to achieve. The first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. When you're in the stylist's chair, be sure to communicate your desired length and the level of spikiness you want. Your stylist will then use clippers or scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.

Once your hair is cut, the stylist will then use texturizing shears or a razor to create the spiky texture. This process involves cutting small sections of hair at different lengths to create a choppy, edgy effect. The stylist may also use products like pomade or wax to help hold the spikes in place.

Types of Short Spiky Haircuts

There are several different types of short spiky haircuts that you can choose from, depending on your personal style and preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Pixie cut with spiky layers
  • Faux hawk with high spikes
  • Bowl cut with choppy layers
  • Cropped cut with textured spikes

Tips for Styling

Styling short spiky haircuts is relatively easy, but there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a small amount of pomade or wax to create and hold the spikes in place.
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of the spikes to add volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different lengths and levels of spikiness to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care Advice

Like all haircuts, short spiky haircuts require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles and lead to breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and texture of your spiky haircut.

FAQ

Q: Will a short spiky haircut work with my face shape?

A: Short spiky haircuts can work with a variety of face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Talk to your stylist about which type of short spiky haircut will work best for your face shape.

Q: Do I need to have naturally straight hair to get a short spiky haircut?

A: No, you don't need to have naturally straight hair to get a short spiky haircut. However, you may need to use a straightening iron or other styling tools to achieve the desired texture and shape.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with a short spiky haircut?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preferences, but most people with short spiky haircuts can wash their hair every 2-3 days.

In conclusion, short spiky haircuts for women are a bold and edgy way to update your look. Whether you opt for a pixie cut with spiky layers or a faux hawk with high spikes, there are plenty of options to choose from. By following these styling and hair care tips, you can keep your short spiky haircut looking fresh and stylish.
