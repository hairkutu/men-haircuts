Is Crimping Your Hair In Style

Written by Frank Mar 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to switch things up? Crimping your hair may be the perfect solution for you! In this article, we will discuss whether or not crimping your hair is in style, how to crimp your hair, and tips for maintaining your crimped hairstyle.

Description

Crimping is a technique that involves creating small, zigzagging waves in your hair. It was a popular hairstyle in the 1980s and has recently made a comeback in the fashion world. Crimping your hair is a great way to add texture and volume to your hair, and it can be done on both short and long hair.

How to Crimp Your Hair

Before crimping your hair, it's important to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections, then use a crimping iron to create the zigzag waves. Clamp the iron onto each section of hair, hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is crimped. To finish off the look, you can add a hair accessory such as a headband or hair clips.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1:

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 2:

Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 3:

Section your hair into small sections.

Step 4:

Take one section of hair and clamp the crimping iron onto it. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step 5:

Repeat the process on all sections of hair until your entire head is crimped.

Step 6:

Add a hair accessory such as a headband or hair clips to finish off the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Crimped Hairstyle

To maintain your crimped hairstyle, it's important to avoid washing your hair too often. Over-washing can cause the crimped waves to loosen and lose their texture. Instead, try using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh. You can also use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is crimping your hair damaging?

A: Crimping your hair can be damaging if done too frequently or with a high heat setting on your crimping iron. It's important to use a heat protectant spray before crimping your hair and to avoid using the crimping iron on wet hair.

Q: Can I crimp my hair if it's already curly or wavy?

A: Yes, you can still crimp your hair if it's already curly or wavy. Crimping will add even more texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially after crimping it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair and cause it to lose its texture and volume.

Conclusion

Crimping your hair is a fun and trendy hairstyle that can add texture and volume to your hair. By following our step-by-step guide and tips for maintaining your crimped hairstyle, you can achieve the perfect look that's both stylish and healthy for your hair.
