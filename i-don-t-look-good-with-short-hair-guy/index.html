I Don t Look Good With Short Hair Guy A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Aprilia Aug 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you one of the many men out there who thinks they don't look good with short hair? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many men feel like short hair doesn't suit them, but the truth is that there's a perfect hairstyle for everyone. In this guide, we'll help you find the right haircut to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Description

Short hair can be a tricky thing to get right, especially if you're not used to it. It can be easy to feel like your hair is too thin, your head is too round, or that you just don't have the right face shape for short hair. However, the truth is that there's a perfect cut out there for everyone, no matter what your hair type or face shape may be. Whether you want a classic short back and sides, a longer fringe, or a textured crop, we'll help you find the right style for you.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for you is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes suit different haircuts, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. If you have a round face, for example, a longer fringe or textured crop can help to balance out your features. If you have a square jawline, a short back and sides can help to accentuate your strong features.

Next, think about your hair type. If you have thick, wavy hair, a textured crop or messy fringe can help to show off your natural texture. If you have thin hair, a shorter cut can help to add volume and body. It's also important to consider your personal style. Do you prefer a more classic, timeless look, or do you like to experiment with bolder, trendier styles?

Step by step

1. Consider your face shape: Different face shapes suit different haircuts, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your features.

2. Think about your hair type: Thick, wavy hair suits textured crops and messy fringes, while thin hair can benefit from shorter cuts.

3. Consider your personal style: Classic styles like short back and sides are timeless, while more modern styles like the textured crop can be bolder and trendier.

4. Look for inspiration: Browse magazines, blogs, and social media for inspiration and ideas.

5. Consult a professional: A hairstylist can help you choose the right cut for your face shape and hair type, and can also give you tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the right one.

- Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain your chosen style.

- Use hair products like pomade, wax, or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair suit everyone?

A: Yes! There's a perfect haircut out there for everyone, no matter what your face shape or hair type may be.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your chosen style and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, a trim every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain your style.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair, especially if you're sporting a shorter cut. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. If you're using products like gel or wax, be sure to wash them out thoroughly at the end of the day to avoid buildup and irritation.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try out new styles until you find the one that's right for you. Good luck!
