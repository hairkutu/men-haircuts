How To Cut Your Own Hair Medium Short

Written by William Mar 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the hair salon? Learning how to cut your own hair medium short can save you both. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and professional look without leaving your home. In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect medium-short haircut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Medium Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

How to Cut Your Own Hair Medium Short

Before we start cutting, it is important to have the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers with different guards
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Once you have gathered your tools, follow these steps to cut your own hair medium short:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section. Use the clippers with a #3 guard to trim the hair from the nape of your neck to the top of your ears.
  4. Switch to a #2 guard and trim the hair from the top of your ears to the crown of your head.
  5. Blend the two lengths by using the clippers without a guard or scissors. Make sure to blend the hair evenly.
  6. Move to the side sections. Use the clippers with a #3 guard to trim the hair from the bottom of your sideburns to the top of your ears.
  7. Switch to a #2 guard and trim the hair from the top of your ears to the temple.
  8. Blend the two lengths by using the clippers without a guard or scissors. Make sure to blend the hair evenly.
  9. Move to the top section. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Start at the front and work your way to the back.
  10. Blend the top section with the sides and back by using the scissors to cut at a 45-degree angle. This will create a seamless transition between the different sections.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter. You can always cut more, but you cannot add hair back.
  • Use a mirror or ask someone to help you with the back section.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated. Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but it is worth it in the end.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my own hair with just scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with just scissors. However, it may be more challenging to achieve an even and precise cut without clippers.

How often should I cut my own hair?

It depends on your hair growth and maintenance. On average, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my own hair?

Don't panic! Hair grows back, and mistakes can be fixed. If you are unsure, take a break and come back to it later. You can also seek help from a professional stylist.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent damage.
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own hair medium short at home. Not only will you save money and time, but you will also have the satisfaction of achieving a professional and stylish look.
