How To Do A Pineapple Bun For Long Curly Hair

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your long curly hair getting in your face all day? A pineapple bun might just be the solution you’ve been looking for. This trendy hairstyle is not only practical but also stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to do a pineapple bun for long curly hair, step by step. Here’s a table of content to help you navigate the article:

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do a Pineapple Bun for Long Curly Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for a Perfect Pineapple Bun
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to achieve the perfect pineapple bun:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, you'll need to gather some supplies. These include a hairbrush, a hair tie, bobby pins, and a silk scarf (optional).

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Using a hairbrush, gently brush your hair to remove any tangles. If your hair is too curly, you can use your fingers to detangle it.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Flip your head upside down and gather all of your hair at the top of your head. Use your hands to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Secure with a Hair Tie

Using a hair tie, secure your hair into a high ponytail. Make sure it’s not too tight as this can cause breakage.

Step 5: Create the Pineapple

Take the hair tie and create a small loop with your hair, leaving the ends sticking out. This will create the pineapple shape.

Step 6: Secure with Bobby Pins

Using bobby pins, secure the ends of your hair around the base of the pineapple. Use as many bobby pins as necessary to keep it in place.

Step 7: Optional Silk Scarf

If you want to add an extra touch of luxury to your pineapple bun, wrap a silk scarf around the base of the ponytail. This will also help to protect your hair while you sleep.

Tips for a Perfect Pineapple Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pineapple bun:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before creating the pineapple bun
  • Use a hair tie that’s gentle on your hair, like a scrunchie or a spiral hair tie
  • Don’t pull your hair too tightly as this can cause breakage
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a seamless look
  • Experiment with different silk scarf colors and patterns to add some personality to your pineapple bun

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the pineapple bun:

Is the pineapple bun suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the pineapple bun can be done on all hair types, but it’s particularly great for long curly hair.

How long does it take to do a pineapple bun?

A pineapple bun can be done in just a few minutes, making it a great option for those who are short on time.

Can I sleep with a pineapple bun?

Yes, you can sleep with a pineapple bun. In fact, it’s a great way to protect your curls while you sleep.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your long curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo that’s free of sulfates and parabens
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz
  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet

Now that you know how to do a pineapple bun for long curly hair, you can rock this stylish and practical hairstyle anytime. Remember to take care of your hair by following the tips we’ve provided, and you’ll have healthy and beautiful curls for years to come.
