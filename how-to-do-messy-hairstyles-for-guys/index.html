How To Do Messy Hairstyles For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Do Messy Hairstyles for Guys: A Step-by-Step Guide | 2023 Hair Care Tips and FAQ

If you're looking for an easy and low-maintenance hairstyle, messy hairstyles are the way to go. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to do messy hairstyles for guys that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going for a casual look or a more formal one, these hairstyles are sure to impress.

How to Do Messy Hairstyles for Guys

Messy hairstyles are all about creating a relaxed and effortless look. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair instead of a comb or brush.
  • Don't worry about making your hairstyle look perfect. Messy hairstyles are meant to be imperfect and relaxed.

Popular Messy Hairstyles for Guys

Here are some popular messy hairstyles for guys:

  • The Tousled Look
  • The Bedhead Look
  • The Messy Bun
  • The Side-Swept Look

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to do a messy hairstyle for guys:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's slightly damp.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to your hair and work it in with your fingers.
  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  4. For a messy bun, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it in a loose bun.
  5. For a side-swept look, use your fingers to sweep your hair to one side.
  6. For a bedhead look, use your fingers to create random spikes and tousles in your hair.
  7. Don't worry about making your hairstyle look perfect. The key to a messy hairstyle is imperfection.

And that's it! With these simple steps, you can create the perfect messy hairstyle for any occasion.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hairstyle:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair every day. Messy hairstyles look better when your hair has a little bit of natural oil in it.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using a comb or brush. Use your fingers to style your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I do a messy hairstyle with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Messy hairstyles work great with short hair. Just make sure to use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: How do I make my messy hairstyle last all day?

A: Use a strong-hold texturizing spray or pomade to keep your hair in place. You can also use hairspray to set your hairstyle.

Q: Can I do a messy hairstyle for a formal occasion?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to keep your hair looking neat and avoid overdoing it with the tousling and volume.

Hair Care for Messy Hairstyles

Here are some tips for hair care when it comes to messy hairstyles:

  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from texturizing sprays and pomades.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. They can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a messy hairstyle.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create the perfect messy hairstyle for any occasion. Give it a try and see how easy it is to achieve a relaxed and effortless look.
