Hairstyles That Suit A Pear Shaped Face

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your pear shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we'll explore the different hairstyles that are perfect for this face shape. From how to identify a pear shaped face to the best haircuts and styles, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Identifying a Pear Shaped Face

A pear shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and wide, prominent cheekbones and jawline. The jawline is usually wider than the forehead and can give the appearance of a "pear" shape. If you have a pear shaped face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features and highlights your best assets.

Best Haircuts for Pear Shaped Faces

When it comes to haircuts for pear shaped faces, you want to choose styles that add volume to the top of your face and balance out the width at the bottom. The following haircuts are perfect for pear shaped faces:

Short Bob

A short bob with layers can add volume and balance out your features. The layers should be shorter at the top and longer at the bottom to create a balance. This style can be worn straight or with loose waves for added volume.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, adding layers can create volume at the top of your face. The layers should start at the chin and continue to the ends of your hair. You can style this look with loose waves or straight hair.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can balance out your features and add volume to the top of your face. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. You can style this look with a side part or messy waves for added volume.

Hairstyles that Suit a Pear Shaped Face

In addition to haircuts, there are several hairstyles that are perfect for pear shaped faces:

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can balance out the width of your jawline and add volume to the top of your face. The bangs should be longer at the sides and shorter in the middle to create a balance.

Updos

Updos can create volume at the top of your face and showcase your best assets. The best updos for pear shaped faces are those that are swept to the side or have a lot of volume at the top.

Loose Waves

Loose waves can add volume to the top of your face and balance out your features. To create loose waves, use a curling iron or hot rollers and brush out the curls for a natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect haircut and hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Consider getting a professional blowout to add volume to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colors are best for pear shaped faces?

Warm hair colors like caramel, honey, and golden blonde can complement a pear shaped face. Avoid cool hair colors like ash blonde or platinum as they can make your jawline appear wider.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part with a pear shaped face?

A middle part can make a pear shaped face appear wider. Consider wearing your hair in a side part to balance out your features.

Hair Care for Pear Shaped Faces

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining your hairstyle and keeping your hair healthy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to suit your pear shaped face. Remember to consider your hair type, face shape, and personal style when choosing a haircut and hairstyle. Happy styling!
