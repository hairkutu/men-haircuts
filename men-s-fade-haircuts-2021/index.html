Men s Fade Haircuts 2021 The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a fade might be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we'll discuss the most popular men's fade haircuts in 2021, how to get them, and how to take care of them. Let's dive in!

What is a Fade Haircut?

A fade haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that features a gradual transition from short to long hair. The hair is cut short on the sides and back and gradually fades into longer hair on top. Fades can be low, medium, or high, depending on how high the fade goes up the head.

Types of Fade Haircuts

There are several types of fade haircuts to choose from, including:

Low Fade

A low fade features a gradual transition from short to long hair that usually stops at the temples. This is a great option for guys who want a subtle look.

Mid Fade

A mid fade features a more noticeable transition from short to long hair that usually stops around the ear. This is a good option for guys who want a slightly more dramatic look.

High Fade

A high fade features a very noticeable transition from short to long hair that usually stops at the top of the head. This is a great option for guys who want a bold, edgy look.

Bald Fade

A bald fade, also known as a skin fade, features hair that gradually fades into the skin. This is a great option for guys who want a very clean, polished look.

How to Get a Fade Haircut

If you're interested in getting a fade haircut, here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Decide on the type of fade you want (low, mid, high, or bald).
  2. Choose the length you want on top.
  3. Show your barber a picture of the haircut you want.
  4. Ask your barber to start with a longer guard and gradually work their way down.
  5. Make sure your barber blends the fade into your hair on top.
  6. Ask your barber to clean up the edges and trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Maintaining a Fade Haircut

To keep your fade looking fresh, here are some tips you should follow:

  • Get regular haircuts every 2-4 weeks.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a matte or low-shine product for a natural look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make your fade look less defined.
  • If you have a high or bald fade, make sure to shave the hair on the back of your neck regularly to keep it looking clean.

FAQ

Q: How long does a fade last?

A: A fade typically lasts 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Q: Can I get a fade with curly hair?

A: Yes, fades can look great on curly hair! Just make sure to find a barber who has experience cutting curly hair.

Q: Can I get a fade if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, a fade can actually be a good option for guys with thinning hair, as it can create the illusion of thicker hair on top.

Hair Care Tips for Fade Haircuts

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a fade haircut:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make your fade look less defined.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull.
  • If you have dandruff, use a shampoo specifically designed for dandruff to keep your scalp healthy.

By following these tips and getting regular haircuts, you can keep your fade looking fresh and stylish all year long.
