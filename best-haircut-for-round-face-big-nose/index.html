Best Haircut For Round Face Big Nose

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that will flatter your round face and big nose? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best haircuts that will complement your features and enhance your beauty. In this blog post, we will discuss how to choose the best haircut for round face big nose, step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the look, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to maintain your new hairstyle.

Description

Having a round face and big nose can be challenging when it comes to choosing the right haircut. You want to find a hairstyle that will balance out your features and make you feel confident. A good haircut can do wonders for your appearance and boost your self-esteem.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Face Big Nose

The best way to choose the right haircut for your face shape and nose size is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can provide you with expert advice on what hairstyles will suit your face and personal style. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face, like a high ponytail or an updo.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs or a bob that ends at your jawline.
  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers, as they can create movement and texture to your hair.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe, as it can draw attention away from your nose.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Once you have chosen the perfect haircut, here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair according to your chosen haircut.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to add shine and tame any frizz.
  6. Finish off with hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use heat protectant spray before using any styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV hair protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for round face big nose:

What is the best haircut for a round face with a big nose?

The best haircut for a round face with a big nose is one that adds height to your face and has layers that create movement and texture to your hair. A side-swept fringe can also draw attention away from your nose.

Can a bob haircut work for a round face with a big nose?

A bob haircut that ends at your jawline can add width to your face and emphasize your nose. It's best to choose a bob that is longer and has layers.

What hair color is best for a round face with a big nose?

The best hair color for a round face with a big nose is one that complements your skin tone and eye color. Highlights or lowlights can add dimension to your hair and draw attention away from your nose.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important to keep your new haircut looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with lukewarm water to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as it can damage your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.

Now that you know the best haircut for round face big nose, go ahead and book that salon appointment or grab your styling tools and get to work. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and have fun experimenting with different looks.
