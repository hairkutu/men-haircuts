How To Undercut Your Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and edgy? If so, an undercut might be just what you need. The undercut is a popular hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of your head is shaved or cut very short while leaving the top long. This creates a bold and stylish look that is perfect for both men and women. In this article, we will show you how to undercut your own hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Table of Contents

  • How to Undercut Your Own Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Undercutting Your Own Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your Undercut

How to Undercut Your Own Hair

Before we get started, it's important to gather the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Now that you have everything you need, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section off the top of your hair using hair ties or clips. This will help you focus on the sides and back.

Step 3: Using your clippers, start at the bottom of your head and work your way up. Use a longer guard for the first pass and then switch to a shorter guard for a closer cut. Be sure to blend the hair between the long and short sections to create a smooth transition.

Step 4: Once you have finished the sides and back, remove the hair ties or clips and trim the top of your hair to your desired length. Use scissors to cut any stray hairs and create a clean edge.

Step 5: Style your undercut however you like. You can slick it back, add volume, or leave it natural. Get creative!

Tips for Undercutting Your Own Hair

Undercutting your own hair can be a bit tricky, so here are some tips to help you out:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always go shorter, but you can't go back once you've cut too much.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to spend a little extra time to get it right than to have to fix a mistake later.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. It's always better to have someone else check your work to make sure it looks good.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I undercut my own hair if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, it might be easier to get a professional to do it for you if you have very long hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my undercut touched up?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you keep it. Generally, you should get it touched up every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my undercut a different color?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's best to get it done professionally to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care for Your Undercut

Now that you have your new undercut, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your undercut.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat or chemicals, as this can damage it.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you should be able to undercut your own hair with confidence. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with your new style!
