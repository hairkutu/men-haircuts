How To Air Dry Thick Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick and frizzy hair, you know the struggle of trying to style it. Blow-drying can take forever, and using a straightener can damage your hair over time. That's why air-drying is a great option for those with thick frizzy hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to air dry your hair to achieve a natural and beautiful look.

How to Air Dry Thick Frizzy Hair

Air drying thick frizzy hair can seem like a daunting task, but with the right techniques and products, it can be easy and effective. Here are the basic steps:

1. Start with clean hair

Before air drying, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help tame frizz and nourish your hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it hydrated. This will also help prevent frizz and make your hair easier to work with.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will also help distribute the leave-in conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

4. Let your hair air dry

Now it's time to let your hair air dry. Avoid touching or brushing your hair as it dries, as this can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair hang loose and allow it to dry naturally.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to air drying your thick frizzy hair:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help tame frizz and nourish your hair.

2. Towel dry your hair.

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Be careful not to rub your hair too vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner.

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle your hair and keep it hydrated. This will also help prevent frizz and make your hair easier to work with.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb.

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will also help distribute the leave-in conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

5. Let your hair air dry.

Now it's time to let your hair air dry. Avoid touching or brushing your hair as it dries, as this can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair hang loose and allow it to dry naturally.

Tips for Air Drying Thick Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you air dry your thick frizzy hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to gently dry your hair.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer or any other heat styling tools.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair after it dries to add shine and tame frizz.
  • If you need to touch up your hair after it dries, use a curling wand or straightener on low heat.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to air dry thick frizzy hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours to air dry thick frizzy hair, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I use a diffuser to air dry my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help air dry your hair. This can help reduce frizz and add volume to your hair.

Q: How often should I air dry my hair?

A: You can air dry your hair as often as you'd like. However, it's important to also use other styling methods to switch up your look and prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you manage your thick frizzy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment to help smooth and tame frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Air drying your thick frizzy hair can be a great way to achieve a natural and beautiful look without damaging your hair. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a frizz-free and healthy-looking mane. Happy air drying!
