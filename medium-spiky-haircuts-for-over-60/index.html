Medium Spiky Haircuts For Over 60 How To Rock It

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you in your 60s and looking for a fresh new hairstyle? Look no further than the medium spiky haircut! This trendy and youthful look is perfect for those who want to add a bit of edge to their style. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the medium spiky haircut for over 60, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

The medium spiky haircut is a style that features short to medium length hair that is styled in a spiky manner. This look is achieved by using a styling product, such as gel or wax, to create spikes that stand up from the head. The style is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. It is a great option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

How to Achieve the Medium Spiky Haircut

The first step in achieving the medium spiky haircut is to choose the right length for your hair. This will depend on your hair type and face shape. Generally, hair that falls between the chin and shoulders is the ideal length for this style.

Once you have the right length, you will need to choose a styling product that works for your hair. Gel or wax will work best for this style, as they provide hold and control. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and use your fingers to create spikes that stand up from the head. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more polished look.

Step by Step Guide

If you're new to the medium spiky haircut, here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of gel or wax to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to create spikes that stand up from the head.
  4. If you want a more polished look, use a comb or brush to shape the spikes.
  5. Finish with hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Medium Spiky Haircut

To keep your medium spiky haircut looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that provides hold and control.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

1. Is the medium spiky haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the medium spiky haircut can be customized to suit any hair type.

2. Will the medium spiky haircut make me look younger?

The medium spiky haircut can add a youthful edge to your style, but it is not a magical fountain of youth.

3. How long does it take to style the medium spiky haircut?

The amount of time it takes to style the medium spiky haircut will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, it should take no longer than 10-15 minutes.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use high-quality products that are gentle on your hair. Look for products that are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the medium spiky haircut is a trendy and youthful look that is perfect for those in their 60s who want to add some edge to their style. With the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve this look in no time. Give it a try and see how it can transform your look!
