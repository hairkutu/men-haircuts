Hairstyles for Thin Wavy Hair Over 60

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and more prone to breakage. This can be especially difficult for women over 60 who have naturally wavy hair. However, with the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can still rock beautiful waves and look your best. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for thin wavy hair over 60 and share some tips on how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thin Wavy Hair Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Taking Care of Thin Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Thin Wavy Hair

How to Style Thin Wavy Hair Over 60

The key to styling thin wavy hair over 60 is to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. Layers are a great option, as they can give your hair more movement and body. You can also consider getting a bob, which is a classic hairstyle that works well for women of all ages.

When it comes to styling your hair, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Wavy Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thin wavy hair over 60:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your hair with your hands as you go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or rollers, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Once you've curled all of your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate the curls and create more texture.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Taking Care of Thin Wavy Hair

To keep your thin wavy hair looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for thin wavy hair over 60?

The best hairstyles for thin wavy hair over 60 are those that add volume and texture to your hair. Layers and bobs are both great options.

What is the best way to style thin wavy hair?

The best way to style thin wavy hair is to use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves, and to use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.

How can I prevent my thin wavy hair from looking flat?

To prevent your thin wavy hair from looking flat, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care Routine for Thin Wavy Hair

Here's a hair care routine to follow if you have thin wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your hair with your hands as you go.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to separate the curls and create more texture.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.


With these tips and hairstyles, you can embrace your thin wavy hair over 60 and look your best. Remember to take care of your hair with a good hair care routine, and avoid using too many heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down. Follow these tips, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair that looks great at any age!
