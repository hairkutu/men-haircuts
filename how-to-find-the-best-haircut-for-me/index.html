How To Find The Best Haircut For Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut but don't know where to start, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you find the best haircut for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle refresh, we've got you covered.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can find the perfect haircut, you need to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that will complement your features and enhance your natural beauty. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, start by pulling your hair back off your face and standing in front of a mirror. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheeks and a rounded chin, with the width and length of the face being roughly equal.
  • Square: Prominent jaw and forehead, with the face being roughly as wide as it is long.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and chin, with the cheekbones being the widest part of the face.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin.
  • Oblong: Long and narrow face, with the forehead, cheekbones, and chin being roughly equal in width.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will flatter your features.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that will complement it. Here are some tips to help you choose the right style:

  • Oval: Almost any haircut will work with an oval face shape, so choose a style that reflects your personal taste and style.
  • Round: Choose a haircut that adds height to the top of your head and creates angles around your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut.
  • Square: Soften the angles of your face with a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Diamond: Add width to your forehead and chin with a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Heart: Balance the width of your forehead and cheekbones with a side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob.
  • Oblong: Create the illusion of width with a layered haircut that adds volume around your face.

Remember, these are just guidelines, and ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know your face shape and have some ideas for haircuts that will flatter your features, it's time to start your search. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect haircut:

  1. Start by browsing through magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram for inspiration.
  2. Save photos of haircuts that catch your eye and bring them to your stylist.
  3. Ask your stylist for their opinion on which haircuts will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  4. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different haircuts will look on you.
  6. Book a consultation appointment with your stylist to discuss your options and get their expert advice.
  7. Take the plunge and get your new haircut!

Remember that communication is key when it comes to getting the perfect haircut. Be honest with your stylist about your preferences, and don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the results.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new 'do:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are tailored to your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use heat protectant products when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have when trying to find the best haircut for their face shape:

Can I still get a haircut that doesn't flatter my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions, and ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, focus on the features you want to highlight and choose a haircut that flatters them.

What if I don't know how to describe the haircut I want?

Bring in photos of haircuts you like or use descriptive words like "layered," "textured," or "blunt" to help your stylist understand what you're looking for.

Conclusion

Finding the best haircut for your face shape and personal style can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to a new and improved 'do. Remember to communicate openly with your stylist, take good care of your hair, and most importantly, choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
