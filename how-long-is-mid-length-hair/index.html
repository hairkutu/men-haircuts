How Long Is Mid Length Hair

Written by Frank Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to hair length, there are a few different categories: short, medium, and long. But what exactly do these categories mean in terms of actual length? In this article, we'll be focusing specifically on mid length hair and answering the question: how long is mid length hair?

Table of Contents

  1. Definition of Mid Length Hair
  2. How to Measure Mid Length Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Measuring Mid Length Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Definition of Mid Length Hair

Mid length hair is typically defined as hair that falls anywhere between the chin and the shoulders. This can vary slightly depending on factors such as hair texture and face shape, but in general, mid length hair falls in the 10-14 inch range.

Mid length hair is a popular choice for many people because it offers a good balance between the ease of short hair and the versatility of long hair. It's long enough to be styled in a variety of ways, but short enough to be low maintenance and easy to care for.

How to Measure Mid Length Hair

If you're unsure whether your hair falls into the mid length category, the easiest way to determine its length is to measure it. To do this, you'll need a tape measure and a mirror.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start measuring, make sure your hair is dry and styled the way you normally wear it. This will give you the most accurate measurement.

Step 2: Find Your Starting Point

Next, find the point on your head where you want to start measuring. For mid length hair, this is typically at the center of your chin.

Step 3: Measure Your Hair

Using the tape measure, measure the length of your hair from the starting point to the bottom. Make sure the tape measure is straight and level.

Step-by-Step Guide to Measuring Mid Length Hair

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps to measure your mid length hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Brush your hair so it's smooth and tangle-free.
  3. Stand in front of a mirror with the tape measure in hand.
  4. Find the center of your chin and place the beginning of the tape measure at that point.
  5. Hold the tape measure straight down the length of your hair.
  6. Read the measurement at the bottom of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hair

Once you've determined that your hair is mid length, you might be wondering how to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Is mid length hair the same as shoulder length hair?

A: Not necessarily. While mid length hair can fall anywhere between the chin and the shoulders, shoulder length hair specifically refers to hair that falls right at the shoulders.

Q: Can mid length hair be styled in an updo?

A: Yes! Mid length hair is versatile enough to be styled in a variety of ways, including updos.

Q: Is mid length hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Generally speaking, yes. However, the exact length and style of mid length hair that works best for you may depend on your face shape and other factors.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your mid length hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Be gentle when towel-drying your hair, as rough handling can cause damage.

Now that you know how long mid length hair is and how to take care of it, you're ready to embrace this versatile and stylish hair length!
