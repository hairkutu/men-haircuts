How To Style Long Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair doesn’t have to be reserved for the young. In fact, many women over 50 are embracing their long locks and finding new ways to style it. Whether you’re looking to create a sleek and sophisticated look or want to add some volume and texture to your hair, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share some of our favorite tips and tricks for styling long hair over 50.

How to Style Long Hair Over 50

Step 1: Find the Right Cut

The first step to styling long hair over 50 is finding the right cut. This may mean getting a trim to remove split ends or opting for layers to add volume and texture. Your hairstylist can help you determine the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make all the difference when it comes to styling long hair over 50. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, whether that’s fine, thick, curly, or straight. You may also want to consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Long hair offers a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. You can wear it straight and sleek, add some waves or curls, or try a half-up, half-down style. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Over 50

Tip 1: Use Hot Rollers

If you’re looking to add some volume and texture to your long hair, hot rollers are a great tool to have on hand. Simply roll your hair around the rollers and let them cool before removing. You’ll be left with bouncy, voluminous curls that are perfect for any occasion.

Tip 2: Try a Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great way to keep long hair out of your face while still looking stylish. To create the look, simply pull your hair back into a loose bun and use bobby pins to secure it in place. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.

Tip 3: Add Some Highlights

If you’re looking to add some dimension to your long hair, consider adding some highlights. This can help break up a solid color and add some depth to your locks. Talk to your hairstylist about the best color options for your hair type and skin tone.

FAQ About Styling Long Hair Over 50

Q: Is it okay to have long hair over 50?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can be flattering at any age, as long as it’s well-maintained and styled appropriately.

Q: What are some easy hairstyles for long hair over 50?

A: Some easy hairstyles for long hair over 50 include a simple ponytail, a messy bun, or loose waves.

Q: How can I add volume to my long hair?

A: You can add volume to your long hair by using hot rollers, teasing the roots, or opting for layers when getting a haircut.

Hair Care for Long Hair Over 50

When it comes to maintaining long hair over 50, it’s important to take good care of your locks. This means using the right hair products, getting regular trims to prevent split ends, and avoiding heat styling tools whenever possible. You may also want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Styling long hair over 50 can be fun and rewarding. With the right cut, products, and styling techniques, you can achieve a look that’s both stylish and age-appropriate. So go ahead and embrace your long locks – you deserve to feel beautiful at any age!
