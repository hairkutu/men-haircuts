Which Guard Cuts The Most Hair

Written by Daniel May 19, 2023 · 6 min read
Which guard cuts the most hair?

In this article, we will explore the different types of guards that are used in hair clippers and determine which guard cuts the most hair. We will also provide tips on how to use the guard effectively and how to take care of your hair after using the guard.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Guards are attachments that are placed on hair clippers to control the length of the hair that is cut. There are many different types of guards available, with each guard cutting a different length of hair. The guard that cuts the most hair is the #1 guard, which cuts hair to a length of ⅛ inch or 3mm. This guard is perfect for those who want a very short haircut or buzz cut.

How to

Using a guard is very easy. Simply attach the guard to the clipper and start cutting your hair. Make sure to move the clipper in the direction of hair growth and use a comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades. It is important to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter haircut.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use a guard:

  1. Attach the guard to the clipper.
  2. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.
  3. Move the clipper in the direction of hair growth.
  4. Use a comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades.
  5. Trim the hair evenly.
  6. Check for any missed spots and trim as needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you use the guard effectively:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use a comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades.
  • Move the clipper in the direction of hair growth.
  • Trim the hair evenly.
  • Check for any missed spots and trim as needed.

FAQ

What is the difference between guards?

The difference between guards is the length of hair that they cut. The #1 guard cuts hair to a length of ⅛ inch or 3mm, while the #8 guard cuts hair to a length of 1 inch or 25mm.

Can I use the #1 guard on long hair?

No, the #1 guard is not recommended for long hair. It is designed to cut very short hair or for a buzz cut.

Do I need to oil my hair clippers?

Yes, it is important to oil your hair clippers after each use. This will help to keep the blades sharp and prevent rusting.

Hair Care

After using the #1 guard, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it soft.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length.

Now that you know which guard cuts the most hair, you can confidently use it to achieve the haircut that you want. Remember to follow the tips and take care of your hair after using the guard.
