How Do You Know If A Hair Color Suits You

Written by Luffy Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re considering changing your hair color, the first question that comes to mind is, “Will this color suit me?” Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it’s not impossible. In this blog post, we’ll break down how to know if a hair color suits you and provide you with some tips on how to choose the best color for your skin tone and hair type.

Table of Contents

  • How To Know If A Hair Color Suits You
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips After Coloring

How To Know If A Hair Color Suits You

There are a few factors to consider when choosing the right hair color for you:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have warm undertones, you should choose warm hair colors like golden or honey blonde, auburn, or warm brown shades. If you have cool undertones, you should opt for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or black. Neutral skin tones can pull off any hair color.

Eye Color

Your eye color can also help determine the best hair color for you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like honey blonde or auburn will complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colors like ash brown or black will make your eyes pop.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have dark hair, it’s best to stick with shades that are only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color. If you have light hair, you can go a little bolder with your color choices.

Step-By-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine if a hair color suits you:

  1. Identify your skin tone.
  2. Determine your eye color.
  3. Consider your natural hair color.
  4. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  5. Try on a wig or use a virtual hair color app to see how the color looks on you.
  6. Consult with a professional stylist to ensure the color will work with your hair type and texture.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider the maintenance involved in maintaining the color.
  • Choose a color that complements your lifestyle.
  • Start with a semi-permanent color if you’re unsure about committing to a permanent color.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with highlights or lowlights.
  • Consider the season and your wardrobe when choosing a hair color.

FAQs

How can I tell if a hair color will suit me without trying it on?

You can use a virtual hair color app or try on a wig to see how the color will look on you.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it’s best to opt for a natural hair dye or consult with a professional stylist who uses products that are safe for sensitive skin.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the type of hair color you choose. Permanent hair color needs to be touched up every four to six weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to six weeks.

Hair Care Tips After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it’s essential to take care of it properly to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hair color that suits you and enhances your natural beauty.
