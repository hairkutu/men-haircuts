How To Cut Curly Wavy Hair At Home

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Curly wavy hair can be challenging to manage, especially when it comes to cutting it. Going to the salon can get expensive, and sometimes, you just want to take matters into your own hands. Cutting curly wavy hair at home may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this article, we'll go over how to cut curly wavy hair at home, step by step.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Wavy Hair at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Wavy Hair

How to Cut Curly Wavy Hair at Home

The first step in cutting curly wavy hair at home is to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.

Next, wash and condition your hair as you normally would and let it air dry or dry it with a diffuser. This will help you see your natural curl pattern and make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and then divide it into four sections, two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section of hair out of the way.

Step 2: Start with the Back

Begin by unclipping the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.

Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting straight across the hair. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Move to the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the side sections. Start by unclipping one of the side sections and combing it straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.

Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting at an angle to create layers. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.

Step 4: Finish with the Front

Finally, unclip the front sections of your hair and comb them straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.

Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting at an angle to create layers. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to cutting curly wavy hair at home:

  1. Gather the right tools: sharp hair cutting scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.
  2. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would and let it air dry or dry it with a diffuser.
  3. Section your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.
  4. Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.
  5. Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting straight across the hair. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.
  6. Move on to the side sections. Start by unclipping one of the side sections and combing it straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.
  7. Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting at an angle to create layers. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.
  8. Finally, unclip the front sections of your hair and comb them straight down. Hold the hair between your middle and index fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards.
  9. Using your hair cutting scissors, make small snips, cutting at an angle to create layers. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.
  10. Once you've finished cutting all sections of your hair, use your comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as necessary.

Tips for Cutting Curly Wavy Hair at Home

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curly wavy hair at home:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting it.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to make small snips and check your progress as you go.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with small snips and work your way up.

FAQs

Can I cut my curly wavy hair when it's wet?

No, it's best to cut curly wavy hair when it's dry. This will help you see your natural curl pattern and make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it's important to use hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut. Regular scissors can cause hair to split and create uneven ends.

How often should I cut my curly wavy hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care for Curly Wavy Hair

Now that you've learned how to cut curly wavy hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Here are some tips for hair care for curly wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Cutting curly wavy hair at home may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Just remember to take your time, make small snips, and check your progress as you go. And don't forget to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding damaging heat styling tools. Happy cutting!
