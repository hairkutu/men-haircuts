How To Tell What Hairstyle Looks Best On You Male

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be daunting, especially for men who are not familiar with different haircuts and styles. However, finding the perfect haircut that suits your face shape and personality can make a significant difference in your overall appearance. In this article, we will guide you on how to tell what hairstyle looks best on you male, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you choose the right haircut that complements your facial features. There are six main face shapes: oval, square, rectangle, round, heart, and diamond.

To determine your face shape, you can do the following:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  • Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  • Note down the measurements and compare them to the following:

Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones. Your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is rounded.

Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same width. Your chin is square, and your jawline is sharp.

Rectangle: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same width. Your chin is slightly rounded.

Round: Your face length and width are almost the same, and your cheeks are full. Your jawline is soft and rounded, and your chin is not prominent.

Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your face narrows down to a small chin. Your jawline is soft and rounded.

Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower. Your chin is pointed.

Hair Types and Textures

Another important factor to consider when choosing the right hairstyle is your hair type and texture. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Each hair type has different textures, such as fine, medium, and thick.

Here are some characteristics of each hair type:

  • Straight: Your hair is smooth and lacks volume. It is easy to style and maintain.
  • Wavy: Your hair has a slight curl and is thicker than straight hair. It can be challenging to style, but it offers volume and movement.
  • Curly: Your hair has a noticeable curl, and it can be challenging to manage. It is prone to frizz and dryness.
  • Kinky: Your hair has tight coils and is the most fragile hair type. It requires a lot of moisture and care to maintain.

Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Once you have determined your face shape and hair type, you can choose from a range of hairstyles that suit your features. Here are some popular hairstyles for different face shapes:

Oval: You can choose from a range of hairstyles, including a buzz cut, slicked-back hair, or a messy fringe.

Square: A short, textured cut or a crew cut looks great on square faces. You can also add some height to your hairstyle to balance your features.

Rectangle: A side-swept fringe or a layered cut with texture can soften your features and add volume.

Round: A short, textured cut with height at the top can elongate your face shape. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides.

Heart: A textured crop, messy fringe, or a side-swept style with volume on top can balance your features.

Diamond: A textured side-parted style or a slicked-back look can complement your features.

Tips to Find the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you find the right hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you can devote to styling your hair.
  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your stylist.
  • Ask your stylist for recommendations based on your features and lifestyle.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a buzz cut?

A: A buzz cut looks great on men with an oval or square face shape. However, if you have a round or heart-shaped face, a buzz cut may not be the best option.

Q: How do I maintain my hairstyle?

A: Use the right hair products for your hair type and style. Wash your hair regularly and visit your stylist for regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features and personality. Remember to experiment with different styles and consult with your stylist for recommendations. With the right haircut, you can boost your confidence and transform your appearance.
