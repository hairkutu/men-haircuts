How To Style A Textured Pixie Cut A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you rocking a textured pixie cut but struggling to style it? Look no further! This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your pixie cut with ease. From hair care tips to styling techniques, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style a Textured Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling

Hair Care

Before diving into styling techniques, it's important to prioritize hair care. A healthy base will make styling much easier and more effective. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

- Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping natural oils

- Use a nourishing conditioner to keep hair hydrated

- Apply a weekly hair mask to provide extra moisture

- Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage

By prioritizing hair care, your hair will be in optimal condition for styling.

How to Style a Textured Pixie Cut

Now for the fun part - styling! Here are the basic steps for styling a textured pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add even more texture
  5. Use a flat iron to add movement and shape to your hair
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place

With these simple steps, you can achieve a stylish and textured pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair

Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair. This will add volume and help your style hold throughout the day.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Focus on lifting the roots of your hair to create more volume.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add even more texture

Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add even more texture. Apply the spray throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 5: Use a flat iron to add movement and shape to your hair

Use a flat iron to add movement and shape to your hair. Create loose waves or curls to add dimension to your style.

Step 6: Finish with a light hold hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place. Hold the hairspray a few inches away from your hair and mist lightly.

Tips for Styling

Here are a few tips for styling your textured pixie cut:

  • - Use a teasing comb to add extra volume at the roots
  • - Experiment with different texturizing sprays to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • - Play with different flat iron techniques, such as twisting the hair around the iron or creating beachy waves
  • - Don't be afraid to use bobby pins or hair clips to create a unique style

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping natural oils.

Can I still style my hair if it's not freshly washed?

Absolutely! In fact, some people find that their hair is easier to style when it's not freshly washed. Simply apply a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oils before styling.

What's the best way to add volume to my hair?

Using a volumizing mousse at the roots of your hair is a great way to add volume. You can also try blow drying your hair upside down to add even more lift.

How do I prevent heat damage when using a flat iron?

Always use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron. Additionally, avoid using the highest heat setting and limit heat styling as much as possible.

What's the best way to maintain my textured pixie cut?

Regular trims and hair appointments are key to maintaining a pixie cut. Additionally, use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Congratulations, you're now a pro at styling your textured pixie cut!
