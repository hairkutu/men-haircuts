Haircuts For Square Face Over 40 The Complete Guide

Are you in your 40s and have a square face shape? Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for square face over 40, with tips on how to style and care for your hair. So, grab a cup of coffee and read on!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with cheekbones that are almost as wide as the forehead. The goal of a haircut for a square face is to soften the angles and create a more oval shape. This can be achieved by adding layers, volume, and length around the face.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a square face shape, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Consulting with a hairstylist who specializes in working with square face shapes can also be helpful. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, as they can emphasize the squareness of the face.
  • Opt for soft layers and side-swept bangs to add movement and soften the angles.
  • Consider a shoulder-length or longer cut, as this can elongate the face and create a more oval shape.
  • Add volume to the crown of the head to draw attention away from the jawline.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling some of the best haircuts for square face over 40:

Long Layers

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush to create volume at the roots.
  2. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the mid-lengths and ends of the hair.
  3. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

Shoulder-Length Bob

  1. Apply a heat protectant to damp hair and blow-dry with a flat brush to create a smooth finish.
  2. Use a straightening iron to smooth any flyaways and add shine.
  3. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can help maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a good blow dryer and curling iron.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find your signature look.

FAQ

What if I have fine hair?

If you have fine hair, opt for a shorter haircut with layers to add movement and volume. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and limp.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and opt for a haircut that enhances your curls. Layers can help add volume and shape, while a shoulder-length or longer cut can help weigh down the curls and prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and beautiful haircut. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to book that appointment with your hairstylist and rock that new haircut!
