How To Style Aging Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby May 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair goes through changes that can make styling a bit more challenging. However, with the right techniques and products, you can still achieve a great look. In this article, we'll cover how to style aging hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To Style Aging Hair
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling, it's important to take care of your hair. As we age, our hair can become more brittle and prone to breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By taking care of your hair, you'll have a better canvas to work with when it comes to styling.

How To Style Aging Hair

Now let's dive into how to style aging hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant if you plan to use hot tools.

Step 2: Choose your style

Decide what type of style you want to go for. Do you want to wear your hair straight, curly, or somewhere in between? Look for inspiration online or in magazines.

Step 3: Prep your hair

If you're going for a curly look, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. If you're going for a straight look, apply a smoothing serum. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair. If you're going for a curly look, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls. If you're going for a straight look, use a flat iron to smooth your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your aging hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add body to your hair.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add movement to your hair.
  • If you have thinning hair, consider getting a shorter haircut to make your hair appear fuller.
  • Use a root touch-up spray to cover any gray hairs.

FAQ

What hairstyles are best for aging hair?

Shorter hairstyles are often recommended for aging hair, as they can make your hair appear fuller. However, there's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It ultimately depends on your hair type, face shape, and personal style.

What products should I use on aging hair?

Look for products that are specifically formulated for aging hair. These products often contain nourishing ingredients like keratin and argan oil to help strengthen and hydrate your hair.

Can I still use hot tools on aging hair?

Yes, but it's important to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools. You should also avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Styling aging hair doesn't have to be a challenge. By taking care of your hair and using the right techniques and products, you can still achieve a great look. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
