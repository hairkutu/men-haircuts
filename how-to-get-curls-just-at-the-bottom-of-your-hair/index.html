How To Get Curls Just At The Bottom Of Your Hair

Written by Daniel May 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some flair to your hairstyle? Curls just at the bottom of your hair can add a fun and playful touch to your look. Whether you're going for a casual or formal style, this is a great way to switch things up. In this article, we'll show you how to get curls just at the bottom of your hair, step-by-step.

Description

Getting curls at the bottom of your hair is a great way to add volume and texture to your hairstyle. It's a simple and easy technique that you can do at home with just a few tools. Curling just the bottom section of your hair gives a fresh and modern twist to your look without being over the top.

How to Get Curls Just at the Bottom of Your Hair

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by separating the top half of your hair from the bottom half. Secure the top half of your hair with a clip or hair tie.

Step 3: Curl the Bottom Section

Take a small section of hair from the bottom half and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until you've curled all the hair in the bottom section.

Step 4: Release the Top Section

Once you've finished curling the bottom section, release the top section of your hair. Use a brush or comb to blend the curls together for a seamless look.

Step 5: Add Some Hairspray

Finish off your look by adding some hairspray to hold the curls in place. This will help your curls stay bouncy and beautiful all day long.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size for tighter curls.
  • For looser curls, use a larger barrel size.
  • Don't curl your hair too close to the roots, as this can make your curls look unnatural.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Finish off your look with some hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl just the ends of my hair?

A: Yes, you can curl just the ends of your hair for a similar look. Simply take small sections of hair and curl just the ends around a curling iron.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curls should last anywhere from 4-8 hours.

Q: Can I do this style on short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve this look on shorter hair. Use a smaller curling iron and take smaller sections of hair to curl.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially when you're using heat styling tools. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on wet hair.
  • Don't use heat styling tools every day.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve curls just at the bottom of your hair. It's a great way to switch up your look and add some fun and playfulness to your hairstyle. Try it out for yourself and see how it can transform your look!
