How To Style Your Hair With Loose Curls

Written by Bardi Mar 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your same old straight hair and looking for a change? Loose curls can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more glamorous and effortless. In this article, we will guide you on how to style your hair with loose curls.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have all the necessary tools: a curling iron, heat protectant spray, hairbrush, and hairspray. Here are the steps to achieve loose curls:

Step 1: Preparing your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and apply a heat protectant spray to avoid any damage to your hair. Dry your hair with a towel and detangle it with a hairbrush.

Step 2: Sectioning your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, and clip them up to make it easier for you to curl. Start from the bottom sections and move upwards.

Step 3: Curling your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and wrap it around the curling iron, away from your face. Hold it for 10-15 seconds and release it. Repeat this step until you have curled all your hair.

Step 4: Separating your Curls

Once you have curled your hair, separate the curls with your fingers. Do not use a hairbrush as it will make your curls look frizzy and unnatural.

Step 5: Applying Hairspray

Finally, apply hairspray to set your curls in place. You can use a light or strong hold depending on your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your loose curls look even better:

  • Use a curling iron with a bigger barrel to achieve loose curls.
  • Do not curl your hair too tightly as it will make your curls look unnatural.
  • Apply a hair serum to add shine to your curls.
  • Leave your curls untouched for a few hours to make them look more natural and effortless.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair with loose curls:

How long do loose curls last?

Loose curls can last for up to 2-3 days depending on your hair type and the weather.

Can I achieve loose curls with naturally straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve loose curls with naturally straight hair. However, it might take longer to curl your hair and might need more hairspray to set your curls in place.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair, especially if you frequently style it with heat tools. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid any damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Now that you know how to style your hair with loose curls, it's time to try it out yourself. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles.
