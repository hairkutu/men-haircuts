How To Gel My Hair Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that will stay put all day long, gelling your hair back is a great option. This classic look is popular among men and women alike, and it's easy to achieve with just a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to gel your hair back, including tips for getting the perfect hold and maintaining your style throughout the day.

How to Gel My Hair Back - Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start gelling your hair back, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then pat it dry with a towel. Avoid using a hair dryer if possible, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 2: Apply Gel

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel to your hands and rub them together to evenly distribute the product. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, smoothing the gel through your hair as you go. Be sure to use enough gel to achieve the desired hold, but don't overdo it or your hair will look greasy and weighed down.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Once you've applied the gel, use a comb to smooth your hair back and create the desired shape. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, combing your hair in the opposite direction of its natural growth. This will help to create a sleek and polished look.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To make sure your hair stays in place all day long, finish your style with a spritz of hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray lightly, focusing on the areas where your hair tends to come loose. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Gelling Your Hair Back

Here are a few additional tips to help you get the perfect gelled back hairstyle:

  • Choose the right gel for your hair type - look for a product that's specifically designed for your hair texture and thickness.
  • Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed - it's easier to add more product than to remove it if you've used too much.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid causing damage to your hair.
  • Don't touch your hair too much once you've gelled it - this can cause the product to break down and your style to come loose.

FAQ

Can I gel my hair back if it's thin or fine?

Yes, you can still achieve a great gelled back look even if your hair is thin or fine. Just be sure to choose a gel that's lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

How long will my gelled back hairstyle last?

This will depend on the specific product you're using and how active you are throughout the day. With a good quality gel and hairspray, your style should last for several hours without needing to be touched up.

How do I remove gel from my hair?

To remove gel from your hair, simply wash it with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. You may need to shampoo twice to completely remove all traces of the product.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish gelled back hairstyle that will look great all day long. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect look for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
