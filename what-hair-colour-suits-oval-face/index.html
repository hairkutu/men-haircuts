What Hair Colour Suits Oval Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hair colour that will complement your oval face shape? Look no further because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss the best hair colours that suit oval faces, and we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect shade for your skin tone and personality.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off almost any hair colour. However, there are certain hair colours that can enhance the natural beauty of an oval face and make it stand out even more. The best hair colours for oval faces are those that complement the skin tone and bring out the best features of the face.

How To Choose The Right Hair Colour For Oval Face

When it comes to choosing the right hair colour for an oval face, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, you need to determine your skin tone. Are you warm or cool toned? This will help you choose the right shade that will complement your skin tone and make your eyes pop. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colours such as golden blondes, copper reds, and honey browns. If you have cool undertones, opt for cool hair colours such as ash blondes, platinum blondes, and cool browns.

Another thing to consider is your personality. Do you want a bold and vibrant hair colour, or do you prefer a more natural and subtle look? If you want a bold look, opt for bright hair colours such as hot pink, electric blue, or fiery red. If you prefer a more natural look, opt for hair colours that are closer to your natural shade, such as highlights or lowlights.

Step By Step Guide On Choosing Hair Colour For Oval Face

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect hair colour for oval faces:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Decide on the type of hair colour you want (warm or cool).
  3. Consider your personality and the look you want to achieve.
  4. Choose a shade that will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural features.
  5. Consult with a professional hair stylist to ensure the best results.

Tips For Choosing Hair Colour For Oval Face

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour for your oval face:

  • Consider your skin tone and natural features when choosing a hair colour.
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist to ensure the best results.
  • Be open to trying new shades and colours.
  • Experiment with highlights and lowlights for a more natural look.
  • Protect your hair from damage by using high-quality hair care products.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing hair colour for oval face:

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different colour than my natural shade?

A: Yes, you can! However, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural features.

Q: What type of hair colour should I choose if I have warm undertones?

A: Opt for warm hair colours such as golden blondes, copper reds, and honey browns.

Q: What type of hair colour should I choose if I have cool undertones?

A: Opt for cool hair colours such as ash blondes, platinum blondes, and cool browns.

Hair Care For Coloured Hair

After you've chosen your perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the vibrancy and health of your locks. Here are some tips for hair care for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent colour fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Choosing the perfect hair colour for oval face can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shade that complements your skin tone and natural features. Remember to take care of your coloured hair to ensure its vibrancy and health.
