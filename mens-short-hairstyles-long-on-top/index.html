Mens Short Hairstyles Long On Top The Latest Trend In 2023

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out, mens short hairstyles long on top might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. Not only is it versatile and easy to maintain, but it also looks great on almost any face shape and hair type.

Description

Mens short hairstyles long on top are characterized by a short or buzzed back and sides, with longer hair on the top of the head. This longer hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to

If you're thinking about trying out this hairstyle, here's how you can get it:

  1. Start by visiting a trusted hairstylist who has experience with this hairstyle. They can help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Once you have your hair cut, you can style it in a variety of ways. One popular option is to use a matte pomade or wax to create a messy, textured look. Alternatively, you can use a comb and some gel to slick your hair back for a more polished look.
  3. To maintain your hairstyle, you'll need to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting mens short hairstyles long on top:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that you like and bring a picture to your hairstylist.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to cut the back and sides of your hair short or buzzed, leaving the top longer.
  3. Style your hair using your preferred styling product.
  4. Maintain your hairstyle by visiting your hairstylist regularly for trims.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting mens short hairstyles long on top:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for your hair.
  • Be prepared to spend a few extra minutes styling your hair in the morning if you opt for a more polished look.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

1. Can I get mens short hairstyles long on top if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to work with a hairstylist who has experience cutting curly hair. They can help you determine the best length and style for your hair type.

2. Will mens short hairstyles long on top look good on me?

Mens short hairstyles long on top can look good on almost any face shape and hair type. It's important to work with a hairstylist to find a style that suits you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Mens short hairstyles long on top are a stylish and versatile option for men of all ages. Whether you opt for a messy, textured look or a more polished style, this haircut is sure to turn heads. Just remember to work with a hairstylist you trust and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
