Which Haircut Suits Thick Curly Hair

Written by Petter May 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right haircut to suit your hair type. Thick curly hair can be unruly and difficult to manage, which is why it is essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your natural curls. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for thick curly hair, and provide tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

Description

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, the volume and texture of curly hair can create stunning hairstyles that turn heads. On the other hand, it can be a challenge to find the right haircut that complements your curls without making them look frizzy or out of control.

The key to finding the right haircut for thick curly hair is to choose one that adds shape and definition to your curls while keeping them under control. The right haircut can help to elongate your face and balance the volume of your hair, resulting in a flattering and stylish look.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for thick curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Length: Longer hair can weigh down curls, making them less bouncy and defined. A shorter haircut can help to add volume and shape to your curls.
  • Layers: Layers can help to add definition to your curls, making them look more voluminous and bouncy. However, too many layers can make your hair frizzy and difficult to manage.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help to frame your face and create a more polished look. However, they can be tricky to manage with curly hair, so it's essential to choose a style that works with your natural curls.

Some of the best haircuts for thick curly hair include:

  • Long Bob: A long bob, or "lob," is a versatile haircut that works well with thick curly hair. It falls just above the shoulders, which helps to elongate the face and balance out the volume of your hair.
  • Layered Pixie: A layered pixie cut can add volume and definition to your curls, making them look more bouncy and voluminous.
  • Curly Shag: A curly shag is a layered haircut that works well with thick curly hair. It adds volume and definition to your curls while keeping them under control.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your thick curly hair, it's essential to know how to style it properly to keep it looking great. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around to dry your hair evenly.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls. Hold the curling iron or wand vertically and wrap sections of hair around it, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Thick curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for thick curly hair:

Q: Can I get bangs with thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get bangs with thick curly hair. However, it's essential to choose a style that works with your natural curls. Side-swept bangs tend to work well with curly hair, as they can be blended in with the rest of your curls.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I get a layered haircut with thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get a layered haircut with thick curly hair. However, it's important to choose a style that isn't too heavily layered, as this can make your hair frizzy and difficult to manage.

Hair Care Products for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for thick curly hair:

  • DevaCurl No-Poo Original Zero Lather Conditioning Cleanser
  • SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel
  • Moroccanoil Treatment

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your thick curly hair, you can achieve a stunning and stylish look that complements your natural curls. Remember to take care of your hair with the right products and styling techniques to keep it healthy and looking great.
