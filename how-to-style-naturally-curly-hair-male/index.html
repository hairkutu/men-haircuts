How To Style Naturally Curly Hair Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is a blessing, but it can also be a curse if you don't know how to style it. As a male with naturally curly hair, you might find it challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair texture and face shape. But don't worry! In this guide, we will walk you through the steps on how to style your curly hair and keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Understanding Your Hair Type

The first step to styling your curly hair is to understand your hair type. Not all curly hair is the same, and each hair type has unique characteristics that require different styling methods. Generally, there are three types of curly hair:

  • Type 2: Wavy hair that is not too curly
  • Type 3: Curly hair that has more defined curls
  • Type 4: Coily hair that is tightly curled

Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right haircut and hair products for your hair.

Choosing the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference in how your curly hair looks. When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. For curly hair, it's best to go for longer haircuts that allow your curls to shine. Avoid short haircuts that can make your curls look frizzy and unkempt.

Best Haircuts for Curly Hair

  • Long layers
  • Taper fade
  • Undercut
  • Curly quiff
  • Side part

Shampooing and Conditioning

Shampooing and conditioning your curly hair is essential to keep it healthy and moisturized. When shampooing, use a sulfate-free shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner that will hydrate your curls and prevent frizz. Avoid washing your hair too often as it can dry out your hair and make it look dull.

Choosing the Right Hair Products

Choosing the right hair products is crucial to getting the perfect hairstyle for your curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain natural ingredients that won't damage your hair. Here are some products you should consider:

  • Curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl defining cream
  • Hair gel
  • Hair oil

Drying Your Hair

Drying your curly hair can be tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle. After washing your hair, gently squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel as it can cause frizz. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without damaging them.

Styling Your Hair

Styling your curly hair can be a lot of fun, but it can also be challenging if you don't know what you're doing. Here are some tips to help you style your curly hair:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your damp hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls
  • Separate your curls into sections and twist them to define your curls
  • Use a hair gel to hold your curls in place
  • Avoid using a brush as it can cause frizz

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your curly hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Use hair accessories like headbands and hair ties to style your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

FAQs

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.

What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

You can use a curl defining cream or hair gel to tame frizz and define your curls.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair with a flat iron, but it's best to avoid doing it too often as it can damage your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your curly hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as it can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Final Thoughts

Styling your naturally curly hair as a male might seem challenging, but with the right techniques and hair products, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. With these tips, you'll be able to rock your curly hair with confidence!
