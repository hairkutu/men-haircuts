How To Curl Ends Of Hair Outwards A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jul 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair look? Do you want to add a little bit of bounce and volume to your hair? Look no further! In this article, we'll teach you how to curl the ends of your hair outwards for a chic and trendy look. Follow these steps for a flawless and effortless hairdo.

How to Curl Ends of Hair Outwards

Curling the ends of your hair outwards is a simple and easy process. All you need is a curling iron or a straightener, some hairspray, and a few minutes. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Applying heat to wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Curling Iron or Straightener

Set your curling iron or straightener to the desired temperature. If you have thin or fine hair, use a lower temperature. For thicker or coarser hair, use a higher temperature.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Start curling your hair from the middle of the strand and work your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron or straightener horizontally, and twist your hair outwards. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this step for all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Apply Hairspray

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, apply a light coat of hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Curling the Ends of Your Hair Outwards

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect curl:

  • Use a curling iron or straightener with a ceramic or tourmaline coating. These materials distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron or straightener on your hair for too long. This can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a smaller curling iron or straightener for tighter curls, and a larger one for looser curls.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to avoid damage.
  • Don't forget to apply hairspray to keep your curls in place.

FAQs

Can I curl the ends of my hair outwards with a straightener?

Yes, you can! Simply twist your hair outwards as you glide the straightener down the length of your hair.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use. Applying a light coat of hairspray can help your curls last longer.

Hair Care

Curling your hair can cause damage and breakage, especially if you do it frequently. To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair. Also, avoid using a high temperature on thin or fine hair.

Additionally, make sure to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or tugging on it.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect outward curl for a chic and trendy look. Happy curling!
