Best Haircuts For Square Face Older Woman Tips And Ideas

Written by Oliver Apr 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are an older woman with a square face shape, choosing the right haircut can make all the difference. A good haircut can enhance your best features and draw attention away from areas you might be self-conscious about. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for square face older women, how to choose the right one, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Square Face
  • Best Haircuts for Square Face Older Women
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Square Face

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for your square face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a style that will soften the angles of your face. This can be done by adding layers, side-swept bangs, or waves.

Another thing to consider is the length of your hair. If you have a square face, you may want to avoid a short bob or pixie cut, as these can accentuate the squareness of your jawline. Instead, opt for longer styles that will add length to your face, such as a shoulder-length cut or a long layered style.

Lastly, consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a blunt cut to add volume. If you have thick hair, layers can help to thin it out and make it more manageable.

Best Haircuts for Square Face Older Women

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a haircut for your square face, here are some of the best styles for older women:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for square face shapes, as they add length and movement to the hair. This style is perfect for those with thick or wavy hair, as it can help to thin it out and make it more manageable.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften the angles of a square face and draw attention to the eyes. This style works well with both short and long haircuts, and can be customized to suit your individual face shape.

Soft Waves

Soft waves can be achieved with a curling iron or by simply scrunching your hair with a styling product. This style adds movement and texture to the hair, and can help to soften the angles of a square face.

Shoulder-Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut is a great option for older women with square faces, as it adds length and draws attention away from the jawline. This style can be worn straight or wavy, and can be customized with layers or bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut

Now that you have chosen the perfect haircut for your square face, here is a step-by-step guide to getting it:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in haircuts for square face shapes.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want and discuss any modifications that may need to be made to suit your individual face shape.
  3. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic.
  4. After your haircut, ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have your new haircut, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for square face older women:

What is the best haircut for a square face?

The best haircut for a square face is one that adds length and softens the angles of the jawline. This can be achieved with long layers, side-swept bangs, or soft waves.

Can older women wear short haircuts with a square face?

While short haircuts can be flattering on some square face shapes, they may accentuate the squareness of the jawline in others. It is best to consult with a stylist to determine which length and style will work best for you.

What is the easiest haircut to maintain for older women with a square face?

A shoulder-length cut with layers is a great option for older women with square faces, as it is easy to maintain and can be customized to suit your individual face shape.

Hair Care for Square Face Older Women

Lastly, here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair as an older woman with a square face:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and ideas, you are well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your square face. Remember to consult with a stylist and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
