Best Haircut For Long Slim Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the best haircut for your long, slim face? If so, you're not alone. Many people with this face shape have trouble finding the right hairstyle that complements their features. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, you can find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for a long slim face, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the best haircut for a long slim face, it's important to understand what this face shape looks like. A long slim face typically has a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a long jawline. The length of the face is longer than its width. With this face shape, it's essential to create balance and proportion by choosing the right haircut.

How To

The best haircut for a long slim face is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face. This can be achieved with layers, waves, and curls. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they will only elongate the face further. Instead, opt for a haircut that falls just below the shoulders or chin. A bob or lob is an excellent choice for this face shape.

Step by Step

Here's how to get the best haircut for a long slim face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with this face shape.
  2. Ask for a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of the face.
  3. Consider adding layers or waves to create movement and texture.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight.
  5. Choose a length that falls just below the shoulders or chin.
  6. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for a long slim face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they will only elongate the face further.
  • Consider side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous, as they can overwhelm a long slim face.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume to the sides of the face.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about the best haircut for a long slim face:

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a long slim face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but make sure to add some movement and texture with layers or waves.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail with a long slim face?

A: Yes, but make sure to add volume to the sides of the face with layers or waves to create balance.

Q: What is the best hair color for a long slim face?

A: Adding highlights or lowlights can create dimension and depth, which is essential for this face shape. Talk to your hairstylist about the best hair color for your skin tone and face shape.

Hair Care

Now that you have the best haircut for your long slim face, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can have the best haircut for your long slim face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with this face shape to get the best results.
