Best Short Haircut Styles For Round Faces

Description

Choosing the right haircut for your round face can be a challenge. You want to find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Short haircuts are a popular choice for round faces because they can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. In this article, we will explore the best short haircut styles for round faces and provide tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

How to

If you have a round face, there are a few key things you should keep in mind when choosing a short haircut. First, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that add height and length to your face. Second, consider your hair texture and choose a style that complements it. For example, if you have fine hair, a pixie cut may be a good choice, while if you have thick hair, a bob or lob may be better.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best short haircut styles for round faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can work well for round faces. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The longer layers help to add height to the face and create a more balanced look.

Bob

A bob is another popular short haircut style for round faces. This style can be cut to chin-length or slightly longer, with a blunt cut or layers. The key is to keep the hair close to the face to create a slimming effect.

Lob

A lob, or long bob, is a great option for those who want a slightly longer short haircut. This style is typically cut to shoulder-length or just above, with a slight angle to the front. This helps to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to a short haircut can help to slim the face and add dimension. The bangs should be long enough to graze the eyebrows, and swept to one side of the face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short haircut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding subtle highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and height.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to create different styles.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face is one that adds height and length to the face. A pixie cut, bob, lob, or a style with side-swept bangs can all work well.

Can I still wear my hair curly if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to keep the curls loose and voluminous, rather than tight and close to the head.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts typically require more frequent trims than longer styles. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product that can weigh down your hair. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. And don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.
