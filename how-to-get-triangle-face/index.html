How To Get Triangle Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 01, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to achieve a more angular and defined facial structure, you may be wondering how to get a triangle face. Luckily, there are several methods you can try to help shape your face into a more triangular shape. In this article, we'll take you through how to get triangle face, step by step.

What Is A Triangle Face?

A triangle face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrower jawline. This shape is often considered desirable as it gives the face a more angular and defined appearance.

How To Get Triangle Face

1. Try Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone and define your facial muscles, which can help to shape your face into a more triangular shape. Some exercises you can try include:

  • Jaw clenching
  • Cheek puffing
  • Chin lifts
  • Smiling fish

Try incorporating these exercises into your daily routine to see results.

2. Contour Your Face

Contouring can help to create the illusion of a more defined and angular face shape. Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face you want to appear more narrow, such as your jawline and temples. Then, use a highlighter to accentuate the areas you want to appear more prominent, such as your cheekbones and forehead.

3. Get A Haircut

The right haircut can help to accentuate your facial features and create the illusion of a more triangular face shape. Consider getting a haircut with layers, which can help to frame your face and add dimension. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides, as this can make your face appear wider.

4. Consider Dermal Fillers

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, dermal fillers can help to add volume to areas of your face that need it, such as your cheekbones. This can help to create a more angular and defined appearance.

Tips For Maintaining A Triangle Face Shape

Once you've achieved your desired triangle face shape, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your face looking angular and defined:

  • Stay hydrated to keep your skin looking plump and youthful
  • Get plenty of sleep to reduce puffiness and dark circles under your eyes
  • Stick to a healthy diet to maintain a healthy weight and prevent bloating
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage your skin and cause premature aging

FAQs

What if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, you can still try the methods outlined in this article to create the illusion of a more triangular face shape. However, keep in mind that your natural face shape is beautiful and unique, so embrace it!

How long does it take to see results?

The amount of time it takes to see results will depend on the method you choose and your individual body type. However, with consistent effort, you should start to see changes within a few weeks.

Hair Care Tips For Triangle Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for a triangle face shape, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your facial structure. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider
  • Consider a hairstyle with layers, which can help to frame your face and add dimension
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape
  • Use a hair serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving a more defined and angular triangle face shape. Remember, the most important thing is to embrace your natural beauty and be confident in your own skin!
