How To Give Men s Fade Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give a men's fade haircut, whether you're a barber or just want to try it out on yourself or a friend, this guide will provide you with all the information you need. A fade haircut is a popular style that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter from the top of the head down to the sides and back, creating a fade effect. Read on to learn how to give a men's fade haircut.

How to Give Men's Fade Haircut

Follow these steps to give a men's fade haircut:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. If the hair is wet, it will be harder to cut and you may not get the desired result.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

To give a fade haircut, you'll need clippers with different guard sizes. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to shorter guards for the fade effect.

Step 3: Create the Fade Effect

Begin by cutting the hair with the longest guard size on the top of the head. Then, use a slightly shorter guard size to cut the sides and back of the head. Gradually switch to even shorter guard sizes as you go down the sides and back of the head to create the fade effect.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use a blending guard to blend the hair between the different guard sizes, creating a smooth and seamless transition between the longer and shorter hair.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use a trimmer to clean up the edges around the ears and the back of the neck for a polished look.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style the hair as desired using hair products.

Tips for Giving a Men's Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving a men's fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to shorter guard sizes for the fade effect.
  • Use a blending guard to create a smooth transition between the longer and shorter hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. A good fade haircut takes time and precision.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for feedback from the person you're cutting.

FAQ About Men's Fade Haircut

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's fade haircut:

What is a fade haircut?

A fade haircut is a popular style that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter from the top of the head down to the sides and back, creating a fade effect.

How long does a fade haircut last?

A fade haircut can last anywhere from two to four weeks, depending on how fast the hair grows.

Can I give myself a fade haircut?

Yes, you can give yourself a fade haircut, but it may be easier to have someone else help you, especially if it's your first time.

Hair Care After Men's Fade Haircut

After getting a men's fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade haircut.

Giving a men's fade haircut may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a simple and satisfying process. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and follow these steps for a great result.
