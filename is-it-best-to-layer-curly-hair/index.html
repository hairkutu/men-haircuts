Is It Best To Layer Curly Hair

Written by Christine May 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It adds character and personality to your overall look, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. One of the most common questions asked by curly-haired individuals is whether or not it is best to layer their hair. In this article, we will discuss the benefits and drawbacks of layering curly hair and provide some tips on how to manage it.

Description

Layering curly hair refers to the process of cutting the hair at different lengths to create depth and dimension. This technique can be used to add volume to flat hair or to thin out thick hair. Layering is also popular among curly-haired individuals because it can help to define the curls and reduce frizz.

How to Layer Curly Hair

If you are considering layering your curly hair, it is important to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. Curly hair can be tricky to cut because it can shrink up to 50% when it dries. A stylist who is familiar with curly hair will know how to cut it to avoid shrinkage and create a flattering shape.

Before you get your hair cut, it is important to communicate with your stylist about what you want. Bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like and discuss what will work best for your hair type and face shape. It is also important to talk about how much maintenance you are willing to do at home.

Step by Step Guide to Layering Curly Hair

If you are interested in layering your curly hair at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

  • Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  • Comb or brush your hair while it is wet to remove any tangles.
  • Part your hair where you normally would.
  • Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and twist it away from your face.
  • Use scissors to cut the twisted section of hair at an angle. This will create a subtle, layered effect.
  • Repeat this process on the other side of your head and then move to the back of your head.
  • Continue to twist small sections of hair and cut them at an angle until you have achieved the desired amount of layers.
  • When you are finished, shake out your hair to see how it looks.
  • If you need to make any adjustments, twist and cut additional sections of hair as needed.

Tips for Layering Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering curly hair:

  • Start small. You can always cut more hair later, but you can't put it back on.
  • Be gentle. Curly hair can be fragile, so make sure you are using sharp scissors and cutting slowly.
  • Experiment with different angles. Cutting at different angles will create different types of layers.
  • Don't overdo it. Too many layers can make curly hair look frizzy and unkempt.

FAQ

Q: Will layering my curly hair make it easier to manage?

A: Yes, layering can help to reduce frizz and make your curls more defined. However, it is still important to use the right hair products and techniques to keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I layer my curly hair at home?

A: Yes, you can layer your curly hair at home, but it is recommended that you seek the advice of a professional stylist before attempting it on your own.

Q: Will layering my curly hair make it look thinner?

A: Yes, layering can make curly hair look thinner because it removes some of the bulk. However, it is important to be careful not to over-layer your hair, as this can make it look too thin and frizzy.

Hair Care for Layered Curly Hair

After you have layered your curly hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

In conclusion, layering curly hair can be a great way to add volume and definition to your curls. However, it is important to find a stylist who is experienced with curly hair and to communicate your wants and needs before getting your hair cut. If you choose to layer your hair at home, be sure to start small and be gentle. With the right care and maintenance, your layered curly hair can look beautiful and healthy.
