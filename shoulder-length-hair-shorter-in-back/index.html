Shoulder Length Hair Shorter In Back A Relaxed Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by William May 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy yet low-maintenance hairstyle, shoulder length hair shorter in back might just be the perfect fit for you. This hairstyle is all about having shorter layers in the back while keeping the front layers longer, creating a flattering and effortless look. Here's everything you need to know about how to achieve this style, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Shoulder length hair shorter in back is a hairstyle that's become increasingly popular in recent years. The style features shorter layers at the back of the head, with the front layers kept longer. The result is a look that's versatile, easy to maintain, and perfect for anyone who wants to add some interest to their hair without committing to a drastic cut.

How to

If you're ready to try this style, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

The first step is to consult with a professional stylist who can help you determine if this style is right for you. Your stylist will be able to assess your hair type, face shape, and other factors to help you decide if this look will work for you.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Once you've decided to go for this style, you'll need to determine the length of the back layers. Typically, the back layers should be about one to two inches shorter than the front layers.

Step 3: Create the Layers

To create this look, your stylist will create shorter layers at the back of your head, while keeping the front layers longer. This will add volume and movement to your hair, and create a flattering shape.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been cut in this style, you can style it in a variety of ways. You can wear it straight and sleek, or add some waves or curls for a more relaxed look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying this style:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add waves or curls to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and definition to your layers.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle:

Is this style right for me?

This style can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if it's right for you.

How often do I need to get it trimmed?

You'll need to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of the layers.

Can I still wear my hair up?

Yes! This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, including in a ponytail or updo.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect shoulder length hair shorter in back look. Whether you're going for a sleek or relaxed vibe, this style is sure to turn heads.
