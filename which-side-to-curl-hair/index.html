Which Side To Curl Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you wondering which side to curl your hair? You’re not alone! Many people struggle with this question, and it can be challenging to know which way to go. In this blog post, we’ll cover everything you need to know about which side to curl your hair, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of which side to curl your hair, let's take a moment to talk about why it matters. The direction in which you curl your hair can have a significant impact on the final look. Curling your hair towards your face can create a more romantic and whimsical look, while curling away from your face can give you a more modern and edgy vibe. So, it's essential to consider the occasion and the overall look you're going for before deciding which side to curl your hair.

How to

Now that you understand why it's essential to consider the direction of your curls let's talk about how to determine which side to curl your hair. The general rule of thumb is to curl your hair away from your face on the side you part your hair. For example, if you have a side part on the right side of your head, you should curl your hair away from your face on the right side. Similarly, if you have a side part on the left side of your head, you should curl your hair away from your face on the left side. This will create a more natural-looking flow and frame your face beautifully.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to curling your hair in the right direction:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly.
  2. Part your hair on the side you prefer.
  3. Section your hair using clips or hair ties.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around your curling iron or wand.
  5. Curl your hair away from your face, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release it.
  7. Repeat this process until you've curled all of your hair.
  8. Run your fingers through your curls to loosen them up and create a more natural look.
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a high-quality curling iron or wand for best results.
  • Don't curl too close to your roots to avoid a dated look.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls for a more natural look.
  • Let your curls cool down before touching them to help them set.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which side to curl your hair:

Should I curl my hair towards or away from my face?

If you have a side part, you should curl your hair away from your face on the side you part your hair for a more natural look.

What if I don't have a side part?

If you don't have a side part, you can create one by using a comb to part your hair on the side you prefer.

What if I want a more dramatic look?

If you want a more dramatic look, you can curl your hair towards your face on one side and away from your face on the other side.

Hair Care

Finally, it's essential to take care of your hair to ensure your curls look their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

And there you have it! A complete guide to which side to curl your hair. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect curls every time.
