Best Hair Products For Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the best hair products for over 50. As we age, our hair changes, and it's important to use products that cater to our hair's needs. In this post, we'll cover the best hair products for over 50, how to use them, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair becomes more fragile, thinner, and drier. We need to use products that help to nourish and protect our hair. The best hair products for over 50 are those that cater to these specific needs. In this guide, we'll cover products that help to promote hair growth, add volume, and provide moisture.

How to Choose the Best Hair Products for Over 50

Choosing the best hair products for over 50 can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some things to consider when selecting hair products:

1. Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing hair products. Is your hair dry, oily, or normal? Is it curly or straight? There are products that cater to each hair type, so make sure to select products that suit your hair's needs.

2. Ingredients

Pay attention to the ingredients in your hair products. Look for products that are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates. Natural ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter can help to nourish and protect your hair.

3. Hair Concerns

Identify your hair concerns and select products that cater to those concerns. Do you want to promote hair growth? Add volume? Reduce frizz? There are products that can help with each of these concerns.

Best Hair Products for Over 50

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that cater to your hair type and concerns. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients. Some great options include:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo
  • OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo
  • Living Proof Full Shampoo
  • Briogeo Scalp Revival Charcoal + Coconut Oil Micro-Exfoliating Shampoo

For conditioner, consider:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Conditioner
  • OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Conditioner
  • Living Proof Full Conditioner
  • Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask

2. Hair Oil

Hair oil can help to nourish and protect your hair. Look for oils that are lightweight and don't weigh down your hair. Some great options include:

  • MoroccanOil Treatment Light
  • Carol's Daughter Black Vanilla Moisture & Shine Hair Oil
  • Briogeo Farewell Frizz Rosarco Milk Leave-In Conditioning Spray
  • Living Proof Perfect Hair Day 5-in-1 Styling Treatment

3. Hair Mask

A hair mask can help to deeply nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for masks that contain natural ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil. Some great options include:

  • Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask
  • Shea Moisture Raw Shea Butter Deep Treatment Masque
  • Carol's Daughter Almond Milk Ultra-Nourishing Hair Mask
  • Living Proof Restore Mask Treatment

Step by Step Guide to Using Hair Products

Here's a step-by-step guide to using the best hair products for over 50:

1. Shampoo

Wet your hair and apply shampoo to your scalp. Massage your scalp with your fingertips for a few minutes to stimulate blood flow. Rinse thoroughly.

2. Conditioner

Apply conditioner to the lengths and ends of your hair. Leave on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

3. Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to the lengths and ends of your hair. Avoid the roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.

4. Hair Mask

Apply a hair mask to your hair once a week. Leave on for 20-30 minutes, then rinse thoroughly. For best results, use a hair steamer or wrap your hair in a warm towel.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Protect Your Hair From Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This helps to prevent breakage and damage.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

1. Can hair products really make a difference for over 50 hair?

Yes! Using hair products that cater to your hair's needs can help to nourish and protect your hair, promote hair growth, and add volume.

2. What should I look for in hair products for over 50?

Look for products that cater to your hair type and concerns, contain natural ingredients, and are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates.

3. How often should I use a hair mask?

Use a hair mask once a week for best results.

Hair Care: Wrapping Up

And there you have it, our ultimate guide on the best hair products for over 50. Remember to choose products that cater to your hair's needs, and follow our tips for maintaining healthy hair. If you have any questions, feel free to leave a comment below.
