Does Long Or Short Hair Make You Look Skinny

Written by Eveline May 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Long or short hair? This has been a constant debate in the world of beauty and fashion. Some people believe that long hair makes you look skinny, while others swear by short hair. In this blog post, we will discuss the truth behind the myth and give you tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your body type.

Description

Many people believe that long hair makes you look skinny because it elongates your face and neck. On the other hand, short hair can make you look wider because it exposes more of your face and neck. However, these assumptions are not entirely true.

Your hairstyle does not necessarily determine your body shape. Other factors such as your clothing, posture, and diet play a more significant role in how you look. Therefore, you should choose a hairstyle based on your face shape and personal preference, not your body type.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially if you don't know where to start. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. You can use a mirror and a measuring tape to measure your face's width and length and compare them with the following guide:

  • Round Face: Face length and width are equal.
  • Oval Face: Face length is greater than the width, and the forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Square Face: Face length, width, and jawline are almost equal.
  • Heart Face: Face length is greater than the width, and the forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Diamond Face: Face length is greater than the width, and the cheekbones are wider than the forehead and jawline.
  • Oblong Face: Face length is greater than the width, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle That Compliments Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that compliments it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round Face: Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face and elongates your features. Long layered hair, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails are great options.
  • Oval Face: Most hairstyles look great on an oval face. You can choose a hairstyle that highlights your best features, such as long layers, blunt bangs, or a pixie cut.
  • Square Face: Choose a hairstyle that softens your features and adds roundness to your face. Soft waves, side-swept bangs, and layered haircuts are great options.
  • Heart Face: Choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead and jawline. A side-swept bob, long layers, or a messy updo are great options.
  • Diamond Face: Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin. Layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and bobs are great options.
  • Oblong Face: Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face and shortens your features. A layered bob, side-swept bangs, or a messy updo are great options.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, you need to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for keeping your hairstyle looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after each wash to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

Remember to maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims and using the right products for your hair type. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best every day.
