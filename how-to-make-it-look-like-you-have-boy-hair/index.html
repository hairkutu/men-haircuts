How To Make It Look Like You Have Boy Hair

Written by Robby Sep 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new? Maybe you're interested in the androgynous look and want to make it look like you have boy hair. Whatever your reason, achieving this style is easier than you might think. In this article, we'll go over the steps you can take to make it look like you have boy hair, as well as some tips and strategies for maintaining your new look.

Description

The androgynous look has been gaining in popularity in recent years, with many people embracing a more gender-neutral or non-binary appearance. One way to achieve this look is by making it look like you have boy hair. This involves creating a short, choppy haircut that is typically associated with male hairstyles. However, this look can be achieved by anyone, regardless of gender or hair type.

How to

Step 1: Choose a haircut

The first step in making it look like you have boy hair is to choose a haircut that will work for you. This may involve going to a stylist and getting a choppy, layered cut that falls just above or below the ears. You'll want to avoid any styles that are too feminine or have too much volume, as these will detract from the androgynous look you're trying to achieve.

Step 2: Style your hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to start styling. To create the boyish look you're going for, use a texturizing product like pomade or wax to add definition and volume to your hair. You can also try blow-drying your hair with your fingers to create a tousled, messy look that is reminiscent of a boy's haircut.

Step 3: Accessorize

To complete the look, consider adding some accessories that are typically associated with male fashion. This could include hats, beanies, or even a necktie. By incorporating these elements into your outfit, you can create a cohesive androgynous look that is sure to turn heads.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut to add some edge to your haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to try bold, unconventional colors like platinum blonde or neon pink.
  • Find inspiration from male celebrities or fashion icons to create a look that feels authentic to you.

FAQ

What if my hair is too long?

If your hair is too long to achieve the boyish look you're going for, consider cutting it or getting a shorter haircut. You can also try using hair ties or bobby pins to create a faux bob that will give you the appearance of shorter hair.

Can I still wear feminine clothing with boy hair?

Of course! The androgynous look is all about playing with gender norms and creating a unique style that is true to you. You can still wear dresses, skirts, and other feminine clothing items while sporting a boyish haircut.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using products like pomade or wax to style it. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to maintain your new look.

Conclusion

Making it look like you have boy hair is a fun and unique way to switch up your style and embrace the androgynous look. By following these steps and experimenting with different styles and products, you can create a look that is authentic to you and sure to turn heads. So go ahead and give it a try – who knows, you might just discover a new favorite look!
