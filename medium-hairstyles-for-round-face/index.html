Medium Hairstyles For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing medium hairstyles that are perfect for those with round faces. From how to style your hair to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Medium Hairstyles for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Medium Hairstyles for Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles for Round Face

Description

Medium hairstyles are perfect for those with round faces as they help elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. The key is to find a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head while keeping the length below the chin. This helps to balance out the roundness of your face.

How to Style Medium Hairstyles for Round Face

When it comes to styling medium hairstyles for round face, the options are endless. You can opt for a layered look, a bob, or even add some bangs. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and personality.

Layered Look

A layered look is a great option for those with round faces. It helps to add volume to the top of your head and draws attention away from the roundness of your face. You can opt for long layers or short layers, depending on your preference.

Bob

A bob is another great option for those with round faces. It helps to elongate the face and makes it appear slimmer. You can opt for a classic bob or add some layers for a modern twist.

Bangs

If you’re not ready to commit to a full-on haircut, why not add some bangs? Side-swept bangs are perfect for those with round faces as they help to elongate the face and bring attention to the eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style medium hairstyles for round face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  3. If you’re going for a layered look, use a curling iron to add some waves to your hair. If you’re going for a bob, use a straightener to make your hair sleek and straight.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Styling Medium Hairstyles for Round Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium hairstyles for round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Don’t be afraid to add layers to your hair as they can help to balance out the roundness of your face.
  • When adding bangs, opt for side-swept bangs as they help to elongate the face.

FAQs

What is the best medium hairstyle for a round face?

The best medium hairstyle for a round face is one that adds volume to the top of your head while keeping the length below the chin. A layered look, a bob, or adding some bangs are all great options.

Should I avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of my head?

Yes, it’s best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head as they can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles for Round Face

Maintaining healthy hair is key to achieving the perfect hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know all about medium hairstyles for round face, it’s time to find the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to keep in mind your face shape and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. Happy styling!
