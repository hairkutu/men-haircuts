Ways To Put Up Long Thin Hair Styles And Tips

Written by Bardi Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long thin hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, there are many ways to put up long thin hair that are both stylish and easy to achieve. In this article, we'll go over some popular styles and tips for putting up long thin hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Up Long Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Putting Up Long Thin Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

How to Put Up Long Thin Hair

Before we get into specific styles, here are some general tips for putting up long thin hair:

  • Use hair ties that won't damage your hair, such as ones made of fabric or silicone.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Don't pull your hair too tightly when putting it up, as this can also cause breakage.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

Styles for Putting Up Long Thin Hair

Now, let's take a look at some popular styles for putting up long thin hair:

1. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a classic and easy style for putting up long thin hair. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also add volume by gently teasing the hair at the crown before putting it up.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is another easy and stylish way to put up long thin hair. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around the base, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands around your face for a more relaxed look.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to put up long thin hair while adding some texture and interest. You can try a simple three-strand braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or bobby pins.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for putting up long thin hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the style you want to try.
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail or braid, depending on the style.
  4. Secure the end of the ponytail or braid with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  5. Use hairspray or other styling products to keep the style in place.

Tips for Putting Up Long Thin Hair

Here are some additional tips for putting up long thin hair:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or dry shampoo, to add texture and volume to your hair before putting it up.
  • Try using hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest to your style.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I put up long thin hair without using any hair ties?

A: While it's possible to put up long thin hair without using hair ties, it can be difficult to achieve a secure hold. It's recommended to use a hair tie or bobby pins to keep your style in place.

Q: Will putting up my long thin hair cause damage?

A: Putting up your hair can cause damage if you pull it too tightly or use hair ties that are too tight. Be gentle when putting up your hair and use hair ties that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful long thin hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and styles, you can put up your long thin hair in a variety of ways that are both stylish and easy to achieve. Experiment with different looks and find what works best for you!
