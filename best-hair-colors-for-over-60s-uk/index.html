Best Hair Colors For Over 60S Uk

Written by Petter Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair naturally loses its pigment and becomes more prone to damage. This is why it's important to choose the right hair color that complements our skin tone and hides any imperfections. In this blog post, we'll discuss the best hair colors for over 60s in the UK, along with some tips on how to maintain healthy hair.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. As we age, our skin tone changes, and some colors that used to look great on us might not be as flattering anymore. Additionally, our hair becomes more fragile and prone to breakage, so it's important to choose a color that won't cause further damage.

How To

Before choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your skin tone. Cool skin tones look best with ashy or silver shades, while warm skin tones look great with golden or caramel shades. If you're not sure what your skin tone is, you can ask a hairdresser to help you determine it.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start looking for hair colors that complement it. Some of the best hair colors for over 60s in the UK include:

1. Silver or Platinum

Silver or platinum shades are great for cool skin tones and can make you look sophisticated and elegant. They can also help to brighten up your complexion and make you look more youthful.

2. Warm Brown

Warm brown shades, such as chestnut or caramel, are great for warm skin tones and can add depth and dimension to your hair. They're also very versatile and can work for both casual and formal occasions.

3. Red

Red shades, such as strawberry blonde or copper, can add warmth and vibrancy to your hair. They're also great for cool skin tones and can make your complexion look brighter.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone.

2. Look for hair colors that complement your skin tone.

3. Choose a hair color that won't cause further damage to your hair.

4. Consult with a hairdresser if you're not sure what color to choose.

5. Maintain healthy hair by using a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims.

Tips

1. Avoid drastic color changes that might damage your hair.

2. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prolong the life of your hair color.

3. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

4. Choose a color that complements your style and personality.

FAQ

What hair colors should I avoid if I'm over 60?

If you're over 60, you should avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light, as they can make you look washed out or harsh. You should also avoid colors that are too trendy or bold, as they might not be as flattering as you age.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's recommended to consult with a hairdresser first, especially if you're not experienced with hair color. A hairdresser can help you choose the right color and provide tips on how to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it's important to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. You should also avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair. Getting regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, the best hair colors for over 60s in the UK depend on your skin tone and personal preferences. By choosing the right color and maintaining healthy hair, you can look and feel your best at any age.
