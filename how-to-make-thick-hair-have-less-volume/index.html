How To Make Thick Hair Have Less Volume

Written by Petter May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have thick hair that always seems to have too much volume? You're not alone. Many people with thick hair struggle to keep it under control. But don't worry, there are ways to make your thick hair have less volume. In this article, we'll show you how to do just that.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Thick Hair Have Less Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Thick Hair Volume
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Hair

How to Make Thick Hair Have Less Volume

There are several things you can do to make your thick hair have less volume:

  • Get a haircut
  • Use the right products
  • Blow dry your hair properly
  • Straighten your hair
  • Try different hairstyles

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your thick hair have less volume:

  1. Get a haircut that thins out your hair. Ask your hairstylist to use thinning shears, which will remove some of the bulk from your hair.
  2. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add body to your hair without making it too big.
  3. Apply a smoothing product to your hair before blow drying. This will help to tame frizz and reduce volume.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down, pulling your hair straight as you go.
  5. Straighten your hair with a flat iron. This will help to smooth out any remaining volume.
  6. Try different hairstyles that work with your hair type. A sleek ponytail or a low bun can help to keep your hair under control.

Tips for Managing Thick Hair Volume

Here are some additional tips for managing thick hair volume:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look even bigger.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz and volume.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair, as this will help to distribute natural oils and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it more prone to frizz and volume.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick hair volume:

Q: Can I use a volumizing product on my thick hair?

A: Yes, but be careful not to use too much. A small amount can help to add body, but too much can make your hair look too big.

Q: Will using a flat iron damage my hair?

A: It can if you use it too often or at too high of a temperature. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the flat iron every day.

Q: Can I still have volume in my hair if I want?

A: Of course! You can still add volume to your hair if you want, but be sure to use the right products and techniques to keep it under control.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can make your thick hair have less volume while still keeping it healthy and beautiful. Remember, the key is to use the right products and techniques for your hair type.
