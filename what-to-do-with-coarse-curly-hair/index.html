What To Do With Coarse Curly Hair A Guide To Taming And Styling

Written by Christine Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have coarse curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage and style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can tame your locks and achieve the perfect look. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about what to do with coarse curly hair, from hair care to styling tips.

Description

Coarse curly hair is characterized by thick strands that are tightly curled or coiled. This hair type can be challenging to manage, as it tends to be dry, brittle, and prone to frizz. However, with the right care and styling techniques, you can make the most of your natural texture and achieve a beautiful, healthy look.

How to Care for Coarse Curly Hair

The key to taming coarse curly hair is to keep it healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips for caring for your locks:

1. Wash your hair less frequently

Coarse curly hair tends to be dry, so you don’t want to strip it of its natural oils by washing it too often. Aim to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.

2. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair, so look for a shampoo that is sulfate-free and designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your locks moisturized and prevent frizz.

3. Condition, condition, condition

Conditioner is your friend when it comes to coarse curly hair. Use a hydrating conditioner after every wash, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks moisturized.

4. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools can be damaging to curly hair, so try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to minimize damage.

How to Style Coarse Curly Hair

Once you’ve got your hair care routine down, it’s time to move on to styling. Here are some tips for achieving the perfect look:

1. Use a curl defining product

A good curl defining product can work wonders for coarse curly hair. Look for a product that will help to define your curls without weighing them down.

2. Try the pineapple method

The pineapple method is a popular technique for preserving curls overnight. To do this, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie. This will help to prevent your curls from getting crushed while you sleep.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles

Coarse curly hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from braids to updos to half-up/half-down styles. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

4. Embrace the frizz

Finally, don’t be afraid to embrace the frizz. Coarse curly hair has a natural texture that can be beautiful when styled correctly. Work with your curls and embrace the natural volume and texture.

Hair Care FAQ

Q: Can I brush my coarse curly hair?

A: It’s generally not recommended to brush curly hair, as this can disrupt the natural curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your locks.

Q: What should I do if my hair is still frizzy after styling?

A: If you’re experiencing frizz after styling, try using a frizz-fighting product, such as a serum or oil. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help minimize frizz.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Coarse curly hair can be a challenge to manage and style, but with the right care and styling techniques, you can make the most of your natural texture and achieve a beautiful, healthy look. Remember to keep your locks moisturized, experiment with different styles, and embrace your natural texture. With a little patience and practice, you’ll be able to tame your locks and achieve the perfect look.
