How To Fix A Wedding Dress That Is Too Short

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 Â· 10 min read

In every bride's dream wedding, the wedding dress is a vital part of the occasion. It's the centerpiece that makes the bride look stunning and the talk of the wedding. However, sometimes, a bride may find that her wedding dress is too short, which can cause some distress. But, there's no need to worry, because in this blog post, we will show you how to fix a wedding dress that is too short.

Description

When a bride finds out that her wedding dress is too short, it can be frustrating and overwhelming. However, it's essential to remain calm and know that there are solutions. The most common reason why a wedding dress may be too short is that the bride has purchased the wrong size or has lost weight since the wedding dress was fitted. It can also be due to the dress shrinking in the wash or the bride wearing the wrong shoes. Whatever the reason may be, it's essential to find a solution to fix it.

How to Fix a Wedding Dress That is Too Short

Fixing a wedding dress that is too short is not as complicated as it may seem. Here are some tips on how to fix a wedding dress that is too short.

1. Add a Lace Hem

If your wedding dress has a lace hem, you can add more lace to the hem to make it longer. This will not only add length to your dress but also enhance its beauty.

2. Add a Ruffle Hem

If your wedding dress is made of a lightweight fabric, adding a ruffle hem can add length to it. The ruffle hem will also add volume to the bottom of your dress, making it look more elegant and elongating it.

3. Wear High Heels

If your wedding dress is too short, wearing high heels can help add some length to your dress. However, make sure to practice walking in your shoes before your big day to avoid any discomfort or accidents.

4. Add a Tulle Skirt

If your wedding dress is too short and you don't want to add more fabric to it, adding a tulle skirt underneath can be a great option. The tulle skirt will add length to your dress and also give it a fuller look.

5. Let Out the Hem

If your wedding dress was fitted with a hem, you can let it out to add some length to your dress. However, this may not work if the hem was already let out during the initial fitting.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to fix a wedding dress that is too short:

Step 1: Determine the length of the dress you need

Before you start fixing your wedding dress, you need to determine how much length you need to add.

Step 2: Choose the method you want to use

Choose the method you want to use to fix your wedding dress. You can add a lace or ruffle hem, wear high heels, add a tulle skirt, or let out the hem.

Step 3: Take your dress to a professional tailor

If you're not confident in fixing your wedding dress yourself, take it to a professional tailor. They will be able to give you advice on the best method to use and fix your dress for you.

Step 4: Practice walking in your shoes

If you decide to wear high heels, make sure to practice walking in them before your big day. This will help you avoid any discomfort or accidents.

Step 5: Try on your dress after it's been fixed

After your dress has been fixed, try it on to make sure it fits perfectly. This will give you peace of mind on your big day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix your wedding dress:

  • Make sure to purchase the correct size when buying your wedding dress.
  • Try on your dress with the shoes you plan to wear on your big day.
  • Take your dress to a professional tailor if you're not confident in fixing it yourself.
  • Practice walking in your shoes before your big day to avoid any discomfort or accidents.
  • Make sure to try on your dress after it's been fixed to ensure it fits perfectly.

FAQ

Q: Can I fix my wedding dress myself?

A: Yes, you can fix your wedding dress yourself if you're confident in your sewing skills. However, it's recommended to take it to a professional tailor if you're not confident.

Q: How long does it take to fix a wedding dress?

A: The time it takes to fix a wedding dress depends on the method used and the extent of the alterations. It can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks.

Q: Can I wear flats with my wedding dress?

A: Yes, you can wear flats with your wedding dress. However, make sure to take the length of your dress into consideration and ensure it's not too short.

Hair Care

When fixing a wedding dress that is too short, it's essential to also take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Get a trim a few weeks before your big day to ensure your hair looks healthy and fresh.
  • Wash your hair the morning of your wedding to ensure it's clean and fresh.
  • Use a hair mask a few days before your wedding to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Fixing a wedding dress that is too short can be stressful, but with these tips, you can ensure your dress looks perfect on your big day. Remember to take care of your hair as well to complete your stunning look.
