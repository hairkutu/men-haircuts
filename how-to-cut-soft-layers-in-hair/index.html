How To Cut Soft Layers In Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking dull and lifeless? Adding soft layers can give you the volume and movement you’ve been looking for. In this post, we’ll show you how to cut soft layers in hair with our step-by-step guide. We’ll also share some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Soft Layers in Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Soft Layers in Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips After Cutting Soft Layers

How to Cut Soft Layers in Hair

Cutting soft layers in hair is a technique used to add volume and movement to your hair. It involves cutting the hair at different lengths, creating layers that blend together seamlessly. It’s important to have the right tools and technique to achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Now, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Make sure it’s completely dry before you begin cutting.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.
  3. Start with the bottom section of your hair. Comb through it to get rid of any tangles.
  4. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers.
  5. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle of the section and working your way down to the end. Make sure you cut at an angle to create a soft layer.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for the rest of the bottom section.
  7. Move on to the next section and repeat steps 3-6 until you’ve cut all the sections of your hair.
  8. When you’ve finished cutting all the sections, use a comb to blend the layers together.
  9. Check for any unevenness and make any necessary adjustments.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Soft Layers in Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect soft layers:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more later.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don’t cut too much at once. It’s better to cut a little at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Cut the hair at an angle to create a soft layer.
  • Make sure the layers blend together seamlessly.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting soft layers in hair:

Q: Can I cut soft layers in curly hair?
A: Yes, you can cut soft layers in curly hair. However, it’s important to use the right technique to avoid creating frizz or unevenness. Q: How often should I cut soft layers in my hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how much you want to maintain the look. On average, cutting soft layers every 8-12 weeks is recommended. Q: Can I cut soft layers in my own hair?
A: It’s possible to cut soft layers in your own hair, but it’s recommended to have a professional do it to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care Tips After Cutting Soft Layers

After cutting soft layers in your hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the layers.

Now that you know how to cut soft layers in hair, it’s time to give it a try. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to achieve the perfect look.
