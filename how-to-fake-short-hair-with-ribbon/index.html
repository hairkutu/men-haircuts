How To Fake Short Hair With Ribbon

Written by Daniel Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you dreaming of having short hair, but are not ready to commit to a haircut? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to fake short hair with ribbon. With just a few easy steps, you can achieve the illusion of having a chic and trendy short hairstyle.

How To:

Step 1: Start by gathering your hair in a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Take a ribbon that matches your hair color and tie it around the base of your ponytail. Make sure to secure it tightly.

Step 3: Divide your ponytail into two sections and twist them together.

Step 4: Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins.

Step 5: Use a comb or your fingers to gently pull out some hair from the top of your head for a more natural look.

Step 6: Finish off the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

- Use a ribbon that matches your hair color for a seamless look.

- If you have thick hair, use a larger ribbon to make sure it covers the entire ponytail.

- Experiment with different ribbon styles and textures for a unique look.

FAQ:

Q: Will this hairstyle work on all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can work on all hair types, from straight to curly.

Q: How long will this hairstyle last?

A: With the right amount of hairspray, this hairstyle can last all day.

Hair Care:

Make sure to use a good quality hairspray that will keep your hairstyle in place without damaging your hair. Also, be gentle when removing the bobby pins to avoid any hair breakage or damage.

Overall, faking short hair with ribbon is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a haircut. Give it a try and see how you like it!
