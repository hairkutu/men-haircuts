What Should A Bride Over 50 Wear

Written by Robby Apr 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As a bride over 50, you may have different ideas about what you want to wear on your wedding day compared to when you were younger. You may want to choose a dress that flatters your figure, suits your style, and represents your personality. In this post, we will discuss how to choose the right attire for a bride over 50, including hair care, tips, and frequently asked questions.

Description: What to consider when choosing a dress?

When choosing a dress, it is important to consider your body shape, skin tone, and personal style. You want to choose a dress that flatters your figure and makes you feel confident on your big day. Consider the following:

Body Shape:

If you have an apple-shaped body, consider a dress with a fitted bodice and a flowing skirt to balance out your figure. If you have a pear-shaped body, consider a dress with a fitted bodice and a full skirt to accentuate your waist. If you have an hourglass-shaped body, consider a dress that accentuates your curves.

Skin Tone:

If you have warm skin tones, consider dresses in ivory, champagne, or gold. If you have cool skin tones, consider dresses in white, silver, or pale pink.

Personal Style:

Consider your personal style when choosing your dress. If you prefer a more traditional look, consider a classic A-line dress. If you prefer a more modern look, consider a dress with a high-low hemline or a jumpsuit.

How to Choose a Dress?

Choosing a dress can be overwhelming, but there are some steps you can take to make the process easier:

Set a Budget:

Before you start shopping, set a budget for your dress. This will help you narrow down your options and avoid overspending.

Research Styles:

Research different styles of dresses and make a list of the ones you like. This will help you narrow down your options and make the shopping process easier.

Try on Dresses:

When you go dress shopping, try on different styles to see what flatters your figure and makes you feel confident. Be open to trying on dresses you may not have considered before.

Accessorize:

Once you have chosen your dress, accessorize it with jewelry, shoes, and a veil or headpiece. These accessories will help complete your look and make you feel like a bride.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Dress

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing a dress:

Step 1: Set a Budget

Before you start shopping, set a budget for your dress.

Step 2: Research Styles

Research different styles of dresses and make a list of the ones you like.

Step 3: Try on Dresses

When you go dress shopping, try on different styles to see what flatters your figure and makes you feel confident.

Step 4: Accessorize

Once you have chosen your dress, accessorize it with jewelry, shoes, and a veil or headpiece.

Tips for Choosing the Right Attire

Here are some tips for choosing the right attire:

Choose Comfortable Shoes:

You will be on your feet for most of the day, so choose comfortable shoes that you can wear all day.

Consider the Weather:

Consider the weather when choosing your dress. If you are getting married in the summer, choose a lightweight dress. If you are getting married in the winter, choose a dress with long sleeves.

Consider the Venue:

Consider the venue when choosing your dress. If you are getting married on a beach, choose a dress that is lightweight and easy to move in. If you are getting married in a church, choose a dress that is more traditional.

FAQs

What should a bride over 50 wear?

A bride over 50 should wear a dress that flatters her figure, suits her style, and represents her personality.

What colors are best for a bride over 50?

If you have warm skin tones, consider dresses in ivory, champagne, or gold. If you have cool skin tones, consider dresses in white, silver, or pale pink.

Should a bride over 50 wear a veil?

It is up to the bride to decide if she wants to wear a veil. A veil can add a traditional touch to your wedding day look.

Hair Care for Brides Over 50

When it comes to hair care, there are a few things you can do to ensure your hair looks its best on your wedding day:

Get a Trim:

Get a trim a few weeks before your wedding day to ensure your hair looks healthy and fresh.

Use Quality Hair Products:

Use quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Consider a Hair Accessory:

Consider a hair accessory, such as a flower or a headband, to add some extra flair to your wedding day look.

Conclusion

Choosing the right attire for a bride over 50 may seem daunting, but with these tips and steps, you can find the perfect dress for your big day. Remember to consider your body shape, skin tone, and personal style when choosing your dress, and don't forget to accessorize to complete your look. Lastly, take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best on your wedding day.
