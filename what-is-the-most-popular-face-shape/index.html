Description

Have you ever wondered what the most popular face shape is? Well, wonder no more! In this article, we will discuss the most popular face shape and how to determine your own face shape. We will also provide you with hair care tips for different face shapes.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we reveal the most popular face shape, let's first determine your own face shape. Here's how:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead, Cheekbones, and Jawline

Using a measuring tape, measure the widest part of your forehead, the widest part of your cheekbones, and the widest part of your jawline. Write down these measurements.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements, compare them to the following face shapes:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, cheekbones are high and prominent
  • Round: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width and have a strong, angular jaw
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline and chin is pointed
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, forehead and jawline are narrow
  • Triangle: Jawline is the widest part of the face, forehead is narrow

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle works well with an oval face shape, but avoid styles that cover your forehead.
  • Round: Add volume and height on top of your head to elongate your face. Avoid blunt bangs and chin-length haircuts.
  • Square: Soften your strong jawline with layers and waves. Avoid straight and blunt hairstyles.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and chin-length bobs work well with a heart-shaped face. Avoid full and voluminous hairstyles.
  • Diamond: Create width at the forehead and chin with layers and bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add height on top of your head.
  • Triangle: Balance your jawline with volume and layers on top of your head. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Here are some additional hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Oval: Keep your hair away from your forehead to avoid covering your face. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Round: Use a straightening iron to create sleek and straight hairstyles. Avoid adding volume to the sides of your head.
  • Square: Use a deep conditioning treatment to soften your hair and add shine. Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or angular.
  • Heart: Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.
  • Diamond: Use a smoothing serum to tame any flyaways. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your head.
  • Triangle: Use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your jawline.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shapes:

  • What is the most popular face shape? The most popular face shape is oval.
  • Can face shape change? No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and cannot be changed.
  • Can hairstyles change the appearance of my face shape? Yes, certain hairstyles can make your face shape appear different.
  • What if my face shape doesn't fit into any of these categories? It's possible that you have a combination of face shapes or a unique face shape. Consult with your hairstylist for personalized hair care tips.
