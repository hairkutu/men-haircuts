How To Make Non Heat Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of using heat on your hair to achieve curls? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to make non heat curls that will leave you with bouncy, beautiful locks. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Make Non Heat Curls

Non heat curls are achieved by using various techniques that do not require heat tools like curling irons or flat irons. Some of the most popular non heat curling methods include:

  • Braiding
  • Twisting
  • Bantu knots
  • Flexi rods
  • Roller sets

Each of these methods produces different types of curls, so it's important to choose the one that will give you the look you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

For this tutorial, we'll be using the braiding method. Here's how to make non heat curls using braids:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. If your hair is dry, mist it lightly with water.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The more sections you have, the tighter your curls will be.
  3. Take one section and divide it into three strands.
  4. Braid the three strands together, starting as close to your scalp as possible.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the ends of your hair.
  6. Tie off the braid with a hair elastic.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 for each section of hair.
  8. Leave the braids in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  9. Remove the hair elastics and gently unravel the braids.
  10. Use your fingers to style your curls as desired.

Voila! You now have non heat curls.

Tips for Non Heat Curling

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when making non heat curls:

  • Use clean, damp hair
  • Apply a styling product, like mousse or curl cream, to help hold your curls
  • Don't wrap your hair too tightly around the curling tool
  • Be patient! Non heat curls take longer to set than heat curls
  • Protect your curls while you sleep by wearing a silk or satin scarf or using a silk or satin pillowcase

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will non heat curls last?

Non heat curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type, the method you use, and how well you maintain your curls.

Can non heat curls damage my hair?

No, non heat curls do not damage your hair like heat tools can. However, you should still take care to protect your hair from breakage and dryness.

Can I make non heat curls on short hair?

Yes, non heat curls can be done on hair of any length. You may just need to adjust the size of your sections or the type of curls you make.

Hair Care for Non Heat Curls

To keep your non heat curls looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of moisture
  • Protect your curls from heat, wind, and sun damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt your curls
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful non heat curls without damaging your hair. Happy styling!
