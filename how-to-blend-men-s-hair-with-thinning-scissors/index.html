How To Blend Men s Hair With Thinning Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning scissors are a great tool for achieving a natural-looking blended haircut. They work by removing some of the hair while leaving the rest intact, creating a gradual transition between the longer and shorter hair. In this article, we'll show you how to blend men's hair with thinning scissors in a few easy steps.

How to Blend Men's Hair with Thinning Scissors

Blending men's hair with thinning scissors is a simple process that can be done by anyone. The key is to use the right tools and follow the right steps. Here's what you'll need:

  • Thinning scissors
  • Regular scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to blend men's hair with thinning scissors:

  1. Wash and dry the hair. This will make it easier to work with.
  2. Section the hair. Use hair clips to divide the hair into sections, starting from the back of the head and moving forward. Leave one section of hair down to work on at a time.
  3. Use the regular scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. This will create a base for the haircut.
  4. Use the thinning scissors to blend the hair. Hold the scissors parallel to the hair and make small cuts, starting from the middle of the hair and working your way to the ends. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  5. Move to the next section of hair and repeat the process. Make sure to blend the hair evenly on both sides of the head.
  6. Check the hair in the mirror. Look for any unevenness or spots that need more blending. Use the thinning scissors to make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Finish the haircut. Use the regular scissors to clean up any stray hairs and create the final shape.

Tips for Blending Men's Hair with Thinning Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when blending men's hair with thinning scissors:

  • Start with dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to work with and may result in a different final look than expected.
  • Use the right thinning scissors. Look for scissors with a higher number of teeth for more blending and a lower number for less blending.
  • Practice on a mannequin or volunteer before working on a real client. This will help you get comfortable with using the scissors and avoid any mistakes.
  • Be patient. Blending hair with thinning scissors takes time and precision. Don't rush the process or you may end up with an uneven haircut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about blending men's hair with thinning scissors:

  • Can thinning scissors be used on all types of hair? Thinning scissors can be used on most hair types, but they work best on thick or coarse hair.
  • How often should I blend my hair with thinning scissors? This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it's recommended to blend hair every 4-6 weeks.
  • Can I blend my hair with thinning scissors at home? Yes, you can blend your hair with thinning scissors at home, but it's recommended to get professional training or practice on a mannequin first.

Hair Care Tips for After Blending with Thinning Scissors

After blending men's hair with thinning scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut and prevent split ends.

Blending men's hair with thinning scissors is a great way to achieve a natural-looking haircut. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it. Follow the steps in this guide and you'll be on your way to a perfectly blended haircut in no time.
