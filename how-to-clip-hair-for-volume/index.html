How To Clip Hair For Volume A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of limp, lifeless hair? Do you want to add more volume to your locks? Clipping your hair is a simple and effective way to add volume to your hair without using any products or heat tools. In this article, we'll show you how to clip hair for volume in a few easy steps.

  • Tips for Clipping Hair for Volume

How to Clip Hair for Volume

Clipping your hair is a quick and easy way to add volume to your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start clipping it. Wet hair will not hold the clips properly.

Step 2: Choose Your Clips

Choose the right clips for your hair. You can use butterfly clips or alligator clips. Butterfly clips are better for thicker hair, while alligator clips are better for finer hair. You'll need at least six clips for this process.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into six sections. Start by dividing your hair in half horizontally, then divide each half into three sections vertically.

Step 4: Clip Your Hair

Take one section of hair and clip it to the crown of your head. Make sure the clip is secure and tight. Repeat this process for all six sections of your hair.

Step 5: Wait

Leave the clips in your hair for at least 30 minutes. You can leave them in longer if you want more volume.

Step 6: Remove the Clips

Remove the clips from your hair. Shake your hair out to give it more volume. Style your hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to clip hair for volume:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Choose Your Clips

Choose the right clips for your hair. You can use butterfly clips or alligator clips. Butterfly clips are better for thicker hair, while alligator clips are better for finer hair. You'll need at least six clips for this process.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into six sections. Start by dividing your hair in half horizontally, then divide each half into three sections vertically. Use a comb to make sure the sections are even and straight.

Step 4: Clip Your Hair

Take one section of hair and clip it to the crown of your head. Use a butterfly clip or an alligator clip to secure it tightly. Make sure the clip is close to your scalp to give your hair more lift. Repeat this process for all six sections of your hair.

Step 5: Wait

Leave the clips in your hair for at least 30 minutes. You can leave them in longer if you want more volume. You can also use a hair dryer to speed up the process.

Step 6: Remove the Clips

Remove the clips from your hair. Shake your hair out to give it more volume. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Clipping Hair for Volume

Here are some tips to make sure you get the best results when clipping your hair for volume:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair more body before clipping it.
  • Clip your hair when it's about 80% dry to help speed up the process.
  • Make sure the clips are tight and secure to give your hair more lift.
  • Leave the clips in your hair for at least 30 minutes to get the best results.
  • Use a hair dryer to speed up the process if you're in a hurry.

FAQs

Q: How long should I leave the clips in my hair?

A: Leave the clips in your hair for at least 30 minutes to get the best results. You can leave them in longer if you want more volume.

Q: What type of clips should I use?

A: You can use butterfly clips or alligator clips. Butterfly clips are better for thicker hair, while alligator clips are better for finer hair.

Q: Can I use this method on curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use this method on curly hair. Just make sure your hair is dry before clipping it.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and voluminous:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair more body.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can easily add more volume to your hair without using any products or heat tools. Give it a try and enjoy your new bouncy, voluminous locks!
