Best Long Hairstyles For Round Faces Male A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can accentuate your features and create a flattering look. In this article, we will discuss the best long hairstyles for round faces male, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Styling Tips
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and shape. Long hairstyles work well for round faces, as they elongate the face and create a slimming effect. Layers add texture and movement, while a side part or swept-back look can help to create angles and definition.

Some of the best long hairstyles for round faces male include:

1. Long Layered Cut

A long layered cut is a great option for round faces, as it adds volume and movement while creating a slimming effect. Layers can be added throughout the length of the hair, or focused around the face to frame the features. This style can be worn straight or wavy, and looks great with a side part.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and definition around the face, which is especially important for those with round features. This style works well with a longer haircut, and can be worn straight or with a slight wave. Side-swept bangs can be cut to any length, depending on the desired effect.

3. Textured Layers

Textured layers add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. This style works well with a slightly messy, tousled look, and can be achieved through styling products like sea salt spray or texturizing paste.

Styling Tips

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's important to style your hair properly to achieve the desired effect. Here are some styling tips for the best long hairstyles for round faces male:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a texturizing product like sea salt spray or texturizing paste to add definition and hold.
  • Try a side part or swept-back look to create angles and definition around the face.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, like curling or straightening, to find the look that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best long hairstyles for round faces male:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a texturizing product like sea salt spray or texturizing paste.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, using a side part or swept-back look to create angles and definition.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for the best long hairstyles for round faces male:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Try different parting styles to create different looks and angles.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best cut and style for your face shape and features.

FAQ

What is the best long hairstyle for a round face?

The best long hairstyle for a round face is a layered cut with texture and movement, paired with a side part or swept-back look.

Can I wear my hair straight or do I need to add waves?

You can wear your hair straight or add waves, depending on your preference. Straight hair can create a sleek, polished look, while waves can add texture and movement.

Do I need to use styling products to achieve the best long hairstyles for round faces male?

Styling products can help to add texture, definition, and hold to your hair, but they are not necessary. You can achieve a great look with just a blow dryer and round brush, or by using a curling iron or straightener.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut and styling, you can achieve the best long hairstyles for round faces male and feel confident and stylish every day.
