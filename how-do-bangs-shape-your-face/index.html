How Do Bangs Shape Your Face A Complete Guide

Are you thinking of getting bangs but not sure if they will suit your face shape? Bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, it's important to know how different types of bangs can shape your face.

What are Bangs?

Bangs, also known as a fringe, are a hair styling technique where hair is cut and styled to fall over the forehead. They can be cut straight across, side-swept, or layered, and can vary in length and thickness.

How Do Bangs Shape Your Face?

Bangs can be used to accentuate or balance out different face shapes. Here's how different types of bangs can shape your face:

Straight Across Bangs

Straight across bangs can make a round face appear even rounder, so they are best suited for longer face shapes. They can also make a high forehead appear shorter.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften and balance out angular features on square and heart-shaped faces. They can also make a wide forehead appear narrower.

Layered Bangs

Layered bangs can add volume and texture to fine hair. They can also soften sharp angles on angular face shapes.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

When choosing the right bangs for your face shape, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style as well. Here are some tips:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, go for side-swept bangs that fall diagonally across your forehead. Avoid straight across bangs, which can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, go for soft, wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. Avoid blunt, straight across bangs, which can make your features appear harsher.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, go for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side. Avoid blunt, straight across bangs that can make your forehead appear even wider.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pretty much pull off any type of bangs! Try experimenting with different lengths and styles to find what suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Own Bangs

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape, with the point of the triangle at the center of your forehead.
  3. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  4. Cut the hair straight across, starting from the outer corners and working your way towards the center.
  5. Check your work and make any adjustments as necessary.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you have your bangs, it's important to maintain them with regular trims and styling. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush for a smooth finish.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb oil and sweat between washes.

FAQs about Bangs and Face Shape

Q: Can bangs make my face appear slimmer?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can help balance out wider features and make your face appear slimmer.

Q: Can bangs make my forehead appear shorter?

A: Yes, straight across bangs can make a high forehead appear shorter.

Q: Can bangs make my face appear rounder?

A: Yes, straight across bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. If you have a round face shape, go for side-swept bangs instead.

Conclusion

Bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape to flatter your features. With the right maintenance and styling, bangs can be a fun and versatile addition to your hair routine.
