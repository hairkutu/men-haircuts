Simple Hairdos For Medium Length Hair Easy

Written by Eveline May 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have medium length hair, there are plenty of simple and easy hairstyles that you can try out. Whether you're looking for something quick and easy for a busy day or a more polished look for a special occasion, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore some of the best simple hairdos for medium length hair easy that you can try out.

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you have straight, wavy or curly hair, there is a hairstyle that will suit you. Simple hairdos for medium length hair easy are perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spend styling their hair. These hairstyles are quick and easy to do, but they still look stylish and put together.

How to

One of the easiest hairstyles for medium length hair is a simple ponytail. To create this look, brush your hair back and gather it into a high or low ponytail. Secure it with an elastic band and you're done. You can also add some volume to your ponytail by backcombing the crown of your head before you gather your hair into a ponytail.

Another simple hairdo for medium length hair is a messy bun. To create this look, gather your hair into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, relaxed look.

If you want to add some waves to your hair, a simple way to do this is by using a curling iron. You can create loose waves by wrapping sections of your hair around the curling iron barrel and holding it for a few seconds. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more relaxed look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush your hair and gather it into a high or low ponytail.

Step 2: Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.

Step 3: For a more voluminous look, backcomb the crown of your head before gathering your hair into a ponytail.

Step 4: To create a messy bun, gather your hair into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun.

Step 5: Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, relaxed look.

Step 6: To add waves to your hair, use a curling iron to wrap sections of your hair around the barrel.

Step 7: Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the hair.

Step 8: Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more relaxed look.

Tips

When creating a ponytail, make sure to position it at the right height for your face shape. A high ponytail can elongate your face, while a low ponytail can balance out a round face.

When creating a messy bun, don't worry about making it perfect. The point of this hairstyle is to look relaxed and effortless.

When using a curling iron, make sure to use heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What are some other simple hairdos for medium length hair easy?

Other simple hairdos for medium length hair easy include a half-up, half-down hairstyle, a side braid, and a top knot.

How can I make my hair look fuller?

You can make your hair look fuller by using volumizing products, backcombing the crown of your head, and adding waves or curls to your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. Use heat protectant spray when using a curling iron or straightener. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. And finally, eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these simple hairdos for medium length hair easy, you can create a variety of stylish looks without spending a lot of time or effort. Whether you're heading to work, running errands, or going out for a night on the town, these hairstyles will keep you looking and feeling your best.
