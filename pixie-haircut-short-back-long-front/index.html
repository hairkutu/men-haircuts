The Pixie Haircut Short Back Long Front A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that is low maintenance, the pixie haircut short back long front is the perfect choice. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years and is perfect for anyone looking for a dramatic change or a fresh new look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the pixie haircut short back long front, including how to get it, hair care tips, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Pixie Haircut Short Back Long Front
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for the Pixie Haircut

Description

The pixie haircut short back long front is a haircut where the back of the hair is cut short, while the front is left longer. This creates a dramatic and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out. This haircut is a great option for those who want to have a low-maintenance hairstyle that is still stylish and trendy.

How to Get the Pixie Haircut Short Back Long Front

The first step in getting a pixie haircut short back long front is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Once you have found a stylist, you should discuss with them the type of look you are going for and the length of the haircut you want. It's important to be clear with your stylist about your expectations so that they can deliver the look you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the pixie haircut short back long front:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  2. Once your hair is dry, section it off into four parts: the back, the sides, and the top.
  3. Start at the back of your head and cut your hair short, using a comb and scissors. You can also use clippers if you prefer a more dramatic look.
  4. Once the back is cut, move on to the sides and cut them to the desired length, blending them into the back.
  5. Finally, cut the top of your hair, leaving it longer than the back and sides. This will create the signature short back long front look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie haircut short back long front:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the look that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the pixie haircut short back long front:

1. Is the pixie haircut short back long front suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types, but it may require some extra effort for those with thicker or coarser hair.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look of your pixie haircut short back long front.

3. Can I style my hair differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as slicking it back or adding waves.

Hair Care Tips for the Pixie Haircut

Here are some hair care tips that will help keep your pixie haircut short back long front looking its best:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as this can damage the hair and cause it to break.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Overall, the pixie haircut short back long front is a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for anyone looking for a dramatic change. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your pixie haircut looking its best and turn heads wherever you go.
