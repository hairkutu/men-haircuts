How To Make Your Hair Curl Up For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you want to add some curls to your hair for a new look? Many guys struggle with how to make their hair curl up, but don't worry, it's not as difficult as you might think. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curl up for guys.

Description

Before we begin, let's talk about the different types of hair curls. There are two main types of curls: natural curls and styled curls. Natural curls are the curls that you were born with, whereas styled curls are the ones that you create with the help of hair products and styling tools. In this article, we'll be focusing on styled curls.

How to

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to creating styled curls is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

After washing your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a mousse, curl cream, or gel. Make sure to evenly distribute the product through your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls. To do this, place a section of your hair in the diffuser and cup it with your hand. Move the diffuser towards your scalp and hold it in place for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until your hair is dry.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If you want more defined curls, you can use a curling iron. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that is suitable for your hair length. Section your hair and wrap each section around the curling iron, holding it in place for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finish with a Hairspray

To help your curls stay in place, finish with a hairspray. Choose a hairspray with a strong hold that is suitable for your hair type. Hold the hairspray can about 12 inches away from your hair and spray evenly over your curls.

Tips

- If you have naturally curly hair, you can enhance your curls by using a curl-enhancing product and blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment.

- If you have straight hair, you can create curls by using a curling iron and a curl-enhancing product.

- Don't overdo it with the product. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a flat iron to create curls?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to create curls. To do this, section your hair and twist each section before clamping it with the flat iron. Hold it in place for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, your curls should last for a few hours to a whole day.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. This means washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, make sure to get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to create curls in your hair and switch up your look. Remember to experiment with different products and styling tools to find what works best for you.
