How To Make Semi Curly Hair Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of having semi curly hair that doesn't quite satisfy your desire for bouncy, voluminous curls? Look no further than this complete guide on how to make semi curly hair curly. In this article, we'll cover everything from the basics to advanced techniques to give you the curly locks of your dreams.

Table of Contents

  • The Basics
  • How to Make Semi Curly Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Process
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Semi Curly Hair

The Basics

Semi curly hair is hair that has a slight wave or curl to it, but not enough to be considered full-on curly hair. To make semi curly hair curly, you'll need to use a combination of techniques to enhance the natural curl pattern of your hair.

Some factors that can affect your hair's curl pattern include genetics, hair care routine, and styling techniques. If you're unsure about your hair type or how to care for it, consult a hair stylist or professional for advice.

How to Make Semi Curly Hair Curly

There are several techniques you can use to make semi curly hair curly:

1. Scrunching

Scrunching involves applying a curl-enhancing product, like mousse or gel, to damp hair and scrunching it upwards towards your scalp. This technique helps to define your curls and create volume.

2. Diffusing

Diffusing is a technique that involves using a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair while maintaining your natural curl pattern. To diffuse, start by applying a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and then use the diffuser attachment to dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting.

3. Twisting

Twisting involves taking small sections of hair and twisting them around your finger to create defined curls. This technique works best on damp hair and can be used in combination with a curl-enhancing product.

4. Roller Sets

Roller sets can be used to create voluminous, bouncy curls. To do a roller set, start by applying a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and then use rollers to set your hair in place. Allow your hair to dry completely before removing the rollers.

Step-by-Step Process

Here's a step-by-step process for making semi curly hair curly:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, like mousse or gel, to damp hair. Use a generous amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp to enhance your natural curl pattern. Repeat this process until you've scrunched all of your hair.

Step 4: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while maintaining your natural curl pattern. Start by using a low heat setting and increase the heat as needed.

Step 5: Twist your hair

Take small sections of hair and twist them around your finger to create defined curls. Repeat this process until you've twisted all of your hair.

Step 6: Roller set your hair

Use rollers to set your hair in place and create voluminous, bouncy curls. Allow your hair to dry completely before removing the rollers.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can disrupt your natural curl pattern
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and defined
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as they can damage your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I make semi curly hair curly without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use techniques like scrunching, twisting, and roller sets to enhance your natural curl pattern without using heat styling tools.

Q: What should I do if my curls fall flat?

A: Try using a curl-refreshing spray or reapplying a curl-enhancing product to revive your curls throughout the day.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care for Semi Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your semi curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments on your hair, as they can damage your natural curl pattern
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz

With these tips and techniques, you can transform your semi curly hair into bouncy, voluminous curls that will turn heads. Remember to consult a hair stylist or professional if you're unsure about your hair type or how to care for it.
