What Are The 8 Pop Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

If you're a fan of Animal Crossing, you know that one of the best parts of the game is customizing your character. And what's a better way to do that than changing up your hairstyle? In this article, we'll be talking about the 8 Pop Hairstyles in Animal Crossing, how to get them, and some tips for hair care in the game. So, let's get started!

What are the 8 Pop Hairstyles?

The 8 Pop Hairstyles in Animal Crossing are the following:

  • Bun
  • Mohawk
  • Pixie
  • Ponytail
  • Short Bob
  • Top Knot
  • Twist
  • Updo

Each hairstyle has its own unique look that can suit different personalities and outfits. Whether you want to look cute or edgy, there's a style for you!

How to Get the Pop Hairstyles

To get the Pop Hairstyles in Animal Crossing, you'll need to purchase them from the Able Sisters shop. The hairstyles will be available for purchase once you've unlocked the shop, which happens after you've spent a certain amount of money at the Nook's Cranny store.

Once you've unlocked the Able Sisters shop, head over and check out the selection of hairstyles. You'll find the Pop Hairstyles under the "Top 8 Pop Hairstyles" section of the menu.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the Pop Hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  1. Unlock the Able Sisters shop by spending a certain amount of money at Nook's Cranny.
  2. Visit the Able Sisters shop.
  3. Check out the "Top 8 Pop Hairstyles" section of the menu.
  4. Choose the hairstyle you want to purchase.
  5. Pay for the hairstyle.
  6. Head over to a mirror in your house or the Able Sisters shop to try on the hairstyle.

Tips for Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair in Animal Crossing:

  • Visit Harriet the hair stylist in the Shampoodle shop to change your hair color and eye color.
  • Wear a hat or headband to change up your look without changing your hairstyle.
  • Use a mirror to see what your hairstyle looks like from different angles.
  • Use a hairbrush to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my hairstyle after I've purchased one?

A: Yes, you can change your hairstyle at any time by purchasing a new one from the Able Sisters shop.

Q: Can I change my hair color?

A: Yes, you can change your hair color by visiting Harriet the hair stylist in the Shampoodle shop.

Q: Can I wear a hat with any hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat or headband with any hairstyle.

Q: Can I use a hairbrush on my character's hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hairbrush to keep your character's hair looking neat and tidy.

And there you have it! The 8 Pop Hairstyles in Animal Crossing, how to get them, and some tips for hair care in the game. Have fun customizing your character's look!
