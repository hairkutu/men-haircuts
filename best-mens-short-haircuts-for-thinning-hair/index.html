Best Mens Short Haircuts For Thinning Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you one of the many men out there who are struggling with thinning hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. In fact, it's estimated that by the age of 50, around 85% of men will experience some degree of hair loss. But the good news is that there are plenty of great haircuts out there that can help you to look and feel your best, even with thinning hair.

Why Choose a Short Haircut?

When it comes to thinning hair, shorter haircuts are generally the way to go. This is because shorter haircuts can help to create the illusion of thicker hair by reducing the contrast between your scalp and your hair. Additionally, shorter haircuts are often easier to style and maintain, which can be a big plus if you're dealing with hair loss.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for You

Choosing the right short haircut for your thinning hair can be a bit of a challenge, but there are a few things to keep in mind that can help you to make the right choice. Here are some tips:

  • Consider your face shape: Different face shapes are better suited to different haircuts, so make sure you choose a haircut that complements your face shape.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have thin, fine hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick, coarse hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that helps to thin it out.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you don't have a lot of time to spend on styling your hair, you'll want to choose a low-maintenance haircut that looks great with minimal effort.
  • Consult a professional: If you're not sure which haircut is right for you, consider consulting a professional hairstylist who can help you to choose a style that works with your hair type and face shape.

The Best Short Haircuts for Men with Thinning Hair

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for your thinning hair, let's take a look at some of the best short haircuts for men:

1. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that can work well for men with thinning hair. This cut involves trimming your hair short all over with clippers, which can help to create the illusion of thicker hair. Plus, it's easy to maintain and looks great with minimal effort.

2. The High and Tight

The high and tight is another classic short haircut that can work well for men with thinning hair. This cut involves keeping your hair short on the sides and back while leaving a bit more length on top. This can help to create the illusion of thicker hair on top while still maintaining a clean, polished look.

3. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a versatile short haircut that can work well for men with thinning hair. This cut involves keeping your hair short all over with clippers while leaving a bit more length on top. This can help to create the illusion of thicker hair on top while still maintaining a clean, polished look.

4. The Side Part

The side part is a classic short haircut that can work well for men with thinning hair. This cut involves keeping your hair short on the sides and back while leaving a bit more length on top. You can then style your hair to the side for a polished, professional look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've chosen the right short haircut for your thinning hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemical treatments like hair dyes and relaxers, which can further damage your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions about the best short haircuts for men with thinning hair? Here are some frequently asked questions:

Q: Is it possible to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, there are a few things you can do to make your hair look thicker. Choosing the right haircut is one of them, but you can also try using hair thickening products like volumizing shampoos and mousses.

Q: Will a short haircut make my hair grow back thicker?

A: No, a short haircut won't actually make your hair grow back thicker. However, it can create the illusion of thicker hair by reducing the contrast between your scalp and your hair.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still style your short haircut. Depending on the cut you choose, you may be able to use a bit of product like wax or gel to create different looks.

Conclusion

Thinning hair can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to hold you back. With the right short haircut, you can look and feel your best no matter what. Whether you opt for a buzz cut, high and tight, crew cut, or side part, there's a short haircut out there that will work for you. Just remember to take good care of your hair and consult a professional if you're not sure which cut is right for you.
