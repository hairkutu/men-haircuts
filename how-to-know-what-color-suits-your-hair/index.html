How To Know What Color Suits Your Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you contemplating coloring your hair, but unsure of what color would flatter you the most? Choosing the right hair color is essential to enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty. In this article, we will take you through a step-by-step guide on how to know what color suits your hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care After Coloring

Description

The first thing to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural color of your skin, which can be warm, cool, or neutral. If your skin tone is warm, you will look best in hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, rich brown, or copper. If your skin tone is cool, you will look best in hair colors with cool undertones, such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy. If your skin tone is neutral, you can experiment with both warm and cool hair colors.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Determining your skin tone is easy. Here are three simple methods:

1. Vein Test

Look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear green, your skin tone is warm. If they appear blue, your skin tone is cool. If they appear both green and blue, you have a neutral skin tone.

2. Jewelry Test

Take a look at the jewelry you usually wear. If you prefer gold jewelry, your skin tone is warm. If you prefer silver jewelry, your skin tone is cool. If you look good in both, you have a neutral skin tone.

3. Sunburn Test

Think back to the last time you got sunburnt. If your skin turned pink or red, your skin tone is cool. If your skin turned golden brown, your skin tone is warm. If your skin turned both pink and brown, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you have determined your skin tone, it's time to choose the right hair color. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Determine Your Undertones

Based on your skin tone, determine if you have warm or cool undertones. This will help you choose the right hair color with the same undertones.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence the hair color that suits you. For instance, if you have blue eyes, platinum blonde or ash brown hair color can make your eyes pop. On the other hand, if you have brown eyes, rich brown or dark red hair color can be a great choice.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also be a factor when choosing a hair color. For instance, if you have naturally dark hair, going for a lighter shade can be a dramatic change. However, if you have naturally blonde hair, going for a darker shade can give you a more sophisticated look.

4. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence the hair color you choose. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance hair color can be a great choice. On the other hand, if you have a more laid-back lifestyle, you can experiment with different hair colors and styles.

5. Seek Professional Help

If you're still unsure about what hair color suits you, seek professional help. A hair stylist can help you choose the right hair color based on your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe
  • Consider your makeup preferences when choosing a hair color
  • Choose a hair color that complements your personality
  • Choose a hair color that flatters your face shape

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. However, you should choose a hair dye that is free of harsh chemicals and irritants. You can also do a patch test before dyeing your hair to check for any allergic reactions.

2. How often should I color my hair?

The frequency of coloring your hair depends on the type of hair color and your hair growth rate. Generally, permanent hair color can last up to six weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to four weeks.

3. Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, you should follow the instructions carefully and choose a hair dye that is suitable for your hair type and color.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its shine and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after coloring
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight for long periods
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can find the perfect shade that flatters you. Follow the tips and guidelines in this article to choose the hair color that suits you best. Happy coloring!
