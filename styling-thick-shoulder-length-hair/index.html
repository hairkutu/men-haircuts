Styling Thick Shoulder Length Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you blessed with thick shoulder length hair but struggling with styling it? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide to help you style your hair like a pro. From how to section your hair to the best styling tools, we have got you covered. Read on to discover our tips and tricks for styling thick shoulder length hair.

Description

Thick shoulder length hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it has volume and body, it can also be difficult to manage and style. This is why it is essential to use the right styling techniques and products to achieve your desired look.

How to Section Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it is important to section it properly. This will make the styling process easier and ensure that you do not miss any sections. Start by dividing your hair into three sections: the top, middle, and bottom. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Top Section

Start by sectioning the top section of your hair, starting from the crown of your head. Use a hair clip to secure it in place.

Middle Section

Next, section the middle section of your hair, starting from the bottom of your top section to your ears. Secure it with a hair clip.

Bottom Section

Finally, section the bottom section of your hair, starting from the bottom of your middle section to the nape of your neck. Secure it with a hair clip.

How to Style Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you have sectioned your hair, it is time to style it. Here are some tips and tricks for styling thick shoulder length hair:

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is essential to achieve a smooth and sleek finish. Use a round brush to help distribute the heat evenly and add volume to your hair. Start by blow drying the bottom section of your hair, then move on to the middle and top sections.

Use a Flat Iron

If you want to achieve a straight and sleek look, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair.

Curl Your Hair

If you want to add some texture and volume to your hair, use a curling iron to create loose curls. Start from the middle section of your hair and work your way down to the ends.

Tips for Styling Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling thick shoulder length hair:

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a huge difference in the overall look and feel of your hair. Opt for products that are specifically designed for thick hair, such as volumizing mousse or thickening spray.

Avoid Overstyling

While it can be tempting to style your hair every day, overstyling can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your styling to a few times a week and use heat protectant products to minimize damage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

What is the Best Haircut for Thick Shoulder Length Hair?

The best haircut for thick shoulder length hair is a layered cut. This will help to reduce bulk and add movement to your hair.

How Can I Add Volume to My Thick Shoulder Length Hair?

You can add volume to your thick shoulder length hair by using volumizing products, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush to lift your roots.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will help to remove excess oil and dirt. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or as needed.

Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner will help to hydrate your hair and prevent breakage. Apply it to the middle and ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat styling can cause damage and breakage to your hair. Try to limit your heat styling and use heat protectant products to minimize damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your thick shoulder length hair like a pro. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to achieve your desired look.
