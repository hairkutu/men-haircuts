Best Pixie Haircuts For Over 50 A Guide To Flattering Short Hairstyles

Written by Petter May 06, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re over 50 and looking for a fresh new hairstyle, a pixie cut might be just what you need. Pixie haircuts are short and sassy, and they can be customized to suit your unique style and personality. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the best pixie haircuts for over 50, and provide tips and advice for keeping your hair healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Best Pixie Haircut for Over 50
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Haircut
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut
  4. FAQs About Pixie Haircuts for Over 50
  5. Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

How to Choose the Best Pixie Haircut for Over 50

When choosing a pixie haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips for finding the perfect pixie haircut:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a pixie cut with volume on top can help elongate your face.
  • Consider your hair texture. If you have thin or fine hair, a layered pixie cut can add volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, a choppy pixie cut can help thin it out.
  • Think about your personal style. Do you prefer a classic look or something more edgy and modern? A sleek, polished pixie cut might be perfect for a classic look, while a messy, textured pixie cut can give you a more contemporary vibe.

Best Pixie Haircuts for Over 50

Now that you know how to choose a pixie haircut, let’s take a look at some of the best styles for women over 50:

  1. The Classic Pixie Cut: This timeless style features short, layered hair with longer pieces on top that can be styled in a variety of ways.
  2. The Textured Pixie Cut: This modern style is choppy and layered, with lots of texture and movement.
  3. The Side-Swept Pixie Cut: This asymmetrical style features longer hair on one side that can be swept across the forehead for a dramatic effect.
  4. The Edgy Pixie Cut: This bold style features a shaved or undercut section on one side, with longer hair on top that can be styled in various ways.
  5. The Messy Pixie Cut: This casual style is tousled and textured, with lots of volume and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Haircut

If you’ve decided to take the plunge and get a pixie haircut, here’s what to expect:

  1. Consultation: Your stylist will discuss your desired style, hair type, and face shape to determine the best pixie haircut for you.
  2. Preparation: Your hair will be washed and combed to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Cutting: Your stylist will use scissors and/or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length and style.
  4. Styling: Your stylist will blow-dry and style your hair, using products to add volume, texture, and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut

Once you’ve got your pixie haircut, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.
  • Use styling products to add volume, texture, and hold.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

FAQs About Pixie Haircuts for Over 50

Q: Will a pixie haircut work for my face shape?

A: Yes! There are many different pixie styles that can flatter all face shapes. Consult with your stylist to find the best style for you.

Q: Can I still style my pixie haircut in different ways?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.

Q: Will a pixie haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: While a pixie cut does require regular trims to maintain its shape, it can actually be less maintenance than longer hairstyles.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

Here are some tips for keeping your pixie haircut looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

A pixie haircut can be a bold and stylish choice for women over 50. With the right style and care, you can rock a short and sassy look that flatters your unique features and personality. So go ahead and take the plunge – you might just fall in love with your new pixie cut!
