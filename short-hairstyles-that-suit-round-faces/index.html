Short Hairstyles That Suit Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by the width and length of the face being almost the same, with rounded cheeks and a soft jawline. When choosing a short hairstyle, it is essential to consider the face shape to ensure that the hairstyle flatters your features.

In this article, we will explore different short hairstyles that suit round faces, giving you a variety of options to choose from.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle that suits a round face, it is essential to consider the following:

  • The length of the hair
  • The shape of the cut
  • The texture of the hair
  • The styling options available

Short hairstyles that suit round faces typically have layers and volume to add height and elongate the face. Avoid hairstyles that add width or volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs or straight bangs.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the short hairstyles that suit round faces:

Short Bob

  1. Start by dividing the hair into sections.
  2. Using a straightening iron, straighten the hair from the root to the tips.
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of the head and backcomb it to create volume.
  4. Smooth the hair over the backcombed section and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat the process for the rest of the hair.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by washing and conditioning the hair.
  2. Blow-dry the hair with a round brush to add volume.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the hair.
  4. Using your fingers, style the hair into a messy, textured look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a short hairstyle that suits a round face:

  • Consider consulting a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width or volume to the sides of the face.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers and volume to add height and elongate the face.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the best look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles that suit round faces:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and elongates the face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume at the crown.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, it is best to avoid straight bangs that add width to the face. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or textured fringe that soften the face.

Can I still wear a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still wear a bob if you have a round face. However, it is important to choose a layered bob that adds height and volume to the crown, rather than a blunt bob that adds width to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the best look for you.

With the right short hairstyle, you can flatter your round face and show off your unique style. Use the tips and techniques in this article to find a short hairstyle that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
