How To Cut Short Curly Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short curly hair can be a challenge to maintain, but cutting it yourself at home can save you time and money. In this post, we'll walk you through the steps to ensure you get the perfect cut every time. Whether you're looking for a simple trim or a complete style change, this guide will help you achieve your desired look.

How to Cut Short Curly Hair Yourself

Before you begin cutting your hair, it's important to gather the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you begin cutting. This will help you see the natural shape and texture of your hair, making it easier to cut. Avoid cutting your hair when it's wet, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you ensure that you cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Begin cutting your hair using the scissors. Start with small sections and cut in a straight line. Avoid cutting too much at once, as this can lead to uneven hair. Remember to keep your scissors sharp to avoid snagging your hair.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you've finished cutting, check for evenness by running your fingers through your hair. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them until they're even.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with the length and shape of your hair, style it as desired. Use a curling iron or hair products to enhance your natural curls.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair Yourself

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with small sections and cut slowly.
  • Avoid cutting your hair when it's wet.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to avoid snagging your hair.
  • Check for evenness after each section.
  • Style your hair after you've finished cutting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. Just be sure to follow the steps outlined in this guide and take your time.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires a different type of hair care than straight hair. Here are a few tips to help you care for your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to cut short curly hair yourself, it's time to give it a try. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect cut every time. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps outlined in this guide.
