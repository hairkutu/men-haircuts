Best Haircut For Long Oblong Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your long oblong face? Look no further! In this guide, we'll go over the best haircuts for long oblong faces, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Description

A long oblong face is characterized by a longer length than width, with a straight jawline and forehead. The key to finding the best haircut for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face, while avoiding styles that elongate the face even further.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for long oblong faces, it's important to determine whether you actually have this face shape. Here's how:

  1. Measure your face from forehead to chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone.
  2. If the length is more than the width, and your jawline and forehead are straight, you have a long oblong face shape.
  3. If the length and width are equal, you have a square face shape.
  4. If the width is greater than the length and you have a rounded jawline, you have a round face shape.

Best Haircuts for Long Oblong Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's go over the best haircuts for long oblong faces:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob with volume at the sides is perfect for adding width to a long oblong face. Ask your stylist for layers that start at chin-level and angle down towards the front.

2. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, opt for long layers that start at the collarbone and angle towards your face. This creates the illusion of width and adds movement to your locks.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long face shape. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at the eyebrow and angle towards the cheekbone.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with textured layers at the sides can add volume and width to a long oblong face shape. This style works especially well for those with fine hair.

Step by Step: How to Achieve the Best Haircut for Long Oblong Face

Follow these steps to achieve the best haircut for long oblong face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Choose a haircut from the list above.
  3. Print out a picture of the style you want and bring it to your stylist.
  4. Discuss any modifications that may be needed to suit your hair texture and face shape.
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Follow these tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner for your hair type.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that elongate the face, such as straight and long hair. Also avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear longer.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a long oblong face?

Absolutely! Opt for a low ponytail with volume at the sides to add width to your face.

What if I don't like any of the suggested haircuts?

That's okay! These are just suggestions. The most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Hair Care for Long Oblong Faces

In addition to regular trims and using quality hair products, it's important to nourish your hair from the inside out. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, and drink plenty of water to keep your locks looking their best.

By following these tips, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your long oblong face shape.
