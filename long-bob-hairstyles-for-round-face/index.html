Long Bob Hairstyles For Round Face The Perfect Look For You

Written by William Jul 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle that doesn’t accentuate your round face? Look no further than the long bob hairstyle! This trendy hairstyle has been around for years and is a go-to for those who want a chic and sophisticated look. In this article, we’ll discuss how to achieve the perfect long bob hairstyle for a round face, step-by-step tips, and hair care to keep your locks looking fabulous.

Description

The long bob hairstyle, also known as a lob, is a versatile cut that falls between the collarbone and shoulders. This hairstyle suits all face shapes, including round faces. It’s a perfect hairstyle that adds volume and angles to your face, making it look slimmer and more defined. The long bob hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style for any occasion.

How to Achieve Long Bob Hairstyles for Round Face?

First things first, it’s always best to consult with a professional hairstylist who can help you determine the best long bob hairstyle for your round face. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long bob hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine the Length

The length of your long bob hairstyle will depend on your face shape. If you have a round face, the ideal length is just below the chin or longer. This length will help elongate your face and add more angles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to the cut, the long bob hairstyle can be styled in many ways. For round faces, it’s best to add layers to the hair, starting from the chin down. This will add volume to the hair, making your face look slimmer.

Step 3: Style Your Long Bob

The long bob hairstyle can be styled in many ways, including straight, wavy, or curly. If you have a round face, it’s best to go for a side-parted hairstyle. This will help elongate your face and add more angles to it.

Step-by-Step Tips

Here are some step-by-step tips to help you achieve the perfect long bob hairstyle for your round face:

Tip 1: Use the Right Hair Products

It’s essential to use the right hair products to keep your long bob hairstyle looking fabulous. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. For styling, use a lightweight mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tip 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair is essential to achieve the perfect long bob hairstyle. Use a round brush to add volume to your hair while blow-drying it.

Tip 3: Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add curls to your long bob hairstyle, use a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 ¼ inches. Curl your hair in sections, starting from the middle of your hair down to the ends.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for hair care to keep your long bob hairstyle looking fabulous:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are crucial to keep your long bob hairstyle looking its best. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Tip 2: Use Hair Masks

Use hair masks once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy. Hair masks help to repair damaged hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling. Also, avoid using hot styling tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ

Q1: Can I get a long bob hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A1: Yes, you can! The long bob hairstyle can be styled in many ways, including straight, wavy, or curly. If you have curly hair, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair to add volume and angles to your face.

Q2: How often should I wash my hair?

A2: It’s recommended to wash your hair every two to three days. Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it look dry and dull.

Q3: Can I style my long bob hairstyle at home?

A3: Yes, you can! The long bob hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style for any occasion. Use a lightweight mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Conclusion

The long bob hairstyle is a perfect hairstyle for those with a round face. It’s versatile, low maintenance, and easy to style for any occasion. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can achieve the perfect long bob hairstyle for your round face. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right hair products, getting regular trims, and protecting it from heat damage. With the right care and maintenance, your long bob hairstyle will look fabulous all year round!
