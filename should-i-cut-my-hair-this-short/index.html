Should I Cut My Hair This Short A Guide To Making The Decision

Written by Petter May 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on whether or not to cut your hair short! This decision can be a tough one, but we’re here to help you through it. Below, we’ll cover everything you need to know about taking the plunge and chopping off your locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you’re considering cutting your hair short, you may be wondering what the benefits and drawbacks are. Short hair can be easier to manage and style, and it can also make you feel more confident and empowered. However, it may take some getting used to and you may feel like you’re missing out on the versatility of longer hair.

Ultimately, the decision to cut your hair short is a personal one. You should do what makes you feel happy and confident.

How To

If you’ve decided to cut your hair short, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture.
  • Consider how much maintenance the style will require.
  • Find a reputable stylist who has experience cutting short hair.

Step By Step

Here’s what you can expect during a short haircut appointment:

  1. The stylist will consult with you about the style you want and make recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.
  2. They will then shampoo and condition your hair.
  3. The stylist will section off your hair and begin cutting it to the desired length.
  4. Once the hair is cut, the stylist may use texturizing techniques or add layers to create movement and volume.
  5. The hair will then be blow-dried and styled.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your short hair:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for short hair.
  • Use styling products to enhance your hair’s texture and volume.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to avoid tangled hair while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can actually make your face look longer and more angular.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short hair can actually make you look more youthful and vibrant.

Q: Will my hair grow back if I regret cutting it?

A: Yes, hair does grow back. However, it may take some time to get back to your desired length.

Hair Care

Caring for short hair is relatively easy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Try using dry shampoo to extend the time between washes.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

So, should you cut your hair short? Ultimately, it’s up to you. We hope this guide has helped you make the decision that’s right for you.
