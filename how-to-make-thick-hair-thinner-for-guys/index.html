How To Make Thick Hair Thinner For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. While it looks great when styled properly, it can be a hassle to manage and can make you feel hot and uncomfortable during the summer months. In this guide, we'll be discussing how to make thick hair thinner for guys, so you can achieve a more manageable and comfortable hairstyle.

Description

Thick hair is characterized by having a lot of hair strands on your head, which can make it difficult to style and manage. This can be especially frustrating during the summer months, when thick hair can make you feel hot and uncomfortable. However, by following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can learn how to make thick hair thinner for guys.

How to Make Thick Hair Thinner for Guys: Step by Step

1. Get a Haircut

One of the easiest ways to make thick hair thinner for guys is to get a haircut. Talk to your hairstylist about your hair type and ask for a haircut that will thin out your hair while still maintaining a stylish look.

2. Use Thinners

Thinners are a type of scissors that are designed to remove some of the bulk from your hair. Ask your hairstylist to use thinners during your haircut to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

3. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can also help make your thick hair thinner. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair, as these can help make your hair appear thinner and more manageable. Avoid heavy products like gels and waxes, as these can weigh down your hair and make it appear thicker.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help make it appear thinner. Use a low heat setting and a round brush to blow dry your hair, which will help create volume and texture while also removing some of the bulk from your hair.

Tips for Making Thick Hair Thinner for Guys

Here are some additional tips for making thick hair thinner for guys:

  • Use a lightweight conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too heavy and bulky.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it appear thicker.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it appear thicker.

FAQs

Can I make my thick hair thinner without cutting it?

While getting a haircut is the easiest way to make thick hair thinner, you can also use thinners, blow dry your hair, and use the right products to achieve a thinner appearance.

What products should I use to make my thick hair thinner?

Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair, such as volumizing shampoos and lightweight conditioners. Avoid heavy products like gels and waxes, as these can weigh down your hair and make it appear thicker.

How often should I wash my thick hair?

Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it appear thicker. Instead, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care for thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it appear thicker.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to make thick hair thinner for guys and achieve a more manageable and comfortable hairstyle. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
