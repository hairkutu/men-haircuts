Best Hairstyle For Round Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face and curly hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape while also embracing your natural curls. In this article, we'll take a look at the best hairstyle for round face curly hair, along with tips and tricks to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wider forehead, soft jawline, and rounded chin. When it comes to curly hair, this face shape can be tricky to style. The key is to find a cut that elongates your face and adds definition to your features. A layered cut is a great option, as it adds volume and shape without overwhelming your face. Opt for longer layers that start around your cheekbones and blend into the rest of your hair.

How To

When it comes to styling curly hair, it's important to work with your natural texture. Start by applying a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to damp hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow-dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or add more shape to your layers.

Step By Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow-dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or add more shape to your layers.

Tips

  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your curly hair, as it can disrupt your natural curl pattern and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they're wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for round face curly hair?

The best haircut for round face curly hair is a layered cut with longer layers starting around the cheekbones.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and not to do it too often, as it can cause damage.

How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your curly hair, avoid brushing it, use a curl-enhancing product, and sleep on a silk pillowcase.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your curly hair, it's important to use products that nourish and hydrate your locks. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant when you do. Trim your hair regularly to keep your layers looking fresh, and avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your curls.

With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock the best hairstyle for round face curly hair. Embrace your natural curls and show off your beautiful features!
