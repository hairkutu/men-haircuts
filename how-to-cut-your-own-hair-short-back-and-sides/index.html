How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Back And Sides

If you're looking for a way to save money and time on haircuts, learning how to cut your own hair short back and sides is a great skill to have. With a few tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to get started.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Back and Sides

The first thing you need to do is gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair before cutting.

Next, decide on the length you want for your hair. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down in size until you get the length you want.

Once you've trimmed the sides and back, it's time to move on to the top of your head. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can either cut straight across or use a point-cutting technique to create texture.

Step by Step Guide

1. Gather all necessary tools: clippers, scissors, comb, mirror, and spray bottle filled with water.

2. Decide on the desired length for the sides and back of your hair.

3. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to the desired length.

4. Start with a longer guard and work your way down in size until you reach the desired length.

5. Dampen the top of your hair with the spray bottle.

6. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

7. Cut straight across or use a point-cutting technique to create texture.

8. Check the back and sides of your head in the mirror to make sure the length is even.

9. Make any necessary adjustments.

10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair

- Take your time and don't rush the process.

- Use a comb to section off your hair and make it easier to cut.

- Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down in size.

- Use the scissors to create texture on the top of your hair.

- Check the back and sides of your head in the mirror often to make sure the length is even.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: It's okay! Mistakes happen. If you make a mistake, try to even it out as best you can or seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. You can also use styling products to achieve your desired look. Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

Learning how to cut your own hair short back and sides can be a great way to save time and money. With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Take your time, be patient, and enjoy the process!
