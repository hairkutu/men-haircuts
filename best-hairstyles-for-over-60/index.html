Best Hairstyles For Over 60 Tips Tricks And Styles

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its natural shine. However, this doesn't mean you can't have fabulous hair in your 60s and beyond. With the right styling, your hair can look beautiful and youthful. Here are some of the best hairstyles for over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Short Hair
  • Medium Hair
  • Long Hair
  • Updos
  • Bangs
  • Gray Hair
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Short Hair

Short hair is a great option for women over 60 because it's easy to maintain and can make you look younger. A pixie cut, for example, can help you achieve a youthful and modern look. You can also try a bob haircut with layers to add volume and texture to your hair.

Medium Hair

Medium-length hair is another great option for women over 60. A shoulder-length bob with layers can create a flattering and youthful look. Another option is to add bangs to your medium-length hair to create a chic and sophisticated look.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can still rock a youthful and stylish look. Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and texture. You can also try a side-swept bang to add a touch of elegance to your long locks.

Updos

Updos are a fantastic option for special occasions or when you want to elevate your look. Consider trying a low chignon or a messy bun for a chic and sophisticated look. You can also add a headband or hairpins to your updo to add some sparkle and glamour.

Bangs

Bangs can make a significant difference in your overall appearance. They can help you look younger and add a touch of sophistication to your look. Consider trying side-swept bangs or a wispy fringe to add some texture and style to your hair.

Gray Hair

Gray hair can be beautiful and elegant, especially when styled correctly. Consider trying a short pixie cut or a bob with layers to add volume and texture to your gray hair. You can also try adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your age, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thinning hair?

A pixie cut or a layered bob can help add volume and texture to thinning hair.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A shoulder-length bob with layers can help elongate a round face.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

Consider adding layers to your hair or using volumizing products to add texture and volume. You can also try a shorter haircut, such as a pixie cut or a bob, to make your hair look thicker.


With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your age and hair type. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. With the right styling and care, you can look fabulous and youthful at any age.
