How To Do Messy Bun With Long Thin Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of struggling to style your long, thin hair? Do you want to achieve a chic and effortless look with a messy bun? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to do a messy bun with long, thin hair.

Description

A messy bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. It is perfect for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair or when you want a relaxed and effortless look. However, creating a messy bun with long, thin hair can be a bit challenging because the hair can easily slip out of the bun. But don't worry, with the right techniques, you can achieve a messy bun that will stay in place all day long.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have all the necessary tools: a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray. Follow these steps to achieve a messy bun with long, thin hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles. Apply some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some texture and volume to your hair. This will make it easier to create the messy bun.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair Up

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure the ponytail is placed at the crown of your head.

Step 3: Create the Bun

Twist your ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Pull out some strands of hair around your face to create a messy look. You can also pull out some strands of hair from the bun to add more texture and volume.

Step 4: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair. Spray some hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Add some hair accessories, such as a headband or a scarf, to make your messy bun look more fashionable.
  • If you have very thin hair, you can use a hair donut to create a fuller-looking bun.
  • Don't worry if your messy bun is not perfect. The beauty of this hairstyle is its imperfection.

FAQ

Can I do a messy bun with short hair?

Yes, you can. However, you may need to use more bobby pins to secure the bun.

How can I make my messy bun look more polished?

You can use a smoothing serum or a pomade to tame any flyaways and make your bun look more polished.

Hair Care

To keep your long, thin hair healthy, make sure you use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.

Now that you know how to do a messy bun with long, thin hair, you can rock this hairstyle on any occasion. Whether you're going to work, school, or a party, a messy bun is the perfect hairstyle for a relaxed and effortless look.
