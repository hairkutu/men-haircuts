Best Straw Hat For Round Face

Written by Petter May 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for the perfect straw hat to complement your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best straw hat for round face shape and how to choose the right one for you. We will also be sharing some tips on how to style your hair with your new hat and how to take care of it to ensure it lasts for years to come.

Description

Straw hats have always been a popular choice for summer as they help protect your face and eyes from the sun's harmful rays. Not only are they functional, but they can also add a stylish touch to any outfit. However, not all hats are created equal, and when it comes to choosing the best straw hat for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind.

How to Choose the Best Straw Hat for Round Face

When choosing a straw hat for a round face, the goal is to find a hat that helps elongate your face and create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. Here are a few things to look for:

1. Brim Width

Look for a hat with a wide brim that is at least 3 inches. This will help balance out the roundness of your face and create a more elongated appearance.

2. Crown Height

A hat with a high crown can help create the illusion of a longer face. Look for a hat with a tall crown that is at least 4 inches.

3. Angled Brim

An angled brim can also help elongate your face. Look for a hat with a brim that slopes downward on one side.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Straw Hat

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a straw hat for a round face, here is a step by step guide to help you find the perfect hat:

Step 1: Measure Your Head

Before you start shopping, measure the circumference of your head to ensure you get the right size hat. Most hats come in sizes small, medium, and large, so be sure to check the sizing chart before making a purchase.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

While this article is focused on round faces, it's important to determine your face shape to ensure you find a hat that flatters your features. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. If you're not sure what your face shape is, ask a friend or consult a stylist.

Step 3: Try on Different Hats

Now it's time to start trying on hats! Look for hats with a wide brim, tall crown, and angled brim. Try on different styles, such as fedoras, sun hats, and cowboy hats, to see what looks best on you.

Step 4: Consider Your Outfit

When choosing a straw hat, it's important to consider the rest of your outfit. Look for a hat that complements your style and matches the colors and patterns of your clothing.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with a Straw Hat

Now that you've found the perfect straw hat, it's time to style your hair to complete the look. Here are a few tips:

1. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help elongate your face and create a more flattering silhouette. Tie your hair up in a ponytail on the top of your head and let the ponytail hang down over one shoulder.

2. Loose Waves

If you prefer to wear your hair down, try adding some loose waves. This will help create a more relaxed look that complements the casual vibe of a straw hat.

3. Braids

Braids are another great option for styling your hair with a straw hat. Try a side braid or a fishtail braid for a fun and playful look.

FAQs

What is the best type of straw hat for a round face?

The best type of straw hat for a round face is one with a wide brim, tall crown, and angled brim. Look for hats like fedoras, sun hats, and cowboy hats.

How do I measure my head for a straw hat?

To measure your head for a straw hat, use a flexible measuring tape to measure the circumference of your head just above your eyebrows and ears. Use this measurement to determine your hat size.

How do I take care of my straw hat?

To take care of your straw hat, avoid getting it wet and store it in a cool, dry place when not in use. Use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dirt or debris, and spot clean with a damp cloth if necessary.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a straw hat, it's important to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Before heading out into the sun, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help protect it from damage.

2. Wear a Scarf

Wearing a scarf around your hair can help protect it from the sun and prevent it from getting tangled in the wind.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

When possible, avoid using heat styling tools on your hair. Instead, opt for more natural styles like braids and loose waves.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect straw hat for your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful all summer long.
