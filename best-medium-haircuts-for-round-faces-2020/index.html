Best Medium Haircuts For Round Faces 2020

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face shape, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your features. In 2020, there were many fantastic medium haircuts for round faces that were on trend and stylish. In this article, we will discuss the best medium haircuts for round faces 2020.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. A medium-length haircut is perfect for a round face shape because it can help elongate the face while still allowing for versatility in styling.

How to

When choosing a medium haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and angles. A long bob that falls just below the chin is a great option because it creates the illusion of a longer face. Layers can also help add dimension and movement to the hair, which can be especially flattering for a round face.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research medium haircuts for round faces.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.

Step 4: Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style.

Tips

When styling medium haircuts for round faces, it's important to add volume and texture to the hair. This can be achieved through the use of volumizing products and by styling the hair in loose waves or curls. Adding highlights or lowlights can also help create depth and dimension in the hair, which can be especially flattering for a round face shape.

FAQ

What are the best medium haircuts for round faces?

The best medium haircuts for round faces are those that create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. A long bob that falls just below the chin, a shoulder-length haircut with layers, or a shaggy cut with choppy layers are all great options.

How can I style my medium haircut for a round face?

To style a medium haircut for a round face, add volume and texture to the hair through the use of volumizing products and by styling the hair in loose waves or curls. Adding highlights or lowlights can also help create depth and dimension in the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your medium haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair. Instead, air dry your hair whenever possible or use a heat protectant spray before using a blow dryer or flat iron. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best medium haircuts for round faces 2020 were those that created the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. A long bob, a shoulder-length cut with layers, or a shaggy cut with choppy layers were all great options. When styling your medium haircut, add volume and texture to the hair through the use of volumizing products and by styling the hair in loose waves or curls. By taking care of your hair with regular trims and high-quality hair care products, you can keep your medium haircut looking its best.
