How To Style Short Fine Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find ways to style your short fine wavy hair? Look no further! In this article, we will go over different techniques and products to help you achieve the perfect look for your hair type.

Description

Short fine wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it is easy to maintain and style. On the other hand, it can be difficult to achieve volume and hold a style. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks to help you embrace your natural waves and make the most out of your short fine hair.

How To

Before we dive into styling techniques, it is important to start with a good foundation. When washing your hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair. After washing, use a lightweight mousse or styling cream to enhance your natural waves.

Step 1: Blow Dry

Start by blow-drying your hair upside down to create volume at the roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair as you dry it. Once your hair is fully dry, flip your hair back and use a diffuser attachment to dry your waves without disturbing them.

Step 2: Curl

If you want to add more definition to your waves, use a curling iron or wand to curl random sections of your hair. Make sure to curl in different directions for a natural look. Once you have curled all the sections of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create more volume.

Step 3: Tease

To add even more volume, tease your hair at the crown. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots. Use a comb to smooth out the top layer of hair and create a seamless look.

Tips

- Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair.

- Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

- Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or updos, to switch up your look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still use heat tools on my fine hair?

A: Yes, but it is important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings. Using a lower heat setting will help prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Should I brush my wavy hair?

A: No, brushing your wavy hair can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and voluminous hair. Make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished. Avoid using hot tools every day and give your hair a break from styling occasionally. Lastly, make sure to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently style your short fine wavy hair and make the most out of your natural waves. Remember to embrace your unique hair texture and have fun experimenting with different styles!
