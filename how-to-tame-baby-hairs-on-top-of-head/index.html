How To Tame Baby Hairs On Top Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Mar 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with pesky baby hairs on top of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with trying to tame these tiny strands of hair that seem to have a mind of their own. But with the right techniques and products, you can easily manage and style your baby hairs to achieve a sleek and polished look. In this article, we'll show you how to tame baby hairs on top of your head in just a few simple steps.

Description

Baby hairs are those short, wispy strands of hair that tend to stick up or curl around your hairline. They can be especially problematic for people with curly or frizzy hair types, as they tend to be more noticeable and harder to control. However, with a little patience and the right styling tools, you can easily tame your baby hairs and incorporate them into your overall hairstyle.

How to Tame Baby Hairs on Top of Head

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

The first step in taming your baby hairs is to wet your hair. This will help to soften and smooth out your hair, making it easier to style. You can either wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, or simply wet your hair with a spray bottle or damp towel.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a gel, pomade, or hair wax, depending on your hair type and styling preferences. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the areas where your baby hairs tend to stick up.

Step 3: Use a Toothbrush or Spoolie Brush

Using a toothbrush or spoolie brush, gently brush your baby hairs down and towards your hairline. This will help to smooth out your hair and keep your baby hairs in place. You can also use a small amount of hairspray to help hold your baby hairs in place.

Step 4: Use a Hairpin

If you have particularly stubborn baby hairs, you can use a hairpin to help hold them in place. Simply slide the hairpin into your hair, just behind your baby hairs, and gently push your baby hairs down towards your hairline. Make sure to position the hairpin in a way that is comfortable and secure.

Tips for Taming Baby Hairs on Top of Head

Tip 1: Don't Overdo It

When it comes to taming your baby hairs, less is more. Don't apply too much product or use too much force when brushing your hair, as this can actually make your baby hairs stand up more.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Products

There are many different styling products available that can help you tame your baby hairs. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and styling needs.

Tip 3: Be Patient

Taming your baby hairs can take some time and practice. Be patient and keep trying different techniques until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What Causes Baby Hairs?

Baby hairs are caused by new hair growth around your hairline. They are particularly common in people with curly or frizzy hair types, as the new hair tends to be more noticeable and harder to control.

Can You Permanently Get Rid of Baby Hairs?

While you can't permanently get rid of baby hairs, you can manage and style them to make them less noticeable. You can also try using hair removal techniques, such as waxing or threading, to remove your baby hairs. However, these methods can be painful and may cause skin irritation.

Hair Care for Taming Baby Hairs on Top of Head

When taming your baby hairs, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily tame your baby hairs and achieve a sleek and polished look. Don't let pesky baby hairs ruin your hairstyle – take control and show off your beautiful locks with confidence!
