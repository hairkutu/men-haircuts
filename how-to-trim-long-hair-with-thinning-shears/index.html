How To Trim Long Hair With Thinning Shears A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Trimming long hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have thick hair that tends to get tangled easily. However, with the right tools and technique, it can be a breeze. In this article, we will guide you on how to trim long hair with thinning shears. Thinning shears are a great tool for removing bulk and creating texture in your hair. Whether you want to create layers, remove split ends or simply thin out your hair, thinning shears are an excellent choice.

How to Trim Long Hair with Thinning Shears

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about what thinning shears are and how they work. Thinning shears are scissors that have teeth on one blade and a smooth edge on the other. When you cut your hair with thinning shears, the teeth on the blade remove some of the hair, creating a softer, more textured look.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

2. Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles.

3. Decide on the length you want to cut your hair.

4. Section your hair into manageable parts. You can use hair clips to hold each section in place.

5. Take one section of hair and hold it straight up.

6. Use the thinning shears to cut into the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

7. Be careful not to cut off too much hair at once. It's better to make small cuts and work your way up.

8. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

9. Once you have finished cutting, brush your hair again to remove any loose hairs.

10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

1. Always use sharp thinning shears. Blunt shears can damage your hair and cause split ends.

2. Use the thinning shears sparingly. Overusing them can make your hair look too thin and wispy.

3. If you are not confident in cutting your hair with thinning shears, consider seeking the help of a professional hair stylist.

FAQ

1. Can I use thinning shears on wet hair?

It is not recommended to use thinning shears on wet hair as the hair can stretch and be cut unevenly.

2. How often should I use thinning shears on my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. It's best to consult with a professional hair stylist for recommendations.

3. Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

Thinning shears can be used on most hair types, but it's best to consult with a professional hair stylist if you have very curly or coarse hair.

Hair Care

After trimming your hair with thinning shears, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

3. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

4. Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using the right technique, you can easily trim your long hair with thinning shears. Remember to take your time and make small cuts to achieve the desired look. Happy trimming!
