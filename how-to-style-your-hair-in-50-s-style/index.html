How To Style Your Hair In 50 S Style

Written by Frank Jul 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to style your hair in the classic 50's style. This iconic look has been a favorite for decades and still remains relevant today. In this article, we will take you through step by step on how to achieve the perfect 50's hairstyle, including tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking fabulous. Whether you're attending a themed party or just want to add a touch of vintage charm to your everyday look, this guide will help you achieve the perfect 50's hairstyle.

Description

The 50's hairstyle is characterized by its voluminous curls and sleek, smooth appearance. This style was popularized by celebrities like Marilyn Monroe and Audrey Hepburn, and has since become a timeless classic. It's perfect for any occasion, from a night out on the town to a formal event.

How to

To achieve the perfect 50's hairstyle, you will need a few tools and products. Here's what you will need:

  • A curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Hair clips
  • A teasing comb
  • A smoothing brush

Now that you have your tools and products, here's how to style your hair in 50's style:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron. Once your hair is dry, use a smoothing brush to remove any tangles and create a smooth base for your curls.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Using your curling iron, create tight curls all over your head. Start at the front and work your way towards the back. Once you've curled your entire head, clip your curls up to set them in place.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, gently tease your hair at the roots to create volume. Start at the crown and work your way towards the front. Once you've teased your hair, smooth the top layer with a smoothing brush.

Step 4: Set Your Hair

Spray your hair with hairspray to set your curls in place. Remove the clips from your curls and gently brush them out to create a soft, voluminous look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 50's hairstyle:

  • Use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls.
  • Tease your hair gently to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different hairsprays to find one that works best for your hair.
  • Accessorize with a headband or scarf for an extra touch of vintage charm.

FAQ

What kind of curling iron should I use?

For tighter curls, use a smaller curling iron. For looser curls, use a larger curling iron.

How do I prevent damage to my hair?

Use a heat protectant before using your curling iron, and avoid using too much heat.

How do I maintain my 50's hairstyle?

Use hairspray to keep your curls in place, and touch up your hair with a smoothing brush throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, be sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

We hope this guide has helped you achieve the perfect 50's hairstyle. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a timeless look that never goes out of style.
