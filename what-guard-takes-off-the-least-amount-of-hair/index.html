What Guard Takes Off The Least Amount Of Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to trim your hair without taking off too much length, you're not alone. Many people want to maintain their hair length while still getting a clean and polished look. One way to achieve this is by using the right guard on your clippers.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use the Right Guard
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using the Right Guard
  • FAQ

Description

A guard is a plastic attachment that fits onto your clippers and determines how much hair is cut. Guards come in different sizes, ranging from 0.5mm to 25mm. The smaller the number, the shorter the hair will be cut. The larger the number, the longer the hair will be cut.

If you're looking to take off the least amount of hair possible, you'll want to use a guard with a higher number. A guard with a size of 6mm or higher will take off very little hair, while still giving you a neat and tidy look.

How to Use the Right Guard

Using the right guard is easy. Simply attach the guard to your clippers and turn them on. Run the clippers over your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to go slowly and evenly, so you don't miss any spots or take off too much hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Choose the right guard size for your desired length.
  2. Attach the guard to your clippers.
  3. Turn on the clippers.
  4. Start trimming your hair from the bottom, working your way up.
  5. Go slowly and evenly to avoid missing spots or taking off too much hair.
  6. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even and tidy.

Tips for Using the Right Guard

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using the right guard:

  • Start with a higher guard number and work your way down if you want shorter hair.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Take breaks if your clippers get too hot or if you're feeling fatigued.
  • Clean and oil your clippers regularly to keep them running smoothly.

FAQ

What guard size should I use for a buzz cut?

If you want a very short buzz cut, you'll want to use a guard with a size of 0.5mm to 3mm. For a slightly longer buzz cut, use a guard with a size of 4mm to 6mm.

What guard size should I use for a crew cut?

A crew cut typically has shorter hair on the sides and back, and slightly longer hair on the top. For the sides and back, use a guard with a size of 1mm to 3mm. For the top, use a guard with a size of 6mm to 12mm, depending on how long you want the hair to be.

Can I use a guard on wet hair?

It's best to use clippers on dry hair, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly. If you must use clippers on wet hair, make sure to go slowly and use a higher guard size to avoid taking off too much length.

Hair Care Tips

After trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.

Using the right guard on your clippers can help you achieve a polished look while maintaining your hair length. Remember to choose a guard with a higher number if you want to take off the least amount of hair possible. With the right technique and a little practice, you can trim your hair at home like a pro.
