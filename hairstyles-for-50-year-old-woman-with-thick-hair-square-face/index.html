Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Thick Hair Square Face

If you're a 50-year-old woman with a square face and thick hair, you're in luck! This combination of features can be a great canvas for a variety of hairstyles that flatter your face shape and show off your hair's natural volume. In this article, we'll share some tips and ideas for hairstyles that will help you look and feel your best.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for a Square Face and Thick Hair

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For a square face, you'll want to choose a style that softens your angular features and adds length to your face. Thick hair can be difficult to manage, but it also provides a lot of volume and body that can be used to your advantage.

Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle:

1. Consider Layers

Layered hairstyles can add movement and texture to thick hair, making it easier to manage and style. For a square face, layers can also soften your jawline and add dimension to your face. Consider a layered bob or long layers that frame your face.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, consider embracing your texture instead of trying to straighten it. Curly hair can add volume and softness to a square face, and it can be styled in a variety of ways. Consider a curly bob or shoulder-length waves.

3. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Blunt cuts can emphasize the squareness of a face and make it appear wider. Instead, consider a style with layers or soft, face-framing angles.

4. Try Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to add softness and dimension to a square face. Consider a side-swept fringe or wispy bangs that graze your eyebrows.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it to achieve the desired look. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, wet hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to protect it from heat damage and help it hold its shape. Use a volumizing mousse or a heat protectant spray.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create a curve at the ends.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a curling iron, flat iron, or hot rollers. Use these tools to create curls, waves, or straighten your hair, depending on your desired look.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to help it hold its shape and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have your hairstyle, there are some things you can do to maintain it and keep it looking great:

1. Schedule Regular Haircuts

To keep your hair looking healthy and stylish, schedule regular haircuts to trim split ends and maintain your style.

2. Use Quality Hair Care Products

Use quality shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. This will help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use heat protectant sprays and avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What are some good hairstyles for a square face?

Some good hairstyles for a square face include layered bobs, long layers, and styles with soft, face-framing angles. Bangs can also be a great way to add softness and dimension to a square face.

How can I add volume to my thick hair?

You can add volume to your thick hair by using volumizing products, blow-drying with a round brush, and using hot rollers or curling irons to create curls and waves.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right hair care routine, you can keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and damage.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

Avoid using excessive heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle and frizzy.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. Whether you prefer a short bob or long, flowing layers, there's a hairstyle out there that's perfect for you. So go ahead and experiment with different looks until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
