Best Rotating Hot Brush For Short Hair Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have short hair and want to achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home, a rotating hot brush can be your best friend. Not only does it add volume and shine to your hair, but it also saves you time and effort. However, with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your needs. In this article, we'll go over the top rotating hot brushes for short hair in the UK, along with some tips and FAQs to help you make an informed decision.

Description

A rotating hot brush is a styling tool that combines the heat of a hair dryer and the movement of a round brush to create a smooth and bouncy blowout. It typically has a barrel that rotates in both directions, bristles that grip the hair, and multiple heat and speed settings. Rotating hot brushes come in different sizes and shapes to suit different hair lengths and types.

How to

Using a rotating hot brush is relatively easy, but it requires some practice and patience. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage.
  2. Section your hair into manageable parts using clips or ties.
  3. Choose the right barrel size for your hair length and wrap a section of hair around it, starting from the roots and moving downwards. Make sure the bristles grip the hair firmly.
  4. Turn on the rotating hot brush and select the desired heat and speed settings. Use a lower heat setting for fine or damaged hair, and a higher setting for thicker or coarser hair.
  5. Let the barrel rotate and glide down the hair section until you reach the ends. You can also rotate the brush manually for more control.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining sections of hair until you achieve the desired style.
  7. Finish with a blast of cool air from the hot brush or a hairspray to set the style.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to using a rotating hot brush for short hair:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or ties. The smaller the sections, the easier it is to style your hair.

Step 3: Choose the right brush size

Select the right brush size for your hair length and type. A smaller barrel is suitable for short hair, while a larger one is better for long hair.

Step 4: Use the brush

Start at the root of your hair and wrap a section around the brush. Turn on the brush and let it rotate, gently pulling the brush down to the ends of your hair. Repeat with each section of hair until you achieve the desired style.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you're done styling your hair, spritz some hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to get the most out of your rotating hot brush:

  • Choose a brush with multiple heat and speed settings to customize your styling.
  • Always use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't overload the brush with too much hair, or it will tangle and damage your hair.
  • Rotate the brush manually for more control and precision.
  • Use a cool shot button to help set your style and add shine.
  • Clean your brush regularly to remove hair and product buildup.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about rotating hot brushes:

What is the best rotating hot brush for short hair in the UK?

Some of the best rotating hot brushes for short hair in the UK are the BaBylissPRO Big Hair 50mm Rotating Hot Air Styling Brush, the Remington Keratin Protect Rotating Hot Brush, and the John Frieda Hot Air Brush.

What is the difference between a rotating hot brush and a straightening brush?

A rotating hot brush creates a blowout-style curl and adds volume to your hair, while a straightening brush straightens and tames frizzy hair.

Can I use a rotating hot brush on wet hair?

No, you should never use a rotating hot brush on wet hair as it can damage your hair and cause breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a hot brush.

How often should I clean my rotating hot brush?

You should clean your rotating hot brush after every use to prevent hair and product buildup. Use a damp cloth or a toothbrush to remove any debris from the bristles and barrel.

Hair Care

Using a rotating hot brush can be a great way to style your short hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products or heat tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

With the right rotating hot brush and proper hair care, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home and keep your short hair looking fabulous.
