Best Facial Hair For Narrow Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a narrow face, finding the right facial hair style can be a challenge. You want to enhance your features without overwhelming your face. In this guide, we will be discussing the best facial hair for narrow face, giving you tips on how to choose the right style, and hair care tips to keep your facial hair looking great.

How to Choose the Right Facial Hair Style for a Narrow Face

Choosing the right facial hair style for a narrow face can be a daunting task. However, there are several factors you need to consider to make the right decision. Here are some tips:

Consider the Shape of Your Face

Before choosing any facial hair style, you need to consider the shape of your face. A narrow face has a length that is longer than the width. The best facial hair style for a narrow face should add width to the face to balance the length.

Consider Your Hair Growth Pattern

Your hair growth pattern can also affect the type of facial hair style you can wear. Some men have patchy beards, and for them, a clean-shaven look is the best option. If your hair growth is concentrated around the chin area, then a goatee or chinstrap beard is the best option.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also affect the type of facial hair style you can wear. If you work in a corporate environment, then a full beard may not be appropriate. In this case, a goatee or mustache may be a better option.

Best Facial Hair Styles for Narrow Face

Now that you know what to consider when choosing facial hair style for a narrow face, let's look at the best options:

1. Short Stubble

Short stubble is a great option for men with a narrow face. It adds width to the face, making it look fuller. This style also gives a rugged and masculine look.

2. Van Dyke Beard

The Van Dyke beard style is a combination of a mustache and a goatee. This style is perfect for a narrow face as it adds width to the chin area, balancing the length of the face.

3. Chinstrap Beard

The chinstrap beard is a narrow line of facial hair that runs from the sideburns to the chin. This style is perfect for a narrow face as it adds width to the chin area, making the face look fuller.

4. Mustache

A mustache is another great option for a narrow face. It adds width to the upper lip, making the face look fuller. You can choose from a variety of mustache styles, including the handlebar mustache or the pencil mustache.

FAQs

Q. Can a full beard work for a narrow face?

A. Yes, a full beard can work for a narrow face, but you need to keep it trimmed and well-groomed. A full beard can add width to the face, making it look fuller.

Q. How do I maintain my facial hair?

A. To maintain your facial hair, you need to trim it regularly and keep it clean. Use a beard oil or balm to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Facial Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your facial hair looking great:

1. Use a Beard Oil

Beard oil helps to moisturize your facial hair, keeping it soft and healthy. Apply a few drops of beard oil daily to keep your facial hair looking great.

2. Trim Regularly

Trimming your facial hair regularly helps to maintain the shape and length. Use a beard trimmer or scissors to trim your facial hair to the desired length.

3. Keep it Clean

Wash your facial hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and debris. Use a beard shampoo or regular shampoo to clean your facial hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right facial hair style for a narrow face can be a challenge, but with the right tips, you can find the perfect style. Remember to consider the shape of your face, your hair growth pattern, and your lifestyle when choosing a facial hair style. And, don't forget to maintain your facial hair with regular trimming and grooming.

With these tips, you can find the best facial hair for narrow face that suits you and enhances your features.
