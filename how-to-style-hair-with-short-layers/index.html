How To Style Hair With Short Layers A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 24, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have short layered hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that looks both chic and effortless. The good news is that there are plenty of options for styling short layers, whether you prefer a sleek look or something more tousled and textured. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of styling short layered hair, from choosing the right products to creating the perfect finish. Let’s get started!

How to Style Hair with Short Layers

Before we dive into the specifics of styling short layered hair, it’s important to understand that there are different types of layers. Some layers are shorter than others, and the placement of the layers can also vary. The type of layering you have will affect how you style your hair. That being said, there are some general tips and tricks that can help you achieve a great look regardless of your specific layering.

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

The first step in styling short layered hair is to choose the right products. Depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve, you may need different products to help you get there. Here are some options to consider:

  • A volumizing mousse or spray can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.
  • A texturizing spray or powder can help give your hair a more tousled, messy look.
  • A styling cream or pomade can help add definition and hold to your layers.
  • A heat protectant spray is essential if you plan to use heat tools like a curling iron or flat iron.

Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen your products, it’s time to start styling. Begin by blow drying your hair using a round brush. This will help add volume and shape to your layers. You can either blow dry your hair straight or add some curls using the brush.

Step 3: Add Texture

If you want a more textured, tousled look, you can add some texture to your hair using a texturizing spray or powder. Apply the product to your roots and mid-lengths, then use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look.

Step 4: Create Waves or Curls

If you want to add some waves or curls to your hair, you can use a curling iron or flat iron. For loose waves, use a large barrel curling iron and wrap sections of hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel curling iron and wrap smaller sections of hair around the barrel.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve styled your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray to help set your look in place. Use a flexible hold hairspray to avoid making your hair look stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Styling Short Layered Hair

Styling short layered hair can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a great look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right products for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a texturizing spray or powder to create a messy, undone look.
  • Use heat tools like a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to set your look in place.

FAQs

What are short layers?

Short layers are layers of hair that are cut shorter than the rest of the hair. They can be used to add volume, texture, and movement to the hair.

Can you style short layered hair?

Yes, you can style short layered hair in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and messy.

What products should I use to style short layered hair?

The products you use to style short layered hair will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Some options to consider include volumizing mousse or spray, texturizing spray or powder, styling cream or pomade, and heat protectant spray.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

Maintaining healthy, beautiful hair is essential when it comes to styling short layered hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By taking good care of your hair, you’ll be able to achieve the best possible results when styling your short layered hair.

Conclusion

Styling short layered hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, as long as you have the right products and techniques. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or something more textured and messy, there are plenty of options to choose from. By following the tips and tricks in this guide, you’ll be able to achieve a great look every time.
