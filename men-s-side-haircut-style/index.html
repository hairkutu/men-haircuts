Men s Side Haircut Style A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and modern haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the men's side haircut style. This versatile and trendy haircut is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. In this article, we will guide you through how to achieve the perfect side haircut style, step by step. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new look and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular haircut.

What is the Men's Side Haircut Style?

The men's side haircut style is a popular haircut that has been trending for several years now. This haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. The hair on the sides is usually faded or tapered, while the hair on the top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or curly. This haircut is popular because it is easy to maintain and style, and it looks great on almost anyone.

How to Achieve the Men's Side Haircut Style

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving the men's side haircut style is to choose the right length for your hair. The hair on the sides should be short, but the length will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. A good rule of thumb is to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you find the length that works best for you.

Step 2: Determine the Fade or Taper

The next step is to determine whether you want a fade or a taper on the sides of your hair. A fade is a gradual transition from short to long hair, while a taper is a more subtle transition. If you want a more dramatic look, go for a fade. If you want a more subtle look, opt for a taper.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once you have determined the length and the fade or taper, it's time to style the top of your hair. The style you choose will depend on your personal preference and the occasion. Some popular styles for the men's side haircut style include the slicked-back look, the messy look, and the curly look.

Step 4: Finish with Product

To complete the men's side haircut style, finish with some hair product. This will help hold your hair in place and give it a polished finish. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Side Haircut Style

Now that you have achieved the perfect men's side haircut style, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to help you keep your new haircut looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your curls.

FAQs About the Men's Side Haircut Style

Q: Is the men's side haircut style suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the men's side haircut style is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my men's side haircut style?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my men's side haircut differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, the men's side haircut style is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions.

Final Thoughts

The men's side haircut style is a popular and trendy haircut that is perfect for any occasion. By following our step-by-step guide and tips for maintaining your new haircut, you can achieve the perfect look and keep it looking fresh and stylish. Try out different styles and products to find what works best for you, and enjoy your new look!
