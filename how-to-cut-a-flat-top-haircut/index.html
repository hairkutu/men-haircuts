How To Cut A Flat Top Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that’s both classic and modern, the flat top haircut is a great choice. This style has been around since the 1950s, but it’s still popular today thanks to its clean, sharp lines and versatility. Whether you’re a barber or just want to try cutting your own hair, we’ve got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to cut a flat top haircut.

Description of a Flat Top Haircut

The flat top haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that’s cut flat and horizontal. The hair on top is usually styled with a small amount of product to give it texture and hold. This style works best for men with straight or wavy hair, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and preferences.

How to Cut a Flat Top Haircut

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair, then comb it straight up from the crown of the head. Use clippers to buzz the sides and back of the head, starting with a longer guard and gradually going shorter until you reach the desired length. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Next, use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on top. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting the hair straight across in a horizontal line. You can use a comb to help guide the hair and ensure it’s all the same length. Use scissors to add texture if desired.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

Use scissors or clippers to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter until there’s a smooth transition between the two lengths. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Tips for Cutting a Flat Top Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect flat top haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb to ensure the hair is cut straight and even.
  • Add texture with scissors or a razor to give the hair more movement and volume.
  • Use a small amount of product to style the hair on top and keep it in place.

FAQs About Flat Top Haircuts

How often should I get a flat top haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I cut my own flat top haircut?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut your own flat top haircut. However, it’s recommended to have a professional barber do it for best results.

What face shapes suit a flat top haircut?

A flat top haircut can work for most face shapes, but it’s especially flattering for square and oval faces.

Hair Care for a Flat Top Haircut

To keep your flat top haircut looking fresh and stylish, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a small amount of product to style the hair on top.
  • Trim the hair on the sides and back every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, which can damage the hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock a flat top haircut with confidence and style. Whether you’re a barber or just want to try cutting your own hair, this classic yet modern style is sure to turn heads.
