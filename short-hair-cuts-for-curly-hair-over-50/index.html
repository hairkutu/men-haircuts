Short Hair Cuts For Curly Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new, fresh haircut? Do you have curly hair and are wondering what styles will work best for you? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss short hair cuts for curly hair over 50. From how to style them to tips and tricks for keeping your locks healthy, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short hair cuts for curly hair over 50 can be a great way to change up your look and embrace your natural texture. Short hair can also be easier to manage and style, making it a popular choice for women over 50. However, finding the right cut for your curls can be a challenge, as not all styles work well with curly hair.

In general, shorter cuts tend to work best for curly hair over 50. This is because longer hair can weigh down your curls and make them look limp or lifeless. Shorter cuts can also help to add volume and definition to your curls.

How to

When it comes to short hair cuts for curly hair over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to find a stylist who is experienced with curly hair. Curly hair requires different cutting techniques than straight hair, so it's important to find someone who knows how to work with your curls.

Next, you'll want to consider the shape of your face when choosing a cut. Some cuts will work better for certain face shapes than others. For example, a pixie cut may not be the best choice for someone with a round face, as it can make your face look even rounder.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short hair cut for curly hair over 50:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced with curly hair.
  2. Consider the shape of your face when choosing a cut.
  3. Bring pictures of styles you like to your stylist.
  4. Ask your stylist to cut your hair while it's dry, as this will allow them to see how your curls will fall.
  5. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in layers to add volume and definition to your curls.
  6. Use styling products specifically designed for curly hair to enhance your curls and keep them looking their best.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining short hair cuts for curly hair over 50:

  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair cuts for curly hair over 50:

Q: Will a short hair cut make my curls look frizzy?

A: Not necessarily. With the right cut and styling products, you can enhance your curls and keep them looking smooth and defined.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a short hair cut?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair with a short hair cut. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using a flat iron on wet hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can snag and damage your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right short hair cut for your curls, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance look that works for you.
