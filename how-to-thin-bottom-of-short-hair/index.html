How To Thin Bottom Of Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short hair, you might find that the bottom of your hair can become too thick and heavy, making it difficult to style. Thinning the bottom of your hair can help to give it more movement and texture, making it easier to style and manage. In this article, we'll show you how to thin the bottom of short hair in a few simple steps.

How to Thin Bottom of Short Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to thinning the bottom of your short hair is to wash it thoroughly. This will help to remove any dirt, oil or product buildup from your hair, which can make it difficult to work with.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Once you have washed your hair, it's important to towel dry it gently. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll need to section your hair into manageable sections. Use clips or hair ties to secure each section in place.

Step 4: Thin Your Hair

Using thinning shears, start to thin out the bottom of your hair. Take small sections of hair at a time and work your way around your head, making sure to blend the thinned sections into the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you work, be sure to check your progress in a mirror. Make sure that the thinned sections are blended evenly into the rest of your hair.

Step 6: Finish Your Style

Once you have thinned out the bottom of your hair, you can finish your style as desired. Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips for Thinning Bottom of Short Hair

Here are some useful tips to keep in mind when thinning the bottom of your short hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid over-thinning
  • Hold the thinning shears at a slight angle for a more natural look
  • Be gentle when thinning your hair to avoid damage and breakage
  • Don't overdo it - it's better to under-thin than over-thin

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Thin the Bottom of My Short Hair at Home?

Yes, you can thin the bottom of your short hair at home using thinning shears. However, if you're not confident in your ability to do this yourself, it's best to consult a professional stylist.

How Often Should I Thin the Bottom of My Hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, it's a good idea to have your hair thinned every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Will Thinning the Bottom of My Hair Damage It?

If done correctly, thinning the bottom of your hair should not cause any damage. However, if you're not careful or use the wrong tools, you could end up damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Thinned Hair

After thinning the bottom of your short hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair

Thinning the bottom of your short hair can be a great way to give it more movement and texture. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a beautiful, natural-looking hairstyle that is easy to manage.
