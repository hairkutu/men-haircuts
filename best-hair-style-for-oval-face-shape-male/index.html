Best Hair Style For Oval Face Shape Male

Written by Petter May 01, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have an oval face shape and looking for the best hairstyle that suits you, then you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the best hairstyle for your oval face shape, and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair as well.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. It is characterized by a slightly narrower chin and forehead, with a gently rounded hairline. This face shape is well-balanced, and almost any hairstyle can suit it. However, there are some hairstyles that can enhance your features and make you look even better.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape Male

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is important to enhance your features and create a balanced look. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for oval face shape male:

1. Consider Your Hair Type

The first thing you should consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Different hairstyles suit different hair types. For example, if you have thick and curly hair, a short back and sides with a textured top can suit you well. On the other hand, if you have thin hair, a layered haircut with a side part can add volume to your hair.

2. Look for Hairstyles that Balance Your Face

When choosing a hairstyle, you should look for ones that balance your face shape. Oval face shapes are well-balanced, so you can choose almost any hairstyle. However, if you want to enhance your features, you can go for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, or hairstyles with bangs that cover your forehead.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle for you. If you have an active lifestyle and don’t have much time to style your hair, you can go for a low-maintenance haircut that requires little-to-no styling. On the other hand, if you have a more formal lifestyle, you can go for a classic haircut that is more polished and sophisticated.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape Male

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to choose the best hairstyle for oval face shape male:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking at yourself in the mirror and tracing the outline of your face with a washable marker. Once you have traced your face shape, you can determine which shape it is.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start researching hairstyles that suit your face shape. You can look for inspiration online, in magazines, or by asking your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

After you have found some hairstyles that you like, you should consider your hair type. Different hairstyles suit different hair types, so you should choose a hairstyle that works well with your hair type.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Lastly, you should consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you should choose a low-maintenance haircut that requires little-to-no styling. On the other hand, if you have a more formal lifestyle, you can go for a classic haircut that is more polished and sophisticated.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have chosen the best hairstyle for your oval face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain your hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in maintaining your hairstyle. Use products that are specially designed for your hair type and hairstyle.

2. Schedule Regular Haircuts

Scheduling regular haircuts can help maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh. It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it difficult to maintain your hairstyle. Use heat-protectant products and avoid using hot tools too frequently.

FAQ

What are some hairstyles that suit oval face shape male?

Hairstyles that suit oval face shape male include short back and sides with a textured top, layered haircut with a side part, and hairstyles with bangs that cover your forehead.

Can oval face shape male have any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face shape male can choose almost any hairstyle, but it’s important to choose a hairstyle that balances your face shape and suits your hair type and lifestyle.

How often should oval face shape male get a haircut?

Oval face shape male should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape Male

Finally, taking care of your hair is important to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips for hair care for oval face shape male:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot water, as it can damage your hair.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair can help keep it soft and smooth. Use a conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and apply it after washing your hair.

3. Avoid Excessive Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it difficult to maintain your hairstyle. Avoid using hot tools too frequently and use heat-protectant products when using hot tools.

In conclusion, choosing the best hairstyle for oval face shape male is important to enhance your features and create a balanced look. Consider your hair type, lifestyle, and face shape when choosing a hairstyle, and take care of your hair to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking healthy.
