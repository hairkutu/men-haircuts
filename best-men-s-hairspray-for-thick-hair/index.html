Best Men s Hairspray For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to styling thick hair, finding the right hairspray can make all the difference. Thick hair can be difficult to manage and style, but with the right hairspray, you can achieve a long-lasting hold and a polished look. In this article, we'll be discussing the best men's hairspray for thick hair, including how to use it and tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Hairspray on Thick Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Description

The best men's hairspray for thick hair is one that provides a strong hold without weighing down your hair. Look for hairsprays that are specifically designed for thick hair, as these will have a stronger hold and offer more volume. Some popular hairsprays for thick hair include:

  • Aveda Control Force Firm Hold Hair Spray
  • Baxter of California Clay Effect Style Spray
  • Hanz de Fuko Style Lock Hairspray
  • Redken Brews Hairspray
  • Sebastian Shaper Plus Extra Hold Hairspray

These hairsprays are formulated with ingredients that provide a strong hold and protect your hair from humidity and frizz. They also add shine and volume to your hair, making it easier to style and manage.

How to Use

Using hairspray on thick hair is a simple process, but there are some tips and tricks to keep in mind for the best results. Here's how to use hairspray on thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Hairspray works best on clean hair, so make sure to wash and dry your hair before styling.
  2. Choose the right hairspray. Look for a hairspray that is designed for thick hair and provides a strong hold.
  3. Hold the can at arm's length. This will ensure that the hairspray is evenly distributed and won't weigh down your hair.
  4. Spray in short bursts. Don't spray too much hairspray at once, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural. Instead, spray in short bursts, focusing on the areas that need the most hold.
  5. Style your hair as desired. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired, and then spray a final layer of hairspray to set the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to using hairspray on thick hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Choose the right hairspray for your hair type.
  3. Hold the can at arm's length and spray in short bursts.
  4. Focus on the areas that need the most hold, such as the crown or the sides of your head.
  5. Style your hair as desired.
  6. Spray a final layer of hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Using Hairspray on Thick Hair

Here are some tips for using hairspray on thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid spraying too much hairspray at once, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair before applying hairspray.
  • Don't touch your hair too much after applying hairspray, as this can cause the style to fall apart.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can hairspray damage my hair?
A: Hairspray can cause build-up and make your hair look dull, but it won't damage your hair if used in moderation.

Q: How often should I use hairspray?
A: It's best to use hairspray sparingly, as too much can make your hair look stiff and unnatural. Use it only when necessary to achieve the desired hold.

Q: How do I remove hairspray from my hair?
A: To remove hairspray from your hair, simply wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner as usual.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, thick hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

With the right hairspray and hair care routine, you can achieve a polished, stylish look that lasts all day. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type, and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
