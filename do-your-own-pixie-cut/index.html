Do Your Own Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A pixie cut is a popular hairstyle that can give you a bold and edgy look. It's a great option if you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without going too drastic. However, getting a pixie cut at a salon can be expensive, and you might not always get the exact look you want. That's why learning how to do your own pixie cut can be a game-changer. In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to do your own pixie cut.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that's usually cut close to the scalp, with longer layers on top. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. Pixie cuts can range from super short to slightly longer, and they can be styled in many different ways. Some popular styling options include adding volume at the crown, side-swept bangs, or a sleek, polished look.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have some hair clips on hand to help section your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be difficult to cut accurately, and it can also shrink as it dries, which can affect your final look.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, back, and two sides. Clip each section out of the way, leaving just the section you want to cut loose.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start at the back of your head and use your comb to lift a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut along your fingers, taking off a small amount of hair at a time. Repeat this process, working your way up to the crown of your head. Be sure to keep your scissors at an angle, so you don't end up with a blunt, choppy cut.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you've cut the back, move on to the sides. Pull a section of hair out from the side of your head and cut it to your desired length. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top. Use your comb to lift a section of hair from the top of your head, and cut it to your desired length. Repeat this process, working your way from front to back.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing your own pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes - hair grows back!
  • If in doubt, start with a shorter length and work your way longer.

FAQ

Is it easy to do your own pixie cut?

It can be challenging to cut your own hair, especially if you're new to it. However, with the right tools and a little practice, you can achieve a great pixie cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon and have a professional fix it. Alternatively, you can try styling your hair in a different way to hide the mistake.

Can I do a pixie cut on curly hair?

Absolutely! A pixie cut can look great on curly hair. Just make sure you cut it when it's dry, so you can see how the curls will fall.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims will also help keep your pixie cut looking fresh.

Overall, doing your own pixie cut can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve a great look that's all your own.
