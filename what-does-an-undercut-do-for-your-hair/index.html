What Does An Undercut Do For Your Hair

Written by Frank Apr 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you've been thinking about changing up your hairstyle, you might have heard about the undercut. This popular hairstyle trend involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top long. But what does an undercut actually do for your hair? In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits of this stylish cut and give you some tips for maintaining it.

Description: What is an Undercut?

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top long. The shaved portion of the hair is often cut very short, creating a stark contrast with the longer hair on top. This creates a bold and edgy look that is popular with both men and women.

How to: Getting an Undercut

If you're thinking about getting an undercut, it's important to find a skilled stylist who can create the look you want. Here are the basic steps involved in getting an undercut:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

There are many different styles of undercut to choose from, so it's important to do your research and find a look that suits your face shape and personal style. Some popular styles include the disconnected undercut, the side-swept undercut, and the slicked-back undercut.

Step 2: Shave the Sides and Back

Once you've chosen your style, your stylist will use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head. The length of the hair that is shaved will depend on the style you've chosen.

Step 3: Cut and Style the Top

After the sides and back have been shaved, your stylist will cut and style the hair on top of your head. This may involve trimming the ends, adding layers, or creating a specific shape or style.

Step 4: Maintain Your Look

To maintain your undercut, you'll need to visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups. You may also need to style your hair daily using products like pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips: Maintaining Your Undercut

Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut and ensuring it looks its best:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups
  • Use high-quality hair products to style your hair
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair

FAQ: Common Questions About Undercuts

Q: Is an undercut suitable for all hair types?

A: While an undercut can look great on many different hair types, it may not be suitable for very fine or thin hair. Talk to your stylist to see if an undercut is a good choice for your hair type.

Q: How long does an undercut take to grow out?

A: This will depend on how short the shaved portion of your hair is, as well as how fast your hair grows. On average, it can take several months to grow out an undercut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes! While an undercut can make wearing certain hairstyles more difficult, you can still wear your hair up in a ponytail, bun, or other style.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Hair Healthy

Whether you have an undercut or not, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know what an undercut can do for your hair, it's up to you to decide if this bold and edgy look is right for you. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle update, an undercut can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and express your unique style.
