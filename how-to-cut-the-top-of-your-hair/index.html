How To Cut The Top Of Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few months to get your hair cut? Or maybe you just want to save some money and try cutting your own hair at home. Whatever your reason may be, cutting the top of your hair can be a bit tricky if you don’t know what you’re doing. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut in the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut the Top of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut the Top of Your Hair

The first thing you need to do is gather all the tools you’ll need. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. Make sure you have good lighting in the room so you can see what you’re doing.

Next, decide how much hair you want to cut off. If you’re just looking to trim your ends, you won’t need to cut off much. But if you’re looking for a dramatic change, you may need to cut off several inches.

It’s important to note that cutting hair while it’s wet is easier than cutting it when it’s dry. So, if your hair is dry, spritz it with some water to dampen it before you begin.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut the top of your hair:

  1. Start by combing your hair straight up. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut off the desired amount with your scissors.
  2. Continue to cut your hair in small sections, working your way from the front to the back of your head.
  3. Use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head. Hold the mirror in one hand and the scissors in the other, and carefully trim any areas that need it.
  4. Once you’ve finished cutting, comb your hair to make sure it’s even. If you notice any areas that need to be touched up, repeat the process until you’re satisfied with the results.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut off less than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t cut your hair straight across. Instead, angle the scissors slightly to create a more natural-looking cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don’t rush through it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about cutting the top of their hair:

  • Can I cut the top of my hair with regular scissors? It’s not recommended. Regular scissors aren’t sharp enough to cut hair cleanly, which can lead to split ends and damage.
  • How often should I cut the top of my hair? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your style. On average, most people cut their hair every 6-8 weeks.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don’t panic! If you accidentally cut off too much hair, wait for it to grow back or visit a professional stylist to fix the mistake.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Cutting the top of your hair can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. Just remember to take your time, start with small sections, and follow these tips for the best results.
