Description

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off any hairstyle. However, if you're looking for a new shoulder length hairstyle, there are some styles that flatter your face shape more than others. In this article, we'll explore the best shoulder length hairstyles for oval face shapes, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining them.

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle for an oval face shape, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. For example, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered look to add texture and movement. If you have fine hair, a blunt cut may be better to create the illusion of thickness.

It's also important to consider the length of the hairstyle. Shoulder length hair is a great option for oval face shapes, as it can help balance out the proportions of the face. However, you can also experiment with longer or shorter lengths to see what looks best on you.

Hairstyles to Avoid

While oval face shapes can pull off almost any hairstyle, there are a few styles to avoid. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make your face appear longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for an oval face shape:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. If you want to add volume, use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots.
  5. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair, if desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hairstyle for an oval face shape:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • If you have bangs, make sure to trim them regularly to keep them from getting too long and covering your face.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some good shoulder length hairstyles for oval face shapes?

Some good shoulder length hairstyles for oval face shapes include layered cuts, blunt cuts, long bobs, and side-swept bangs.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or bun with an oval face shape?

Yes! Oval face shapes can pull off ponytails and buns with ease. Just make sure to keep the style loose and not too tight, as this can create unwanted tension on your hairline.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional hair care tips for shoulder length hairstyles for oval face shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can lead to heat damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid breakage when styling your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal parts, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face shape. Remember to consider your personal style and hair texture when choosing a hairstyle, and always take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
