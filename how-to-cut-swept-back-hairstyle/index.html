How To Cut Swept Back Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to get a new haircut that is both stylish and easy to maintain? A swept back hairstyle might just be the perfect choice for you. This classic hairstyle has been popular for many years and is still in vogue today. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a swept back hairstyle so that you can achieve the perfect look.


Description

The swept back hairstyle is a classic look that involves combing the hair back and away from the face. It is a popular hairstyle for men of all ages and can be worn in a variety of ways. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and is perfect for those who want a stylish yet low-maintenance look.


How to Cut Swept Back Hairstyle

Cutting a swept back hairstyle is relatively easy, but it requires some skill and patience. Here are the steps you can follow to cut a swept back hairstyle:


Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in cutting a swept back hairstyle is to choose the right length. The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference, but it should be long enough to comb back. If you have short hair, you may need to grow it out before you can achieve a swept back hairstyle.


Step 2: Decide on the Parting

The next step is to decide on the parting. A swept back hairstyle can be parted in the middle or on the side. If you have a natural part, you can use this as a guide. If not, experiment with different partings to see which one looks best on you.


Step 3: Section the Hair

Once you have decided on the length and parting, section the hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.


Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Start by cutting the top section of the hair. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. It is important to cut the hair evenly and to avoid cutting too much at once.


Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Next, cut the side sections of the hair. Use the top section as a guide to ensure that the sides are cut evenly. Remember to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much at once.


Step 6: Blend the Back Section

The back section of the hair should be blended into the sides to create a natural look. Use scissors to cut the hair at an angle, blending the back into the sides.


Step 7: Style the Hair

Once you have finished cutting the hair, style it into a swept back hairstyle. Use a comb to comb the hair back and away from the face. You can use a hair dryer to add volume and to help the hair stay in place.


Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect swept back hairstyle:

  • Use sharp scissors to cut the hair.
  • Cut the hair when it is dry to ensure that you are cutting it to the desired length.
  • Start by cutting small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair requires skill and practice.

FAQ

Can I cut a swept back hairstyle myself?

While it is possible to cut a swept back hairstyle yourself, it is recommended that you visit a professional hairdresser to ensure that the haircut is done correctly.


What face shape is best suited for a swept back hairstyle?

A swept back hairstyle looks great on most face shapes, but it is particularly suited to those with a square or oval face shape.


Hair Care

Now that you have a swept back hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut a swept back hairstyle, you can achieve this classic look with ease. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can create a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that will look great on you.
