Do Big Glasses Suit Round Faces

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering if big glasses are a good style choice for you. While it's true that certain frames can complement your face shape better than others, the size of the glasses is only one factor to consider.

  • How to Choose Glasses for Round Faces

Description

Round faces have soft curves and full cheeks, and are usually about as wide as they are tall. If you have a round face, you'll want to look for glasses that help balance out the proportions of your face. Big glasses can work well for round faces, as long as they're not too overwhelming.

When it comes to choosing glasses for a round face, you'll want to look for frames that have some angles and sharp lines. Avoid round or circular frames, which can make your face look even rounder. You'll also want to consider the width of the frames, as wider frames can help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

How to Choose Glasses for Round Faces

When you're shopping for glasses, keep these tips in mind:

  • Look for frames with sharp angles and straight lines, rather than round or circular frames.
  • Choose glasses that are wider than your face, which can help create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Avoid frames that are too small, as they can make your face look larger.
  • Consider the color of the frames, as well as the shape. Darker frames can help create definition and contrast with your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing glasses for a round face:

  1. Measure the width of your face, from temple to temple.
  2. Look for glasses that are wider than your face width measurement.
  3. Avoid round or circular frames, and look for frames with sharp angles and straight lines.
  4. Choose glasses with a bold or thick frame, as these can help balance out the proportions of your face.
  5. Consider the color of the frames, and choose a color that complements your skin tone.
  6. Try on several different styles and sizes to see what looks best on your face.

Tips for Wearing Big Glasses with a Round Face

Once you've chosen your glasses, here are some tips for wearing them:

  • Make sure the glasses fit your face properly, and don't slide down your nose or sit too high on your face.
  • Consider wearing your hair up or back, to avoid covering up your face and hiding your glasses.
  • Experiment with different makeup styles, to find a look that complements your glasses and your face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to go bold with your glasses, and choose a style that makes a statement.

FAQs

Q: Can round faces wear big glasses?

A: Yes, round faces can wear big glasses, as long as the frames have some angles and sharp lines to balance out the proportions of the face.

Q: How do I choose glasses for a round face?

A: Look for frames that are wider than your face, with sharp angles and straight lines. Avoid round or circular frames, and consider the color of the frames as well.

Q: What are some tips for wearing big glasses with a round face?

A: Make sure the glasses fit properly, experiment with different hairstyles and makeup looks, and don't be afraid to choose a bold style.

Hair Care Tips for Big Glasses and Round Faces

If you have a round face and big glasses, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and doesn't compete with your glasses. Here are some tips:

  • Consider a hairstyle with layers or angles, to help create definition and balance out the proportions of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too full or voluminous, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Try a side part or a hairstyle that's swept to one side, to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Consider getting bangs, which can help balance out the proportions of your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Remember, when it comes to choosing glasses for a round face, there's no one-size-fits-all answer. It's all about finding a style that you love and that makes you feel confident. With these tips, you'll be able to choose the perfect glasses for your face shape and rock them with style!
