How To Get Korean Soft Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of Korean beauty trends, you've probably noticed that soft curls are all the rage in Korea. This hairstyle is effortless and chic, and it's perfect for any occasion. The good news is that you don't have to be Korean to get these gorgeous curls. In this article, we'll show you how to get Korean soft curls, step by step.

Description

Korean soft curls are a type of loose curl that adds volume and texture to your hair. These curls are achieved by using a curling iron or a hair straightener. The curls are loose and bouncy, giving your hair a natural and effortless look.

How to Get Korean Soft Curls

Getting Korean soft curls is easy, and you don't need any special skills or tools. Here's how:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting Korean soft curls is to start with clean hair. Shampoo and condition your hair, and then towel-dry it. Avoid using any heavy products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it harder to curl.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start curling your hair, apply a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy. Spray the heat protectant all over your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Hair Straightener

Use a curling iron or hair straightener to curl your hair. If you're using a curling iron, wrap a section of hair around the barrel, hold it for a few seconds, and then release. If you're using a hair straightener, clamp the section of hair between the plates, twist it, and then pull it down to the ends.

Step 5: Repeat Until All Sections are Curled

Repeat the curling process until all sections of your hair are curled. Make sure that you curl your hair in different directions to create a natural and effortless look.

Step 6: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you're done curling your hair, use a paddle brush to brush out your curls. This will loosen them up and give your hair a softer look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best Korean soft curls:

  • Use a curling iron or hair straightener with a ceramic or tourmaline coating.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can make your curls look unnatural.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  • Use a serum or oil to add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Q: How long will Korean soft curls last?

A: Korean soft curls can last up to 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and the products you use. To make your curls last longer, use a hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much.

Q: Can I get Korean soft curls if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can get Korean soft curls even if you have naturally straight hair. You just need to use a curling iron or hair straightener to create the curls.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Now that you know how to get Korean soft curls, it's time to try it out for yourself. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, and it's sure to turn heads wherever you go.
