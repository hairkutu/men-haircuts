How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Men A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're trying to achieve a short, sharp look. However, with the right tools and a little bit of guidance, it's possible to achieve a great haircut from home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to cutting your own hair short as a man, and provide some tips and tricks to help you along the way.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Men: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a hair clipper with guards, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp during the cutting process.

Step 2: Decide on Your Style

Before you start cutting, decide on the style you want to achieve. Short, cropped hair can be achieved with clippers, while a choppy, layered look will require scissors. Look up some inspiration photos online and choose a style that you like.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

If you're going for a short, cropped look, start by using the clippers with the shortest guard. Work your way up through the guards until you achieve your desired length. Make sure to go over your hair a few times to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Use Scissors for Layered Look

If you're going for a choppy, layered look, start by cutting the top of your hair with scissors. Use the comb to create small sections, and cut the hair to your desired length. Use the comb to blend the sections together for a natural look.

Step 5: Trim the Sides and Back

After you've cut the top of your hair, use the clippers to trim the sides and back. Use the shortest guard to start, and work your way up until you achieve your desired length. Make sure to blend the sides and back with the top for a seamless look.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, use the mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments with the scissors or clippers to achieve a balanced look.

Step 7: Clean Up

After you've finished cutting, clean up any stray hairs with a brush or towel. Take a shower to remove any loose hair, and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Short Men

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp during the cutting process.
  • Make small cuts with scissors to avoid cutting off too much hair at once.
  • Go over your hair a few times with the clippers to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get better.

FAQs

Can I Cut My Own Hair If I Have Curly Hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if you have curly hair. However, it can be more challenging to achieve an even cut. Take your time and make small cuts to avoid cutting off too much hair at once.

What If I Make a Mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional hairdresser to fix any issues. Alternatively, you can try to fix the mistake yourself by blending the hair with scissors or clippers.

Do I Need to Use Hair Products After Cutting My Hair?

Using hair products after cutting your hair is optional. If you want to style your hair, you can use a small amount of hair wax or gel to achieve your desired look.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair Short Men

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.

Cutting your own hair can be a rewarding experience, but it does require some practice and patience. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks to achieve a great haircut from home. If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional hairdresser for the best results.
