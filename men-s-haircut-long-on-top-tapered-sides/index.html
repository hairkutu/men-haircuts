Men s Haircut Long On Top Tapered Sides A Guide To Achieving This Trendy Look

Description | How To Achieve This Look | Step By Step Guide | Tips & Tricks | FAQs | Hair Care Tips

Description:

Long on top tapered sides is one of the most popular hairstyles for men in recent years. This style is characterized by long hair on the top of the head, with the sides and back tapered or faded for a clean and sharp look. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from a messy and textured look to a slick and polished style. It's a versatile haircut that suits almost any face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How To Achieve This Look:

The long on top tapered sides haircut requires some maintenance, but it's relatively easy to achieve. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. You can opt for a shorter length, like a crew cut or a buzz cut, or a longer length, like a fringe or a textured crop. It's essential to choose a length that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Once you've decided on the top length, it's time to cut your hair. You can do it yourself with a pair of scissors or clippers or visit a professional hairstylist. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and maintain the desired length.

Step 3: Taper the Sides and Back

The next step is to taper the sides and back of your head. You can do it with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. Start with a longer guard size and gradually decrease it until you achieve the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair seamlessly for a natural look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

The final step is to style your hair. You can use a hair product, like wax, pomade, or gel, to achieve the desired look. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you the most.

Step By Step Guide:

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the long on top tapered sides haircut:

Step 1:

Wash your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2:

Decide on the top length and cut your hair accordingly.

Step 3:

Section off the hair on the sides and back of your head.

Step 4:

Start tapering the hair with a longer guard size.

Step 5:

Gradually decrease the guard size until you achieve the desired length.

Step 6:

Blend the hair seamlessly for a natural look.

Step 7:

Style your hair with a hair product of your choice.

Tips & Tricks:

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect long on top tapered sides haircut:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist if you're not confident cutting your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products until you find the one that works for you.
  • Maintain your haircut by trimming the hair regularly.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the hair product evenly.

FAQs:

Q: Can I achieve this look if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a different hair product, like a curl-enhancing cream or mousse, to achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Q: Can I style my hair differently every day?

A: Yes, you can. The long on top tapered sides haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways, depending on your mood and occasion.

Hair Care Tips:

Maintaining a long on top tapered sides haircut requires some hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like hairdryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect long on top tapered sides haircut and maintain it for a long time. Experiment with different styles and hair products until you find the one that suits you the most. Have fun and enjoy your stylish new haircut!
