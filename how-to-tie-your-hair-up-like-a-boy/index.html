How To Tie Your Hair Up Like A Boy

Written by Robby May 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and looking for a new way to tie your hair up? Why not try a boyish look? It’s easy, effortless and perfect for those days when you’re on the go. In this article, we’ll show you how to tie your hair up like a boy in simple steps that you can follow at home. So, let’s get started!

Description

Tying your hair up like a boy is a popular trend that has been around for a while. It’s a quick and easy way to style your hair without spending too much time on it. This style is perfect for those who want to look cool, edgy and confident. It’s also a great option for those with short hair or those who want to grow out their hair but don’t want to deal with the awkward in-between stage.

How to

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to tie your hair up like a boy:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to tie your hair up.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Next, gather all your hair at the back of your head. Use your hands to gather your hair into a ponytail. Make sure it’s tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Now it’s time to tie your hair up. Use a hairband or a scrunchie to tie your ponytail. Make sure it’s secure and tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is tied up, it’s time to style it. You can leave it as it is for a casual look or you can use a comb or your fingers to create a messy look. You can also use a hair gel or wax to add some texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when tying your hair up like a boy:

  • Use a hairband or a scrunchie that matches your outfit.
  • Try to tie your hair up high on your head for a more edgy look.
  • Use a hair gel or wax to add some texture to your hair.
  • Don’t tie your hair up too tightly as it can cause headaches or hair breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about tying your hair up like a boy:

Is it okay to tie my hair up every day?

Yes, it’s okay to tie your hair up every day as long as you don’t tie it up too tightly. It’s also important to let your hair down and give it a break every once in a while.

Can I tie my hair up like a boy if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still tie your hair up like a boy even if you have short hair. You can use bobby pins or hair clips to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair even when it’s tied up. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a hair tie that won’t damage your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair up when it’s wet as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as it can cause build-up and damage your hair.

That’s it! Now you know how to tie your hair up like a boy. It’s easy, effortless and perfect for those days when you’re on the go. So, go ahead and try it out!
