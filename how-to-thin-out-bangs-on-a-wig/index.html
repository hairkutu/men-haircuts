How To Thin Out Bangs On A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a wig with bangs that are too thick, you may feel self-conscious or uncomfortable. Luckily, there is a way to thin them out yourself, without damaging the wig. In this article, we'll show you how to thin out bangs on a wig in a few simple steps.

Description

Thinning out bangs on a wig is a process of removing some of the hair from the bangs to make them less dense. This can be done with scissors or thinning shears, depending on your preference.

How To Thin Out Bangs on a Wig

Follow these steps to thin out your wig's bangs:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You will need:

  • A wig with bangs
  • Scissors or thinning shears
  • A comb
  • A mirror

Step 2: Brush the Bangs

Use a comb to brush the bangs straight down, so they are even and easy to work with.

Step 3: Decide How Much to Thin the Bangs

Decide how much hair you want to remove from the bangs. If you're unsure, start with a little and work your way up. You can always remove more hair later, but you can't put it back once it's gone.

Step 4: Cut the Bangs

Hold the scissors or thinning shears at a slight angle and cut into the bangs, removing small sections of hair at a time. Take breaks to check your progress in the mirror and make sure you're thinning the bangs evenly.

Step 5: Blend the Bangs

After you've thinned the bangs to your liking, use the scissors or thinning shears to blend any harsh lines or uneven patches.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Check your work in the mirror to make sure the bangs are even and natural-looking. If you need to make any adjustments, do so carefully.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while thinning out bangs on a wig:

  • Take your time and work slowly.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Use a light touch when cutting with thinning shears.
  • Blend the bangs well to avoid harsh lines.

FAQ

Can I thin out bangs on a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can thin out bangs on a synthetic wig. However, be careful not to use too much heat or cut too much hair, as synthetic fibers can melt or fray easily.

Can I thin out bangs on a curly wig?

Yes, you can thin out bangs on a curly wig. However, be careful not to thin them too much, as curly hair tends to bounce back and appear thicker than it is.

Hair Care

Thinning out bangs on a wig is a simple process, but it's important to take care of your wig properly to maintain its longevity. Store it in a cool, dry place when not in use, and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner when washing it. Avoid using heat tools on synthetic wigs, and be careful not to tug or pull on the hair when brushing or styling it.

With these tips, you can confidently thin out bangs on your wig and achieve a natural, flattering look.
