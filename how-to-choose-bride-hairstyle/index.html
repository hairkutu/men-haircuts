How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle For Your Wedding Day

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task. With so many options to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. In this article, we’ll give you some tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your big day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose Bride Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your Wedding Day

How to Choose Bride Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day is to consider the overall style of your wedding. Are you having a formal or informal wedding? Is your wedding taking place indoors or outdoors? These factors will help you determine the appropriate hairstyle for your wedding day.

The second step is to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles will look better on different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a hairstyle with volume on top will help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a hairstyle with soft, rounded edges will help soften your features.

Next, consider your hair type. Do you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair? Different hairstyles will work better with different hair types. For example, an updo will work better for thick, curly hair, while a sleek, straight style will work better for fine, straight hair.

Step by Step Guide

Once you’ve considered the above factors, follow these steps to choose the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day:

  1. Research different hairstyles online or in bridal magazines.
  2. Make a list of your favorite styles.
  3. Book a hair trial with your hairstylist.
  4. Bring pictures of your favorite styles to the hair trial.
  5. Try on your wedding dress and veil during the hair trial.
  6. Take pictures of each hairstyle to compare later.
  7. Choose your favorite hairstyle and book your wedding day appointment.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day:

  • Consider the weather on your wedding day. If it’s going to be hot and humid, an updo may be a better option.
  • Don’t be afraid to add hair accessories like flowers, jewels, or a veil to your hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your wedding dress and overall wedding style.
  • Make sure the hairstyle you choose is comfortable and will last throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a bride hairstyle:

What if I can’t decide on a hairstyle?

Book a hair trial with your hairstylist and try on different styles until you find the perfect one for you.

Should I wear my hair up or down on my wedding day?

This depends on your personal preference and the overall style of your wedding. If you’re having a formal wedding, an updo may be more appropriate. If you’re having an informal wedding, wearing your hair down may be more appropriate.

How soon before my wedding should I book my hair trial?

You should book your hair trial at least three months before your wedding day.

Hair Care for Your Wedding Day

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your ends healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips, you’ll be able to choose the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day and ensure that your hair looks its best.
