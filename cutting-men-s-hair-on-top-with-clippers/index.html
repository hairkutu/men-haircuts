Cutting Men s Hair On Top With Clippers A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 08, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every month for a simple haircut? Do you want to save some money and learn how to cut your own hair? Cutting men's hair on top with clippers is not as complicated as it seems. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair on top with clippers, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

Cutting men's hair on top with clippers is a popular haircutting technique that involves using a set of electric clippers to trim the hair on the top of the head. This style is perfect for men who want to maintain a short, neat hairstyle without having to visit a barber or hair salon every few weeks. Clippers are easy to use and can help you achieve a precise and even cut every time.

How To: Cutting Men's Hair on Top with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's hair on top with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a set of electric clippers, a comb, a pair of scissors, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure your clippers are fully charged and have a sharp blade.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel dry it thoroughly. Wet hair can be difficult to cut with clippers, so make sure it is completely dry before you start.

Step 3: Choose Your Clipper Guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes and are designed to cut hair to a specific length. Choose the guard that corresponds to the desired length of your hair. If you are not sure which guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair at the front of your head, working your way towards the back. Use the clipper guard to trim the hair evenly, making sure to go over each section several times to ensure an even cut. Make sure to keep the clipper at a consistent angle and apply gentle pressure to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 5: Blend and Taper

After you have cut the top of your hair, it's time to blend and taper the sides and back. Use the scissors to trim the hair around the ears and neckline, making sure to blend it smoothly into the hair on top. Use the clippers without a guard to taper the hair at the back of your head and create a natural-looking fade.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting, use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head for any uneven patches or stray hairs. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a clean and even cut.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair on Top with Clippers

• Start with clean, dry hair
• Use a sharp blade and a consistent angle
• Go over each section several times to ensure an even cut
• Use scissors to blend and taper the sides and back
• Check your work with a mirror
• Don't rush the process
• Practice makes perfect!

FAQ

Q: Can I use clippers to cut other parts of my hair?
A: Yes, clippers can be used to cut hair on the sides and back of your head as well. Just make sure to use the appropriate guard and technique for each section.

Q: Can I cut my hair with clippers without a guard?
A: Yes, you can use clippers without a guard to create a taper or fade on the sides and back of your head. Just be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its shape and style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Trim your hair every few weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

With these simple tips and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Whether you're looking to save money or just want to try something new, cutting men's hair on top with clippers is a great option. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and practice regularly to perfect your technique. Happy cutting!
