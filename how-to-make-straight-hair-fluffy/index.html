How To Make Straight Hair Fluffy Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Having straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s easy to manage and style, it can also be limp and lifeless. If you’re looking to add some volume and bounce to your straight hair, then you’ve come to the right place. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to make straight hair fluffy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Straight Hair Fluffy
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep Your Hair Fluffy
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Hair

How to Make Straight Hair Fluffy

There are several ways to make straight hair fluffy. Some of them are:

Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to your straight hair is by blow-drying it upside down. This technique helps to lift your roots and create a more voluminous look.

Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush while blow-drying your hair can also help to add volume. Wrap your hair around the brush and pull the brush away from your roots to create a lift.

Add Layers

Adding layers to your hair can also help to create a more voluminous look. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start at your cheekbones or chin.

Use Hair Rollers

Another way to add volume to your straight hair is by using hair rollers. Wrap your hair around the rollers and leave them in for a few hours or overnight. When you remove the rollers, you’ll have bouncy, voluminous hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make straight hair fluffy:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush.
  4. When your hair is completely dry, use hair rollers to add more volume.
  5. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the rollers and style your hair as desired.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Fluffy

Here are some tips to keep your hair fluffy:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair fluffy without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use hair rollers or braids to add volume to your hair without using heat.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair or don’t sweat much, you can wash your hair less frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for fluffy hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when styling it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your straight hair fluffy and full of volume. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve the bouncy, voluminous hair of your dreams.
