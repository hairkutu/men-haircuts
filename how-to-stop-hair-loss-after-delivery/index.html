How To Stop Hair Loss After Delivery Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

After giving birth, you may notice that your hair is falling out more than usual. This is completely normal, but it can be frustrating and even alarming for some new mothers. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to help you stop hair loss after delivery.

How to Stop Hair Loss After Delivery

There are many ways to stop hair loss after delivery, but it's important to remember that it's a natural process. You can't completely stop hair loss, but you can reduce it and promote healthy hair growth.

Here are some ways to stop hair loss after delivery:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy, balanced diet can help prevent hair loss after delivery. Make sure to include plenty of protein, iron, and vitamins A, C, and E in your diet.

2. Take Supplements

You may want to consider taking supplements to help stop hair loss after delivery. Biotin, iron, and vitamin D are all important for healthy hair growth.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp healthy. Aim for at least eight glasses of water a day.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, like braids and ponytails, can cause hair loss. Try to wear your hair down or in loose styles to reduce tension on your hair.

5. Be Gentle When Brushing Your Hair

Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling it out. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles.

6. Reduce Stress

Stress can cause hair loss, so try to reduce stress in your life. Exercise, meditation, and deep breathing can all help reduce stress.

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to stop hair loss after delivery:

Step 6: Reduce Stress

Try to reduce stress in your life through exercise, meditation, and deep breathing.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss after delivery:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help promote healthy hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that's gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as much as possible.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Make sure you're getting enough sleep every night. Lack of sleep can contribute to hair loss.

4. Consult a Doctor

If you're experiencing excessive hair loss, you may want to consult a doctor. They can help determine if there's an underlying medical condition causing your hair loss.

FAQs

Q: Is it normal to lose hair after delivery?

A: Yes, it's completely normal to lose hair after delivery. Hormonal changes can cause hair to fall out more than usual.

Q: How long does hair loss after delivery last?

A: Hair loss after delivery typically lasts for a few months. After that, your hair should start to grow back normally.

Q: Can breastfeeding cause hair loss?

A: Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair loss. The hormonal changes that occur during breastfeeding can affect hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent hair breakage and frizz.

3. Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing damage and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every other day.

By following these tips and tricks, you can stop hair loss after delivery and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and don't hesitate to consult a doctor if you're experiencing excessive hair loss.
