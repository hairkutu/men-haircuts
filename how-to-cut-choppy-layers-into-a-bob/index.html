How To Cut Choppy Layers Into A Bob

Written by Bardi May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and edgy new hairstyle? A choppy bob with layers might be the perfect choice for you. This style is ideal for those who want to add texture and volume to their hair while keeping it short and low maintenance. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of how to cut choppy layers into a bob.

Description

A choppy bob with layers is a stylish and trendy haircut that features short hair with choppy layers cut throughout. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair. Choppy layers give the hair a tousled and edgy look that is easy to style and maintain.

How to Cut Choppy Layers into a Bob

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and detangle it using a wide-toothed comb. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts, two in the front and two in the back. Clip the top sections away and start cutting the back sections first.

Step 3: Cut the back sections

Start by cutting the hair in the back to the desired length. Use a razor or scissors to cut choppy layers throughout the back sections. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create a textured and edgy look.

Step 4: Cut the front sections

Once you have cut the back sections, move on to the front sections. Again, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create choppy layers. Make sure the front sections are slightly longer than the back sections to create a graduated look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you have cut your hair into a choppy bob with layers, style it using a small amount of texturizing paste or pomade. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy and edgy look.

Tips

  • Use a sharp pair of scissors or a razor to cut your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. Leave some length to create choppy layers.
  • Start cutting your hair from the back and move to the front sections.
  • Use a texturizing paste or pomade to style your hair and add texture and volume.

FAQ

1. Is a choppy bob with layers suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a choppy bob with layers is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often should I trim my choppy bob with layers?

You should trim your choppy bob with layers every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your hair.

3. Can I color my choppy bob with layers?

Yes, you can color your choppy bob with layers. However, it is recommended to consult a professional hairstylist before coloring your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair into a choppy bob with layers, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Cutting choppy layers into a bob is a fun and trendy hairstyle that can give you a fresh and edgy look. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to achieve the perfect choppy bob with layers. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture.
