Best Swimsuit Styles for Over 60

When it comes to choosing the best swimsuit style for women over 60, comfort, coverage, and support are key. Here are some of the best swimsuit styles to consider:

1. One-Piece Swimsuits

One-piece swimsuits are a classic and timeless option for women of all ages. They provide full coverage, support, and can even help smooth out any problem areas. Look for one-piece swimsuits with tummy control panels, thicker straps, and higher necklines for added comfort and coverage.

2. Tankinis

If you prefer a two-piece swimsuit, tankinis are a great option. They provide the coverage and support of a one-piece, but with the convenience of a two-piece. Look for tankinis with longer tops that cover the stomach area and high-waisted bottoms for added coverage and support.

3. Swim Dresses

Swim dresses are a stylish and comfortable option for women over 60. They provide full coverage, support, and are great for hiding any problem areas. Look for swim dresses with built-in bras, adjustable straps, and A-line skirts for added comfort and coverage.
