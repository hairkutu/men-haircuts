How To Cut Your Own Fringe Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're in need of a quick hair change without having to book an appointment at the hair salon, why not try cutting your own fringe hair? Not only is it an easy way to switch up your look, but it is also a cost-effective option. In this article, we'll guide you on how to cut your own fringe hair step-by-step, provide some tips, answer some frequently asked questions, and give some hair care advice to make sure your new fringe stays fabulous.

How to Cut Your Own Fringe Hair

Before starting, make sure you have the necessary tools such as sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to cutting your own fringe hair:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length and style of your fringe. Do you want it short or long, wispy or straight across? Once you've decided, mark the length with your fingers on your forehead.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by combing it straight down the middle. Then, separate the hair you want to cut from the rest of your hair by parting it in the middle of your forehead.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using the mark you made earlier as a guide, start cutting your hair in a straight line. Hold the scissors at a slight angle to create a softer, more natural look. Cut a little at a time to avoid cutting too much off.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Check your work by combing your fringe down and checking if it's even on both sides. Make any necessary adjustments by trimming the ends.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut your own fringe hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it may appear longer and you may end up cutting too much.
  • Take your time and cut a little at a time to avoid making mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own fringe hair:

Is it easy to cut my own fringe hair?

Yes, cutting your own fringe hair is easy as long as you follow the steps and take your time.

Do I need to go to a hair salon to cut my own fringe hair?

No, you don't need to go to a hair salon to cut your own fringe hair. You can do it in the comfort of your own home.

Can I cut my own fringe hair if I have curly hair?

Cutting your own fringe hair can be a bit more challenging if you have curly hair. It's best to straighten your hair first before cutting it to get an accurate length.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your own fringe hair, here are some tips to keep it looking fabulous:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

And there you have it, a simple guide on how to cut your own fringe hair. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to achieve the desired look. Good luck!
