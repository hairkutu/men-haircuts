How To Style Short Hair With Shaved Sides

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Short hair with shaved sides is a bold and edgy hairstyle that has become more and more popular in recent years. This style can be both masculine and feminine, depending on how it’s styled. In this article, we’ll show you how to style short hair with shaved sides to achieve a variety of different looks.

Description

Short hair with shaved sides is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair on the sides of the head very short or shaving it completely. The remaining hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished. This style is often worn by those who want a low-maintenance but high-impact look.

How to Style Short Hair with Shaved Sides

The first step in styling short hair with shaved sides is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or wax, to help hold your style in place.

Step 1: Messy and Textured

If you want a messy and textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Sleek and Polished

If you want a sleek and polished look, use a comb to smooth your hair down and create a side part. Apply a small amount of hair gel to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Step 3: Braids and Twists

If you want to add some extra detail to your hairstyle, try incorporating braids or twists into your hair. You can braid or twist the hair on top of your head, or create a braided or twisted design on the shaved sides.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect style with short hair and shaved sides:

  • Experiment with different lengths on the top of your head to find the right balance for your face shape.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to try different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair looking sharp and well-maintained.

FAQ

Q: Is short hair with shaved sides easy to maintain?

A: Yes, short hair with shaved sides is very low-maintenance. You may need to visit your stylist more frequently to keep the sides shaved, but otherwise, this style is easy to care for.

Q: Can short hair with shaved sides be worn by both men and women?

A: Yes, short hair with shaved sides is a versatile style that can be worn by anyone. The way it’s styled can make it look more masculine or feminine.

Hair Care

When styling short hair with shaved sides, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it strong.

With these tips, you can achieve a variety of different looks with short hair and shaved sides. Whether you want a messy and textured style or a sleek and polished look, this edgy hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
