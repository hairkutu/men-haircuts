How To Style A Lob With Thick Hair

Written by Frank Jul 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great? A lob (long bob) is a timeless and versatile haircut that can be customized for any hair type, including thick hair. In this article, we will share how to style a lob with thick hair step-by-step, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

How to Style a Lob with Thick Hair

A lob is a great haircut for thick hair because it can help manage the weight of your hair and provide a flattering shape. Here are some steps to follow when styling a lob with thick hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your locks. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the roots and smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

2. Add some texture.

Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair to add definition and separation. This will help create a tousled, beachy look that is perfect for a lob. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create additional texture.

3. Use a straightener or curling iron.

Depending on the look you want to achieve, you can use a straightener or curling iron to style your lob. For a sleek, straight look, use a flat iron to smooth out your hair and create a polished finish. For a more relaxed, wavy look, use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.

4. Finish with a hairspray.

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place. Choose a spray that provides a flexible hold so that your hair remains touchable and natural-looking.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling a lob with thick hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Product

Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair to add texture and volume. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create a tousled, beachy look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a straightener or curling iron to style your hair. For a sleek, straight look, use a flat iron to smooth out your hair. For a more relaxed, wavy look, use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a flexible hold hairspray to keep your lob in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Hair

Here are some tips for styling thick hair:

1. Use the right products

Choose products that are specifically designed for thick hair, such as volumizing shampoos, texturizing sprays, and flexible hold hairsprays.

2. Don't overwash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every third day.

3. Use heat protectant

When using hot tools like a straightener or curling iron, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

5. Embrace your natural texture

If you have naturally thick, wavy hair, embrace it! Work with your hair's natural texture instead of trying to fight it.

FAQs

Can a lob work for thick hair?

Yes, a lob can work for thick hair! In fact, a lob can help manage the weight of your hair and provide a flattering shape.

What products should I use to style my lob?

You should use products that are specifically designed for thick hair, such as volumizing shampoos, texturizing sprays, and flexible hold hairsprays.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every other day or every third day to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your locks healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Avoid using hot tools too often

Using hot tools like a straightener or curling iron too often can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of these tools to once or twice a week.

4. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

5. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to style a lob with thick hair, you can rock this chic and versatile haircut with confidence. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.
