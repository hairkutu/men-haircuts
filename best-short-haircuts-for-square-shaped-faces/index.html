Best Short Haircuts For Square Shaped Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a square shaped face, finding the right haircut can be challenging. You want a cut that will flatter your face shape, highlight your best features, and make you feel confident. In this guide, we’ll discuss the best short haircuts for square shaped faces, how to choose the right cut, and how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Haircut
  2. Best Short Haircuts for Square Shaped Faces
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Short Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a square shaped face, you want to choose a style that softens the angles of your face. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a cut that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your strong jawline.
  • Avoid straight bangs as they can make your face appear more square.
  • Choose a cut with layers that frame your face.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe to soften your face.
  • Try a pixie cut with longer bangs to create a more oval shape.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Shaped Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, here are some of the best short haircuts for square shaped faces:

Short Bob

A short bob with layers that frame your face is a great option for square shaped faces. This cut adds volume to the top of your head and softens your jawline.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with longer bangs can create a more oval shape and balance out your strong jawline. This cut is also low maintenance and easy to style.

Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) with side-swept bangs is a great option for square shaped faces. The layers add volume to the top of your head and the side-swept bangs soften your face.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob with longer layers on one side of your face can create a more oval shape and soften your face. This cut also adds volume to the top of your head.

Textured Crop

A textured crop with layers and choppy bangs can add volume to the top of your head and soften your face. This cut is also low maintenance and easy to style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut

Styling your short haircut can be easy with the right tools and products. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  4. Style your hair using a flat iron or curling wand.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut’s shape.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to absorb oil and add volume.

FAQ

What is a square shaped face?

A square shaped face has a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features.

What short haircuts should I avoid if I have a square shaped face?

Avoid straight bangs and blunt cuts that accentuate your jawline.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with a short haircut. Use bobby pins to secure any shorter pieces in place.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require different care than longer hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

With the right haircut and care, you can rock a short hairstyle with a square shaped face. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
