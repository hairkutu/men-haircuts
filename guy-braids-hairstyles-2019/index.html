Guy Braids Hairstyles 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try out in 2019? Why not consider getting braids? Braids have been a popular hairstyle for centuries and are versatile enough for both men and women. In this comprehensive guide, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about guy braids hairstyles in 2019.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Guy braids hairstyles have been gaining popularity in recent years, and 2019 is no exception. Braids are a great hairstyle for those who want to keep their hair out of their face, or for those who want to try something new. There are many different types of braids to choose from, including cornrows, box braids, and twists.

How to

Getting braids is a relatively easy process, but it does take some time and patience. Here are the basic steps to getting guy braids:

  1. Decide on the type of braid you want
  2. Choose a stylist who is experienced in braiding
  3. Cleanse and condition your hair before your appointment
  4. Sit still and be patient while your stylist braids your hair
  5. Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step process for getting guy braids:

  1. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly
  2. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry
  3. Section your hair into the desired size and shape of the braids
  4. Start braiding your hair, using a comb to keep the sections neat and tidy
  5. Continue braiding until you have completed the entire head
  6. Use rubber bands to secure the ends of the braids
  7. Trim any loose ends or flyaways
  8. Apply a styling product to keep the braids in place

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting guy braids:

  • Choose a stylist who is experienced in braiding
  • Cleanse and condition your hair before your appointment
  • Be prepared to sit still for several hours
  • Use a styling product to keep the braids in place
  • Be gentle when washing and brushing your braids to avoid damaging them

FAQs

Here are some common questions people have about guy braids hairstyles:

Q: How long do guy braids last?

A: Guy braids can last anywhere from a few weeks to several months, depending on the type of braid and how well they are cared for.

Q: Are guy braids easy to maintain?

A: Guy braids can be relatively easy to maintain, but they do require some special care. It's important to keep them clean and moisturized, and to avoid pulling or tugging on them.

Q: Can I swim with guy braids?

A: Yes, you can swim with guy braids, but it's important to protect them from chlorine and salt water. You can wear a swim cap or use a protective spray to help prevent damage.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your guy braids:

  • Wash your braids regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Moisturize your scalp with oil or a leave-in conditioner
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids
  • Protect your braids from heat damage by avoiding hot styling tools
  • Trim any loose ends or flyaways to keep your braids neat and tidy

With this comprehensive guide, you're now ready to try out guy braids hairstyles in 2019. Whether you opt for cornrows, box braids, or twists, these hairstyles are sure to make a statement and keep you looking stylish all year long.
