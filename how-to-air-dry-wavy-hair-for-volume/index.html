How To Air Dry Wavy Hair For Volume

Do you want to achieve voluminous, bouncy waves without the use of heat styling tools? Air drying your hair is the perfect solution! Not only is it a healthier alternative, but it also allows your natural waves to shine. In this article, we'll dive into the steps and tips on how to air dry wavy hair for volume.

How to Air Dry Wavy Hair for Volume

Air drying your hair may seem like a simple process, but there are a few steps you can take to ensure maximum volume and definition. Here's how:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before air drying, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for wavy hair to enhance your natural texture. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup or residue.

Step 2: Detangle Gently

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair. Starting from the ends and working your way up, be careful not to pull or tug on your hair. This step is crucial to prevent any knots or tangles that can weigh down your waves.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is designed for wavy hair to enhance your natural texture and add volume. A lightweight mousse, gel, or cream will do the trick. Apply it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it upward towards your scalp. This motion will encourage your waves to form and separate, creating more volume. Continue scrunching until your hair feels damp but not soaking wet.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've finished scrunching, let your hair air dry naturally. Avoid touching or manipulating your hair while it dries, as this can disrupt your waves and cause frizz. You can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer on the cool setting to speed up the drying process if needed.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve maximum volume and definition with air-dried wavy hair:

  • Try plopping your hair with a t-shirt or microfiber towel to enhance your waves even more.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and preserve your waves overnight.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Can I air dry my hair overnight?

Absolutely! Just make sure your hair is completely dry before going to bed to avoid any creases or kinks.

How long does it take for wavy hair to air dry?

The drying time can vary depending on your hair type and texture, but it usually takes between 2-4 hours. If you're in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer on the cool setting to speed up the process.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, voluminous waves. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy waves without the use of heat styling tools. Embrace your natural texture and let your waves shine!
