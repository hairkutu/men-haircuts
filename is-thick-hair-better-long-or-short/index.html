Is Thick Hair Better Long Or Short

Written by Frank Jul 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to hair length, there are many factors to consider. One of these is whether thick hair is better long or short. Some people believe that long hair is better for thick hair, while others believe that short hair is the way to go. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of each option and help you decide which one is right for you.

Description

Thick hair is a blessing and a curse. It can be voluminous and gorgeous, but it can also be difficult to manage. When it comes to hair length, thick hair can be a challenge. Long hair can be heavy and difficult to style, while short hair may not have enough weight to hold a style. So, which one is better?

How to

If you're trying to decide whether to go for a long or short hairstyle, there are a few things to consider. Here are some tips:

Consider your face shape

The shape of your face can have a big impact on how your hair looks. If you have a round face, shorter hair can help to elongate your face. If you have a long face, longer hair can help to balance out your features.

Think about your lifestyle

If you're an active person who likes to exercise, shorter hair may be more practical. On the other hand, if you have a more relaxed lifestyle, longer hair may be easier to manage.

Consider your hair texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in which length is better for you. If you have thick, coarse hair, longer styles may be easier to manage. If you have fine hair, shorter styles may be more flattering.

Step by Step

Now that you've considered these factors, it's time to decide which length is right for you. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Do your research

Look at pictures of hairstyles that you like and think about which length would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Consult with a stylist

A professional stylist can help you determine which length will work best for you. They can also provide advice on how to maintain and style your hair.

Step 3: Experiment

If you're unsure about which length to go for, try a few different styles. You can always grow your hair out or cut it shorter if you change your mind.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you decide on the right length for your hair:

  • Consider the weather in your area. If it's hot and humid, shorter hair may be more comfortable.
  • Think about the amount of time you're willing to spend styling your hair. Longer styles may require more maintenance.
  • Consider your personal style. If you prefer a more classic look, longer hair may be more appropriate. If you like to experiment with your style, shorter hair may be a better fit.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair length:

Q: Will cutting my hair shorter make it appear thinner?

A: Shorter hair can give the illusion of thicker hair. However, if you have very thin hair, cutting it too short may make it appear even thinner.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I cut it short?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style short hair. You can still wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun, or you can try a more creative updo.

Q: Is it better to cut my hair myself or go to a salon?

A: It's always best to go to a salon to have your hair cut. A professional stylist can ensure that your hair is cut evenly and that the style is flattering for your face shape.

Hair Care

No matter which length you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Whether you choose long or short hair, remember that it's just hair and it will grow back. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your style!
