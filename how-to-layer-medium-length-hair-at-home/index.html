How To Layer Medium Length Hair At Home

Written by Oliver Apr 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Layering your hair is a great way to add dimension and movement to your hair. It’s also perfect for those with medium length hair who are looking for a change. While many people opt to go to the salon to get their hair layered, it is possible to do it at home. In this article, we will walk you through the steps of how to layer medium length hair at home.

Description

Layering medium length hair is a great way to add volume, texture, and dimension to your hair. It involves cutting different lengths of hair to create a layered effect. This technique can be done on all hair types and is perfect for those who want to switch up their look without completely changing their hairstyle.

How To

Here are the steps to layer medium length hair at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Once you’ve washed your hair, dry it completely with a hair dryer. This will make it easier to cut your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by creating a part down the middle of your head. Then, create two sections by parting your hair at the back of your head. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair by taking a small section from the bottom layer. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Make sure to cut at an angle to create a layered effect. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way up to the top layer.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you’ve finished cutting all the layers, blend them together by taking small sections of hair and cutting them at an angle. This will help create a seamless transition between each layer.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you’ve finished cutting your hair, check your work by running your fingers through your hair. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your layers are even.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering your medium length hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take your time and don’t rush the process
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as it can cause your hair to shrink up when it dries

FAQ

Can I layer my hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can layer curly hair. However, it’s important to be cautious when cutting curly hair. Start with small sections and cut at an angle to avoid cutting too much off.

How often should I layer my hair?

It’s recommended to layer your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume of your layers.

Hair Care

After you’ve layered your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Now that you know how to layer medium length hair at home, give it a try and enjoy your new look!
