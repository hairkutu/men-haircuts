Square Face With Short Hair How To Rock The Look

Written by Luffy Jul 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a square face shape and are considering going for a short hairdo, you're in luck! Short haircuts can be incredibly flattering for square faces, highlighting your features and creating a balanced look. In this article, we'll show you how to rock square face with short hair, step by step.

Description: What is a Square Face Shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular cheekbones. The width and length of the face are almost equal, creating a boxy or square appearance. Square faces often have a more masculine or sharp look, but with the right haircut and styling, you can soften the features and create a more feminine, balanced look.

How to: Finding the Right Short Haircut for Square Faces

The key to rocking a short haircut with a square face is to find a style that accentuates your best features and balances out the sharpness of your face shape. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut:

1. Consider your hair texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in determining the best short haircut for your square face. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can work well to create volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a layered bob or shaggy pixie can help to control the bulk and create a more defined shape.

2. Play with angles

Creating angles in your haircut can help to soften the sharpness of your jawline and create a more balanced look. Consider a bob with angled layers or a pixie cut with longer side-swept bangs.

3. Don't be afraid of volume

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help to elongate your face shape and create a more oval appearance. Consider a layered pixie or a messy bob with tousled waves.

Step by Step: How to Style Short Hair for Square Faces

Once you've found the perfect short haircut for your square face, it's time to style it! Here's how to achieve a chic, effortless look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to your hair to add texture and hold.

3. Use a curling iron

Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls to your hair. Focus on the ends of your hair to create a soft, feminine look.

4. Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to set the style and keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips: How to Maintain Short Hair for Square Faces

Now that you've got your short haircut and styling routine down, it's important to maintain your hair to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Schedule regular trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to keep the shape and length looking just right. Schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage

When using heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Experiment with accessories

Adding a headband, scarf, or statement hair clip can add a fun, trendy touch to your short haircut.

FAQ: Common Questions About Square Face with Short Hair

Q: Will a short haircut make my square face look too masculine?

A: Not at all! With the right haircut and styling, short hair can be incredibly feminine and flattering for square faces.

Q: How do I know if a certain short haircut will look good on me?

A: It's always a good idea to bring some inspiration photos to your hairstylist to see if a certain haircut will work well with your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and half-up styles.

Hair Care: Products for Short Hair with Square Faces

When it comes to hair care products for short hair with square faces, it's important to choose products that will help to add texture, volume, and hold. Here are some of our top picks:

1. Texturizing spray

A texturizing spray can help to add volume and texture to your short haircut, creating a more defined and dynamic look.

2. Heat protectant spray

When using heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Hairspray

A light hold hairspray can help to set your style and keep it in place throughout the day.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a short haircut with your square face shape. Remember to choose a style that works best for your hair type and face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques and hair accessories. Happy styling!
