How To Diffuse Wavy Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Robby May 12, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have wavy or curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. Hair diffusers are a popular tool that can help you achieve the perfect curls or waves. In this guide, we’ll show you how to diffuse wavy curly hair step by step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Diffuse Wavy Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Diffusing Wavy Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Curly Hair

How to Diffuse Wavy Curly Hair

Diffusing wavy curly hair involves using a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair while keeping your curls intact. Here’s how to diffuse wavy curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can dry out your hair and strip it of its natural oils, which can make it difficult to style.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair while it’s still wet. This will help define your curls and keep them moisturized.

Step 3: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer. Make sure the diffuser is facing upwards towards your scalp.

Step 4: Start Diffusing

Start diffusing your hair on a low heat setting. Use a medium speed and cup the diffuser around your curls. Hold for a few seconds before moving on to the next section.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

Once your hair is mostly dry, switch to the cool air setting to set your curls in place. This will help prevent frizz and make your curls look more defined.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step by step guide on how to diffuse wavy curly hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair gently without rubbing it too hard.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair while it’s still damp. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to style.

Step 4: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer. Make sure the diffuser is facing upwards towards your scalp.

Step 5: Start Diffusing

Start diffusing your hair on a low heat setting. Use a medium speed and cup the diffuser around your curls. Hold for a few seconds before moving on to the next section. Work in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 6: Focus on the Roots

Use the diffuser to dry your roots first, as they take the longest to dry. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around in circular motions.

Step 7: Dry the Length of Your Hair

Once your roots are dry, move on to the length of your hair. Cup the diffuser around your curls and hold for a few seconds before moving on to the next section. Make sure you’re using a low heat setting and a medium speed.

Step 8: Finish with Cool Air

Once your hair is mostly dry, switch to the cool air setting to set your curls in place. This will help prevent frizz and make your curls look more defined.

Tips for Diffusing Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when diffusing wavy curly hair:

  • Don’t touch your hair too much while diffusing. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Use a diffuser with wide prongs to help distribute heat evenly and prevent your curls from getting tangled.
  • Use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more if needed. Too much product can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Consider using a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This can help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Can I diffuse my hair every day?

Diffusing your hair every day can cause damage and dryness. It’s best to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

What kind of hair dryer should I use?

Look for a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment and multiple heat and speed settings. Make sure the hair dryer is lightweight and easy to hold for extended periods of time.

Can I use a diffuser on straight hair?

A diffuser is designed to enhance and define curls, so it’s not recommended for use on straight hair.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your wavy curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage and disrupt your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to keep it moisturized and define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.

By following these tips and using a diffuser, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls or waves without damaging your hair. Happy styling!
