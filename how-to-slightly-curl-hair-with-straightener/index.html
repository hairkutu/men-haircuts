How To Slightly Curl Hair With Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone who wants to add a little wave to your hair but don't want to invest in a curling iron, then a straightener can be a great tool to achieve a slight curl in your hair. It's an easy and effortless way to add some texture to your hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to slightly curl hair with straightener.

How to Slightly Curl Hair with Straightener

Before we get started, here are some of the things you will need:

  • A hair straightener
  • Heat-protectant spray
  • Hairbrush
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to achieve a slight curl in your hair using a straightener:

  1. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles.
  2. Apply a heat-protectant spray to your hair to prevent any heat damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using hair clips.
  4. Take one section of hair and clamp the straightener near the roots.
  5. Twist the straightener away from your face and pull it down towards the end of your hair.
  6. Release the straightener and let your hair fall naturally.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 for each section of hair.
  8. Once you have curled all sections, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls slightly.
  9. If you want to add more hold to your curls, apply a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Slightly Curling Hair with Straightener

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slight curl with your straightener:

  • Use a straightener with rounded edges to avoid any unwanted kinks in your hair.
  • Don't clamp the straightener too tightly as it can leave creases in your hair.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use smaller sections of hair for tighter curls and larger sections of hair for looser curls.
  • Always apply a heat-protectant spray to your hair before using any heat styling tool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about slightly curling hair with a straightener:

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. On average, the curls can last for up to 24 hours.

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, you can use this method on short hair. However, you may need to use smaller sections of hair and a smaller straightener.

Is it safe to use a straightener on my hair?

Yes, it is safe to use a straightener on your hair as long as you take the necessary precautions. Always use a heat-protectant spray and don't apply too much heat to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to slightly curl hair with straightener, give it a try and see how it works for you. With a little practice, you can achieve effortless waves in no time.
