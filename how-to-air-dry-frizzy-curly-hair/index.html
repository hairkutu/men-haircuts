How To Air Dry Frizzy Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have frizzy curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to tame. You may have tried various products and techniques to get your hair to behave, but have you ever tried air-drying your hair? It's a great way to create beautiful, natural-looking curls without the use of heat styling tools. In this article, we'll show you how to air dry frizzy curly hair in a way that will leave you with gorgeous, bouncy curls.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Air Dry Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Air Drying Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

How to Air Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step to air-drying frizzy curly hair is to start with clean, well-conditioned hair. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner. After shampooing and conditioning, gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them from frizzing up. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Once you've applied your product, you can either let your hair air dry naturally or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process. If you're using a diffuser, be sure to use it on the lowest heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to air-drying frizzy curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, well-conditioned hair.
  2. Gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a loose spiral.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry naturally or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently shake out the twists to reveal your beautiful, bouncy curls.

Tips for Air Drying Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when air-drying frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream evenly throughout your hair.
  • Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a loose spiral to help define your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz.
  • If you're in a hurry, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you air dry frizzy curly hair overnight?

A: It's not recommended to air dry frizzy curly hair overnight as it can cause your hair to become tangled and matted. It's best to air dry your hair during the day when you can keep an eye on it and make sure it's drying evenly.

Q: How can I prevent frizz when air drying my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz when air drying curly hair, be sure to use a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream and divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a loose spiral. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer if you're in a hurry.

Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

If you have frizzy curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your hair after washing.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible.

With these tips and techniques, you can air dry frizzy curly hair and enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls without the use of heat styling tools. Give it a try and see how easy it can be to achieve a natural, effortless look!
