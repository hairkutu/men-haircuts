How To Figure Out The Best Hairstyle For You

Written by Bardi May 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're anything like me, choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options out there, it's hard to know where to start. But fear not, because I'm here to help you figure out the best hairstyle for you. In this article, I'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to tips on finding the right stylist.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out the best hairstyle for you is to determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down your options and choose a style that flatters your features. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

Oval

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead, you have an oval face shape. Lucky for you, almost any hairstyle will look good on you!

Round

If your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and full cheeks, you have a round face shape. Try a longer hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head to elongate your face.

Square

If your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and forehead, you have a square face shape. Soften your features with a layered or textured hairstyle that falls around your face.

Heart

If your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline, with a pointed chin, you have a heart face shape. Opt for a hairstyle that adds width at the jawline, like a bob with layers that frame your face.

Diamond

If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at both the forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume at the top of your head, like a short, layered cut.

Oblong

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead, you have an oblong face shape. Try a hairstyle with volume and width around the sides of your face, like a shoulder-length bob.

Tips on Finding the Right Stylist

Once you've determined your face shape and have an idea of what type of hairstyle will work for you, it's time to find the right stylist. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Ask for recommendations from friends and family
  • Look up reviews online
  • Check out the stylist's portfolio to see if they have experience with your hair type and desired style
  • Schedule a consultation to discuss your options and make sure you're comfortable with the stylist

FAQ

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on the length and style of your hair, but generally every 6-8 weeks is a good rule of thumb to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

Don't panic! Give it a few days to see if it grows on you. If not, talk to your stylist about your concerns and see if they can make any adjustments.

How can I keep my hair healthy?

Regular trims, using heat protectant when styling, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can all help keep your hair healthy and strong.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength

With these tips in mind, you'll be well on your way to figuring out the best hairstyle for you. Remember to take your time, do your research, and work with a stylist you trust to achieve the perfect look.
