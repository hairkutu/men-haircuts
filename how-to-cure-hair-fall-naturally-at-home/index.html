How To Cure Hair Fall Naturally At Home

Hair fall is a common problem faced by many people. It can be caused by various factors such as stress, poor diet, hormonal imbalances, and genetics. Hair fall can be reduced and even eliminated by using natural remedies that can be easily made at home. In this article, we will discuss how to cure hair fall naturally at home with simple and effective remedies.

  • How to Cure Hair Fall Naturally at Home
  • Step by Step

How to Cure Hair Fall Naturally at Home

Here are some natural remedies that can help cure hair fall:

1. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. It contains enzymes that can nourish and strengthen hair follicles. Apply aloe vera gel on your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Egg Mask

Eggs are a rich source of protein which is essential for healthy hair growth. Beat an egg and apply it on your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 20 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural ingredient that can help stimulate hair growth. It contains sulfur which can improve blood circulation to the hair follicles. Apply onion juice on your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Green Tea

Green tea is a natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. It contains antioxidants that can help prevent hair loss. Brew green tea and apply it on your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to cure hair fall naturally at home:

  1. Choose a natural remedy
  2. Prepare the ingredients
  3. Apply the remedy on your scalp and hair
  4. Leave it on for the recommended time
  5. Wash it off with a mild shampoo

Tips

Here are some tips to help prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Reduce stress
  • Avoid harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Massage your scalp regularly
  • Get enough sleep

FAQ

Q: How often should I use these natural remedies?

A: You can use these remedies once a week for best results.

Q: Can I use these remedies if I have colored hair?

A: Yes, these remedies are safe to use on colored hair.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips to take care of your hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these natural remedies and tips, you can cure hair fall naturally at home and achieve healthy and strong hair.
