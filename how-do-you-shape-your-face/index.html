How Do You Shape Your Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your plain face and looking for ways to enhance your features? Shaping your face is an easy and effective way to give your face a new look. In this article, we will discuss how to shape your face, step by step. Whether you have a round, square, or heart-shaped face, we’ve got you covered.

How to Shape Your Face

Shaping your face involves highlighting and contouring your face to create the desired look. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in shaping your face is to determine your face shape. This will help you know which areas to highlight and contour. There are four main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped

Once you have identified your face shape, you can proceed to the next step.

Step 2: Highlight Your Face

Highlighting involves using a light concealer or foundation to bring out your features. Here are the areas where you should apply highlighter:

  • Under the eyes
  • Bridge of the nose
  • Cupid’s bow
  • Forehead
  • Chin

Apply the highlighter using a brush or sponge, and blend well. This will create a bright and youthful look.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

Contouring involves using a darker shade of foundation or powder to create shadows on your face. Here are the areas where you should apply contour:

  • Temples
  • Cheekbones
  • Jawline
  • Sides of the nose

Apply the contour using a brush or sponge and blend well. This will create depth and definition on your face.

Step 4: Blend Well

The key to achieving a natural look is blending. Use a beauty blender or brush to blend the highlighter and contour well. This will ensure that there are no harsh lines on your face.

Tips for Shaping Your Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaping your face:

  • Use a matte bronzer for contouring
  • Choose a highlighter that matches your skin tone
  • Blend well to avoid harsh lines
  • Use a light hand when applying makeup
  • Practice makes perfect

FAQ

What is the best product for contouring?

The best product for contouring is a matte bronzer. This will create a natural shadow effect on your face.

What is the best way to blend contour and highlight?

The best way to blend contour and highlight is to use a beauty blender or brush. This will ensure that the makeup is blended well and there are no harsh lines.

Can shaping your face change your features permanently?

No, shaping your face is a temporary way to enhance your features. It does not change your features permanently.

Hair Care Tips

Shaping your face also involves styling your hair in a way that complements your face shape. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look wider
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Shaping your face is an easy and fun way to enhance your features. With the right makeup and hair care tips, you can achieve a new look that complements your face shape. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.
