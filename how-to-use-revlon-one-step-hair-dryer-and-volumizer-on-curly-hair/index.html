How To Use Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer On Curly Hair

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a revolutionary hair styling tool that can give you a salon-like blowout at home. It can be especially useful for those with curly hair who want to straighten their hair and add volume without using multiple styling tools. In this article, we will discuss how to use Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on curly hair to achieve a flawless, silky-smooth look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is an all-in-one styling tool that combines the power of a hair dryer and a styling brush. It has a unique oval brush design that can help you create smooth, voluminous hair with ease. The brush is made of nylon pin and tufted bristles that can detangle, smooth, and style your hair while the hot air is blowing.

How To

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be used on damp hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that all sections are evenly dried.

Step 3: Start styling

Turn on the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer. Choose the heat setting that suits your hair type. The tool has two heat and speed settings. For curly hair, it is recommended to use the low heat setting. Place the brush at the root of your hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process for each section of your hair. For extra volume, use the brush to lift your hair at the root while drying.

Step 4: Finish styling

Once you have styled all sections of your hair, switch off the tool and let it cool down. Gently run your fingers through your hair to separate the strands and create a natural look. You can also add a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Tips

  • Always use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • For best results, use the tool on damp hair.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to style and ensure even drying.
  • Use the low heat setting for curly hair to avoid frizz and damage.
  • For extra volume, use the brush to lift your hair at the root while drying.

FAQ

Can I use Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use the tool on wet hair. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water before using the tool.

Can I use the tool on all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be used on all hair types. However, it is recommended to use the low heat setting for curly hair to avoid damage and frizz.

How do I clean the tool?

You can clean the tool by wiping it with a damp cloth. Make sure the tool is unplugged before cleaning.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair in good condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using styling tools.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, you can achieve a flawless, salon-like blowout at home. Happy styling!
