Low Maintenance Medium Length Men s Hairstyles 2020 A Guide

If you're looking for a low maintenance hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to manage, a medium length cut may be just what you need. In 2020, there were plenty of great options for men's medium length hairstyles, and many of these styles are still popular today. Here's a guide to some of the best low maintenance medium length men's hairstyles of 2020, and how you can achieve them.

How to Choose the Right Low Maintenance Medium Length Men's Hairstyle

When choosing a low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • If you have a round face, choose a cut that adds height to the top of your head, like a textured quiff or messy fringe.
  • If you have a square face, opt for a style that softens your jawline, like a side-swept fringe or a layered cut.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and try a layered cut that enhances your curls.
  • If you have fine hair, choose a style that adds volume and texture, like a messy crop or a tousled fringe.
  • If you have thick hair, opt for a cut that thins out your hair, like a layered cut or a disconnected undercut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Low Maintenance Medium Length Men's Hairstyle

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose a cut that suits your face shape and hair type.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair to the desired length and style.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, like wax, pomade, or clay.
  5. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair using your fingers or a comb.
  6. Style your hair into the desired shape, using your fingers or a brush to create texture and volume.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray or matte powder to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Low Maintenance Medium Length Men's Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and in shape.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about low maintenance medium length men's hairstyles:

  • Q: Are medium length hairstyles suitable for all face shapes?
  • A: Yes, there are medium length hairstyles that suit all face shapes. It's important to choose a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural shape.
  • Q: Can I achieve a low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle with curly hair?
  • A: Yes, you can embrace your natural curls and achieve a low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle with the right cut and styling products.
  • Q: How often should I visit my stylist to maintain my low maintenance medium length men's hairstyle?
  • A: It's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair trimmed and in shape.

Hair Care Tips for Low Maintenance Medium Length Men's Hairstyles

Here are some general hair care tips for low maintenance medium length men's hairstyles:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Dry your hair gently with a towel, avoiding rubbing it too hard.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, including hair dryers and styling tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

There you have it, a guide to low maintenance medium length men's hairstyles for 2020. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can achieve a stylish and easy-to-maintain look that suits your face shape and personal style.
