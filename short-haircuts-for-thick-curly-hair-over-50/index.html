Short Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 50 and have thick curly hair? Do you want a low-maintenance haircut that looks stylish and trendy? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for thick curly hair over 50. Whether you have long, medium or short hair, we have got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are perfect for women over 50 with thick curly hair. They are easy to maintain, and they can make you look younger and more stylish. Short haircuts can also add volume to your hair, which is great for women with thinning hair. The best short haircuts for thick curly hair over 50 are pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for your thick curly hair, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. You should also consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your hair. Once you have chosen a cut, you can style your hair with a diffuser, which will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. You can also use a curl cream or mousse to enhance your curls.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling short curly hair:

  1. Cleanse and condition your hair with products formulated for thick curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair.
  3. Dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any curls that need a little extra help.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick curly hair over 50:

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: No, a short haircut can actually make you look younger and more stylish.

Q: What is the best short haircut for thick curly hair over 50?

A: The best short haircuts for thick curly hair over 50 are pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts.

Q: How often should I trim my short curly hair?

A: You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care products that are great for women with short curly hair:

  • DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler: This curl cream defines and enhances curls without weighing them down.
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel: This gel helps to control frizz and define curls in humid weather.
  • Moroccanoil Treatment: This oil nourishes and conditions hair, making it soft and shiny.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for thick curly hair over 50, it's time to schedule your next appointment with your hairstylist. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob cut, or layered cut, you are sure to look stylish and trendy with your new short haircut.
