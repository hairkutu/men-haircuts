How To Make Layers On Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Description

Layers can add depth and dimension to short hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. But how do you achieve layers on hair that's already short? This guide will walk you through the steps to create layers on short hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining and styling your new look.

How to

1. Start with clean, dry hair. 2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. 3. Take a small section of hair from the top section and comb it straight up. 4. Use scissors to make a diagonal cut, starting at the ends of the hair and working your way up towards the scalp. 5. Repeat this process on the rest of the top section, making sure to cut at different angles to create layers of varying lengths. 6. Move on to the side sections, using the same diagonal cutting technique. 7. Finally, tackle the back section, pulling hair straight up and cutting diagonally to create layers. 8. Check your work in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments.

Step-by-step

1. Wash and dry your hair. 2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. 3. Take a small section of hair from the top section and comb it straight up. 4. Use scissors to make a diagonal cut, starting at the ends of the hair and working your way up towards the scalp. 5. Repeat this process on the rest of the top section, making sure to cut at different angles to create layers of varying lengths. 6. Move on to the side sections, using the same diagonal cutting technique. 7. Finally, tackle the back section, pulling hair straight up and cutting diagonally to create layers. 8. Check your work in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut. - Start with small sections of hair, and work your way up to larger sections. - Don't be afraid to mix up the angles of your cuts to create more natural-looking layers. - If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, consider enlisting the help of a friend or professional stylist.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have layers if my hair is really short? A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to take small sections of hair and cut at varying angles to create layers of different lengths. Q: Will cutting layers make my hair look thinner? A: No, cutting layers can actually make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Care

To maintain your new layered look, be sure to use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and trim your ends every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends. Additionally, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help boost volume and add texture to your hair.
