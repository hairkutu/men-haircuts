How To Flatten The Top Of A Wig

Are you tired of your wig looking bulky and unnatural on top? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many wig wearers struggle with this issue, but it's actually quite easy to fix. In this article, we'll show you how to flatten the top of a wig using simple techniques that you can do at home.

How to Flatten the Top of a Wig

Flattening the top of a wig may seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. Here's what you need to do:

1. Use a Wig Cap

A wig cap can help to flatten the top of your wig and make it look more natural. Simply put the wig cap on your head, making sure that it covers all of your natural hair. This will create a smooth base for your wig to sit on.

2. Adjust the Wig Cap

Once you have the wig cap on, adjust it so that it fits snugly on your head. You don't want it to be too tight, but you also don't want it to be loose. The wig cap should be flat against your head, with no bumps or creases.

3. Use Bobby Pins

If your wig still looks bulky on top, you can use bobby pins to flatten it. Simply take a bobby pin and slide it underneath the wig, so that it's resting on top of the wig cap. Then, push the bobby pin down towards your head, so that it's holding the wig in place. Repeat this process with several bobby pins, until the top of the wig is flat.

Step-by-Step Guide

To help you flatten the top of your wig, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Put on a Wig Cap

Before you put on your wig, make sure that you have a wig cap on. This will help to create a smooth base for your wig to sit on.

Step 2: Adjust the Wig Cap

Once you have the wig cap on, adjust it so that it fits snugly on your head. Make sure that there are no bumps or creases in the wig cap.

Step 3: Put on Your Wig

Now it's time to put on your wig. Place the wig on your head, making sure that it's centered and straight.

Step 4: Use Bobby Pins

If your wig still looks bulky on top, use bobby pins to flatten it. Slide a bobby pin underneath the wig, resting it on top of the wig cap. Push the bobby pin down towards your head, holding the wig in place. Repeat this process with several bobby pins, until the top of the wig is flat.

Tips for Flattening a Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you flatten the top of your wig:

1. Use a Wig Stand

If you're having trouble flattening your wig while it's on your head, try using a wig stand. This will allow you to see the top of the wig more easily, and make it easier to adjust.

2. Use a Hair Straightener

If your wig is made of synthetic hair, you can use a hair straightener to flatten the top. Simply set the straightener to a low heat, and run it over the top of the wig, pressing down gently.

3. Use Hairspray

If your wig is made of human hair, you can use a light hairspray to help flatten the top. Simply spray the hairspray onto the top of the wig, then use a brush to smooth it down.

FAQs

Q: Will flattening the top of my wig damage it?

A: No, flattening the top of your wig will not damage it. As long as you use gentle techniques, such as using bobby pins or a hair straightener on a low heat, your wig will be fine.

Q: How often should I flatten the top of my wig?

A: This will depend on how often you wear your wig and how much you move around in it. If you wear your wig every day, you may need to flatten the top more often. If you wear it less frequently, you may only need to do it once in a while.

Hair Care Tips for Wigs

To keep your wig looking great, here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash it regularly

Wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner, and follow the instructions carefully.

2. Store it properly

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a box to keep it from getting tangled or damaged.

3. Avoid heat

Avoid exposing your wig to heat, as this can damage the fibers. Don't use a hair dryer, straightener, or curling iron on your wig.

By following these tips, you can keep your wig looking great and natural, with a flat top that looks just like your own hair.
