What Hair Colour Is Best For Your Skin Tone

Written by Aprilia Mar 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair colour, it's important to consider your skin tone to find the hue that will complement your complexion the most. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what hair colour for your skin tone is the best choice. Here's a breakdown of what to consider when making your decision.

Description

Your skin tone is determined by the natural pigments in your skin, which can range from fair to deep. This means that different hair colours will look better on different people depending on their skin tone. Choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone will highlight your best features and make you look your best.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing a hair colour that suits your skin tone is to determine what tone your skin has. There are three main categories: cool, warm, and neutral. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose hair colours that have cool tones as well. This means shades like ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown. Avoid warm colours like golden blonde or copper, as they will clash with your skin tone.

Warm Skin Tones

For those with warm undertones, you'll want to choose hair colours with warm tones as well. This includes shades like golden blonde, honey brown, and copper. Avoid cool colours like ash blonde or platinum, as they will clash with your skin tone.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have neutral undertones, you're lucky! You can pull off both cool and warm hair colours. However, you may want to avoid extreme shades like platinum or bright red, as they can wash you out.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Colour

1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.

2. Choose a hair colour with tones that match your skin tone (cool for cool, warm for warm).

3. Consider the level of maintenance required for the hair colour you choose.

4. Consult with a professional stylist for their opinion and expertise.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

- Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair from fading.

- Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the colour.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

- Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hair colour for fair skin?

For fair skin, cool tones like platinum blonde, ash brown, and cool reds are typically the most flattering.

What is the best hair colour for medium skin?

Medium skin tones can pull off a wide range of hair colours, but warm shades like honey blonde, chestnut brown, and warm reds are particularly flattering.

What is the best hair colour for dark skin?

For dark skin tones, rich shades like deep brown, black, and warm reds are typically the most flattering.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right hair colour for your skin tone can make a huge difference in your overall look. By considering your skin tone and choosing a hair colour with complementary tones, you can highlight your best features and look your best. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep your new colour looking fresh and vibrant!
