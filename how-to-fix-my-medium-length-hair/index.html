Description

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to manage, but it can be tricky to style. If you're struggling to find ways to fix your medium length hair, you've come to the right place! This step by step guide will show you how to create beautiful hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion.

How to Fix My Medium Length Hair

There are many different ways to style medium length hair, depending on your hair type, texture, and personal preference. Here are some popular ways to fix your medium length hair:

  • Half up, half down
  • Braids and twists
  • Ponytails
  • Buns and chignons
  • Curls and waves
  • Sleek and straight

Experiment with different styles to find the ones that work best for you. Don't be afraid to mix and match different techniques to create a unique look that reflects your personal style.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to fix your medium length hair:

Half Up, Half Down

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and bring them together at the back of your head.
  3. Tie the two sections of hair together with a hair tie.
  4. Fluff up the hair at the crown of your head to create volume.

Braids and Twists

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it all the way down to the end.
  3. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  4. Twist the two braids together at the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

Ponytails

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a high ponytail.
  2. Tie the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins.

Buns and Chignons

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Curls and Waves

  1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or hot rollers.
  2. Brush through your curls to create soft waves.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair serum to tame any frizz.

Sleek and Straight

  1. Start by blow drying your hair straight with a paddle brush.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten any remaining waves or curls.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful medium length hair:

  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing medium length hair:

How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people find that washing their hair every other day works well.

What's the best way to add volume to medium length hair?

Try blow drying your hair upside down, using a volumizing spray, or using a round brush when blow drying to add volume.

How can I prevent frizz in my medium length hair?

Use a smoothing serum or oil, avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, and try using a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some products that are great for caring for medium length hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair serum or oil
  • Hairspray or styling gel

Remember to always read the labels and instructions on hair care products before using them, and consult a hair care professional if you have any questions or concerns about caring for your medium length hair.

Fixing your medium length hair doesn't have to be a challenge. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create beautiful hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Remember to take good care of your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the ones that work best for you.
