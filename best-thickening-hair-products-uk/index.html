Best Thickening Hair Products Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of having thin and limp hair? Do you want to add volume and thickness to your hair without damaging it? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best thickening hair products UK that can help you get the hair of your dreams.

Description

Thickening hair products are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. These products contain ingredients that penetrate your hair shaft and plump up each strand, giving the appearance of fuller, thicker hair. They come in various forms, such as shampoos, conditioners, serums, and sprays. These products are especially helpful for people with fine or thin hair who want to achieve a fuller look.

How to Choose the Best Thickening Hair Products UK

Choosing the right thickening hair product can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available in the market. Here are some factors to consider when selecting the best thickening hair products UK:

Hair Type

Consider your hair type before choosing a thickening hair product. For example, if you have curly hair, you may need a product that doesn't weigh your hair down. On the other hand, if you have straight hair, you may need a product that adds more texture and volume.

Ingredients

Check the ingredients of the product before buying it. Look for products that contain natural ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen, as these ingredients can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

Reviews

Read reviews of the product before buying it. Customer reviews can give you an idea of how effective the product is and whether it's worth the investment.

Top 5 Best Thickening Hair Products UK

1. Nioxin System 1 Cleanser Shampoo

The Nioxin System 1 Cleanser Shampoo is a popular choice for people with fine, thinning hair. It contains a blend of natural ingredients such as peppermint oil, white tea extract, and nettle extract that help remove impurities and add volume to your hair.

2. L'Oreal Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo

The L'Oreal Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo is a budget-friendly option that contains filloxane, a molecule that penetrates the hair shaft and expands it from within, giving the appearance of thicker hair.

3. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

The Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray is a lightweight spray that adds volume and texture to your hair. It contains keratin amino acids that help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

4. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Go Big Volumising Treatment

The Bumble and Bumble Thickening Go Big Volumising Treatment is a leave-in treatment that contains a blend of natural ingredients such as acacia gum, aloe vera, and hydrolyzed soy protein that help add volume and thickness to your hair.

5. Kerastase Resistance Bain Volumifique Shampoo

The Kerastase Resistance Bain Volumifique Shampoo is a luxurious shampoo that contains a combination of natural ingredients such as hyaluronic acid, intra-cylane, and gluco-peptide that help add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Using Thickening Hair Products

Here is a step-by-step guide to using thickening hair products:

Step 1: Shampoo your hair

Start by washing your hair with a thickening shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, focusing on the roots. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply conditioner

Apply a thickening conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Apply a thickening serum or spray

Apply a thickening serum or spray to your damp hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots. Blow-dry your hair as usual.

Tips for Using Thickening Hair Products

Here are some tips for using thickening hair products:

Tip 1: Use the products consistently

For best results, use the thickening hair products consistently. Follow the instructions on the label and use the products as directed.

Tip 2: Avoid using too much product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use the products sparingly, and only apply them to the roots and mid-lengths of your hair.

Tip 3: Experiment with different products

Not all thickening hair products work for everyone. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and needs.

FAQs

Q: Can thickening hair products cause hair damage?

A: No, thickening hair products are designed to be gentle on your hair and scalp. However, using too much product or using it too frequently can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Q: Can thickening hair products work for people with curly hair?

A: Yes, thickening hair products can work for people with curly hair. Look for products that add volume and texture without weighing your hair down.

Q: Can I use thickening hair products if I have coloured hair?

A: Yes, you can use thickening hair products if you have coloured hair. However, avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its colour.

Hair Care Tips for Thickening Hair

Here are some hair care tips that can help you achieve thicker, fuller hair:

Tip 1: Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and thickness. Include foods that are high in protein, such as eggs, fish, and chicken.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat styling tools

Using heat styling tools such as hairdryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look thinner. Avoid using these tools as much as possible and let your hair air-dry instead.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help get rid of split ends and prevent breakage, making your hair look fuller and healthier.

Conclusion

Thickening hair products can be a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair. With so many options available in the market, it's important to choose the right product that works for your hair type and needs. Follow the tips and guidelines mentioned in this article, and you'll be on your way to achieving the hair of your dreams.
