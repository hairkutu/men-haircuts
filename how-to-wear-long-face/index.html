How To Wear Long Face Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine Mar 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Having a long face can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style your hair. While some hairstyles can make your face look even longer, others can help balance out your features and make you look more proportionate. In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and hair care advice on how to wear long face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Long Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Wear Long Face

The key to wearing a long face is to choose hairstyles that add width and volume to your face. This can be achieved through various techniques, such as adding layers, bangs, or waves. Here are some hairstyles that work well for long faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, which can help balance out your long face. Ask your stylist to cut long layers that start at your chin or below, as this will draw attention away from your forehead and make your face look more proportionate.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten your forehead and make your face look less elongated. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs diagonally, so they blend in with the rest of your hair. Avoid straight-across bangs, as these can make your face look even longer.

3. Waves and Curls

Waves and curls can add volume and width to your hair, which can help balance out your long face. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add loose waves or curls to your hair. Avoid tight curls, as these can make your face look even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear long face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to choose hairstyles that add volume and width to your hair.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that works well for long faces, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, or waves and curls. Consult with your stylist to find a hairstyle that complements your features and personality.

Step 3: Maintain Your Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized, and using a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to wear long face:

  • Try a side-parted hairstyle, as this can help balance out your features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as these can make your face look even longer.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to your hair, as this can make your face look even longer.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as these can make your face look even longer.

FAQs

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that are too flat, straight, or short, as these can make your face look even longer. You should also avoid hairstyles that add height to your hair, as this can make your face look even longer.

Q: Can bangs work for long faces?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can work well for long faces, as they can help shorten your forehead and make your face look less elongated.

Q: Do I need to use heat tools to style my hair?

A: No, you don't need to use heat tools to style your hair. There are many heat-free styling techniques, such as braiding, twisting, or air-drying your hair, that can add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some additional hair care tips for long faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this can help prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or clips that are too tight, as these can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips, tricks, and hair care advice, you can confidently wear your long face and look your best. Remember to consult with your stylist to find a hairstyle that complements your features and personality.
