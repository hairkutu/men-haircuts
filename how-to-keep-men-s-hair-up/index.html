How To Keep Men s Hair Up Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 · 11 min read

If you're a guy with medium to long hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it up and out of your face. Whether you're running errands, working out, or just trying to look your best, keeping your hair up can be a real challenge. But it doesn't have to be! In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and techniques for keeping men's hair up and out of the way.

How to Keep Men's Hair Up

Keeping men's hair up requires more than just a simple hair tie or clip. You need to have the right tools, products, and techniques to keep your hair in place all day long. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair up:

1. Use a Hair Tie

The easiest way to keep men's hair up is to use a hair tie. While it may seem like a simple solution, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you use a hair tie that's strong enough to hold your hair in place. Second, avoid using hair ties that are too tight, as they can cause damage to your hair and scalp. Finally, position the hair tie at the top of your head for the most secure hold.

2. Try a Hair Clip

If you're looking for a more secure hold, try using a hair clip. Hair clips come in a variety of sizes and styles, so you can choose one that works best for your hair type and length. For longer hair, try using a jaw clip or claw clip, which can hold more hair in place. For shorter hair, a small bobby pin or snap clip may be all you need.

3. Use Hair Gel or Pomade

If you're looking for a more stylish way to keep your hair up, try using hair gel or pomade. These products can help you create a variety of looks, from a slicked-back style to a messy, tousled look. Apply the product to damp hair and use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style. Just be sure to choose a product that's right for your hair type and won't leave your hair feeling greasy or weighed down.

Step-by-Step: How to Keep Men's Hair Up

Now that you know some of the basics of keeping men's hair up, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and well-conditioned. This will help prevent tangles and make it easier to comb or brush your hair into place.

Step 2: Choose Your Tool

Decide whether you want to use a hair tie, clip, or styling product to keep your hair up. Choose the tool that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Step 3: Position Your Hair Tie or Clip

If you're using a hair tie or clip, position it at the top of your head for the most secure hold. For longer hair, you may want to use two hair ties or clips for added support.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

If you're using styling product, apply it to damp hair and work it through evenly. Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To help keep your hair in place all day long, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help lock in your style and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Keeping Men's Hair Up

Here are some additional tips to help you keep men's hair up:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

If you have fine or thinning hair, try using a texturizing spray to add volume and hold. These sprays can help give your hair more grip, making it easier to keep in place.

2. Don't Overdo It

While it may be tempting to use a lot of product or pull your hair back tightly, this can actually cause damage to your hair and scalp. Use only what you need to keep your hair up, and avoid pulling your hair back too tightly.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques for keeping your hair up. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, undone style, there's a technique that will work for you.

FAQ: How to Keep Men's Hair Up

Q: Can I use a hair tie on short hair?

A: Yes! Even if you have short hair, you can use a hair tie or clip to keep it up and out of your face. Just be sure to choose a hair tie or clip that's appropriate for your hair length.

Q: Can I use hairspray on my hair tie?

A: No! While hairspray can help hold your hair in place, it's not meant to be used on hair ties or clips. Instead, spray the hairspray onto your hair after you've secured it in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I'm styling it daily?

A: It depends on your hair type and the products you're using. If you're using a lot of styling product, you may need to wash your hair more often to prevent buildup. However, if you're using a light styling product and your hair isn't getting too oily, you may be able to go a few days between washes.

Hair Care for Men

Keeping your hair up is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair looking and feeling its best:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that's gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft, smooth, and tangle-free. Choose a conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and use it every time you wash your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers can cause damage to your hair over time. Limit your use of these tools and always use a heat protectant spray or serum.

Keeping men's hair up requires a combination of the right tools, products, and techniques. With the tips and tricks in this article, you'll be able to keep your hair up and out of your face all day long. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
