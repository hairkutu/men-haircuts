What Hair Colour Makes You Look Younger A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and loses its natural shine, making us look older than we actually are. But did you know that choosing the right hair colour can help you look younger? In this article, we will discuss what hair colour makes you look younger, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to maintain your youthful appearance. So, let's get started!

Description

Choosing the right hair colour can make a significant difference in how youthful you appear. As we age, our skin tone changes, and our hair naturally loses its colour and texture. By choosing the right hair colour, you can balance these changes and create a more youthful appearance.

When it comes to choosing a hair colour that makes you look younger, there are a few things to consider. First, you need to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone will determine the best hair colour for you. Second, you need to consider the texture of your hair. Coarse or frizzy hair may require a different colour than fine, straight hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour is essential to achieving a youthful appearance. The first step is to determine your skin tone. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow or gold undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones.

If you have a warm skin tone, you should opt for hair colours that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, or auburn. If you have a cool skin tone, you should choose hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, burgundy, or black.

Another factor to consider when choosing a hair colour is your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you should choose hair colours with cool undertones. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you should opt for warm undertones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Youthful Hair Colour

Now that you know how to choose the right hair colour, it's time to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a youthful hair colour:

  1. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.
  2. If you're unsure about the colour, consider trying on a wig or using a virtual hair colour tool to see how it looks.
  3. If you're dyeing your hair at home, follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test beforehand.
  4. If you're going to a salon, make sure to communicate your desired hair colour with your stylist.
  5. Maintain your hair colour by using products specifically designed for coloured hair and avoiding heat styling tools.

Tips and Tricks to Maintain Your Youthful Appearance

In addition to choosing the right hair colour, there are other tips and tricks you can use to maintain your youthful appearance:

  • Get regular haircuts to remove split ends and maintain the shape of your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Use products specifically designed for coloured hair to maintain the vibrancy of your hair colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using UV-protective products.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

What hair colour makes you look younger?

Hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, or auburn, can make you look younger if you have a warm skin tone. Hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, burgundy, or black, can make you look younger if you have a cool skin tone.

Can going lighter make you look younger?

Yes, going lighter can make you look younger as it can brighten up your face and create a more youthful appearance. However, it's essential to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Can going darker make you look younger?

Yes, going darker can make you look younger as it can create a more sophisticated and youthful appearance. However, it's essential to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Hair Care for a Younger Appearance

Maintaining a youthful appearance requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking young:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using UV-protective products.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair colour, you can maintain a youthful appearance and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, it's not just about the colour of your hair, but also how you take care of it.
