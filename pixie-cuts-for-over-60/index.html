Pixie Cuts For Over 60 A Guide To Short Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a fresh, new hairstyle, a pixie cut may be just what you need. Not only is it a chic and modern look, but it's also low-maintenance and easy to style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about pixie cuts for over 60, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Pixie Cuts
  • Tips for Maintaining Pixie Cuts
  • FAQs About Pixie Cuts for Over 60
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

How to Get a Pixie Cut

The first step in getting a pixie cut is finding the right hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience working with short hair and who understands the unique needs of mature hair. Once you've found a hairstylist you trust, the next step is to choose the right pixie cut for your face shape and hair type.

There are many different styles of pixie cuts, from classic and simple to edgy and bold. Some popular options for over 60 include the layered pixie, the choppy pixie, and the angled pixie. Talk to your hairstylist about which style would work best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Pixie Cuts

Styling a pixie cut is easy and quick, making it a great choice for busy women over 60. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips.
  3. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and crown.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQs About Pixie Cuts for Over 60

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: No! A well-styled pixie cut can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I still color my hair with a pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut can be a great canvas for experimenting with hair color.

Q: Will a pixie cut work with my hair texture?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can work with any hair texture, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Even though pixie cuts are low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your pixie cut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your strands.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair some extra TLC.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

With the right cut, styling, and hair care, a pixie cut can be a fabulous and flattering choice for women over 60. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve a chic and modern look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
