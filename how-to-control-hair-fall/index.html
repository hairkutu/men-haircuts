How To Control Hair Fall Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're experiencing hair fall, you're not alone. It's a common problem that affects both men and women. In fact, according to the American Academy of Dermatology, it's normal to lose 50 to 100 hairs a day. However, if you're losing more than that, it's important to take action. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to control hair fall.

How to Control Hair Fall

The first step in controlling hair fall is to identify the root cause. There are many reasons why you might be experiencing hair fall, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Poor diet
  • Stress
  • Medical conditions
  • Using harsh hair products

Once you've identified the cause, you can take steps to address it. Here are some things you can do:

1. Change Your Diet

Eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein, iron, and vitamin D. You can also consider taking a multivitamin or a hair supplement.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair fall, so it's important to find ways to manage it. You can try yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises. You can also consider talking to a therapist if you're feeling overwhelmed.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Using harsh hair products can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Look for products that are gentle and free from sulfates, parabens, and other harmful chemicals. You can also consider using natural hair products.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

Step-by-step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to control hair fall:

Step 1: Identify the Cause

Figure out why you're experiencing hair fall. Is it due to genetics, hormonal changes, poor diet, stress, medical conditions, or using harsh hair products?

Step 2: Address the Cause

Once you've identified the cause, take steps to address it. For example, if it's due to poor diet, start eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.

Step 3: Use Gentle Hair Products

Switch to gentle hair products that are free from harmful chemicals. Look for natural hair products that are designed to promote healthy hair growth.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use them on a low heat setting and use a heat protectant spray.

Step 5: Be Patient

It takes time for hair to grow back, so be patient. Stick to your hair care routine and you should start to see results in a few months.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow to the hair follicles.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body produces hormones that can interfere with hair growth.

Q. Can genetics cause hair fall?

A. Yes, genetics can play a role in hair fall. If your parents or grandparents experienced hair loss, you may be more likely to experience it as well.

Q. Can hair fall be reversed?

A. In some cases, hair fall can be reversed. If you address the root cause and take steps to promote healthy hair growth, you may be able to regrow lost hair.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a sample hair care routine that can help control hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Massage your scalp with a hair oil or serum.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Manage stress through exercise or meditation.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help control hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent, as it can take time to see results.
