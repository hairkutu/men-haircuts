How To Get The Smell Of Cooking Oil Out Of Your Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When cooking with oil, the smell can easily transfer to your hair, leaving it smelling greasy and unpleasant. If you're wondering how to get the smell of cooking oil out of your hair, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some easy and effective tips for removing the smell of cooking oil from your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Smell of Cooking Oil Out of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Preventing Cooking Oil Smells in Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to get rid of the cooking oil smell from your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to getting rid of the cooking oil smell is to wash your hair thoroughly. Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for oily hair, or one that has a clarifying formula. This will help to remove any residue that may be causing the smell.

Step 2: Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help to remove strong smells from your hair. Mix equal parts apple cider vinegar and water and apply it to your hair after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out with water.

Step 3: Apply Baking Soda

Baking soda is another natural remedy that can help to remove unpleasant smells from your hair. Mix a tablespoon of baking soda with water to make a paste. Apply it to your hair and massage it into your scalp. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out with water.

Step 4: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep it soft and smooth. This will also help to mask any remaining cooking oil smell.

Tips for Preventing Cooking Oil Smells in Your Hair

Here are some tips to help prevent cooking oil smells in your hair:

  • Wear a hair cap or scarf when cooking to prevent your hair from absorbing the smell
  • Use a hooded range or exhaust fan to help absorb the cooking smells before they can reach your hair
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum before cooking to help protect your hair from absorbing the cooking oil smell

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular vinegar instead of apple cider vinegar?

A: Yes, you can use regular vinegar, but apple cider vinegar is more effective at removing odors.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to prevent cooking oil smells?

A: It's best to wash your hair after cooking with oil to prevent the smell from setting in. You can also try washing your hair every other day to help prevent buildup.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and smelling great:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair

By following these tips, you can easily get rid of the cooking oil smell from your hair and keep it looking and smelling great.
