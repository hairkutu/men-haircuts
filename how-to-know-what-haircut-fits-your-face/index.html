How To Know What Haircut Fits Your Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 · 8 min read

Figuring out which haircut suits your face shape can be a daunting task. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to determine which style will enhance your natural features. In this article, we’ll provide you with an in-depth guide on how to know what haircut fits your face, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and FAQ.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you best. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangular. While some haircuts work well on multiple face shapes, it’s essential to choose a style that flatters your natural features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect haircut, you need to determine your face shape. Follow these steps to figure out which category you fall into:

Oval

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded chin and forehead, you likely have an oval face shape. This shape is considered the most versatile, as most haircuts complement it well.

Round

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape, with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with no prominent angles, you likely have a round face shape.

Square

Square face shapes have prominent angles, with a strong jawline and forehead. If your face is roughly the same width from forehead to jaw, with a square jawline, you likely have a square face shape.

Heart

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrower jawline and chin. If your face is wider at the temples and narrows toward the chin, you likely have a heart-shaped face.

Diamond

Diamond face shapes are characterized by high cheekbones, a narrow forehead and chin, and a pointed jawline. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows toward the forehead and jaw, you likely have a diamond face shape.

Triangular

A triangular face shape has a wide jawline, with a narrower forehead and cheekbones. If your face is widest at the jawline and narrows toward the forehead, you likely have a triangular face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Haircut

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to find the perfect haircut. Follow these steps:

  1. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for inspiration on social media, in magazines, or on hair salon websites.
  2. Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you have a busy schedule, choose a style that is easy to maintain.
  3. Book a consultation with a hairstylist. They can provide you with expert advice on which haircuts will suit your face shape and lifestyle.
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to visualize yourself with different haircuts.
  5. Take the plunge and get the haircut you’ve been dreaming of!

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect haircut:

  • Consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, a layered haircut can help reduce bulk. If you have thin hair, a blunt cut can create the illusion of thickness.
  • Choose a haircut that complements your natural features. If you have a prominent nose, avoid hairstyles that draw attention to it.
  • Be open to trying new things. If you’re used to long hair, consider a dramatic chop. You may be surprised at how much you love your new look!

Frequently Asked Questions

What haircut suits a round face?

For a round face shape, a long, layered haircut can create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt bobs, as they can make your face appear wider.

What haircut suits a heart-shaped face?

For a heart-shaped face, a chin-length bob or layered haircut works well. Avoid styles that add volume to the crown of your head, as they can make your forehead appear wider.

What haircut suits a square face?

A square face shape looks great with a long, layered haircut or a side-swept bang. Avoid blunt bobs or straight-across bangs, as they can emphasize your strong jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, it’s essential to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your new ‘do:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape and choose the perfect haircut, it’s time to book that salon appointment! With these tips, you’ll be sure to find a style that flatters your natural features and complements your lifestyle.
