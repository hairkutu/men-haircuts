How To Style Thick Layered Hair

Description

Thick layered hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can provide volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles that will make your thick hair look gorgeous.

How to Style Thick Layered Hair

When it comes to styling thick layered hair, it's important to use products that will provide hold and texture without weighing down your locks. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift to your roots
  • Apply a lightweight mousse or gel to damp hair to add texture and hold
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume
  • Use a round brush to smooth out your hair while blow drying, focusing on the ends to create a soft, layered look
  • Finish with a texturizing spray or hair spray to hold your style in place

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick layered hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or gel to your roots and throughout your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down until it's about 80% dry
  4. Section your hair into 2-3 parts using hair clips or elastics
  5. Starting with the bottom section, use a round brush to smooth out your hair while blow drying
  6. Repeat with the remaining sections, focusing on the ends to create a layered look
  7. Once your hair is completely dry, use a texturizing spray or hair spray to hold your style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick layered hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, updos, or half-up half-down styles
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to add volume and texture to your roots
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and round brush to make styling easier
  • Avoid using heavy products, such as oils or serums, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick layered hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, such as every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I use a curling iron or straightener on thick layered hair?

A: Yes, you can use hot tools to create curls or straighten your hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand to prevent damage.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my thick layered hair?

A: Use a smoothing serum or cream to tame frizz and flyaways. You can also try sleeping on a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and static.

Hair Care

To keep your thick layered hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause breakage and split ends
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished

With these tips and techniques, you can style your thick layered hair with confidence and ease. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or tousled waves, your hair will look beautiful and voluminous.
