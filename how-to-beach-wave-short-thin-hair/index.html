How To Beach Wave Short Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 01, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short, thin hair, you might think that beach waves are out of the question. But with the right technique and products, you can achieve effortless, tousled waves that will give your hair a much-needed boost of volume and texture. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps for creating beach waves on short, thin hair, along with some tips and tricks for maintaining your new style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Beach Wave Short Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Beach Waves
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Thin Hair

How to Beach Wave Short Thin Hair

Before we get started, it’s important to note that the key to beach waves on short, thin hair is to create volume and texture. This can be achieved through the use of products, tools, and techniques that add body and movement to your hair. Here are a few things you’ll need:

  • Sea salt spray
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray or powder

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create beach waves on short, thin hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel-dry it until it’s damp. Apply a sea salt spray throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. This will help to create texture and volume.

Step 2: Divide and Conquer

Divide your hair into small sections, starting at the back of your head. Use a curling iron or wand to create small, loose waves, making sure to curl away from your face. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 3: Work Your Way Up

Continue curling small sections of hair, working your way up to the top of your head. Make sure to vary the size and direction of your curls to create a natural, tousled look.

Step 4: Shake It Out

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently shake out the curls. This will create a more relaxed, beachy look.

Step 5: Add Texture

If you want even more texture and volume, you can use a texturizing spray or powder to create a matte, tousled finish. Spritz it throughout your hair, then use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step 6: Lock It In

Finally, use a light-hold hairspray to set your style in place. Hold the can a few inches away from your head and mist it all over your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Beach Waves

Here are a few tips and tricks for keeping your beach waves looking great:

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can break up the waves and create frizz.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in braids to create even more texture and volume.

FAQs

Can I create beach waves on short, thin hair?

Yes! With the right technique and products, you can achieve beachy waves on short, thin hair.

What products do I need to create beach waves?

You’ll need sea salt spray, a curling iron or wand, hairspray, and texturizing spray or powder.

How do I maintain my beach waves?

Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes, avoid brushing too much, and consider sleeping with your hair in braids to create more texture and volume.

Hair Care for Short Thin Hair

When you have short, thin hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and thickness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can create beautiful beach waves on your short, thin hair. Have fun experimenting and finding the perfect style for you!
