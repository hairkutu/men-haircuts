How To Tie A Full Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and trendy? If so, then you should consider tying a full man bun! This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. A full man bun is stylish, versatile, and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to tie a full man bun in relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair to how to tie the perfect bun. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Step 1: Brush Your Hair
  • Step 2: Gather Your Hair
  • Step 3: Tie Your Hair
  • Tips for Tying a Full Man Bun
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Full Man Bun

Preparation

Before you start tying your full man bun, you need to prepare your hair. This means washing and conditioning it to make sure that it's clean and healthy. You should also apply a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to help keep your hair in place. Once your hair is clean and styled, you're ready to start tying your man bun.

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

The first step in tying a full man bun is to brush your hair. This will help to remove any tangles and ensure that your hair is smooth and easy to work with. You can use a comb or brush to do this, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Next, you need to gather your hair into a ponytail. You can do this by using your hands to pull your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or elastic band. Make sure that the ponytail is positioned where you want your bun to be.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Now it's time to tie your hair into a bun. Start by twisting the ponytail around the base of the hair tie, creating a loop. Then, wrap the remaining hair around the loop until you reach the end. Finally, tuck the end of your hair under the hair tie to secure the bun in place.

Tips for Tying a Full Man Bun

  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color to make your bun look more natural.
  • Don't tie your bun too tight, as this can cause headaches and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different bun styles, such as a messy bun or a low bun, to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How long does my hair need to be to tie a full man bun?

A: Your hair should be at least 6 inches long to tie a full man bun. However, the longer your hair is, the larger your bun can be.

Q: Can I tie a man bun if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still tie a man bun if you have thin hair. Just make sure to use a hair tie or elastic band that is gentle on your hair and doesn't cause breakage.

Hair Care for a Full Man Bun

Once you've tied your full man bun, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. This means washing and conditioning it regularly, using a heat protectant when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals or treatments that can damage your hair. You can also apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Now that you know how to tie a full man bun, you can try out this trendy hairstyle and rock your new look with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.
