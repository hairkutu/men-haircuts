How To Hide Bald Crown Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Baldness is a common problem faced by both men and women. While some people embrace their baldness, others may feel self-conscious about it. If you have a bald crown and want to hide it, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to hide your bald crown.

Description

A bald crown is a circular patch of baldness on the top of the head. It’s a common type of hair loss that can affect both men and women. Baldness can be caused by various factors such as genetics, age, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions. If you’re experiencing baldness, it’s important to consult a doctor to determine the underlying cause.

How to Hide Bald Crown

Here are some tips and tricks on how to hide your bald crown:

1. Hairstyles

The easiest way to hide your bald crown is by choosing the right hairstyle. Opt for hairstyles that cover the top of your head such as a slicked-back hairstyle, a side-parted hairstyle, or a comb-over hairstyle. You can also try a buzz cut or a shaved head if you don’t mind going bald.

2. Hairpieces

If you want a quick fix for your bald crown, you can try using hairpieces such as wigs, toupees, or hair fibers. These products can cover your bald spot and give you a fuller head of hair. Make sure to choose a hairpiece that matches your hair color and texture for a natural look.

3. Scalp Micropigmentation

Scalp micropigmentation is a non-surgical procedure that involves tattooing tiny dots on the scalp to create the illusion of hair follicles. This procedure can camouflage your bald crown and give you a more youthful appearance. However, it’s important to choose a reputable clinic and a skilled technician for the best results.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to hide your bald crown:

Step 1: Choose the right hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that covers your bald spot and suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Use hairpieces

If you want a quick fix, try using hairpieces such as wigs, toupees, or hair fibers.

Step 3: Consider scalp micropigmentation

If you want a more permanent solution, consider scalp micropigmentation. Choose a reputable clinic and a skilled technician for the best results.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to hide your bald crown:

1. Use hair volumizing products

Use volumizing products such as mousse or hairspray to add volume to your hair and create the illusion of fuller hair.

2. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails or braids as they can pull on your hair and worsen your baldness.

3. Protect your scalp from the sun

Wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun’s harmful UV rays.

FAQ

Q: Can baldness be reversed?

A: It depends on the underlying cause of baldness. Some types of hair loss such as male pattern baldness are genetic and cannot be reversed. However, other types of hair loss such as alopecia areata or telogen effluvium can be treated with medications or lifestyle changes.

Q: Is scalp micropigmentation painful?

A: The procedure is generally painless as a numbing cream is applied to the scalp before the procedure. However, some people may experience mild discomfort during the procedure.

Q: How long does a hairpiece last?

A: It depends on the type of hairpiece and how well you take care of it. Wigs and toupees can last up to a year or more with proper care, while hair fibers may need to be reapplied every few days.

Hair Care

Aside from hiding your bald crown, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips on how to maintain healthy hair:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and biotin to promote hair growth.

2. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes or relaxers that can damage your hair and scalp.

3. Massage your scalp

Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow to your hair follicles and promote hair growth.

In conclusion, baldness can be a challenging issue to deal with, but there are various ways to hide your bald crown and maintain healthy hair. Whether you choose to try a new hairstyle, use a hairpiece, or opt for scalp micropigmentation, it’s important to choose a solution that suits your needs and preferences. Remember to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth.
