Can You Get A Haircut In Witcher 3

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing game that features a vast open-world environment. Players can explore the world, complete quests, and interact with various characters. One of the most common questions asked by players is, "Can you get a haircut in Witcher 3?" The short answer is yes, you can. In this blog post, we will discuss how to get a haircut in Witcher 3, step by step, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a game that allows players to customize their character, Geralt of Rivia. One way to customize Geralt is by changing his hairstyle. This feature was not available in the previous Witcher games, but it was added to Witcher 3. Players can visit various barbershops throughout the game and choose from multiple hairstyles.

How to Get a Haircut in Witcher 3

Getting a haircut in Witcher 3 is a straightforward process. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Find a Barber

The first step is to find a barber. There are several barbershops located throughout the game world. You can look for a barber icon on the map or ask the locals for directions.

Step 2: Go to the Barber

Once you have found a barber, walk up to them and interact with them. This will bring up a menu of available hairstyles.

Step 3: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose the hairstyle you want from the menu. You can preview the hairstyle on Geralt before making a final decision.

Step 4: Pay the Barber

Once you have chosen a hairstyle, the barber will cut Geralt's hair. Afterward, you will need to pay the barber for their services.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting a great haircut in Witcher 3:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits Geralt's overall look and personality.
  • Consider the weather conditions in the game world when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Some barbershops offer unique hairstyles that are not available elsewhere.

FAQs

Can you change your hairstyle at any time?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle at any time by visiting a barber.

Can you dye your hair?

No, you cannot dye your hair in Witcher 3.

Are there any benefits to getting a haircut?

There are no gameplay benefits to getting a haircut, but it can help to enhance Geralt's overall appearance.

Hair Care

In addition to getting a haircut, players can also maintain Geralt's hair by using hair care products. These products can be purchased from various merchants throughout the game world. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use hair care products regularly to keep Geralt's hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Consider using a hair tonic to promote hair growth.
  • Protect Geralt's hair from the sun and weather by wearing a hat or hood.

Conclusion

Getting a haircut in Witcher 3 is a fun way to customize Geralt's appearance. With this guide, you now know how to get a haircut, some tips, FAQs, and hair care to keep Geralt's hair looking its best. So, go out there, find a barber, and get a new hairstyle!
