Spiky Hair Style For Man A Guide To Rocking The Look

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to spice up your hairstyle, then a spiky hairdo might be the perfect choice for you. Spiky hair is a popular style for men that can give you a cool, edgy look. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about spiky hair style for man, including how to create the look, tips for maintaining your spiky hair, and answers to some common questions.

Description

Spiky hair is a hairstyle that involves creating short, sharp spikes on top of the head, while keeping the sides and back of the hair short or shaved. The spikes can be achieved using hair gel, pomade, wax or other hair styling products. Spiky hair style for man is a versatile look that can be tailored to suit different hair types and lengths.

How to Create a Spiky Hair Style for Man

Creating a spiky hair style for man is relatively easy and can be done in just a few steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not completely dry.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel or Wax

Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your hair. Rub it between your palms and then run your fingers through your hair to evenly distribute the product.

Step 3: Create the Spikes

Using your fingers or a comb, create spikes by pulling small sections of hair upwards and away from your head. You can make the spikes as short or long as you like. Be sure to keep the spikes uniform in size and shape.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your spiky hair in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold the spikes in place and prevent them from falling flat.

Tips for Maintaining Your Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your spiky hair style for man:

- Use a good quality hair gel or wax that will hold your spikes in place without making your hair feel greasy or heavy. - Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause the spikes to fall flat. - When sleeping, try not to lay on your hair to keep the spikes intact. - Regularly wash your hair to prevent product buildup, which can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style.

FAQ

How long does it take to create a spiky hair style for man?

Creating a spiky hairdo can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes, depending on the length and thickness of your hair and how many spikes you want to create.

Can spiky hair style for man work for all hair types?

Yes, spiky hair can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the technique for creating spikes may differ depending on your hair type.

How often should you wash your hair when you have a spiky hair style?

It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential, especially when you're styling it regularly. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style. - Get regular haircuts to remove split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and neat. - Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to rock a spiky hair style for man that looks great and is easy to maintain. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, spiky hair can add a touch of style and personality to your overall appearance.
