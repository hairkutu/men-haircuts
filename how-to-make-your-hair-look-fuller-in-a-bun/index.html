How To Make Your Hair Look Fuller In A Bun

Written by Robby Jul 07, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin or fine hair, you might feel like your buns always look flat and lack volume. Fortunately, there are some easy tricks you can use to make your hair look fuller in a bun. In this article, we'll share some tips and step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a voluminous bun that will make your hair look thicker and fuller.

How to Make Your Hair Look Fuller in a Bun

Before we dive into the specifics, let's talk about what makes hair look fuller. Essentially, the key is to add volume and texture to your hair. This can be achieved by using the right products, styling techniques, and hair accessories. Here are some general tips for making your hair look fuller in a bun:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots before blow-drying
  • Tease your hair at the crown to create volume
  • Use a hair donut or sock bun to create a fuller bun shape
  • Add texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let's walk through the steps of creating a fuller bun:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your roots, focusing on the crown of your head.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Tease the hair at the crown of your head by backcombing small sections of hair. This will create a "lifted" effect and add volume to the roots.
  5. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  6. Insert a hair donut or sock bun into the ponytail. Make sure the donut is hidden under the hair.
  7. Spread your hair evenly around the donut, covering it completely.
  8. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure it feels tight and secure.
  9. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to the bun.
  10. Finish with a light hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Fuller in a Bun

Here are a few additional tips for achieving a fuller-looking bun:

  • Use a hair donut or sock bun that is close to your hair color. This will help the bun blend in better and look more natural.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair. This can help create volume and make your bun look fuller.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep the bun in place.
  • Experiment with different bun styles, such as a messy bun or a braided bun, to see what works best for your hair type and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about making your hair look fuller in a bun:

Can I use a regular hair tie instead of a hair donut?

Yes, you can use a regular hair tie to create a bun. However, using a hair donut or sock bun can help create a fuller, more voluminous shape.

What if my hair is too short for a bun?

If your hair is too short to create a full bun, you can try using a half-up, half-down style with a hair accessory, such as a barrette or hair clip, to add volume and texture to the top section of your hair.

How often should I wash my hair to keep it looking full?

Everyone's hair is different, but in general, washing your hair every other day can help prevent oil buildup and keep your hair looking fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker, Fuller Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips that can help promote thicker, fuller hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products and other hair care products.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can create a fuller-looking bun that will make your hair look thick and voluminous.
