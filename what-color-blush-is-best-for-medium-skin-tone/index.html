What Color Blush Is Best For Medium Skin Tone

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a medium skin tone, you may find it challenging to choose the right color blush for your complexion. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to determine which shade will complement your skin tone the best. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect color blush for medium skin tone.

Description

Medium skin tone refers to a skin color that falls between fair and dark complexions. It is usually described as olive or tan with warm undertones. Choosing the right blush color for medium skin tone can be difficult as you want to avoid looking too washed out or too overdone.

How to Choose the Right Blush Color for Medium Skin Tone

When it comes to choosing the perfect blush color for medium skin tone, you want to keep in mind the undertones of your skin. Warm undertones usually look best with peachy or coral shades, while cool undertones look great with rosy or berry shades.

Step by Step

1. Determine your undertones. Look at the veins on your wrist, if they appear greenish, you have warm undertones. If they appear bluish, you have cool undertones.

2. Choose the right shade. For warm undertones, choose peach or coral shades. For cool undertones, choose rosy or berry shades.

3. Test the blush. Always test the blush on your skin before purchasing. Apply a small amount on your cheek and blend it in. The right shade will look natural and complement your skin tone.

Tips

- If you have a neutral undertone, you can wear both warm and cool shades.

- If you have a deeper medium skin tone, you can wear darker shades of blush such as brick or terracotta.

- If you have a lighter medium skin tone, you can wear lighter shades of blush such as peach or pink.

FAQ

What if I have dark circles under my eyes?

If you have dark circles under your eyes, you may want to avoid blush shades with too much pink or red as it can emphasize the darkness. Instead, opt for peach or coral shades that will brighten up your complexion.

Can I wear bronzer with blush?

Yes, you can wear bronzer with blush. Apply the bronzer first to the areas where the sun hits your face, such as your forehead, nose, and cheekbones. Then, apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Skin Tone

When it comes to hair colors, medium skin tone looks great with warm tones such as golden blonde, caramel, or copper. You can also experiment with deeper shades such as chocolate brown or mahogany.

It's essential to keep your hair healthy by using a good shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect blush color for your medium skin tone can be challenging, but by keeping your undertones in mind, you can find a shade that complements your complexion. Remember to test the blush before purchasing and experiment with different shades to find the perfect one for you.
