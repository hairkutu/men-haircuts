How Do I Know What Hair Color Suits Me Best

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're looking for a change. With so many colors to choose from, it can be challenging to determine what will suit you best. However, with the right guidance, you can easily find a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personality.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The first thing you need to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. Determine whether you have warm, cool, or neutral undertones. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warmer hair colors like brown or blonde. If you have cool undertones, you should choose cooler hair colors like black or red. If you have neutral undertones, you can wear almost any hair color.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop, while others can clash with your eye color. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors like blonde or copper can make your eyes stand out. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colors like black or ash blonde can bring out the richness in your eye color.

How to

Here are some steps to help you determine what hair color suits you best:

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If it's hard to tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider your eye color

Think about what colors make your eyes stand out. If you have blue eyes, warmer hair colors like blonde or copper can make them pop. If you have brown eyes, cooler hair colors like black or ash blonde can bring out the richness in your eye color.

Step 3: Determine your personality

Your hair color should reflect your personality. If you're a more outgoing person, you may want to opt for a bolder hair color like red or purple. If you're more reserved, a natural hair color like brown or blonde may be a better fit.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the right hair color:

Step 1: Research

Look online for inspiration and ideas. Create a Pinterest board or save pictures of hair colors you like.

Step 2: Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you determine what hair color will suit you best. They can also recommend a shade that complements your skin tone and eye color.

Step 3: Test it Out

If you're unsure about a hair color, try a temporary hair dye or a wig to see how it looks on you before committing to a permanent color.

Tips

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair color is a fun way to change up your look.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and fade your hair color.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist before doing so, as they can recommend a shade that will suit you best.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the color and how quickly your hair grows. On average, hair color should be touched up every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To maintain your hair color, it's important to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will prevent your hair color from fading and keep it looking vibrant. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and fade your hair color. If you're going to be in the sun for an extended period of time, wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent your hair color from fading.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and steps, you can find a hair color that will make you look and feel your best.
