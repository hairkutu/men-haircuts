How To Measure To Find Your Face Shape

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you ever wondered what your face shape is? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and makeup looks. In this article, we will discuss how to measure to find your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Gather Your Materials
  • Step 2: Measure Your Forehead
  • Step 3: Measure Your Cheekbones
  • Step 4: Measure Your Jawline
  • Step 5: Measure Your Face Length
  • Tips for Measuring Your Face Shape
  • FAQs About Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

The first step in measuring your face shape is to gather your materials. You will need a tape measure and a mirror.

Step 2: Measure Your Forehead

The next step is to measure your forehead. Start at the widest part of your forehead (usually the hairline) and measure across to the other side. Write down this measurement.

Step 3: Measure Your Cheekbones

Now, measure your cheekbones. Start at the point where your cheekbones are the widest (usually below the outer corner of your eyes) and measure across to the other side. Write down this measurement.

Step 4: Measure Your Jawline

Measure your jawline next. Start at the tip of your chin and measure across to the point below your ear where your jawbone starts to angle upwards. Double this measurement to get the full length of your jawline. Write down this measurement.

Step 5: Measure Your Face Length

Finally, measure the length of your face. Start at the center of your hairline and measure down to the tip of your chin. Write down this measurement.

Tips for Measuring Your Face Shape

  • Make sure your hair is pulled back and out of the way so that you can get accurate measurements.
  • Use a soft measuring tape for the most accurate results.
  • Take each measurement twice to ensure accuracy.

FAQs About Face Shape

Q: What are the different face shapes?

A: The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Q: Why is it important to know my face shape?

A: Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and makeup looks.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. If you have a round face shape, try a hairstyle with height at the crown to elongate your face. If you have a square face shape, try a soft, layered hairstyle to soften your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, try a chin-length bob to balance out your features. If you have a diamond face shape, try a hairstyle with volume at the top and tapered at the bottom to balance out your features.

Now that you know how to measure to find your face shape, you can choose the most flattering hairstyles and makeup looks for your unique features. Happy measuring!
