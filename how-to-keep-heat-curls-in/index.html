How To Keep Heat Curls In A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have naturally straight hair, heat curls can be a great way to add some volume and texture to your locks. However, keeping those curls in place can be a challenge. In this post, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to keep heat curls in, so you can enjoy your bouncy, curly hair all day long.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Heat Curls In
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Heat-Curled Hair

How to Keep Heat Curls In

Before we dive into the specifics of how to keep heat curls in, let's talk briefly about what heat curls are and how to create them. Heat curls are created using a hot tool like a curling iron or wand. To create heat curls, you'll need to:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Apply a heat protectant spray or serum
  • Section your hair and curl small sections at a time, holding each curl for 10-15 seconds
  • After curling, let your hair cool completely before touching it

Now that you know how to create heat curls, let's talk about how to keep them in.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to keep your heat curls in place:

  1. Apply a lightweight hairspray before curling your hair to help hold the curls in place
  2. After curling each section of hair, clip the curl up and let it cool completely before releasing it
  3. Once all of your hair is curled and cooled, gently comb through it with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb
  4. Apply a medium-hold hairspray to help set the curls and prevent them from falling out throughout the day
  5. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause the curls to fall out

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your heat curls in:

  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand to create tighter curls that will last longer
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots before curling to add extra lift and hold
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve the curls overnight
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your curls to help prevent frizz and keep them looking shiny

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping heat curls in:

  • How do I prevent my heat curls from falling out?
    To prevent your heat curls from falling out, use a lightweight hairspray before curling your hair, let each curl cool completely before releasing it, and avoid touching your hair throughout the day.
  • How long will my heat curls last?
    The length of time your heat curls will last depends on a variety of factors, including your hair type, the size of your curls, and the products you use. Generally, you can expect your heat curls to last anywhere from a few hours to a full day.
  • Can I use heat protectant spray after curling my hair?
    While it's best to apply heat protectant spray before curling your hair, you can still apply it after if you forget. Just be sure to let your hair cool completely before applying the spray.

Hair Care for Heat-Curled Hair

Keeping your heat curls in place is just one part of the equation. To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your heat-curled hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your heat curls in place and enjoy bouncy, curly hair all day long. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
