Best Hairstyle For Round Face Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman over 60 with a round face and struggling to find the best hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we'll explore the best hairstyles for round faces over 60 and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair texture and face shape changes. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that compliments your features and makes you feel confident. If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face as it will make your face appear wider. The ideal hairstyle for a round face is one that elongates your face shape and creates the illusion of a slimmer face.

How To

The best hairstyle for a round face over 60 is a layered bob. A layered bob is a versatile haircut that can be styled in various ways, making it an ideal hairstyle for any occasion. The layers add volume to the crown of your head, elongating your face shape and creating the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before deciding on a layered bob haircut, consult with your hairstylist. They can assess your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your layered bob should be chin-length or longer. Avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline as it will make your face appear wider.

Step 3: Add Layers

The layers in your bob should start at your cheekbones and continue down to the ends of your hair. The layers add volume to the crown of your head, elongating your face shape.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

There are various ways to style a layered bob. You can add curls or waves to the ends of your hair, or you can straighten your hair for a sleek look. Whatever style you choose, make sure it flatters your features and makes you feel confident.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face over 60:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that elongates your face shape.
  • Consult with your hairstylist before choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a length that is chin-length or longer.
  • Add layers to your hairstyle to create volume at the crown of your head.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face over 60?

The best hairstyle for a round face over 60 is a layered bob. The layers add volume to the crown of your head, elongating your face shape and creating the illusion of a slimmer face.

What length should I choose for my layered bob?

The length of your layered bob should be chin-length or longer. Avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline as it will make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair becomes more fragile, and it's essential to take care of our hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your layered bob.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful at any age.
