How To Get Rid Of Brassy Tones In Blonde Hair At Home

Written by Christine Apr 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having brassy tones in your blonde hair? Don't worry, you don't have to go to the salon to fix it. You can easily get rid of those brassy tones at home with a few simple steps. In this article, we'll show you how to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair at Home
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When blonde hair starts to turn brassy, it can be frustrating. Brassy tones are often caused by exposure to the sun, chlorine, or hard water. These elements can cause blonde hair to turn yellow or orange, making it look unnatural. However, you can easily fix this problem at home. With the right products and techniques, you can get rid of those brassy tones and restore your blonde hair to its natural beauty.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair at Home

The first step to getting rid of brassy tones in blonde hair is to use a purple shampoo. Purple shampoo contains violet pigments that neutralize yellow and orange tones in blonde hair. Use purple shampoo once a week to keep your hair looking bright and beautiful.

Another way to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair is to use a toner. Toner is a color-depositing product that helps to neutralize unwanted tones in blonde hair. To use a toner, mix it with a developer and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out.

You can also use a hair mask to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair. Hair masks are deep conditioning treatments that help to restore moisture and shine to your hair. Look for a hair mask that contains purple pigments to help neutralize brassy tones.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair with purple shampoo

Start by washing your hair with purple shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your hair and scalp, then rinse it out. Use purple shampoo once a week to keep your hair looking bright and beautiful.

Step 2: Mix toner with developer

If your hair is still brassy after using purple shampoo, you can use a toner to neutralize the unwanted tones. Mix the toner with a developer according to the instructions on the package.

Step 3: Apply toner to your hair

Apply the toner to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the toner on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out.

Step 4: Use a hair mask

If your hair is still brassy after using purple shampoo and toner, you can use a hair mask to restore moisture and shine to your hair. Look for a hair mask that contains purple pigments to help neutralize brassy tones. Apply the hair mask to your hair, leave it on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing it.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and make brassy tones more noticeable.
  • Wear a hat or use a hair protectant spray when you're outside to protect your hair from the sun.
  • If you have hard water, consider installing a water softener to prevent brassy tones in your blonde hair.

FAQ

What causes brassy tones in blonde hair?

Brassy tones in blonde hair are often caused by exposure to the sun, chlorine, or hard water. These elements can cause blonde hair to turn yellow or orange, making it look unnatural.

Can I get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair at home?

Yes, you can get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair at home. Use a purple shampoo, toner, or hair mask to neutralize unwanted tones and restore your hair to its natural beauty.

How often should I use purple shampoo?

You should use purple shampoo once a week to keep your hair looking bright and beautiful.

Hair Care

To keep your blonde hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools on your hair, and protect your hair from the sun. With the right hair care routine, you can keep your blonde hair looking beautiful and brassy-free.

Now that you know how to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair at home, you can keep your hair looking its best. Use purple shampoo, toner, and hair masks to neutralize unwanted tones and restore your hair to its natural beauty. With the right products and techniques, you can have beautiful, brassy-free blonde hair.
