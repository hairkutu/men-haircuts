How To Look Good After 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi May 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you in your 60s and wondering how to age gracefully? Do you want to look your best, even as you enter your golden years? In this article, we will share some tips on how to look good after 60. From skincare to fashion, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Skincare
  • Fashion
  • Makeup
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Skincare

As you age, your skin loses its elasticity and becomes more prone to wrinkles and age spots. However, with the right skincare routine, you can keep your skin looking youthful and radiant. Here are some tips:

1. Use a gentle cleanser: Avoid harsh soaps that can dry out your skin. Instead, use a gentle cleanser that will remove dirt and makeup without stripping your skin of its natural oils.

2. Moisturize: Moisturizing is key to keeping your skin hydrated and smooth. Look for a moisturizer that contains hyaluronic acid, which can help plump up your skin and reduce the appearance of fine lines.

3. Wear sunscreen: Sun damage is one of the main causes of premature aging. Protect your skin by wearing sunscreen with at least SPF 30 every day, even if it’s cloudy outside.

4. Drink plenty of water: Staying hydrated is important for your overall health and can also help keep your skin looking young and fresh.

Fashion

Just because you’re in your 60s doesn’t mean you can’t dress stylishly. Here are some fashion tips to keep in mind:

1. Invest in quality pieces: Instead of buying cheap clothes that will fall apart after a few washes, invest in high-quality pieces that will last for years.

2. Embrace your personal style: Don’t feel like you have to follow the latest trends. Wear what makes you feel comfortable and confident.

3. Choose flattering cuts: As you age, your body shape may change. Choose clothes that flatter your current figure and don’t be afraid to try different styles.

Makeup

Makeup can help enhance your natural beauty and make you feel more confident. Here are some makeup tips for women over 60:

1. Use a primer: A primer can help smooth out your skin and make your makeup last longer.

2. Choose a light foundation: Heavy foundation can settle into fine lines and wrinkles, making them more noticeable. Instead, opt for a light, sheer foundation that will even out your skin tone without looking cakey.

3. Define your brows: As you age, your brows may become thinner. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in any sparse areas and create a more defined shape.

Hair Care

Your hair is one of your most noticeable features, so it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for women over 60:

1. Keep it hydrated: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.

2. Add volume: As we age, our hair can become thinner. To add volume and thickness, try using a volumizing shampoo and blow-drying your hair upside down.

3. Embrace your natural color: Instead of constantly dyeing your hair, embrace your natural color. Gray hair can be beautiful and elegant.

FAQ

1. How can I reduce the appearance of age spots?

You can use a skin-lightening cream or serum that contains ingredients like vitamin C or hydroquinone. However, it’s important to talk to your dermatologist before using any new skincare products.

2. What’s the best way to apply makeup on mature skin?

Use a light touch and blend well. Avoid heavy or cakey products that can settle into fine lines and wrinkles.

3. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

We hope these tips have been helpful in showing you how to look good after 60. Remember, age is just a number – you can look and feel your best at any age!
