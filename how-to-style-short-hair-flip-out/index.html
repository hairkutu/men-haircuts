How To Style Short Hair Flip Out A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 19, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it in different ways. One of the most popular short hair styles is the flip out, a look that's both chic and timeless. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to style short hair flip out with ease.

How to Style Short Hair Flip Out

Before we begin, it's essential to have the right tools at hand. You'll need a hairbrush, a hairdryer, a curling iron, and hair spray. Here's how to style short hair flip out:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or blow-dry it. It's essential to start with clean hair to get the best results.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant product to your hair to prevent damage from the heat styling tools. This step is crucial to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's smooth and ready for styling.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl your hair away from your face. Take small sections of hair and curl them up towards the crown of your head. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 5: Flip Out Your Hair

Using your fingers or a hairbrush, flip out the ends of your hair. This will give your hair the signature flip-out look. You can also use a hairdryer and a round brush to flip out your hair. Place the brush under the hair at the ends and blow-dry outwards.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Spray

Once you've flipped out your hair, spray it with a light hold hair spray to keep the style in place. Avoid using too much hair spray as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Styling Short Hair Flip Out

Here are some tips to help you style short hair flip out:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight curls.
  • Use a hairdryer and a round brush to flip out your hair for a more natural look.
  • Don't use too much hair spray to avoid a stiff look.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the right one for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair flip out:

Can I style short hair flip out without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer and a round brush to flip out your hair for a more natural look. Place the brush under the hair at the ends and blow-dry outwards.

What kind of hair spray should I use to style short hair flip out?

Use a light hold hair spray to keep the style in place without making it look stiff or unnatural.

How often should I wash my hair if I style it with short hair flip out?

It depends on your hair type and how often you use styling products. Typically, it's recommended to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Short Hair Flip Out

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's essential to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair flip out:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to style short hair flip out, you can rock this timeless look with ease. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
