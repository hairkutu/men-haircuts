Is Dyed Blonde Hair Unprofessional

Written by Daniel Apr 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Blonde hair is a popular hair color choice for many people. It is a versatile color that can suit different skin tones, and it can give you a fresh and youthful look. However, some people wonder if dyed blonde hair is professional, especially in the workplace. In this article, we will explore the topic of whether dyed blonde hair is unprofessional and give you some tips on how to make it work in a professional setting.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Dyed blonde hair is not necessarily unprofessional, but it can depend on the workplace and the industry. In some conservative industries, such as law and finance, natural hair colors are preferred. However, in creative industries, such as fashion and advertising, dyed hair can be a way to express your individuality and creativity.

It is also important to consider the shade of blonde you choose. If you choose a very light or platinum blonde, it can come across as more playful and youthful, which may not be appropriate in a conservative workplace. A darker, more natural-looking blonde may be more suitable for a professional setting.

How to

If you want to dye your hair blonde and maintain a professional appearance, there are a few things you can do:

  • Choose a darker, more natural-looking shade of blonde.
  • Keep your hair well-groomed and avoid overly-styled looks.
  • Consider your makeup and clothing choices to ensure they complement your hair color.
  • Ask your employer or HR department about any hair color policies or guidelines.

Step by step

If you have decided to dye your hair blonde, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Research different shades of blonde and choose one that complements your skin tone.
  2. Decide whether you want to dye your hair at home or go to a salon.
  3. If dyeing your hair at home, follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test to avoid any allergic reactions.
  4. If going to a salon, choose a reputable one and bring a photo of the shade you want.
  5. After dyeing your hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color and protect your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining dyed blonde hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and fade the color.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

1. Is dyed blonde hair unprofessional for men?

Like for women, dyed blonde hair for men can also depend on the workplace and industry. However, in general, natural hair colors are more accepted for men in professional settings.

2. Can I dye my hair blonde if I have dark hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde if you have dark hair, but it may require bleaching first. It is best to consult a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

3. How often do I need to touch up my dyed blonde hair?

This can depend on the individual and the color of the dye, but generally, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

Hair Care

Maintaining dyed blonde hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair and prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the color and dry out your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

In conclusion, dyed blonde hair can be professional as long as you choose the right shade and maintain a well-groomed appearance. It is always best to consult with your employer or HR department to ensure you are following any hair color policies or guidelines. With proper care and attention, you can rock your blonde hair in any setting.
