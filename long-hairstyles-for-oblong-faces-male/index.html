Long Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Male A Complete Guide

Are you a male with an oblong face shape and looking for a long hairstyle that suits you? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss some of the best long hairstyles for oblong faces male, how to style them, tips, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. The goal of choosing a long hairstyle for an oblong face male is to add width and volume to the sides of the face to balance out the length. Long hairstyles that work best for oblong face males are those that have volume or texture on the sides and top.

How to

The first step in choosing a long hairstyle for an oblong face male is to consider the hair type. Long hairstyles work best for men with thick hair, but can also work for those with fine hair. The next step is to choose a hairstyle that adds volume or texture to the sides and top of the head. This can be achieved through layers, curls, waves, or a messy top. Lastly, consider the maintenance of the hairstyle and if it fits your lifestyle.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some of the best long hairstyles for oblong faces male:

Layered Cut

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides and top of the head.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Curly Hair

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining cream to towel-dried hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, scrunching the curls as you go.
  4. Apply a curl-enhancing spray to set the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling a long hairstyle for oblong faces male:

  • Choose a hairstyle with volume or texture on the sides and top of the head to balance out the length of the face.
  • Consider the maintenance of the hairstyle and if it fits your lifestyle.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or curl-defining cream to add volume to the hair.
  • Blow-dry the hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides and top of the head.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz.
  • Set the style in place with a hairspray or curl-enhancing spray.

FAQ

What are some long hairstyles for oblong faces male?

Some long hairstyles that work best for oblong face males are layered cuts, messy tops, curls, and waves.

What hair type works best for long hairstyles for oblong faces male?

Long hairstyles work best for men with thick hair, but can also work for those with fine hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long hairstyle for oblong faces male looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or curl-defining cream to add volume to the hair.
  • Blow-dry the hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides and top of the head.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways or frizz.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

There you have it, our complete guide on long hairstyles for oblong faces male. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume or texture to the sides and top of the head to balance out the length of the face. Follow our tips and hair care guide to keep your hairstyle looking its best. Happy styling!
