How To Cut A Pixie Bob At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and trendy, a pixie bob might be just what you need. This short hairstyle has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among women of all ages. But instead of spending money at the salon, why not try cutting your own pixie bob at home? With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut in the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut a Pixie Bob at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, there are a few things you'll need to gather:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clippers with guards
  • A comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • A mirror

Once you have your tools ready, it's time to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own pixie bob at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use sectioning clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the Back

Start with the back of your hair, using your clippers with a guard to trim the hair to your desired length. Use a higher guard for longer hair and a lower guard for shorter hair.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Next, use your clippers to trim the sides of your hair. Use a shorter guard than you did for the back, since the sides should be shorter than the back.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Use your scissors to cut the top of your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to your desired length. Work in small sections, making sure to cut evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Edges

Use your scissors to blend the edges of your hair, making sure there are no harsh lines between the different sections. This will give your pixie bob a more natural look.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Use your mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair is even and looks the way you want it to.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your favorite products. A pixie bob looks great with a little bit of texture or volume.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Bob

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your own pixie bob:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure your hair is even.
  • Use your comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Cutting the back of your hair can be tricky, so it's okay to ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQs

Can I cut my own pixie bob if I've never cut my hair before?

While it's possible to cut your own hair if you've never done it before, it can be tricky. It's a good idea to watch some tutorials and practice on a mannequin head before attempting to cut your own hair.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or try to blend the mistake into the rest of your hair. If all else fails, you can always go to a salon and have a professional fix it for you.

Do I need to use clippers to cut a pixie bob?

No, you can use scissors to cut your hair if you prefer. However, clippers can make the process quicker and easier, especially for the back and sides of your hair.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Bob

Now that you've cut your own pixie bob, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Visit a salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your pixie bob looking its best.

Cutting your own pixie bob at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a stylish and trendy haircut without leaving your house.
