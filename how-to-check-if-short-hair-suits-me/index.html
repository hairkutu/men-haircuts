How To Check If Short Hair Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 24, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you thinking about cutting your hair short but unsure if it will suit you? Short hair can be a bold and liberating move, but it's understandable to feel hesitant about such a drastic change. In this article, we'll guide you on how to check if short hair suits you. We'll cover everything from face shape to hair texture, so you can make an informed decision about whether or not to take the plunge.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Texture
  • Lifestyle
  • Personality
  • Hair Salons

Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, and the same goes for short hair. Here's a breakdown of which short hairstyles suit which face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck - pretty much any short hairstyle will suit you. Consider a pixie cut or a bob with a side parting.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid short hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for a bob with long layers or a short, choppy pixie cut.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, consider a short bob with long layers or a side-swept fringe. Avoid blunt cuts that accentuate your jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, a short bob with choppy layers or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs will suit you well.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, a textured pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs will flatter your features.

Remember, these are just guidelines - don't be afraid to experiment with different short hairstyles to find one that suits you best.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you. Here's a breakdown of which short hairstyles suit which hair textures:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is versatile and can suit a variety of short hairstyles, including bobs, pixie cuts, and blunt cuts.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can look great in short hairstyles with layers, such as a textured bob or a shaggy pixie cut.

Curly Hair

Curly hair can be more difficult to manage in a short hairstyle, but a layered bob or a curly pixie cut can look great if styled properly.

Remember, your hair texture will affect how your short hairstyle looks, so it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you. Short hair can be low-maintenance and practical, but it may not be suitable for everyone. Consider the following:

  • Do you have a job that requires a certain hairstyle?
  • Do you have time to style your hair every day?
  • Do you participate in sports or other activities that require your hair to be tied back?

If you lead an active lifestyle or don't have much time to spend on your hair, a short hairstyle may be a good option for you.

Personality

Your personality is another factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you. Short hair can be a bold and confident statement, so it's important to choose a style that reflects your personality. If you're outgoing and adventurous, a pixie cut may be a good option for you. If you're more reserved, a bob with side-swept bangs may be a better fit.

Hair Salons

If you're still unsure if short hair suits you, consider visiting a hair salon for a consultation. A professional stylist can offer advice on which short hairstyles will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They can also offer suggestions on how to style your hair and which products to use.

Hairstyles

If you've decided to go for a short hairstyle, here are some popular options to consider:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Lob (long bob)
  • Shaggy cut
  • Crop

Remember, these are just a few options - there are countless short hairstyles to choose from.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when deciding if short hair suits you:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you have to spend on your hair.
  • Visit a hair salon for a consultation if you're unsure.
  • Choose a short hairstyle that reflects your personality.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to check if short hair suits you:

Will short hair suit me if I have a round face?

Yes, short hair can suit a round face. Consider a bob with long layers or a short, choppy pixie cut.

Can I still tie my hair back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still tie your hair back if you have short hair. Consider using hair accessories such as clips or headbands to keep your hair in place.

Will short hair be easier to manage than long hair?

Short hair can be easier to manage than long hair, but it depends on the style you choose. Some short hairstyles require more maintenance than others.

Hair Care

Once you've decided on a short hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair strong and healthy.
  • Visit a hair salon regularly for trims and touch-ups.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to rock your new short hairstyle with confidence!
