How To Style Shaved Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Shaved hair can be a bold and daring look that can transform your appearance. However, some people may find it challenging to style shaved hair. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to style shaved hair, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  1. How to Style Shaved Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Shaved Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Shaved Hair

How to Style Shaved Hair

Styling shaved hair can be a fun and creative process. Here are some styling options:

1. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a popular and easy style for shaved hair. It requires little maintenance and can be done at home with clippers. You can choose to leave the hair a bit longer on top and gradually taper it down to the sides and back.

2. Mohawk

A mohawk is a bold and edgy style that can be achieved by shaving the sides and leaving a strip of hair on top. You can choose to spike the hair or leave it flat.

3. Undercut

An undercut is a stylish and versatile option for shaved hair. It involves shaving the sides and back while leaving the top long. The top can be styled in various ways, such as a slick back or messy look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style shaved hair:

1. Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and easy to style.

2. Towel dry your hair

Use a towel to dry your hair gently. Avoid rubbing your hair as this can cause damage.

3. Apply styling product

Apply a styling product that is suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

4. Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair. You can experiment with different styles and techniques to achieve the look you want.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your style with a light mist of hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Styling Shaved Hair

Here are some tips for styling shaved hair:

1. Choose the right product

Choose a styling product that is suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you have thick hair, you may need a stronger hold product.

2. Don't overdo it

Use a small amount of product and don't overdo it. Too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. Shaved hair can be versatile and allows you to try out different looks.

FAQs

1. Is shaving your hair damaging?

No, shaving your hair is not damaging. However, you should take care of your scalp and ensure it is healthy and moisturized.

2. Can I shave my hair at home?

Yes, you can shave your hair at home using clippers. However, if you are unsure, it is best to go to a professional stylist.

3. How often should I shave my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to achieve. It is recommended to shave your hair every two to three weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care for Shaved Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your shaved hair:

1. Moisturize your scalp

Shaved hair can cause your scalp to become dry and itchy. Use a moisturizer or oil to keep your scalp healthy and hydrated.

2. Protect your scalp from the sun

When you have shaved hair, your scalp is exposed to the sun. Use a hat or sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Styling shaved hair can be a fun and creative process. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the look you want while keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and embrace your new look!
