Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces Male A Guide To Flattering Haircuts

Written by Bardi May 04, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a man with a round or chubby face? Do you find it difficult to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men with fat faces struggle to find the perfect haircut that flatters their facial features. That's why we've put together this guide to long hairstyles for fat faces male. We'll cover everything from the best cuts to how to style them, so you can look and feel your best.

Description

Having a round or chubby face can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that suits you. You want something that will balance out your facial features and create a more elongated appearance. Long hairstyles are a great option for fat faces because they can help to create a more vertical line, making your face appear slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right long hairstyle for your face shape is crucial in achieving a flattering look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider the Length

The length of your hair is essential when it comes to choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Long hair that falls below the shoulders can help to elongate your face, creating a more balanced appearance. However, if your hair is too long, it can add width to your face, making it look rounder. So, it's essential to find the right length for your face shape.

Think About the Layers

Layering your hair can help to add volume and texture, which is great for creating a more balanced look. However, too many layers can make your face appear wider, so it's essential to find the right balance. Talk to your stylist about the best layering technique for your face shape.

Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can add weight to your face, making it look rounder. If you want to have bangs, opt for side-swept bangs instead. They can help to create a more elongated appearance, and they're easier to style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces Male

Styling long hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hairstyles for fat faces male:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. If you have thick hair, use a volumizing shampoo to add body and texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to your hair. This will help to add volume and texture, making it easier to style.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a brush to create volume and texture. Start at the roots, and work your way to the ends. Use a round brush to create a more voluminous look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a comb or your fingers. You can create a side part or a center part, depending on your preference. You can also add some texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces Male

Long hairstyles require more maintenance than short haircuts. Here are some tips to keep your long hair looking healthy and stylish:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. It's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Use Heat Protectant Products

Heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair, so it's essential to use a heat protectant product before styling. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and damaged. It's recommended to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type.

FAQs

What are the best long hairstyles for fat faces male?

Some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces male include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and textured styles.

How do I know if a long hairstyle will suit my face shape?

The best way to know if a long hairstyle will suit your face shape is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that flatters your facial features.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

Absolutely! Long hairstyles allow for more styling options, so you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or bun. Just be sure to use hair ties that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care for Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces Male

Long hairstyles require more care than short haircuts. Here are some tips to keep your long hair healthy and shiny:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and moisturize your hair, leaving it soft and shiny. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Avoid Using Hot Tools Too Often

Hot tools can cause damage to your hair, so it's essential to use them sparingly. If you must use hot tools, be sure to use a heat protectant product.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a long hairstyle that flatters your facial features and makes you feel confident. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type. With a little bit of care and maintenance, you can keep your long hair looking healthy and stylish.
