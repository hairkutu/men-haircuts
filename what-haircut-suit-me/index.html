What Haircut Suits Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Style

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that just doesn't suit you? It's frustrating spending time and money on a new hairstyle only to be disappointed with the end result. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to find the perfect haircut for you. From determining your face shape to considering your lifestyle, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

What Is My Face Shape?

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for you is determining your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in which hairstyle will suit you best. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Once you have these measurements, compare them to the chart below:

If you have trouble determining your face shape, there are also online quizzes and tools that can help.

What Haircuts Suit Oval Face Shapes?

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Consider a long bob, layered cut, or blunt bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head or cover your face.

What Haircuts Suit Round Face Shapes?

For those with round face shapes, try a layered cut, long bob, or side-swept bangs. These styles will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more angular shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.

What Haircuts Suit Square Face Shapes?

If you have a square face shape, consider a layered cut, long bob, or side-swept bangs. These styles will soften your angles and create a more balanced look. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.

What Haircuts Suit Heart Face Shapes?

Heart face shapes can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Consider a long bob, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut. These styles will highlight your cheekbones and balance your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

What Haircuts Suit Diamond Face Shapes?

For those with diamond face shapes, try a layered cut or side-swept bangs. These styles will soften your angles and highlight your cheekbones. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.

What Haircuts Suit Triangle Face Shapes?

If you have a triangle face shape, consider a layered cut, long bob, or side-swept bangs. These styles will balance your face shape and create a more even appearance. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Do you have a busy schedule and limited time for styling? Are you a fitness enthusiast who needs a style that can withstand sweat? Consider these factors when selecting your new haircut. A low-maintenance cut like a bob or pixie cut may be best for those with busy schedules, while a longer layered cut may be more suitable for those who have more time for styling.

Ask for Professional Advice

When in doubt, ask for professional advice. A hairstylist can help you determine your face shape and recommend hairstyles that will suit you best. They can also consider your lifestyle and personal preferences when making recommendations.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its style and health. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can a haircut change my face shape?

A haircut cannot change your face shape, but it can create the illusion of a different shape. For example, a layered cut can soften the angles of a square face shape and make it appear more oval.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends.

What should I bring to my hair appointment?

Bring a photo of the haircut you're interested in, as well as any questions or concerns you may have. Your hairstylist will be happy to answer your questions and provide advice.

We hope this guide has helped you determine what haircut suits you best. Remember to consider your face shape, lifestyle, and personal preferences when selecting your new style. And don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Happy hairstyling!
