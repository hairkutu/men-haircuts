Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50 2021

Written by Oliver Jun 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're over 50 and have thick hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can work well with thick hair and are perfect for women over 50. In this article, we'll be exploring some of the best short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 in 2021.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have a lot of hair, it can be difficult to manage and style. This is especially true as we get older and our hair starts to thin out. Short hairstyles can be a great option for women over 50 with thick hair, as they are easy to maintain and can make you look younger and more vibrant.

How to

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair over 50, it's important to consider your face shape and personal style. Some popular short hairstyles for thick hair over 50 include the pixie cut, the bob, and the layered cut. Here are some tips for each:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with thick hair. This cut is very short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It's a low-maintenance style that can be styled in a variety of ways. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short, layered cut with a longer fringe.

Bob

The bob is another classic short hairstyle that works well with thick hair. This cut is usually chin-length or shorter, with layers that add volume and movement. It's a versatile style that can be styled straight or wavy. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with layers.

Layered Cut

The layered cut is a great option for women over 50 with thick hair. This cut adds texture and movement to your hair, while also reducing bulk. It's a low-maintenance style that can be styled in a variety of ways. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a layered cut with shorter layers around the face.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your short hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too hard.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a flat iron or curling iron.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thick hair over 50:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, you can style your short hair in a variety of ways, from straight and sleek to curly and voluminous.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a short hairstyle?

A: You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thick hair, look for products that add volume and reduce frizz. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.

Overall, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can work well with thick hair over 50. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, a bob, or a layered cut, there is a style that will suit your face shape and personal style. By following these tips and caring for your hair properly, you can keep your short hairstyle looking great for years to come.
