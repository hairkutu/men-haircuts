Is It Okay To Relax Short Hair Tips And Faqs

Written by Luffy Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Relaxing hair has been a popular trend for decades. It can give you the sleek and straight look that you desire. However, one question that many people ask is, “is it okay to relax short hair?”

  • How to Relax Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Relaxing Short Hair

  • Hair Care After Relaxing Short Hair

Description of Hair Relaxing

Hair relaxing is a chemical process that uses harsh chemicals to break down the bonds in your hair, making it straighter and easier to manage. The chemicals used in the process can cause damage to your hair, so it’s important to take proper care of your hair before and after the process.

How to Relax Short Hair

If you’re thinking of relaxing your short hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure that your hair is healthy and strong enough to withstand the process. Second, consult with a professional stylist to determine the best type of relaxer for your hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Relaxing Short Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to relaxing short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly to your scalp to protect it from the chemicals.
  4. Apply the relaxer to your hair, starting at the back and working your way up to the front.
  5. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse the relaxer out of your hair thoroughly.
  7. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the chemical process.
  8. Condition your hair with a deep conditioner to restore moisture.

Tips for Relaxing Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when relaxing short hair:

  • Don’t relax your hair too often. Over-relaxing can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a mild relaxer if your hair is thin or fragile.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.

FAQs About Relaxing Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing short hair:

1. Can I relax short hair at home?

While it is possible to relax your hair at home, it is recommended that you go to a professional stylist to ensure that the process is done safely and correctly.

2. How long does a hair relaxer last?

A hair relaxer can last anywhere from 6-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

3. Can I color my hair after relaxing it?

It is not recommended to color your hair immediately after relaxing it. Wait at least two weeks before coloring to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care After Relaxing Short Hair

After relaxing your short hair, it’s important to take proper care of it to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent further damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to restore moisture.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, relaxing short hair is okay as long as you take proper care of it before and after the process. Consult with a professional stylist, use a mild relaxer, and moisturize your hair regularly to avoid damage and breakage. If you follow these tips, you can achieve the sleek and straight look that you desire without compromising the health of your hair.
