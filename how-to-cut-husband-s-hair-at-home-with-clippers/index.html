How To Cut Your Husband s Hair At Home With Clippers

Written by Robby Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time, cutting your husband's hair at home with clippers is a great option. With a little bit of practice, you can achieve professional-looking results in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of how to cut your husband's hair at home with clippers.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Husband's Hair at Home with Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Husband's Hair at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After a Haircut

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Get the Right Tools

The first step to cutting your husband's hair at home with clippers is to get the right tools. You'll need a good pair of clippers, a comb, a pair of scissors, and a cape or towel to drape over your husband's shoulders to catch the hair.

2. Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Next, you'll need to choose the right clipper guard. The clipper guard determines the length of the hair that will be cut. If your husband wants a shorter cut, choose a smaller guard. If he wants a longer cut, choose a larger guard.

3. Start with the Sides

Begin by starting on the sides of your husband's head. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use the longer clipper guards for the top of the head and the shorter ones for the sides and back.

4. Move to the Back

After you've finished the sides, move to the back of your husband's head. Again, use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length.

5. Trim the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, it's time to trim the top. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to trim any longer hair that needs to be cut. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length.

6. Blend the Hair

After you've trimmed the top, it's time to blend the hair. Use the clippers to blend the sides and back with the top of the head. This will create a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

7. Trim the Neckline

Finally, trim the neckline. Use the clippers to trim the hair around the neckline to create a clean, even look.

Tips for Cutting Your Husband's Hair at Home

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down. You can always take more hair off, but you can't add it back.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go. This will help you see any areas that need more attention.
  • Take your time. It's better to go slow and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.
  • Watch some tutorials online to get a better understanding of the process.

FAQs

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: If you make a mistake, it's not the end of the world. You can always take more hair off to even it out or leave it and try to fix it next time. Remember, practice makes perfect!

Q: How often should I cut my husband's hair?

A: It depends on how fast his hair grows and how short he likes to keep it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame to aim for.

Hair Care After a Haircut

After you've finished cutting your husband's hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips to keep his hair looking great:

  • Shampoo and condition his hair regularly.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep his hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to keep the hair looking healthy.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you should be able to cut your husband's hair at home with clippers like a pro. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to try new things and experiment with different lengths and styles!
