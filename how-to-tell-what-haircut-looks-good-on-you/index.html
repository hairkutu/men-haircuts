How To Tell What Haircut Looks Good On You

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't flatter your face shape or features? Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you'll be able to tell what haircut looks good on you in no time. In this article, we'll go over how to determine your face shape, what haircuts suit each face shape, and some bonus tips on maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Determining Your Face Shape
  2. Haircuts for Each Face Shape
  3. Bonus Tips for Healthy Hair
  4. FAQ

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining what haircut looks good on you is figuring out your face shape. The four main face shapes are round, square, heart, and oval. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've created.

Now that you have an idea of your face shape, let's discuss what haircuts suit each shape best.

Haircuts for Each Face Shape

Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to elongate it with a haircut that adds height and volume to the top of your head. Consider a pixie cut, a bob with layers, or a side-swept bang.

Square Face

A square face is defined by a strong jawline and angular features. Softening those angles with a layered haircut can give a more feminine look. Consider a long bob, a shag cut, or a layered pixie cut.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is wider at the forehead and tapers down to a pointy chin. To balance out the face, consider a haircut that adds volume to the chin area. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs, a lob with layers, or a long bob with a side part are all great options.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck - this face shape suits most haircuts! Consider a blunt bob, a layered shag cut, or a side-swept bang to frame the face.

Bonus Tips for Healthy Hair

Now that you know what haircut looks good on you, it's important to maintain healthy hair to keep your new 'do looking fresh. Here are some bonus tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when heat styling.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQ

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, look for haircuts that flatter the most prominent features of your face.

What if I don't want to cut my hair?

If you don't want to cut your hair, consider a new hair color or style instead. Bangs or layers can also change up your look without sacrificing length.

What if I'm still unsure?

If you're still unsure what haircut looks good on you, consult with a hairstylist. They can offer professional advice based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

By following these tips, you'll be able to determine what haircut looks good on you and maintain healthy hair. Don't be afraid to try something new - a fresh haircut can do wonders for your confidence!
