Are Square Faces Pretty Tips And Tricks For Flattering Haircuts And Makeup

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a square-shaped face, you might be wondering if it's pretty or not. The truth is, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and a square face can be just as beautiful as any other face shape. In fact, many famous women such as Angelina Jolie, Keira Knightley, and Olivia Wilde have square faces and are considered some of the most beautiful women in the world.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong, angular jawline and a broad forehead. The width and length of the face are almost equal, giving it a boxy appearance. While some people might find this face shape too masculine or harsh, others appreciate its striking features and edgy look.

How to Enhance Your Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to enhance your features and flatter your face:

1. Go for Soft, Rounded Hairstyles

Soft, rounded hairstyles can help balance out the sharp angles of a square face. Consider getting a layered haircut with soft waves or curls, or try a long bob that grazes your shoulders. Avoid blunt, straight haircuts that can make your face look even more angular.

2. Use Makeup to Soften Your Features

Makeup can also help soften your features and create a more feminine look. Use a light, sheer foundation to even out your skin tone, and add a touch of blush on the apples of your cheeks to create a soft, rounded effect. For your eyes, try a smoky eye with soft, blended edges or a cat-eye with a slight flick at the outer corners.

3. Experiment with Statement Accessories

Statement accessories such as bold earrings, chunky necklaces, or oversized sunglasses can draw attention away from your jawline and add some interest to your outfit. However, avoid wearing accessories that are too small or delicate, as they can get lost against the strong lines of your face.

Step by Step Guide to Flattering Your Square Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to enhancing your square face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can start enhancing your features, you need to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and compare them to each other. If they are roughly equal, you have a square face shape.

Step 2: Choose a Flattering Hairstyle

Look for hairstyles that soften your features and add some roundness to your face. Consider getting layers, waves, or curls, and avoid blunt, straight cuts that can make your face look boxy.

Step 3: Use Makeup to Create Softness

Use a light foundation to even out your skin tone, and add a touch of blush on the apples of your cheeks to create a soft, rounded effect. For your eyes, try a smoky eye with soft, blended edges or a cat-eye with a slight flick at the outer corners.

Step 4: Experiment with Accessories

Try bold accessories such as statement earrings or chunky necklaces to draw attention away from your jawline and add some interest to your outfit.

Tips for Maintaining a Square Face Shape

Once you've found a flattering hairstyle and makeup routine, here are some tips for maintaining your square face shape:

1. Keep Your Skin Healthy

Healthy, glowing skin can help enhance your natural beauty and make your features stand out. Make sure to cleanse, moisturize, and protect your skin from the sun to keep it looking its best.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin and body healthy, which can in turn enhance your features and make you feel more confident.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is crucial for overall health and wellbeing, but it can also help reduce puffiness and dark circles around your eyes, which can detract from your square face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I Still Look Pretty with a Square Face Shape?

A: Yes! Beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and a square face can be just as beautiful as any other face shape.

Q: What Hairstyles Should I Avoid if I Have a Square Face Shape?

A: Avoid blunt, straight haircuts that can make your face look even more angular. Instead, look for soft, rounded hairstyles such as layered cuts and waves.

Q: Can Makeup Really Enhance My Features?

A: Yes! Makeup can help create the illusion of softer, more feminine features, and draw attention away from your jawline.

Hair Care for Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for square faces, it's important to choose products that enhance your natural texture and add volume and body. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners, and use a lightweight mousse or styling cream to add texture and definition to your hair.

When styling your hair, avoid pulling it back tightly or creating severe, angular styles that can emphasize your square face shape. Instead, opt for soft, loose hairstyles that enhance your natural texture and add some roundness to your face.

Final Thoughts

Having a square face shape doesn't mean you're not pretty â it simply means you have a unique and striking look that can be enhanced with the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories. With these tips and tricks, you can create a flattering, feminine look that accentuates your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
