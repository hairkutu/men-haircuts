Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Over 60

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 60 and having an oval face? Do you want a hairstyle that suits your face shape? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for oval face over 60 that will make you look stylish and younger.

Description

An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape, as it suits almost all types of hairstyles. However, as we age, our hair becomes thin and weak, and our face loses its firmness. Therefore, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that complements our face shape and age.

How To Choose The Best Hairstyle?

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture
  • Your hair color
  • Your lifestyle and personality

Once you have considered these factors, you can choose the best hairstyle that suits you.

Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Over 60

The best hairstyle for oval face over 60 is a short bob. A short bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits all face shapes, especially oval faces. A short bob is easy to maintain, and it makes you look stylish and younger.

Step By Step Guide To Get A Short Bob

Follow these steps to get a short bob:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and condition it.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight.
  3. Section your hair and start cutting it from the back to the front.
  4. Trim your hair to your desired length and style it with a hairbrush.
  5. Apply some hair serum to add shine and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your short bob:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too many products as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add some variety to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for oval face over 60:

Q. Can I dye my hair?

A. Yes, you can dye your hair, but choose a color that suits your skin tone and age.

Q. Can I get a layered haircut?

A. Yes, you can get a layered haircut, but make sure it doesn't make your hair look thin and weak.

Q. Can I get a pixie cut?

A. Yes, you can get a pixie cut, but it may not suit everyone. Consult your hairstylist before getting a pixie cut.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for oval face over 60 is a short bob. It is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits all face shapes, especially oval faces. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to get a perfect short bob and maintain healthy hair.
