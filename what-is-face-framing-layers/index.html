What Are Face Framing Layers

Written by Eveline Jul 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to add some dimension and style to your hair? Maybe you’ve heard about face framing layers but aren’t quite sure what they are. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about face framing layers, including what they are, how to get them, and how to take care of your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Definition
  • How to Get Face Framing Layers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Face Framing Layers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Face Framing Layers

Definition

Face framing layers are a hairstyle technique that involves cutting layers of hair around the face to enhance the facial features. These layers can be angled or tapered, and can be shorter or longer depending on the look you’re going for. Face framing layers can be added to any hair length, from short to long.

How to Get Face Framing Layers

To get face framing layers, you should visit a hair salon and work with a stylist who has experience cutting this type of hairstyle. It’s important to bring pictures of the look you want to achieve, as well as to communicate your preferences and any concerns you have about the cut. Your stylist will then work with you to create a personalized look that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a basic step-by-step guide to getting face framing layers:

  1. Visit a hair salon and consult with a stylist about your desired look
  2. Wash and dry your hair
  3. Section your hair and secure it with clips
  4. Start cutting your layers, working from the front to the back
  5. Blend the layers together for a seamless look
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Face Framing Layers

Once you have your new face framing layers, it’s important to take care of them properly to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools on your hair
  • Trim your layers every 6-8 weeks to keep them looking fresh
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh down your layers

FAQs

What face shapes work best with face framing layers?

Face framing layers can work with any face shape, but the length and angle of the layers may vary depending on your face shape. A stylist can help you determine the best cut for your face shape.

Can I get face framing layers if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Face framing layers can be added to any hair type, including curly hair. Your stylist can work with you to create a cut that enhances your natural curl pattern.

How do I know if face framing layers are right for me?

If you’re looking for a way to add some dimension and style to your hair, face framing layers may be a good option. It’s important to consult with a stylist to determine if this cut will work with your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care for Face Framing Layers

Proper hair care is important to maintain the look of your face framing layers. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when styling

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your face framing layers looking great for weeks to come!
