How To Box Braid Your Own Hair Male

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Box braids are a great protective hairstyle that can last for weeks. They are especially popular among men with natural hair or those who want to protect their hair from damage. In this article, we will guide you on how to box braid your own hair male. We will cover everything from how to prepare your hair to how to care for your braids once they are done.

  • How to Box Braid Your Own Hair Male
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Box Braid Your Own Hair Male

Before you start box braiding your hair, there are a few things you need to do to prepare:

  • Wash and condition your hair
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb
  • Trim your ends if necessary
  • Gather all the tools you will need (rat-tail comb, hair clips, rubber bands, braiding hair, etc.)

Once you have prepared your hair and tools, follow the step-by-step guide below to box braid your own hair male.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Section your Hair

Start by parting your hair into four sections using a rat-tail comb. Clip each section of hair to keep it out of the way.

Step 2: Add Braiding Hair

Take a small section of braiding hair and fold it in half. Place the folded end of the hair under your natural hair, then tie a knot with the braiding hair and your natural hair. This will create a base for your braid.

Step 3: Begin Braiding

Divide your hair and the braiding hair into three sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the new middle section. Repeat this process, adding more braiding hair as you go, until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Secure the End

Once you have braided to the end of your hair, secure the end with a rubber band. You can also dip the ends of your braids in hot water to seal them and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat steps 2-4 until all of your hair is braided.

Tips for Box Braiding Your Own Hair Male

  • Take breaks if you feel your arms getting tired
  • Use a rat-tail comb to section your hair evenly
  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly while wearing box braids
  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a satin or silk bonnet or scarf

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to box braid your own hair?

The time it takes to box braid your own hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your braiding skill level. On average, it can take anywhere from 4-10 hours.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can you wash box braids?

Yes, you can wash box braids. However, it is important to be gentle when washing and to avoid soaking your braids in water for too long.

Hair Care for Box Braids

When wearing box braids, it is important to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent damage and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly with a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids
  • Protect your braids at night with a satin or silk bonnet or scarf
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, which can cause damage to your hair and scalp

Now that you know how to box braid your own hair male, you can try this protective hairstyle at home. With the right tools and a little practice, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look that will protect your hair from damage.
