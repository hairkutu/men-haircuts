How To Do Half Up Half Down Bun Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of wearing your curly hair down all the time? Do you want to switch things up and try a new hairstyle? Look no further than the half up half down bun! This easy and versatile hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a day at the office or a night out with friends. In this article, we'll show you how to do half up half down bun curly hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

The half up half down bun is a great option for those with curly hair because it allows you to show off your curls while still keeping your hair out of your face. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Hair elastic
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray

Step-by-Step:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair as you normally would. Make sure your curls are defined and bouncy.

Step 2: Section off the top half of your hair

Using a comb, part your hair horizontally from ear to ear. Clip the top half of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Create a bun with the bottom half of your hair

Gather the bottom half of your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 4: Secure the top half of your hair

Take the top half of your hair and gather it into a ponytail on top of your head. Secure it with a hair elastic.

Step 5: Create a second bun

Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • If you have shorter hair, you can still do a half up half down bun! Just make sure to leave some pieces out around your face for a more relaxed look.
  • If you want to add some extra volume to your curls, use a diffuser when blow drying your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the placement of your buns. You can make them as high or as low as you want.

FAQ:

Can I do a half up half down bun with straight hair?

Absolutely! While this tutorial is specifically for curly hair, the half up half down bun works for any hair type.

What if I don't have any bobby pins?

You can use hair ties instead! Just make sure to tuck the ends of your hair under the bun to hide the tie.

How do I make my curls last all day?

Use a curl defining product before styling your hair, and finish with a strong hold hairspray.

Hair Care:

Curly hair requires a little extra TLC to look its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and strong.

Now that you know how to do half up half down bun curly hair, it's time to give it a try! This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and will have you looking stylish and put together in no time.
