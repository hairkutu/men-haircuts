How To Layer Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some dimension and texture to men's hair, then layering is the way to go. Layered haircuts are versatile and can give men a fresh, modern look. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to layer cut men's hair with scissors. We'll cover the tools you'll need, the techniques to use, and some tips for achieving a great look. Let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before you start layering, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Haircutting cape
  • Hair clips

How to Layer Cut Men's Hair with Scissors

Follow these steps to layer cut men's hair with scissors:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. Comb through it to remove any tangles or knots. Then, section off the hair into manageable sections using hair clips. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of the hair to the desired length.

Step 2: Create the Guide Cut

Take a small section of hair from the top of the head and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at a slight angle, starting at the ends and working your way up. This will create a guide cut for the rest of the layers.

Step 3: Layer the Hair

Working from the top of the head down, take small sections of hair and hold them between your fingers. Use the guide cut to cut the hair at the same angle, creating layers. Be sure to cut each section to the same length as the guide cut.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you've layered the entire head of hair, go back through and blend the layers together. Take small sections of hair and use the scissors to cut into the ends, creating a more natural, blended look.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

Remove the hair clips and comb through the hair to check for any uneven spots. Use the scissors to make any necessary adjustments. Finish the cut by trimming the sideburns and neckline.

Tips for Layer Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layer cutting men's hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid mistakes.
  • Remember to blend the layers together for a more natural look.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid hand fatigue.

FAQ

Q: Can I layer cut my own hair?

A: It's possible to layer cut your own hair, but it can be difficult without experience. It's recommended to have someone else do it for you.

Q: How often should I layer cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how you want it to look. Generally, every 6-8 weeks is a good time frame for a trim.

Q: Can layer cutting damage my hair?

A: Layer cutting shouldn't damage your hair if done correctly. Be sure to use sharp scissors and work carefully to avoid mistakes.

Hair Care

After layer cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair when styling. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to layer cut men's hair with scissors, you can give it a try yourself or take these tips to your barber. With a little practice, you can create a stylish, layered look that's sure to turn heads!
