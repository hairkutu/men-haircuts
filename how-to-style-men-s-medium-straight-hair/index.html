How To Style Men s Medium Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium length straight hair, you might be wondering how to style it to look your best. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there are many ways to style men's medium straight hair. In this guide, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to achieve different hairstyles and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Medium Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Style Men's Medium Straight Hair

Before we get started, it's important to know what kind of medium straight hair you have. Is it thick or thin? Do you have any natural waves or curls? These factors will affect how you style your hair.

Generally, men's medium straight hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including:

  • Side part
  • Slicked back
  • Messy textured
  • Undercut
  • Buzz cut

Depending on the occasion and your personal style, you can choose one of these hairstyles or experiment with different variations.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the most popular men's medium straight hairstyles:

Side Part

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Using a comb, create a deep side part on one side of your head
  3. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingers and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  4. Smooth down the sides of your hair with the comb
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place

Slicked Back

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a generous amount of pomade or gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  3. Using a comb, slick your hair back away from your face
  4. Smooth down the sides of your hair with the comb
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place

Messy Textured

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing cream or paste to your fingers and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look by pulling and twisting sections of your hair
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Straight Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling men's medium straight hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that matches your hair type and desired style
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair
  • Keep your hair healthy by using a conditioner and avoiding heat styling tools

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's medium straight hair:

What kind of hair products should I use for medium straight hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired style. Pomade, wax, gel, and texturizing cream are all good options for men's medium straight hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Can I style my hair with just my fingers?

Yes, you can use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. However, using a comb or brush can help create a more polished look.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Straight Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your men's medium straight hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools, or use them sparingly
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can find the perfect look for your men's medium straight hair.
