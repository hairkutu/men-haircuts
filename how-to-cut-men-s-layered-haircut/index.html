How To Cut Men s Layered Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to cut men's layered haircut. Layered haircuts are the perfect way to add dimension and texture to any hairstyle. Whether you're looking for a messy, tousled look or a sleek, sophisticated style, a layered haircut can help you achieve your desired look. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting a men's layered haircut, provide you with some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Layered Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care

How to Cut Men's Layered Haircut

Before we get into the step by step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need to cut a men's layered haircut:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Hair spray

Now, let's move onto the step by step guide.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see the natural texture of the hair.

Step 2: Section the hair into four parts: top, sides, back, and crown. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the back section. Use the clipper to trim the hair to the desired length. Then, use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line.

Step 4: Move onto the sides section. Use the clipper to trim the hair to the desired length. Then, use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line.

Step 5: Move onto the top section. Use the comb to separate a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Repeat this process throughout the top section of the hair.

Step 6: Move onto the crown section. Use the comb to separate a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Repeat this process throughout the crown section of the hair.

Step 7: Blend the layers together. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, blending the layers together to create a seamless look.

Step 8: Use hair spray to set the hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Before you start cutting, make sure you have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve.

Tip 2: Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut a little bit at a time and gradually build up the layers.

Tip 3: Use the comb to separate the hair and create a guide for cutting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I get a layered haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: While it's possible to cut your own hair, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure you get the best results.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to cut a men's layered haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

We hope this guide has been helpful in teaching you how to cut a men's layered haircut. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect layered haircut in no time!
