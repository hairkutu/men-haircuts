What Colours Best Suit My Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Apr 02, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to fashion and style, choosing the right colours that complement your skin tone is essential. Wearing the wrong colours can make you look washed out, dull, and unflattering. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on what colours best suit your skin tone. From warm to cool skin tones, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Best Colours for Warm Skin Tone
  • Best Colours for Cool Skin Tone
  • Tips for Choosing Colours
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Before we dive into the best colours for your skin tone, it is essential to determine whether you have a warm, cool, or neutral skin tone. There are a few ways to determine your skin tone:

  • Vein Test: Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you have a neutral skin tone.
  • Jewellery Test: Pay attention to whether gold or silver jewellery looks better on you. If gold looks better, you have a warm skin tone. If silver looks better, you have a cool skin tone. If both look good, you have a neutral skin tone.
  • Eye and Hair Colour: Your eye and hair colour can also indicate your skin tone. Cool skin tones usually have blue, grey, or green eyes with blonde, brown or black hair. Warm skin tones usually have brown, amber or hazel eyes with red, blonde or brown hair.

If you still can't determine your skin tone, it is most likely neutral, and you can wear both warm and cool colours.

Best Colours for Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, your best colours are warm colours such as earthy shades, oranges, yellows, and reds. Some of the best colours for warm skin tones include:

  • Earthy shades such as olive, khaki, and brown
  • Oranges such as peach, coral, and terracotta
  • Yellows such as mustard, lemon, and golden
  • Reds such as tomato red, brick red, and warm burgundy
  • Warm neutrals such as beige, cream, and taupe

When selecting colours, look for shades that have a warm undertone. Avoid colours that are too cool or pastel shades that can clash with your skin tone.

Best Colours for Cool Skin Tone

If you have a cool skin tone, your best colours are cool colours such as blues, purples, and pinks. Some of the best colours for cool skin tones include:

  • Blues such as navy, baby blue, and periwinkle
  • Purples such as lavender, lilac, and grape
  • Pinks such as rose, magenta, and fuchsia
  • Cool neutrals such as grey, white, and silver

When selecting colours, look for shades that have a cool undertone. Avoid colours that are too warm or earthy shades that can clash with your skin tone.

Tips for Choosing Colours

Here are some general tips for choosing colours that complement your skin tone:

  • Consider the occasion and setting. Some colours may be appropriate for formal events, while others are more suitable for casual occasions.
  • Experiment with different shades and tones to find what works for you.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match colours that complement each other.
  • Consider your hair and eye colour as they can also impact how colours look on you.
  • When in doubt, opt for neutral colours such as black, white, and grey.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a neutral skin tone?

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear both warm and cool colours.

Can I wear black if I have a warm skin tone?

Yes, you can wear black, but it is best to pair it with warm colours or accessories to balance it out.

Can I wear white if I have a cool skin tone?

Yes, you can wear white, but it is best to pair it with cool colours or accessories to balance it out.

Hair Care Tips

Choosing the right hair colour can also complement your skin tone. Here are some hair care tips:

  • For warm skin tones, opt for warm shades such as golden or honey blonde, copper or ginger red, and warm brown shades.
  • For cool skin tones, opt for cool shades such as platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension and complement your skin tone.
  • Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair colour for your skin tone.

In conclusion, choosing the right colours that complement your skin tone can make a huge difference in your overall look and style. Whether you have a warm, cool, or neutral skin tone, there are plenty of colours to choose from. By following these tips and experimenting with different shades and tones, you can find the perfect colours that will make you look and feel your best.
