How Do I Determine My Natural Hair Type

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our hair care blog where we’ll be discussing how to determine your natural hair type. It’s essential to know your hair type so that you can select the right products to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. In this article, we’ll be covering how to determine your hair type, the different hair types, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

How to Determine Your Natural Hair Type

The first step to determining your hair type is to look at the shape of your hair follicles. The shape of your hair follicles will determine the texture of your hair. Here are the three basic hair types:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is the easiest hair type to identify. It is typically shiny, and the strands are mostly uniform in size. If you have straight hair, it will be easy to manage, and you won’t have to worry about frizz or tangles.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair is characterized by its loose, flowing texture. It’s not as straight as straight hair, but it’s not as curly as curly hair. If your hair falls somewhere in between these two categories, you have wavy hair.

Curly Hair

Curly hair is the most difficult hair type to manage. It has a tight, coiled texture that can be prone to frizz and tangles. It’s also the most delicate hair type, and it requires a lot of care to keep it healthy.

Different Types of Hair

Now that you know the basic hair types, it’s time to determine your specific hair type. Here are the different types of hair:

Type 1 – Straight Hair

Type 1 hair is straight and has no curl pattern. It is the easiest hair type to manage.

Type 2 – Wavy Hair

Type 2 hair has a slight wave pattern. It is more prone to frizz than straight hair.

Type 3 – Curly Hair

Type 3 hair has a tighter curl pattern than type 2. It’s prone to frizz and requires a lot of care to keep it healthy.

Type 4 – Coily Hair

Type 4 hair has a tight coil pattern. It is the most delicate hair type and requires a lot of care.

Tips for Managing Different Hair Types

Now that you know your hair type, here are some tips for managing it:

Straight Hair Tips

  • Wash your hair every other day.
  • Use a light conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Wavy Hair Tips

  • Wash your hair every other day.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Curly Hair Tips

  • Wash your hair once a week.
  • Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Coily Hair Tips

  • Wash your hair once a week.
  • Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Moisturize your hair daily.
