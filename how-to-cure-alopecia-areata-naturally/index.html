How To Cure Alopecia Areata Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you or someone you know is suffering from alopecia areata, a condition where hair falls out in patches due to an autoimmune disorder, you may be wondering if there are natural remedies to help cure it. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to cure alopecia areata naturally.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cure Alopecia Areata Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Preventing Alopecia Areata
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Alopecia Areata

Description

Alopecia areata is an autoimmune disorder that causes hair loss in patches on the scalp. It can also affect other parts of the body, such as the eyebrows, eyelashes, and beard. The condition is caused by the body's immune system attacking hair follicles, which leads to hair loss.

There is no known cure for alopecia areata, but there are natural remedies and lifestyle changes that can help manage the condition.

How to Cure Alopecia Areata Naturally

The following natural remedies can help cure alopecia areata:

  • Essential oils
  • Vitamins and minerals
  • Diet changes
  • Stress management

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Use Essential Oils

Essential oils such as lavender, peppermint, and rosemary can help stimulate hair growth by increasing blood flow to the scalp. Mix a few drops of your preferred essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut or jojoba oil, and massage onto the scalp. Leave on for at least 30 minutes before washing off.

Step 2: Take Vitamins and Minerals

Vitamins and minerals such as vitamin D, iron, and zinc can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. You can take supplements or consume foods rich in these nutrients such as leafy greens, nuts, and fish.

Step 3: Make Diet Changes

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in proteins and antioxidants can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Include foods such as eggs, nuts, and berries in your diet to help manage alopecia areata.

Step 4: Manage Stress

Stress can exacerbate alopecia areata. Incorporate stress-reducing activities such as yoga, meditation, and deep breathing exercises into your daily routine.

Tips for Preventing Alopecia Areata

The following tips can help prevent alopecia areata:

  • Avoid harsh hair treatments such as perms and coloring
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a regular basis
  • Avoid brushing hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals

FAQ

What causes alopecia areata?

Alopecia areata is caused by an autoimmune disorder where the body's immune system attacks hair follicles, leading to hair loss.

Is there a cure for alopecia areata?

There is no known cure for alopecia areata, but lifestyle changes and natural remedies can help manage the condition.

Can stress cause alopecia areata?

Stress can exacerbate alopecia areata, but it is not the sole cause of the condition.

Hair Care Tips for Alopecia Areata

Managing alopecia areata requires proper hair care. The following tips can help:

  • Use a mild shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a regular basis
  • Avoid brushing hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair

By following these natural remedies and lifestyle changes, you can manage alopecia areata and promote hair growth. Remember to consult with your healthcare provider before making any significant changes to your diet or lifestyle.
