Can You Fix Tension Alopecia

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are reading this article, it probably means that you or someone you know is experiencing hair loss due to tension alopecia. This condition is caused by prolonged tension on the scalp and can be caused by tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, and extensions. In this article, we will be discussing ways that you can fix tension alopecia.

Description

Tension alopecia is a type of hair loss that is caused by prolonged tension on the scalp. This tension can be caused by tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, and extensions. The constant pulling on the hair follicles can cause them to become damaged, leading to hair loss.

How to Fix Tension Alopecia

There are several ways that you can fix tension alopecia. These include:

1. Stop Wearing Tight Hairstyles

The first and most important step in fixing tension alopecia is to stop wearing tight hairstyles. This will give your hair follicles a chance to rest and heal. You can opt for looser hairstyles such as twists or buns.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for 5-10 minutes each day.

3. Use Essential Oils

Essential oils such as lavender and peppermint can help to stimulate hair growth. Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil and massage it into your scalp.

4. Take Supplements

Taking supplements such as biotin and vitamin E can help to promote hair growth and strengthen hair follicles. Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to fix tension alopecia:

Step 1: Stop wearing tight hairstyles

The first step in fixing tension alopecia is to stop wearing tight hairstyles. Opt for looser hairstyles such as twists or buns.

Step 2: Massage your scalp

Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for 5-10 minutes each day.

Step 3: Use essential oils

Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil and massage it into your scalp.

Step 4: Take supplements

Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix tension alopecia:

  • Avoid using heat tools such as flat irons and curling irons
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients
  • Get enough sleep

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to fix tension alopecia?

A: It can take several months to a year to see significant improvement in hair growth.

Q: Can tension alopecia be permanent?

A: In some cases, tension alopecia can be permanent. It is important to seek treatment as soon as possible to prevent permanent hair loss.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential in preventing and fixing tension alopecia. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles
  • Avoid using heat tools
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients
  • Get enough sleep
  • Massage your scalp regularly
  • Use essential oils
  • Take supplements

In conclusion, tension alopecia can be fixed with the right treatment and care. It is important to seek treatment as soon as possible to prevent permanent hair loss. Remember to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent future hair loss.
