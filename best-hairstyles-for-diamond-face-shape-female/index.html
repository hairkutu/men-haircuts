Description

If you have a diamond face shape, you have a narrow forehead and chin with wider cheekbones. This unique face shape requires a hairstyle that balances out the width of the cheekbones while softening the angles of the forehead and chin. In this blog post, we will discuss the best hairstyles for diamond face shape females and provide tips for styling and caring for your hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for diamond face shape females, it's important to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and take a photo of your face.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the photo with a pen or marker.
  3. Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Characteristics of Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has the following characteristics:

  • Narrow forehead and chin
  • Wider cheekbones
  • Angular jawline
  • Elongated face shape

Best Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape Female

Now, let's dive into the best hairstyles for diamond face shape females:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that works well for diamond face shape females. The short length of the cut draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones, balancing out the width of your face. It also softens the angles of your forehead and chin, creating a more feminine look.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that flatters diamond face shape females. The length of the cut should fall just above or below the jawline, creating a soft and rounded look. You can also add bangs to the bob cut to further balance out the width of your cheekbones.

3. Shoulder-Length Layers

If you prefer longer hair, shoulder-length layers are a great option for diamond face shape females. The layers add volume and movement to your hair, while the length balances out the width of your cheekbones. You can also add side-swept bangs to the layers to soften your forehead.

4. Side-Parted Hairstyles

Side-parted hairstyles work well for diamond face shape females because they create asymmetry, balancing out the width of your cheekbones. You can wear your hair in a side-parted pixie cut, bob cut, or shoulder-length layers.

Tips for Styling Diamond Face Shape Hair

Here are some tips for styling your diamond face shape hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this will make your face appear longer.
  • Add volume to the sides of your hair to balance out the width of your cheekbones.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to soften your forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or straight, as this will emphasize the angles of your forehead and chin.

FAQs

What haircuts should diamond face shape females avoid?

Diamond face shape females should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as this will make your face appear longer. They should also avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or straight, as this will emphasize the angles of the forehead and chin.

What is the best length for diamond face shape hair?

The best length for diamond face shape hair is a pixie cut, bob cut, or shoulder-length layers. These hairstyles balance out the width of the cheekbones while softening the angles of the forehead and chin.

Hair Care for Diamond Face Shape Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your diamond face shape hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that are too tight, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect look for your diamond face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful!
