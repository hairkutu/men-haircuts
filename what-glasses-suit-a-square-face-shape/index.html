What Glasses Suit a Square Face Shape?

Are you on the hunt for glasses that will flatter your square face shape? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss what glasses suit a square face shape and provide you with some helpful tips to make the shopping process easier.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have a square face shape, you likely have a strong jawline and angular features. When selecting glasses, you want to choose a style that will soften those sharp edges and add some curves to your face. The right glasses can balance your features and make you look your best.

How To

Before you start shopping for glasses, it's essential to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a tape measure. If all three are roughly the same width, you likely have a square face shape.

Step By Step

  1. Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a tape measure.
  2. If all three are roughly the same width, you likely have a square face shape.
  3. Start shopping for glasses that will soften the sharp edges of your face and add some curves.

Tips

When selecting glasses for a square face shape, consider frames that are round or oval. These shapes will balance your features and add some curves to your face. Avoid frames that are too angular or square, as they will only emphasize the sharp edges of your face.

Another tip is to choose glasses that are wider than your cheekbones. This will create the illusion of a narrower face and help balance your features. Additionally, glasses with nose pads can help adjust the fit and prevent them from sliding down your nose.

FAQ

What types of glasses should I avoid if I have a square face shape?

Avoid glasses with sharp angles or square frames, as they will only emphasize the angular features of your face. Additionally, glasses that are too narrow can make your face appear wider and more boxy.

What types of glasses should I look for if I have a square face shape?

Look for glasses with round or oval frames, as they will balance your features and add some curves to your face. Additionally, glasses that are wider than your cheekbones can help create the illusion of a narrower face.

Hair Care

When selecting glasses for a square face shape, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. Soft, flowing hairstyles can help balance the sharp angles of your face and complement your glasses. Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or severe, as they will only emphasize the squareness of your face.

Now that you know what glasses suit a square face shape, you can start shopping with confidence. Remember to choose frames that soften your features and add some curves to your face. With the right glasses, you'll look and feel your best!
