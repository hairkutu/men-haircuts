Mens Haircuts Short Sides Fade A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Description

Short sides fade is a popular mens haircut that has been around for years. It's a classic look that is still trendy and stylish. The haircut features a gradual taper from the top of the head down to the sides and back, with the hair getting shorter as it goes. This style is incredibly versatile and can be worn by men of all ages, hair types, and face shapes.

How to Get a Mens Haircuts Short Sides Fade

If you're looking to get a mens haircuts short sides fade, you should start by finding a barber who is experienced in this style. It's important to choose a barber who knows how to create a smooth transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Before you go to the barber, decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. You can either show your barber a picture of the style you're aiming for or describe it in detail. You should also decide on how gradual you want your fade to be.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a mens haircuts short sides fade:

  1. Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair.
  2. Show your barber a picture or describe the style you want.
  3. The barber will start by cutting the top of your hair to the desired length.
  4. The barber will then use clippers to create the fade on the sides and back of your head.
  5. The barber will gradually reduce the length of the hair on the sides and back until it blends seamlessly with the longer hair on top.
  6. The barber will then use scissors to blend any remaining harsh lines.
  7. The barber will finish by styling your hair with a product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircuts Short Sides Fade

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircuts short sides fade:

  • Visit your barber every 2-3 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a quality hair product to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant if you do.
  • Cover your hair with a hat or scarf in harsh weather conditions.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts short sides fade:

Is a short sides fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a short sides fade can be adapted to suit all face shapes. Your barber can adjust the length and shape of the haircut to complement your features.

Can a short sides fade be worn with a beard?

Yes, a short sides fade can be worn with a beard. In fact, the two styles complement each other well.

What hair products are best for styling a short sides fade?

There are many hair products that are suitable for styling a short sides fade, including pomade, wax, gel, and clay. Choose a product that suits your hair type and provides the hold you need.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircuts Short Sides Fade

Here are some hair care tips for mens haircuts short sides fade:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how to achieve and maintain a mens haircuts short sides fade, it's time to book an appointment with your barber and try out this classic style.
