Half Up Half Down Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Mother Of The Bride

Written by Luffy Apr 19, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you excited about your daughter's wedding but not sure about what kind of hairstyle to choose for the big day? If you have medium length hair, then half up half down wedding hairstyles can be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is elegant, versatile, and works well with almost any type of dress. In this article, we will give you some tips and ideas on how to create a beautiful half up half down wedding hairstyle for medium length hair.

Description

The half up half down wedding hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that has been popular for many years. This style is perfect for mothers of the bride who want a formal and elegant look without being too over the top. This hairstyle is easy to create and can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences. Half up half down wedding hairstyles are perfect for medium length hair because they allow you to show off your hair's length and texture while still keeping it out of your face.

How to

To create a half up half down wedding hairstyle for medium length hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight or use a flat iron to straighten it.
  3. Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  4. Grab a section of hair from one side of your head and twist it towards the back of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.
  6. Take the remaining hair and curl it using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  7. Once your hair is curled, use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add volume.
  8. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and wrap it around the bobby pins to hide them.
  9. Finish with hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to create a half up half down wedding hairstyle for medium length hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and free from any tangles. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a healthy and shiny look.

Step 2: Blow Dry or Straighten Your Hair

If you have naturally straight hair, then you can skip this step. If not, use a blow dryer or flat iron to straighten your hair. This will make it easier to style and curl.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Decide whether you want a middle or side part. Use a comb to create a straight part down the middle or on the side of your head.

Step 4: Twist and Pin Your Hair

Take a section of hair from one side of your head and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it with bobby pins. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Take the remaining hair and curl it using a curling iron or hot rollers. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face to create a natural-looking wave.

Step 6: Tease Your Hair

Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add volume. This will give your hair a more formal and elegant look.

Step 7: Hide the Bobby Pins

Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and wrap it around the bobby pins to hide them. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

Step 8: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating a half up half down wedding hairstyle for medium length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Experiment with different types of curls to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair more body and texture.
  • Add accessories such as flowers, pearls, or a tiara to make your hairstyle more glamorous.
  • Consider getting a trim or a haircut a few weeks before the wedding to ensure that your hair is healthy and looks its best.

FAQ

What kind of hair accessories can I use with a half up half down wedding hairstyle?

You can use a variety of hair accessories such as flowers, pearls, rhinestones, and a tiara. Make sure to choose accessories that match your dress and personal style.

What type of curling iron should I use for a half up half down wedding hairstyle?

You can use a curling iron with a one-inch barrel to create loose waves or a half-inch barrel to create tighter curls. Experiment with different sizes to find the one that works best for your hair.

How can I make my half up half down wedding hairstyle last all day?

Use a good quality hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair. If you have fine hair, consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair more body and texture.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair in the weeks leading up to the wedding. This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Eating a healthy diet and getting enough sleep can also help improve the condition of your hair.

With these tips and ideas, you can create a beautiful and elegant half up half down wedding hairstyle for medium length hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the one that works best for you. Have fun and enjoy the wedding!
