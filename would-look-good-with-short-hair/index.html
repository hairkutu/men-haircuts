What Would Look Good With Short Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 04, 2023 · 12 min read

Short hair can be a bold and empowering choice for women, but it can also be daunting to figure out what styles and accessories would complement it best. In this guide, we’ll give you some tips on what would look good with short hair, including hairstyles, makeup, and fashion choices. So if you’re thinking of making the chop or already have short locks, read on!

Description

Short hair can be a versatile and low-maintenance option for women who want a bold and stylish look. However, it can be challenging to know what would complement your short hair, especially if you’re used to having longer locks. In this guide, we’ll provide some inspiration for hairstyles, accessories, and fashion choices that would look good with short hair. Whether you’re looking for a casual, edgy, or elegant look, we’ve got you covered!

How to Style Short Hair

One of the benefits of having short hair is that it’s easy to style and maintain. However, that doesn’t mean you can’t experiment with different looks and textures. Here are some hairstyles that would look good with short hair:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. You can opt for a textured, messy look with layers and bangs, or a sleek, polished style with a side part and tapered sides. A pixie cut can also be accessorized with headbands, clips, and hats.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another versatile and timeless hairstyle that would look good with short hair. You can go for a blunt, chin-length bob, a layered, textured bob, or a asymmetrical bob with a shorter back and longer front. A bob cut can be styled with waves, curls, or straightened for a sleek look.

3. Shaved Head

A shaved head is a bold and daring hairstyle that can be both edgy and elegant. You can choose to shave your entire head or just one side or back. A shaved head can be styled with bold makeup, statement earrings, and chunky necklaces for a fierce and confident look.

Accessories for Short Hair

When it comes to accessories, less is often more when you have short hair. However, there are some items that can add a pop of color or texture to your look:

1. Headbands

Headbands are a simple and easy way to add some flair to your short hair. You can opt for a thin, metallic headband for a minimalist look, or a thick, colorful headband for a more playful vibe. Headbands can also help keep your hair out of your face and add some volume to your roots.

2. Hair Clips

Hair clips are another accessory that can add some sparkle or texture to your short hair. You can go for a simple, silver clip for a chic look, or a bejeweled, oversized clip for a statement piece. Hair clips can also be used to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle or to secure your bangs.

3. Hats

Hats are a stylish and practical accessory for short hair, especially during the colder months. You can choose from a variety of styles, such as beanies, fedoras, berets, or bucket hats, depending on your outfit and mood. Hats can also help protect your scalp and hair from the sun and wind.

Fashion Choices for Short Hair

Your fashion choices can also complement your short hair and enhance your overall look. Here are some tips for dressing up with short hair:

1. Statement Earrings

Statement earrings can draw attention to your face and add some glamour to your short hair. You can go for bold, oversized hoops, tassels, or chandeliers, depending on your outfit and occasion. Statement earrings can also be paired with a simple, monochrome outfit for a chic and effortless look.

2. High Necklines

High necklines can elongate your neck and create a sophisticated silhouette with short hair. You can go for turtlenecks, mock necks, or collared shirts, depending on your style and mood. High necklines can also be paired with statement earrings or bold lipstick for a striking effect.

3. Tailored Jackets

Tailored jackets can add some structure and elegance to your short hair. You can opt for a classic, black blazer, a denim jacket, or a leather moto jacket, depending on your outfit and occasion. Tailored jackets can also be paired with high-waisted pants or skirts for a polished and professional look.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair may require less maintenance than longer hair, but it still needs some TLC to stay healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

1. Moisturize Your Scalp

Short hair can expose your scalp to the sun, wind, and pollutants, so it’s essential to keep it moisturized and protected. You can use a scalp oil, a leave-in conditioner, or a hair mask to nourish your scalp and hair. Massage the product into your scalp with your fingertips and let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you style your short hair with hot tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum. Heat can damage your hair cuticles and make them brittle and prone to breakage. Apply the heat protectant evenly over your hair and comb through to distribute it.

3. Trim Regularly

Short hair may not need as much trimming as longer hair, but it still needs to be maintained to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and styled. You can also ask your stylist for tips on how to style your short hair at home.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my short hair?

Yes, you can dye your short hair to change its color or add some highlights. However, make sure to choose a hair dye that is suitable for your hair type and scalp and follow the instructions carefully. You can also ask your stylist for advice on which hair dye would look good with your short hair.

2. Can I wear a ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail with short hair, but it may require some creativity and accessories. You can use a hair tie or a scrunchie to gather your hair at the back or on the side and create a mini ponytail. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair and add some volume.

3. How can I make my short hair look fuller?

You can make your short hair look fuller by using volumizing products, such as mousse, spray, or powder. Apply the product to your roots and massage your scalp to create some lift and texture. You can also blow-dry your hair upside down or use a round brush to add some volume and shape.

Now that you’ve got some inspiration and tips on what would look good with short hair, it’s time to experiment and have fun with your new look. Remember to choose hairstyles, accessories, and fashion choices that make you feel confident and empowered. Short hair may be a bold and daring choice, but it can also be a stylish and low-maintenance option for women of all ages and backgrounds.
