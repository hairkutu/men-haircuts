Men s Fade Haircuts Short On Top A Guide To The Latest Trend In Men s Hairstyles

Written by Bardi Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that's stylish and easy to maintain? Then look no further than the men's fade haircuts short on top trend. This popular hairstyle is versatile, modern, and suits all hair types and face shapes. Whether you're looking for a low fade, mid fade, or high fade, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about this chic hairstyle.

What is a Fade Haircut?

A fade haircut is a hairstyle that gradually fades from short to long, creating a smooth and seamless transition. The hair is typically cut short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer. This hairstyle is popular among men because it's low maintenance, stylish, and suits all face shapes.

How to Get a Men's Fade Haircut Short on Top

If you're planning to get a men's fade haircut short on top, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get this hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade Type

There are three types of fades: low fade, mid fade, and high fade. The low fade starts near the ear and gradually fades to the neckline, while the mid fade starts at the temple and gradually fades into the neckline. The high fade starts at the top of the head and gradually fades into the neckline. Choose the fade type that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of the Top

The length of the top will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a shorter top, while if you have a square face, go for a longer top. Discuss with your hairstylist to determine the length that's right for you.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Your hairstylist will trim the top of your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors or a razor to add texture and volume to the hair.

Step 4: Fade the Sides and Back

Your hairstylist will use clippers to fade the sides and back of your hair. They'll start with a longer guard and gradually switch to a shorter guard to create a seamless transition.

Step 5: Blend the Fade

To create a smooth and seamless transition, your hairstylist will blend the fade with the top of your hair. They may use scissors or a comb to blend the hair.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Fade Haircut Short on Top

Maintaining a men's fade haircut short on top is easy. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the fade and trim the top.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a styling product to the top of your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer as it can damage your hair.

FAQs

1. What face shapes suit a men's fade haircut short on top?

A men's fade haircut short on top suits all face shapes, but the length of the top should be adjusted according to the face shape.

2. How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain a men's fade haircut short on top?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the fade and trim the top.

3. Can I style my men's fade haircut short on top in different ways?

Yes, you can style your men's fade haircut short on top in different ways. You can slick it back, comb it over, or add texture and volume.

Hair Care for Men's Fade Haircuts Short on Top

Men's fade haircuts short on top require minimal hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a styling product to the top of your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the fade and trim the top.

In conclusion, men's fade haircuts short on top are a popular and stylish hairstyle that suits all face shapes and hair types. By following the steps outlined in this guide and maintaining proper hair care, you can rock this chic hairstyle with confidence.
