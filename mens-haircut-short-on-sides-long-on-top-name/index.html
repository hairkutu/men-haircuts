Description

The mens haircut short on sides long on top name is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes. As the name suggests, this hairstyle features short hair on the sides and long hair on the top. The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a stylish and trendy look without spending too much time on styling their hair.

How to Style Mens Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top Name

To style the mens haircut short on sides long on top name, you will need a few basic hair styling products, including a styling gel or wax, a comb or brush, and a hair dryer. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it is damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel as this can damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling gel or wax to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the top section. Use your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while using a comb or brush to create the desired style. For a sleek and polished look, use a round brush to smooth out your hair. For a messy and textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair while blow drying it.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step Guide

If you are unsure about how to style the mens haircut short on sides long on top name, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in styling the mens haircut short on sides long on top name is to choose the right length for your hair. The length of the hair on the top should be longer than the sides, but it should also be manageable and easy to style.

Step 2: Decide on the Style

There are several different styles you can create with the mens haircut short on sides long on top name. You can opt for a slicked-back look, a messy and textured look, or a side-swept style. Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure to wash and condition it. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling gel or wax to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the top section. Use your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top Name

To maintain the mens haircut short on sides long on top name, here are a few tips to follow:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, especially when it is wet.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shape is best for the mens haircut short on sides long on top name?

The mens haircut short on sides long on top name suits most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with oval or square faces.

Can I style the mens haircut short on sides long on top name with curly hair?

Yes, you can style the mens haircut short on sides long on top name with curly hair. However, you may need to use additional hair styling products to tame your curls and create the desired style.

How long should the hair on the top be for the mens haircut short on sides long on top name?

The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. However, it is important to keep the hair on the sides short to create a contrast.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top Name

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily maintain your mens haircut short on sides long on top name and keep it looking stylish and trendy. So, go ahead and try this hairstyle for a fresh and modern look!
