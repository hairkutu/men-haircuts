How To Style Short Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short thick wavy hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style it. If you want to make the most of your locks, you need to know exactly how to style them. This guide will provide you with all the information you need to style your short thick wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short thick wavy hair is hair that is shorter in length and thicker in texture than other hair types. It has a natural wave to it, which can be both a blessing and a curse. While the wave adds volume and texture to the hair, it can also make it difficult to style.

How To

To style short thick wavy hair, you need to start with the right haircut. A layered haircut can help to reduce the bulk of the hair while adding movement and texture. You should also consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help to frame your face and balance out the volume of your hair.

Products

The right hair products can make a huge difference when it comes to styling short thick wavy hair. Here are some of the best products for this hair type:

  • Texturizing spray: This can help to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Mousse: This can help to define your waves and hold your style in place.
  • Hair oil: This can help to tame frizz and add shine to your hair.

Tools

When it comes to styling short thick wavy hair, you need the right tools. Here are some of the best tools for this hair type:

  • Curling iron: This can help to define your waves and add volume to your hair.
  • Straightening iron: This can help to smooth out your hair and create a sleek look.
  • Hair dryer: This can help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's how to style short thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to enhance your natural wave pattern.
  4. Use a curling iron to define your waves. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap sections of hair around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to tame any frizz.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your short thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid brushing your hair once it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different products and tools to find what works best for your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I straighten my short thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your short thick wavy hair using a straightening iron. However, keep in mind that excessive heat styling can damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often.

Can I wear my short thick wavy hair up?

Yes, you can wear your short thick wavy hair up. A messy bun or braid can be a great way to style your hair on days when you don't have time to wash it.

How often should I wash my short thick wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with short thick wavy hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others prefer to wash it every few days. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short thick wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short thick wavy hair like a pro. Experiment with different products and tools to find what works best for your hair, and don't be afraid to try new styles!
