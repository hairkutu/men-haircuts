Hairstyles With Short Layers On Top

Written by Robby Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are looking for a hairstyle that will give you a fresh and trendy look, then short layers on top might be the perfect choice for you. This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit any hair type and face shape. In this article, we will discuss different hairstyles with short layers on top, and provide you with useful tips on how to maintain your hair.

Description

Short layers on top refer to a hairstyle that has shorter hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are kept longer. This style adds volume and texture to your hair, and can be worn in a variety of ways. It is a popular choice for men and women of all ages, and can be easily customized to suit your personal style.

How To

If you want to try out a hairstyle with short layers on top, here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before you make any drastic changes to your hair, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can give you advice on which style will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step 2: Choose a Style

Once you have consulted with a hairstylist, you can choose a style that suits your preferences. There are many options to choose from, including a short layered pixie cut, a choppy bob, or a layered shaggy hairstyle.

Step 3: Cut and Style Your Hair

After you have chosen a style, your hairstylist will cut your hair to create short layers on top. They will then style your hair using products that will add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some useful tips on how to maintain your hair with short layers on top:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to define the layers and add texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layers on top:

1. Can short layers on top work for all hair types?

Yes, short layers on top can be adapted to suit any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often do I need to get a trim?

It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

3. Can I style my hair differently with short layers on top?

Yes, short layers on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including messy, sleek, and curly styles.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as this is less damaging than using a brush.

With these tips and ideas, you can confidently go for a hairstyle with short layers on top. This style is easy to maintain and can give you a fresh and trendy look.
