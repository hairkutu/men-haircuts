Square Face Shape With Short Hair A Guide For 2023

If you have a square face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we will be discussing how to rock short hair with a square face shape in 2023. From how to determine your face shape to tips on hair care, we've got everything you need to know to look your best.

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for square face shapes, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a closer look at your face shape. A square face shape has a strong jawline and a forehead that is roughly the same width as the jawline.
  3. If you're still unsure, try measuring your face. A square face shape will have equal length and width.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to move on to the fun part - choosing a haircut!

Short Haircuts for Square Face Shapes

Short haircuts can be incredibly flattering for square face shapes. Here are some of our top picks:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short haircut that looks great on square face shapes. The short layers create a soft, feminine look that balances out the strong jawline.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for square face shapes. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers that fall around the jawline to soften the face.

Asymmetrical Cut

If you're looking for a more edgy haircut, consider an asymmetrical cut. This style features longer layers on one side of the head and shorter layers on the other, creating a dramatic look that flatters square face shapes.

How to Style Short Hair for Square Face Shapes

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your square face shape, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

Choosing the right products for your hair type is essential when it comes to styling short hair. Opt for a lightweight mousse or gel that won't weigh your hair down.

Blow Dry Correctly

When blow drying your hair, make sure to use a round brush to create volume at the roots. Focus the blow dryer on the roots, then work your way down to the ends.

Experiment with Parting

Changing up your part can completely transform your look. If you normally part your hair in the middle, try a side part instead.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair with a Square Face Shape

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, make sure to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Use Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so make sure to protect it with a hat or hair sunscreen when spending time outdoors.

FAQs About Square Face Shapes and Short Hair

What is the best short haircut for a square face shape?

Some of the best short haircuts for square face shapes include the pixie cut, bob cut, and asymmetrical cut.

Can square face shapes pull off bangs?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to soften a square face shape. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that fall just above the eyebrows.

How often should I wash my short hair?

Short hair tends to get greasy faster than longer hair, so you may need to wash it more often. However, everyone's hair is different, so it's best to experiment to find out what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair with a Square Face Shape

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your short haircut looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for a formula that is sulfate-free and contains natural ingredients.

Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help keep it soft and healthy. Use a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Be gentle when styling your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to rock short hair with a square face shape in 2023. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, use the right products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
