What Hairstyle Will Make Me Look Thinner

Written by Robby Jun 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to make your face look slimmer? One easy solution is to try out a new hairstyle. By choosing the right cut and style, you can create the illusion of a more slender face. Keep reading to find out what hairstyle will make me look thinner.

Description

When it comes to hairstyles that make you look thinner, the key is to add volume on top and keep the sides flat. This creates a more oval-shaped face, which is often considered more slender. Additionally, having longer layers around the face can help to frame the face and draw attention away from any areas you may want to minimize.

How To

To create a hairstyle that makes you look thinner, you'll need to choose a cut that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs. This will create a more elongated shape and draw attention away from your cheeks. If you have a square face, try a layered bob that hits just above the jawline. This will soften the angles of your face and make it appear more slender.

Step By Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape.

3. Consult with a stylist to determine the best cut and style for you.

4. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face to create volume on top.

5. Consider adding bangs to frame your face.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle to make you look thinner, keep in mind that it's all about creating balance. You want to add volume on top to create an oval-shaped face, but you also want to keep the sides flat to avoid adding width. Additionally, it's important to choose a cut that you feel comfortable with and can maintain on a daily basis.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I want to look thinner?

A: Yes! Long hair can be slimming, as long as it's layered and styled correctly. Consider adding face-framing layers and side-swept bangs to create a more slender look.

Q: What if I have a small forehead?

A: If you have a small forehead, consider adding volume on top with a pompadour or quiff style. This will create the illusion of a larger forehead and help to balance out your face.

Hair Care

When maintaining a hairstyle that makes you look thinner, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-conditioned. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Additionally, use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.

Now that you know what hairstyle will make you look thinner, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new cut and style. With a little bit of experimentation, you're sure to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
