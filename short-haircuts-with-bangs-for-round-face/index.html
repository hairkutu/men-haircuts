Short Haircuts With Bangs For Round Face

Written by Daniel Sep 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what kind of haircut would suit you best. Short haircuts with bangs are a great option and can help to elongate your face and create more definition. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right short haircut with bangs for your round face, as well as some tips for hair care and maintenance.

Description

A round face has soft curves and a circular shape. The width and length of the face are roughly the same, and the jawline is not very defined. Short haircuts with bangs can help to balance out the face by adding height and volume to the crown, creating the illusion of a longer face shape.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut with Bangs

When choosing a short haircut with bangs for a round face, it's important to consider the length and style of the cut. Here are some tips:

1. Length

Short haircuts that fall just below the chin or above the shoulders are great options. These lengths will help to elongate the face and create more definition. Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make the face appear wider.

2. Style

Layered haircuts are great for a round face, as they can create movement and texture. Side-swept bangs can also help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs that can make the face appear wider.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting a short haircut with bangs for a round face:

1. Consultation

Before getting your haircut, schedule a consultation with your stylist. Discuss your face shape and the type of haircut you are looking for. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and style for your face.

2. Shampoo and Conditioner

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to gently dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously.

3. Cutting

Your stylist will begin by cutting your hair to the desired length. They will then add layers and texture to create movement and volume. Finally, they will cut your bangs to the desired length and style.

4. Styling

Your stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, round brush, and other styling tools. They may also recommend products to help you maintain your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut with bangs:

1. Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Schedule appointments with your stylist every 4-6 weeks.

2. Product Use

Use styling products that are suitable for your hair type and style. For example, use volumizing products to add volume to your hair, and smoothing products to control frizz.

3. Heat Protection

Use heat protection products when using heat styling tools such as a blow dryer or flat iron. This will help to protect your hair from damage and keep it healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face and create more definition.

Q: What kind of products should I use for my short haircut with bangs?

A: Use products that are suitable for your hair type and style. For example, use volumizing products to add volume to your hair, and smoothing products to control frizz.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut with bangs trimmed?

A: Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Schedule appointments with your stylist every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Wash and Condition

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to gently dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protection product to help protect your hair.

3. Protect from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protection product to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of fruits and vegetables, as well as protein and healthy fats. This will help to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

In conclusion, short haircuts with bangs can be a great option for a round face. By choosing the right length and style, you can create the illusion of a longer face shape and more defined jawline. Remember to maintain your haircut with regular trims and use products that are suitable for your hair type and style. By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.
