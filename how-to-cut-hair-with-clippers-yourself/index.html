How To Cut Hair With Clippers Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save money and time by cutting your hair at home, using clippers can be a great option. With a little practice and the right technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut without leaving your house. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair with clippers yourself, including tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair with Clippers Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Hair with Clippers Yourself

Before you start cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need:

  • Clippers with different guard sizes
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • Optional: a cape or towel to cover your clothes

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Choose the right guard size: Depending on the length you want to achieve, select the appropriate guard size. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  2. Wash and dry your hair: It's easier to cut clean and dry hair, so make sure to wash and dry your hair before you start.
  3. Part your hair: Use a comb to part your hair where you want to start cutting.
  4. Start cutting: Hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and start cutting from the bottom, moving upward. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even. Repeat until you've cut all the hair in that section.
  5. Move to the next section: Use the same technique to cut the hair in the next section. Make sure to blend the sections together for a seamless look.
  6. Cut the sides and back: Use a shorter guard size for the sides and back of your head. Start from the bottom and move upward, making sure to blend the sections together.
  7. Use scissors for the top: For the top of your head, use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start from the back and move forward, using the comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even.
  8. Trim the edges: Use scissors to trim any stray hairs around the ears and neckline.
  9. Check your work: Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots or missed hairs. Make any necessary adjustments.
  10. Clean up: Use a brush or towel to remove any loose hairs from your head and clothes. Wash and disinfect your clippers.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even.
  • Blend the sections together for a seamless look.
  • Use scissors for the top of your head.
  • Trim the edges with scissors.
  • Check your work with a mirror.
  • Clean and disinfect your clippers after use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It takes practice and the right technique, but it's possible to achieve a professional-looking cut at home.

What guard size should I use?

The guard size you use depends on the length you want to achieve. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

What do I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always make adjustments or even out any uneven spots. Use scissors or a shorter guard size to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your cut.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Cutting your hair with clippers yourself can be a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips and hair care advice to get the best results.
