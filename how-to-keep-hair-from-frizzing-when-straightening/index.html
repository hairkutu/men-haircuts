How To Keep Hair From Frizzing When Straightening

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on how to keep hair from frizzing when straightening. Frizzy hair is a common problem that many people face, especially when using heat styling tools like flat irons. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve smooth and shiny hair without any frizz. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Hair from Frizzing When Straightening
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair from Frizzing
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

How to Keep Hair from Frizzing When Straightening

The first thing to consider when straightening your hair is the quality of your flat iron. Invest in a high-quality flat iron that has ceramic or tourmaline plates. These materials help to distribute heat evenly, preventing hot spots that can cause frizz. Also, make sure that your flat iron has adjustable heat settings that you can control based on your hair type.

Before straightening, make sure that your hair is completely dry. Wet hair is more vulnerable to damage and can cause frizz. Use a blow dryer or air dry your hair before straightening.

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before straightening. This will create a barrier between your hair and the heat, preventing damage and frizz. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

When straightening, use small sections of hair and run the flat iron over each section slowly. Don't rush the process as this can cause more damage and frizz. Also, avoid going over the same section of hair multiple times as this can cause heat damage.

Once you have finished straightening your hair, apply a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and cause frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to keep hair from frizzing when straightening:

  1. Invest in a high-quality flat iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates.
  2. Make sure your hair is completely dry before straightening.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  4. Use small sections of hair and run the flat iron over each section slowly.
  5. Apply a lightweight hair oil or serum once you have finished straightening.

Tips to Prevent Hair from Frizzing

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair from frizzing:

  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my hair every day?

A: It's not recommended to straighten your hair every day as this can cause damage and breakage. Give your hair a break and try different hairstyles to avoid heat damage.

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it's wet?

A: No, it's not recommended to use hot tools on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for hair care if you have frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently detangle your hair when wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often and let your hair air dry when possible.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair from frizzing when straightening and achieve a smooth and shiny look. Remember to use high-quality products and take care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Happy styling!
