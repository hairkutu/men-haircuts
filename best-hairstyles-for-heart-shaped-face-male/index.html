What is a Heart Shaped Face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. It is also known as an inverted triangle face shape. This facial shape is highly sought after in the fashion industry, as it is considered to be the most attractive. However, choosing the right hairstyle for this facial shape can be tricky.

Best Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face Male

The best hairstyles for heart shaped face male are those that balance out the width of the forehead and cheekbones, while creating the illusion of a wider jawline. Here are some hairstyles that work well for this facial shape:

Short and Textured

A short and textured haircut can work wonders for a heart shaped face. This style creates the illusion of a wider jawline, and draws attention away from the forehead. A textured cut adds volume to the hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

Side Part

A side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for almost any facial shape, including heart shaped faces. This style adds volume to the hair, and creates the illusion of a wider jawline. It also draws attention away from the forehead, making it look smaller.

Long and Layered

A long and layered haircut can also work well for heart shaped faces. This style adds volume to the hair, and creates the illusion of a wider jawline. Layers also add movement and texture to the hair, making it look more interesting.

Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can work well for heart shaped faces. This style draws attention away from the forehead, and creates the illusion of a wider jawline. It also makes the hair look thicker and fuller.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face, it is important to consider the width of the forehead and cheekbones, as well as the length of the chin. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead, such as a fringe or bangs.
  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the hair at the sides, which will balance out the width of the forehead and cheekbones.
  • Consider hairstyles that create the illusion of a wider jawline, such as a side part or textured haircut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make the chin look narrower, such as a chin-length bob or a center part.
  • Look for hairstyles that add movement and texture to the hair, which will make it look more interesting.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here is a step by step guide to styling your hair for a heart shaped face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or wax.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a comb to create a side part, if desired.
  5. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and in shape.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product, such as a mousse or wax, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush, to add volume and texture.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most attractive hairstyle for a heart shaped face male?

The most attractive hairstyle for a heart shaped face male is one that balances out the width of the forehead and cheekbones, while creating the illusion of a wider jawline. Short and textured hairstyles, side parts, long and layered haircuts, and buzz cuts all work well for this facial shape.

Can a heart shaped face male wear a fringe?

It is best for a heart shaped face male to avoid a fringe, as it adds volume to the forehead and can make it look wider. Instead, opt for a side part or textured haircut, which will draw attention away from the forehead and create the illusion of a wider jawline.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my hairstyle?

It is recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair trimmed and in shape. This will help to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Conclusion

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a heart shaped face male can be tricky, but it is not impossible. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can find a hairstyle that works well for your facial shape, and maintain it with ease. Remember to visit your hairstylist regularly, use the right hair care products, and style your hair with confidence.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it is also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and shiny hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat, by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
