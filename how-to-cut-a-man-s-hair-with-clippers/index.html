How To Cut A Man s Hair With Clippers Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting men's hair with clippers is a great way to save time and money while still achieving a professional-looking haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily give yourself or someone else a fresh new haircut. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to cut a man's hair with clippers.

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. These include:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Clippers guards
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle
  • Towel

Make sure your clippers are clean and well-oiled before you begin.

Step 2: Decide on the Haircut

Before you start cutting, decide on the style of haircut you want to achieve. You can find inspiration from magazines, online tutorials, or by consulting with your client.

Step 3: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it thoroughly with a towel or hairdryer.

Step 4: Section the Hair

Use hair clips to section the hair into manageable sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 5: Choose the Right Clippers Guard

Choose the right clippers guard based on the length of hair you want to cut. For shorter hair, use a smaller guard, and for longer hair, use a larger guard.

Step 6: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair with the clippers, working against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 7: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back of the head. Be sure to blend the hair evenly for a natural look.

Step 8: Clean up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the hairline at the back of the neck. Use a smaller guard for a more precise cut.

Step 9: Check for Uneven Spots

Check for any uneven spots or missed areas. Use the clippers or scissors to fix any mistakes.

Step 10: Style the Hair

Use a comb and styling products to style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a bigger guards and work your way down to a smaller guard for a more gradual cut.
  • Use the mirror to check the back of the head while cutting.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep the hair damp while cutting.
  • Take breaks if necessary to avoid fatigue.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

How often should you cut a man's hair?

It depends on the style of haircut and the rate of hair growth. Generally, a man's hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks.

How do you clean and maintain your clippers?

Clean your clippers after every use with a brush and disinfectant spray. Oil the blades regularly to keep them sharp and prevent rust.

How do you fix a mistake while cutting hair?

Use scissors or clippers to fix any mistakes. If the mistake is too severe, you may need to cut the hair shorter or consult a professional.

Hair Care

To maintain a healthy head of hair, it's important to:

  • Wash and condition the hair regularly
  • Use heat protectant products before using styling tools
  • Avoid using too much product
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Drink plenty of water

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

Conclusion

Cutting a man's hair with clippers may seem daunting at first, but with practice and the right tools, it can be easy and even enjoyable. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
