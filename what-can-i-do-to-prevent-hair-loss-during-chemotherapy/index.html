What Can I Do To Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy

Written by William May 20, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Chemotherapy is a common treatment for cancer, but it comes with a range of side effects. One of the most common side effects of chemotherapy is hair loss. However, there are steps you can take to prevent or minimize hair loss during chemotherapy. In this blog post, we will discuss what you can do to prevent hair loss during chemotherapy.

Table of Contents

  • Understanding Chemotherapy
  • Hair Loss During Chemotherapy
  • How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy
  • Hair Care During Chemotherapy
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy is a treatment that involves the use of drugs to destroy cancer cells. It can be given orally, through a vein, or as an injection. Chemotherapy drugs travel throughout the body and kill cancer cells. However, they can also affect healthy cells, including hair follicles.

Hair Loss During Chemotherapy

Hair loss during chemotherapy is a common side effect. It can affect any part of the body where hair grows, including the scalp, eyebrows, eyelashes, and pubic area. Hair loss can occur gradually or suddenly, and it can be temporary or permanent.

Chemotherapy drugs affect hair follicles in different ways. Some drugs cause hair to fall out in large clumps, while others cause hair to thin gradually. The extent of hair loss can also depend on the dosage and frequency of chemotherapy treatments.

How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy

Although hair loss is a common side effect of chemotherapy, there are steps you can take to prevent or minimize it. Here are some tips:

1. Use Cold Caps

Cold caps are designed to reduce blood flow to the scalp, which can help protect hair follicles from chemotherapy drugs. They are worn before, during, and after chemotherapy treatments. Cold caps are available in different sizes and can be rented from some hospitals or cancer centers.

2. Opt for a Scalp Cooling System

A scalp cooling system is a device that circulates cold air or liquid to the scalp during chemotherapy treatments. It works by reducing the temperature of the scalp, which can help protect hair follicles. Scalp cooling systems are available at some hospitals or cancer centers.

3. Consider Hair Growth Products

There are several products available that claim to help prevent hair loss during chemotherapy. These products typically contain ingredients that stimulate hair growth or protect hair follicles. However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before using these products to ensure they are safe and effective.

4. Avoid Harsh Hair Treatments

During chemotherapy treatments, it is important to avoid harsh hair treatments that can damage hair follicles. This includes coloring, perming, or using hot styling tools. It is also recommended to use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid further damage to the hair.

Hair Care During Chemotherapy

Even if you take steps to prevent hair loss during chemotherapy, it is still important to take care of your hair during treatment. Here are some tips:

1. Be Gentle

During chemotherapy treatments, hair can be fragile and prone to breakage. It is important to be gentle when washing and styling hair. Use a soft-bristled brush or wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling on hair.

2. Moisturize

Chemotherapy drugs can cause dryness and itching of the scalp. It is important to moisturize the scalp and hair using a mild, fragrance-free moisturizer. Avoid using products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals that can irritate the scalp.

3. Cover Up

If you experience hair loss during chemotherapy, you may want to consider covering up with a wig, hat, or scarf. This can help protect your scalp from the sun and keep you comfortable during treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will I Lose All of My Hair During Chemotherapy?

Not everyone who undergoes chemotherapy will lose all of their hair. The extent of hair loss can depend on the type of chemotherapy drugs used, the dosage and frequency of treatment, and individual factors such as age and health.

2. Will My Hair Grow Back After Chemotherapy?

Hair loss during chemotherapy is usually temporary, and hair will typically grow back after treatment is completed. However, it can take several months for hair to grow back to its original length and thickness.

3. Are There Any Long-Term Effects of Chemotherapy on Hair?

In some cases, chemotherapy can cause permanent hair loss. This is more common with high-dose treatments or treatments that involve radiation therapy. However, the risk of permanent hair loss is relatively low, and most people will experience regrowth after treatment.

4. Can I Color My Hair During Chemotherapy?

It is not recommended to color your hair during chemotherapy treatments. The chemicals in hair dye can damage hair follicles and make hair more prone to breakage. It is best to wait until after treatment is completed to color your hair.

5. Can I Use Hair Extensions During Chemotherapy?

It is not recommended to use hair extensions during chemotherapy treatments. Hair extensions can put additional stress on hair follicles and increase the risk of breakage or hair loss.

In conclusion, there are steps you can take to prevent or minimize hair loss during chemotherapy. It is important to discuss your options with a healthcare professional and take care of your hair during treatment. Remember that hair loss during chemotherapy is a common side effect, and there is no need to feel ashamed or embarrassed. Focus on your health and well-being, and know that your hair will grow back in time.
