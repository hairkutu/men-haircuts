Can You Cut Curly Hair With Clippers

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering whether you can use clippers to cut your curly hair? If so, you're not alone. Many people with curly hair are hesitant to use clippers because they're not sure if it's the right tool for the job. The good news is that you can use clippers to cut curly hair, but there are a few things you need to keep in mind.

Description

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, and finding the right tool to cut it can be a challenge. Clippers are often associated with straight hair, but they can be used on curly hair as well. However, it's important to use the right technique and to be careful when using clippers on curly hair.

How to

If you're new to cutting hair with clippers, it's important to start with a clean, dry head of hair. Use a comb to separate the hair into sections, and then use the clippers to cut each section. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary. Be sure to move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth, and use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting curly hair with clippers:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a comb to separate your hair into sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Choose your guard

Choose a guard that's appropriate for your hair length. If you're not sure which one to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary.

Step 4: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers, moving in the direction of the hair growth. Use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 5: Check your work

After you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even and well-groomed.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you cut curly hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary.
  • Move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth.
  • Use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Check your work with a mirror to ensure that your hair is even and well-groomed.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair with clippers:

Can you cut curly hair with clippers?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut curly hair. However, it's important to use the right technique and to be careful when using clippers on curly hair.

What guard should I use to cut curly hair with clippers?

Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary. The guard you choose will depend on your hair length and the style you're going for.

How do I avoid cutting too much hair when using clippers on curly hair?

Use a light touch and move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair and ensure that your hair looks even and well-groomed.

Hair Care

When cutting curly hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair healthy and looking great.
