How Long Is A Number 3 Haircut In Cm

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're getting a haircut, you might be wondering how long a number 3 haircut is in cm. A number 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, clean look that's easy to maintain. It's also a great option for women who want a short, pixie-style haircut. In this article, we'll explain how long a number 3 haircut is in cm and give you some tips on how to care for your hair after your haircut.

Description

A number 3 haircut is cut with a clipper guard that leaves the hair 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. This means that the hair will be relatively short, but not completely shaved. It's a good option for people who want a short, low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and put-together.

How to

If you're getting a number 3 haircut, you can expect your hairstylist or barber to use clippers with a number 3 guard attached. They'll start at the bottom of your head and work their way up, cutting the hair evenly all around your head. They may use scissors to clean up the edges and make sure the haircut looks neat and tidy.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 3 haircut:

  1. Choose a reputable hairstylist or barber
  2. Decide if you want any specific styling or shaping
  3. Ask for a number 3 haircut
  4. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist or barber works their magic
  5. Ask for any touch-ups or adjustments if necessary
  6. Pay and tip your hairstylist or barber

Tips

After your number 3 haircut, you'll want to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow-drying or straightening
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Consider using a styling product, like wax or gel, to add some texture or hold to your hair

FAQ

Here are some common questions about number 3 haircuts:

What other haircuts are similar to a number 3?

A number 4 or 5 haircut is slightly longer than a number 3, while a number 2 is slightly shorter. A buzz cut is much shorter, with the hair shaved close to the scalp.

Can I get a number 3 haircut with scissors?

It's possible to cut hair to a specific length using scissors, but clippers with a guard are typically used for number 3 haircuts to ensure an even length all around.

Will a number 3 haircut suit me?

A number 3 haircut is a versatile style that can suit most face shapes and hair types. If you're not sure if it will work for you, ask your hairstylist or barber for their opinion.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important, no matter what style you have. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow-drying or straightening
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Use a styling product, like wax or gel, to add some texture or hold to your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know how long a number 3 haircut is in cm, you're ready to head to the salon or barber shop and get your new look. Remember to take care of your hair after your haircut to keep it looking great!
