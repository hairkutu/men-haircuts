Is It Better To Cut Hair Curly Or Straight

Written by Robby Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, there are a lot of things to consider. One of the most common questions people have is whether it's better to cut hair curly or straight. In this blog post, we'll explore the pros and cons of both options and help you decide which one is right for you.

Description

Cutting curly hair is different from cutting straight hair. Curly hair is more textured and has a different shape when dry than when wet. This makes it important for the stylist to have experience with cutting curly hair. On the other hand, cutting straight hair is easier because it's easier to see the lines and texture of the hair.

How to Cut Curly Hair

If you're looking to cut curly hair, it's important to find a stylist who has experience with curly hair. They should understand the texture of your hair and be able to cut it in a way that enhances your natural curls. It's also important to cut curly hair when it's dry, as this will give the stylist a better idea of how the curls will look once the hair is styled.

How to Cut Straight Hair

Cutting straight hair is easier and can be done when the hair is wet. It's important to make sure the hair is evenly wet before cutting, as this will ensure that the hair is cut evenly. Straight hair can be cut in layers or bluntly, depending on the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

How to Cut Curly Hair

  1. Wash and dry the hair.
  2. Section the hair into small sections.
  3. Cut each section of hair individually, starting at the bottom.
  4. Cut the hair dry to see how the curls will look once the hair is styled.
  5. Shape the hair to create the desired look.

How to Cut Straight Hair

  1. Wash and evenly wet the hair.
  2. Section the hair into small sections.
  3. Cut each section of hair individually, starting at the bottom.
  4. Cut the hair evenly to create the desired look.
  5. Shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

  • Make sure to cut the hair dry to see how the curls will look once the hair is styled.
  • Cut each section of hair individually to ensure that the curls are cut evenly.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying curly hair to enhance the curls.
  • Avoid using a razor when cutting curly hair, as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Cutting Straight Hair

  • Make sure the hair is evenly wet before cutting.
  • Cut each section of hair individually to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
  • Use a razor to add texture to straight hair.
  • Blunt cuts work well for those with fine hair, while layers work well for those with thick hair.

FAQ

Can you cut curly hair when it's wet?

It's not recommended to cut curly hair when it's wet because the curls will look different once the hair is dry. Cutting curly hair when it's dry will give the stylist a better idea of how the curls will look once the hair is styled.

Can you cut straight hair when it's dry?

It's not recommended to cut straight hair when it's dry because it can cause split ends. Cutting straight hair when it's wet will ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

How often should you get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care

Whether you have curly or straight hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

In conclusion, whether it's better to cut hair curly or straight depends on your hair type and the desired look. It's important to find a stylist who has experience with your hair type and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
