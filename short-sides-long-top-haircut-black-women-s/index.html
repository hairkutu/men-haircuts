If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, then the short sides long top haircut is the perfect choice for black women. This trendy and versatile hairstyle is a great way to show off your unique style and personality. In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about the short sides long top haircut for black women, including how to achieve the look, tips for styling, and hair care.

  • How to Achieve the Look

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a style that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This hairstyle is characterized by having short hair on the sides while leaving the hair on top longer. The contrast between the two lengths creates a visually striking look that is both edgy and stylish. This hairstyle is perfect for black women who want to stand out and showcase their individuality.

How to Achieve the Look

The short sides long top haircut is a look that can be achieved in a variety of ways. The most important thing to consider when getting this haircut is the length of the hair on top and the sides. Typically, the hair on top should be at least 3 inches long, while the sides should be buzzed or shaved. This creates a stark contrast between the two lengths and gives the hairstyle its signature look.

Choosing the Right Haircut

Before getting the short sides long top haircut, it's important to choose the right haircut that will suit your face shape and hair type. Speak to your hairstylist about the best options for your hair, and consider bringing in pictures of hairstyles you like to give them an idea of the look you're going for.

Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to achieve the short sides long top haircut at home, here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Using clippers or scissors, buzz or shave the sides of your head until they are the desired length.
  3. Leave the hair on top longer, and use scissors to trim it to the desired length.
  4. Style your hair using your preferred products.

Tips for Styling

Once you've achieved the short sides long top haircut, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips for styling this edgy and bold hairstyle:

  • Use a good quality styling product to hold the hair in place and add texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as slicking the hair back or creating a messy, textured look.
  • Add some color to your hair to make the style pop even more.

FAQ

What face shape does the short sides long top haircut suit?

The short sides long top haircut suits a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and square.

What hair type works best with the short sides long top haircut?

The short sides long top haircut works best on thick, curly or wavy hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is important when sporting the short sides long top haircut. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and well-maintained.

With these tips, you can achieve the perfect short sides long top haircut for black women. This edgy and bold hairstyle is a great way to showcase your unique style and personality. So, go ahead and give it a try!
