Bob Haircuts For Round Faces Over 60

Written by Frank Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are over 60 and have a round face, then you might be wondering which haircut would be the best for you. Bob haircuts have been popular for many years and are still trendy today. They are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit any face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best bob haircuts for round faces over 60, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep them looking great.

Description

A bob haircut is a short or medium-length haircut that is usually cut straight around the head at jaw level. It can be styled in many different ways, including straight, wavy, or curly. Bob haircuts are popular because they are easy to maintain, versatile, and can be customized to suit any face shape.

How to

When choosing a bob haircut for a round face over 60, it is important to consider the length and style of the bob. A shorter bob that is cut just above the jawline can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. A longer bob that falls below the jawline can help to soften the face and make it appear more youthful.

When styling a bob haircut for a round face over 60, it is important to use products that will add volume and texture to the hair. A volumizing mousse or spray can help to add volume to the roots of the hair, while a texturizing spray can help to add texture and movement to the ends of the hair.

Step by Step

The following are the steps to follow when getting a bob haircut for a round face over 60:

  1. Choose the length and style of the bob
  2. Find a hairstylist who specializes in bob haircuts
  3. Bring a picture of the bob haircut you want to your hairstylist
  4. Discuss any concerns you have about the haircut with your hairstylist
  5. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment
  6. Sit still and let your hairstylist work their magic
  7. Style your bob haircut at home using the right products and tools

Tips

The following are some tips for getting and maintaining a bob haircut for a round face over 60:

  • Choose a bob haircut that complements the shape of your face
  • Use products that will add volume and texture to your hair
  • Style your bob haircut using the right tools, such as a round brush and hair dryer
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray

FAQ

What is the best bob haircut for a round face over 60?

The best bob haircut for a round face over 60 is a shorter bob that is cut just above the jawline. This can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

What products should I use to style my bob haircut?

You should use products that will add volume and texture to your hair, such as a volumizing mousse or spray and a texturizing spray.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my bob haircut?

You should visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to maintain your bob haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your bob haircut looking great, it is important to take care of your hair. This includes washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair. You should also eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

In conclusion, a bob haircut is a great choice for a round face over 60. By choosing the right length and style, using the right products and tools to style your hair, and taking care of your hair with a healthy diet and regular visits to your hairstylist, you can keep your bob looking great for years to come.
