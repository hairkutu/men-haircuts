How To Cut Hair On Top Of Head Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 20, 2023 · 7 min read

If you want to learn how to cut hair on top of head, this guide is for you. Cutting hair on the top of the head is a skill that can come in handy in many situations. Whether you want to save money by cutting your hair at home, or you want to help a friend or family member, this guide will show you the steps you need to follow.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut Hair on Top of Head
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Cutting Hair on Top of Head
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care after Cutting

Description

Cutting hair on the top of the head involves trimming the hair that grows on the scalp. It is different from cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head. When cutting hair on the top of the head, you need to be careful not to cut too much, as this can lead to an uneven or unflattering haircut.

How to Cut Hair on Top of Head

If you want to cut hair on top of head, here are the things you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clip or hair tie
  • Spray bottle with water

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by parting it down the middle from front to back. Then, divide the hair into three sections: left, right, and back. Use a hair clip or hair tie to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the back section and comb it straight up. Use your scissors to trim the ends of the hair. Repeat this process with small sections of hair from the left and right sections.

Step 4: Check the Length

Check the length of the hair by running your fingers through it. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them to match the rest of the hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by taking small sections of hair from the right, left, and back sections and combing them straight up. Use your scissors to trim the ends of the hair. Be sure to blend the layers so that they look natural.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Finish the cut by checking the length of the hair again and making any necessary adjustments. Use your comb to comb the hair into the desired style.

Tips for Cutting Hair on Top of Head

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any split ends or damage to the hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair if it becomes too dry or difficult to work with.
  • Cut the hair in a well-lit room to avoid any mistakes or uneven cuts.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair on top of the head?

Yes, you can cut your own hair on top of the head. Just be sure to follow the steps outlined in this guide and use caution when cutting.

How often should I cut my hair on top of the head?

The frequency of haircuts depends on your personal preference and hair type. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, regular scissors can damage the hair and cause split ends. It is important to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your hair on top of the head, be sure to take care of it properly. This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, and using hair products that are appropriate for your hair type.

Cutting hair on top of the head can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and safely. Follow the steps outlined in this guide, and you will be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time!
