Are you tired of your current hairstyle in Animal Crossing Happy Home Designer? Want to switch things up and try out a new look? Well, you're in luck because changing your hair in the game is easy and fun! In this article, we'll walk you through the process step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your virtual locks looking fabulous.

How to Change Your Hair

To change your hair in Animal Crossing Happy Home Designer, you'll need to visit the beauty salon in the game. The salon is located in the shopping district of your town, and it's marked by a sign with a pair of scissors.

Once you enter the salon, you'll be greeted by Harriet the poodle. She's the owner of the salon and will be the one to help you change your hair. Simply talk to her and select the "Makeover" option to get started.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide on how to change your hair:

  1. Visit the beauty salon in the shopping district of your town.
  2. Talk to Harriet the poodle and select the "Makeover" option.
  3. Choose the hairstyle you want from the available options.
  4. Customize your hair further by selecting a hair color and highlights.
  5. Confirm your new look and pay the fee for the makeover.
  6. Enjoy your fresh new hairstyle!

Tips for Choosing a New Hairstyle

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your character's personality and style. Do they prefer a more classic look or something edgy and trendy?
  • Think about the season and weather in the game. During the summer, you may want a shorter, lighter hairstyle, while in the winter, a longer, warmer style may be more appropriate.
  • Experiment with different colors and highlights to add some variety to your look.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new and step out of your comfort zone!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about changing your hair in Animal Crossing Happy Home Designer:

How much does a makeover cost?

The cost of a makeover varies depending on the hairstyle you choose, but it typically ranges from 2,000 to 4,000 bells.

Can I change my hair color without getting a full makeover?

Yes, you can change your hair color and highlights without getting a full makeover. Simply select the "Hair Color" option when talking to Harriet in the salon.

Can I change my hair back to its original style?

Yes, you can change your hair back to its original style by visiting the salon and selecting the "Restore" option.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you have a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair in Animal Crossing Happy Home Designer:

  • Visit the salon regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your hair tangle-free.
  • Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel or spray, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using an umbrella.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to change your hair in Animal Crossing Happy Home Designer and keep it looking fabulous. So go ahead and try out that new hairstyle you've been dreaming of!
