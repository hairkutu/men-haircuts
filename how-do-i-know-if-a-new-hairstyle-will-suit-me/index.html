How Do I Know If A New Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by William May 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you thinking of getting a new hairstyle but not sure if it will suit you? Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure what will look good on you. In this article, we will discuss how you can know if a new hairstyle will suit you.

How to Determine If a New Hairstyle Will Suit You

There are several factors to consider when determining if a new hairstyle will suit you or not. These include:

1. Face Shape

The shape of your face plays a crucial role in determining what hairstyle will suit you. There are six basic face shapes, including oval, round, heart, square, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has specific hairstyles that complement its features.

2. Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is another factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. Different hairstyles work best with specific hair types, such as straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in your hairstyle choice. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have a more formal lifestyle, you may prefer a more polished and elegant hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a New Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose a new hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a new hairstyle is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. Identify the widest part of your face, your jawline, and your forehead. Then, compare your features to the six basic face shapes and determine which one you have.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. Determine if you have straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair. This will help you choose a hairstyle that works best with your hair type.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Once you have determined your face shape and hair texture, look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, and hairstyle websites to find styles that you like and that will complement your features.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional Hairstylist

Finally, consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They can also provide advice on hair care and styling tips for your new hairstyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Hair Type

Choose a hairstyle that works well with your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, avoid heavy and bulky styles that can weigh your hair down.

2. Choose a Style that Complements Your Face Shape

Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, opt for a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling.

4. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Your hair will grow back, and you can always try a new look.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I try on different hairstyles before choosing one?

Yes, you can use virtual hairstyle try-on tools available on some websites to see how different hairstyles will look on you.

2. How often should I get a new hairstyle?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, you may need to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair. If you have thick hair, you may be able to go longer between trims.

3. What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different style that will better suit you.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Here are some hair care tips to keep your new hairstyle looking great:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you use hot styling tools, such as a straightener or curling iron, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that suits you and keeps you looking great. Remember, don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
