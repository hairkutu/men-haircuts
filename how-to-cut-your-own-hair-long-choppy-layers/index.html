How To Cut Your Own Hair Long Choppy Layers

Written by Eveline May 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when you want to add layers. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect long choppy layers at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your own hair long choppy layers, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How To Cut Your Own Hair Long Choppy Layers
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Cut Your Own Hair Long Choppy Layers

Before we get started, you will need a few tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Once you have gathered these tools, follow these easy steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section up and out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take one of the back sections down and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and cut straight across. Repeat this step for the other back section.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Release one of the front sections and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and cut at a slight angle. This will create a longer layer that blends into the bottom layer. Repeat this step for the other front section.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Now it's time to cut the top layer. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and cut at a slight angle. This will create a longer layer that blends into the middle layer. Repeat this step for the rest of the top layer.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Take a comb and comb through your hair, blending the layers together. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect long choppy layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take breaks to avoid getting tired or frustrated.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair wet?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be deceiving and result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Can I add more layers?

Yes, you can add more layers by following the same steps and cutting smaller sections of hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain healthy hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a heat protectant when using heat tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair long choppy layers, you can save money and achieve the perfect look at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
