How To Cut Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post on how to cut thick coarse wavy hair. If you have this hair type, you know how difficult it can be to maintain and style. But fear not, we have some tips and tricks to help you achieve a great haircut that works for you.

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be a challenge to cut, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut. This hair type tends to be frizzy and can become unruly if not cut properly. It is important to use the right tools and products to get the best results.

How to

The first step in cutting thick coarse wavy hair is to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It is also important to have a good understanding of the different types of haircuts that work well for this hair type.

Once you have the right tools, start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut. After washing, towel dry your hair and comb it out.

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use the comb to hold the hair in place, and then use the scissors to cut the hair at a 45-degree angle. This will help to add layers and texture to your hair.

Continue cutting each section until you have completed the entire head. Make sure to take your time and be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step by Step

Step 1: Gather your tools

To cut thick coarse wavy hair, you will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Wash and condition your hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure to wash and condition it. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Section your hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

Step 4: Cut your hair

Use the comb to hold the hair in place, and then use the scissors to cut the hair at a 45-degree angle. This will help to add layers and texture to your hair. Continue cutting each section until you have completed the entire head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut for thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Use a sharp pair of scissors
  • Cut your hair in sections
  • Cut at a 45-degree angle to add layers and texture
  • Take your time and be careful not to cut too much hair at once
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair in place

FAQ

Q: What types of haircuts work well for thick coarse wavy hair?

A: Layered haircuts and bob haircuts work well for thick coarse wavy hair. These haircuts help to add texture and shape to your hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair if I have thick coarse wavy hair?

A: It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care

To keep your thick coarse wavy hair looking its best, it is important to use the right hair care products. Look for products that are designed for this hair type, such as shampoos and conditioners that contain moisturizing ingredients. It is also important to avoid using heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair.

We hope this blog post on how to cut thick coarse wavy hair has been helpful. Remember to take your time and use the right tools and techniques to achieve a great haircut.
