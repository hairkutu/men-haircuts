What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz Find Out Now

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering changing your hair colour, you might be wondering which shade would suit you best. With so many options to choose from, it can be tough to know where to start. Fortunately, taking a quiz can help you narrow down your options and find the perfect hue for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

Description

The "What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz" is a series of questions designed to help you determine which hair colour would be the most flattering for you. These quizzes typically ask about your skin tone, eye colour, natural hair colour, and other factors that can influence which shades will look best on you.

While no quiz can be 100% accurate, taking one can give you a starting point for your search. You can use the results as a jumping-off point to explore different shades and styles, or you can take the recommendations as gospel and head straight to the salon for a change.

How To Take The Quiz

There are many quizzes available online that will help you find your ideal hair colour. Here's how to take one:

Step 1: Find a quiz

Do a quick online search for "what hair colour would suit me quiz," and you'll find plenty of options to choose from. Look for quizzes that are well-reviewed and have a lot of positive feedback from users.

Step 2: Answer the questions

Most quizzes will ask you a series of questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability. Don't be afraid to ask a friend for their opinion if you're unsure about your answer.

Step 3: Get your results

After you've answered all the questions, you'll receive your results. Some quizzes will give you a specific shade recommendation, while others will provide a range of colours that would suit you.

Tips for Finding Your Ideal Hair Colour

While taking a quiz can be helpful, there are other factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're low-maintenance and don't want to spend a lot of time on upkeep, choose a shade that doesn't require a lot of touch-ups.
  • Think about your natural hair colour. If you're looking for a subtle change, choose a shade that's close to your natural colour.
  • Take your skin tone into account. If you have warm undertones, choose a warm hair colour like golden blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones, choose a cool hair colour like ash blonde or platinum.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Hair colour can always be changed, so don't be afraid to try something new and see how it looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding your ideal hair colour:

What if I don't like the results of the quiz?

The quiz is just a starting point. If you don't like the recommended shade, you can always explore other options and find something that suits you better.

How often should I touch up my colour?

It depends on the shade and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

What if I'm not sure which colours I like?

Look for inspiration online or in magazines. You can also bring in pictures to your stylist to show them the look you're going for.

Hair Care

Once you've found your ideal hair colour, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for coloured hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Remember, changing your hair colour can be a fun way to express your personal style. With a little research and experimentation, you're sure to find a shade that makes you look and feel your best.
