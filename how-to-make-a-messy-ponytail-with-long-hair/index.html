How To Make A Messy Ponytail With Long Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long hair, you know that styling it can be challenging. You may have tried different hairstyles, but a messy ponytail is always a great option. Not only is it quick and easy to do, but it also gives off a cool and effortless vibe. In this blog post, we'll show you how to make a messy ponytail with long hair, step by step.

Description

A messy ponytail is a hairstyle that is casual and relaxed. It's perfect for those days when you don't have much time to style your hair or when you want to achieve a laid-back look. This hairstyle is suitable for any face shape, hair type, and occasion.

How to

Follow these simple steps to make a messy ponytail with long hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Apply some texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil in your hair.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure to leave some strands loose to create a messy look.

Step 3: Create volume

Use your fingers to gently pull some strands of hair at the crown to create volume. This will give your ponytail a messy and effortless look.

Step 4: Create the messy look

Use your fingers to tousle your ponytail and create a messy look. You can also pull some strands of hair at the front to frame your face.

Step 5: Secure your ponytail

Secure your ponytail with a hair tie. You can use a clear elastic or a scrunchie to add some texture to your ponytail.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your messy ponytail look even better:

  • Use a hairbrush to tease your hair at the crown to create more volume.
  • Apply some hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.
  • Add some accessories like a hairband or a hair clip to make your ponytail look more stylish.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I make a messy ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes, you can make a messy ponytail with short hair. However, you may need to use some hair extensions to add length and volume to your hair.

Q: Can I wear a messy ponytail to a formal event?

A: While a messy ponytail is a casual hairstyle, you can still wear it to a formal event. Just make sure to style it properly and add some accessories to make it look more elegant.

Q: How do I prevent my messy ponytail from falling apart?

A: To prevent your ponytail from falling apart, use a hair tie that is strong and secure. You can also apply some hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and shiny, make sure to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Making a messy ponytail with long hair is easy and fun. With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a cool and effortless look in no time. Try it out and see how it works for you!
