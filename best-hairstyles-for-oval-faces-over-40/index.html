Description

As we age, our face shape changes and so does our hair. It can be a challenge to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters our oval face shape and makes us look younger. If you have an oval face and are over 40, then you should consider trying out some of the best hairstyles that will enhance your features and make you look fabulous. This article will guide you on how to choose the best hairstyles for oval faces over 40, step by step guide, tips, FAQs and hair care.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Faces Over 40

Choosing the best hairstyle for oval faces over 40 can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

1. Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture plays a vital role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have thin hair, then you should consider getting a layered cut that will add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, then you can opt for a blunt cut that will help you manage your hair easily.

2. Determine your face shape

Although you have an oval face, it's essential to determine the exact shape of your face. You can do this by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are nearly the same, then you have an oval face. If you have a rounder forehead, then you have a heart-shaped face, and if you have a wider jawline, then you have a square-shaped face.

3. Consider your lifestyle

Your lifestyle plays a vital role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style your hair, then you should consider getting a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, then you can opt for a more complex hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide for Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 40

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval faces over 40, along with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve them:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are great for oval faces as they add volume and movement to the hair. Here's how to achieve this hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse from the roots to the ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a great hairstyle that flatters oval faces. Here's how to achieve this hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a smoothing serum from the roots to the ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Cut your hair into a bob with bangs, making sure that the length flatters your face.
  5. Finish with a smoothing serum to add shine and control frizz.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great low maintenance hairstyle for oval faces over 40. Here's how to achieve this hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse from the roots to the ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Cut your hair into a pixie cut, making sure that the length flatters your face.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 40

Here are some tips for maintaining the best hairstyles for oval faces over 40:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have an oval face and over 40?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair, but make sure that you choose a color that flatters your skin tone and enhances your features.

Q: What is the best haircut for oval faces over 40 with curly hair?

A: The best haircut for oval faces over 40 with curly hair is a layered cut that adds volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care for Oval Faces Over 40

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair for oval faces over 40:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as it can weigh down your hair.

Choosing the best hairstyle for oval faces over 40 can be a challenge, but with the right tips and guidance, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you look younger. Follow the step-by-step guide above and the tips for maintaining healthy hair, and you'll be on your way to rocking the best hairstyle for oval faces over 40.
