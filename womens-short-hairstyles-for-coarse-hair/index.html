Women s Short Hairstyles For Coarse Hair Tips And Ideas

Written by Robby Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your thick and coarse hair? Do you want to find a short hairstyle that not only looks great but is also easy to maintain? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for women with coarse hair. From pixie cuts to bobs, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Coarse Hair

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to understand what type of hair you have. Coarse hair is thick, rough, and difficult to manage. It can also be frizzy and prone to breakage. When choosing a hairstyle, consider the texture and density of your hair. Short hairstyles work well for coarse hair because they help to reduce the weight and bulk of your hair.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal styling, but there are a few things you can do to make it look its best. Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add definition and hold to your hair. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use a blow dryer or diffuser to dry your hair quickly and add volume. Finally, use a flat iron or curling iron to create texture and movement in your hair.

Best Short Hairstyles for Coarse Hair

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for women with coarse hair. It's low maintenance and easy to style. Plus, it adds a touch of edge and sophistication to your look. To style, apply a small amount of mousse or gel to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also add side-swept bangs for a softer look.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle for women with coarse hair. It's versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. You can opt for a blunt, chin-length bob or a layered bob with soft, textured ends. To style, use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and movement to your hair. You can also add highlights or lowlights to create dimension.

Shaggy Pixie

The shaggy pixie is a modern take on the classic pixie cut. It features choppy layers and textured ends, which add volume and movement to your hair. To style, apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. This hairstyle works well for women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a bold and edgy hairstyle that works well for women with coarse hair. It features a longer length on one side and a shorter length on the other, which adds asymmetry and balance to your look. To style, use a flat iron or curling iron to create texture and movement in your hair. You can also add highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

Q: Can I color my coarse hair?

A: Yes, you can color your coarse hair. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my coarse hair?

A: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair and prevent frizz. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Coarse Hair

Coarse hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your coarse hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or relaxers, on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your coarse hair. Whether you opt for a pixie cut or bob, remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
