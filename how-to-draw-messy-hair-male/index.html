Are you struggling with drawing messy hair for your male characters? Don't worry, you're not alone. Messy hair can be challenging to draw, especially for beginners. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to draw messy hair male. We'll also provide tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect messy hair look.

How to Draw Messy Hair Male

Drawing messy hair requires a bit of skill and practice, but it's not impossible. Follow these steps to create the perfect messy hair look for your male characters.

Step 1: Draw the Head

Start by drawing the head of your male character. Make sure to draw it in the right proportion and size. You can draw a basic circle and add a line in the middle to divide the face in half. This line will help you place the eyes, nose, and mouth in the right position.

Step 2: Sketch the Hairline

Draw the hairline of your character. Keep in mind that messy hair is not perfectly combed, so the hairline should be a bit uneven. You can create some strands of hair that fall over the forehead or behind the ears.

Step 3: Add the Hair

Start adding the hair strands. Messy hair has different lengths and directions, so create strands that go in different directions. You can use short, quick strokes to create the texture of the hair. Make sure to leave some gaps to create the messy look.

Step 4: Add Shadows and Highlights

To make the hair look more realistic, add some shadows and highlights. Darken some areas to create shadows and add highlights to the areas that catch the light. This will give the hair more depth and dimension.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to draw messy hair male:

  1. Draw the head in the right proportion and size.
  2. Sketch the hairline, creating some unevenness.
  3. Add hair strands in different directions and lengths.
  4. Create texture by using short, quick strokes.
  5. Leave some gaps to create the messy look.
  6. Add shadows and highlights to create depth and dimension.

Tips for Drawing Messy Hair Male

Here are some tips to help you draw messy hair for your male characters:

  • Study different hairstyles to get inspiration.
  • Use short, quick strokes to create texture.
  • Don't be afraid to leave gaps to create the messy look.
  • Add shadows and highlights to create depth and dimension.
  • Use references to help you with the placement of the hair strands.

FAQs

How do I make the hair look more messy?

To make the hair look more messy, leave some gaps between the hair strands and create strands that go in different directions. You can also add some flyaway hairs or strands that fall over the forehead or behind the ears.

How do I create depth and dimension in the hair?

You can create depth and dimension in the hair by adding shadows and highlights. Darken some areas to create shadows and add highlights to the areas that catch the light. This will give the hair more depth and make it look more realistic.

How can I improve my hair drawing skills?

The best way to improve your hair drawing skills is to practice. Study different hairstyles and practice drawing them. Use references to help you with the placement of the hair strands. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new techniques.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

If you have messy hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

Now that you know how to draw messy hair male, it's time to practice. Remember to use references and experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you. With practice, you'll be able to create the perfect messy hair look for your male characters.
