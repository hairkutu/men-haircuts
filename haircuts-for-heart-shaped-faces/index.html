Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces The Perfect Match

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know that finding the perfect haircut can be a daunting task. Your face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrower chin. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you the best haircuts for heart shaped faces that will enhance your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn.

If the widest part of your face is at your forehead and your face tapers to a point at your chin, you have a heart shaped face.

Best Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the perfect haircut. Here are some of the best haircuts for heart shaped faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for heart shaped faces because they help to balance out the wider forehead and narrow chin. Layers can also add volume and movement to your hair, which is perfect if you have thin or fine hair. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while balancing out your forehead. This style works well with both short and long hair and can be worn straight or curled.

Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great option for heart shaped faces because it adds volume to the crown of your head, which helps to balance out your wider forehead. This style can be worn straight or with loose waves.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice for heart shaped faces, but it can look amazing if done correctly. The key is to add volume to the crown of your head and keep the sides and back short. This style works best with straight or wavy hair.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you have the perfect haircut, here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and curl to the ends of your hair.
  • Avoid heavy or blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

FAQs

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a heart shaped face. Just be sure to add volume to the crown of your head to balance out your wider forehead.

What should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid heavy or blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead appear even wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are very short or flat to the head, as they can accentuate your narrow chin.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your hair if you have a heart shaped face:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

With these tips and haircuts for heart shaped faces, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
