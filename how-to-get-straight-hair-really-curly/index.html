How To Get Straight Hair Really Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair? Do you want to achieve big, bouncy curls? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting straight hair really curly. From tools to techniques, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Curling Techniques
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before we begin, let's gather the tools you'll need:

  • Curling iron (with different barrel sizes)
  • Curling wand (with different barrel sizes)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Wide-tooth comb

Prepping Your Hair

Prepping your hair is crucial for achieving bouncy curls. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent any knots or tangles while curling.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts. Use a clip or hair tie to secure the top section of your hair, leaving the bottom section loose.

Curling Techniques

There are different techniques for curling your hair. Here are some of our favorites:

Technique 1: Using a Curling Iron

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold it for a few seconds, then release. Repeat until all sections are curled.

Technique 2: Using a Curling Wand

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand barrel. Hold it for a few seconds, then release. Repeat until all sections are curled.

Finishing Touches

After curling, it's time to add the finishing touches:

Step 1: Apply Hairspray

Apply a generous amount of hairspray to your hair to hold the curls in place.

Step 2: Shake Your Hair

Shake your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Step 3: Add Volume

Flip your hair upside down and use your fingers to add volume to the roots.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

To keep your curls looking great, follow these tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to avoid frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting straight hair really curly:

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Q: Can I curl my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can curl your hair without heat using methods such as braiding or twisting.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a different approach. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve big, bouncy curls in no time! Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
