What Is A Number 4 Haircut In Mm A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what a number 4 haircut in mm is? Look no further! In this post, we will break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including its length, how to get it, and how to take care of it. Let's dive in!

Description

A number 4 haircut in mm refers to the length of the hair that will be left after the haircut. In millimeters, a number 4 haircut is approximately 12mm long. This haircut is considered a medium length, making it a popular choice for those who want a bit of length but still want to keep their hair relatively short.

How to Get a Number 4 Haircut in mm

If you're interested in getting a number 4 haircut in mm, the first step is to find a reputable barber or hair stylist. Once you've found someone you trust, you can ask for a number 4 haircut and they will know exactly what you mean.

During the haircut, the barber or stylist will use clippers to cut the hair to the desired length. They will typically start with a longer guard, such as a number 5 or 6, and work their way down to a number 4 to ensure that the hair is cut evenly and gradually.

Step by Step Guide

If you're interested in cutting your own hair or want to provide your barber with more specific instructions, here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a number 4 haircut in mm:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Attach a number 5 or 6 guard to your clippers
  • Starting at the base of your neck, use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion
  • Work your way up the back of your head, using the same upward motion until you reach the crown
  • Repeat this process on the sides of your head, starting at the bottom and working your way up
  • Switch to a number 4 guard and repeat the process, making sure to blend the hair evenly
  • Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments

Tips for Maintaining a Number 4 Haircut in mm

Once you have your number 4 haircut in mm, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the length and style. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups

FAQ

Is a number 4 haircut in mm suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 4 haircut in mm can work for all hair types, although it may look slightly different depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Can I style my hair differently with a number 4 haircut in mm?

Absolutely! While a number 4 haircut in mm is a simple and classic style, there are plenty of ways to switch it up. Try adding some texture with a styling cream or wax, or parting your hair in a new direction.

How often should I get a touch-up for my number 4 haircut in mm?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length of your hair. As a general rule, it's a good idea to visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up.

Hair Care

Maintaining your number 4 haircut in mm is all about proper hair care. Here are a few additional tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can damage the hair and cause breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized

And there you have it! With this guide, you should now have a clear understanding of what a number 4 haircut in mm is, how to get it, and how to maintain it. Whether you're a fan of short hair or just looking to switch up your style, a number 4 haircut in mm is a great choice.
