Long Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50 Tips And Ideas

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a woman over 50 and have a square face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle. Square faces have a strong jawline and a broad forehead, which makes it important to find a hairstyle that balances out these features. In this article, we will discuss some long hairstyles that are perfect for square faces over 50.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 50

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for square faces over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a style that softens your features and creates balance. Second, you want to consider your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style and preferences.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Long Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 50

1. Determine your face shape - You can do this by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape.

2. Consider your hair type and texture - Long hairstyles can work for all hair types, but you want to choose a style that complements your natural texture. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered cut that enhances your curls.

3. Think about your personal style - Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. If you prefer a more classic look, you may want to opt for a simple, straight cut. If you like to experiment with your hair, you may want to try layers or bangs.

Tips for Choosing the Best Long Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 50

1. Avoid blunt cuts - Blunt cuts can make square faces look harsh and angular. Instead, opt for layers or soft, face-framing angles.

2. Consider adding bangs - Bangs can help soften a square jawline and make your features appear more balanced. Side-swept or wispy bangs are especially flattering for square faces.

3. Experiment with length - Long hair can work for square faces, but you may want to avoid hair that falls straight down. Layers or waves can add movement and soften your features.

FAQ About Long Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a square face. However, you may want to avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize your strong jawline.

Q: What are some celebrity examples of long hairstyles for square faces over 50?

A: Some celebrities with square faces over 50 who wear long hairstyles include Diane Keaton, Helen Mirren, and Meryl Streep. These women often opt for layered cuts or soft waves to balance out their features.

Long Hairstyle Maintenance and Hair Care Tips

When it comes to maintaining a long hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure you are using the right products for your hair type and texture. Second, you want to avoid over-styling or using too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a long hairstyle:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

- Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners every day

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Final Thoughts

Long hairstyles can be a great option for square faces over 50, as long as you choose the right style for your face shape and personal preferences. Consider adding layers, bangs, or waves to soften your features and create balance. And don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and the right products for your hair type.
