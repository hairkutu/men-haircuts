What To Do To Cover Grey Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of seeing grey hairs every time you look in the mirror? Fortunately, there are several ways to cover grey hair and maintain a youthful appearance. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of covering grey hair, including how to do it, tips to make it last longer, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally turns grey. While some people embrace their grey hair, others prefer to cover it up. Covering grey hair can be done at home or by a professional stylist. It's important to choose a method that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

How To

Step 1: Choose a Hair Color

The first step is to choose a hair color that matches your natural hair color. If you're unsure which color to choose, consult with a professional stylist or use a hair color chart. Keep in mind that if you're covering a significant amount of grey hair, you may need to choose a shade that's slightly darker than your natural hair color.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying hair color, it's important to prepare your hair. This includes washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and then drying it completely. If you have sensitive skin, apply a barrier cream around your hairline to prevent staining.

Step 3: Apply Hair Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color package to mix the color and developer. Apply the color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb or brush to distribute the color evenly. Leave the color on for the recommended time, and then rinse it out thoroughly.

Step 4: Condition Your Hair

After rinsing out the hair color, apply a deep conditioner to your hair. This will help to restore moisture and prevent damage. Leave the conditioner on for 5-10 minutes, and then rinse it out thoroughly.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your hair color last longer:

  • Avoid washing your hair for the first 48 hours after coloring.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can cause damage and fade your hair color.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to sunlight, as it can fade your hair color.

FAQ

What is the best way to cover grey hair?

The best way to cover grey hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some popular methods include using hair color, highlights, lowlights, or hair extensions.

How often should I color my hair to cover grey?

The frequency of coloring your hair depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your grey hair grows back in. On average, most people need to color their hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

Will coloring my hair damage it?

Coloring your hair can cause damage if it's done too frequently or if the wrong products are used. It's important to use a high-quality hair color and to follow the instructions carefully. Additionally, it's important to condition your hair regularly to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. This includes washing your hair regularly, using conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat and chemicals. Additionally, getting regular haircuts can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

Now that you know how to cover grey hair, you can choose a method that works best for you. Whether you prefer to do it at home or go to a professional stylist, the key is to choose a method that's safe, effective, and long-lasting. With the right care and maintenance, you can enjoy beautiful, youthful-looking hair for years to come.
