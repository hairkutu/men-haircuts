What Hair Colour Would Suit My Skin Tone Quiz The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair colour and want to try something new, but not sure what would suit your skin tone? Look no further, as we have created a comprehensive quiz to help you determine what hair colour would suit your skin tone. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of taking the quiz, provide tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Take the Quiz

To take the quiz, simply answer the following questions:

  1. What is your skin tone?
  2. What is your eye colour?
  3. What is your natural hair colour?
  4. What is your style preference?

Once you have answered these questions, we will analyze your answers and provide you with a hair colour recommendation. It's that easy!

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

The first step in determining what hair colour would suit your skin tone is to understand your skin tone. There are generally two types of skin tones: warm and cool. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone.

Step 2: Identify your eye colour

Your eye colour is also an important factor in determining what hair colour would suit your skin tone. If you have warm-toned skin, consider hair colours with warm undertones such as golden, caramel, or honey. For cool-toned skin, ash or platinum blonde, cool brown, or black hair colours would suit you best.

Step 3: Consider your natural hair colour

It's important to consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new hair colour. If you have light hair, it's easier to go lighter or darker. However, if you have dark hair, it's best to stay within a few shades of your natural hair colour to avoid damage.

Step 4: Determine your style preference

Lastly, consider your style preference. Do you want a dramatic change or a subtle one? Do you want to go bold with bright colours or keep it simple with natural tones?

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing a new hair colour:

  • Consider your skin's undertones
  • Choose a shade that complements your eye colour
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much maintenance the hair colour will require
  • Try temporary hair colour first before committing to a permanent change
  • Consult a professional hair stylist for advice

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair colour would suit my skin tone quiz:

Q: Can I take the quiz if I have dyed hair?

A: Yes, you can still take the quiz if you have dyed hair. However, keep in mind that the results may not be as accurate as if you had natural hair.

Q: How often should I change my hair colour?

A: It's recommended to wait at least six to eight weeks before changing your hair colour to avoid damage.

Q: How can I maintain my new hair colour?

A: Use colour-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoid excessive heat styling, and protect your hair from the sun.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Use a hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair

Now that you have all the information you need, take our quiz and find out what hair colour would suit your skin tone!
