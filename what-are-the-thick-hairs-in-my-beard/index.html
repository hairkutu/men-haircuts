What Are The Thick Hairs In My Beard

Written by Eveline Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a beard, you may have noticed that some of the hairs are thicker than others. These thick hairs are known as terminal hairs, and they are an important part of your beard's overall appearance. In this article, we will explore what terminal hairs are, why they matter, and how to take care of them properly.

What Are Terminal Hairs?

Terminal hairs are the thick, coarse, and darker hairs that grow in your beard. They are typically found on the chin, jawline, and mustache areas. These hairs are different from vellus hairs, which are softer and shorter hairs that cover most of your face.

Terminal hairs are usually the last hairs to grow in your beard, and they can take several months to fully develop. They are also the most visible hairs in your beard, which is why they play a crucial role in your beard's overall appearance.

Why Do Terminal Hairs Matter?

Terminal hairs are important for a few reasons. Firstly, they provide structure and shape to your beard. Without terminal hairs, your beard would look patchy and uneven.

Secondly, terminal hairs are the most visible hairs in your beard. They are the hairs that people will notice first, so it's important to keep them healthy and well-groomed.

How to Take Care of Terminal Hairs

If you want to keep your terminal hairs looking their best, there are a few things you can do:

1. Use Beard Oil

Beard oil is a great way to keep your terminal hairs healthy and moisturized. It can also help to prevent split ends and breakage. Apply a few drops of beard oil to your beard every day to keep it looking its best.

2. Brush Your Beard

Brushing your beard regularly can help to distribute the natural oils throughout your beard. It can also help to detangle any knots and prevent breakage. Use a boar bristle brush or a beard comb to brush your beard every day.

3. Trim Your Beard

Regular trimming is essential if you want to keep your terminal hairs looking their best. Use a pair of scissors or a beard trimmer to trim your beard every few weeks. This will help to keep your beard looking neat and tidy.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet can have a big impact on the health of your beard. Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables to keep your beard looking its best.

FAQ

Q. Can I Make My Terminal Hairs Thicker?

A. Unfortunately, you cannot make your terminal hairs thicker. Terminal hairs are determined by genetics, so their thickness is predetermined. However, you can keep your terminal hairs healthy and well-groomed by following the tips outlined in this article.

Q. Will Trimming My Beard Make My Terminal Hairs Thicker?

A. Trimming your beard will not make your terminal hairs thicker. However, it can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your beard look thicker and fuller.

Hair Care Tips for Terminal Hairs

Now that you know what terminal hairs are and why they matter, it's time to start taking care of them properly. By following the tips outlined in this article, you can keep your terminal hairs looking their best and enjoy a healthy, well-groomed beard.
