How To Have A High Ponytail Step By Step Guide And Tips

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read
How to Have a High Ponytail: Step-by-Step Guide and Tips | Hair Care 2023

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Want to try something new? A high ponytail is a great option for a quick and easy hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. In this article, we will show you how to have a high ponytail step-by-step with helpful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for a High Ponytail

How to Have a High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. Here are the steps to create a high ponytail:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Brush your hair thoroughly to ensure that there are no tangles or knots. If your hair is not already clean, wash and condition it before styling. You can also apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Use a hairbrush to gather all of your hair at the top of your head. Make sure it is centered and smooth. You can use a comb to create a straight part in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Secure your hair

Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure your hair in a ponytail. Make sure it is tightly secured and does not slip or fall down. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.

Step 4: Finishing touches

You can add some extra volume to your ponytail by gently teasing the hair with a brush. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin. Finally, you can use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you are a visual learner, here is a step-by-step guide on how to have a high ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair thoroughly.
  2. Gather your hair at the top of your head.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie or elastic band.
  4. Tease the hair for extra volume.
  5. Wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band.
  6. Secure the section with a bobby pin.
  7. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for a High Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color to avoid it being visible.
  • Tease the hair at the crown of your head for extra volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  • Wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it and create a more polished look.
  • Apply hairspray to hold the style in place and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about high ponytails:

How do I make my ponytail higher?

To make your ponytail higher, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can also tease the hair at the crown of your head for extra height.

How do I hide the elastic band?

You can hide the elastic band by wrapping a small section of hair around it and securing it with a bobby pin.

Can I wear a high ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a high ponytail can be dressed up for a formal event by adding accessories such as a hair clip, ribbon, or headband.

Hair Care for a High Ponytail

Here are some tips for hair care when wearing a high ponytail:

  • Avoid wearing a tight ponytail every day to prevent hair breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair before and after styling.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

In conclusion, a high ponytail is a versatile and easy hairstyle that anyone can try. With these tips and steps, you can have a high ponytail in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Have fun and experiment with different variations of the high ponytail!
