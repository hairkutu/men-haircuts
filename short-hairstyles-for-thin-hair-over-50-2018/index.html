Short Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 50 2018 Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 08, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you are over 50 and have thin hair, you might wonder what hairstyles will flatter you. In 2018, there were many trendy and chic short hairstyles for thin hair that were perfect for mature women. These hairstyles are easy to maintain, youthful, and stylish. Here are some tips and tricks for choosing and styling short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 in 2018.

Description

Thin hair can be challenging to style, especially when you are over 50. However, short hairstyles can give your hair volume, texture, and movement, making it look fuller and healthier. Short haircuts also flatter your face shape and highlight your best features, such as your eyes, cheekbones, or jawline. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are many short hairstyles that suit your hair type and personal style.

How to

Here are some steps to follow when choosing and styling short hairstyles for thin hair over 50:

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist

Before you decide on a short hairstyle, it's essential to talk to a professional hairstylist who knows your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. They can recommend the best short hairstyles for your thin hair, taking into account your preferences, maintenance level, and styling tools. They can also show you pictures of short haircuts for inspiration and guide you through the styling process to achieve the desired look.

Step 2: Choose a flattering haircut

Once you have consulted with a hairstylist, you can choose a short haircut that suits your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Some of the most popular short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 in 2018 were pixie cuts, bobs, shags, and layered hairstyles. These haircuts can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look thicker and more youthful. You can also opt for bangs or side-swept fringe to frame your face and hide wrinkles or fine lines.

Step 3: Use the right hair products

To maintain and style your short hair, you need to use the right hair products that suit your hair type and needs. For example, if you have fine or thin hair, you should use volumizing products, such as mousse, root-lifting spray, or dry shampoo. These products can add body and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and bouncier. You should also use a lightweight hairspray or serum to hold your hairstyle in place without weighing it down or causing buildup.

Step 4: Style your hair with the right tools

To style your short hair, you should use the right tools that can create volume, texture, and definition. Depending on your haircut and hair type, you can use a blow dryer, a curling iron, a straightener, or a diffuser to achieve your desired look. You should also use a round brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create soft waves or curls. Don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat-protectant spray or oil.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling short hairstyles for thin hair over 50:

  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with a hairstylist for advice and inspiration.
  • Use volumizing products to add body and texture to your hair. Avoid heavy or oily products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Style your hair with the right tools that can create volume, texture, and definition. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat-protectant spray or oil.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find your signature look. Stay open to new trends and styles that suit your personality and lifestyle.
  • Maintain your short hair by getting regular trims and using hydrating or nourishing hair treatments. Avoid over-washing or over-drying your hair, as it can strip away natural oils and make it brittle or frizzy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thin hair over 50:

Q: Can I wear short hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can wear short hair if you have thin hair. In fact, short haircuts can make your hair look thicker and fuller by adding volume and texture. Just make sure to choose a flattering haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Q: What are some popular short hairstyles for thin hair over 50?

A: Some popular short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 are pixie cuts, bobs, shags, and layered hairstyles. These haircuts can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look thicker and more youthful. You can also opt for bangs or side-swept fringe to frame your face and hide wrinkles or fine lines.

Q: How do I style my short hair if I have thin hair?

A: To style your short hair if you have thin hair, use volumizing products, such as mousse, root-lifting spray, or dry shampoo. These products can add body and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and bouncier. You should also use the right tools, such as a round brush, a curling iron, or a straightener, to create volume, texture, and definition.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair healthy and shiny, you should follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and needs. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo that doesn't contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. Avoid over-washing or over-drying your hair, as it can strip away natural oils and make it brittle or frizzy.
  • Condition your hair with a hydrating or nourishing conditioner that can detangle and soften your hair. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off with cool water.
  • Treat your hair with a hair mask or oil once a week to restore its moisture and vitality. Massage the product into your scalp and hair, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it off.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools or exposing your hair to direct sunlight or heat for a long time. Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat, using a heat-protectant spray or oil, or air-drying your hair.
  • Get regular trims and haircuts to maintain your short hair and remove split ends or damaged strands. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hairstyle fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can choose and style short hairstyles for thin hair over 50 in 2018 that flatter your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts, products, and tools to find your perfect look. Remember to take care of your hair by following a hair care routine that nourishes and protects it from damage. Enjoy your new short hairstyle and embrace your natural beauty!
