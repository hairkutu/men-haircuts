Oval Face With Short Hair A Guide To Styling And Maintaining Your Look

Written by Frank Jun 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face and are considering a short hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect short haircut for your face shape, and provide you with tips and tricks for maintaining your new look.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of haircuts and styles.

Short haircuts are a great option for oval faces, as they accentuate the natural symmetry of the face and highlight the cheekbones. There are many different types of short haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and cropped styles.

How To

Before you dive into a new haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles, so make sure you're prepared for regular trips to the salon.

Once you've found a short haircut that you love, it's important to communicate your vision to your stylist. Bring in photos of hairstyles that inspire you, and be clear about your desired length and texture.

After your haircut, it's important to maintain your look with regular trims and styling. Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type, and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works for you.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. Oval faces are characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin.

Step 2: Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration online or in magazines to find short haircuts that you love. Consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a haircut.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Stylist

Bring in photos of hairstyles that inspire you, and be clear about your desired length and texture. Your stylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and fits your lifestyle.

Step 4: Maintain Your Look

Regular trims and styling are essential for maintaining a short haircut. Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type, and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works for you.

Tips

- Consider your hair texture when choosing a short haircut.

- Be clear about your desired length and texture with your stylist.

- Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works for you.

- Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for oval faces?

Pixie cuts, bobs, and cropped styles are all great options for oval faces.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles, so plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks.

What are some styling tips for short hair?

Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works for you. Consider using a texturizing spray to add volume and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish look. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair over time.

Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right haircut and proper maintenance, short hair can be a stylish and versatile option for oval faces. Follow these tips and tricks to find the perfect short haircut for you.
