Best Hairstyles For Fine Hair Round Face In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 27, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have fine hair and a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Thankfully, there are several hairstyles that can make your hair look fuller and give your face a more defined shape. In this article, we’ll take a look at the best hairstyles for fine hair round face in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Fine Hair Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fine Hair Round Face

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for fine hair round face, it’s essential to determine your face shape. To do this, you’ll need a mirror and a tape measure. Here are the steps:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point
  3. Measure the distance between your jawline and your earlobe
  4. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin
  5. Compare your measurements to the following face shapes:
    • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width
    • Heart: Forehead is wider than the chin, and the face tapers to a point at the chin
    • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width
    • Round: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the face is as wide as it is long

Best Hairstyles for Fine Hair Round Face

Now that you’ve determined your face shape let’s take a look at the best hairstyles for fine hair round face:

Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to fine hair, giving it a fuller appearance. The layers should start at the chin and continue down to the ends of the hair.

Bob

A classic bob is a great option for round faces because it can create the illusion of a longer face. To make the hair look fuller, add some layers or texture to the ends.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a daring option for round faces, but it can also be incredibly flattering. Keep the hair on top of the head longer to create height, and add texture to the sides to make the face appear slimmer.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can add dimension to a round face, giving it a more angular appearance. Make sure the bangs are long enough to sweep to the side and blend into the rest of the hair.

Messy Bun

A messy bun can be a great option for fine hair because it can create the illusion of volume. Pull the hair back into a low or high bun and leave some strands loose around the face to soften the look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots
  3. Blow-dry the hair using a round brush to add volume
  4. Add texture to the ends of the hair using a curling iron or flat iron
  5. Use a comb to part the hair to the side
  6. If you have bangs, sweep them to the side
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down fine hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, which can damage the hair

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a middle part with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a middle part with a round face, but it’s essential to add volume to the hair to create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Should I avoid short hairstyles with a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering on round faces, but it’s essential to add texture and volume to the hair to avoid a flat look.

Hair Care for Fine Hair Round Face

Here are some tips for caring for fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to the hair
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners, which can weigh down fine hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, which can strip the hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, which can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water

There you have it – the best hairstyles for fine hair round face in 2023. Whether you opt for long layers, a classic bob, or a pixie cut, there are plenty of options to flatter your features and make your hair look fuller. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage, and you’ll be sure to have healthy, beautiful hair all year round.
