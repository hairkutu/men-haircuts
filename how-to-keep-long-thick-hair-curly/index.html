How To Keep Long Thick Hair Curly Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long, thick hair, keeping your curls intact can be a challenge. But with the right techniques and products, you can maintain your luscious locks and keep them looking curly and bouncy all day long. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks for how to keep long thick hair curly.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Keep Long Thick Hair Curly
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to keep long thick hair curly, it's important to start with proper hair care. This means using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Additionally, keeping your hair moisturized with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil can help prevent frizz and breakage.

How to Keep Long Thick Hair Curly

Now that we've covered the basics of hair care, let's talk about how to keep long thick hair curly. The first step is to start with wet hair. If your hair is dry, it's more difficult to achieve and maintain curls. After washing your hair, gently pat it dry with a towel and avoid rubbing it vigorously.

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or curl cream. This will help define your curls and keep them in place. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while maintaining the shape of your curls.

Another option is to use a hair styling tool, such as a curling iron or wand, to create curls in your hair. This method is best for touch-ups or if you want to create more defined curls. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for how to keep long thick hair curly:

  • Don't touch your curls too much. The more you play with them, the more likely they are to frizz and lose their shape.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your curls overnight.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to straighten my curls?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to straighten your curls if you want a different look. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using it too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair daily, while others can go several days without washing. Aim to wash your hair when it starts to feel oily or dirty.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

A: Using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil can help prevent tangles by keeping your hair moisturized. Additionally, avoiding tight hairstyles and using a wide-tooth comb to detangle can also help.

Q: Can I still get curls if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use the same techniques and products to create curls in straight hair. Just be sure to use a curl-enhancing product and a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your long thick hair looking curly and fabulous all day long. Remember to start with good hair care habits and use the right products and tools to achieve your desired look.
