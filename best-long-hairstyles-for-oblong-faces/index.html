Best Long Hairstyles For Oblong Faces

Written by Daniel Sep 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you know that not all hairstyles work for you. Finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape can be challenging. However, don't worry as we're here to help you out. In this article, we'll be discussing the best long hairstyles for oblong faces that will make you look stunning. So, let's get started!

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a straight jawline. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar widths. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face is to create width and balance.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Oblong Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, it's essential to keep in mind that you want to create width and balance. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face and avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.

Step by Step Guide for Best Long Hairstyles for Oblong Faces

Here are some of the best long hairstyles for oblong faces:

1. Long Layered Cut

A long layered cut is the perfect hairstyle for oblong faces. The layers add volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can also add some side-swept bangs to soften your features and add some dimension to your hair.

2. Side-Swept Waves

Side-swept waves are another great option for oblong faces. The waves add volume and width to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can achieve this look by using a curling iron to create loose waves and then sweeping your hair to one side.

3. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that works well for oblong faces. The low ponytail adds width to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can also add some volume to the top of your head by teasing your hair before pulling it back.

4. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for oblong faces. The waves add volume and width to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You can achieve this look by using a sea salt spray and scrunching your hair to create loose waves.

Tips to Keep in Mind

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.
  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Soft, side-swept bangs can help soften your features and add dimension to your hair.
  • Teasing your hair can add volume to the top of your head.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head. This includes high ponytails, top knots, and slicked-back hairstyles.

What hairstyles should I choose if I have an oblong face?

Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. This includes long layered cuts, side-swept waves, low ponytails, and beachy waves.

Hair Care for Oblong Faces

When it comes to hair care for oblong faces, it's essential to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Additionally, using a heat protectant before styling your hair can help prevent damage and breakage.

Now that you know the best long hairstyles for oblong faces, it's time to try them out and see which one works best for you. Remember to keep these tips in mind when choosing a hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Good luck!
