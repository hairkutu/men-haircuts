Does Short Hair Look Bad On Round Faces

Description

For years, there has been a misconception that short hair looks bad on round faces. However, that is not necessarily true. With the right haircut and styling, short hair can look stunning on round faces.

Choosing the right haircut for your face shape is crucial. You want to highlight your best features and draw attention away from areas that you are not as confident about. With a round face, you want to lengthen and slim your face. This can be achieved with certain short haircuts and styling techniques.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Faces

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, you want to consider your hair texture and density, as well as your face shape. Some short haircuts that work well for round faces include:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Short shag

These haircuts help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness. It is also important to avoid haircuts that add width to the face, such as blunt bobs and heavy bangs.

Face Shape Analysis

Before choosing a short haircut for your round face, it is important to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the widest part of your face, which is typically from cheekbone to cheekbone. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

If the width and length of your face are equal, you have a round face shape. If the length is longer than the width, you have an oval face shape. If the width is longer than the length, you have a square face shape. And if the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are equal, you have a heart-shaped face.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Faces

If you have decided to get a short haircut for your round face, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts for round faces
  2. Bring pictures of short haircuts that you like
  3. Discuss your hair texture and density with the hairstylist
  4. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape
  5. Ask for layers or texture to add dimension and movement to your hair
  6. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth to your hair

Tips for Styling Short Hair on Round Faces

Styling short hair on a round face can be tricky, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a flattering look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add height to the crown of your head
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting the roots as you go
  • Part your hair to the side to create asymmetry and lengthen your face
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can add width to your face
  • Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face shape

FAQs

Q: Can I still get a short haircut if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, a short haircut can still look great if you have a double chin. Consider choosing a haircut that falls below your chin, such as a bob, to help elongate your face.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a chubby face?

A: No, you can still get a short haircut if you have a chubby face. Look for haircuts that add height to your crown and length to your face, such as a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Q: Is it okay to add layers to short hair on a round face?

A: Yes, adding layers to short hair can add dimension and movement to your hair, which can be flattering on a round face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Round Faces

Short hair on a round face requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as oils from your hands can weigh down your hair

In conclusion, short hair does not look bad on round faces, as long as you choose the right haircut and styling techniques. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts for round faces, and consider your face shape, hair texture, and density before making a decision. Follow these tips for styling and hair care, and you'll have a stunning short haircut that flatters your face shape.
