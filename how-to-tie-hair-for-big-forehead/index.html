How To Tie Hair For Big Forehead Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a big forehead, you might feel self-conscious about it. However, there are plenty of ways to style your hair that can help balance out your features and make you feel confident. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for tying your hair in a way that flatters your big forehead.

How to Tie Hair for Big Forehead: Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for tying your hair in a way that flatters your big forehead:

  1. Start by parting your hair to one side. This will help create the illusion of more volume and balance out your forehead.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and twist it away from your face.
  3. Secure the twisted section with a bobby pin behind your ear.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3 on the other side of your head.
  5. Gather the rest of your hair into a low ponytail or bun at the nape of your neck.
  6. Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure the ponytail or bun in place.
  7. Optional: tease the hair at the crown of your head for more volume.

Tips for Tying Hair for Big Forehead

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when tying your hair for big forehead:

  • Try out different partings to see what works best for you. A side part can help balance out your features, but a middle part can also be flattering.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle. Bangs can help cover up your forehead and bring attention to your eyes.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and ponytails, to see what you like best.
  • Use hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest to your hairstyle and draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Consider consulting with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what hairstyle would work best for your face shape and features.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big forehead?

Absolutely! There are plenty of updo hairstyles that can be flattering for big forehead. Just make sure to leave some hair down in the front to help balance out your features.

Should I avoid wearing bangs if I have a big forehead?

Not necessarily. Bangs can be a great way to cover up a big forehead and draw attention to your eyes. Just make sure to choose a style of bangs that works for your face shape.

What if I don't want to tie my hair back?

There are plenty of hairstyles that don't involve tying your hair back. Consider trying out a long, layered haircut or a style with soft waves to flatter your big forehead.

Hair Care for Big Forehead

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and tricks, you can tie your hair in a way that flatters your big forehead and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
