Hairstyle For Oval Face Asian Girl A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline May 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you an Asian girl with an oval face? Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your features? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles for oval face Asian girls. From short to long, curly to straight, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

An oval face is considered to be the most versatile and balanced face shape. It is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow jawline. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, making it easy for oval face Asian girls to experiment with different looks.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face, it is important to consider the length, texture, and volume of the hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Length

Oval face Asian girls can rock any length of hair. However, it is important to keep in mind the texture and volume of the hair. If you have thin hair, shorter hairstyles will give the illusion of thicker hair. If you have thick hair, longer hairstyles will balance out the volume.

2. Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in the hairstyle you choose. If you have naturally curly hair, embrace it! Curly hairstyles look great on oval face Asian girls. If you have straight hair, consider adding some waves or curls for added texture.

3. Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help balance out an oval face shape. Try adding layers or opting for a voluminous hairstyle like a high ponytail or messy bun.

Step by Step

Now that you have some tips in mind, here are some step-by-step instructions to achieve some popular hairstyles for oval face Asian girls:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is perfect for oval face Asian girls with thin hair. Here's how to achieve the look:

  • Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair wax to the ends of your hair to add texture.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on oval face Asian girls of all ages. Here's how to achieve the look:

  • Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair to add shine.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. Here's how to achieve the look:

  • Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic band.
  • Twist your ponytail around the elastic band to create a bun.
  • Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  • Loosen some strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  • Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oval face Asian girl:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and fits your daily routine.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality. Your hairstyle should reflect your individual style and personality.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles. Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oval face Asian girls:

Q: Can oval face Asian girls wear bangs?

A: Yes! Oval face Asian girls can wear any type of bangs. However, it is important to consider the length and texture of your hair.

Q: Should oval face Asian girls avoid certain hairstyles?

A: No! Oval face Asian girls can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it is important to consider your hair texture and volume.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important when choosing a hairstyle for an oval face Asian girl. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock any hairstyle as an oval face Asian girl. Don't be afraid to experiment and try something new. Remember, your hairstyle should make you feel confident and beautiful!
