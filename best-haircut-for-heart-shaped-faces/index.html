The Best Haircut For Heart Shaped Faces Tips And Styles

Written by Oliver Mar 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that complements your heart-shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we’ll guide you through the best haircut for heart shaped faces, along with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new style. Whether you’re looking for a short bob, long layers, or something in between, we’ve got you covered.

What is a Heart Shaped Face?

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It’s often called a “V” or “inverted triangle” shape, and it’s one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to haircuts. The goal is to create balance by drawing attention away from the forehead and towards the chin.

The Best Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for heart-shaped faces because they add volume and movement to the lower half of the face. This helps balance out the width of the forehead, making the chin appear more prominent. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at the jawline and gradually get longer towards the ends.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is another great option for heart-shaped faces. The bob adds volume to the chin area, while the side-swept bangs draw attention away from the forehead. This creates a balanced look that elongates the face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a bob that ends at the jawline and side-swept bangs that start at the crown.

3. Pixie Cut

If you’re looking for a shorter style, a pixie cut is a great option for heart-shaped faces. This style adds volume to the top of the head, making the forehead appear narrower. The tapered sides and back draw attention to the chin, creating a balanced look. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short cut with tapered sides and back.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your heart-shaped face, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new style:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help keep your hair healthy and looking great. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture. For example, if you have fine hair, look for lightweight products that won’t weigh your hair down.

2. Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, it’s important to get regular trims. This will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look frizzy and damaged.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair, making it look dull and lifeless. To protect your hair, use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don’t like my new haircut?

If you’re not happy with your new haircut, don’t be afraid to speak up. Talk to your stylist and let them know what you don’t like about the cut. They may be able to make some adjustments to help you achieve the look you want.

Can I still wear my hair up with these haircuts?

Absolutely! These haircuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and ponytails.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and texture. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it look dry and damaged.

Conclusion

When it comes to finding the best haircut for heart shaped faces, the key is to create balance by drawing attention away from the forehead and towards the chin. Whether you prefer long layers, a bob with side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut, there’s a style out there that will complement your face shape. With the right products, regular trims, and a little bit of care, you can maintain your new style and look amazing every day!
