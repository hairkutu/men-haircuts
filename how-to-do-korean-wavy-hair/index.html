How To Do Korean Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight locks and want to switch things up with some gorgeous Korean wavy hair? Look no further! In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve those luscious waves. From the tools you'll need to how to maintain your style, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prep Your Hair
  • Curl Your Hair
  • Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Tools You'll Need

Before we begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • 1-inch curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Comb or brush
  • Hairspray

Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Comb or brush your hair to ensure it's tangle-free.

Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling! Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for 10-15 seconds before releasing. Continue curling sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curl to create a more natural look. Remember to curl the hair away from your face for the sections framing your face.

Style Your Hair

Once you've curled your entire head, it's time to style it. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Apply a light hairspray to help hold the waves in place. If you want to add some dimension, use a hair straightener to straighten the ends of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Look

To keep your Korean wavy hair looking fresh, try these tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your waves from falling out.
  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and prolong your style.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your waves and make them look greasy.

FAQs

1. How long will my Korean wavy hair last?

If you take care of your hair properly, your waves should last up to 2-3 days.

2. Can I achieve Korean wavy hair on naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to use a curling iron to create more defined waves.

3. Is it necessary to use a heat protectant spray?

Yes! Using a heat protectant spray can help prevent damage to your hair from the curling iron.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

Now that you know how to achieve Korean wavy hair, give it a try and see how it transforms your look! With a little practice, you'll be a pro in no time.
