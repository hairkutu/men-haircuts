How To Cut Side Swept Bangs On A Wig The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline May 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your wig's dull and lifeless look? Do you want to add some personality and flair to it? Then, why not try cutting some side swept bangs? Not only will it give your wig a fresh new look, but it will also frame your face beautifully. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut side swept bangs on a wig step by step.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs on a Wig

What You Will Need:

  • A wig
  • A comb
  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • Hair clips

Step by Step Guide:

Follow these simple steps to cut side swept bangs on your wig:

  1. First, put on your wig and comb it thoroughly.
  2. Then, divide the section of hair that you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Use hair clips to secure the rest of the hair away from the section you're cutting.
  4. Next, comb the section of hair downwards, making sure that it's straight and even.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers, and cut it to your desired length. It's better to start with a longer length and gradually trim it as needed.
  6. Once you've cut the hair, comb it to make sure that it's even.
  7. Repeat the process on the other side.
  8. Finally, style your bangs by combing them to the side.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect side swept bangs on your wig:

  • Always use a pair of sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Cut the hair gradually to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Comb the hair frequently to make sure that it's even.
  • If your wig is made of synthetic hair, use a lower heat setting when styling it to avoid damaging the fibers.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side swept bangs on a wig:

Can I cut bangs on a curly wig?

Yes, you can. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it's dry. So, make sure to cut it longer than your desired length.

How do I trim my bangs if I cut them too short?

If you accidentally cut your bangs too short, don't panic. Wait for them to grow out a little bit, and then trim them gradually until you achieve your desired length.

Hair Care:

To maintain your wig's side swept bangs, make sure to comb them regularly and trim them as needed. For synthetic hair wigs, use a wig conditioner to keep them soft and shiny. For human hair wigs, use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair.

With these simple steps and tips, you can easily cut side swept bangs on your wig and give it a whole new look. Go ahead and try it out, and don't forget to share your before and after pictures with us!
