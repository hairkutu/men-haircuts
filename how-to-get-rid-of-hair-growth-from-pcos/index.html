How To Get Rid Of Hair Growth From Pcos In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects many women. One of the common symptoms of PCOS is hair growth in unwanted areas such as the face, chest, back, and stomach. If you're struggling with hair growth from PCOS, don't worry. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of hair growth from PCOS effectively.

Table of Content

  • How to Get Rid of Hair Growth from PCOS
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Manage Hair Growth from PCOS
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for PCOS

How to Get Rid of Hair Growth from PCOS

There are several methods to get rid of hair growth from PCOS. These include:

  • Shaving
  • Waxing
  • Threading
  • Laser Hair Removal
  • Electrolysis

However, keep in mind that these methods are not permanent solutions and may need to be repeated regularly. If you're looking for a long-term solution, you may want to consider medication or hormone therapy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of hair growth from PCOS:

  1. Choose a hair removal method that suits you best.
  2. Prepare your skin before hair removal by cleaning and exfoliating the area.
  3. If you're using a hair removal cream, apply it on the area and leave it on for the recommended time.
  4. If you're shaving, make sure to use shaving cream to avoid irritated skin.
  5. If you're waxing, make sure to apply the wax in the direction of hair growth and pull it off in the opposite direction.
  6. If you're threading, make sure to use a thread that is suitable for your skin type.
  7. If you're considering laser hair removal or electrolysis, consult with a professional to determine the best course of action.
  8. After hair removal, moisturize the area to prevent ingrown hairs and irritation.

Tips to Manage Hair Growth from PCOS

Aside from hair removal methods, here are some tips to manage hair growth from PCOS:

  • Consult with a doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific case of PCOS.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your skin that may cause irritation.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to manage PCOS symptoms.
  • Consider taking medication or hormone therapy to manage PCOS symptoms.
  • Stay active and exercise regularly to manage weight and hormone levels.

FAQs

Is hair growth from PCOS permanent?

No, hair growth from PCOS is not permanent. However, it may need to be managed regularly.

What is the best hair removal method for PCOS?

The best hair removal method for PCOS is the one that suits your skin type and preference. Consult with a professional to determine the best course of action for you.

Can medication or hormone therapy help manage hair growth from PCOS?

Yes, medication or hormone therapy may help manage hair growth from PCOS. Consult with a doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific case of PCOS.

Hair Care for PCOS

Aside from managing hair growth, it's also important to take care of your hair when you have PCOS. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair that may cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking supplements that promote healthy hair growth.

Managing hair growth from PCOS may be challenging, but it's important to remember that it's a common symptom and there are many ways to manage it effectively. Consult with a professional to determine the best course of action for you and remember to take care of your hair in the process.
