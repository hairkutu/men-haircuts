How To Pick The Right Blonde Hair Color

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about going blonde, it's important to pick the right shade for your skin tone and hair type. With so many shades of blonde to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to choosing the perfect blonde hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 3: Choose the Right Shade of Blonde
  • Step 4: Consult with a Professional
  • Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking the right blonde hair color is to determine your skin tone. This will help you choose a shade of blonde that complements your natural complexion. There are three basic skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral.

Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones and look best with cool shades of blonde like platinum, ash blonde, and silver blonde. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones and look best with warm shades of blonde like honey blonde, golden blonde, and caramel blonde. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones and can pull off both warm and cool shades of blonde.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Another important factor to consider when choosing a blonde hair color is your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it may take multiple sessions to achieve your desired shade of blonde. It's also important to consider how much maintenance you're willing to do. If you have naturally dark hair and want a light blonde shade, you may need to touch up your roots every few weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

Once you've determined your skin tone and considered your natural hair color, it's time to choose the right shade of blonde. Here are some popular shades of blonde to consider:

  • Platinum blonde: a cool, pale blonde shade
  • Ash blonde: a cool, muted blonde shade
  • Honey blonde: a warm, golden blonde shade
  • Caramel blonde: a warm, caramel-toned blonde shade
  • Golden blonde: a warm, sunny blonde shade
  • Strawberry blonde: a warm, reddish-blonde shade

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

While it's possible to dye your hair blonde at home, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional. A hair stylist can help you choose the best shade of blonde for your skin tone and hair type. They can also help you achieve your desired shade without damaging your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your blonde hair from turning brassy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your color from fading.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair blonde at home, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional. A hair stylist can help you choose the best shade of blonde for your skin tone and hair type. They can also help you achieve your desired shade without damaging your hair.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my roots?

A: If you have naturally dark hair and want a light blonde shade, you may need to touch up your roots every few weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for your blonde hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for blonde hair.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or salt water, as it can cause your color to fade.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and consulting with a professional, you can find the perfect shade of blonde for your hair type and skin tone.
