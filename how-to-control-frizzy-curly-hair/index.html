How To Control Frizzy Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it under control. Frizz is a common problem for those with curly hair, and it can be frustrating to deal with. However, there are some simple steps you can take to control frizzy curly hair and keep your locks looking gorgeous.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Control Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step in controlling frizzy curly hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will be more effective at taming frizz. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

Another important step is to avoid over-washing your hair. Curly hair is naturally dry, and washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to frizz. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use a gentle shampoo that is designed for curly hair.

When you do wash your hair, be sure to use a conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Look for a conditioner that is designed for curly hair and contains ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for controlling frizzy curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  4. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, scrunching it in with your fingers.
  5. Let your hair air dry, or use a diffuser on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for controlling frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair and make frizz worse.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this can help to reduce frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling frizzy curly hair:

  • Can I use a hair oil to control frizz? Yes, using a hair oil can help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Look for oils like argan oil, coconut oil, or jojoba oil.
  • Can I use a hair mask to control frizz? Yes, using a hair mask can help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Look for a mask that is designed for curly hair and contains ingredients like shea butter or avocado oil.
  • Can I use hairspray to control frizz? Yes, using a hairspray can help to hold your curls in place and reduce frizz. Look for a hairspray that is designed for curly hair and contains a flexible hold.

Hair Care

In addition to using the right products and styling techniques, it's important to take good care of your curly hair. Here are some tips for keeping your locks healthy and frizz-free:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using tight elastics or hair ties, as they can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips and taking good care of your curly hair, you can keep frizz at bay and enjoy gorgeous, healthy locks.
