Long Face Short Hair Black Woman A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Jun 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a black woman with a long face and short hair? Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect look for your long face and short hair. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks on how to maintain your hairstyle and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

What is a Long Face Short Hair?

A long face is characterized by a face shape that is longer than it is wide. This face shape is often referred to as an oblong or rectangular face shape. Short hair, on the other hand, is any hairstyle that falls above the shoulders. A long face short hair black woman is someone with a long, rectangular face shape who has opted for a short hairstyle.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for a Long Face Short Hair Black Woman?

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face short hair black woman, it is important to choose a style that will balance out the length of your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Go for a Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is cut close to the head. This hairstyle is perfect for a long face because it adds volume to the sides of your face, balancing out the length. It is also a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for busy women.

2. Try a Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for a long face short hair black woman. A bob cut that falls just above the chin will help to add width to your face, making it appear less long. You can also add layers to your bob cut to create more volume and texture.

3. Add Bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out a long face. Adding bangs to your short hairstyle will draw attention to your eyes and forehead, making your face appear shorter. You can choose from a variety of bang styles, including side-swept, blunt, or choppy.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Achieve the Perfect Look

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving the perfect look for a long face short hair black woman is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and texture. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture. This will also help to protect your hair from heat damage during styling.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair until it is completely dry. Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using a flat iron or curling iron, style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Remember to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Once you have styled your hair, add some finishing touches, such as hair spray or hair oil, to add shine and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have achieved the perfect look for your long face short hair, it is important to maintain your hairstyle to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Get Regular Trims

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it is important to get regular trims. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. Silk is also gentle on your hair and skin.

3. Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

While it may be tempting to style your hair every day, over-styling can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit the amount of heat styling you do and opt for low-maintenance hairstyles when possible.

FAQs

1. Will a Short Hairstyle Make My Face Look Longer?

Not necessarily. A short hairstyle can actually help to balance out the length of your face, as long as you choose the right style for your face shape.

2. Can I Wear Accessories with a Short Hairstyle?

Absolutely! You can wear accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and hats with a short hairstyle. Just make sure to choose accessories that complement your hairstyle and face shape.

3. How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

This will depend on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry or curly hair, you may be able to wash your hair every few days.

Hair Care for Long Face Short Hair Black Woman

Now that you know how to achieve and maintain the perfect look for your long face short hair, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair Regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help to keep your hair soft and healthy. Use a deep conditioner that is designed for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair. To prevent heat damage, use a heat protectant spray and limit the amount of heat styling you do.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect look for your long face short hair black woman and keep your hair healthy and strong. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Happy styling!
