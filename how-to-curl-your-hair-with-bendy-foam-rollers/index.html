How To Curl Your Hair With Bendy Foam Rollers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of using hot tools to curl your hair? Do you want to achieve bouncy curls without damaging your hair? Bendy foam rollers are a great alternative to curling irons and hot rollers. They are gentle on your hair and can give you beautiful curls that last all day. In this article, we will show you how to curl your hair with bendy foam rollers.

How to Curl Your Hair with Bendy Foam Rollers

Bendy foam rollers are an easy and affordable way to curl your hair. They come in different sizes to create different types of curls or waves. Here are the materials you will need:

  • Bendy foam rollers
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve beautiful curls with bendy foam rollers:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb or brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl.
  3. Take a small section of hair and roll it onto a bendy foam roller. Make sure the ends of your hair are tucked in, so they don't stick out.
  4. Bend the ends of the roller together to secure it in place. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  5. Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 2-3 hours or overnight for best results.
  6. Remove the rollers by gently bending them in the opposite direction of the curl. Do not pull them out, or you may damage your hair.
  7. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create a natural look.
  8. Spray your curls with hairspray to hold them in place.

Tips for Using Bendy Foam Rollers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results with bendy foam rollers:

  • For tighter curls, use smaller rollers and smaller sections of hair.
  • For looser waves, use larger rollers and larger sections of hair.
  • Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 2-3 hours or overnight for best results.
  • For extra hold, apply hairspray to each section of hair before rolling it onto the foam roller.
  • Don't use too much tension when rolling your hair onto the foam roller. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQs

How long do I leave bendy foam rollers in my hair?

For best results, leave the rollers in your hair for at least 2-3 hours or overnight.

Can I use bendy foam rollers on wet hair?

No, it's best to use bendy foam rollers on dry hair. Wet hair can cause the rollers to slip or not hold the curl as well.

How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

Use hairspray to hold your curls in place. You can also use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and prevent them from falling out.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while using bendy foam rollers:

  • Don't use bendy foam rollers too often, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools on your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with bendy foam rollers, you can achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. Follow these steps and tips for the best results, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
