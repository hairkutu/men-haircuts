How To Make Your Side Bangs Look Good A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have side bangs, you know how tricky they can be to style. They never seem to stay in place, and they can easily create a messy look if not done right. But don't worry! With these simple tips and tricks, you can make your side bangs look good every day. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect side bangs.

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Start with a Clean Slate
  • Step 2: Section Your Bangs
  • Step 3: Blow Dry Your Bangs
  • Step 4: Style Your Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Side Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Step 1: Start with a Clean Slate

Before you start styling your side bangs, make sure you start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and towel dry it until it's damp. Avoid applying any heavy products to your hair, as they can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Using a fine-toothed comb, section off your side bangs from the rest of your hair. Determine the length and shape that you want your bangs to be, and make sure they're even on both sides. Clip the rest of your hair back to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Bangs

Using a round brush, blow dry your side bangs from the roots to the tips. Make sure the brush is angled downward to create a smooth, sleek look. Use a low heat setting, and avoid using a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once your side bangs are dry, it's time to style them. You can use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look, or a curling iron to create soft, bouncy curls. Whatever style you choose, make sure you use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Bangs

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • If your bangs start to lose shape throughout the day, use a small amount of hairspray to keep them in place.

FAQs

How often should I trim my side bangs?

You should trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

What kind of brush should I use to blow dry my side bangs?

Use a round brush with natural bristles to create a smooth, sleek look. Avoid using metal brushes, as they can damage your hair.

Can I style my side bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your side bangs without heat by using a hair roller or bobby pins. Simply roll your bangs around the roller or pin, and leave them in for a few hours or overnight. When you take them out, you'll have soft, bouncy curls.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Aside from trimming your bangs regularly, there are a few things you can do to keep them looking their best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as they can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your side bangs look good every day. It may take some practice, but with a little patience and effort, you'll be able to achieve the perfect side bangs.
