How To Get Volume In Curly Hair Without Heat

Are you tired of using heat tools to add volume to your curly hair? Heat can damage your hair over time and it's not always necessary to achieve the volume you want. There are many ways to add volume to your hair without heat. In this article, we will show you how to get volume in curly hair without heat.

How to Get Volume in Curly Hair Without Heat

There are many ways to add volume to your curly hair without using heat. Here are some tips:

Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair can create volume, especially if you do it when your hair is damp. Divide your hair into sections, braid each section and leave it in overnight. In the morning, take out the braids and fluff your hair with your fingers.

Use Hair Rollers

You can use hair rollers to add volume to your curly hair without heat. Roll your hair onto the rollers when it's damp and leave them in overnight. In the morning, take out the rollers and fluff your hair with your fingers.

Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can create a lot of volume, but it can also damage your hair if you do it too often. To tease your hair, take a small section of hair and backcomb it with a comb. Repeat this process until you have the desired volume.

Use a Volumizing Mousse

Applying a volumizing mousse to your hair can help create volume without heat. Apply the mousse to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. This will create volume and define your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get volume in curly hair without heat:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight.
  5. In the morning, take out the braids and fluff your hair with your fingers.
  6. Alternatively, use hair rollers to add volume to your hair.
  7. Roll your hair onto the rollers when it's damp and leave them in overnight.
  8. In the morning, take out the rollers and fluff your hair with your fingers.
  9. If you want to tease your hair, take a small section of hair and backcomb it with a comb.
  10. Repeat this process until you have the desired volume.

Tips for Adding Volume to Curly Hair Without Heat

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to your curly hair without heat:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid using too much heat.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause frizz and damage your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a hair dryer to add volume to my curly hair?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to add volume to your curly hair. However, be careful not to use too much heat, as this can damage your hair over time.

How often should I tease my hair?

You should avoid teasing your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit teasing to once or twice a week.

What kind of volumizing mousse should I use?

Look for a volumizing mousse that is lightweight and won't weigh down your hair. You can also look for a mousse that is specifically designed for curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, as heat can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can add volume to your curly hair without using heat. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
