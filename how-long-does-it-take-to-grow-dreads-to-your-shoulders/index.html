How Long Does It Take To Grow Dreads To Your Shoulders

Written by Bardi May 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Dreadlocks or dreads are a popular hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It is a trendy and unique way to style hair, and many people are now opting for this hairstyle. Dreads can be grown to any length, but one of the most popular styles is growing them to your shoulders. However, the question that many people ask is, how long does it take to grow dreads to your shoulders? In this article, we will provide you with all the necessary information about growing dreads to your shoulders.

Description

Dreads are strands of hair that are matted and knotted together. They are formed by not combing or brushing your hair, which causes the hair to matt and form knots. Dreads can be grown to any length, and the time it takes to grow them depends on various factors.

How to Grow Dreads to Your Shoulders

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to grow dreads to your shoulders:

Step 1: Stop Combing and Brushing Your Hair

The first step in growing dreads is to stop combing and brushing your hair. This will cause your hair to start matting and forming knots.

Step 2: Keep Your Hair Clean

It is essential to keep your hair clean when growing dreads. Use a residue-free shampoo to wash your hair regularly. Avoid using conditioners or any other hair products that can cause buildup in your hair.

Step 3: Palm Roll Your Hair

Palm rolling your hair helps to form knots and tighten your dreads. To palm roll your hair, take a dreadlock and roll it between your palms in a clockwise direction.

Step 4: Be Patient

Growing dreads to your shoulders takes time, and you need to be patient. It can take anywhere from six months to a year or more, depending on various factors.

Tips for Growing Dreads to Your Shoulders

Here are some tips to help you grow dreads to your shoulders:

  • Don't use any hair products that contain wax or heavy oils
  • Avoid using a hairdryer as it can damage your hair
  • Avoid using rubber bands or elastics to tie your dreads
  • Be gentle when washing and drying your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreads to your shoulders:

How long does it take to grow dreads to your shoulders?

The time it takes to grow dreads to your shoulders depends on various factors such as hair type, length, and thickness. It can take anywhere from six months to a year or more.

Can you speed up the process of growing dreads?

No, you cannot speed up the process of growing dreads. It takes time and patience to grow dreads to your desired length.

How do you maintain dreads once they reach your shoulders?

To maintain your dreads, you need to keep them clean, palm roll them regularly, and avoid using hair products that can cause buildup. You can also get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Dreads

Here are some tips for taking care of your dreads:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo
  • Avoid using conditioners or any other hair products that can cause buildup
  • Palm roll your hair regularly to keep your dreads tight
  • Avoid using rubber bands or elastics to tie your dreads
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Growing dreads to your shoulders takes time, patience, and proper hair care. Following the above steps and tips will help you grow healthy and beautiful dreads to your desired length. Remember to be patient and enjoy the process.
