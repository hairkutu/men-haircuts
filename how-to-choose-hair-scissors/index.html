How To Choose Hair Scissors A Beginner s Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to start cutting hair as a hobby or profession? One of the most important tools you'll need is a good pair of hair scissors. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we'll break down the key factors to consider when selecting hair scissors.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose Hair Scissors
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing Hair Scissors
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Choose Hair Scissors

The first thing to consider is the type of hair you'll be cutting. Thicker, coarser hair requires a different type of scissor than thin, fine hair. Look for scissors that are specifically designed for the type of hair you'll be cutting.

The next factor to consider is the length of the scissors. Scissors come in various lengths, ranging from 4.5 inches to 7 inches. Longer scissors are better for cutting long hair, while shorter scissors are better for precision cutting and detailing.

The material of the scissors is also important. Stainless steel is the most common material used for hair scissors, as it is durable and easy to clean. However, higher quality scissors may be made of titanium or cobalt for added strength and sharpness.

Blade Types

The type of blade on the scissors is another important factor to consider. There are two main types of blades:

  • Straight blades are better for blunt cuts and removing length. They are also easier to sharpen.
  • Curved blades are better for precision cutting and creating layers. They are also more ergonomic, as they follow the natural curve of the hair.

Handle Types

The handle of the scissors is also important. There are two main types of handles:

  • Offset handles have a shorter thumb handle and a longer finger handle. They are more comfortable for people with smaller hands or those who use scissors for extended periods of time.
  • Opposing handles have the thumb and finger handles at the same level. They are better for people with larger hands or those who prefer a more traditional handle.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect hair scissors:

  1. Determine the type of hair you'll be cutting.
  2. Select the appropriate length for your needs.
  3. Choose the material of the scissors.
  4. Select the blade type that best suits your cutting style.
  5. Choose the handle type that is most comfortable for you.
  6. Consider the weight of the scissors.
  7. Read reviews and ask for recommendations from professionals.
  8. Try the scissors out before purchasing, if possible.
  9. Invest in a quality pair of scissors that will last for years to come.

Tips for Choosing Hair Scissors

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair scissors:

  • Invest in a good quality pair of scissors, as they will last longer and provide better results.
  • Consider purchasing a sharpening tool to keep your scissors sharp and in good condition.
  • Try out different scissors before making a purchase to find the one that feels most comfortable in your hand.
  • Ask for recommendations from other professionals in the industry.
  • Consider purchasing a case or holder to protect your scissors when not in use.

FAQs

What is the difference between straight and curved blades?

Straight blades are better for blunt cuts and removing length, while curved blades are better for precision cutting and creating layers.

What is the best material for hair scissors?

Stainless steel is the most common material used for hair scissors, but higher quality scissors may be made of titanium or cobalt for added strength and sharpness.

Do I need to sharpen my scissors?

Yes, it is important to sharpen your scissors regularly to maintain their sharpness and effectiveness.

Hair Care Tips

Proper hair care is important to maintain healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as they can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair scissors, you'll be well on your way to becoming a skilled hair cutter!
