How To Make Black Nappy Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for ways to make your black nappy hair curly, you're not alone. Many people with this hair type find it difficult to achieve curls that last. However, with the right techniques and products, you can transform your hair into beautiful, bouncy curls. In this guide, we'll show you how to make black nappy hair curly in a few simple steps.

How to Make Black Nappy Hair Curly

Before we begin, it's important to understand that black nappy hair requires a lot of moisture to remain healthy. In order to make your hair curly, you'll need to use products that add moisture and hydration to your strands. Here's how to make black nappy hair curly:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

The first step in making your hair curly is to shampoo and condition your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner to add moisture to your hair. Leave the conditioner in your hair for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After shampooing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your strands. This will help to detangle your hair and add moisture to your curls. Be sure to apply the leave-in conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Cream

Next, apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and add bounce to your hair. Be sure to apply the cream evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser to Dry Your Hair

Once you've applied the curl enhancing cream, use a diffuser to dry your hair. This will help to set your curls and add volume to your hair. Be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Use a Curling Wand to Define Your Curls

If you want to add more definition to your curls, use a curling wand to curl your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the curling wand to avoid damaging your hair. Curl small sections of your hair at a time, holding the curling wand in place for a few seconds before releasing it.

Tips for Making Black Nappy Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making black nappy hair curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by wrapping it in a silk scarf or bonnet
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often, as this can cause damage

FAQ: Making Black Nappy Hair Curly

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly without using heat. Try using a twist-out or braid-out technique to create curls without using heat.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair once a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: What products should I use to make my hair curly?

A: Use products that add moisture and hydration to your hair, such as a sulfate-free shampoo, deep conditioner, leave-in conditioner, and curl enhancing cream.

Hair Care for Black Nappy Hair

When caring for your black nappy hair, it's important to keep it moisturized and hydrated. Use products that add moisture to your hair, and avoid using products that contain alcohol. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using heat tools, and avoid using heat on your hair too often. Finally, be sure to trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your black nappy hair curly and beautiful. With a little bit of patience and practice, you can achieve the perfect curls that you've always wanted.
