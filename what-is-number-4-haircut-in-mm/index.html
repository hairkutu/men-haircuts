What Is Number 4 Haircut In Mm

Written by Luffy Aug 21, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re someone who loves to get a haircut, then you must have heard of the different types of haircuts available. One of the most popular ones is the Number 4 Haircut. But what exactly is it? In this blog post, we will explore what a Number 4 Haircut is in mm and how you can get it done.

Description

A Number 4 Haircut is a type of buzz cut that leaves your hair at 1/2 inch or 12.7 mm in length. It is a relatively short haircut that is perfect for people who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. This type of haircut is popular among men, but women can also opt for this style.

How to

If you’re looking to get a Number 4 Haircut, you can either go to a barber or do it yourself at home. Here are the steps to get a Number 4 Haircut:

Step 1: Get the right tools

You will need a good quality clipper with a guard number 4. If you’re doing it at home, make sure you have a mirror, a comb, and a pair of scissors.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Attach the guard

Attach the guard number 4 to your clipper. If you’re not sure which one it is, check the manual that came with your clipper.

Step 4: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair by running the clipper over your head. Make sure you go over each section of hair evenly. If you’re doing it at home, use a comb to lift your hair and cut it with scissors to create a more even look.

Step 5: Check for uneven spots

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check for any uneven spots. If you find any, use your clipper or scissors to fix them.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect Number 4 Haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard if you’re not sure about the length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Use scissors to trim your sideburns and neckline.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Number 4 Haircut:

Is the Number 4 Haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, it is suitable for all hair types.

Can women get a Number 4 Haircut?

Yes, women can get a Number 4 Haircut.

How often should I get a Number 4 Haircut?

You should get a Number 4 Haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the length.

Hair Care

After getting a Number 4 Haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many hair products.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

In conclusion, a Number 4 Haircut is a popular and easy-to-maintain hairstyle that can be done at home or at a barber shop. With the right tools and techniques, you can get a perfect Number 4 Haircut in mm. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
