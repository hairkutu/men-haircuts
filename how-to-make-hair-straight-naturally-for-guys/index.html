How To Make Hair Straight Naturally For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a guy with curly or wavy hair, you may be looking for ways to make it straight without using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Luckily, there are plenty of natural methods you can try to achieve straighter hair without damaging your locks. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to make hair straight naturally for guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Straight Naturally for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

How to Make Hair Straight Naturally for Guys

There are several natural methods you can use to make your hair straight:

  • Blow drying
  • Hair brushing
  • Hair wrapping
  • Coconut milk and lemon juice
  • Milk and honey
  • Aloe vera gel and olive oil
  • Egg and olive oil
  • Banana and yogurt

Each of these methods can help you achieve straighter hair, but some may work better for you than others depending on your hair type and texture. It may take some trial and error to find the method that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to make hair straight naturally for guys using some of the most popular methods:

Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair can help straighten it out. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp but not dripping wet.
  2. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a round brush to pull it straight as you blow dry it.
  4. Repeat this process until all sections are dry and straight.

Hair Brushing

Brushing your hair can also help straighten it out. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp but not dripping wet.
  2. Apply a smoothing serum or hair oil to your hair to help it stay straight.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and brush each section straight as you blow dry it.
  4. Repeat this process until all sections are dry and straight.

Hair Wrapping

Hair wrapping involves wrapping your hair around your head to help it stay straight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp but not dripping wet.
  2. Apply a straightening cream or gel to your hair to help it stay straight.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around your head, securing it with clips or bobby pins.
  4. Leave your hair wrapped for several hours or overnight.
  5. Unwrap your hair and brush it out to reveal straight hair.

Coconut Milk and Lemon Juice

Coconut milk and lemon juice can help straighten your hair naturally. Here's how to use this method:

  1. Mix 1 cup of coconut milk with 1 tablespoon of lemon juice.
  2. Apply the mixture to your hair and massage it in.
  3. Cover your hair with a shower cap and leave it on for 30 minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair with water and shampoo as usual.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you've achieved straight hair using one of the methods above, you'll want to maintain it for as long as possible. Here are some tips to help you do that:

  • Avoid getting your hair wet or sweating too much, as this can cause it to revert back to its natural state.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it appear greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

FAQs

Can I make my hair permanently straight without using chemicals?

No, natural methods can only temporarily straighten your hair. If you want permanent straightening, you'll need to use chemical treatments like a relaxer or keratin treatment.

Is it safe to use heat styling tools to straighten my hair?

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair if used too frequently or at high temperatures. It's best to use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

How often should I use natural methods to straighten my hair?

It's best to use natural methods sparingly to avoid damaging your hair. Once a week or every other week is usually sufficient.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

Once you've achieved straight hair, you'll want to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for straight hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and using natural methods to straighten your hair, you can achieve the sleek, straight look you desire without damaging your locks. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
