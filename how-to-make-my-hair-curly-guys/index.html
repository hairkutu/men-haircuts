How To Make My Hair Curly Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy who wants to add some texture to your hair, making it curly is a great way to do it. While some men are naturally blessed with curly locks, others need a little help to achieve the look. In this guide, we'll show you how to make your hair curly guys, whether you have straight or wavy hair. Read on to learn more.

Description

Curly hair can add a lot of personality to your look. It's a great way to switch up your style and make a statement. But if you don't have naturally curly hair, it can be a challenge to achieve the look. That's where this guide comes in. We'll show you how to make your hair curly guys, step by step.

How to Make My Hair Curly Guys: Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in making your hair curly is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry it until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply Curl Enhancing Product

Next, apply a curl enhancing product to your hair. This could be a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from roots to ends.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into several sections, using clips or hair ties to keep them separated. The more sections you create, the tighter your curls will be.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly, wrapping it around your finger as you go. Hold the twisted section of hair for a few seconds, and then release it. Repeat this process with each section of hair, until your entire head is twisted.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Once you've twisted all of your hair, let it air dry. Don't use a blow dryer, as this can disrupt the formation of the curls. You can speed up the drying process by sitting under a hooded dryer or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Step 6: Separate the Curls

Once your hair is completely dry, gently separate the curls with your fingers. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly Guys

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making your hair curly:

  • Use a curl enhancing product that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Twist your hair tightly for tighter curls, or loosely for looser curls.
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying, as this can disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a light hold hairspray to set the curls.

FAQ

Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without using heat. The twisting method we described above doesn't require any heat, and there are also products like foam rollers and flexi rods that can create curls without heat.

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, tighter curls will last longer than looser curls. Using a light hold hairspray can help to prolong the life of your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.

Conclusion

Making your hair curly guys is easier than you might think. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a curly look that suits your style. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
