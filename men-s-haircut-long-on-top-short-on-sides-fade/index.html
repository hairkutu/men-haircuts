Men s Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides Fade

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and for good reason. It's versatile, low-maintenance, and looks great on any face shape. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting this haircut, provide you with some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The men's haircut long on top short on sides fade is a popular hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head with shorter sides that gradually fade into the skin. This haircut is often styled with a side part or slicked back for a more polished look, but can also be left messy for a more relaxed vibe. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and modern look without spending too much time on their hair.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides Fade

Getting the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade is a relatively easy process. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Length

The first step in getting the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade is to decide how long you want your hair to be on the top. This will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the hair shorter on top to create a more elongated look.

Step 2: Decide on the Fade

Next, you'll need to decide on the fade. The fade is the gradual transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides. There are several types of fades, including the low fade, high fade, and mid fade. The type of fade you choose will depend on your personal preference and the look you're going for.

Step 3: Choose Your Styling Technique

Finally, you'll need to choose your styling technique. The men's haircut long on top short on sides fade can be styled in a variety of ways, including a side part, slicked back, or messy. Choose the style that best suits your personality and lifestyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides Fade

Now that you've got your new haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Invest in styling products, such as pomade or wax, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Consider investing in a good quality hair dryer to help you style your hair.

FAQ

1. Is the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade is suitable for all face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length of the hair on top to suit your face shape.

2. How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

3. Can I style my hair in different ways?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that best suits your personality and lifestyle.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Overall, the men's haircut long on top short on sides fade is a versatile and stylish haircut that can be customized to suit any face shape and personal style. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, you can keep your hair looking fresh and stylish for months to come.
