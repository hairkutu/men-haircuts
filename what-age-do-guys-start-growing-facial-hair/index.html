What Age Do Guys Start Growing Facial Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 30, 2023 · 9 min read

Facial hair is often associated with masculinity, and it’s no wonder why most guys are curious about when they can start growing their own. While there’s no specific age when facial hair starts to grow, there are certain factors that may influence its growth. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about what age do guys start growing facial hair.

Table of Contents

  • Factors That Influence Facial Hair Growth in Men
  • How to Grow Facial Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing and Maintaining Facial Hair
  • FAQs About Facial Hair Growth in Men
  • Facial Hair Care: Dos and Don’ts

Factors That Influence Facial Hair Growth in Men

Facial hair growth is influenced by several factors, including genetics, hormones, and age. Here’s a breakdown of each:

Genetics

Genetics play a significant role in determining when a guy will start growing facial hair. If your father or brothers have a thick beard or mustache, chances are you’ll be able to grow one too. However, if your family members don’t grow facial hair, it doesn’t mean you won’t be able to – it just means you may have to wait a little longer.

Hormones

Testosterone is the hormone responsible for facial hair growth in men. As boys enter puberty, their bodies start to produce more testosterone, which leads to the growth of facial hair. However, the level and timing of testosterone production can vary from person to person, which is why some guys may start growing facial hair earlier or later than others.

Age

Most boys start to develop facial hair between the ages of 14 and 16. However, some may start as early as 11, while others may not see any growth until they’re in their late teens or early twenties. It’s important to note that facial hair growth is a gradual process – it doesn’t happen overnight. So, if you’re not seeing any growth yet, don’t worry – it will come in due time.

How to Grow Facial Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking to grow facial hair, there are several things you can do to encourage its growth. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Let It Grow

The first step to growing facial hair is to let it grow. This means putting down the razor and letting your hair grow out. It may be tempting to shave off any patchy or uneven growth, but resist the urge – it’s all part of the process.

Step 2: Keep It Clean

As your facial hair grows, it’s important to keep it clean. Use a gentle cleanser to wash your face and beard, and be sure to rinse thoroughly. This will help prevent any irritation or breakouts.

Step 3: Moisturize

Facial hair can be dry and itchy, especially in the early stages of growth. To keep your beard healthy and hydrated, use a beard oil or balm to moisturize your hair and skin.

Step 4: Trim It Up

Once your beard starts to fill in, you can start trimming it to keep it looking neat and tidy. Invest in a good pair of scissors or clippers and trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.

Tips for Growing and Maintaining Facial Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow and maintain your facial hair:

  • Be patient – facial hair growth takes time, so don’t get discouraged if you’re not seeing results right away.
  • Eat a healthy diet – a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth.
  • Exercise regularly – regular exercise can help improve circulation, which can promote hair growth.
  • Avoid stress – stress can lead to hair loss, so try to manage your stress levels as much as possible.
  • Get enough sleep – getting enough sleep is essential for promoting overall health, including hair growth.

FAQs About Facial Hair Growth in Men

Q: Will shaving make my facial hair grow back thicker?

A: No, shaving does not make facial hair grow back thicker. It may appear thicker because the hair is blunt at the end, but it will eventually grow back to its normal texture and thickness.

Q: Can I use hair products on my facial hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair products on your facial hair, but be sure to choose products that are specifically designed for facial hair. Regular hair products may contain ingredients that can irritate the skin or clog pores.

Q: How often should I wash my facial hair?

A: It’s recommended to wash your facial hair once or twice a week, depending on how oily or dirty it gets. Over-washing can strip the hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Facial Hair Care: Dos and Don’ts

Here are some dos and don’ts to keep in mind when caring for your facial hair:

  • Do use a beard oil or balm to moisturize your hair and skin.
  • Do trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.
  • Don’t over-wash your facial hair, as this can lead to dryness and breakage.
  • Don’t use regular hair products on your facial hair, as they may contain ingredients that can irritate the skin or clog pores.

In conclusion, there’s no specific age when guys start growing facial hair. It’s influenced by genetics, hormones, and age, and can vary from person to person. However, by following the tips and advice outlined in this article, you can encourage healthy facial hair growth and maintain a neat and tidy beard or mustache.
