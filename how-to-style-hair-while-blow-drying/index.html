How To Style Hair While Blow Drying Tips And Tricks

If you want to achieve a salon-worthy hairstyle, blow drying is a great way to do it. But not all of us know how to style our hair while blow drying. In this article, we will teach you some tips and tricks on how to style your hair while blow drying, step by step. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips.

How to Style Hair While Blow Drying

Before we start, it's important to know that blow drying can cause damage to your hair if not done correctly. Make sure that you have a heat protectant spray and a good quality hair dryer. Here are the steps on how to style your hair while blow drying:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and remove any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. Make sure to distribute the spray evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips. This will make it easier for you to blow dry your hair.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair using a round brush. Use the brush to pull your hair up and away from your scalp while blow drying. This will create volume and lift.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat the process for each section of your hair until you have blow dried all of it.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished blow drying your hair, you can style it the way you want. Use a hair spray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style your hair while blow drying:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair and remove any excess water
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  4. Section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips
  5. Start blow drying your hair using a round brush
  6. Use the brush to pull your hair up and away from your scalp while blow drying
  7. Repeat the process for each section of your hair
  8. Once you have finished blow drying your hair, you can style it the way you want
  9. Use a hair spray to hold your style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks on how to style your hair while blow drying:

  • Use a good quality hair dryer with different heat settings
  • Choose the right brush for your hair type
  • Blow dry your hair from the roots to the ends for best results
  • Use a cool shot button to set your style in place
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair to avoid damage
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style hair while blow drying:

Can I blow dry my hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage to your hair. It's best to let your hair air dry naturally as much as possible.

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

No, you should never use a round brush on wet hair as it can cause damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to remove tangles.

How can I add volume to my hair while blow drying?

Use a round brush to lift your hair up and away from your scalp while blow drying. This will create volume and lift.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals

Now that you know how to style your hair while blow drying, you can achieve a salon-worthy hairstyle at home. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage. With the right tools and techniques, you can have beautiful, healthy hair.
