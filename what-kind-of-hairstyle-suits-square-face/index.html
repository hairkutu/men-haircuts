What Kind Of Hairstyle Suits Square Face

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but none of them seem to suit your square face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that suit square faces. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

  • Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and is characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline of the same width. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a square face is to soften the angles of the face and create a more oval-shaped appearance.

The right hairstyle can make a significant difference in how you look and feel. It can boost your confidence and enhance your features, making you look more attractive. Therefore, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face, it is essential to consider the length and texture of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Soft Layers: Soft layers can help to soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to shorten the length of your face and create a more oval shape.
  • Long Hairstyles: Long hairstyles can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.
  • Curly or Wavy Hairstyles: Curly or wavy hairstyles can add volume and create a more rounded appearance.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a hairstyle for a square face, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look. To achieve this hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to shorten the length of your face and create a more oval shape. To achieve this hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it straight.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front and comb it to the opposite side.
  4. Clip the section of hair in place and cut it to the desired length.
  5. Release the section of hair and style it with a round brush.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle for your square face, it is essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair short if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a square face. However, it is essential to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape. Short hairstyles with soft layers and side-swept bangs can help to soften the angles of your face.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a square face. However, it is essential to create volume at the crown of your head to balance out your face shape. You can also add soft layers around your face to soften the angles.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as it can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swim cap or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know what kind of hairstyle suits square face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember, the right hairstyle can make a significant difference in how you look and feel, so choose wisely!
