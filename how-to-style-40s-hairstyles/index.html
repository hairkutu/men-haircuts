How To Style 40S Hairstyles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a vintage lover who adores the elegant and classy hairstyles of the 1940s? Well, you're in luck because this guide will help you achieve iconic 40s hairstyles with ease. Whether you have short hair or long hair, there's a 40s-inspired hairdo that will suit you. Keep reading to learn how to style 40s hairstyles.

Description

The 1940s was an era of glamour, elegance, and style. Women during this time had a passion for dressing up and looking their best, especially when it came to their hairstyles. The hairstyles of the 40s were characterized by their soft waves, intricate rolls, and feminine curls. These hairstyles were often adorned with flowers, hats, and scarves.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a curling iron, hairpins, a comb, and hairspray. Here are some steps to follow:

For Short Hair

If you have short hair, you can still create 40s-inspired hairstyles. Here's how:

  1. Start by curling your hair using a curling iron. Make sure the curls are tight.
  2. Take small sections of hair and roll them towards the back of your head.
  3. Secure the rolls with hairpins.
  4. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.

For Medium-Length Hair

If you have medium-length hair, you can go for a classic 40s style like victory rolls:

  1. Start by curling your hair using a curling iron.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and roll it towards the back.
  4. Secure the roll with hairpins.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.

For Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can create glamorous 40s hairstyles like Hollywood waves:

  1. Start by curling your hair using a curling iron.
  2. Brush out your curls to create soft waves.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and roll it towards the back.
  4. Secure the roll with hairpins.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a classic 40s hairstyle:

  1. Start by curling your hair using a curling iron.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and roll it towards the back.
  4. Secure the roll with hairpins.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side of your head.
  6. Take the remaining hair and roll it towards the back of your head.
  7. Secure the roll with hairpins.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.
  9. Add a hair accessory like a flower or a scarf for a touch of glamour.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 40s hairstyle:

  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with accessories like flowers or scarves.
  • Practice makes perfect, so don't get discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.
  • Use a curling iron with a smaller barrel for tighter curls.

FAQs

What products should I use to achieve 40s hairstyles?

You'll need a curling iron, hairpins, a comb, and hairspray to achieve 40s hairstyles.

Can I achieve 40s hairstyles on short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve 40s hairstyles on short hair. Try creating rolls or pin curls.

Is it necessary to use hair accessories?

No, it's not necessary to use hair accessories, but they can add a touch of glamour to your hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. Use a heat protectant before using a curling iron, and don't use too much hairspray as it can dry out your hair. Make sure you're also getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to style 40s hairstyles, go ahead and try them out for your next special occasion or even for your everyday look. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 40s-inspired hairstyle in no time.
