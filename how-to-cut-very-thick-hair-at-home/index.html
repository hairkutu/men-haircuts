How To Cut Very Thick Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Cutting very thick hair can be a daunting task, especially if you are not a professional hairdresser. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home. In this post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut very thick hair at home.

How to Cut Very Thick Hair at Home

Before we get started, let's go over the tools you will need to cut very thick hair at home.

Tools You Will Need

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-toothed comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle

Once you have these tools, you are ready to start cutting your very thick hair. Follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wet your hair. Spray your hair with water using a spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and prevent split ends.

Step 2: Section your hair. Use hair clips to divide your hair into sections. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 3: Comb your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will make it easier to cut.

Step 4: Cut your hair. Start cutting your hair from the bottom section. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 5: Check your hair. Once you have finished cutting, check your hair in the mirror to make sure it is even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut very thick hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Cut slowly and carefully.
  • Don't cut too much hair off at once.
  • Check your hair frequently to make sure it is even.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut very thick hair at home?

A: Yes, you can cut very thick hair at home as long as you have the right tools and techniques.

Q: What type of scissors should I use to cut very thick hair?

A: Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.

Q: How often should I cut my very thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and desired hairstyle. As a general rule, it is recommended to cut your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your very thick hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting very thick hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips, and take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and beautiful.
