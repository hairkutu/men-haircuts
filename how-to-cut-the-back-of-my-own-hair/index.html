How To Cut The Back Of My Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon to get your hair cut? Do you want to save some cash and cut your own hair at home? Cutting the back of your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to cut the back of your own hair.

Description

Cutting the back of your own hair can be a challenging task, but with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut in the comfort of your own home. With the right tools and techniques, you can save money and time by cutting your own hair. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut the back of your own hair.

How to Cut the Back of My Own Hair

Before we begin, you will need a few tools to get started. You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, a hand mirror, and a large mirror. Once you have gathered your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be difficult to cut and can lead to uneven results. Once your hair is clean and dry, comb it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

To make it easier to cut the back of your own hair, divide it into sections. Use your comb to create a center part, and then divide your hair into two sections. Clip one section out of the way and focus on the other section.

Step 3: Use Your Hand Mirror to View the Back of Your Head

Stand in front of a large mirror and hold a hand mirror behind your head. This will allow you to see the back of your head and the section you are working on. Adjust the mirrors as needed to get a clear view.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair in the back section. Begin at the bottom and work your way up. Take small sections of hair at a time and cut straight across. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have finished cutting the back section, release the clipped section and check your progress. Use your hand mirror to view the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat steps 2-5 on the other side of your head. Remember to cut small sections of hair at a time and to cut evenly on both sides.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your own hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up
  • Use sharp scissors to get a clean cut
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Check your progress frequently using your hand mirror
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you are unsure or uncomfortable

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of your own hair:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, a hand mirror, and a large mirror.

How do I view the back of my head?

Use a hand mirror to view the back of your head while standing in front of a large mirror.

Hair Care

Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save money and time, but it is important to take care of your hair after cutting it. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a good quality hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Cutting the back of your own hair may seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it. Follow these steps and tips, and you will be on your way to achieving a salon-worthy haircut at home. Happy cutting!
