How To Trim Top Of Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to trim the top of men's hair with clippers. With the help of this guide, you'll learn how to trim your hair at home, save money, and look your best. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the best techniques for achieving the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You'll Need
  2. Preparing Your Hair
  3. Cutting Your Hair
  4. Cleaning Up
  5. Tips for a Great Cut
  6. Frequently Asked Questions
  7. Hair Care After the Cut

Tools You'll Need

Before you start, you'll need a few tools to trim your hair with clippers:

  • Clippers with adjustable guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair cape or towel
  • Handheld mirror
  • Clipper oil

Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in and your blades are clean and sharp. Dull blades can pull hair and cause uneven cuts.

Preparing Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to lie. If you have longer hair, you may want to section it off and clip it up to work on one area at a time.

Step 1: Choose Your Guard Length

Choose the guard length you want to use based on the length of hair you want to leave on the top of your head. If you're not sure, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 2: Start at the Front

Starting at the front of your head, place the clippers at your hairline and move them back in a straight line. Use the comb to lift the hair as you go for an even cut. Repeat this step until you've cut the front section of your hair.

Step 3: Work Your Way Back

Now move to the sides of your head, using the same technique of placing the clippers at the hairline and moving them back in a straight line. Use the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut. Work your way around the sides and back of your head until you've trimmed all the hair on top.

Cleaning Up

Once you've trimmed the top of your hair, it's time to clean up the edges. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs around your ears, neck, and hairline. Then, remove the guard from your clippers and use them to trim around the edges for a clean finish.

Tips for a Great Cut

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great cut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you're not sure what length you want.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Trim small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 3-4 weeks to maintain your desired length and style.

Can I use clippers to trim my beard?

Yes, you can use clippers to trim your beard. Just make sure you use the appropriate guard length and take your time.

Do I need to oil my clippers?

Yes, you should oil your clippers before and after each use to keep them running smoothly and to prolong their lifespan.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've trimmed your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Regular trims will also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Congratulations, you've learned how to trim the top of men's hair with clippers! With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a great cut at home and save money on trips to the barber. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and maintain your clippers for the best results.
