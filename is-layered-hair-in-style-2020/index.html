Is Layered Hair In Style 2020

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layered hair has been a popular hairstyle for years, but is it still in style in 2020? The answer is a resounding yes! Layered hair is still a trendy and stylish option for those looking to switch up their hairstyle. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve layered hair, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Description

Layered hair is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair into different lengths, creating layers. These layers add volume and texture to the hair, giving it a more dynamic look. This hairstyle can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type, making it a versatile option for anyone looking for a change.

How to Achieve Layered Hair

If you're looking to achieve a layered hairstyle, it's best to go to a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair and determine the best way to cut it to achieve the desired look. However, if you're feeling brave and want to try it at home, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to cut evenly.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Using sharp scissors, start cutting your hair in a downward motion. Cut at an angle to create layers. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up.

Step 4: Check your work

Once you've cut all the layers, check your work to ensure that it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Hair

Once you've achieved the perfect layered hairstyle, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance the layers and add texture.
  • Avoid using a lot of heat on your hair, as this can damage the layers and make them look frizzy.

FAQ

What face shapes does layered hair look best on?

Layered hair can look great on any face shape, but it's particularly flattering on those with round or square faces. The layers help to create the illusion of a longer face.

What hair types work best with layered hair?

Layered hair can work with any hair type, but it's particularly flattering on those with thin or fine hair. The layers help to add volume and texture to the hair.

Can I still wear my hair up with layers?

Absolutely! In fact, layered hair can look great in a ponytail or updo. The layers add texture and dimension to the style.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially if you have layered hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, layered hair is still in style in 2020 and is a great option for those looking to switch up their hairstyle. Whether you go to a professional hairstylist or try it at home, make sure to maintain your layers properly to keep them looking fresh and healthy.
