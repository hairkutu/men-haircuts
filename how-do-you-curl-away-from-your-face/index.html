How Do You Curl Away From Your Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curling your hair is an excellent way to add volume and dimension to your locks. However, it can be challenging to achieve the perfect curl, especially when you want to curl away from your face. Fortunately, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve the desired look with ease. In this post, we'll show you step-by-step how to curl away from your face and provide you with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect curl.

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl Away from Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs about Curling Away from Your Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Curl Away from Your Face

Curling away from your face means that the curls will be facing outwards, giving you a fuller and more voluminous look. To achieve this look, you'll need to use a curling iron with a barrel that's larger than 1 inch in diameter. This will help create loose and romantic curls that frame your face perfectly. Here's how to curl away from your face:

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl away from your face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  2. Once your hair is dry, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid heat damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. For thick hair, divide it into four sections. For thin hair, divide it into six sections.
  4. Take a section of hair and hold the curling iron horizontally, with the barrel facing away from your face.
  5. Place the section of hair at the base of the curling iron and wrap it around the barrel, holding the ends with your fingers.
  6. Hold the curling iron in place for about 10 seconds.
  7. Release the curl and repeat the process with the other sections of your hair.
  8. Once you've finished curling your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls for a more natural look.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Away from Your Face

Curling away from your face can be tricky, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect curl:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel larger than 1 inch in diameter to create loose and romantic curls.
  • Make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid heat damage.
  • Hold the curling iron horizontally and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding the ends with your fingers.
  • Hold the curling iron in place for about 10 seconds to ensure that the curl sets.
  • Use your fingers to separate the curls for a more natural look.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQs about Curling Away from Your Face

Here are some FAQs about curling away from your face:

Q: Can I curl away from my face with a straightening iron?

A: No, you can't curl away from your face with a straightening iron. A curling iron is the best tool for creating curls.

Q: How long should I hold the curling iron in place?

A: You should hold the curling iron in place for about 10 seconds to ensure that the curl sets.

Q: What's the best heat setting to use?

A: The heat setting you use will depend on your hair type. For fine hair, use a lower heat setting. For thick hair, use a higher heat setting.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause the curls to lose their shape.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.

Now that you know how to curl away from your face, it's time to give it a try. With the right technique and tools, you'll be able to achieve the perfect curl in no time.
