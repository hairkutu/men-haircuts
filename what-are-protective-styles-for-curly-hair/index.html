What Are Protective Styles For Curly Hair

Written by Petter May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be prone to damage and breakage. Protective styles are a great way to keep your curly hair healthy and looking great. But what are protective styles for curly hair? In this article, we’ll go over the basics of protective styling, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips for success, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Protective styles are hairstyles that help to protect hair from damage and breakage. They work by keeping the hair in a low-manipulation state, which means that the hair is not being styled or manipulated on a daily basis. This can help to prevent damage caused by heat styling, chemical treatments, and other factors that can be harmful to curly hair.

How to

The first step in creating a protective style for curly hair is to start with clean, moisturized hair. This means that you should shampoo and condition your hair before beginning the styling process. Once your hair is clean and moisturized, you can begin to create your protective style.

There are many different types of protective styles that you can try, including braids, twists, bantu knots, and more. To create a basic protective style, start by sectioning your hair into small sections. You can use clips to keep the sections separate and to prevent them from getting tangled.

Once your hair is sectioned, you can begin to create your protective style. For example, if you’re doing braids, you can start at the root of the hair and work your way down to the ends, braiding each section tightly. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is braided. Then, you can either wear your braids down or style them into an updo.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.

2. Section your hair into small sections.

3. Choose a protective style, such as braids or twists.

4. Begin creating your protective style, starting at the root of the hair and working your way down to the ends.

5. Repeat the process for each section of hair.

6. Style your protective style as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating and maintaining protective styles for curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Keep your hair moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet.
  • Avoid using tight hair accessories that can pull on your hair.

FAQ

What are some good protective styles for curly hair?

There are many different protective styles that you can try, including braids, twists, bantu knots, and more. The key is to choose a style that will keep your hair protected and low-manipulation.

How often should I do a protective style?

It’s a good idea to do a protective style once every few weeks. This will give your hair a break from daily manipulation and allow it to grow and thrive.

Can I still wash my hair while it’s in a protective style?

Yes, you can still wash your hair while it’s in a protective style. Just be careful not to disturb the style too much, and make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Hair Care

Protective styles are a great way to keep your curly hair healthy and looking great. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can create beautiful, low-manipulation styles that will help to protect your hair from damage and breakage. So why not give it a try?
