How To Cut My Hair Short Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're used to leaving it in the hands of a professional. However, with the right tools and technique, you can give yourself a stylish and professional-looking short haircut right at home. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know to confidently cut your hair short yourself.

How to Cut My Hair Short Myself

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties
  • Spray bottle of water

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start cutting. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles or knots that could make the cutting process more difficult. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to see the length and shape of the cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to create a part in the middle of your hair, then divide your hair into four sections: front, back, and two sides. Use hair ties to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Begin with the back section of your hair. Take the hair tie out and use the comb to separate a small section of hair at the nape of your neck. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut straight across. Repeat this process, working your way up the back of your head, until you've cut all the hair in the back section.

Step 4: Cut the Sides of Your Hair

Start with one side of your hair. Remove the hair tie and use the comb to separate a small section of hair near your ear. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle, following the shape of your head. Repeat this process, working your way up the side of your head, until you've cut all the hair on that side. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the Front of Your Hair

Finally, it's time to cut the front of your hair. Remove the hair tie from the front section and use the comb to separate a small section of hair near your forehead. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this process, working your way back towards the crown of your head, until you've cut all the hair in the front section.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use the scissors to trim any uneven pieces. If you want a shorter cut, you can use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. Use the mirror to check your hair from all angles and make any final adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short Yourself

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more off until you've achieved your desired length.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take breaks if you start to feel tired or frustrated.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member if you're having trouble.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair short myself?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and technique, anyone can give themselves a stylish and professional-looking short haircut right at home.

What tools do I need to cut my hair short myself?

You'll need scissors, a comb, clippers (optional), a mirror, hair ties, and a spray bottle of water.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! Take a break and step away from the mirror for a few minutes. Then, use the scissors to trim any uneven pieces and make any necessary adjustments.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair Short Yourself

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like blow dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair short yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut right at home. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care guidelines to ensure your short cut looks great and stays healthy.
