How To Check If A Hair Color Suits You

Written by Robby Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to check if a hair color suits you. Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're unsure if it will complement your skin tone or facial features. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to determine if a hair color suits you. We’ll also answer some frequently asked questions and provide some hair care tips. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if a Hair Color Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine if a Hair Color Suits You

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's discuss the factors that determine if a hair color will suit you or not. These factors include:

  • Skin Tone
  • Eye Color
  • Natural Hair Color
  • Facial Features

It's important to consider all of these factors when choosing a hair color. Keep in mind that some hair colors may look great on one person but may not suit another.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps you can follow to determine if a hair color suits you:

  1. Consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, consider hair colors such as golden blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool undertones, consider hair colors such as ash blonde, platinum, or auburn.
  2. Take your eye color into account. If you have blue eyes, consider hair colors such as platinum blonde, honey blonde, or light brown. If you have green eyes, consider hair colors such as red, copper, or auburn. If you have brown eyes, consider hair colors such as dark brown, black, or chestnut.
  3. Think about your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, consider going a few shades lighter or adding highlights. If you have light hair, consider going darker or adding lowlights.
  4. Consider your facial features. If you have a round face, consider hair colors that add dimension such as highlights or balayage. If you have a square face, consider hair colors that soften your features such as caramel or honey highlights.
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see what a hair color would look like on you before committing to it.
  6. Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Don't forget about your eyebrows. Consider tinting your eyebrows to match your new hair color.
  • Take care of your hair. Use products specifically designed for color-treated hair and avoid excessive heat styling.
  • Experiment with temporary hair color before committing to a permanent change.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining if a hair color suits you:

  • What if I have multiple skin tones? If you have a mix of warm and cool undertones, consider choosing a hair color that falls somewhere in between. For example, a neutral shade such as beige or champagne may work well.
  • What if I have gray hair? Consider choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. Gray hair tends to make cool undertones appear cooler, so consider warm hair colors such as golden blonde or copper.
  • What if I have a unique facial feature such as a birthmark or scar? Consider choosing a hair color that complements or distracts from the feature. For example, if you have a birthmark on your forehead, consider choosing a hair color with bangs to cover it up.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, which can cause damage and fade your hair color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine if a hair color suits you and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized advice and recommendations.
