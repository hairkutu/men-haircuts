How To Look After Frizzy Curly Hair Tips To Tame Your Mane

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 ¬∑ 8 min read

Frizzy curly hair can be a challenge to manage. It can be rough, dry, and prone to breakage. But with the right hair care routine, you can tame your mane and keep it looking healthy and shiny. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to look after frizzy curly hair.

How to Look After Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step in looking after frizzy curly hair is to understand your hair type. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair due to its shape, which makes it difficult for the natural oils produced by the scalp to reach the ends of the hair. Moreover, when curly hair is damaged, it becomes prone to frizz and breakage. To prevent this, you need to follow a hair care routine that nourishes and moisturizes your curls.

1. Wash your hair less frequently

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to frizz. Instead, wash your hair 2-3 times a week using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair and strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair and restore its moisture. Apply the treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends, and leave it on for 20-30 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your curls. This will help to hydrate and define your curls, while also reducing frizz. Apply the leave-in conditioner to damp hair and comb it through using a wide-tooth comb.

4. Don't towel dry your hair

Towel drying your hair can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the excess water out of your curls. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause friction and lead to frizz.

5. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair

If you blow-dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz and enhance your curls. Direct the airflow towards your roots and scrunch your curls upwards to help them hold their shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to look after frizzy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair 2-3 times a week using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair and restore its moisture.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair after washing and comb it through using a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Don't towel dry your hair. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the excess water out of your curls.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to minimize frizz and enhance your curls.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will minimize breakage and reduce frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What causes frizzy curly hair?

Frizzy curly hair is caused by a lack of moisture, damage, or humidity. When curly hair is dry, the cuticles on the hair shaft become raised, causing the hair to look frizzy. Damage, such as split ends, can also cause frizz. Humidity can cause curly hair to absorb excess moisture from the air, causing the hair to become swollen and frizzy.

What hair products are best for frizzy curly hair?

Some of the best hair products for frizzy curly hair include sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, deep conditioning treatments, leave-in conditioners, and hair oils. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain ingredients such as shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

How often should I deep condition my hair?

You should deep condition your hair once a week to nourish and moisturize your curls.

Essential Hair Care Products for Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some essential hair care products for frizzy curly hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Microfiber towel or old t-shirt
  • Diffuser attachment for blow-drying

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can manage your frizzy curly hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.
