Best Non Heated Hair Rollers Uk A Guide To Perfect Curls

If you're looking for a way to achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without the use of heat, then you're in the right place. Non-heated hair rollers are a great way to create curls that last all day long without damaging your hair. In this post, we'll be discussing the best non-heated hair rollers in the UK, how to use them, and some tips for maintaining your curls.

Description

Non-heated hair rollers come in a variety of shapes and sizes, including foam, sponge, and velcro rollers. They work by allowing your hair to cool in a curled position, which sets the curl in place. These types of rollers are ideal for those who want to avoid heat damage, have fine or delicate hair, or are looking for a more natural way to achieve curls.

How to Use Non-Heated Hair Rollers

Using non-heated hair rollers is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness of your hair. For thicker hair, you may need to create more sections.

Step 2: Roll Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and place the roller at the end. Roll your hair up towards your scalp, making sure the roller is secure. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 3: Wait for Your Hair to Set

Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes, or until your hair is completely dry. If you're short on time, you can use a hair dryer to speed up the process.

Step 4: Remove the Rollers

Once your hair is dry, carefully remove the rollers. Run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls and create a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to using non-heated hair rollers, then follow this step-by-step guide to achieve beautiful curls.

Step 1: Choose Your Rollers

There are many different types of non-heated hair rollers available, so choose the ones that are right for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you begin rolling.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, using clips to keep them in place.

Step 4: Roll Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and place the roller at the end. Roll your hair up towards your scalp, making sure the roller is secure. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Wait for Your Hair to Set

Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes, or until your hair is completely dry.

Step 6: Remove the Rollers

Carefully remove the rollers from your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls and create a more natural look. You can also use hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

To make your curls last longer, try these tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or foam before rolling your hair to give your curls more hold
  • Use hairspray to hold the curls in place
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after rolling it, as this can cause the curls to fall out
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to help maintain your curls overnight

FAQ

What are the best non-heated hair rollers in the UK?

Some of the best non-heated hair rollers in the UK include:

  • Denman Thermoceramic Self-Grip Rollers
  • Lee Stafford CoCo LoCo Wonderball Rollers
  • Babyliss Thermo-Ceramic Rollers
  • Toni & Guy Style Fix Waver Rollers

How long should I leave non-heated hair rollers in my hair?

You should leave non-heated hair rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes, or until your hair is completely dry.

Can you sleep in non-heated hair rollers?

You can sleep in non-heated hair rollers, but it's best to use soft, foam rollers to avoid discomfort.

Hair Care Tips

Using non-heated hair rollers is a great way to create beautiful curls without damaging your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent breakage.

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight when styling, as this can cause breakage and damage

By following these tips and using non-heated hair rollers, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that last all day long.
